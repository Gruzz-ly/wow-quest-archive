    ==Description== [[Kul Tiras]] must be unified before it can be of any help to the Alliance.

    Let's get you briefed on the current situation in each of our major territories.

    Then you can take a look at the map behind me and make the call on where you want to go first.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|1|94|}} * 1,650 [[XP]] * [[Come Sail Away]]

    ==Progress== Have you figured out your next move? Use the planning map to help you decide.

    We'll support you however we can.

    ==Notes== On acceptance, a short [[Battle for Azeroth in-game cinematics|in-engine cutscene]] plays as Taelia provides a brief introduction of the three zones' storylines: {{#ev:youtube|v39utRvCdik|urlArgs=start=9&end=97}} :{{text|say|Taelia|[[Kul Tiras]] is made up of several major houses. They were unified under [[Daelin Proudmoore|Daelin Proudmoore's]] leadership, but that gradually changed after [[Invasion of Durotar#Admiral's fall|his defeat at Theramore]].}} :{{text|say|Taelia|[[Katherine Proudmoore|Lord Admiral Katherine Proudmoore]] now rules in his place, commanding both the military and the guard.}} :{{text|say|Taelia|Despite our efforts to maintain order, Kul Tiras has seen an increase in crime, corruption, and piracy. People are losing faith in the [[Proudmoore Admiralty]].}} :{{text|say|Taelia|To the west lies [[Drustvar]], a mountainous region providing most of the kingdom's ore... and some of our toughest warriors.}} :{{text|say|Taelia|[[House Waycrest]] has been a loyal supporter of Proudmoore's rule for centuries, but their recent absence from council meetings has given rise to troubling rumors.}} :{{text|say|Taelia|In [[Stormsong Valley]], to the north, the [[tidesage]]s of [[House Stormsong]] craft and bless the ships of our legendary fleet.}} :{{text|say|Taelia|But nobody has heard from the fleet in months! The tidesages have withdrawn into their cloisters and seem uninterested in finding our missing ships.}} :{{text|say|Taelia|We must unite the houses beneath the Proudmoore banner and locate the missing fleet. Otherwise, Kul Tiras could fall apart before this war even begins.}}

    It is possible to replay the cutscene. :{{gossip|Can you brief me on Kul Tiras again?}}

    Interact with the [[Scouting Map]] behind Taelia to view the three scouting reports. Pick one to start that zone's storyline: ;[[Drustvar (quest)]]: Lord and Lady Waycrest are strong allies of the Proudmoores. Investigate their sudden absence and the troubling rumors from Drustvar. ;[[Stormsong Valley (quest)]]: The legendary Kul Tiran fleet has gone missing. Seek answers from the reclusive ocean mystics of House Stormsong. ;[[Tiragarde Sound (quest)]]: Expose the corruption in Boralus and earn Katherine Proudmoore's trust.

    This concludes the introduction to Kul Tiras and the [[Battle for Azeroth intro experience]]. Next, [[Genn Greymane]] offers [[The War Campaign (Alliance)]], and get started on the chosen zone storyline.
