    Siege of Boralus: Lady Ashvane's Return

    {{Questbox | id = 52153 | name = Siege of Boralus: Lady Ashvane's Return | category = Siege of Boralus | type = Dungeon | faction = Alliance | level = {{level|Siege of Boralus}} | levelreq = {{level|Siege of Boralus min}} | start = [[Katherine Proudmoore]] | end = [[Taelia]] | reputation = +350 [[Proudmoore Admiralty]] | rewards = [[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]]) | money = {{cost|93|60|}} | previous = [[Thros, the Blighted Lands (quest)]] | next = [[A Nation United (quest)]] }}

    ==Objectives== Complete the [[Siege of Boralus]] dungeon. * Siege of Boralus completed

    ==Description== If [[Priscilla Ashvane|Ashvane]] thinks we will sit idly by as she attempts a coup, she is sorely mistaken. The citizens of [[Boralus]] will fight her to a man.

    I'll need you on the front lines to aid them, . Go with [[Taelia]] to the docks and engage the enemy.

    With a little luck, we may just make it out of this alive.

    ==Rewards== * {{cost|93|60|}} * +350 reputation with [[Proudmoore Admiralty]] [[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]])

    ==Completion== That was incredible! I had no idea [[Jaina Proudmoore|Lady Jaina]] was so powerful.

    ==Notes== [[File:Ashvane invasion fleet.jpg|thumb|Ready to strike.]] While the quest is active, the Ashvane invasion fleet can be seen in [[Boralus Harbor]], ready to attack it. The [[battleship]]s and [[destroyer]]s are crewed by [[Great Sea Privateer]]s.

    On accept: :{{text|say|Katherine Proudmoore|Taelia! Take and muster the militia. Jaina and I will prepare the defenses.}} : ''Katherine turns to Jaina.'' :{{text|say|Katherine Proudmoore|It seems our homecoming will have to wait.}} : ''Jaina salutes.'' :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Understood... [[Lord Admiral]].}} : ''The Proudmoores head into the keep. Taelia turns to you.'' :{{text|say|Taelia|I'll head to the outskirts and alert the citizens. Hurry to the docks. I'll meet you there!}} : ''She mounts [[Galeheart]] and takes off.''

    If you look out into the harbor, you'll see eight pirate ships loaded with privateers: three main ships that are the same class as ''[[The Relentless]]'', and five [[transport ship]]s of the same design built by the [[garrison]] in [[Draenor (alternate universe)|alternate Draenor]].

    This quest requires completing the [[Siege of Boralus]] dungeon on any difficulty (Heroic is available since patch 8.1, while previously it was Mythic-only). However, the following cinematic appears to only work on Mythic difficulty.

    :{{text|yell|Lady Priscilla Ashvane|You dare scar my beautiful pet?!}} :{{text|say|Jaina Proudmoore|You've done well, heroes, but Boralus is not out of danger yet.}} :{{text|say|Jaina Proudmoore|Speak to me when you're ready to put an end to Ashvane's fleet.}}

    Speak to Jaina. :You've done so much for Boralus, this day, heroes. Now it's my turn. :{{Gossip|Finish the fight.}} :{{gossip|I'm ready to leave.}}
