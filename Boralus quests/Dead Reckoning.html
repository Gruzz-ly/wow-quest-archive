    ==Objectives== Attend the discussion in [[Unity Square]]. * Speak with [[Anduin Wrynn]] * Attend the meeting

    ==Description== While I have some concerns, I cannot deny the bravery, heroism and sacrifices we have made. That you have made, . Thank you for all the aid you have given to the [[Alliance]].

    The future of our [[Alliance War Campaign|ongoing war campaign]] is why I have asked everyone here. I wish to look at what we've done, so we can decide on the best way forward.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|11|70|}} * 8,950 [[XP]] *[[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]]) * +2,000 reputation with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]

    ==Progress== This war may have been necessary, but it is my hope it will be over soon. Then we can begin rebuilding.

    ==Completion== We may be on the cusp of winning but we cannot lose sight of ourselves or we will have lost what is most important and our victories will be for nothing.

    I hope it will be as [[Halford Wyrmbane|High Commander Wyrmbane]] and [[Mathias Shaw|Master Shaw]] say it is and we will see a conclusion soon. Then... Then we can begin the process of rebuilding and healing - Ourselves as well as [[Azeroth]] and the [[Horde]].

    ==Notes== ;Speak with Anduin Wrynn There can be no peace without action.
