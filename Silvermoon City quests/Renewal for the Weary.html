    Objectives

    Restore the Light to 10 weary channelers. Stay Awhile and Listen (Optional) Sunwell Channeler Renewed (10) *Faol's Benediction (provided)

    Description

    The Vanguard of the Light are maintaining a difficult ritual at the Sunwell. It requires focus, discipline and is quite exhausting.

    Take my staff. I've had it since I was Archbishop in Stormwind. It is meaningful to me and my faith. The Light responds to meaning and intention. It should renew the Vanguard's spirits so they can maintain the ritual a while longer.

    I can't approach any closer to the Sunwell, so you will have to do this for me.

    Rewards

    You will receive one of: * Wraps of Renewal * Gloves of Renewal * Hands of Renewal * Touch of Renewal

    You will also receive: 23g 40s 13,365 XP

    Completion

    Thank you, , for doing it in his stead. It was necessary to keep the Voidstorm at bay.
