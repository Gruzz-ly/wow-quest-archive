    The King and the Council

    {{questbox | image=The King and the Council - Varian and the SI7.jpg | imagecaption=Varian and the SI:7 | name=The King and the Council | start=[[Lorewalker Cho]] {{coords|83|29.8|Vale of Eternal Blossoms}} | id=32806 | type=Scenario | level={{level|Vale of Eternal Blossoms}} | category=Pandaren Campaign | experience=471000 | rewards=[[Greater Cache of Treasures]] | money={{cost|45|60}} | next=[[The Warchief and the Darkness]] }}

    == Objectives == Complete the "[[Blood in the Snow]]" scenario. You can queue from the dungeon finder or use [[Lorewalker Cho's Dream Brew]]. *"Blood in the Snow" scenario complete

    == Description == I am glad you are here, . I have been using my dream brew to experience events around the world, but the more I learn, the more confused I become.

    For instance, [[Varian Wrynn|King Wrynn]] has asked the [[dwarf|dwarves]] of [[Ironforge]] for more troops, but they do not all support him.

    I will tell you more, but I would also like to hear from your experience firsthand...

    == Rewards == You will receive: {{cost|45|60}} * 471000 [[XP]] [[Greater Cache of Treasures]]

    == Progress == It is not my place to make stories. I simply tell them.

    == Completion == So, King Wrynn and the [[Dark Iron clan|Dark Iron dwarves]] saved the city, and bitterly divided [[Council of Three Hammers]] gained a new unity under the Alliance banner. How interesting!

    But what is happening within the Horde?

    == Notes == [[File:The King and the Council - Zandalari appear.jpg|thumb|Zandalari appear]] [[File:The King and the Council - Council of Three Hammers.jpg|thumb|The Council of Three Hammers]] [[File:The King and the Council - Moira bowing to Varian.jpg|thumb|Moira bowing to Varian]]

    As a breadcrumb to start this quest, [[Lorewalker Cho]] sends a letter via the in-game mail system: {{book|Please See Me in the Vale!|content=Friend!

      My humble land has become the center of a worldwide storm. Events are moving very quickly between the Alliance and the Horde. Can you help me make sense of it all? Please visit me at the [[Seat of Knowledge]] in the [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms]], above the entrance to [[Mogu'shan Palace]]. We must speak!

      Your friend,
      Lorewalker Cho}}

    Get there and speak with him to start this quest. On accept, Cho begins a story: :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Once upon a time - moments ago, in fact - a great [[Varian Wrynn|King]] sought the help from a trio of powerful [[dwarf|dwarves]]: The [[Council of Three Hammers]].}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|The King's [[SI:7|scouts]] had discovered that [[Zandalar tribe|Zandalari trolls]] threatened the [[Ironforge|dwarven capital]].}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Surely, the king thought, if he helped them with their [[troll]] problem, they would give him more troops for his campaign against the [[Horde]].}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|But what the King did not realize is that the dwarves did not trust one another!}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|"We cannot help you," said the [[Muradin Bronzebeard|Bronzebeard dwarf]], "For if we turn our back on the [[Dark Iron clan|Dark Irons]], they will defeat the [[Wildhammer clan|Wildhammer]] and take over Ironforge!"}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|"We cannot help you," said the [[Kurdran Wildhammer|Wildhammer dwarf]]. "For if we turn our back on the Dark Irons, they will defeat the [[Bronzebeard clan|Bronzebeard]] and take over Ironforge!"}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|The King was furious. "If none of you help, then Ironforge will fall under siege!"}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Only one dwarf offered to help the King. "We will lend you our strength," said [[Moira Thaurissan|Moira]], of the Dark Iron. "We will trust our dwarven brothers and we will show them what loyalty means!"}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|And so Moira and the High King set out in the snow to save the city, leaving the others to think about their actions.}} :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|What happens next? I must know!}} Use the [[Dungeon Finder]] interface or [[Lorewalker Cho's Dream Brew]] on the table at the base of the steps to queue for the [[Blood in the Snow]] scenario. See the scenario's article for a walkthrough.
