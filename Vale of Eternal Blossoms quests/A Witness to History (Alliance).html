    A Witness to History (Alliance)

    {{questbox | image=Gate of the August Celestials.jpg | imagecaption=Gate of the August Celestials | name=A Witness to History | start=Anduin Wrynn | faction=Alliance | id=31512 | level= | category=Kun-Lai Summit | end=Matron Vi Vinh | reputation=+75 Golden Lotus | experience=64000 | money=5g 10s | previous=A Celestial Experience (Alliance) | other-faction = A Witness to History (Horde) }}

    Objectives

    Speak with Anduin Wrynn at the Gate of the August Celestials. *Speak to Anduin at the Gate of the August Celestials

    Description

    The celestials are allowing us back into the Vale! This will be a sight to behold.

    We shouldn't waste any time. Head to the Gate of the August Celestials at the southern edge of Kun-Lai Summit and speak with me there.

    Rewards

    You will receive: 5g 10s 64000 XP +75 reputation with Golden Lotus*

    Completion

    Welcome to the Shrine of Seven Stars.

    I wish I could bring you better tidings, , but the situation here in the Vale is quite dire.

    There is much to do, but first, rest, and explore the city.

    Welcome to the Vale.

    Notes

    thumb|Celestials arriving thumb|Yu'lon speaking thumb|Gate opened

    Exit the temple and bank right to get to the flight master across the bridge. Fly to Westwind Rest and run due south to get to the gate. Speak with Anduin: :This is a historic moment, . What do you think lies beyond the gates? : :''A cutscene begins as the camera pulls back to show a wide view of the Gate of the August Celestials. The four August Celestials arrive, setting up in front of the gate: Niuzao stands sentinel in front of the right statue as Chi-Ji does the same on the left. Xuen walks up, directly facing the doors as Yu'lon flys up and hovers to the left of Xuen as all of the onlookers kneel before them. Sunwalker Dezco walks up to flank Xuen's left as Anduin does the same on the right. As they also kneel, the player phases in between them and kneels as well.'' : :''Xuen steps forward and makes a deep, earth-trembling roar, at which the gates' inner gear system unlocks and the doors swing forward on their own.     The Kun-Lai Refugees have had their lines removed and despawn shortly after entering the now-ruined Vale. Matron Vi Vinh also ends this quest and the quest chain, explaining after turn-in where a player can go.

    :

    The Vale of Eternal Blossoms is not phased, so walking through the gate leads the players into the destroyed Vale, complete with level 90 sha mobs. Players below level 90 must therefore exercise caution when entering.
