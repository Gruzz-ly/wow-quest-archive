    Stage 1: The Broken Shore

    thumb|World map :''Travel to the Broken Shore.''

    Once the cutscene finishes, the player is on Captain Russo's ship, sailing toward the Broken Shore. Up to 40 players (20 Alliance and 20 Horde) can take part in the scenario and the difficulty will scale to handle all comers. In the first twenty seconds, other players who also say they are ready to start the scenario will join the instance automatically. After 20 seconds, the NPCs start talking:

    : : : : : : : : : :''The ship turns, and the fel energy above the Tomb of Sargeras comes into view.'' : : :''A cutscene takes place as siege cannons start bombarding the ships with fel fireballs.''

    Stage 2: Storm The Beach

    thumb|A spire of woe. :''Destroy all demons and structures on the beach.'' 33 Demons slain 3 Fel Lords slain *3 Spires of Woe destroyed

    Once the cutscene finishes, Captain Russo's ship is now ashore. On the beach, Vol'jin and Thrall are each leading small squads of Darkspear Headhunters, Darkspear Witch Doctors and Earthen Ring Shaman. Several Cannons-In-Boxes are also here, manned by Bilgewater Blastmasters. A few Silvermoon Blood Knights are also there. Many Horde ships are wrecked around the shoreline and several Wind Riders as well as Riding Bats are dead on the ground. Players then gain the For the Horde buff which makes them stronger.

    : : : : :

    The NPCs charge toward one of the three spires, guarded by 11 demons (among them Felguard Legionnaires and Felstalker Dreadhounds) and a Fel Lord (among them Fel Lord Kurrz, Fel Lord Darakk and Fel Lord Razzar) each. Start killing the demons as well as the Anchoring Crystals surrounding the Spires of Woe. Thrall realizes that they're important: : :

    ;Random yells * * * * *

    After destroying the first spire: : : :

    In small groups, follow the NPCs - don't take the lead. Let Vol'jin and Thrall tank! Repeat the process for the second spire: : :

    Destroy the third spire to advance to the next stage.

    Stage 4: Find The Others

    thumb|A Legion ship bombarding the Alliance's forces. :''Locate Sylvanas and Baine.'' *Sylvanas and Baine found : : : :

    Vol'jin, Thrall, and their troops start running uphill to the north. Keep up. : : :

    As the party crests the hill, looking over a ridge to the north, the Tomb of Sargeras itself comes into view. A cutscene shows the environs, where a huge demon city and the wreck of the Horde gunship that transported Sylvanas Windrunner can be seen:

    : : :''Legion ships start warping in as the camera tracks to Sylvanas' position, with Baine in tow.'' : :''Sylvanas is surrounded by Dark Rangers, Deathguard Elites, Silvermoon Priests and Thunder Bluff Braves. The cutscene ends as Sylvanas' forces are about to attack another band of demons. The Legion ships starts bombarding the area.'' : : Follow Vol'jin and Thrall down the hill, dodging the bombardment.

    If you die here, you will be resurrected by Horde Priests working similarly to those in battlegrounds.

    Stage 5: Destroy the Portal

    :''Destroy the demon portal to stop reinforcements.'' *Shielded Anchors shattered. (4) : : : : :

    Finish off the Mo'arg Painbringer, then rush the portal. Another painbringer will run out of the portal, but let the NPCs tank it. Focus instead on the Eredar Chaos Guards protecting the Shielded Anchors.

    ;Random yells * * * * * *

    Stage 6: Raze the Black City

    thumb|The Black City seen from a ridge. :''Assault the demon city.'' * Assault the demon city. (0/100%)

    : : : :

    As the party is about to enter the city, many dead Argent Lightbringers can be seen in a gruesome display. They come across a live but wounded one: : : : :

    Many of the crusaders are impaled on spikes or put on body piles, but some of them are still alive and can be saved. Start killing demons, freeing trapped crusaders, commandeering unattended cannons, breaking portals, destroying spires, and so on. Don't wander around alone - stick with the friendly NPCs! Freed crusaders will act as guardians to the player. Doom Shrooms, Moongrasses, Chaos Clovers, Bleakthorns and Felblooms are local plants that can be clicked on to provide buffs.

    Fill the bar to continue.

    Stage 8: Krosus

    :''Kill Krosus.'' * Krosus slain. : :

    Krosus runs through the lava, stopping at the edge of the pool, conveniently in range of melee attacks. As always, let the NPCs tank. The Krosus encounter is a tank-and spank, and Krosus has a Slam attack that damages enemies within ~10 yards or so, an Orb of Destruction that drops a fireball on a ~30-yard radius area of effect, and a channeled Fel Storm that drops fel fireballs all over the arena for a few seconds. Every 25%, he will swap to face the other faction, and the Alliance gets first crack.

    At 75%, Krosus turns to attack the Horde. : :

    As the Horde were dealing with trash mobs in the first phase, so now will Alliance deal with trash mobs from 75-50%.

    At 50%, Krosus turns back to face the Alliance. : :

    Rinse and repeat down to 25%: : :

    Clear out the trash mobs, then finish Krosus off.

Now, if you approach the demon portal too much you will be insta-killed by the gaze of Sargeras himself,http://www.wowhead.com/spell=181559/gaze-of-sargeras with the message "''You feel a staggering presence on the other side of the portal touch you for a moment...''". : : : :

    The demonic hordes become even more numerous on the ridge as Varian continues to battle Gul'dan's forces down below: : : :

    More demons, as well as blasts of felfire coming from Legion ships summoned by the eredar Vizuul the Twisted. All of the demons who will later be leading Legion Invasions are also there. : : : :

    The next wave starts to attack as a cutscene plays, ending the scenario and the quest.
