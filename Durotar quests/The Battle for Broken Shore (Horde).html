    The Battle for Broken Shore (Horde)

    {{Questbox |id=40518 |name=The Battle for Broken Shore |faction=Horde |category=Durotar |level={{level|legion}} |start=[[Stone Guard Mukar]] {{co|55.6|11.0|Durotar}} |end=[[Eitrigg]] {{co|57.2|10.5|Durotar}} |rewards=Item level 685 weapon |shareable=No |previous=[[To Be Prepared (Horde)]] |next=[[Fate of the Horde]] |other-faction=The Battle for Broken Shore (Alliance) }}

    ==Objectives== Take [[Captain Russo]]'s ship to the [[Broken Shore]]. * Ship taken to the Broken Shore * Broken Shore assaulted

    ==Description== Your [[warship]] awaits at the end of the dock, . May your weapon bring you many kills today.

    [[Vol'jin|The Warchief]] has already made landfall, I will wager you will simply need to follow the trail of [[demon]] blood.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Bonegrinder of the Horde Vanguard |Longbow of the Horde Vanguard |Dagger of the Horde Vanguard |Poleaxe of the Horde Vanguard |Quickblade of the Horde Vanguard |Battle Mace of the Horde Vanguard |Staff of the Horde Vanguard |Mageblade of the Horde Vanguard |Waraxe of the Horde Vanguard |Splitter of the Horde Vanguard }}

    ==Completion== Glad to see you back on your feet, . Many good soldiers who sailed for the Broken Shore were not so fortunate.

    ==Notes== ;[[Captain Russo]] :Welcome aboard, .

    :We're all stocked and ready to ship out just as soon as you're ready.

    ''{{gossip|I am ready to face the Legion.}}''

    ''Are you prepared to face the Legion at the Broken Shore?'' Accept/Cancel

    :''A cutscene plays, showing the Horde fleet shipping out.''

    Inside the scenario begins the Horde perspective of the [[battle for the Broken Shore]].

    ===Stage 1: The Broken Shore=== [[File:WorldMap-BrokenShoreIntro.jpg|thumb|World map]] :''Travel to the Broken Shore.''

    Once the cutscene finishes, the player is on [[Captain Russo]]'s ship, sailing toward the Broken Shore. Up to 40 players (20 Alliance and 20 Horde) can take part in the scenario and the difficulty will scale to handle all comers. In the first twenty seconds, other players who also say they are ready to start the scenario will join the instance automatically. After 20 seconds, the NPCs start talking:

    :{{text|say|Captain Russo|Smell that sea air! It feels good to be out in the open water again.}} :{{text|say|[[Du'pre]]|I'm sure dat payment from Orgrimmar had nothin' ta do with it.}} :{{text|say|Captain Russo|There's an old goblin saying that goes "the best bets use other people's money."}} :{{text|say|Du'pre|Dat assumes we gonna make it back.}} :{{text|say|Captain Russo|Any messages from Orgrimmar?}} :{{text|say|Du'pre|[[Sylvanas Windrunner|Sylvanas]] an' da main fleet arrived a few hours ago. Demons coverin' da whole island.}} :{{text|say|Captain Russo|Already? How'd they get so many here so fast?}} :{{text|say|Du'pre|I got da feelin' we gonna find out.}} :{{text|say|Captain Russo|Alright Horde troops, this is your stop, a beach lined with demons! Bring her in snappy now!}} :''The ship turns, and the fel energy above the [[Tomb of Sargeras]] comes into view.'' :{{text|say|Captain Russo|What's that ahead?}} :{{text|say|Du'pre|Looks like trouble. Big trouble.}} :''A cutscene takes place as siege cannons start bombarding the ships with fel fireballs.''

    ===Stage 2: Storm The Beach=== [[File:Spire of Woe.jpg|thumb|A [[spire of woe]].]] :''Destroy all demons and structures on the beach.'' 33 Demons slain 3 Fel Lords slain *3 Spires of Woe destroyed

    Once the cutscene finishes, [[Captain Russo]]'s ship is now ashore. On the beach, [[Vol'jin]] and [[Thrall]] are each leading small squads of [[Darkspear Headhunter]]s, [[Darkspear Witch Doctor]]s and [[Earthen Ring Shaman (Battle for the Broken Shore)|Earthen Ring Shaman]]. Several [[Cannon-In-A-Box|Cannons-In-Boxes]] are also here, manned by [[Bilgewater Blastmaster]]s. A few [[Silvermoon Blood Knight (Broken Shore)|Silvermoon Blood Knights]] are also there. Many Horde ships are wrecked around the shoreline and several [[Wind Rider (NPC)|Wind Riders]] as well as [[Riding Bat]]s are dead on the ground. Players then gain the '''For the Horde''' buff which makes them stronger.

    :{{text|say|Thrall|Looks like our reinforcements have arrived.}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Good ting! We don't got much beach left ta retreat to.}} :{{text|say|Thrall|There's only one way out of this one, old friend.}} :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|Listen up! Dere's only one way offa dis beach, and it be t'rough alla dose demons ahead of us. But dey don't know who dey be dealin' wit. Let's show dem what it means ta be Horde!}} :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|Keep them away from the spires!}}

    The NPCs charge toward one of the three spires, guarded by 11 demons (among them [[Felguard Legionnaire (Legion)|Felguard Legionnaires]] and [[Felstalker Dreadhound]]s) and a Fel Lord (among them [[Fel Lord Kurrz]], [[Fel Lord Darakk]] and [[Fel Lord Razzar]]) each. Start killing the demons as well as the [[Anchoring Crystal]]s surrounding the [[Spire of woe|Spires of Woe]]. Thrall realizes that they're important: :{{text|yell|Thrall|There's something odd about the crystals surrounding that spire. They feel... wrong... like they're in two places at once. Hmm.}} :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|Horde, make for dem crystals! We gonna see if ya hunch is right.}}

    ;Random yells {{text|yell|Thrall|Back to the Nether!}} {{text|yell|Thrall|Lok'tar ogar!}} {{text|yell|Vol'jin|Die.}} {{text|yell|Vol'jin|Dis one's mine.}} *{{text|yell|Vol'jin|For da Horde!}}

    After destroying the first spire: :{{text|yell|Thrall|Yes... they must anchor it to this world.}} :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|Two more ta go.}} :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|Crush them!}}

    In small groups, follow the NPCs - don't take the lead. Let Vol'jin and Thrall tank! Repeat the process for the second spire: :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|Keep it up! We almost got dem!}} :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|Your corpses will feed my hounds.}}

    Destroy the third spire to advance to the next stage.

    ===Stage 3: Defeat the Commander=== :''Slay Fel Commander Azgalor.'' * [[Fel Commander Azgalor]] slain

    :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|Weaklings. Must I do everything myself? Very well, prepare yourselves!}} :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|You gonna die just like all da others.}} :{{text|yell|Thrall|Focus on the commander!}}

    Azgalor lands in the center of the beach. Again, let the NPCs tank him. He has the same area of effect attacks as the fel lords, so they're easy to dodge.

    At 75%: :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|You will all burn!}} :{{text|yell|Thrall|Felfire rains from the sky! Look out!}}

    At 30%: :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|Looks like you chose da wrong side.}} :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|The master will mend my flesh again and again. You have not won. You cannot win. We are endless. We are Legion!}}

    Death: :{{text|yell|Fel Commander Azgalor|I... will.. return...}}

    ===Stage 4: Find The Others=== [[File:The Battle for Broken Shore - Felship.jpg|thumb|A [[Legion ship]] bombarding the Alliance's forces.]] :''Locate Sylvanas and Baine.'' *Sylvanas and Baine found :{{text|say|Thrall|It is good to fight with you again, Warchief.}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Ain't never gonna get used to you callin' me dat.}} :{{text|say|Thrall|There's only one way to the tomb, are you ready?}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Let's get movin'.}}

    Vol'jin, Thrall, and their troops start running uphill to the north. Keep up. :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Sylvanas' ship must 'av crashed just ahead.}} :{{text|say|Thrall|They're expanding faster than we've seen before.}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Ya, mon. Dis ain't da same Legion.}}

    As the party crests the hill, looking over a ridge to the north, the [[Tomb of Sargeras]] itself comes into view. A cutscene shows the environs, where a huge [[Black City|demon city]] and the [[Darkspear's Might|wreck]] of the Horde [[gunship]] that transported [[Sylvanas Windrunner]] can be seen:

    :{{text|say|Thrall|What?}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Dat's no invasion force. Dat's an army!}} :''[[Legion ship]]s start warping in as the camera tracks to Sylvanas' position, with Baine in tow.'' :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Nock your arrows! Hold! Fire!}} :''Sylvanas is surrounded by [[Dark Ranger (Broken Shore)|Dark Rangers]], [[Deathguard Elite]]s, [[Silvermoon Priest]]s and [[Thunder Bluff Brave (Broken Shore)|Thunder Bluff Braves]]. The cutscene ends as Sylvanas' forces are about to attack another band of demons. The Legion ships starts bombarding the area.'' :{{text|yell|Thrall|They're alive!}} :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|Let's move!}} Follow Vol'jin and Thrall down the hill, dodging the bombardment.

    If you die here, you will be resurrected by [[Horde Priest (Broken Shore)|Horde Priests]] working similarly to those in [[battleground]]s.

    ===Stage 5: Destroy the Portal=== :''Destroy the demon portal to stop reinforcements.'' *Shielded Anchors shattered. (4) :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|It's about time you showed up!}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|You looked like you could use a hand.}} :{{text|say|Baine Bloodhoof|Their reinforcements are endless!}} :{{text|say|Thrall|Those crystals again... if we can get close to them, we can bring this thing down.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|You heard him, rangers, we cut those demons down! Fire a volley on my mark... NOW!}}

    Finish off the [[Mo'arg Painbringer]], then rush the portal. Another painbringer will run out of the portal, but let the NPCs tank it. Focus instead on the [[Eredar Chaos Guard]]s protecting the [[Shielded Anchor]]s.

    ;Random yells {{text|say|Baine Bloodhoof|To the earth with you!}} {{text|say|Baine Bloodhoof|Take heart, heroes!}} {{text|say|Baine Bloodhoof|For the Earth Mother!}} {{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|One shot, one kill.}} {{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Too easy.}} {{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Right to the face!}}

    ===Stage 6: Raze the Black City=== [[File:The Battle for Broken Shore - Black City.jpg|thumb|The [[Black City]] seen from a ridge.]] :''Assault the demon city.'' * Assault the demon city. (0/100%)

    :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Well, that was invigorating. Now how did they manage to get so many demons here so quickly?}} :{{text|say|Thrall|This invasion is unlike any I have ever witnessed, and I don't think we've seen the last of their surprises.}} :{{text|say|Baine Bloodhoof|These [[Legion Structures|structures]] rose from the ground in moments, spewing forth demons by the hundreds in the blink of an eye.}} :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|And now we know how to destroy them. It's them or us. Let's keep moving to make sure it's not us.}}

    As the party is about to enter the city, many dead [[Argent Lightbringer]]s can be seen in a gruesome display. They come across a live but wounded one: :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|This is [[Argent Crusade]] insignia, can you speak? Where is the rest of the crusade?}} :{{text|say|Argent Lightbringer|Dead... dying... they descended on us in seconds... nowhere to run...}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|An no sign of [[Tirion Fordring|Tirion]].}} :{{text|yell|Thrall|We will find him. Horde forces, keep your eyes open for survivors, rescue as many as you are able!}}

    Many of the crusaders are impaled on spikes or put on body piles, but some of them are still alive and can be saved. Start killing demons, freeing trapped crusaders, commandeering unattended cannons, breaking portals, destroying spires, and so on. Don't wander around alone - stick with the friendly NPCs! Freed crusaders will act as guardians to the player. [[Doom Shroom]]s, [[Moongrass]]es, [[Chaos Clover]]s, [[Bleakthorn]]s and [[Felbloom]]s are local plants that can be clicked on to provide buffs.

    Fill the bar to continue.

    ===Stage 7: The Highlord=== :''Get to Tirion.'' *Tirion found

    :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Dey be retreatin'.}} :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|This was too easy, something's wrong.}} :{{text|yell|Thrall|There, across the chasm, {{Au|Gul'dan}}! He has Tirion!}} :''The camera tracks across the chasm and some ways away to find Tirion, suspended over a pit of fel lava by Gul'dan!'' :{{text|yell|Vol'jin|All da Horde, get ta Thrall!}}

    On the other side of the cutscene, all players and NPCs have been teleported to Thrall's position near the chasm. He is forming an earth bridge to clear the gap. :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Anyone have any ideas how we're going to get across this thing?}} :{{text|say|Thrall|Spirits of earth, aid me!}} :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Convenient.}}

    Cross the earth bridge and follow the trail of dead [[Argent Lightbringer]]s to the [[Tomb Approach]]. [[Tirion Fordring]], Gul'dan, and the Alliance forces will be there as well. :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Tirion!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Gul'dan! You will pay for this!}}

    :{{text|say|Highlord Tirion Fordring|Stay back... it's a trap... the [[Light]] will protect me...}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Hah, you fool! You stand before the temple of a GOD. Your pitiful Light cannot reach you here. How fortunate that your friends have arrived just in time to watch you die.}} :''[[Krosus]], a boss-level demon, appears from the lava.'' :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Destroy him.}} :''Krosus channels some kind of breath attack on Tirion.'' :{{text|yell|Highlord Tirion Fordring|The Light... will... aah! Aaahhhh!!}} :''Tirion's lifeless corpse falls beneath the surface of the lava pool.'' :{{text|yell|Thrall|Fordring!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|No!}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|All you have worked for, all you have sacrificed, just to see your champions fall to ash one by one.}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Destroy them!}}

    ===Stage 8: Krosus=== :''Kill Krosus.'' * [[Krosus]] slain. :{{text|yell|Krosus|You will know fear.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Monsters from the felfire! Horde forces, be ready!}}

    Krosus runs through the lava, stopping at the edge of the pool, conveniently in range of melee attacks. As always, let the NPCs tank. The Krosus encounter is a tank-and spank, and Krosus has a Slam attack that damages enemies within ~10 yards or so, an Orb of Destruction that drops a fireball on a ~30-yard radius area of effect, and a channeled Fel Storm that drops fel fireballs all over the arena for a few seconds. Every 25%, he will swap to face the other faction, and the Alliance gets first crack.

    At 75%, Krosus turns to attack the Horde. :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|He's heading your way, Windrunner!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Winderunner|Let's go, Horde, it's time to show the Alliance how we slay demons.}}

    As the Horde were dealing with trash mobs in the first phase, so now will Alliance deal with trash mobs from 75-50%.

    At 50%, Krosus turns back to face the Alliance. :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|All yours, Alliance.}} :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|We've got him this time! All forces, concentrate your fire!}}

    Rinse and repeat down to 25%: :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|You've got another shot at the kill, Sylvanas!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Winderunner|Consider your job done then, Wrynn.}}

    Clear out the trash mobs, then finish Krosus off.

    ===Final Stage: Hold the Ridge=== :''Hold the ridge until the Alliance have stopped Gul'dan.'' * Ridge held.

    :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Ha, the bigger they are...}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Winderunner|No time to gloat, Wrynn. We've got to stop Gul'dan before he summons another one of those things!}}

    Thrall quickly summons another earth bridge to get up to the ridge overlooking Gul'dan. Just on the other side, Varian and Sylvanas pause for a moment. :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|We'll take the ridge and cover your flank.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|Sylvanas, thank you.}} :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Good luck, Varian.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|And you.}}

    At the top of the ridge, a great line of demons approaches as Sylvanas and her dark rangers take up position to support the Alliance as Varian confronts Gul'dan below: :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|There is nowhere to run, Gul'dan. Give up now, and I will grant you a swift death.}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Run? Ha. You are right, human. There is nowhere to run from my master. Now, witness the true might of Lord Sargeras!}}

    After the first group of demons is felled, another group spawns. Now, if you approach the demon portal too much you will be insta-killed by the gaze of [[Sargeras]] himself,http://www.wowhead.com/spell=181559/gaze-of-sargeras with the message "''You feel a staggering presence on the other side of the portal touch you for a moment...''". :{{text|say|Baine Bloodhoof|Down the ridge behind us, another portal!}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|We've got ta hold dis ridge, no matter da cost.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Take up positions. We hold them off. We have to.}} :{{text|say|Vol'jin|Even more demons be pouring from that portal. Get ready!}}

    The demonic hordes become even more numerous on the ridge as Varian continues to battle Gul'dan's forces down below: :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|We will never fall to you, Gul'dan. The combined forces of Azeroth will triumph today.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Less chatting, Wrynn. Kill him already!}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|I have seen the end of your pitiful world, Wrynn. You will perish in felfire, like ten thousand worlds before your own. The legion is ENDLESS.}}

    More demons, as well as blasts of felfire coming from [[Legion ship]]s summoned by the eredar [[Vizuul the Twisted]]. All of the demons who will later be leading [[Legion Invasion]]s are also there. :{{text|yell|Baine Bloodhoof|I cannot... take much more of this...}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Pull Baine back to my position, close ranks!}} :{{text|yell|Thrall|There are... too many of them...}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Everyone pull back to me! We make our stand here!}}

    The next wave starts to attack as a cutscene plays, ending the scenario and the quest.
