    Objectives

    Enjoy the feast! Enjoy the Feast! (100%) Brawl with Boss Magor and the Peons

    Description

    You have successfully completed the om'gora! Congratulations, .

    Now it is time for us to celebrate! We have prepared a feast for all in attendance, in your honor!

    Go, revel in the glory you have earned. Enjoy yourself. If any of these orcs get out of line, remind them why you were the one who was chosen to complete the new rite first!

    Rewards

    You will receive: Money* XP*

    Progress

    The Kosh'harg has been a great success, better than I had dared hope. Have you had your fill of the festivities?

    Completion

    Yes, I think it's time we draw this celebration to a close. There is such a thing as partying too hard!

    Notes

    On accept: :

    The screen fades to black. Upon fading back in, Razor Hill has entered a new phase: the feast is in full swing and almost all of the named visitors are now crowding around the tables, which are loaded with food. The player has a Guest of Honor debuff, occasionally causing nearby Rowdy Grunts and Warlocks to turn hostile and challenge them to a brawl. :

    Filling up the quest bar is done by defeating brawlers (5%) or by eating the food on the tables, each type of which gives a different 5-minute buff: Chimearok Chops with Cheese*: (2%) Grilled Talbuk Steak: (2%) Hearty Clefthoof Ribs: (2%) Oasis Crawfish Tails*: (2%) Plainstrider Drumsticks: (2%) Whole Roast Boar: (10%) Orcish Brew*: Gets the character slightly drunk. (2%) The Strong Stuff: Gets the character very drunk. (5%) Blackrock Coffee: Sobers the character up.

    thumb|Boss Magor and the Peons. In addition, Boss Magor is standing at the northern end of town with his Orc Peons and can be challenged to a brawl: :The Kosh'harg has gone off without a hitch! I think these peons have earned a break! :What do you say, ? Are you ready for the peons to join the party? : :

    Magor and the Peons run into the center of town and become hostile. Magor is an elite who occasionally casts "Bootstorm", a whirlwind ability that creates Booterangs that can be tossed at him or any of the other hostile orcs. :

    Bring Magor to 30% health to end the fight. :
