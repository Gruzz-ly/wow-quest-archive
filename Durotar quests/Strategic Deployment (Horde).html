    ==Description== For years, I guarded the gates of [[Orgrimmar]]. The city's greatest weakness has always been its multiple entrances. We should strike at all of them to seize victory.

    [[Anduin Wrynn|Wrynn]] and I have drawn up orders for deployment. The [[mage]]s will cover our flank. [[Genn Greymane|Greymane]] will close in from the west, and the elves will take the north. We will send [[Mayla Highmountain|Mayla]] to reinforce [[Thunder Bluff]]'s defenses in case [[Sylvanas Windrunner|Sylvanas]] launches a counterattack.

    Bring the orders to our [[Commander (rank)|commanders]].

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 17,850 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== If we do not win here, then we lose everything.

    ==Completion== No matter what our strategy, taking Orgrimmar will be no easy task. I should know.
