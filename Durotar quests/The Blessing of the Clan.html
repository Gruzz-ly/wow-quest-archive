    The Blessing of the Clan

    {{Questbox | id = 72467 | name = The Blessing of the Clan | image = The Blessing of the Clan.jpg | imagecaption = The chieftains inside the Razor Hill Barracks | category = Heritage | faction = Horde | race = Orc | level = {{level|bfa heritage}} | start = [[Farseer Aggralan]]{{Co|52.21|43|Durotar}} | previous = [[The Kosh'harg]] | next = [[The Long Knives]] }}

    ==Objectives== Choose a clan to champion during your [[om'gora]]. *Clan Chosen

    ==Description== To earn the blessing of the clan, a young [[orc]] must learn that the clan is only as strong as its members. Prove your strength, and worth, to the living orcs.

    Since you were not born into a clan, you must first choose a clan to champion, and be accepted by the clan's chieftain.

    The chieftains have gathered in [[Razor Hill Barracks|the barracks]]. Join a clan, and complete the task given to you.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: [[Money]] [[XP]]

    ==Progress== The clan representatives are inside the barracks. You must choose one to join.

    ==Completion== Your path has been chosen. Are you ready to proceed?

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{Text|say|Farseer Aggralan|The clans come together anew. Choose a clan to champion in your om'gora.}}

    [[Thrall]], [[Eitrigg]], [[Jorin Deadeye]], [[Gorgonna]], [[Gorfax Angerfang]], and [[Thega Graveblade]] are standing inside the Razor Hill Barracks. Speak to any of the chieftains to join that clan. This choice is purely cosmetic: the only things that change are the quest giver and quest text in the following quest, [[The Long Knives]], and the visual effect of the [[Clan Banner]] toy rewarded later in the chain. The latter can be freely changed by speaking to [[Envoy Korg]] after the questline is completed.

    After choosing a clan to join, the player is given a "Are you sure you wish to join this clan?" message to confirm the choice, after which the chieftain walks out of the Barracks to resume their previous position next to Aggralan while the other chieftains remain standing inside.

    After a clan has been picked, the other chieftains revert to their previous gossip text, with the exception of Eitrigg who gains a slightly different set: :It warms my heart for so many of us to be together again, and not to march off to war!

    As does Thrall: :So many answered our call to join the Kosh'harg! It is good to see everyone again.
