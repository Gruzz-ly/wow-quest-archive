    Break Him

    {{questbox |name=Break Him |id=72554 | disambigcat = Azure Span | displaycat = The Azure Span | category = Azure Span |level= |levelreq= |start=Matron Fherg |rewards=Reading Glasses |money=28g 9s |experience=9,500 |reputation=+6000 Winterpelt Furbolg |previous=Gathering Together, More Effort, More Reward |next=Path of Trust (at Skill 75) }}

    Break Him concludes the Winterpelt Language Skill 50 portion of the Winterpelt Furbolg storyline. The next quest starts at Skill 75 with Path of Trust.

    Objectives

    Find Saza and kill the large worm, Icebreaker. Saza* Found Icebreaker* slain

    Description

    File:Break Him description text.jpg

    You handled those little worms with such ease!

    However... Saza hasn't come back. The giant worm, Icebreaker, has been hunting us for weeks. It took two of our elders while we gathered food and injured me when I tried to avenge them. And now, with Saza missing, I fear the worst.

    Find Saza, and get rid of that worm!

    Rewards

    You will receive: * Reading Glasses * 28g 9s * 9,500 XP * +6000 reputation with Winterpelt Furbolg

    So. It was Icebreaker?

    Our bright, brave cub... Oh, Saza...

    Anyone here would gladly give up their lives in trade for Saza's. Icebreaker's death brings some comfort, but it will not bring her back.

    Saza's loss will be felt by all of us.
