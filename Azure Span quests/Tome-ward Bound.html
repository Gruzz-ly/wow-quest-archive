    ==Description== We must stop that tree! With these ley crystals, I can finally help.

    Speak with me when you are ready. I'll open a portal into the [[Azure Archives]] and try to bring out something useful.

    Stand back, it may be rather unstable.

    I'm glad we were able to give the spirits of those tuskarr some peace.
