    {{questbox |name=Gathering Together |id=72552 | disambigcat = Azure Span | displaycat = The Azure Span | category = Azure Span |level={{level|df-max}} |levelreq={{level|df-max}} |start=[[Elder Mhaz]] {{co|57.4|21.1|Azure Span}} |money={{cost|28|9}} |experience=9,500 |previous=[[A Living History]] |next=[[Break Him]] }} [[File:Best-root Bush.jpg|thumb|Best-root Bush]]

    ==Objectives== Gather 8 roots for [[Elder Mhaz]]. * [[Best-root Tuber]] (8)

    ==Description== [[File:Gathering Together description.jpg]]

    I haven't heard an outsider speak our tongue. Your grasp is a bit basic...

    But only a friend of the clan would speak it at all!

    I am Elder Mhaz. If it is safe to return home, we should set off soon. But many of us are weak with sadness of the heart--and grumbling of the stomach. I would not put you at risk gathering fresh meat, but could you gather roots for us?

    We have already gathered ones that are close. I hope this comfort food will lift our spirits enough to return!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== [[File:Gathering Together progress text.jpg]]

    The roots grow around the bases of the large trees. We have not ventured east because there are many worms. Perhaps there are more there?

    ==Completion== [[File:Gathering Together completion text.jpg]]

    I'm glad to see you have returned safely!
