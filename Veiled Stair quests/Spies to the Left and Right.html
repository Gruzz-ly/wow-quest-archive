    Spies to the Left and Right

    {{Questbox | id = 56186 | name = Spies to the Left and Right | category = Heart of Azeroth | level = {{level|bfa-max}} | levelreq = {{level|bfa-max}} | start = [[Kalecgos]] | end = [[Left]] | reputation = +250 [[Champions of Azeroth]] | rewards = [[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]]) | experience = 17,850 | money = {{cost|23|40}} | shareable = No | previous = [[Whispers of N'Zoth]] | next = [[Black Winged Shadow]] }}

    ==Objectives== Seek out Wrathion at the Tavern in the Mists. * Seek out [[Wrathion]] at the [[Tavern in the Mists]] * [[Lurking Observer]] slain (6) * [[Torn Journal Page 7|Wrathion's Journal Page collected]]

    ==Description== [[Ebonhorn]] cannot resist the whispers for long. The dark forces that once corrupted the [[black dragonflight]] seek to overwhelm the [[Azeroth|world]] once again.

    We need a way to fight back. While I am loathe to mention him, I do know of someone who has been studying the [[Old God]]s in order to better combat their insidious power: Wrathion.

    The Black Prince has associates in [[Pandaria]], in the Tavern of the Mists at the [[Veiled Stair]]. I suggest you begin your search there.

    Please hurry, .

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40}} * 17,850 [[XP]] * +250 [[reputation]] with [[Champions of Azeroth]] *[[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]])

    ==Progress== The Black Prince isn't here. But we might be able to help you find him.

    ==Completion== Good work getting rid of those spies.

    Things have been quiet here for a long time. And now, suddenly, all eyes are on us. So to speak.

    The Black Prince is notoriously secretive about his plans. I'm not in the habit of reading other people's journals, but I'm guessing he expected you to feel differently about it.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{text|say|Kalecgos|Hurry, champion. Find Wrathion. He may be Ebonhorn's only hope.}}

    Get to the [[Shrine of Two Moons]] or the [[Shrine of Seven Stars]] in the [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms]]. Then either take the flight path or fly to the [[Tavern in the Mists]] on the [[Veiled Stair]].

    Speak with Left: :We were told to stay here and wait for further orders. And so we've waited. When do we get our chance for some action?

    :{{Gossip|quest=|Ebonhorn is in trouble. Where is Wrathion?}} ::You know as much as we do. He hasn't made contact with us since handing us those journal pages.

    :{{Text|say|[[Left]]|The Black Prince told us to expect you. Said his brother would need his help eventually.}} :{{Text|say|Left|We're supposed to deliver a message to you... but not while we are being watched.}} :{{Text|say|[[Right]]|Get rid of our uninvited guides. Then we can talk.}}

    Head outside and defeat six [[Lurking Observer]]s. Once that's done, return to Left:

    :{{Gossip|quest=|You said you had a message for me?}} :{{text|say|Left|Here. The prince wanted you to have these. For your eyes only.}}

    Left provides [[Torn Journal Page 7]]. Read or not, then speak with Left to continue.
