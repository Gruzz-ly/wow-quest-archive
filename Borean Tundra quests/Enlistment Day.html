    ==Objectives== Meet with [[General Arlos]] inside the [[barracks]] in [[Valiance Keep]].

    ==Description== You've come to enlist, right? Normally you'd have to stand in line like the other recruits. Normally you'd be out of luck because the recruitment ledger's gone missing and I can't process anyone until the new books come.

    Fortunately for you, , I've heard about you and what you did in [[Outland]]. To be honest, you're a cut above these poor saps. Lines aren't for you.

    Go to the barracks and speak to General Arlos. He'll be more than happy to meet with someone of your caliber.

    ==Completion== We've been expecting your arrival, . Welcome to [[Northrend]].
