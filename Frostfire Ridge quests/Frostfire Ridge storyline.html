    Frostfire Ridge storyline

    thumb|Frostfire Ridge zone map

    The Frostfire Ridge storyline is the Horde starting experience in Frostfire Ridge after getting through Warlords of Draenor intro experience. Here, players quickly set up their Garrison at Frostwall in the south-center portion of the zone, then work to help shore up the Frostwolf clan's defenses.

    The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement You'll Get Caught Up In The... Frostfire!

    In addition to the overarching zone storyline, a huge number of vignettes can also be found all over Frostfire Ridge. These vignettes are one-time events: killing rare or rare-elite mobs, finding out-of-the-way treasure chests, or small events like fending off waves of attackers.

    Main storyline

    Foothold in a Savage Land

    thumb|The level 1 Garrison at Frostwall thumb|Zone map after the garrison is establieshed thumb|Building the Barracks****

    After landing in Frostfire, players need to get settled, and quickly set up their Garrison at Frostwall. This portion of the quest chain serves as an introduction and tutorial to the garrison system.

    Farseer Drek'Thar continues the quest chain outside with Den of Wolves. In addition, the Ashran breadcrumb opens with Ashran Appearance (Horde).

    Siege of Bladespire Citadel

    thumb|The taking of Bladespire Citadel****

    Drek'Thar sends players to Wor'gol to begin the siege of Bladespire Citadel, outpost of the ogre empire.

    Once taken, Durotan sees to the defense of Wor'gol, starting with Moving In (Frostfire Ridge).

    Defense of Wor'gol

    thumb|Wor'gol under attack thumb|Circle of Blood* *thumb|Sootstained Mining Camp****

    After claiming Bladespire Citadel, and some loot from the sack of the citadel, the news comes that Wor'gol is under attack by the ! Along the way are several side quest chains.

    After repelling the invasion and delivering Drek'Thar's vision to Durotan back at the Citadel, he forks the storyline, beginning both the "Ga'nar's Vengeance" and "Thunder's Fall" chapters.

    Thunder's Fall

    thumb|Grom'gar****

    Once "Ga'nar's Vengance" and "Thunder's Fall" chapters are both completed, the Thunderlord Clan will launch an invasion (Thunderlord Invasion!) on the Garrison. When this is repelled, the chain continues with The Battle of Thunder Pass.

    The Battle of Thunder Pass

    After this, players are directed to Gorgrond via a quest The Secrets of Gorgrond (Horde).

    Side quests

    All of these side quests are optional to the main zone storyline, but reward plenty of gold, faction standing, and maybe a follower or two:

    Darkspear's Edge

    thumb|Cracking Plains. Rokhan at the garrison has ordered a small camp set up on the Cracking Plains to the north to gather some supplies. It's up to the commander to actually do the gruntwork, though.

    Bones of Agurak

    thumb|Agurak's Fall

    After winning the Battle of Thunder Pass, a short quest chain takes place at the Bones of Agurak, south of the Iron Siegeworks.

    Dagg

    During both "Ga'nar's Vengance" and "Thunder's Fall" chapters, you can run into an ogre in the cage called Dagg. Releasing him from the cage in both cases causes him to appear in your garrison, where he joins as a follower. This can be done as both Horde and Alliance.

    Bonus objectives

    Completing every bonus objective awards the achievement Frostfire Fridge.

    Forbidden Glacier

    Just outside Wor'gol to the northeast, an ogre experiment has gone wrong, knocking Mor'dul Tower out of time and summoning a bunch of Malevolent Breaths that have frozen a squad of Frostwolves.

    *Forbidden Glacier (bonus objective)

    Grimfrost Hill

    thumb|Ogres to kill at Grimfrost Hill****

    One of the bonus objectives of Frostfire Ridge takes place at Grimfrost Hill. Hidden nearby, a mortally-wounded Outrider Urukag also has a few quests to give.

    Frostbite Hollow

    A bonus objective take place at Frostbite Hollow, north of the Magnarok.

    Garrison buildings

    There is a number of quest chains dedicated to unlocking various buildings in the garrison. Their availability is determined by your level, the garrison's level, and your current primary professions.

    Level 2 upgrade

    At level 15, Gazlowe offers a quest to upgrade the Garrison to level 2, and awards the commander all Level 1 blueprints with the exception of the Salvage Yard. *Bigger is Better (Horde)

    Mine

    After reaching level 15 and garrison level 2, Gorsol wants the commander to clear out the mine: *Things Are Not Goren Our Way (Horde)

    Fishing Hut

    After reaching level 20 and garrison level 2, Mak'jin wants the commander to find someone who knows how to fish on Draenor:

    Looking For Help (Horde)

    Icespine Stingers

    Proving Your Worth (Horde)

    Anglin' In Our Garrison (Horde)

    *Fish Fight

    Herb Garden

    After reaching level 30 and garrison level 2, Tarnon wants the commander to clear out the herb garden: *Clearing the Garden (Horde)

    Level 3 upgrade

    After reaching level 35 and garrison level 2, Gazlowe offers a quest to upgrade the garrison to level 3. For a cost, of course. *My Very Own Fortress

    Menagerie

    After reaching level 35 and garrison level 3, Serr'ah wants the commander's pets to deal with pests around the garrison. This is a Pet Battle System-related building. *Pets Versus Pests (Horde)
