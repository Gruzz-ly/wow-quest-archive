    Frostfire Ridge storyline

    {{shortcut|frquest}} [[File:VZ-Frostfire Ridge.jpg|thumb|Frostfire Ridge zone map]]

    The '''Frostfire Ridge storyline''' is the Horde starting experience in [[Frostfire Ridge]] after getting through [[Warlords of Draenor intro experience]]. Here, players quickly set up their [[Garrison]] at [[Frostwall]] in the south-center portion of the zone, then work to help shore up the [[Frostwolf Orcs|Frostwolf clan]]'s defenses.

    The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[You'll Get Caught Up In The... Frostfire!]]

    In addition to the overarching zone storyline, a huge number of [[:Category:Frostfire Ridge vignettes|vignettes]] can also be found all over Frostfire Ridge. These vignettes are one-time events: killing rare or rare-elite mobs, finding out-of-the-way treasure chests, or small events like fending off waves of attackers.

    ==Main storyline== ===Foothold in a Savage Land=== [[File:Frostwall level 1.jpg|thumb|The level 1 Garrison at Frostwall]] [[File:WorldMap-FrostfireRidge terrain1.jpg|thumb|Zone map after the garrison is establieshed]] [[File:Build Your Barracks.jpg|thumb|Building the [[Barracks (garrison)|Barracks]]]]

    After landing in Frostfire, players need to get settled, and quickly set up their [[Garrison]] at [[Frostwall]]. This portion of the quest chain serves as an introduction and tutorial to the garrison system.

    {{Foothold in a Savage Land}}

    [[Drek'Thar (alternate universe)|Farseer Drek'Thar]] continues the quest chain outside with [[Den of Wolves]]. In addition, the [[Ashran]] breadcrumb opens with [[Ashran Appearance (Horde)]]. {{Clear}} ===Siege of Bladespire Citadel=== [[File:To the Slaughter.jpg|thumb|The taking of [[Bladespire Citadel]]]]

    Drek'Thar sends players to [[Wor'gol]] to begin the siege of [[Bladespire Citadel]], outpost of the ogre empire.

    {{Siege of Bladespire Fortress}}

    Once taken, Durotan sees to the defense of Wor'gol, starting with [[Moving In (Frostfire Ridge)]].

    ===Defense of Wor'gol=== [[File:Save Wolf Home.jpg|thumb|[[Wor'gol]] under attack]] [[File:Circle of Blood (Frostfire Ridge).jpg|thumb|[[Circle of Blood (Frostfire Ridge)|Circle of Blood]]]] [[File:Sootstained Mining Camp.jpg|thumb|[[Sootstained Mining Camp]]]]

    After claiming [[Bladespire Citadel]], and some loot from the sack of the citadel, the news comes that [[Wor'gol]] is under attack by the {{Au|Thunderlord clan}}! Along the way are several side quest chains.

    {{Defense of Wor'gol}}

    After repelling the invasion and delivering Drek'Thar's vision to Durotan back at the Citadel, he forks the storyline, beginning both the "Ga'nar's Vengeance" and "Thunder's Fall" chapters.

    ===Ga'nar's Vengeance=== [[File:Savage Crag.jpg|thumb|The [[Savage Crag]]]]

    {{Ga'nar's Vengeance}}

    ===Thunder's Fall=== [[File:The Strength of Our Bonds.jpg|thumb|[[Grom'gar]]]]

    {{Beneath Thunder's Fall}}

    Once "Ga'nar's Vengance" and "Thunder's Fall" chapters are both completed, the Thunderlord Clan will launch an [[Garrison#Invasion|invasion]] ([[Thunderlord Invasion!]]) on the Garrison. When this is repelled, the chain continues with The Battle of Thunder Pass.

    ===The Battle of Thunder Pass===

    {{The Battle of Thunder Pass}}

    After this, players are directed to [[Gorgrond]] via a quest [[The Secrets of Gorgrond (Horde)]].

    ==Side quests== All of these side quests are optional to the main zone storyline, but reward plenty of gold, faction standing, and maybe a follower or two:

    ===Darkspear's Edge=== [[File:Cracking Plains.jpg|thumb|[[Cracking Plains]].]] [[Rokhan]] at the garrison has ordered a small camp set up on the [[Cracking Plains]] to the north to gather some supplies. It's up to the commander to actually do the gruntwork, though.

    {{Darkspear's Edge}}

    ===Bones of Agurak=== [[File:Agurak's Fall.jpg|thumb|Agurak's Fall]]

    After winning the Battle of Thunder Pass, a short quest chain takes place at the [[Bones of Agurak]], south of the [[Iron Siegeworks]].

    {{Bones of Agurak}}

    ===Dagg=== During both "Ga'nar's Vengance" and "Thunder's Fall" chapters, you can run into an ogre in the cage called [[Dagg]]. Releasing him from the cage in both cases causes him to appear in your garrison, where he joins as a follower. This can be done as both Horde and Alliance.

    • [[Services of Dagg]]

    ==Bonus objectives== Completing every bonus objective awards the achievement [[Frostfire Fridge]]. ===Forbidden Glacier=== Just outside [[Wor'gol]] to the northeast, an ogre experiment has gone wrong, knocking [[Mor'dul Tower]] out of time and summoning a bunch of Malevolent Breaths that have frozen a squad of Frostwolves.

    ===Frostbite Hollow=== A bonus objective take place at [[Frostbite Hollow]], north of the [[Magnarok]].

    *[[Frostbite Hollow (bonus objective)]]

    ==Garrison buildings== There is a number of quest chains dedicated to unlocking various buildings in the garrison. Their availability is determined by your level, the garrison's level, and your current primary professions.

    ===Level 2 upgrade=== At level 15, [[Gazlowe]] offers a quest to upgrade the Garrison to level 2, and awards the commander all Level 1 blueprints with the exception of the [[Salvage Yard]]. *[[Bigger is Better (Horde)]]

    ===Mine=== After reaching level 15 and garrison level 2, [[Gorsol]] wants the commander to clear out the mine: *[[Things Are Not Goren Our Way (Horde)]]

    ===Fishing Hut=== After reaching level 20 and garrison level 2, [[Mak'jin]] wants the commander to find someone who knows how to fish on Draenor:

    [[Looking For Help (Horde)]]

    [[Icespine Stingers]]

    [[Proving Your Worth (Horde)]]

    [[Anglin' In Our Garrison (Horde)]]

    *[[Fish Fight]]

    ===Herb Garden=== After reaching level 30 and garrison level 2, [[Tarnon]] wants the commander to clear out the herb garden: *[[Clearing the Garden (Horde)]]

    ===Level 3 upgrade=== After reaching level 35 and garrison level 2, [[Gazlowe]] offers a quest to upgrade the garrison to level 3. For a cost, of course. *[[My Very Own Fortress]]

    ===Menagerie=== After reaching level 35 and garrison level 3, [[Serr'ah]] wants the commander's pets to deal with pests around the garrison. This is a [[Pet Battle System]]-related building. *[[Pets Versus Pests (Horde)]]
