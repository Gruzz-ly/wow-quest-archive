    The Battle of Thunder Pass (quest)

    {{Questbox | id = 34124 | name = The Battle of Thunder Pass | faction = Horde | level={{level|Frostfire Ridge}} | category=Frostfire Ridge | start = [[Lokra]] | end = {{Au|Durotan}} | reputation = +500 [[Frostwolf Orcs]] | experience = 20,960 | rewards = Item Level 525 Helm | money = {{cost|24|40}} | previous = [[To Thunder Pass]] | next = [[The Secrets of Gorgrond]] }}

    ==Objectives== Defend [[Frostfire Ridge]] from the [[Iron Horde]]. * Speak with {{Au|Durotan}}. * Defend Frostfire Ridge from the Iron Horde.

    ==Description== The Iron Horde has an army tenfold our number marching down the [[Thunder Pass]] as we speak.

    Durotan is amassing our forces at the mouth of the pass. He is doing what he can to mount a defense but war on this scale is not what we [[Frostwolf Orcs|Frostwolves]] are masters of.

    He will need your expertise.

    Please, go to him.

    ==Rewards== You will be able to choose one of these rewards: {{itembox|Item Level 525 Helm |Frostwolf Wind-Talker Cowl |Frostwolf Scout's Cap |Frostwolf Ringmail Cap |Frostwolf Stalwart Cap }}

    You will also receive: {{cost|36|60}} {{itembox| |q1=100|item1=Garrison Resources }}

    ==Completion== This was a costly victory, and a very painful lesson. We chose to refuse the Iron Horde and live as we always have, but I see now they will never respect such neutrality.

    I am not about to wait for the Iron Horde to martial their forces. We will fight with you, and bring the Iron Horde to its knees.

    Commander, the Frostwolves stand ready!

    ==Gains== 20,960 [[XP]] +500 reputation with [[Frostwolf Orcs]]
