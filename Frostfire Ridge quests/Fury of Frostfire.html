    Fury of Frostfire

    {{Questbox | image=Fury of Frostfire.jpg | imagecaption=Fury of Frostfire | name=Fury of Frostfire | start=[[Drek'Thar (alternate universe)|Farseer Drek'Thar]] | faction=Horde | id=32985 | level = {{level|wod-max}} | levelreq = {{level|wod-max}} | category=Frostfire Ridge | experience=13970 | money={{cost|12|20}} | reputation = +350 [[Frostwolf Orcs]] | rewards=[[Firefury Totem]] | previous=[[The Ascent]] | next=[[Ours is the Fury]] }} [[File:Kron (Frostfire Ridge).jpg|thumb|[[Kron]]]] ==Objectives== Defeat Kron. *[[Kron]] slain

    ==Description== The moment I begin the ritual to free [[Exurotus]] it is sure to draw Kron to us.

    It would normally take an army of seasoned warriors to defeat a [[magnaron]]. Today, there is only the three of us.

    Look for my fire totem upon the battlefield. I will try to gain what boons I can from Exurotus. Step near the totems and become one with the Fury.

    With these boons, slay Kron! Lok'tar ogar!

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will receive:|Firefury Totem}}

    You will also receive: *{{cost|12|20}} * 13970 [[XP]] * +350 [[reputation]] with [[Frostwolf Orcs]]

    ==Completion== With Kron defeated I can finish breaching the magic that holds Exurotus, but I may already be too late. So much of the fury has already been expended and drained by the magnaron and goren.

    I fear we may have only bought this spirit a peaceful death.
