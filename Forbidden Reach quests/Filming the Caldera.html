    Filming the Caldera is a standard cataloging World Quest for the Dragonscale Expedition, but in daily quest form.

    Objectives

    Fly over the Caldera of the Menders and capture local wildlife on your camera. Ride the Cataloger's Gyrocopter Take Pictures of Wildlife (0/100%)

    Description

    The arthropods in Caldera of the Menders are truly vicious. One of our gyrocopters went missing there!

    Our expedition sent a unmanned surveillance aircraft there, but it hasn't returned. Can you find the gyrocopter? You could use it to capture the Caldera's wildlife on camera?

    Rewards

    You will receive: 28g 9s 9500 XP 450x Elemental Overflow +350 reputation with Dragonscale Expedition +350 reputation with Dark Talons +350 reputation with Obsidian Warders

    Progress

    Spotted anything interesting down there?

    Completion

    Well done! Extensive photography makes our documentation complete!

    Notes

    The Cataloger's Gyrocopter and the Dragonscale Gyrocopter Pilot are on the platform outside the entrance to the Support Creche, seemingly none the worse for wear. The pilot has this to say: :Howdy! Oh, Daela sent you to find me? Aw, how nice of her. :A huuuuge beetle hit my rotor as I was busy taking pictures of the scythids down there... The bug was shredded to pieces, but I had to make an emergency landing for some repairs. Cleaning the mess took almost as long as the fixing. :Say, since you're here, why don't you want to take a ride and take the pictures of those obnoxious bugs instead of me?
