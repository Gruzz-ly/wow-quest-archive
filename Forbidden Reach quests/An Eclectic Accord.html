    ==Description== While the storm over [[Forbidden Reach|the Reach]] was raging, I gathered allies for our eventual return. I want to ensure they have everything they need, but I can't welcome all of them personally.

    Would you be my envoy, ? Can you make sure they have what they need? We have few supplies to offer and a shortage of assistance.

    The least I can do is offer my gratitude. Please thank them personally on my behalf.
