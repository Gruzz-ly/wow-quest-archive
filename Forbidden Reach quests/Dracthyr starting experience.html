    Dracthyr starting experience

    thumb|Forbidden Reach zone map The *dracthyr* starting experience takes place in an instanced version of the Forbidden Reach zone, and introduces the playable race/hero class combo dracthyr evoker, starting at level 10 (originally 58). Non-evoker dracthyr also start in the Forbidden Reach, but have a drastically shortened storyline that sends them to their faction capital after just a couple minutes.

    The Forbidden Reach itself is an island located off the coast of the Dragon Isles that was fashioned by Neltharion the Earth-Warder to serve as a training ground for his ultimate soldiers, the dracthyr. But in the aftermath of a terrible battle, the island has lain dormant and abandoned for ages until the return of an ancient enemy causes the dracthyr to stir.

    Introduction

    Players are first introduced via an animated cutscene, which depicts flashbacks of their last memories.

    ''Lightning strikes periodically reveal silhouettes of a dracthyr army. A blizzard surrounds your vision, while the sounds of battle fill your ears.''

    ''A flash of Neltharion in his visage and wielding Oathbinder appears.''

    ''Raszageth devastates the army with her lightning breath. You see flashes of her leading proto-drakes in battle against the army and fighting in the sky with dragons, while interspersed with lingering visions of the War Creche.''

    ''You blink repeatedly as you focuses on a panicked dracthyr reaching out towards you. As silence falls, your vision settles upon the War Creche once again.''

    ''Shadows envelop your vision as you fall into stasis.''

    Evoker

    The dracthyr evoker starting storyline begins on the Forbidden Reach with them awakening from a millennia-long slumber. They first encounter blue dragonflight forces guarding their unstable War Creche to prevent their escape. After breaking free and rescuing the Healing Wings and Ebon Scales from their own collapsing chambers, they meet Wrathion and Ebyssian, drawn to investigate the same disturbance that roused the dracthyr. They discover the Primalists attacking the island, who are ultimately successful at freeing Raszageth, who summons a powerful elemental storm. Nozdormu aids the bulk of the dracthyr in fleeing the island to join with the Alliance and Horde and bring them to the Dragon Isles, kicking off the main Dragonflight campaign.

    Throughout this experience, various dracthyr and evoker abilities are introduced through quests. These include the new mechanics of empowered spells (Fire Breath) and skyriding (Soar), as well as signature racial features like Glide (racial) and Visage (racial). The questline is tracked in the achievement Dracthyr, Awaken.

    Other classes

    Dracthyr class selection was limited to evokers in , but expanded afterward. Non-evokers start with the same cutscene as the others, but do not follow the original Forbidden Reach storyline, which took place while the War Creche was under siege by primalists prior to most of the events of ''Dragonflight''. Instead, their awakening seems to take place later, as dracthyr leaders search for more survivors in the creche under calmer circumstances. The questline connects the dracthyr backstory with their new role as adventurers, and sends new characters on to Stormwind or Orgrimmar after just a few minutes. It provides no introduction to class abilities as the evoker storyline does.
