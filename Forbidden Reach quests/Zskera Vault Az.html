    Zskera Vault: Az

    {{questbox |name=Zskera Vault: Az |id=72953 | disambigcat = Forbidden Reach | displaycat = The Forbidden Reach | category = Forbidden Reach |level= |levelreq= |start=Pathfinder Jeb |end=Pathfinder Jeb |money=28g 9s |experience=9,500 |reputation=+250 Dragonscale Expedition |previous=Exploring Our Past |next=The Scary Vault of Ur }} thumb|Zskera Vault: Az

    Zskera Vault: Az is the first of the Zskera Vaults in the Forbidden Reach. It contains 28 doors, each needing a Zskera Vault Key. Next week players explore The Scary Vault of Ur.

    Objectives

    Enter and explore Zskera Vault: Az. Speak with Pathfinder Jeb to enter the vault Secure the Vault Doors opened with Zskera Vault Keys Climb Web Rope to second floor

    Description

    thumb|Terrorachna* We have discovered a series of vaults below *the island. Neltharion hoarded treasures, accumulated knickknacks, and stashed away the results of his failed experiments.

    When I say he's a hoarder, I'm not kidding! He filled chamber after chamber, and in time, they were all forgotten... until now. Treasure awaits us behind these locked doors! Also, the occasional deathtrap. Also, treasure! Now we have the keys to unlock those doors.

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 28g 9s * 9,500 XP * +250 reputation with Dragonscale Expedition

    Well done. As long as we keep finding keys, we can keep exploring. In a few days, I'm sure we'll explore another vault.

    I think the names of these vaults are numbered. Az, Ur, Ix, Kx... those words must mean something. Don't get me wrong, I'm focused on the treasure, but I wouldn't mind learning a thing or two along the way... or is it "a thing or Ix"? Maybe it is.

    Notes

    Before going in, pick up The Keys You Need from Pathfinder Tacha, One Dragon's Junk... from Voraxian, and Authentic Authorization from Cataloger Wulferd.

    Then speak with Jeb again: :The island has many secrets beneath the surface I aim to discover them all. : :Someone made off with a bunch of our keys! We're going to need them back if we're going to explore the vaults. :Take a quick look around the Forbidden Reach. See if you can find anyone or anything that's taken the keys. It might take some force to get them back. : :''The player enters Zskera Vault: Az.''

    On the other side of the loading screen: : :''Jeb and Tacha run downstairs.''

    Jeb has gossip: : :The scouts did a good job mapping out this area before our arrival. :Lots of detail, although I'm not certain how to interpret this sad monkey face yet... :That very ominous skull up in the mountainside, on the other hand? I can guess what that means.

    As does Tacha: :I hope you came prepared. I expect many dangers here. : :We need vault keys to open the doors inside Zskera Vaults. Then we can get to the treasures inside! :You should search the Forbidden Reach. See if you can find more keys. The more keys we have, the more treasure we can get!

    Head further downstairs: :

    thumb|The Reagent Pot. Jeb and Tacha serve as bodyguards. Clear out the Vault Weaver guarding the entry to the big chamber, then the elite spider Terrorachna in the center of the room. Once clear, counterclockwise from the south is a Reagent Pot which contains some Awakened Air, the Odd Device for One Dragon's Junk... is on a crate in the southwest, an an Unusual Ring is on the ground in the southeast. It starts The Forgotten Ring.

    After using the web rope to get upstairs and opening one (but only one!) of the locked doors, turn in to Jeb to stop Terrorachna from respawning. Then head back to the surface to pick up What's Behind The Next Door, which requires players to open three additional doors.

    Utility lootables

    The rooms are randomized per-player per-week, so here are some generic suggestions:

    Open one of the doors accessed via the web ropes to find the Flask of Windward Steps, which allows jumping between platforms via an Extra Action Button. Other useful finds include the Energized Circlet, which provides a player-centered light source, the Reflective Arcane Ward and Protective Ward of Flames, which reflect their respective elements, and the Positional Displacer, granting a throwable teleport target, which can be used to reach the third and fourth levels of vaults.
