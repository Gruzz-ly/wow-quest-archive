    ==Objectives== Explore [[Stormwind]]. * Visit the Auction House * Visit the Bank * Visit the Embassy * Visit the Portal Room

    ==Description== Perhaps we should get an idea of this city, if it is to be our new home. A scouting mission, if you will.

    Go, see what this place has to offer. I will follow [[Wrathion]] and attempt to help in any way I can.

    Try not to upset the locals any more than you must, though. I doubt many of them are used to seeing [[dragon]]s walk among them.

    Meet up with us once you've gotten the lay of the land.

    ==Progress== How goes the exploration?

    ==Completion== The [[Alliance]] has granted us asylum here in Stormwind.

    It sounds like quite an interesting place.
