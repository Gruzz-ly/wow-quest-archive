    ==Objectives== Accompany [[Anduin Wrynn|King Anduin]] on his walk through [[Stormwind City|Stormwind]]. Speak with Anduin Escort Anduin to [[Lion's Rest]] Return to [[Stormwind Keep]] Anduin's advisors heard

    ==Description== [[Varian Wrynn|My father]] was a legend. He oversaw the rebuilding of Stormwind, stood toe-to-toe with [[dragon]]s, led the [[Alliance]] to numerous victories.

    Then, one day, he was just... gone.

    I wear his crown, I hold his title. But I've done nothing to earn them... or the faith of my people.

    I've spent the war sequestered in the keep, surrounded by guards and advisors. I need to walk the streets of Stormwind, to know whether it is hope or fear that beats in the hearts of my people.

    I welcome your company.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|19|40}} 16,450 [[XP]] *[[Gilded Prayer Beads]]
