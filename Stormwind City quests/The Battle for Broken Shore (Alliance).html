    ==Description== This is your vessel, . She's a fine ship, been through many a campaign. Speak with the ship's [[Captain Angelica|captain]] when you're ready to depart.

    Good luck out there. We've received few reports about what's happening at the Broken Shore, but I'd wager [[Varian Wrynn|King Varian]] has matters well in hand.

    ''{{gossip|I am ready to face the Legion.}}''

    ''Are you prepared to face the Legion at the Broken Shore?'' (Accept/Cancel)

    :''A cutscene plays, showing the third fleet shipping out.''

    Inside the scenario begins the Alliance perspective of the [[battle for the Broken Shore]].

    ===Stage 1: The Broken Shore=== [[File:WorldMap-BrokenShoreIntro.jpg|thumb|World map]] :''Travel to the Broken Shore.''

    Once the cutscene finishes, the player is on [[Captain Angelica]]'s [[battleship]], sailing toward the Broken Shore. Up to 40 players (20 Alliance and 20 Horde) can take part in the scenario and the difficulty will scale to handle all comers. In the first twenty seconds, other players who also say they are ready to start the scenario will join the instance automatically. After 20 seconds, the NPCs start talking: :{{text|say|Captain Angelica|Smooth sailing so far. Give me a status report.}} :{{text|say|First Mate Tidesong|[[Varian Wrynn|King Wrynn]] and the [[Stormwind Fleet]] arrived several hours ago. Reports indicate heavy fighting.}} :{{text|say|Captain Angelica|I haven't seen so much as a seagull for hours now.}} :{{text|say|First Mate Tidesong|There is a foulness in the air, and we are headed directly towards it.}} :{{text|say|Captain Angelica|Directly ahead, Alliance ships! Make for the beach! Hold on everyone, this is going to get worse before it gets better!}} :''The ship turns, and the fel energy above the [[Tomb of Sargeras]] comes into view.'' :{{text|say|Captain Angelica|Light's sake, what is THAT?}} :{{text|say|First Mate Tidesong|It appears to be a great source of [[fel]] energy. I have never seen anything like it.}} :''A cutscene takes place as siege cannons start bombarding the ships with fel fireballs.''

    ===Stage 2: Storm The Beach=== [[File:Spire of Woe.jpg|thumb|A [[spire of woe]].]] :''Destroy all demons and structures on the beach.'' 33 Demons slain 3 Fel Lords slain *3 Spires of Woe destroyed

    Once the cutscene finishes, [[Captain Angelica]]'s battleship is now ashore, burning with fel fire. Many of the [[Deck Hand (human)|deck hands]] are dead, and Angelica and her first mate are trying to put off the flames. On the beach, [[Jaina Proudmoore]] and [[Genn Greymane]] are each leading small squads of [[Kirin Tor Battle-Mage]]s, [[Gilnean Royal Guard (Broken Shore)|Gilnean Royal Guards]] and [[Gilnean Druid]]s. Several [[cannon]]s are also here, manned by [[Ironforge Cannoneer]]s. A few [[Darnassus Sentinel]]s are there as well. Many Alliance battleships are wrecked around the shoreline and several [[Stormwind Gryphon]]s are dead on the ground. Players then gain the '''For the Alliance''' buff which makes them stronger. :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|The [[third fleet]] reinforcements!}} :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|Just in time. We haven't been able to break their line. Now we may have a chance.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|It's now or never, Genn!}} :''Genn shifts to worgen form.'' :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|There's only one way out of this, Alliance, and it's through that line of demons! Cannons, lay down covering fire! All forces, CHARGE!}} :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|Do not let them reach the spires!}}

    The NPCs charge toward one of the three spires, guarded by 11 demons (among them [[Felguard Legionnaire (Legion)|Felguard Legionnaires]] and [[Felstalker Dreadhound]]s) and a Fel Lord (among them [[Fel Lord Kurduz]], [[Fel Lord Rakkan]] and [[Fel Lord Zardak]]) each. Start killing the demons as well as the [[Anchoring Crystal]]s surrounding the [[Spire of woe|Spires of Woe]]. Jaina realizes that they're important: :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Those crystals appear to be anchoring the structures to our dimension. Take them out, and I bet the whole structure would crumble!}} :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|Let's hope you're right.}}

    Random yells: {{text|yell|Genn Greymane|Side by side, don't let them break through!}} {{text|yell|Genn Greymane|Go for the throat!}} {{text|yell|Genn Greymane|This one's mine!}} {{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Laying down a blizzard!}} {{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Putting this one on ice!}} {{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|My magic will tear you apart!}}

    After destroying the first spire: :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|It worked!}} :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|One down, two to go.}} :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|Break them!}}

    In small groups, follow the NPCs - don't take the lead. Let Jaina and Genn tank! Repeat the process for the second spire: :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|One more and the shore is ours!}} :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|You march to your doom!}}

    Destroy the third spire to advance to the next stage.

    ===Stage 3: Defeat the Commander=== [[File:Broken Shore - Wrynn gunship.jpg|thumb|The [[gunship]] that transported [[Varian Wrynn]], later identified as the ''[[Lion's Oath]]''.]] :''Slay Dread Commander Arganoth.'' * [[Dread Commander Arganoth]] slain :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|You wish to face the might of the Legion? Ha, very well. The master will reward me for your souls.}} :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|Finally, enough of your chatter.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|We've got him now! Focus on their commander!}} :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|Your souls will fuel the soul forge!}}

    Arganoth lands in the center of the beach. Again, let the NPCs tank him. He has the same area of effect attacks as the fel lords, so they're easy to dodge.

    At 75%: :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|The legion will crush you! INFERNO!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Look out, [[Felblaze Infernal|infernals]]!}}

    At 30%: :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|You should have stayed home!}} :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|The master will mend my flesh again and again. You have not won. You cannot win. We are endless. We are Legion!}}

    Death: :{{text|yell|Dread Commander Arganoth|I... will.. return...}}

    ===Stage 4: Find Varian=== [[File:The Battle for Broken Shore - Felship.jpg|thumb|A [[Legion ship]] bombarding the Alliance's forces.]] :''Locate King Varian Wrynn.'' :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Is everyone all right?}} :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|Singed, but alive. We lost many good troops.}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|We'll have to mourn them later, we need to move before more demons arrive.}} :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|Agreed.}}

    Jaina, Genn, and their troops start running uphill to the north. Keep up. :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|Varian's forces should have landed just around this hill, let's hope they had more luck.}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|I've never seen the demons expand this quickly.}} :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|Neither have I. Something has changed, this isn't the same legion we've fought before.}}

    As the party crests the hill, looking over a ridge to the north, the [[Tomb of Sargeras]] itself comes into view. A cutscene shows the environs, where a huge [[Black City|demon city]] and the [[Darkspear's Might|wreck]] of the Horde [[gunship]] that transported [[Sylvanas Windrunner]] can be seen: :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Oh no...}} :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|This isn't possible. There are so many of them.}} :''[[Legion ship]]s start warping in as the camera tracks to Varian's position, with Gelbin Mekkatorque in tow.'' :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Hold fast, Alliance! We will not fall this day! Push them back to the portal!}} :''Varian is surrounded by [[Stormwind Knight (Broken Shore)|Stormwind Knights]], [[Stormwind Priest]]s, [[Exodar Vindicator]]s and [[Gnomeregan Tinkerer]]s. Mekkatorque himself is driving a [[steam armor]]. The cutscene ends as Varian's forces are about to attack another band of demons. The Legion ships starts bombarding the area.'' :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Varian!}} :{{text|yell|Genn Greymane|Let's go!}} Follow Genn and Jaina down the hill, dodging the bombardment. If you die here, you will be resurrected by [[Alliance Priest (Broken Shore)|Alliance Priests]] working similarly to those in [[battleground]]s.

    ===Stage 5: Destroy the Portal=== :''Destroy the demon portal to stop reinforcements.'' *4 Shielded Anchors shattered :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|Genn, Jaina, it's good to see you safe.}} :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|And you.}} :{{text|say|Gelbin Mekkatorque|We've got to take down this portal!}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|The anchoring crystals are the key, just get us up there and we'll take care of it.}}

    Finish off the [[Mo'arg Painbringer]], then rush the portal. Another painbringer will run out of the portal, but let the NPCs tank it. Focus instead on the [[Eredar Chaos Guard]]s protecting the [[Shielded Anchor]]s. :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Form up, Alliance! We push them back to the portal! For Azeroth!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Focus on the crystals!}}

    Random yells: * {{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Back where you came from!}} * {{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|We're pushing them back! Keep it up!}} * {{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|For the Alliance!}} * {{text|yell|Gelbin Mekkatorque|Back to the Nether with you!}} * {{text|yell|Gelbin Mekkatorque|For Gnomeregan!}} * {{text|yell|Gelbin Mekkatorque|Taste blaster!}}

    Finish off the last anchor to continue to the next stage.

    ===Stage 6: Raze the Black City=== [[File:The Battle for Broken Shore - Black City.jpg|thumb|The [[Black City]] seen from a ridge.]] :''Assault the demon city.'' * Assault the demon city. (0/100%) :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Heal the wounded, we cannot rest for long.}} Follow the group down the hill. :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|What happened here?}} :{{text|say|Gelbin Mekkatorque|It happened in seconds! We were fighting our way up the shore and then BOOM, explosions of fel energy all around us. And these [[Legion Structures|buildings]] just grew up out of them, filled with demons! I don't know how we're going to be able to push them back at this rate!}} :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|One demon at a time. With me, Alliance, leave nothing standing!}}

    As the party is about to enter the city, many dead [[Argent Dawnbringer]]s can be seen in a gruesome display. They come across a live but wounded one: :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|You're from the [[Argent Crusade|Crusade]]! What happened? Where is [[Tirion Fordring|Tirion]]?}} :{{text|say|Argent Dawnbringer|Don't know... they were on us in seconds... felfire everywhere...}} :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|Save your strength, we will find him.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Keep your eyes open for survivors! Save as many as you can!}}

    Many of the crusaders are impaled on spikes or put on body piles, but some of them are still alive and can be saved. Start killing demons, freeing trapped crusaders, commandeering unattended cannons, breaking portals, destroying spires, and so on. Don't wander around alone - stick with the friendly NPCs! Freed crusaders will act as guardians to the player. [[Doom Shroom]]s, [[Moongrass]]es, [[Chaos Clover]]s, [[Bleakthorn]]s and [[Felbloom]]s are local plants that can be clicked on to provide buffs.

    Fill the bar to continue.

    ===Stage 7: The Highlord=== :''Get to Tirion.''

    :{{text|say|Genn Greymane|They're retreating.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|I get the feeling that the fight isn't over yet.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Varian, I found him! Look, across the chasm, Tirion!}} :''The camera tracks across the chasm and some ways away to find Tirion, suspended over a pit of fel lava by {{Au|Gul'dan}}!'' :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|All forces, to Jaina!}}

    On the other side of the cutscene, all players and NPCs have been teleported to Jaina's position near the chasm. She is forming an ice bridge to clear the gap. :{{text|say|Gelbin Mekkatorque|How are we going to get across?}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|I've got this.}}

    Cross the ice bridge and follow the trail of [[Dessicated Crusader]]s to the [[Tomb Approach]]. [[Tirion Fordring]], Gul'dan, and the Horde forces will be there as well. :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Tirion!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Gul'dan! You will pay for this!}}

    :{{text|say|Highlord Tirion Fordring|Stay back... it's a trap... the [[Light]] will protect me...}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Hah, you fool! You stand before the temple of a GOD. Your pitiful Light cannot reach you here. How fortunate that your friends have arrived just in time to watch you die.}} :''[[Krosus]], a boss-level demon, appears from the lava.'' :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Destroy him.}} :''Krosus channels some kind of breath attack on Tirion.'' :{{text|yell|Highlord Tirion Fordring|The Light... will... aah! Aaahhhh!!}} :''Tirion's lifeless corpse falls beneath the surface of the lava pool.'' :{{text|yell|Thrall|Fordring!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|No!}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|All you have worked for, all you have sacrificed, just to see your champions fall to ash one by one.}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Destroy them!}}

    ===Stage 8: Krosus=== :''Kill Krosus.'' * [[Krosus]] slain :{{text|yell|Krosus|You will know fear.}} :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Brace yourselves, Alliance, it's coming this way!}}

    Krosus runs through the lava, stopping at the edge of the pool, conveniently in range of melee attacks. As always, let the NPCs tank. The Krosus encounter is a tank-and-spank, and Krosus has a Slam attack that damages enemies within ~10 yards or so, an Orb of Destruction that drops a fireball on a ~30-yard radius area of effect, and a channeled Fel Storm that drops fel fireballs all over the arena for a few seconds. Every 25%, he will swap to face the other faction, and the Alliance gets first crack.

    At 75%, Krosus turns to attack the Horde. :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|He's heading your way, Windrunner!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Winderunner|Let's go, Horde, it's time to show the Alliance how we slay demons.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Beasts from the felfire emerge, be careful!}}

    As the Horde were dealing with trash mobs in the first phase, so now will Alliance deal with trash mobs from 75-50%.

    At 50%, Krosus turns back to face the Alliance. :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|All yours, Alliance.}} :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|We've got him this time! All forces, concentrate your fire!}}

    Rinse and repeat down to 25%: :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|You've got another shot at the kill, Sylvanas!}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Winderunner|Consider your job done then, Wrynn.}}

    Clear out the trash mobs, then finish Krosus off.

    ===Final Stage: Stop Gul'dan=== :''Stop Gul'dan from summoning the Legion.'' * Survived

    :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Ha, the bigger they are...}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Winderunner|No time to gloat, Wrynn. We've got to stop Gul'dan before he summons another one of those things!}} :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Everyone, across the chasm!}}

    Uh-oh, when the game asks for players to have 'Survived', that's a sign things are going to go poorly.

    Jaina quickly summons another ice bridge to get up to where Gul'dan was. Just on the other side, Varian and Sylvanas pause for a moment. :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|We'll take the ridge and cover your flank.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|Sylvanas, thank you.}} :{{text|say|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Good luck, Varian.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|And you.}}

    Follow the path up to the [[Tomb of Sargeras]]. Now, if you approach Gul'dan too much you will be insta-killed by the gaze of [[Sargeras]] himself,http://www.wowhead.com/spell=181559/gaze-of-sargeras with the message "''You feel a staggering presence on the other side of the portal touch you for a moment...''". :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|There is nowhere to run, Gul'dan. Give up now, and I will grant you a swift death.}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Run? Ha. You are right, human. There is nowhere to run from my master. Now, witness the true might of Lord Sargeras!}} :''Gul'dan summons a few dozen demon forces.'' :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|Hold steady, we've broken their lines before and we will again. Charge!}}

    The two-dozen non-elites attack and are quickly cut down. :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|We will never fall to you, Gul'dan. The combined forces of Azeroth will triumph today.}} :{{text|yell|Lady Sylvanas Windrunner|Less chatting, Wrynn. Kill him already!}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|I have seen the end of your pitiful world, Wrynn. You will perish in felfire, like ten thousand worlds before your own. The legion is ENDLESS.}} :''The next wave of elites attacks, as Gul'dan summons in another wave of even stronger demons, most of which whom are the named demons defeated earlier in the scenario.''

    Stay alive, let the NPCs tank. After the second wave is defeated... :{{text|yell|King Varian Wrynn|No matter how many demons you throw at us, we will cut you down, monster.}} :''Gul'dan summons an even stronger wave of demons, including many of the named demons players have faced over the past decade.'' :{{text|yell|[[Lord Jaraxxus]]|We return.}} :{{text|yell|[[Brutallus]]|To conquer this puny world.}} :{{text|yell|[[Tichondrius|Tichondrius the Darkener]]|And burn it to a husk.}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|Do you see now, whelp? The fate of this world is sealed. The Legion cannot be stopped.}} :{{text|yell|Gul'dan|And now you will fall.}}

    The next wave starts to attack as a cutscene plays, ending the scenario and the quest.

    ===Finale=== On the other side of the scenario, the player is back on the deck of ''[[the Skyfire]]'' in [[Stormwind Harbor]], having recovered from the battle and gotten up from the cot they were laying on. This isn't the case for all the NPCs, as many of the unnamed soldiers that followed the heroes to battle are still laying injured on deck. Something has visibly changed, though: many [[Gilneas (kingdom)|Gilnean]] soldiers are now on board. [[Gilnean Druid]]s are taking care of the wounded, [[Gilnean Footman (Stormwind City)|Gilnean Footmen]] are standing ready for battle behind [[Darius Crowley]], and Genn is surrounded by two [[Gilnean Royal Guard (Stormwind City)|Gilnean Royal Guards]]. [[Gilnean Gryphon]]s stand ready to transport adventurers.
