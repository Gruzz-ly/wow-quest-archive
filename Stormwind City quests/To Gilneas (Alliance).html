    ==Objectives== Travel to [[Keel Harbor]] in the [[Ruins of Gilneas]]. * Let [[Genn Greymane|Genn]] know you are ready * Arrive in [[Gilneas]] * Talk to [[Tess Greymane|Tess]]

    ==Description== The time has come to reclaim my kingdom, champion. The [[Forsaken]] have left the land, as promised.

    However, in their absence it seems the [[Scarlet Crusade]] have decided to move in and use the city as their own personal stronghold. I mean to move them out.

    Join me in retaking Gilneas.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9|}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * 10x [[Flightstones]]

    ==Progress== Gilneas will be ours once more.

    ==Completion== Well then, looks like I'm in charge now.

    A bit unexpected, but not unwelcome.

    Very well. Let's do this.
