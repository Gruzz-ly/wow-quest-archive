    Objectives

    Complete the Battle for Lordaeron. Travel to Lordaeron Complete the scenario "The Battle for Lordaeron."

    Description

    King Wrynn has mustered our forces, . We mean to launch a full-scale assault on Lordaeron.

    We'll drive the Horde from the keep and reclaim it for the Alliance. It's been far too long since our flag flew in Tirisfal.

    Ready to ship out? Captain Angelica here will see to your transport.

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 19g 40s * 16,450 XP

    Completion

    Ah, good to see you back on your feet, . Many others were not so fortunate.

    King Anduin has returned to Stormwind Keep. He must plan what our next move will be.

    Notes

    On accept

    Speak to Captain Angelica, immediately to Shaw's right:

    Welcome aboard, .

    We're all stocked and ready to ship out just as soon as you're ready.

    The Third Fleet

    thumb|Area map Description: Meet with Genn Greymane inside Brill. Follow Hammond Clay to Brill *Charge the battlefield with Genn

    Upon entering the scenario, players land on North Coast, north of Brill and are at the camp with Stormwind Steed, Stormwind Suppliers, Ironforge Mortar Teams, Stormwind Footmen, Alliance Cannons, Stormwind Lookouts, Darnassian Archers, and General Hammond Clay.

    Follow the general south along the scar in the earth to see Stormwind Knights, Mine Carts, Stormwind Laborers and more troops. The Alliance has taken over the ruins of Brill as well: Tad Deepforge, Dwarven Riflemen, Worgen Battlemages, Alliance Siege Tanks, and Genn Greymane.

    Clearing the Way

    Description: Siege Towers Defended Siege Towers Defended (0/100%) Bonus objective: Shredder Defeated Bonus objective: Rescue the gnome mage

    Follow Genn into the battlefield and clear the enemies, including a Goblin Shredder, Horde Demolishers, Goblin Sappers, Tauren Earthshakers, Darkspear Shaman. You are assisted by Auchenai Ballistae, Glaive Throwers, Exodar Peacekeepers, Hippogryph Archers, Darnassian Huntresses, Druids of the Claw, and Gilnean Mauler. Stormwind Medics and Stormwind Priests treating Gravely Injured Soldiers.

    Just ahead is Field Marshal Cogspark, who has used a Ring of Frost to entomb a handful of Horde defenders. Help free her.

    Focus on machines and destroy them, then mop up nearby units to fill the bar.

    Clearing the Way

    Description: Defeat the Azerite War Machine Defeat the Azerite War Machine

    ''The Azerite War Machine exits the fog of war, obliterating everything in its path.''

    Start attacking it. When the war machine is at 30% health, Sylvanas intervenes:

    ''The Alliance siege towers all have their tops blown off.''

    Finish off the war machine.

    Breach the Wall

    ''Anduin jumps on the war machine and sticks his sword in it, landing the finishing blow.''

    ''Sludge Guards start spraying blight, killing friend and foe alike. Sylvanas appears and raises the fallen.''

    Blighted Grounds

    While killing the undead, also interact with Wounded Soldiers. They can say any of the following: * * * *

    Fall back with King Anduin Wrynn. As the progress bar nears 100%:

    The Light is Fading

    A cinematic plays:

    ''Anduin and Greymane lead Alliance forces back to Brill, away from the cloud of blight. As they reach a clear area, both kings turn and face the walls of Lordaeron.''

    ''A cold wind begins to blow; both kings turn, and Genn growls as a stormcloud approaches them from the rear.''

    ''The cloud parts, revealing a massive Kul Tiran flagship held aloft by arcane magic. Standing at the bow, hair and cloak whipping in the wind, is...''

    ''Jaina Proudmoore raises her hands, channeling a wave of frost magic to clear the blight clouds and freeze the Forsaken catapults and the risen skeletons.''

    ''Anduin leads his rallied troops through the clearing haze towards Lordaeron. As he approaches the base of the walls, he turns and looks upward.''

    ''Jaina uses her magic to swing the ship to port and expose its full starboard side, its arcane-charged broadside cannons blasting a hole in the city's outer walls. Approaching the breach, Anduin dismounts and draws Shalamayne.''

    Finale

    Scenario completed, players will appear at the Stormwind Harbor in a med tent. Halford Wyrmbane is nearby.

    Upon completion of the quest, Tirisfal Glades changes phase to the one that appears in the scenario. Brill has been shattered and the Lion's Wake runs to the North Coast.

    Zidormi is nearby and can changes phases if necessary.
