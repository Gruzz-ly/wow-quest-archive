    ==Objectives== Use Therazane's Portal to travel to the [[Crumbling Depths]]. Accompany [[Stormcaller Mylra]] and [[Thrall]] to recover the [[Doomhammer]]. * Enter the Crumbling Depths * Return to the [[Maelstrom]]

    ==Description==

    You found it! The Doomhammer fell to the Crumbling Depths... But the [[Burning Legion|Legion]] remains a step ahead of us. If we hurry, we can follow the [[demon]] [[Geth'xun]] and retrieve the hammer before any more of [[Deepholm|this realm]] is corrupted.

    [[Therazane]] will open a portal directly there. Let's go!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|58|20|}} * 22,200 [[XP]]

    Come, . The [[Earthen Ring]] needs you. And [[Azeroth]] needs us.
