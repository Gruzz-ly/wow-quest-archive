    Wrath of the Fungalmancer

    {{Questbox | name=Wrath of the Fungalmancer | start=[[Ruberick]] {{co|68.5|26.4|Deepholm}} | id=26583 | level={{level|Deepholm}} | category=Deepholm | reputation=+500 [[Therazane (faction)|Therazane]] | experience=43900 | money={{cost|8|60}} | rewards=[[Mushroom Stompers]] or [[Fungus-Stained Legplates]] or [[Sporeshot]] | previous=[[Doomshrooms]], [[Gone Soft]], [[Unnatural Causes]] }}{{For|the daily version|Glop, Son of Glop}}

    == Objectives == {{questportrait|Fungalmancer Glop.jpg||The shroomgroomer himself.}} Speak to Earthmender Norsala, then kill Fungalmancer Glop. Speak to {{NPC||Earthmender Norsala}} [[Fungalmancer Glop]] slain

    == Description == Their leader hides within [[Fungal Deep|his hole]] ahead. He is the one that brought the plague upon the [[Crimson Expanse|crimson expanse]].

    He must be killed.

    One of your [[Earthen Ring]] companions is already inside. You should speak with her.

    == Rewards == {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards:|Mushroom Stompers|Fungus-Stained Legplates|Sporeshot}} You will also receive: {{cost|8|60}}

    == Progress == I cannot fit inside. Our elementals are not up to the task.

    You must see this done.

    == Completion == Well done, .

    Gorsik shall hear of your deed. {{NPC||Therazane}} shall hear of your deed.

    You are quite vicious for one so small... and so soft.

    == Notes == While up at the [[Verlok Village]] for [[Shaken and Stirred]] and [[Corruption Destruction]], players run across {{NPC||Ruberick}} at the base of the pillars, who offers this quest. Accept it and fly into the cave Ruberick is looking at. Keep clear of the mushrooms. They explode for 4375 to 5625 Nature damage if approached. As the mouth of the cave expands, Norsala awaits.

    Glop has 734,000 health. This is a three-phase fight. After a little running around, Glop gets down to business. Outside of melee, which should be solely on Norsala, he throws Boomshrooms: {{abilities|Throw Boomshroom|range=45|notes=1.59 sec cast}} {{abilities|Boomshroom Explosion|Deals 5250 to 5750 Fire damage after growing to full size.}} Norsala has the following abilities: {{abilities|Lightning Bolt|range=40|Blasts an enemy with lightning, inflicting Nature Damage|notes=~2300 Nature damage}} {{abilities|Chain Lightning|range=30|Strikes an enemy with a lightning bolt that arcs to another enemy. The spell affects up to 3 targets, inflicting less Nature damage to each successive target.|notes=2.39 sec cast. ~3300 Nature damage|cd=6 sec}} {{abilities|Thunderstorm|Call down a bolt of lightning damaging nearby enemies within 15 yards. Deals 3237 to 3762 nature damage to all nearby enemies, knocking them back.}} {{abilities|Lava Burst|range=30|You hurl molten lava at the target, dealing 3244 to 3273 Fire damage. If your Flame Shock is on the target, Lava Burst will deal a critical strike.}} {{abilities|Water Shield|The caster is surrounded by 3 glboes of water, granting 270 mana per 5 sec. When a spell, melee or ranged attack hits the caster, 1157 mana is restored to the caster. Only one globe will activate every few seconds. Lasts 10 min. Only one ELemental Shield can be active on the Shaman at any one time.}} *{{abilities|Chain Heal|range=40|Infuses a wounded ally with healing energy that spreads to another nearby ally. The spell affects up to 3 targets, but the healing energy is diminished by 50% for each successive target.|notes=2.0 sec cast. Heals for around 20,000 health. Will cast on herself if necessary, otherwise on wounded players.}}

    While players are not now eligible for the achievement [[Fungalophobia]], awarded for killing Glop and not taking any damage from his thrown Boomshrooms, this is a good time to practice for the Therazane revered daily quest [[Glop, Son of Glop]].
