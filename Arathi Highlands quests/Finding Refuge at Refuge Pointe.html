    Objectives

    Look for Danath at Refuge Pointe in the Arathi Highlands. * Meet Faerin at Refuge Pointe * Search the body for clues * Follow Faerin up the hill * Speak to Karga

    Description

    If you want to find Lord Trollbane and tell him your theory about Lady Marran, the missive called him to Hammerfall, far to the east of here.

    He may have stopped at Refuge Pointe on the way--it's a Stromic encampment about halfway to the orc town. Because of the frequent bandit attacks in the area, I believe he meant to stop and check in with our people there.

    You might catch up with him there.
