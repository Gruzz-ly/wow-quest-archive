    Description

    Trade in Stromgarde has broken down. We're short on goods and workers because of the attacks.

    Nials was too busy, so I took it upon myself to write official requests for aid.

    Most are simple and straightforward--writs for supplies and such for the Stromic army.

    The desist writ for Joseph is different. He is loudly blaming everyone not human for our woes. Someone needs to officially tell him to shut his yap before he starts a riot.

    As my deputies, would you enforce my requests for aid?
