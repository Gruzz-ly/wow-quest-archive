    {{gossip|How do I acquire Flightstones and Dreaming Crests?}}

    Flightstones are located all over the Dragon Isles. They can be obtained by opening outdoor treasures, completing world quests, and by participating in weekly events, dungeons, and raids.

    Dreaming Crests can be acquired in similar fashion. There are four tiers of crests. The more dangerous the adventure you overcome, the more powerful the crests you will find.

    {{gossip|Tell me about upgrading gear using Flightstones and Dreaming Crests.}}

    Most upgrades will require a varied amount of Flightstones.

    Upgrading gear labeled as Adventurer, Veteran, Champion, Hero, or Myth usually requires the addition of Dreaming Crests. You will need fifteen Crests of the appropriate power level for each upgrade. Cuzolth will let you know which kind of Crest you need for each upgrade.

    If you bring us a piece of equipment less powerful than your equipped gear, it will be cheaper to upgrade and won't require any crests. In this way, we can lean on your own existing power to improve less-powerful gear when possible. }}

    If not already done, complete [[It Starts With a Stone]] and [[Dreaming of Crests]] to be (re)introduced to [[Flightstones]] and crests.
