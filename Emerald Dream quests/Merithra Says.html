    '''Merithra Says''' concludes the "Trouble at the Tree" optional side storyline of the [[Guardians of the Dream campaign]].

    ==Objectives== Give instructions to 5 druids while disguised as Merithra. Get [[Merithra]] disguise from [[Lady Moonberry]] Orders given to [[druid]]s (5)

    ==Description== Some druids are volunteering to help tend to [[Amirdrassil|the tree]] and set up [[Central Encampment|camp]].

    But they're so sad!

    We have to give them a reason to smile. You can give them some orders that remind them to have fun.

    But there's no way they'll take orders from you.

    That's why I got this costume for you. With this and a little spell they'll for sure listen to you!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 47,600 [[XP]] * [[Moonberry's Many Mischief Makers]] * [[Spare Party Hat]] * [[Plump Dreamseed]] * 5x [[Whelpling's Dreaming Crest]]

    ==Progress== You're clearly a master prankster but you'll have to be a master actor as well.

    A costume and a spell can only do so much.

    ==Completion== Your performance as Merithra was unmatched.

    I can hardly tell you two apart!
