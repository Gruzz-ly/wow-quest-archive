    The Right Food... is the third quest in a series of five that eventually rewards the Reins of the Ochre Dreamtalon mount. This quest has both a required 72-hour wait in order to complete and the standard six daily-reset wait before the next quest is offered.

    Objectives

    Bring some fertilizer for the plant. * Ask Professor Ash for advice * Acquire the Fertilizer Ingredients * Combine the Fertilizer Compound using the Plant Matter * Wait for the Fertilizer to finish composting (Optional) * Spread the completed fertilizer around the sapling * Entertain the plant with a few words
