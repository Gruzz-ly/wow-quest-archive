    '''A Better Future, Together''' concludes the "A Call to Aid" side story of the [[Guardians of the Dream campaign]].

    ==Objectives== Help Shae'lune perform the separation ritual on [[Shandris]]. Talk to [[Shae'lune]] about the ritual [[Somnium Bark]] (2) [[Sleeping Glory]] (5) Ingredients mixed in the bowl of moon water *Help Shandris drink the remedy

    ==Description== , we need to work with Shae'lune and help her perform the purification ritual on Shandris.

    Shae'lune says she's only done it on objects before--not people. There is some... risk involved.

    But I've come to know Shandris. She'd do the same for me.

    Talk to Shae'lune and follow her instructions.
