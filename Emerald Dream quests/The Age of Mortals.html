    ==Objectives== Blow the Horn of [[Cenarius]] while next to heroes of [[Azeroth]] and slay [[Fyrakk]]'s forces. * Empower heroes and slay enemies on the [[Field of Flames]] (0/100%) *Meet [[Tyrande]] at [[Wellspring Overlook]]. * [[Horn of Cenarius (Dragonflight)]] (provided)

    ==Description== Once again, the mortal [[race]]s have come together to defend a [[World Tree]]. It is only fitting then that we bolster their efforts with a relic from that past battle. The Horn of Cenarius.

    I entrust the horn to you, . Use it to bestow the blessings of Cenarius to our allies. With luck, it will allow us to break through [[Fyrakk]]'s forces and open a path to [[Wellspring of Life|the temple]].

    And do not forget to lend your strength as well. Every enemy felled is one fewer between us and Fyrakk.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Fearless Faerie's Shoulderpads |Chartreuse Champion's Shoulderplates |Summer Soother's Shoulders |Spirited Stag's Epaulets }}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|28|9|}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * 10x [[Flightstones]]

    ==Completion== Our allies fight with the blessing of Cenarius! It is only a matter of time until Fyrakk's forces break their ranks and flee before us.

    ==Notes== On accept, Tyrande mounts up and runs off.

    Each hero empowered or non-elite enemy slain is worth 3%.
