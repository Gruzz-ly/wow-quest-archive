    ==Description== Ah, that's much better. Now I can think.

    What did you want, again? Oh yes! Questions.

    Ask away, if you must.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * [[Tangled Yarn of Secrets]] * [[Feather of Friends]] * [[Plump Dreamseed]] * 5x [[Whelpling's Dreaming Crest]]

    ==Completion== <Q'onzu and their followers have gone. This must be the answer they deemed you deserved.

    For now, at least.>

    ::I believe Q'onzu has some history with Aviana. Perhaps she may be able to shed some light.

    ::[[Ysera]] would also certainly have much to say, but she is quite busy. Aessina, however, is close with Ysera. Perhaps she knows something.

    ::A denizen of the Dream would be my final suggestion. A dryad, perhaps?

    :{{Gossip|quest=|Do you know anything about Q'onzu?}} ::Don't get me started on that good-for-nothing [[Somnowl|owl]].

    ::Refusing our call to assist against the [[Druids of the Flame]] is not the first time they have shown cowardice in the face of evil. They claim that their domain requires them to stay out of such conflicts, but I find it convenient how their choices always lead to their own comfort.

    ::Surely they're up to their old tricks again. I'm still trying to figure out who allowed them back into the Dream!

    ::I do not wish to speak about Q'onzu any longer. Begone from me, mortal.

    :{{Gossip|quest=|Do you know anything about Q'onzu?}} ::Q'onzu? Ah, yes. Ysera is... quite familiar with that one. As familiar as one can be with such a creature.

    ::There were once rumors that they dug their way out. Preposterous. They can change many things, but they cannot change the nature of this place.
