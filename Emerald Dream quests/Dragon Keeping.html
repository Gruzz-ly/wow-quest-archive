    '''Dragon Keeping''' concludes the "Misfit Dragons" optional storyline of the [[Guardians of the Dream campaign]].

    ==Objectives== Speak to [[Vyranoth]] and complete the Storm the Halls of Valor scenario. Speak with Vyranoth to go to the Halls of Valor Complete Scenario: Storming the Halls of Valor

    ==Description== First he opposes [[dragonkin]]d, then he enslaves them, denying them their birthrights.

    We shall visit these so-called "[[Halls of Valor]]," defeat any who oppose us, and liberate the storm drakes from that which binds them.

    Come along.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|84|27}} * 14,300 [[XP]] * [[Grotto Netherwing Drake]] * [[Pact of the Netherwing]] * [[Plump Dreamseed]] * 8x [[Whelpling's Dreaming Crest]]

    ==Progress== These dragons will be freed.

    ==Completion== The Thorignir are free of that wretched keeper's influence for now--and hopefully for good.

    :We go to these so-called "Halls of Valor".
