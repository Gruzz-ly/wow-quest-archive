    ==Objectives== Find and excise the source of the [[Sor'theril Barrow Den|barrow-den]]'s flame corruption. *Druid of the Flame found

    ==Description== It seems the [[Druids of the Flame]] wasted no time in seeking to expand their influence.

    Deep within these [[barrow den]]s, some manner of corruption has taken root. It will spread if left unchecked, consuming the entire den before expanding outwards to threaten the rest of the [[Emerald Dream|dream]].

    Time is short, . Delve into the barrow, seek out the sources of the spreading flame, and excise them before the smoldering embers can grow and spread.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|28|9}} 10x [[Flightstones]] *10x [[Emerald Dewdrop]]

    ==Progress== Make haste. These flames grow brighter by the moment.

    ==Completion==

    These are not [[Fandral Staghelm]]'s Druids of the Flame. To think that someone as kind and compassionate as [[Norana Morninglight|Norana]] would count herself amongst them...

    We may never truly understand the depth of the scars left by the fallen boughs of [[Teldrassil]].
