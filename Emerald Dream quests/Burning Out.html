    '''Burning Out''' begins an optional side storyline of the [[Guardians of the Dream campaign]], unlocked the week of 14 November 2023.

    ==Objectives== Find [[Solarys Thorngale|Solarys]]'s siblings in the [[Smoldering Copse]]. Find [[Lyandris Thorngale]] Find [[Thaeldus Thorngale]] Find [[Kery'nael Thorngale]] Return to Solarys at the [[Central Encampment]].

    ==Description== Please, I know how this looks, but we are on the same side!

    My siblings and I intended to defect from the [[Druids of the Flame|Flame]] together. However, none of them have escaped or met up with me since I left, and it's been days! I fear the worst.

    Do you think I would be begging for aid, alone and unarmed, if I was not honest? Please, find them. They are all I have left.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * [[Kal'andu]] * [[Plump Dreamseed]] * 3x [[Whelpling's Dreaming Crest]]

    ==Progress== Whatever you need from me, just find them, please!

    Finite moments with my family are worth more than an eternity of regrets. Life itself is worth more than that, but my pleas fell on deaf ears.

    Thank you for saving what little of my family is left.
