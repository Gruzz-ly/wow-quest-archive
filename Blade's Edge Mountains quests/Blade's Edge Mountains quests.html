    Blade's Edge Mountains quests

    [[File:WorldMap-BladesEdgeMountains.jpg|thumb|Zone map]] The '''[[Blade's Edge Mountains]]''' are a contested level 65-68 zone in northeastern part of Outland. The story starts in the south and works its way throughout the zone, returning to the faction area hub often, where you are eventually offered breadcrumbs out to the next zones - either [[Netherstorm]], or [[Shadowmoon Valley]].

    The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[On the Blade's Edge]].

    Optional breadcrumbs: * [[No Time for Curiosity]] or [[Hero's Call: Blade's Edge Mountains!]] * [[The Ogre Threat]] or [[Warchief's Command: Blade's Edge Mountains!]] * [[Mog'dorg the Wizened (Grok)]]
