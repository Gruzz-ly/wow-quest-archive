    Objectives

    Serve tables at the celebration in Bel'ameth. * Speak with Tyrande to join the party (Optional) * Pick up first meal order (Optional) * Tables served (12)

    Description

    At long last... let us celebrate all that we have overcome, and the hope that burns brightly amongst these gathered kaldorei.

    Come, join the festivities.

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 28g 9s * 9,500 XP * 10x Flightstones * 10x Emerald Dewdrop

    Progress

    Have you thoroughly enjoyed yourself, Champion?

    Completion

    So much life and joy resides here beneath these branches. May it always be so.

    On the hill to the east, many characters have arrived and are gathered around tables as well as Night Elf Revelers, Dizzying Revelers, Disoriented Revelers, Irritated Revelers, Dedicated Patrons, Noxious Decorations, and Talking Fish. Find Erugosa or a Vibrant Feast table, then bring it to any Table. Boisterous Children and Boisterous Instigators are also running around and dancing.

    To continue, feed twelve tables, then find the Aspects near the top of the hill. Kalecgos and Merithra both offer quests, A Disquieting Feeling and A Different Enthusiasm, which are required to continue.
