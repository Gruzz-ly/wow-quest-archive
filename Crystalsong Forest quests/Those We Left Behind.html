    Those We Left Behind

    {{Questbox | id = 72670 | name = Those We Left Behind | category = Blue Dragonflight | level = {{Level|Df-max}} | levelreq = {{Level|Df-max}} | start = [[Lanigosa]]{{Co|91.4|57.2|Crystalsong Forest}} | rewards = 10x [[Flightstones]] | money = {{cost|28|9}} | previous = [[Regrets in Crystal]] | next = [[An Arcane Requiem]] }}

    ==Objectives== Ease the restless echoes of [[Shandaral]]. *Echoes soothed (6)

    ==Description== In attempting to defend their home against the fury of the [[blue dragonflight]], Shandaral's [[mage]]s condemned the souls of their people to an eternity of crystalline torment.

    Now their echoes wander aimlessly, reliving their civilization's doom again and again for more than ten thousand years.

    I have found that some of the shades yet retain a shred of sentience. Perhaps they can be reasoned with--convinced to free themselves from the mire of their own ancient fear and pass on at last.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|28|9}}

    {{itembox||q1=10|Flightstones }}

    ==Progress== The people of Shandaral were ensnared in a moment of supreme desperation. Their [[Ghost|spirits]] will not be easily assuaged.

    ==Completion== This crystalline prison is not easy to escape. Still, with luck our work here will let the spirits that yet linger find some modicum of peace.

    ==Notes== Head further and speak with the night elf ghosts. Based on the conversation option, they either attack, or simply vanish, both of which count toward the objective.
