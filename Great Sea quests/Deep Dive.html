    {{Questbox | id = 52765 | name = Deep Dive | category = Horde War Campaign | faction = Horde | level = {{level|zandalar-max}} | levelreq = {{level|zandalar-max}} | start = [[Nathanos Blightcaller]] | end = Automatic | reputation = +150 [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]] | experience = 17,850 | money = {{cost|23|40|}} | previous = [[Journey to the Middle of Nowhere]] | next = [[Seafloor Shipwreck]] }}

    ==Objectives== Speak with Hobart Grapplehammer to dive to the ocean floor, then test your headlamp. * Speak with [[Hobart Grapplehammer|Hobart]] to dive into the [[Great Sea]] * Reach the ocean floor * Test your headlamp

    ==Description== We need you to retrieve [[Marshal M. Valentine]]'s body at the ocean floor below.

    I should warn you: this part of the ocean is going to be very deep, and very dark, and very cold.

    Speak with Hobart at the starboard side of the ship. He's brought all the machinery you'll need to get to the ocean floor.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 17,850 [[XP]] * +150 [[reputation]] with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

    ==Completion==

    ...looks like you made it...

    ...I'll give you instructions...

    ...try not to get eaten.

    (Hobart Grapplehammer: Hobart speaks to you through your helmet. It's very hard to understand him.)

    Seriously. Don't leave your headlamp on for longer than, oh, I dunno, twenty seconds or so. Unless you wanna get eaten by a shark. You see a shark, you turn that light off. Understood?

    Perfect. Let's see here...

    Safety rules three through seventeen are unimportant and boring. You ready to go?

    {{Gossip|Let's dive!}}

    After reaching the bottom, you have the Deep Sea Diving buff and the extra button Headlamp is available. Leaving it on for too long triggers a message:

    :{{Text|emote|You sense something large lurking in the water nearby...}}

    If you leave it on, a [[Colossal Whale Shark|whale shark]] appears and kills you.

    :{{Text|emote|Your headlamp attracted a whale shark!}}

    Use the extra button Headlamp off to turn the light off.

    :{{Text|emote|It seems like it swam away.}}
