    Un'Goro Crater storyline

    thumb|Zone map *Un'Goro Crater is a level zone in Southern Kalimdor. All quests here are neutral and concern exploring the primal wilderness of the crater and its creatures, culminating in a revelation about the purpose of Un'Goro Crater and its connection to the titan*s.

    The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement Un'Goro Crater Quests.

    Marshal's Stand

    Optional breadcrumbs: * Tropical Paradise Beckons * Hero's Call: Un'Goro Crater! * Warchief's Command: Un'Goro Crater!

    At Marshal's Stand, J.D. Collie is researching the power crystals and pylons of the crater. She only offers one quest, but all roads lead back to her for the grand finale. * Crystals of Power

    Nolen Tacker also gives an unrelated side quest: * How to Make Meat Fresh Again

    The volcano and the silithid

    Before venturing to the further reaches of the crater, investigations and a rescue at Fire Plume Ridge must be performed. This is followed by a move against the Slithering Scar, then a journey to the Mossy Pile where the story diverges toward the pylons.

    Next: Mossy Pile and the Golakka Hot Springs

    Mossy Pile

    After reaching the Mossy Pile on the other side of the volcano, a fur hunting expedition ends with crystal pylon contact. A wildlife / engineering mission may be completed at the same time.

    The Northern Pylon

    A-Me 01

    This chain is given by Shizzle and Karna Remtravel at Mossy Pile. It is independent of the main story but convenient to do along the way.

    Golakka Hot Springs

    A brief return back to Marshal's Stand reveals that Nolen Tacker has a friend at the Golakka Hot Springs who may need assistance, ultimately leading to another pylon. Maximillian of Northshire has also taken up camp at the springs, where a few of his tasks overlap with the others.

    The Western Pylon

    Despite starting in Marshal's Stand, this branch only becomes available after Mossy Pile (quest).

    The Ballad of Maximillian

    The Eastern Pylon

    While the breadcrumbs into Un'Goro ultimately lead to Marshal's Stand, players are actually likely to walk right past Garl Stormclaw and Torwa Pathfinder first, as they stand directly at the bottom of the path into the crater from Tanaris.

    Ravasaur Trainers

    Just beyond Garl and Torwa, Horde players can begin the process of obtaining a Whistle of the Venomhide Ravasaur mount. This is the equivalent of the Alliance-only Winterspring Frostsaber earned in Winterspring.

    Venomhide Eggs

    Gorishi Grub (quest)

    World object quests

    Two crates lie in abandoned campsites and can be returned to Marshal's Stand.

    From Crate of Foodstuffs (northeast): * Is This Stuff Still Good?

    From Research Equipment (southwest): * An Abandoned Research Camp

    Finale: The Shaper's Terrace

    Afterwards, the player is sent off towards Silithus via The Dunes of Silithus.
