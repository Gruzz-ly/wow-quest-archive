    A Temple in Need

    {{For|the demo version|A Temple In Need}} {{Questbox | id = 57977 | name = A Temple in Need | category = Bastion | level = {{level|Bastion}} | start = [[Eridia]] {{co|54.1|73.6|Bastion}} | end = [[Eridia]] {{co|59.4|78.0|Bastion}} | experience = 6,800 | money = {{cost|23|63|40}} | previous = [[The Temple of Purity (quest)]] | next = [[A Wayward Disciple?]] }}

    ==Objectives== Aid the disciples within the [[Temple of Purity]]. * Aid [[Disciple Fotima]] {{co|57.4|71.5|Bastion}} * Aid [[Disciple Helene]] {{co|58.0|75.9|Bastion}} * Aid [[Disciple Lykaste]] {{co|60.0|73.7|Bastion}} *Speak to Eridia within the Temple of Purity.

    ==Description==

    Your face is new to me.

    I regret that this shall be your introduction to our temple. As my disciple has informed you, we seek the aid of all available aspirants to tend to those in our care.

    Do not trouble yourself over the specifics of the work that is needed. Those devoted to this temple will guide you. Seek out the disciples within.

    You may find me within the temple when you have given your aid.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|63|40}} * 6,800 [[XP]]

    ==Completion== You have proven yourself adept at this task. You have my gratitude.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{text|say|Eridia|Kleia, assist the paragon. Pelagos, you and our new aspirant will aid the disciples. Go in service.}} :{{text|say|Pelagos|Yes, ascended one.}}

    Cross into the Temple of Purity proper: :{{text|say|Pelagos|The Paragon is there, across the temple... and so many awaiting their rites.}}

    He offers a mandatory quest: [[On the Edge of a Revelation]]. Pick it up before moving onward.
