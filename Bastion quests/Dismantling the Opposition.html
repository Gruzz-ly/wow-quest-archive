    Dismantling the Opposition

    {{Questbox | id = 58182 | name = Dismantling the Opposition | category = Bastion | faction = Kyrian | level = {{level|sl-max}} | levelreq = {{level|sl-max}} | start = [[Mikanikos]] {{co|50.2|20.5|Bastion}} | experience = 9,450 | money = {{cost|25|74|}} | previous = [[A Partner for Eternity]] | next = [[Friend to the Forgelite]] }}

    ==Objectives== Ride Bron, then defeat 30 Forsworn forces and dismantle 2 Colossus in the [[Eternal Forge]]. * [[Bron (goliath)|Bron]] mounted {{co|50.0|20.5|Bastion}} * [[Forsworn]] forces slain (30) * [[Forsworn Colossus]] slain (2)

    ==Description== The Forsworn mobilize!

    We cannot allow them to march.

    Attune to Bron, share your power with him.

    Between the three of us, we shall destroy this new [[centurion]] army!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|25|74|}} * 9,450 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Bron was not meant as a tool of destruction, but will excel at it with you!

    ==Completion== Exhilarating! Bron grows stronger with your power.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{text|say|Mikanikos|Lend Bron your power! Your bond will strengthen him.}}

    Hop on Bron: :{{text|say|Mikanikos|Good opportunity to measure combat prowess. Onward!}}

    In the vehicle interface, players have three abilities:

    {{Abilities|Strongarm Punch|Bron punches the enemies in front of him, dealing Physical damage.|range=10|cd=1.5 sec|id=313795|icon=inv_glove_plate_bastion_d_01|notes=Instant}}

    {{Abilities|Crackdown|Bron smashes the ground in front of him, dealing Physical damage to all enemies in a cone.|range=25|cd=10 sec|id=313790|icon=spell_shaman_thunderstorm|notes=2 sec cast}}

    {{Abilities|Toss Stewards|Bron tosses Mikanikos's forgehands at the targeted Forsworn Colossus to dismantle it.|range=40|cd=10 sec|id=313757|icon=ability_eyeoftheowl|notes=1 sec cast}}

    The '''Crackdown''' ability is the skill of choice. Though the spell description says "cone", it's more of a 10-yard wide line in front of the caster. It does enough damage to one-shot most enemies.

    Forsworn forces include: [[Converted Clawguard]]s, [[Forsworn Punisher]]s, [[Forsworn Overseer]]s, [[Praetorian Artificer]]s, and [[Darksworn Forgehand]]s.

    Get within range of a colossus: :{{text|say|Mikanikos|Take down that colossus!}}

    Toss Stewards off cooldown: :{{text|say|Mikanikos|Forgehands ready for launch!}}

    Objective complete: :{{text|say|Mikanikos|Forsworn thoroughly routed! Let us return.}} :''Bron teleports back to the quest start area.''
