    {{Questbox | id = 59717 | name = Things Remembered | category = Bastion | level = {{level|sl}} | type = World Quest | start = Automatic | reputation = +125 [[Ascended (faction)|The Ascended]] | experience = 6,950 | shareable = No }}

    '''Things Remembered''' is a series of [[world quest]]s in the [[Mnemonic Locus]] and the [[Walk of Recollection]] of [[Bastion]]. Eight characters of Bastion have written down some of their memories here, and if players manage to come across all eight, they will earn the achievement [[What Bastion Remembered]].

    ==Objectives== [[File:Forgotten Memorandum.jpg|thumb|Forgotten Memorandum]] * [[Forgotten Memorandum|Forgotten Memoranda]] (10)

    ==Description== :{{text|say|[[Thenios]]|The [[Forsworn]] have all but destroyed the legacy of this place. Seek out any writings you may find, so we may preserve them.}}

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * 6,950 [[XP]] * +125 reputation with [[Ascended (faction)|The Ascended]]

    ==Completion== :{{text|say|Thenios|How curious. The [[Eonian Archives|Archives]] are blessed to obtain such a recording. Thank you for your efforts.}}
