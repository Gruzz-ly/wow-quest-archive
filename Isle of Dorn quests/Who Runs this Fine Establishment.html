[[Grand-Heister Bokk]] slain {{co|62.4|38.6|Isle of Dorn}} Talk to [[Urtago]] *Return to Urtago in Fallside Outpost.

    ==Description== While you were dealing with the tunnels and the workmasters, I saw a peculiar kobold go into a cave to the northwest. The [[Kobold]]s seemed to respect him more than the others, calling him Grand-Heister Bokk and offering him the food you call "cheese." This makes me think he is the real mastermind.

    If that kobold is in fact the real Ward of this operation, then we should take the opportunity to strike him down.

    I will meet you at this cave.

    ==Rewards== This quest line is part of unlocking: :Earthen Allied Race

    You will receive: {{Cost|23|40}} 10,400 [[XP]]
