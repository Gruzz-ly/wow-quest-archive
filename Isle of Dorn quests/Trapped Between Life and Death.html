    ==Objectives== Find a way out of the pocket dimension. * [[Pursuit of Eternal Life|Scroll]] investigated * [[Fragmented Soul]]s released (3) * Ingredients added to the Cauldron (3) * [[Disfigured Ghoul]] slain (3) * Speak with the Mirror Image * Leave pocket dimension

    ==Description== A pocket dimension. This is old magic.

    [[Malygos]] used pocket dimensions on occasion when he did not wish to be disturbed.

    They were mainly used by mages as research spaces or for testing dangerous spells. Or if a mage wanted to hide what they were doing...

    I wonder who made this one? I supposed it doesn't matter. We need to get out.

    Thankfully, every pocket dimension has an exit. Though, it is normally well hidden.

    Let's find it.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 15x [[Valorstones]]
