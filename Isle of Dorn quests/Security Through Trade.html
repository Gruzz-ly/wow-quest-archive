    {{Questbox | id = 79544 | name = Security Through Trade | category = Isle of Dorn | level = {{Level|Isle of Dorn}} | start = [[Merrix]] | end = [[Bevrusi]] | experience = 5,200 | money = {{cost|14|4|50}} | previous = [[Dornogal's Benefactor]] | next = [[The Market Reeve of the Forgegrounds]] }}

    ==Objectives== Convince Bevrusi to give you access to the cinderbrew speakeasy. * Speak to [[Bevrusi]] * Insist you need the Passrune * See what Bevrusi will trade

    ==Description== If we're to solve this quarrel between [[Steelvein]] and [[Overseer Ardgrin|Ardgrin]] amicably, then we'll need to make some new friends.

    One of Ardgrin's employees, [[Obstorn]], frequents a cinderbrew speakeasy here in [[Dornogal]]. Only [[Machine Speakers]] may enter it.

    Even as [[Councilward]], I am not able to access it. However, there are others in the city who can.

    Bevrusi, a [[Gundargaz]] trader, should be able to give you access. If you can grease her palm, she may be able to supply you access.

    ==Rewards== This quest line is part of unlocking: :Earthen Allied Race

    You will receive: * {{cost|14|4|50}} * 5,200 [[XP]]
