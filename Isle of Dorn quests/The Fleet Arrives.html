    '''The Fleet Arrives''' begins the max-level storyline of [[The War Within (achievement)]]. This quest becomes available after completing the leveling campaign and reaching Renown level 4 with the [[Council of Dornogal]], [[Assembly of the Deeps]], [[Hallowfall Arathi]], and [[Severed Threads]].

    Level 80 [[alt]]s of players who've previously completed the leveling campaign can pick up [[A Time to Come Together]], which skips the leveling campaign and leads directly into The Fleet Arrives.

    ==Objectives== Scout the harbor for ships arriving from the [[Horde]] and [[Alliance]] fleet. [[Telescope (Dornogal)|Telescope]] used {{co|35.1|80.3|Dornogal}} Report back to [[Moira Thaurissan|Moira]]

    ==Description== From the moment we crashed on this isle, it were clear as crystal we'd be needin' allies if we hoped to face [[Xal'atath]].

    [[Jaina]] and [[Thrall]] took off tae muster reinforcements, an' you and I set tae work seein' what could be done here with the [[earthen]].

    An' I have to say we've done well on our own, haven't we?

    But it looks like our reinforcements may have finally arrived. [[Peacekeepers]] spied ships comin' this way. Go take a look an' confirm the good news!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|11|70}} * 5,800 [[XP]] *10x [[Valorstones]]

    ==Completion== I'm glad to see you are well, .

    The journey took longer than anticipated. The capricious sea had its own plans it seems.
