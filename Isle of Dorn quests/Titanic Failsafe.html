    '''Titanic Failsafe''' concludes the second chapter of the max-level [[The War Within (achievement)]] campaign. The storyline continues immediately with [[Gathering Intel]].

    ==Objectives== {{Questportrait|Colossal Titan Construct.jpg|Colossal Titan Construct|This ancient titan machine of war has risen from the depths of the ocean to eradicate the disobedient earthen of Dorn.}} Defeat the colossal titan construct. * [[Shraubendre]] mounted * [[Colossal Titan Construct]] slain {{co|59.1|74.5|Isle of Dorn}}

    ==Description== So the [[titan]]s send their ancient machines against [[Earthen|their disobedient children]].

    Would our creators have us protect the [[Coreway]] at the expense of all else? At the expense of [[Azeroth]] herself? Are we meant to preside over a road untraveled, leading to the world's dead heart?

    No, today we are all [[Unbound]], and we will fight for our freedom. For Azeroth!

    ==Rewards== You will be able to choose one of: * [[Stormrider's Cuffs]] * [[Stormrider's Bindings]] * [[Stormrider's Bracers]] * [[Stormrider's Armplates]]

    You will also receive: * {{cost|46|80}} * 15,250 [[XP]] * 15x [[Valorstones]] * +500 reputation with [[Council of Dornogal]] or 25x [[Resonance Crystals]]

    ==Progress== None on this isle will be safe until that titan construct is dealt with. Go!

    ==Completion== It was a trial by fire, but you've proven that the [[earthen]] of The Isle of [[Dorn]] are ready for the war to come. Where once there was discord, there is now unity of purpose. They are no longer [[Oathsworn]] or Unbound. They are united, just as we should be, against this darkness.

    Jaina has rooted the Colossal Titan Construct just east of [[Golgrin's Reach]] on the shoreline. The construct has two abilities: * {{abilities|Titantic Plasma Bolt|notes=Randomly fired shots, hits for ~500k health.}} * {{abilities|Scorching Beam|A concentrated emission of coherent energy hotter than the sun.|notes=Winds up a cast on the adventurer that ticks 5 times for ~150k health.}}

    As before, Shaubendre has three abilities that fire off when normal skyriding abilities are used:

    {{abilities|Surge Forward|Flap forward. Surge Forward evades Scalechillers, Scalechargers, and other obstacles encountered in time trials and races.|cd=1.5 sec|notes=Instant. Also applies a 2-second Lightning Sheath that absorbs damage.}}

    * {{abilities|Lightning Sheath|The stormrook protects itself and its rider from all damage for 2 sec.}}

    {{abilities|Skyward Ascent|Flap upward.|cd=1.5 sec|notes=Instant. Also fires bolts of lightning forward that hit the construct for ~740k HP each.}}

    {{abilities|Whirling Surge|Spiral forward a great distance, increasing speed. Whirling Surge evades Wingshredders and dismounts airborne enemy players.|cd=30 sec|notes=Also fully-heals the player.}}

    Surge Forward while the bomb debuff is ticking down over the adventurer's head to bubble off the (otherwise unavoidable) beam attack ticks. Spam Skyward Ascent on approach to the construct to damage it, and use Whirling Surge to heal. Plenty of vigor orbs are in the area to help speed vigor recharge.
