    Stones of Dornogal

    {{Questbox | id = 78457 | name = Stones of Dornogal | category = Isle of Dorn | level = {{level|Isle of Dorn}} | start = [[Merrix]] {{co|29.4|59.5|Dornogal}} | end = [[Merrix]] {{co|39.9|26.0|Dornogal}} | experience = 10,400 | money = {{Cost|28|9}} | shareable = Yes | previous = [[Hypocenter]] | next = [[State of the Union]] }}

    ==Objectives== Meet with various [[earthen]] citizens in [[Dornogal]]. * Ask a guard about profession trainers * Speak to Flight Master [[Breem]] * Speak to Innkeeper [[Ronesh]] * Speak to [[Brann Bronzebeard]] * Speak to [[Auditor Balwurz]] Return to Merrix in Foundation Hall in Dornogal. [[Councilward's Signet]] (provided)

    ==Description== I am [[Merrix]], Councilward of Dornogal.

    I appreciate your aid--especially in light of the fraught nature of your arrival. You must forgive Stormward [[Baelgrim]]. Being suspicious is part of his directive.

    A formal discussion of our situation is warranted. If you wish to strike back at the [[nerubian]]s, as we do, meet us at the [[Foundation Hall]]. Baelgrim and I need to review the data so far.

    In the meantime, you are my guest. Take my ring. It will allow you to access Dornogal's amenities.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|28|9}} 10,400 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== The attack seems concentrated on the Coreway. The rest of the city is secure, but we should ready ourselves for anything.

    ==Completion== Baelgrim's vigilance has kept us safe all these years, but today we need help.
