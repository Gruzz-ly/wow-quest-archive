    Urgent Recovery

    {{Questbox | id = 78531 | name = Urgent Recovery | category = Isle of Dorn | level = {{level|tww-intro}} | start = [[Thrall]] {{co|29.4|55.1|Isle of Dorn}} | experience = 10,400 | money = {{Cost|28|9}} | shareable = Yes | previous = [[Violent Impact]] | next = [[Shattered Spires]] or [[Secure the Beach]] }}

    ==Objectives== Aid 7 survivors of the [[Dalaran]] crash. Crash survivors aided (7) [[Dalaran Civilian]]s healed (5) (Optional)

    ==Description== We survived mostly unharmed. Not everyone was so lucky.

    Many who were in Dalaran when it went down are unaccounted for. They will need our help.

    Search the battlefield for survivors. Help them in any way you can.

    We need everyone up and fighting!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|28|9}} 10,400 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== We must save everyone we can.

    ==Completion== It is good to know many survived.

    When this battle is over, we will recover the bodies of those who did not.

    ==Notes== Pick up [[Slay the Saboteurs]] from [[Lady Jaina Proudmoore]] before heading out. On accept of both: :{{Text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|We have to push forward, or we'll be slaughtered!}} :{{Text|say|Thrall|Rescue who you can along the way!}}

    The survivors are [[Brann Bronzebeard]], [[Moira Thaurissan]], [[Dagran Thaurissan II]], [[Archmage Aethas Sunreaver]], [[Ritssyn Flamescowl]], [[Marin Noggenfogger]], and [[Meryl Felstorm]]. They are all marked on the minimap.

    Marin Noggenfogger is found with a few [[Envenomed Contractor]]s, some of whom are dead, a [[Recovering Contractor]] and several [[Underbelly Bruiser]]s. Marin, who is busy gutting a [[Nerubian Saboteur]], has the following buff: {{Abilities|Extracting Venom Glands|id=437318|Extracting venom glands from a dead nerubian.|icon=ability_rogue_poisonedknife}} :{{Text|say|Marin Noggenfogger|Hey, you! Give out these antidotes! I'm guttin' venom sacs fast as I can!}} -or- {{ClassIcon|Rogue}} {{text|say|Shadowblade! Give out these antidotes! I'm guttin' venom sacs fast as I can!}} Loot the [[Hastily-Prepared Antidote]] near him to toss it to an [[Envenomed Contractor]] in the area, who becomes [[Recovering Contractor]].
