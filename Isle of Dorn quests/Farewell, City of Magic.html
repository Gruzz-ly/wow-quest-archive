    '''Farewell, City of Magic''' concludes the [[Fate of the Kirin Tor]] storyline.

    ==Objectives== Place mementos brought by [[Dalaran]] survivors on the boats in the [[Tranquil Strand]]. * Mementos placed on boats (3) * Speak with [[Jaina]]

    ==Description== Not all the [[Kirin Tor]]'s issues can be solved in one day.

    But one way we can act is to honor those who have fallen. After speaking with everyone here, we have cobbled together a ceremony to send off those we have lost... and Dalaran.

    We would be honored if you would join us and participate in remembering our fallen comrades in the Kirin Tor.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * [[Fate of the Kirin Tor]]

    ==Progress== One thing I am sure of is that the Kirin Tor's future is not another floating fortress in the sky.

    No, whatever path the Kirin Tor chooses, it will be firmly rooted here, on Azeroth, among the people it serves.

    ==Completion== I will stay here with all of you and help however I can.
