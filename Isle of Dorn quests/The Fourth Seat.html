    {{Questbox | id = 78461 | name = The Fourth Seat | category = Isle of Dorn | level = {{level|Isle of Dorn}} | start = [[Moira Thaurissan]] {{co|40.5|25.9|Dornogal}} | end = [[Adelgonn]] {{co|41.9|72.6|Isle of Dorn}} | experience = 1,300 | money = {{Cost|2|80|90}} | shareable = Yes | previous = [[State of the Union]] | next = [[Delve into the Earth]] }} [[File:The Fourth Seat.jpg|thumb|Adelgonn and Ebona]]

    ==Objectives== Find [[Adelgonn]] in [[Freywold Village]] in the [[Isle of Dorn]]. * [[Earthen Teleporter]] used * Regroup with [[Baelgrim]] at the top of [[Foundation Hall]] * Adelgonn found

    ==Description== With [[Magni Bronzebeard|me da']] and [[Anduin Wrynn|Anduin]] weakened and [[Alleria Windrunner|Alleria]] nowhere to be found, it's up to us, , to clear the way for [[Thrall]] an' [[Jaina Proudmoore|Jaina]]'s army if we hope tae strike back at the [[nerubian]]s an' [[Xal'atath]].

    But we cannae get to the nerubians without the [[Coreway]]. An' we cannae repair the Coreway without the [[earthen]]'s help.

    If Merrix thinks this Adelgonn an' her "[[Unbound]]" are the only ones that can fix the Coreway, then we need tae find her.

    Time tae sharpen me smile an' put on me diplomat hat. Are ye with me, ?

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|2|80|90}} 1,300 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== If you are with Baelgrim, then the Council and Oathsworn must have accepted you onto our land.

    ==Completion== It seems I have little choice but to hear you and Baelgrim out.

Also available are [[Frayed Legacy]] from [[Freysworn Cruton]], and [[All Ore Nothing]] from [[Germira]].
