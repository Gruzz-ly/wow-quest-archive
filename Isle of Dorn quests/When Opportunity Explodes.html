    '''When Opportunity Explodes''' begins the [[Undermine(d) campaign]], starting the [[patch 11.1.0]] portion of [[The War Within campaign]]. Find [[Renzik "The Shiv"]] in the [[Foundation Hall]] of [[Dornogal]] to begin.

    ==Objectives== Investigate the blasted hole in [[Opportunity Point]]. * Traverse the [[Coreway]] to the [[Ringing Deeps]]. (Optional) * Exit to [[Ringing Deeps]]. (Optional) * Find [[Gazlowe]] at the blasted hole in [[Opportunity Point]].

    ==Description== There ya are, ! Have you heard the latest news? There's a big hole blown in the side of the mountain out by [[Opportunity Point]] in the [[Ringing Deeps]].

    There were [[Opportunity Acquisitions|opportunistic folks]] taking it over, and now there's even more. We'd better hurry over there.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|2|34}} * 1,200 [[XP]]

    ==Completion== It's... a tollbooth?

    You've gotta be kiddin' me.

    I guess you can make money off of anythin'!
