    ==Objectives== Listen to [[Brann Bronzebeard|Brann's]] story of how he lost his treasures and his hat. * Brann's story experienced

    ==Description== Ye may have noticed a change in me fashion.

    Some goblin went and took me hat! And all the treasures we had collected so far!

    I'll tell ye how it all went down.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: [[Delver's Gob-Trotter]] {{cost|23|40}}

    ==Completion== And now ye know the whole story.

    But at least I swiped the button for the buggers ride.

    You take it! I got bigger things to handle.

    ==Notes== Speak with Brann again: {{dialogue|{{gossip|quest=|What happened to your hat and all of our treasures?}}}} :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|The story began one day while I was explorin' Earthcrawl Mines...}}

    This starts a small scenario, set in the [[Earthcrawl Mines]] at Tier 11 with [[Johnny Awesome]] serving as the adventurer, but having already suffered 22 deaths before managing to kill the first Nerubian Lord: :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|I think it's about time we take a break, Johnny.}} :{{text|say|Johnny Awesome|But we just defeated the first group! Perhaps you could lead on from here?}} :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|...Alright lad. Just keep an eye out for those webs.}}

    The UI calls Johnny out for this: :{{abilities|Tier 11|icon=achievement_legionpvp2tier5|Johnny Awesome has no right dragging Brann into this difficulty. Don't be like Johnny.|id=472931}}

    Brann has three abilities:

    {{Abilities|Get Out Of There!|Shout for your fellow delver to get out of the way. Why are they always standing in the way of something bad?|icon=ability_warrior_battleshout|id=472365}}

    {{Abilities|Don't Stand In Those Webs|Alert your fellow delvers about the dangers of standing in nerubian webbing.|icon=inv_ability_web_nova|id=472366}}

    {{Abilities|Pause Story|Stop listening to Brann's story.|icon=ability_revendreth_demonhunter|id=472380}}

    Brann's #1 and #2 abilities will do as warned! Loot the dead nerubian lord to pick up [[Johnny's Share of the Spoils]], which might cover Johnny's repair cost, and a [[Giant Pile of Gold]]: :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Here's your take of the spoils!}}

    In the second room, the floor is carpeted with dead nerubians: :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|That's a lot of dead nerubians. Who did all of this?}} :{{text|say|Johnny Awesome|I'll keep an eye out for any treasures while you dig through those corpses.}}

    Interact with the [[Crispy Nerubian]] on the minecart tracks: :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Hrm... somethin' isn't right here. Looks like some traps took out these nerubians.}} :{{text|say|Johnny Awesome|No need to be cautious Brann. I see some treasure over there!}} :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Don't step there-}} :''Johnny runs over a landmine, knocking him out, stunning Brann, and blasting Brann's hat clean off his head! The Underpin runs up on his mech.'' :{{text|say|The Underpin|Gotcha! More trespassers. I own all the underground... including all these curios ya found in it!}} :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Ye blasted... give me back those curios!}} :{{text|say|The Underpin|And I'll take that hat too! Ya gotta learn ALL the delves belong to the Underpin now!}} :''The Underpin picks up Brann's hat, then runs away.'' :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Ye can take me treasures... me curios... but nobody gets away with takin' me hat!}}

    After the scenario ends, Brann continues: :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|And that's how some overpowered goblin stole me hat! Oh and all of the treasures we earned.}} :{{text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Not tae worry. I swiped the device for his "Gob-Trotter." Yours for the takin'.}}
