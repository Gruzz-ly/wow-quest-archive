    '''Delve into the Earth''' serves as an introduction to [[Delves]], the tentpole solo and small-group feature of {{expac|The War Within}}.

    ==Objectives== Complete the Earthcrawl Mines delve near [[Freywold Village]]. Journey to the [[Earthcrawl Mines]] delve Speak to [[Brann Bronzebeard]] Open the [[Explorers' League Supplies]] Earthcrawl Mines entered Earthcrawl Mines objectives completed Earthcrawl Mines treasure found *Speak to Brann Bronzebeard outside the delve

    ==Description== The [[nerubian]] attack, your meteoric fall...

    And now [[Baelgrim]] is here?

    Baelgrim needs more hands for his [[Coreway]], but I couldn't help even if I wanted to. My people are dying as we speak. Some were abducted by the nerubians during the attack.

    You're not the first outlander I've met. A small fellow, like Moira, arrived here earlier and wasted no time following the nerubians. He took the path west to a nearby cave.

    I would be in your debt if you found my people.

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Battleaxe]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Battlestaff]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Greatmace]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Knife]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Saber]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Scepter]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Spellsword]] [[Arachnophobic Spelunker's Striker]]

    This opens the Delve companion interface. Select a role for Brann, either healer or damage dealer, then run through the instance line to enter the delve. Delves can be completed solo or in role-agnostic groups of up to five players, though parties must be manually formed and cannot be queued for. While leveling, players can choose Tier 1 (minimum challenge) or Tier 2 (moderate challange). At max level, after completing [[The War Within campaign]] and an item level of at least 538, they can choose Tier 3 or higher.

    For a walkthrough, see the [[Earthcrawl Mines]] article.
