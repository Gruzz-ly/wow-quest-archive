    Isle of Dorn storyline

    thumb|Isle of Dorn zone map

    The Isle of Dorn storyline begins The War Within campaign on the Isle of Dorn.

    Intro: Visions of Azeroth

    Magni Bronzebeard, the Speaker of Azeroth, attempted to commune with her to interpret the meaning of the Radiant Song. During the attempt, he lost consciousness. When he awoke, he informed Azeroth's champions that Azeroth had been trying to warn them that Khaz Algar, a titan installation, was in danger. In response, Azeroth's champions assisted in preparing Dalaran for transportation to the island. Once the city was teleported, Archmage Drenden—who had mysteriously reappeared after years of absence—revealed himself to be Xal'atath, the Harbinger of the Void. Xal'atath launched an assault on the city with an army of nerubians under her command. While Khadgar engaged Xal'atath in battle, the champions and their allies descended to the Isle of Dorn. Despite Khadgar's efforts, Xal'atath emerged victorious. She then used the Dark Heart to destroy Dalaran, reducing the floating city to rubble.

    Campaign

    The three-chapter campaign is tracked in The Isle of Dorn (achievement).

    Breaking Point

    thumb|After the fall After Dalaran's crash on the Isle of Dorn, the city's survivors defended themselves against nerubian forces commanded by Queensguard Zirix. During the battle, the earthen Stormward Baelgrim and his Stormriders arrived to aid them. Soon afterward, an explosion went off in Dornogal, the earthen capital. Baelgrim—who remained wary of the survivors—led them there to investigate. Together, they repelled the nerubian attackers, but discovered that the Coreway, an earthen-forged passage leading toward Azeroth's core, had been sabotaged during the assault. In response, Jaina Proudmoore returned to Stormwind to request reinforcements, while Thrall traveled to Orgrimmar to do the same. Alleria Windrunner departed to pursue Xal'atath, leaving the remaining survivors in Dornogal to assist the earthen.

    Earthen Fissures

    thumb|Dead nerubians and trapped earthen

    In Dornogal, the Alliance and Horde assisted the earthen in clearing rubble and rescuing survivors, gaining access to the Archive. Inside, they met two additional members of the Council of Dornogal: Speaker Brinthe and Councilward Merrix. The Council determined that the Coreway could not be rebuilt without the aid of the Unbound, the original architects of Dornogal who had departed the city many years earlier. Councilward Merrix requested that Moira Thaurissan and Baelgrim seek out Adelgonn, the Unbound's leader and a former Stoneward of the Council, in Freywold Village. Adelgonn was reluctant to assist Baelgrim, but the outsiders earned her trust by rescuing her people from the delve Earthcrawl Mines. With Baelgrim's support, Adelgonn pledged the Unbound's assistance in restoring the Coreway. Soon after, a nerubian swarm was sighted advancing toward the Proscenium, where many wounded earthen were being housed. At the site, Alleria Windrunner engaged Queensguard Zirix. Zirix ultimately retreated, with Alleria giving chase. The outsiders and Baelgrim repelled the nerubian assault, securing the Proscenium and protecting the wounded.

    The First Blow

    thumb|Cinderbrew Meadery****

    With Adelgonn's return to the Council of Dornogal, the Unbound and Oathsworn began working together once more. At Councilward Merrix's direction, the Unbound commenced repairs on the Coreway. To safeguard the effort, Baelgrim sought to rally the Stormriders at the Stormperch. Upon arrival, Baelgrim discovered that his second-in-command, Stormguard Gorren, had corrupted the Rookery with a dark influence, leaving the Stormriders nearly destroyed. The outsiders aided Baelgrim in defeating a corrupted beast within the Rookery's depths, but with his forces depleted, Baelgrim devised a new plan to counter the nerubian threat. At the Cinderbrew Meadery, Baelgrim prepared a trap to lure the nerubians inside and ignite the cinderbrew kegs to destroy them. The outsiders assisted in setting the trap, but during the ensuing battle, the nerubians overwhelmed them and sabotaged the fuses. As Queensguard Zirix led his swarm into the meadery, Baelgrim detonated the stores himself, sacrificing his life to ensure the nerubians' destruction. Adelgonn, Wenbrandt, and the outsiders returned to Dornogal to report Baelgrim's death.

    The expansion campaign continues with the Ringing Deeps storyline with Into the Deeps.

    Sojourner

    Most optional storylines are tracked in Sojourner of Isle of Dorn.

    Spores of Dread

    Woldtender Igris, just south of Boskroot Basin, starts the Spores of Dread chapter, though there's an optional breadcrumb at Freywold Village.

    Behind Closed Doors/Merrix and Steelvein

    Behind Closed Doors (also called Merrix and Steelvein) is one of the Allied Races: Earthen-required storylines. It starts in northeastern Dornogal.

    Seven Soldiers

    thumb|Armed pearl farmers gathered at Glimmering Shore****

    At Freywold Village, Aggartha starts recruiting soldiers to help the Glimmering Shore pearl farmers defend against invading gorlocs.

    Lost Lord of the Storm

    At Keepers Terrace in Dornogal, Rooktender Otwin breadcrumbs players to Rambleshire.

    Hope, An Anomaly

    At Freywold Village, Hreka is looking for help with making a delivery.

    The Hermit

    At several locations on the Isle of Dorn, but likely at Crossroads Plaza, players will come across a Dropped Package that starts this storyline.

    A Titanic Expedition

    At Crossroads Plaza, Rannan Korren, Tuberros, and Tharessa Sunstrand are recovering, and Rannan, titan addict that he is, has heard rumors of alleged titan activity at the Three Shields off the northeast coast.

    Remember Me, Earthen

    thumb|Mourning Rise****

    At Crossroads Plaza, Urtago breadcrumbs players to Mourning Rise via the Fallside Outpost. At the rise, kobolds have arrived and are desecrating the graves of fallen earthen. This is one of the storylines required for Allied Races: Earthen.
