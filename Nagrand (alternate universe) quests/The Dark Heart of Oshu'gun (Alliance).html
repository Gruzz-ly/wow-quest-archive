    The Dark Heart of Oshu'gun (Alliance)

    ==Objectives== Enter the Sanctum of the Naaru. * Enter the [[Sanctum of the Naaru]]. * [[Decimatus]] slain

    ==Description== Amongst the timeworn wreckage of the [[Oshu'gun (alternate universe)|Genedar]] is the entrance to the vessel's inner chamber. The [[void]] energy appears to be most concentrated in that place.

    You must enter the inner chamber and face whatever evil lurks within. I fear whatever fate may have befallen the great naaru {{Au|K'ure}} in that place, but regardless the evil there must be purged.

    May the [[Light]] be with you, .

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Shortsword |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Mace |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Mallet |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Greatmaul |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Crossbow |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Dagger |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Staff |Void-Warped Oshu'gun Spellstaff }}

    You will also receive: {{cost|15}} 14,930 [[XP]] *[[Hanging Draenethyst Light]]

    ==Completion== The fate of K'ure could not have been prevented.

    {{Au|Cho'gall}} accelerated the [[naaru]]'s transition into a [[void god]] in hopes of bringing destruction upon {{Au|Draenor}}. Cho'gall must be dealt with, but for now let us honor the memory of the great naaru K'ure.

    K'ure was truly the savior of my people, a friend to the virtuous, and a teacher of [[prophet]]s.

    May the great naaru ever live within our hearts.
