    A Celestial Experience (Alliance)

    {{questbox | image=A Celestial Experience.jpg | imagecaption=Xuen, Anduin, Dezco, Taran Zhu, and Zhi | name=A Celestial Experience | start=Anduin Wrynn | faction=Alliance | id=31394 | level= | category=Kun-Lai Summit | experience=129,000 | money=10g 20s | previous=Temple of the White Tiger (Alliance) (optional) | next=A Witness to History (Alliance) |other-faction=A Celestial Experience (Horde) }}

    This quest requires players to complete three short combat challenges to earn the right to pass through the Gate of the August Celestials. No travel is required. A great deal of NPC dialogue occurs during this quest, primarily between Xuen, Anduin Wrynn, and Taran Zhu, highlighting the conflicts between the Pandaren, Horde, and Alliance.

    Objectives

    Speak to Xuen and pass his tests. Speak to Xuen Pass the First Test Pass the Second Test Pass the Third Test

    Description

    Nobody is allowed into the Vale without the approval of the August Celestials. Xuen, The White Tiger, appears ready to listen to me.

    But Taran Zhu of the Shado-Pan is reluctant to let the Alliance or Horde anywhere NEAR the valley after all that's happened.

    Maybe you can help me make my case. Speak to the White Tiger on our behlalf. Be careful! He may set a number of challenges before you...

    Rewards

    You will receive: 10g 20s

    Progress

    Are you finished with the White Tiger's tasks?

    Completion

    You did it, !

    The White Tiger has agreed to allow us to accompany the Pandaren into the Vale of Eternal Blossoms. An Alliance outpost is already set up within. Now, the hard work of cleaning up the damage can begin!

    Gains

    • 129000 XP
