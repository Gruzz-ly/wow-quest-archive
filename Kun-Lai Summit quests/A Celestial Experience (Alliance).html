    A Celestial Experience (Alliance)

    {{questbox | image=A Celestial Experience.jpg | imagecaption=Xuen, Anduin, Dezco, Taran Zhu, and Zhi | name=A Celestial Experience | start=[[Anduin Wrynn]] {{co|68.8|43.1|Kun-Lai Summit}} | faction=Alliance | id=31394 | level={{level|Kun-Lai Summit}} | category=Kun-Lai Summit | experience=129,000 | money={{cost|10|20}} | previous=[[Temple of the White Tiger (Alliance)]] (optional) | next=[[A Witness to History (Alliance)]] |other-faction=A Celestial Experience (Horde) }}

    This quest requires players to complete three short combat challenges to earn the right to pass through the [[Gate of the August Celestials]]. No travel is required. A great deal of NPC dialogue occurs during this quest, primarily between [[Xuen]], [[Anduin Wrynn]], and [[Taran Zhu]], highlighting the conflicts between the Pandaren, Horde, and Alliance.

    == Objectives == Speak to Xuen and pass his tests. Speak to Xuen Pass the First Test Pass the Second Test Pass the Third Test

    == Description == Nobody is allowed into the [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms|Vale]] without the approval of the [[August Celestials]]. {{NPC||Xuen}}, The White Tiger, appears ready to listen to me.

    But {{NPC||Taran Zhu}} of the [[Shado-Pan]] is reluctant to let the [[Alliance]] or [[Horde]] anywhere NEAR the valley after all that's happened.

    Maybe you can help me make my case. Speak to the White Tiger on our behlalf. Be careful! He may set a number of challenges before you...

    == Rewards == You will receive: {{cost|10|20}}

    == Progress == Are you finished with the White Tiger's tasks?

    == Completion == You did it, !

    The White Tiger has agreed to allow us to accompany the Pandaren into the Vale of Eternal Blossoms. An Alliance outpost is already set up within. Now, the hard work of cleaning up the damage can begin!

    == Gains == * 129000 XP

It also did not refer to any damage to clean up. * Xuen's line beginning, "It is decided", was originally: ::{{text|say|Xuen|It is decided. I will open the gates. The Vale of Eternal Blossoms will be open for all.}}
