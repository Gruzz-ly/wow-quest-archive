    Kun-Lai Summit storyline

    thumb|Zone map

    The *Kun-Lai Summit* storyline takes place after the Valley of the Four Winds and Krasarang Wilds storylines during the Mists of Pandaria campaign. Messenger Grummles show up at Stoneplow, Halfhill, and the Grassy Cline offering a message from Lorewalker Cho in the form of the breadcrumb The Road to Kun-Lai. The summit can be accessed via the Veiled Stair, which begins at the Grassy Cline in the northeast part of Valley of the Four Winds. ''The Spring Drifter'' offers a free boat ride through the level -infested Ancient Passage, dropping adventurers off at Binan Village in the southeast corner of the summit zone.

    The progress of the storyline is tracked in the achievement Slum It in the Summit.

    Near the end of the Shado-Pan Monastery quest chain, adventurers have My Husband... in their quest logs, which is a breadcrumb to the Townlong Steppes storyline.

    The yaungol invasion and making new allies

    thumb|Binan Village* *thumb|Chow Farmstead* *thumb|Fire Camp Ordo* At *Binan Village, the yaungol are attacking. Lorewalker Cho was able to save Taylor||Admiral Taylor and General Nazgrim, along with a few other members of either faction's forces. Looking to get the pandaren to ally with them, not the opposite faction, the adventurer's faction commander instructs the adventurer to help evacuees from Eastwind Rest or Westwind Rest.

    A veritable Christmas tree of breadcrumbs light up upon completion: Cho's Missive, Muskpaw Ranch (quest), The Missing Merchant, and The Shado-Pan. Additionally, if adventurers have already reached level 88, they can choose to skip directly to the Townlong Steppes storyline with Beyond the Wall.

    Shado-Pan

    thumb|Firebough Nook* *thumb|Winter's Blossom* Adventurers first run across the *Shado-Pan at the Shado-Pan Fallback, near the Firebough Nook in the southwest corner of the zone. From there, adventurers travel up to Winter's Blossom, overlooking the Ox Gate, where yaungol are invading. After fending off the attack, Ban Bearheart takes adventurers to the Shado-Pan Monastery in his hot air balloon demanding answers as to why nobody has been able to get any reinforcements of late...

    The Thunder King

    thumb|One Keg* *thumb|Valley of Emperors* *thumb|Zouchin Province* At *One Keg, players run across Lorewalker Cho. He begins Path Less Traveled, which starts a fairly long chain in the northeast part of the zone.

    Side quests

    Inkgill Mere

    thumb|Inkgill Mere* Just to the east of *Binan Village is Inkgill Mere, a jinyu village that has fallen to the corruption of the Sha of Anger.

    The Yak Wash

    thumb|Lao & Son's Yakwash* Northeast of *Westwind and Eastwind is Lao & Son's Yakwash. The yaungol have taken over the Muskpaw Ranch and the Needle Sprite||needle sprites have stolen Lao Muskpaw||Lao Muskpaw's son, Muskpaw Jr.

    Back in Yak sends adventurers (and some of the rescued yaks) up to the Grummle Bazaar.

    The Burlap Trail

    thumb|Grummle Bazaar* *thumb|Burlap Trail* In the shadow of the *Temple of the White Tiger is the Grummle Bazaar, trailhead of the Burlap Trail.

    Footnote - After completing The Leader Hozen, a breadcrumb becomes available from Brother Trailscenter called The Burlap Trail: To Kota Basecamp for those quests there.

    Temple of the White Tiger

    thumb|Gate of the August Celestials At the Temple of the White Tiger, Anduin Wrynn and Sunwalker Dezco are pleading with Xuen to open the gate to the Vale of Eternal Blossoms.

    Kota Peak

    thumb|Kota Basecamp* On the way to *Winter's Blossom from the Shado-Pan Fallback for To Winter's Blossom, adventurers run past the Kota Basecamp.

    Footnote - A variant version of these quests is available from Uncle Tallmug at Tallmug's Camp due south of here. However Kota Peak has a breadcrumb available, and this version does not. Both sets of quests have the same rewards, names and cancel each other out - but each has a unique Quest ID.

    Zouchin Village

    thumb|Zouchin Strand* Up at *Zouchin Village, an early quest provides a breadcrumb to the Zouchin Strand.

    Dailies

    thumb|Temple of the White Tiger* The level-90 daily quests involving *Xuen at the Temple of the White Tiger are not offered every day--only one of the four August Celestials temples will be questable each day.
