    Do a Barrel Roll! (quest)

    {{Questbox | image=Do a Barrel Roll.jpg | name=Do a Barrel Roll! | start=[[Lao-Chin the Iron Belly]] {{co|31.8|60.1|Kun-Lai Summit}} | faction=Neutral | id=30991 | level={{level|Kun-Lai Summit}} | category=Kun-Lai Summit | reputation=+75 [[Shado-Pan]] | experience=162000 | rewards=[[Lao Chin's Last Mug]] | money={{cost|10|60}} | previous=[[Lao-Chin's Gambit]] | next=[[Finish This!]] }}

    ==Objectives== [[File:Osul Invader.jpg|thumb|[[Osul Invader]].]] Pick up a keg near [[Lao-Chin the Iron Belly|Lao-Chin]], roll it downhill at the [[yaungol]], and ignite it near them to destroy 4 Osul Treelaunchers and 50 Osul Invaders. [[Osul Invader]] slain (50) [[Osul Treelauncher]] slain (4)

    ==Description== When I trained as a Brewmaster, I didn't expect to use my knowledge like this...

    Did you know beer catches fire when lit? Well, with yaungol oil mixed in, it's downright explosive.

    I've mixed the oil in with these kegs. Roll them at the yaungol over by the gate, and ignite them when you get close.

    It'll take a few seconds for the keg to blow, but when it does...

    Well, let's just say I wouldn't want to be over there.

    Which is why I'll be over here. Mourning the loss of this brew.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will receive: {{cost|10|60}} |Lao Chin's Last Mug }} * 162000 [[XP]] * +75 [[reputation]] with [[Shado-Pan]]

    ==Progress== Wha? I was just thinking about my poor, poor beer...

    ==Completion== Whoo, that was an explosion! Several, in fact.

    Sad my beer had to go, but at least we're safe, for now.

    ==Notes== Run east a few yards and interact with the Keg Bomb. It starts rolling down the hill and has one ability: *{{abilities|Ignite Keg|Ignite the keg, creating a trail of flame that damages enemies over time. After 3 seconds, it will explode, dealing massive damage to nearby enemies.}} Trigger the keg a few seconds before hitting a huge group of yaungol and a catapult or two. Note that the direction of the keg is controllable by using mouselook or the arrow keys, so use that to get the best possible positioning.
