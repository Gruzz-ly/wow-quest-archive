    The Bear God's Offspring

    {{Questbox | name = The Bear God's Offspring | faction = Horde | level = {{level|Grizzly Hills}} | category = Grizzly Hills | start = [[Windseer Grayhorn]] | experience = 15,700 | reputation = [[Warsong Offensive]] +150 | previous = [[Vordrassil's Fall]] | next = | id = 12231 }}

    ==Objectives== [[Windseer Grayhorn]] in [[Conquest Hold]] wants you to speak to [[Orsonn]] at the [[Rage Fang Shrine|Ragefang Shrine]] and [[Kodian]] at the [[Heart's Blood Shrine]]. * [[Orsonn]]'s Story * [[Kodian]]'s Story

    ==Description== It is possible that the furbolg of Grizzly Hills have the same taint that plagued Vordrassil. They've been involved in a deadly tribal war recently after years of peaceful coexistence.

    The shrines that they guard to the southeast are home to Ursoc's offspring.

    If the corruption has affected the furbolg, it is possible that because of their lineage, Kodian and Orsonn have remained unharmed.

    If that's the case, find them and listen to what they have to say.

    ==Progress== Have you spoken to Ursoc's children yet, ?

    ==Completion== Ursoc's children are wise. Their words explain much of what we've seen so far.
