    Uncertainty (quest)

    {{Questbox | id = 77409 | name = Uncertainty | image = Uncertainty.jpg | category = Krokuun | level = {{Level|Holiday 30}} | type = Important Quest | start = [[Prophet Velen]] {{co|43.2|25.0|Vindicaar}} | end = [[Chieftain Hatuun]] {{co|53.74|68.12|Krokuun}} | experience = 1,200 | money = {{cost|2|80|90}} | previous = [[Prophecy Stirs]] | next = [[Opening Wounds]] }}

    ==Objectives== Speak to Chieftain Hatuun in [[Krokuun]]. Ask [[Grand Artificer Romuul|Romuul]] to teleport you to [[Krokuun]] (Optional) Speak to [[Chieftain Hatuun]] {{co|53.74|68.12|Krokuun}}

    ==Description== Scattered visions came to me as I meditated. Yellow and red. Shattering glass. A rush of warmth. I see my own hand, but I am afraid to grasp it. The scent of [[fel]]--of [[Argus]].

    When I awoke, a message had been delivered. Hatuun wishes to meet.

    After the [[Argus Campaign|defeat]] of the [[Burning Legion|Legion]], I offered to help Hatuun and [[Argussian Reach|his people]] rejoin us. However, they chose to remain on Argus.

    Hatuun has seldom reached out to us since then. Perhaps he has reconsidered.

    I will make the journey to Krokuun. Please, join me.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|2|80|90}} * 1,200 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== I did not expect Velen to come. He has surprised me.

    ==Completion== Let us see if Velen delivers the aid he once promised.

    There is nothing preventing him this time.
