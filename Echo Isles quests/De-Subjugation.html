    ==Objectives== Defeat 10 Kor'kron Overseers, 5 Kor'kron Subjugators, and Gul'tar the Subjugator. [[Kor'kron Overseer (Echo Isles)|Kor'kron Overseer]] slain (10) [[Kor'kron Subjugator]] slain (5) *[[Gul'tar the Subjugator]] slain

    ==Description== The [[Kor'kron]] have been trained to be the finest, most loyal soldiers of the [[Warchief]]. Their presence here means that [[Garrosh Hellscream|Garrosh]] fears an uprising.

    [[Vol'jin]] and his people are as much a part of the [[Horde]] as any [[orc]].

    We must break this occupation and free the [[troll]]s. It pains me to say this, but you must eliminate enough of the Kor'kron so that the trolls can retake their city. Then meet me at [[Darkspear Hold]] so we might challenge their officer directly.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|19|84|50}} 236000 [[XP]] +450 [[reputation]] with [[Dominance Offensive]] +500 reputation with [[Darkspear Trolls]]

    ==Progress== How did it come to this? It is as if the word "honor" is lost on these orcs.

    ==Completion== The orcs have changed. My Horde has changed. Do I kill my fellow orcs until it is once again "my" Horde? Do I have any right to try force the Horde to be what it once was?

    These are troubling times. But for now, I must see to Vol'jin's people.
