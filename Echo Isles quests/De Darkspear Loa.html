    De Darkspear Loa

    {{Questbox | id = 77906 | name = De Darkspear Loa | category = Heritage | faction = Horde | level = {{Level|Heritage}} | race = Troll | start = [[Rokhan]]{{Co|58.58|65.55|Echo Isles}} | end = [[Master Gadrin]]{{Co|58.58|65.55|Echo Isles}} | experience = 8,500 | rewards = 10x [[Flightstones]] | money = {{cost|28|9}} | previous = [[Avatar of Mueh'zala]] }}

    ==Objectives== Destroy Mueh'zala's altar and add Jani's. Destroy [[Mueh'zala]]'s [[Loa Offering|effigy]] Replace Mueh'zala's effigy with [[Jani]]'s

    ==Description== Dat was not a traditional trial, but we only keep tradition if it be helping [[Darkspear tribe|de tribe]].

    It be our strength: to adapt to what comes.

    It be time to shed de past and honor those most important to us. De Darkspear was never de largest [[tribe]], nor de strongest. We endure because we choose our path, and our allies, well.

    Dese [[loa]] and you all worked and sacrificed not just to help [[Bwonsamdi]], but to protect de souls of de Darkspear. Dat be why we honor our loa... and you.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 6,800 [[XP]] 15x [[Flightstones]] (max level) [[Ensemble: Heritage of the Darkspear]] *[[Loa's Blade-Blessing]]

    ==Progress== The effigies need to reflect our favored loa.

    ==Completion== De Darkspear don't just serve a loa because dat loa be strong. De Darkspear serve loa who serve de tribe. Dat be what [[Lukou]], [[Kevo ya Siti]], and [[Jani]] did. So we honor them now.

    De mask you made was not just for de ceremony. Take it, and de raiment of our tribe.

    Wear them and know you are Darkspear.
