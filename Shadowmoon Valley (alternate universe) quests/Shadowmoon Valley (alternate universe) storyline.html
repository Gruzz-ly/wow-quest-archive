    Shadowmoon Valley (alternate universe) storyline

    thumb|Zone map

    The Shadowmoon Valley storyline takes place after completing the Alliance portion of the Warlords of Draenor intro experience in Tanaan Jungle. The main storyline is tracked in the quest achievement As I Walk Through the Valley of the Shadow of Moon.

    There are also a large number of vignettes in Shadowmoon Valley, as in all zones of Draenor.

    Main storyline

    Establishing a Foothold

    thumb|Plant the banner to stake a claim thumb|Zone map after the garrison is established

    After landing, a base of operations is in order. But make sure to clear out the hostiles first!

    After completing "Delegating on Draenor", two quests open up: A Hero's Welcome (quest), which starts the "Shadows Awaken" chapter, and Migrant Workers, which starts the "The Pursuit of Justice" chapter. Additionally, a breadcrumb to Ashran opens up in Ashran Appearance.

    Shadows Awaken

    thumb|Ner'zhul at the observatory

    Yrel wants to go on a welcome back tour, but things quickly go south after the Shadowmoon clan start invading.

    After "Shadows Awaken", two quests open up: Into Twilight, which starts the "A Sister's Sacrifice" chapter, and The Southern Wilds, which starts the "Purifying the Gene Pool" chapter.

    A Sister's Sacrifice

    thumb|Yrel and Velen listening to Ner'zhul

    Ner'zhul has taken Samaara, so it's time to go get her back and figure out what Ner'zhul's grand plan is.

    After all of the step six quests, Velen starts the "The Light Prevails" chapter with Darkness Falls. In addition, the Shadowmoon Clan will launch an invasion (Shadowmoon Invasion! (intro)) on the Garrison. Unlike the Horde version, repelling the invasion is not necessary to finish the storyline.

    The Light Prevails

    thumb|Clearing a path to Karabor

    The Iron Horde has launched their attack on Karabor! It's time to stop Ner'zhul once and for all.

    This concludes the primary Shadowmoon Valley storyline. After completing "The Defense of Karabor", Yrel sends players on to the Gorgrond storyline in The Secrets of Gorgrond (Alliance). That breadcrumb is also available the instant players reach level 92 by speaking with Bodrick Grey at the garrison.

    Don't forget the side-storyline quests in the next sections. In addition, check your mailbox for a mail from Yrel containing the The Prophet's Arcanum, for The Prophet's Final Message.

    Gloomshade Grove

    thumb|King Deathbloom

    The podlings just outside Lunarfall in Gloomshade Grove have started attacking workers, so it's up to the commander to defend against the threat. A breadcrumb to Gloomshade Grove opens up after completing the "Shadows Awaken" chapter back at the garrison, but the chain can be started by finding the Lost Packmule in the Plains of Serenity, just south of the grove.

    The Pursuit of Justice

    thumb|There's a traitor here. Guess who?

    The garrison needs food, so some workers are sent out to Elodor, the breadbasket of Shadowmoon Valley, but things quickly spiral out of control. This side story opens up upon completion of Delegating on Draenor.

    Purifying the Gene Pool

    thumb|How'd they fit the caravan through the portal?

    A breadcrumb to the Drakkorium opens up after completing the "Shadows Awaken" chapter, but the chain can be started by heading there at any point. A traveler (and her caravan) from Eastern Plaguelands makes an appearance!

    Garrison buildings

    Level 2 upgrade

    At level 15, Baros Alexston offers a quest to upgrade the Garrison to level 2, and awards the commander all Level 1 blueprints with the exception of the Salvage Yard. *Bigger is Better (Alliance)

    Mine

    After reaching level 15 and garrison level 2, Timothy Leens wants the commander to clear out the mine: *Things Are Not Goren Our Way (Alliance)

    Fishing Hut

    After reaching level 20 and garrison level 2, Ron Ashton wants the commander to find someone who knows how to fish on Draenor:

    Looking For Help (Alliance)

    Moonshell Claws

    Proving Your Worth (Alliance)

    Anglin' In Our Garrison (Alliance)

    *Fish Fight

    Herb Garden

    After reaching level 30 and garrison level 2, Naron Bloomthistle wants the commander to clear out the herb garden: *Clearing the Garden (Alliance)

    Level 3 upgrade

    After reaching level 35 and garrison level 2, Baros Alexston offers a quest to upgrade the garrison to level 3. For a cost, of course. *My Very Own Castle

    Menagerie

    After reaching level 35 and garrison level 3, Lio the Lioness wants the commander's pets to deal with pests around the garrison. This is a Pet Battle System-related building. *Pets Versus Pests (Alliance)

    Side quests

    Bonus objectives

    There are four sets of bonus objectives in Shadowmoon Valley. Completing them all awards the achievement Shoot For the Moon.

    On the Shadow's Trail

    After establishing the garrison, a chain opens up at Starfall Outpost, just south of the garrison involving the Shadow Council.
