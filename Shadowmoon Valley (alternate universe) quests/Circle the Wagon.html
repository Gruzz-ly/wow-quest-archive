    ==Objectives== [[File:Shadowmoon Raider.jpg|thumb|[[Shadowmoon Raider]].]] [[File:Shadowmoon Voidspeaker.jpg|thumb|[[Shadowmoon Voidspeaker]].]] Find Maraad and the lost laborers and escort them back to Elodor Fields. Locate the Missing Wagon {{co|51.3|28.5|Shadowmoon Valley (alternate universe)}} Defend the Camp {{co|51.3|28.5|Shadowmoon Valley (alternate universe)}} *Escort the Laborers {{co|55.4|33.2|Shadowmoon Valley (alternate universe)}}

    ==Description== One of our wagons fell behind, and disappeared somewhere in the woods to the northwest. [[Vindicator Maraad]] and several of your workers are stranded out there.

    Find them and bring them back!

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|Item level 512 boots|Moonchain Boots|Moonsole Slippers|Lunarlight Plate Stompers|Lunartanned Boots}}

    You will also receive: 50x [[Garrison Resources]] and {{cost|11|80}} 13850 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Did you find them? Check the woods to the northwest. If I know Maraad, he probably tried to take the shelter of a hill.

    ==Completion== I am keeping the sickles. They remind me of a lesson so many of us draenei forget - that the Light lives in every farmer as much as in any priest.

    You fight well, and I am pleased to call you friend. Your laborers will be safe here in Elodor. If you are not convinced, speak to [[Exarch Hataaru (alternate universe)|Exarch Hataaru]] here. His devices secure all the draenei strongholds.

    ==Notes== The group is holed up at an area in [[Saraah's Vale]].
