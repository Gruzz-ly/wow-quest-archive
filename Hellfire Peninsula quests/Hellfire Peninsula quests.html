    Hellfire Peninsula quests

    [[File:WorldMap-Hellfire.jpg|thumb|Zone map]] Hellfire Peninsula is a level 58-60 zone in [[Outland]] and the first zone of ''[[World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade]]''. The progress for the storyline of this zone is tracked by achievement [[To Hellfire and Back]]. Breadcrumbs which lead players into Hellfire Peninsula come from the [[Blasted Lands]] or the notice boards in capital cities.

    Optional breadcrumbs: * [[Hero's Call: Outland!]] * [[Warchief's Command: Outland!]] * (From the [[Blasted Lands]]: [[Through the Dark Portal (quest)]])
