    Hellfire Peninsula quests

    thumb|Zone map Hellfire Peninsula is a level 58-60 zone in Outland and the first zone of ''World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade''. The progress for the storyline of this zone is tracked by achievement To Hellfire and Back. Breadcrumbs which lead players into Hellfire Peninsula come from the Blasted Lands or the notice boards in capital cities.

    Optional breadcrumbs: * Hero's Call: Outland! * Warchief's Command: Outland! * (From the Blasted Lands: Through the Dark Portal (quest))

    While most quests in Hellfire Peninsula are segregated by faction, these are not.

    Consists of three separate quest chains involong goblins. In the first two, the player helps the crew of a crashed zeppelin gather materials of repair, as well as gathering food for them.

    Ravager Egg Roundup

    Quests which take place in Hellfire Peninsula are denoted by a .
