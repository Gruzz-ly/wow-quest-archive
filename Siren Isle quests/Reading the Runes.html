    Reading the Runes

    {{Questbox | id = 85876 | name = Reading the Runes | category = Siren Isle | level = {{Level|Siren Isle}} | levelreq = {{Level|Siren Isle}} | start = [[Bargus]] | experience = 12,200 | money = {{cost|23|40}} | rewards = 100x [[Flame-Blessed Iron]] | previous = [[The Circlet Calls]] }}

    ==Objectives== Examine various Rune-Carved Tablets within the Forgotten Tomb. Re-enter the [[Forgotten Vault (Siren Isle)|Forgotten Tomb]] [[Fury of Thrayir]] examined [[Keys of Thrayir]] examined [[Cyrce and the Galeorn]] examined

    ==Description== My curiosity is still not terminated, . I would like to know more about these [[vrykul]] and why they came to this land.

    The tablets you found were by far the best source of information on the vrykul and whoever this "[[Cyrce]]" is.

    Perhaps there are additional tablets we missed?

    I have my ordained duties here, but please examine them and report back to me.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40}} * 12,200 [[XP]] *100 [[Flame-Blessed Iron]]

    ==Progress== Have you found the tablets yet? What did they say? Were they more relief carvings?

    ==Completion== Fascinating. This "galeorn" you spoke of known as [[Thrayir, Eyes of the Siren (NPC)|Thrayir]] may be worth further investigation.

    Perhaps we could uncover more about its significance along with those runekeys.

    It seems my curiosity will not be terminated any time soon.

    ==Notes== Just like in the intro quests, enter the cave at {{Co|46.37|19.83|Siren Isle}} and interact with the [[Singing Tablet]].

    While in the central chamber, pick up [[The Tattered Journal]].

    {{Book|Keys of Thrayir|content= As Cyrce called for retreat, she bid me to take Thrayir. Great was his fury and terrible were his injuries, but Cyrce need not fear. I would not fail her.

    Thrayir's wrath against our foes had unleashed a storm not seen since our days in the north.

    To have seen so many Sturmvyn fall must have pushed the Galeorn into such a fury that he had started to unravel. I needed to stabilize his wounds.

    While [[Jardfyr]] saw to the [[Forgotten Vault (Siren Isle)|Vaults]] defenses and carved new guardians, I turned to [[Ksvir the Forgotten|Ksvir the Thundered]]. Second only to the might of Cyrce, Ksvir wielded the blessing of Storms, a rumored gift bestowed by [[Thorim]] himself.

    Anguished yet bound by oath as Cyrce command to lead her retreat, Ksvir lent me his gift. To stabilize Thrayir's wounds, we forged five runekeys to harness the tempest that roiled above. Five keys of turbulent waters, torrential rains, thunderous lightning, cyclonic winds, and whirling waterspouts.

    Ksvir would keep one runekey, while the rest were scattered.

    Bound by five, we sealed Thrayir's tortured form in stasis. The fury of the Tempest would slowly heal his wounds and only by the wielder of the five keys, would his stasis break.

    May his wounds mend and may his cries of sorrow drift away.
