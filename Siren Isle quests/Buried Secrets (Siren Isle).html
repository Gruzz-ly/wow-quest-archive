    ==Objectives== Investigate the ruins and find the source of the signal. Examine the [[Singing Tablet]] Follow the Signal through the Vault. [[Glittering Ancient Tablet|Ancient Tablet]] Examined (Optional) Runic Symbol Investigated *Follow the signal into the Central Chamber.

    ==Description== These ruins are... quite old. Well, old by most outlanders' standards.

    Which begs the question: was finding this chamber the unfriendly outlanders' directive, or was it finding the tablet?

    One clue is that my readings suggest energy is flowing through the tablet as a conduit rather than originating from it.

    Shall we take a closer look?

    Perhaps investigation will yield some answers--and the source of the energy readings.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|23|40}}

    ==Completion== By the forge... this is...

    This is most certainly the source of the second signature, though the energies here appear to have fused with the original.

    It's almost like it wanted us to find this place...
