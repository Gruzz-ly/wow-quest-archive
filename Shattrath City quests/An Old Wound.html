    Objectives

    Speak to the soulpriests at Auchindoun. Speak to the Auchenai* soulpriests

    Description

    <Beruun whispers to you.>

    There are ways, I'm sure you know, for the draenei to speak to the dead.

    It's not the same as having a loved one back but...

    Hataaru's ashes were taken to Auchindoun after the soulpriests reclaimed the temple. Maybe they might know a way to help Ataanya with her loss?

    You will receive: 2g 34s 850 XP 10x Flightstones* (max level)

    Completion

    It is good to have visitors!

    The honored dead here are well-cared for, but not many travel to commune with them.

    Their deaths can be a painful reminder of what the draenei have endured.

    ;Upon accept : : : :

    Beruun has new gossip: {{Dialogue|I know Ataanya has been helping the Auchenai rebuild, so hopefully they are willing to help.

    {{Dialogue-child| Hataaru was the Grand Artificer before Romuul, which means he was the leader of all other artificers.

    He created the plans for Shattrath and oversaw its construction, as well as Karabor and all the other great temples.

    He was amazing! I have all of his lectures on my hologem-- well, all of the ones that survived, at least. }}}}

    Head to Auchindoun. Once there, the mausoleum has been reclaimed by the draenei. Auchenai Soulpriests can be seen, some of them in the process of imbuing Vacant Vigilants with souls, others walking the grounds alongside Bound Vigilants. Omenai Curates are working to rebuild the place and Auchenai Vindicators standing guard. Soultender Nemuraan, Amaan the Wise, and Boulestraan the Blinding Light are also there. D'ore can be seen floating in the sky above the Ring of Observance with souls circling it.

    Amaan has some gossip: {{Dialogue| Do you hear that? The music of D'ore.

    When I first heard its sweet melody return to Auchindoun, I wept. We have worked hard to restore our holy temples here, and these small moments remind me why it is worth it.

    The world is ephemeral, but that which is loved will never know true death. }}

    Speak to Nemuraan: {{Dialogue| I apologize, but we are in the midst of a soulbinding ritual.

    There is much to be done for the spirits in our care before we voyage to the Exodar to answer the Prophet's call to the Tishamaat.

    Is there something that requires our urgent attention?

    {{Dialogue-child| Not entirely. Some of us hid from the orcs here.

    After the invaders were driven from its sacred halls, we began the slow work of repairing the damage that the Shadow Council did to Auchindoun.

    {{Dialogue-child| Some.

    Those who died in Shattrath were cremated after the city was reclaimed. Slowly, we have been relocating their ashes to Auchindoun and giving their souls the rites they were denied.

    Eventually, the same will be done to those who yet languish in the "Path of Glory."

    {{Dialogue-child| When Auchindoun was destroyed, most souls housed here were torn to shreds. Many who survived succumbed to madness.
