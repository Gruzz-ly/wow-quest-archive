    City of Light

    {{Questbox | name = City of Light | level = {{level|Terokkar Forest}} | category = Shattrath City | start = [[Khadgar]] | experience = 5500 | previous = [[A'dal (quest)]] | id = 10211 }}

    Talk to [[Khadgar]], who summons one of his servants to take you on a guided tour of [[Shattrath City]], providing you with the background of the [[Aldor]] and [[Scryers]] factions, ultimately providing you with the quest that requires you to choose one of them.

    ==Objectives== Follow [[Khadgar's Servant|Khadgar's servant]] and listen to its story. Return to [[Khadgar]] after completing this task. * City of Light

    ==Description== Do not burden [[A'dal]] with petty inquisitions, . That just might be the most powerful being you ever come face to face with.

    You do not look familiar. I suppose you want to learn about the city?

    You'll have to excuse me if I don't give you a tour myself, but I'm a bit busy discussing strategy with [[A'dal]]. Perhaps you'll find a summoned servant acceptable in my stead? Excellent!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * 5500 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== The stories of men are ephemeral, . The stories of cities last well into the years.

    ==Completion== I hope you learned a thing or two about [[Shattrath]], . It might take some time for things to sink in.

    ==Notes== NOTE: This quest takes approximately 8.5 minutes to complete - [[Khadgar's Servant]] takes you on a guided tour of [[Shattrath City]].

    When you accept the quest, Khadgar's Servant appears next to Khadgar as your guardian. :{{Text|say|Archmage Khadgar|Show our guest around Shattrath, will you? Keep an eye out for pickpockets in the Lower City.}} :{{text|say|Khadgar's Servant|Follow me, stranger. This won't take long.}}

    He takes you out to the [[Terrace of Light]] through the northeast exit from A'dal's chamber. :{{text|say|[[Haggard War Veteran]]|[[Khadgar]] himself is showing you around? You must have made a good impression, .}} :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|Shattrath was once the [[draenei]] capital of this world. Its name means "dwelling of light."}}

    He goes down the northeast ramp into the [[Lower City]]. :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|When the [[Burning Legion]] turned the [[orc]]s against the [[draenei]], the fiercest battle was fought here. The draenei fought tooth and nail, but in the end the city fell.}} :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|The city was left in ruins and darkness... until the [[Sha'tar]] arrived.}} :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|Let us go into the [[Lower City]]. I will warn you that as one of the only safe havens in [[Outland]], Shattrath has attracted droves of refugees from all wars, current and past.}}

    He goes over to the building at {{co|63|15|Shattrath City}} and stops. :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|The [[Sha'tar]], or "born from light" are the [[naaru]] that came to Outland to fight the demons of the [[Burning Legion]].}}

    :{{text|say|[[Sha'nir]]|A newcomer to Shattrath! Make sure to drop by later.     Khadgar's Servant goes over to the north ramp up to the Terrace of Light. As you enter the ramp: :{{text|say|[[Iz'zard]]|Be mindful of what you say, this one's being shown around by Khadgar's pet.}} :{{text|say|[[Arcanist Adyria]]|Are you joking? I'm a Scryer, I always watch what I say. More enemies than allies in this city, it seems.}}

    He then heads back down into the Terrace of Light, and then up the next ramp onto the southern raised causeway, the one that leads to the [[Scryer's Tier]]. He stops at {{co|51|55|Shattrath City}} on the causeway. As he stops: :{{text|say|[[Scryer Arcanist]]|We're rather selective of who we befriend, . On the contrary, the [[naaru]] known as [[Xi'ri]] led a successful incursion into [[Shadowmoon Valley]] - [[Illidan]]'s doorstep.}} :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|There he continues to wage war on Illidan with the assistance of the Aldor and the Scryers. And seeking the favor of one group will cause the others' dislike.}} :{{text|whisper|Khadgar's Servant|Good luck stranger, and welcome to Shattrath City.}}

    When the tour is over, Khadgar's Servant returns you to the upper level of [[A'dal]]'s chamber, and disappears. Don't immediately jump down and talk to [[Khadgar]] - if you do it too soon, you won't be able to complete the quest and will have to do the whole tour over again by abandoning the quest and getting it again from [[Khadgar]]. Wait until you get the completion (less than 15 seconds) and then go talk to [[Khadgar]].

    In previous versions, you could skip following the Servant and simply wait on the northwest balcony for it to arrive and give you the quest completion. It is unknown what version this changed, but as of 7/31/09, this no longer works. Once the Servant has traveled outside your targeting range, it disappears; and you have to abandon the quest and start over.

    ===Additional notes=== Completing this quest gives you access to the quests that require you to decide which faction you want to align with - the [[Aldor]] or the Scryers. [[Allegiance to the Aldor]] [[Allegiance to the Scryers]]
