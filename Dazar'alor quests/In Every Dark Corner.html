    '''In Every Dark Corner''' is a [[Horde]]-only quest that takes place during the [[Faction Assault on Drustvar]]. It pops up automatically upon entering [[Drustvar]] when the assault is active.

    ==Objectives== Lead the [[Horde]] assault into [[Drustvar]]. * [[Alliance]] targets attacked in Drustvar (4) * Complete [[Rokhan]]'s ritual * Alliance army crushed * [[Vindicator Caeduum]] slain

    ==Description== The Alliance's hold on Drustvar is weak. We will conquer it for the Horde!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * 24,700 - 26,800 [[XP]] * +1000 [[reputation]] with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]] [[Azerite Armor Cache]] [[Honorbound Equipment Cache]] *5x [[Honorbound Service Medal]]

    ==Progress== What news do you bring from the front lines of Drustvar?

    ==Completion== Wherever the Alliance hides... we will find them.

    I'm glad Rokhan and the [[Darkspear tribe|Darkspear]] are on our side.

    ==Notes== ;On accept :{{Text|say|[[High Warlord Cromush]]|If the Alliance wants to hide among the monsters of [[Drustvar]], then we will hunt them both. Fight with honor, and fight with strength.}}

    The first objective requires completing [[Faction Assault on Drustvar#Key Horde actions|Drustvar Faction Assault world quests]].

    ;First objective complete :{{text|say|High Warlord Cromush|[[Rokhan]] will coordinate the next phase of our assault. Report to him immediately.}}

    Rokhan is located at [[Krazzlefrazz Outpost]] east of [[Corlain]]. Speak to him: {{dialogue| There be dark [[voodoo]] here, mon... Dis won't be easy.

Eligible mobs are: [[Arom's Stand Watchman|Arom's Stand Watchmen]] (1.5%), [[Purified Soldier]]s (1.5%), [[Purified Healer]]s (1.5%), [[Purified Protector]]s (1.5%), [[Arom's Stand Inquisitor]]s (3%), [[Purified Bulwark]]s (3%), [[Purified Crystal]]s (3%), [[Purified Battery|Purified Batteries]] (5%), [[Purified Warframe]]s (5%), and [[Purified World-Cleanser]]s (10%).
