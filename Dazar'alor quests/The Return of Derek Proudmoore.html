    ==Objectives== Observe Sylvanas's discussion with Princess Talanji. * Observe Lady [[Sylvanas Windrunner]] and Princess [[Talanji]] * Speak with Lady Sylvanas Windrunner at the [[Banshee's Wail]]

    ==Description== I'll admit, I did not think the [[Anduin Wrynn|Little Lion]] had it in him to kill King [[Rastakhan]]. Blood has been spilled, and vengeance must be had.

    Give me a moment to express my condolences, then come with me. We have more to do to win this war.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|2|34|}} *[[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]]) * 1,800 [[XP]] * +2000 reputation with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

    ==Completion== [[Derek Proudmoore]] will be twisted to become a weapon against [[Katherine Proudmoore|Katherine]] and [[Jaina Proudmoore]].

    The [[Alliance|Allliance]]{{sic}} brought an army to kill Rastakhan. All we'll need is one [[Forsaken]] to kill their leaders.

    I will contact you when your aid is further needed. In the meantime, continue the fight against the Alliance, we must survive until this plan is ready.
