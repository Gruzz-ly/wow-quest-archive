The quest '''Living Obsidium''' is a daily quest, part of the [[Firelands Invasion]]. It becomes available after completing [[Additional Armaments]].

==Objectives==
Retrieve 10 Obsidium Meteorites.
* [[Obsidium Meteorite]] (10)

==Description==
Have you seen those fireballs falling out of the sky? I saw one crash in the fields to the north, and the rock that landed was made of pure Living Obsidium!

I can use the material to craft ultra-sturdy heat proof armor and weapons for our troops. Can you bring me a lode?

Go to the [[Magma Springs]] and touch one of the [[Magnetic Stone|magnetic stones]]. Just watch where you're standing when the rain of fire hits, and gather up the rocks before they crumble from the impact.
