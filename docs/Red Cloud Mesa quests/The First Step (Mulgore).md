The great [[Cataclysm (event)|cataclysm]] finally drove them from their dens to spread like locusts across the land, and now you see the result; [[Greatmother Hawkwind|my own mother]], dead from their savagery.

Today, you are of age. Today, you will take up your weapon and fight for the tribe, and avenge the Greatmother. Follow the path to the east, and find my son, [[Grull Hawkwind|Grull]]. He will lead you in our strike against the quilboar.
