==Objectives==
Gather 10 Sharshells in the {{C|Hinterlands}} [[Nightmare Incursions]] and return to [[Field Captain Korlian]].
* [[Starshell]] (10)

==Description==
While these demons distract us here, the [[Emerald Dream]] is being corrupted into [[Emerald Nightmare|nightmare]]. Quick, travel through the Emerald Dream Portal in Seradane and collect Sharshells from the giant [[turtle]]s within the Nightmare Incursions. We can harness their magic to reverse the damage. You will need the assistance of a skinner.
