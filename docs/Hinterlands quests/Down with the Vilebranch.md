==Objectives==
Find [[Doran Steelwing]] at [[Jintha'Alor]] in [[The Hinterlands]].

==Description==
The [[Revantusk tribe|Revantusk]] isn't the only tribe of foul [[troll]]s we're here to take care of! The [[Vilebranch tribe|Vilebranch]] have corrupted this land for years from their stronghold of Jintha'Alor.

We're done sittin' by in [[Aerie Peak]] twiddlin' our thumbs, if you haven't noticed. Doran Steelwing leads the charge against the Vilebranch from a camp the boys took just to the south. Speak with him and join the fight!
