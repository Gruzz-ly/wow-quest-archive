==Objectives==
[[Lard]] at [[Revantusk Village]] in the [[Hinterlands]] wants you to find Lard's Lunch. Return to him when this task is complete.

Lard mentioned that he left it on the island to the north. Watch out for [[Vilebranch tribe|Vilebranch]] [[Forest troll|trolls]].
*[[Lard's Lunch]]
