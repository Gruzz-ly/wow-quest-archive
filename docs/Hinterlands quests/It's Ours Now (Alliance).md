==Objectives==
Kill 5 Vilebranch Blood Drinkers and 5 Vilebranch Soul Eaters in [[Jintha'Alor]].
*[[Vilebranch Soul Eater]] slain (5)
*[[Vilebranch Blood Drinker]] slain (5)

==Description==
Only the strongest and foulest [[Vilebranch tribe|Vilebranch]] are left now. The soul eaters and blood drinkers, foul worshippers of the evil [[god]] [[Hakkar the Soulflayer|Hakkar]].

Don't forget you're not just fighting [[troll]]s here, <name>. You're fighting dark and evil forces that'll churn this land into ash if we let them live!

Show no mercy!
