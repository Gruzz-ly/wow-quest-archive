==Objectives==
Use the Tiki Torch to set fire to 8 Tiki Masks at Jintha'Alor.
*Tiki Maskes Burned (8)
*[[Tiki Torch]] (provided)

==Description==
The [[Vilebranch tribe|Vilebranch]] have hung their tiki masks all across the walls of [[Jintha'Alor]]. We won't tolerate their foul hexes and dark magic.

It's time to send a message, and tear down their defenses, all at once! Take this torch, <class>. Set fire to their tiki masks and watch 'em burn. They'll know the [[Wildhammer dwarf|Wildhammer]] want them gone when they see their dark idols consumed!
