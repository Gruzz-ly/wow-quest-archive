'''Head Hunting: Horrors of the Forge''' becomes available after reaching Renown 11 with the [[Manaforge Vandals]].

==Objectives==
Defeat powerful Elite enemies within Manaforge Omega on any difficulty.
* [[The Bone Melter]] slain
* [[Darkmage Zadus]] slain
* [[Enforcer Jak'tull]] slain
* [[Watcher Gaz'Kreth]] slain

==Description==
By now no doubt you've seen some of the Manaforge's Elite coming to Salhadaar's aid. I'm sending you a list of names now - focus on these targets, and you may uncover spoils to aid your assault.
