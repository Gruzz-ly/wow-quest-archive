'''The Grift''' begins the fourth and final day of questing to acquire the [[Blue Barry]] mount. To begin, reach Renown 9 with the [[K'aresh Trust]] and find [[Ba'eth]] in [[Tazavesh]].

==Objectives==
Enter Untethered Space and recover the items [[Griftah]] requires.
* [[Untethered Space]] entered (Optional)
* [[Prosperity Pebble]] (10)
* [[Backfire Wish Bracelet]] (3)
* [[Expired Luck Potion]]
* [[Mismatched Shoes of Balance]]
* [[Coin of Endless Debt]]

==Description==
Dey say you want the blue beast. Was a great bargain I stumbled upon. Da bazaar full of opportunities. 

Many more bargains to find in spots where treasures lie waiting. 

You know where to look?

Go to dis special place and find treasures, I give the blue beast to you.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 12,200 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Did you find da treasures?

==Completion==
Good work, mon. Griftah be smiling today.

==Notes==
The nearest Phase Conduit to begin [[Phase Diving]] is marked on the map in [[Tazavesh]], near [[Constable Zo'ardaz]].

[[Prosperity Pebble]] objects are marked on the map all over [[Tazavesh]] and the rest of [[K'aresh]]. They also drop from [[Lurking Phaseburrower]]s. [[Backfire Wish Bracelet]]s and the [[Mismatched Shoes of Balance]] drop from elite [[Shadowed Fateshaper]]s near [[Tazarest]]. The [[Coin of Endless Debt]] drops from [[Untethered Rampager]]s on the outer Tazavesh island as well as the rest of K'aresh. The [[Expired Luck Potion]] drops from [[Unraveled Energy Broker]]s, found in [[North Sufaad]] and on the [[Shadow Point]] island outside [[Shan'dorah]] and at the Shadow Points to the west.
