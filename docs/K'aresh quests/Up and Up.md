'''Up and Up''' is a PvP [[world quest]] taking place at {{co|72|10|K'aresh}} on [[Vanquisher's Wake]] in northeastern [[K'aresh]]. Players are tasked with killing seven other players or ten ethereal mobs: [[Arcane Spirit]], [[Unchained Spirit]], and [[Void Spirit]].

==Objectives==
Kill [[Ethereal]] or Players (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Constable Zo'ardaz]]|Our most vicious are exiled to the untethered space in these ruins until only one remains. Accelerate the selection process.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 12,200 [[XP]]
* 500x [[Bloody Tokens]]
* 8x [[Resonance Crystals]]
* 2x [[Weathered Ethereal Crest]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[K'aresh Trust]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Constable Zo'ardaz|When K'aresh is ready, we will reclaim this land. Will it be a peaceful land? That is uncertain.}}
