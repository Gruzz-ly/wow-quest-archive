'''Win-Win Situation''' is the second day of questing to acquire the [[Blue Barry]] mount. To begin, reach Renown 9 with the [[K'aresh Trust]] and find [[Ba'eth]] in [[Tazavesh]].

==Objectives==
Sell 6 Shadowtrade Overstock sold.

==Description==
Not everything you recovered yesterday was mine, but let's be honest--we're going to keep it anyway!

[[Xy'daz]] has been ripping people off for years. It's his turn to take a loss!

Now, this can work in both our favors. You sell off half of the leftover goods for me, and I'll let you keep the rest!
