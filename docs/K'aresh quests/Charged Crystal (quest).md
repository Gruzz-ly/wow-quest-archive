==Objectives==
Meet with Myi Koo, enter the Archival Assault delve, and collect 6 [[Arcane Energy]] Samples and an Uncharged Crystal.

==Description==
An arcane charged crystal is not an item you will find in [[K'aresh]], but there is a way we can make one!

A small helper I know can help you harvest and store [[arcane]] energy. If you return that energy and an uncharged crystal to me, we can make a charged crystal.

Gain Myi Koo's assistance, then travel through the ethereal portal to the Azure Vaults. Collect the energy and crystal.
