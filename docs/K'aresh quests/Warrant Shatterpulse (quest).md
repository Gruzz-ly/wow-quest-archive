==Objectives==
Collect a Calculation Container, Marking Matrix and some Void Fused Resin to create a key.
*[[Calculation Container]]
*[[Marking Matrix]]
*[[Void Fused Resin]]

==Description==
The horrifying monstrosity known as [[Shatterpulse]] lies hidden in a [[Stasis Cell: Shatterpulse|stasis cell]] at [[Shadowpoint]]. It is not kept as a prisoner, but as a weapon to be unleashed upon us when we are least prepared.

Create a key to open Shatterpulse's stasis cell revealing this threat to everyone.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Shatterpulse Cell Key]]
*{{cost|23|40}}
*12,200 [[XP]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
You have created the Shatterpulse Cell Key.
