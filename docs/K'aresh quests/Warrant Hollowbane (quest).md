==Description==
[[Shadowguard]] forces in [[Shadow Point]] have rallied around an enigmatic champion know as [[Hollowbane]].

Though the Hollowbane's existence is unconfirmed, we have located a [[Hollowbane Portal|travel portal]] supposedly used by the champion.

Unlock the portal and expose Hollowbane so they may be confronted and eliminated.
