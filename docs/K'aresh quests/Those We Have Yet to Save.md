'''Those We Have Yet to Save''' concludes the optional "Chasing Echoes" chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. Find [[Om'dalan]] at the [[Ruins of Yaathron]] to begin.

==Objectives==
Recharge 6 Flickering Ephemera.
*[[Flickering Ephemera]] testedÂ (6)
*[[Nether Tuner]] (provided)

==Description==
The fauna here seem to be the same in shape as what would have been found nearby when [[K'aresh]] was still intact, but they are acting rather oddly for their species.

I wonder if we could give them one last push and fully bring them into being. Take the last of the energy. I have a device that should let you channel it into them.

==Notes and trivia==
Use the provided [[Nether Tuner]] on the [[Flickering Ephemera]] in the area. They will either turn into fleshbeasts and attack or shift between other wildlife and die!
