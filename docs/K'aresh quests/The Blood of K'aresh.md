==Description==
In the old days, we used certain deep-mined salts in cleansing processes and rituals. As you can see around you, mining is no longer valid, as these fragile rocks are hanging on to each other.

On the day of the [[Devouring]] of [[K'aresh]], a never-before-seen mineral surfaced. The [[Blood of K'aresh]] is a blue mineral with some deposits to the west of here. Even though K'aresh is dying, I should follow the old traditions to the end.

Please collect deposits from sand striders or veins and bring them to me.
