'''Stealing What is Ours''' begins the optional "Ba'eth's Request" chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. This chapter is unlocked after reaching Renown 9 with the [[K'aresh Trust]] and rewards the [[Blue Barry]] mount after four days of questing. Find [[Ba'eth]] in [[Tazavesh]] to begin.

==Description==
[[Ve'nari]] has called in a lot of favors to get the [[Oasis]] reclamation effort off the ground. Plenty of [[Tazavesh, the Veiled Market|Tazavesh]] merchants have donated to the cause, though some have seen fit to help themselves instead.

A large shipment of stasis-sealed items was stolen from the docks by an untrustworthy importer. I could use someone of your skill to recover the shipment.
