==Objectives==
Combine an Arcane Runed Sigil with a Charged Crystal and a Smuggler's Mark to create an Arcane Lure to reveal [[Arcana-Monger So'zer|So'zer]].
*[[Charged Crystal]]
*[[Smuggler's Mark]]
*[[Arcane Runed Sigil]]

==Description==
Arcana-Monger So'zer is responsible for a spree of thefts throughout [[Tazavesh]] that have resulted in the loss of magic relics, wands, and several crates of highly toxic [[Zo'kita]] fruit.

Informants suggest that So'zer may be compelled to reveal himself if presented with a highly prized [[arcane]] relic.

Create an item tempting enough to trick this criminal into revealing themselves.
