==Description==
I still believe void-infused glass is my ticket out of here.

The problem lies in the creation process itself. The motes you brought me can help stabilize the glass with the help of [[void]] energy. Lucky for us, there are numerous void surges on the edges of the glass crevasse.

I have charged a device with the motes. All you need to do is channel void energy through it! Stand where a surge is about to happen and then collect the glass that's formed. Easy! Oh, it might tingle a bit...
