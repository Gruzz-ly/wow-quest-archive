'''Overwhelm Them''' begins the optional "On a Technicality" chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]] alongside [[Mandatory Time Off]]. Find [[Associate Xy'nell]] outside the [[Xy Imports Offices]] in northeastern Tazavesh to begin. This chapter awards 500 reputation with the [[K'aresh Trust]] and Explorer 1 legs.

Both of these quests are revisited in the world quest [[Overwhelm Them with Mandatory Time Off]].

==Objectives==
Break 8 Xy Imports Crates on the docks in [[Tazavesh]].
