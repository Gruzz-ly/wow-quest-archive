==Description==
I had a necklace that our mother beaded. The [[Sufaadi]] took it when they robbed me. Things from old [[K'aresh]] are rare. If the Sufaadi haven't traded it, it'll be with their other valuables.

I was planning to leave it behind. Sentimentality isn't worth risking your life, but you kept your word--which is more than I can say for my brother. Help me recover the necklace and it's yours.
