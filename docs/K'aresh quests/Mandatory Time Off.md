'''Mandatory Time Off''' begins the optional "On a Technicality" chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]] alongside [[Overwhelm Them]]. Find [[Associate Xy'nell]] outside the [[Xy Imports Offices]] in northeastern [[Tazavesh]] to begin. This chapter awards 500 reputation with the [[K'aresh Trust]] and Explorer 1 legs.

Both of these quests are revisited in the world quest [[Overwhelm Them with Mandatory Time Off]].

==Objectives==
Place 7 Illusion Devices.
*Spy on [[Coordinator Xy'thir]]

==Description==
If my colleagues knew of my plan, they would be in danger of discharging their oaths as good employees. Besides that, they are far too excellent at their jobs.

Take these. They're devices of my own creation. When placed near my colleagues, it will create an illusion of them working.

They will be able to step away and rest. And the business will be under even more pressure without them.
