'''A Key Threat''' introduces players to '''Warrants''', which are unlocked at Renown 3 with the [[K'aresh Trust]]. Players also need to complete the [[Phase Diving]] questline before [[Constable Zo'ardaz]] in [[Tazavesh]] will offer this quest.

Each week, players will be given a different warrant to complete. The final quest awards +400 reputation with the Trust.

==Objectives==
Complete a warrant and eliminate the threat to [[Tazavesh, the Veiled Market|our city]].
* Use a warrant key and eliminate a warrant target

==Description==
Tazavesh is a city rife with turmoil, beset by countless dangers. We could always use your help. Check our [[warrant board]], and see if you have what it takes to eliminate our most wanted threats.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|70|20|}}
* 18,300 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Have you completed a warrant.

==Completion==
Fine work, be sure to check the warrant board for additional threats as they arise.

==Notes==
You have to pick up and complete a quest from the nearby Warrant Board.
