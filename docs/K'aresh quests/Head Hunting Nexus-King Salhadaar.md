'''Head Hunting: Nexus-King Salhadaar''' becomes available after reaching Renown 12 with [[Manaforge Vandals]].

==Objectives==
Defeat [[Nexus-King Salhadaar]] inside [[Manaforge Omega]] on any [[Instance difficulty|difficulty]].

==Description==
The hour of Salhadaar's demise is upon us. While he may posture upon his Voidwing, he grows desperate at the feet of the All-Devouring. The army he commands is not enough for your might - strike him down.

==Rewards==

==Progress==

Though not the end he deserved, we will celebrate his defeat nonetheless. See Zo'ropo to trade for a well-deserved reward. You have my eternal gratitude for deposing him.
