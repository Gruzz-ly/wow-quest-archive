* [[Harvested Energy|Energy Collected]] (4)

==Description==
My people need energy to live. We can harvest it from the pylons that were set out to collect it or hunt the energy scavengers that have siphoned off what is ours.

Let's gather today's meager bounty of energy and then distribute it to my people. If we are to go to war together, it will help us both to know the character of your allies.

You will receive:

* 12,200 [[XP]]

==Progress==
In the Wastes of Merciless K'aresh, survival is all important. We take what we need to ensure our future.

==Completion==
Your help does not go unnoticed. My people will live another day, and hopefully many more, thanks to the energy that's in those crates.
