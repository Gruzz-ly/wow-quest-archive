My people have fought across every inch of the ruins of our world. We gave our oath to [[Nexus-King Salhadaar|King Salhadaar]] and he rewarded us by sending the [[Shadowguard]] to hunt us down.

Salhadaar is long dead, but we knew the Shadowguard would return. In the long years that followed, we secreted caches of weapons so we would always be able to raise a blade in defiance.
