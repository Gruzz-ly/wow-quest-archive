'''Life for Life''' concludes the optional "A Stranger's Gift" chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. Find [[Om'taha]] just outside [[Naakroa]] to begin.

Stop the machine.

==Description==
We're too late! The ritual is self-sustaining. It'll draw in more power until it overloads the machine. [[Tazavesh, the Veiled Market|Tazavesh]], the [[eco-dome]]s--nothing will be safe if the failure cascades. 

If we remove the infusers, the energy should dissipate before it can cause any harm. It's too powerful though. It will tear you apart if you try to get close, and I'm not strong enough to do it alone...

Unbind me. I'll wrap you in my energy. That should be enough to let you get close to the machine.
