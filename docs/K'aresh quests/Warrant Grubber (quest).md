==Description==
A massive worm has been spotted lurking in the [[Scrapdrift Sands]] east of town. The locals have nicknamed it [[Grubber]].

Tragically, we lost deputy [[Zo'wie]] when he went out to investigate. The creature pulled him under the sand.

Devise a method to lure the worm into revealing itself, so it may be eliminated once and for all.
