'''Chipping the Void''' begins an optional chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. Find [[Driruj]] at the [[Oasis]] to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the [[K'aresh Trust]] and Explorer 1 chests.

==Objectives==
Collect 6 Void Glass Samples from the [[Clefts of Unreality]].
*[[Void Glass Sample]]Â (6)

==Description==
Greetings. I am [[Driruj]]. I think you could help with a project of mine. 

The nearby expanse of purple-hued crystal is ripe with potential. The void-infused glass could have many uses, from being a spell reagent to being melted down for crafts. I can't get near it myself, but someone like you will have no trouble at all!

Bring me back some samples of the [[void glass]] so I can assess their worth to future buyers.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|23|40|}}
*12,200 [[XP]]
*10x [[Valorstones]]

==Progress==
I am preparing to move off world before what's left of [[K'aresh]] crumbles away. There are many potential uses, and therefore potential markets, for this newly created void glass.

Potential equals profit, and profit means I can make a new life on a less volatile world.
