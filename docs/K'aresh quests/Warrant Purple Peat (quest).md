==Description==
In restoring the eco domes, much of the contaminated plant life was removed, allowing the soil to heal.

However, one corrupted lasher escaped our notice. Nicknamed "[[Purple Peat]]" by the dome technicians, it continues to spread its roots and seeds into [[Eco-Dome: Primus]].

The plant hides safely in an old stasis cell outside the dome. Construct a device to remove the protective cell and expose the plant so it can be eliminated.
