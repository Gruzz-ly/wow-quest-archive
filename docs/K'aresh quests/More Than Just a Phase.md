'''More Than Just a Phase''' is the weekly [[Phase Diving]] quest, offered by [[Shad'anis]] at [[Overlook Zo'Shuul]] after acquiring the [[Reshii Wraps]] in chapter three of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. Additionally, [[Constable Zo'ardaz]] in [[Tazavesh]] will also start the quest in subsequent weeks.

==Objectives==
Complete activities while phase diving in Untethered Space on [[K'aresh]].
* Complete Phase Diving activities (0/100%)

==Description==
Untethered Space represents an ongoing danger to our people on K'aresh. It is infested with banished souls, broken [[technology]], and monsters of all kinds.

Aid the [[K'aresh]] Trust and defeat our foes in Untethered Space. You may use the Phase Conduits to navigate between the remnants of K'aresh while phase diving.
