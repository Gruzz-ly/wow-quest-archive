'''Break the Assault''' is a bonus objective in [[Tazavesh]] available to players after turning in [[The Darkness Among Us]] and while on [[Barriers to Entry]], [[Sealing the Shadows]], and [[Heroes Among Shadow]] during the first chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]].

==Objectives==
*[[Shadowguard]] slain (30)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{Cost|23|40}}
*12,200 [[XP]]
*15 [[Valorstones]]

==Notes==
Kill [[Shadowguard Infiltrator]]s, [[Shadowguard Dreadnaught]]s, [[Shadowguard Darkweaver]]s, [[Shadowguard Voidripper]]s, [[Shadowguard Duskwalker]]s, and [[Shadowguard Voidtamer]]s across Tazavesh.
