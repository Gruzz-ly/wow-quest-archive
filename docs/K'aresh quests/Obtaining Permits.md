==Description==
There is a difference between securing a permit and securing permission to sell in Tazavesh.

Xy'vez is a licensed dealer. He can buy and sell as he pleases in accordance with the authorities. However, I am glad he knows his place. If you are on my streets, you need my permission.

Not everyone knows this. [[Cartel Ba]] likes to test the waters. I'll award your permission if you teach them a lesson.
