'''Ecological Succession''' is the weekly world quest available to players after unlocking [[:Ecological Succession]] by completing chapter four of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]] the week of 12 August 2025 and the weekly campaign chapter. It takes place at the [[Oasis]] in northeast [[K'aresh]].

==Objectives==
*Help the [[Oasis]] (0/100%)
*Complete repeatable quests (Optional)
*Return anima from a devourer assault (Optional)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Ve'nari]]|There you are--good. There are many tasks that have been waiting on your arrival. Please see to their completion.}}

*5x [[Carved Ethereal Crest]]
