'''Swap Meet''' begins after [[mining]] a piece of [[Inscrutable Ore]] from any [[Desolate Deposit]] on [[K'aresh]]. It acts as a [[breadcrumb]] directing miners to [[Om'myuz]], who trades other ''War Within'' ores in exchange for [[Desolate Talus]].

This quest can only be turned in after completing the first chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]], since Om'myuz is not initially present.
