'''Rout the Bilgefin Invaders''' starts upon entering [[Morheim]] in [[Stormheim]].

==Objectives==
*Rout the [[Bilgefin]] Invaders (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*14,800 [[XP]]
*200x [[Order Resources]]

==Notes==
To fill up the bar:
*kill [[Bilgefin Invader]]s (2%), [[Bilgefin Defender]]s (2%), [[Bilgefin Tideseer]]s (3%), [[Bilgefin Scavenger]]s (3%), [[Grrvrgull the Conqueror]] (10%)
*interact with [[Stolen Vrykul Armament]] (2%) and [[Dry Timber]] (5%)
