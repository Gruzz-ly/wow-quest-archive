'''A Call to Action''' introduces the [[Trial of Valor]] raid, which was first opened in [[patch 7.1.0]]. It is offered by [[Muninn]] at [[Krasus' Landing]].

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Havi.jpg|Havi}}
Find [[Havi]] at [[Valdisdall]].

==Description==

Steel yourself then come to me.
