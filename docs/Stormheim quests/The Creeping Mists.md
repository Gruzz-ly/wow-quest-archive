==Objectives==
*[[Helmouth Mistcaller]] slain (4)
*[[Cursebound Captain]] slain

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Ashildir]]|Once again the accursed mists creep in from the sea. Venture forth and vanquish the [[kvaldir]] before their darkness spreads further.}}

==Rewards==
*+75 reputation with [[Valarjar]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Ashildir|The [[Tideskorn]] earned their name the day the mists took [[Tideskorn Harbor|their greatest harbor]]. Since then, no [[vrykul]] loyal to [[Odyn]] has willingly entered that cursed place.}}

==Notes==
You will be provided with an ability, Call the Val'kyr, which will one-shot the mistcallers and seriously harm the captain, but is on a two minute cooldown.

Mistcallers are easily spotted, billowing mist around them. The captain is down on the east dock.
