'''Drive Back the Tideskorn''' is a bonus objective unlocks after accepting [[Stem the Tide]].

==Objectives==
*Drive Back the [[Tideskorn]] (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*44,400 - 49,350 [[XP]]
*200x [[Order Resources]]

==Notes==
To fill up the bar:
*Kill: [[Servant of Skovald|Servants of Skovald]], [[Runebound Wretch]]es, [[Felskorn Oathbinder]]s, [[Felskorn Zealot]]s, [[Felskorn Chosen]], [[Felskorn Cleaver]]s, [[Soulgazer]], [[Garzareth]], [[Ravathes]], [[Karuas]], and [[Muorg]].
*Interact with: [[Felblood Cup]]s, [[Nether Circle]]s, [[Tideskorn Banner]]s, and [[Vrykul Armament]]s.
