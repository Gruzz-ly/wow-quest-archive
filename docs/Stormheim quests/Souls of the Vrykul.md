* +75 reputation with [[Valarjar]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*[[Nethershard]]
*[[Blood of Sargeras]]
*An Artifact power item
*An equipment item

==Completion==
: {{text|say|Odyn|Well done, hero! You have saved many noble souls from becoming fuel for the Legion's foul magic.}}

==Notes==
Triggers at [[Storm's Reach]] in [[Stormheim]] during [[Legion Assaults]]. To fill up the bar, click on [[Soul Harvester]]s and kill demons that include [[Shadowdirge Soul Flayer]]s, [[Shadowdirge Champion]]s, [[Shadowdirge Soul Reaper]]s, [[Halvonoth|Overseer Halvonoth]], and [[Felclaw Fiend]]s.
