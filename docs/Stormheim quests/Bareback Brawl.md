==Objectives==
*Conquer Beasts or Slay Enemies

==Rewards==
* + 500 Honor
* Varied reward
* +75 reputation with [[Valarjar]]

==Notes==
*Location: [[Storm's Reach]].
*Killing a player grants 15% credit, while defeating (or 'killing') a beast gives 5%.
*Mount vehicles have special abilities:
**[[Storm's Reach Cliffwalker]]-''Ram''-Charge to the target, ramming them and knocking them backwards.
**[[Storm's Reach Warbear]]-''Bear Down''-Stand your ground. Your Warbear becomes an immovable force, immune to grip, knockback and fear effects for 5 sec.
**[[Storm's Reach Greatstag]]-''Storm Leap''-Bound forward to the target location knocking back enemy players.
**[[Storm's Reach Worg]]-''Pounce''-Leap to an enemy, rooting them in place for 2 sec.
**[[Stormtalon]]-In addition to flight in [[Storm's Reach]]-''Lightning Strike''-Lightning strikes the target area, dealing 649,954 Nature damage and knocking back enemies.
*Mounting Stormtalon grants [[Wake the Dragon]].
