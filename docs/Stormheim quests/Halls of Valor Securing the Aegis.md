==Objectives==
Defeat [[God-King Skovald]] in the [[Halls of Valor]] and claim the Aegis of Aggramar.
* Claim the [[Aegis of Aggramar]]
* [[Odyn (tactics)|Odyn]] defeated

==Description==
The [[Felskorn]] may be broken, but the head of the beast yet lives.  The [[God-King]] will still vie for the Aegis if left unchecked.

You, who have completed trials upon trials, must not let this come to pass.  You must claim the Aegis over Skovald's broken bones!

The [[Halls of Valor]] await!
