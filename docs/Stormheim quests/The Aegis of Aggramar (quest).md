==Objectives==
Secure the [[Aegis of Aggramar]] in the [[Chamber of the Guardian]] in [[Dalaran]].
*Central Dalaran teleport used

==Description==
Now that it's out of the hands of the [[Burning Legion|Legion]], the Aegis should be brought back to Dalaran for safekeeping while the hunt continues for the rest of the [[Pillars of Creation]].

It should be safe in the central portal chamber until the time comes when it is needed.

Your quest to drive back the Legion is one step closer to fruition.
