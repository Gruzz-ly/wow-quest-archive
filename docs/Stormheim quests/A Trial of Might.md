==Objectives==
Activate the archive device to learn about the Trial of Might.
* Learn about the Trial of Might
* [[Yotnar]] Defeated

==Description==
I owe you my gratitude for helping make me whole again, outsider. It is clear that you are worthy to take on the trials.

Go now into the vault. There you will find the archive for the first trial you must undertake.

I will meet you inside.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|15|40}} (at level 100)
* 15,050 [[XP]] (at level 100)

==Completion==
The trial is done, and you emerge victorious!

It is a great feat you have done here, but you are not through yet. Many great challenges await you on your path to the [[Aegis of Aggramar|Aegis]].
