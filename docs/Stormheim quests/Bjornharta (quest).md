==Objectives==
Collect 4 Engorged Bear Hearts from [[Voracious Bear]]s in the [[Runewood]].
* [[Engorged Bear Heart]] (4)

==Description==
The afterlife leaves the dead hungry for the delicacies of their former lives. For the [[vrykul]] kings, none such offering is prized more than [[Bjornharta]] - a celebratory feast of an engorged bear heart.

The bears of the Runewood eat well, and have long been prized for their tender meat. Hunt them and find a suitable heart of offer to the ancient rulers. They will not be able to resist such an offering.
