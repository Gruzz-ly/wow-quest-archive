==Objectives==
Find [[Vethir]] in the [[Felskorn Warcamp|Felskorn Camp]].

==Description==
The [[Halls of Valor]] is your destiny to claim, but it is already in the claiming.  The [[God-King]] befouls its steps with [[demons]] and wickedness.

Your dragon allies have come to Halls' defense, but they need your aid.  The [[vrykul]] have taken the leader of the storm dragons, the one you call Vethir.

Free your dragon friend, champion, and wash the [[Felskorn]] from the [[Gates of Valor]]!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|15|40}} (at level 100)
*15,050 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Valarjar]]
