==Description==

Deeper within [[Haustvald]], the mystics posess a special altar which reuinite the body and spirit of a fallen warrior. With this, we may be able to return Ashildir's spirit into her bones, and out of the grasp of these foul mystics.

Go outsider, I will rally her guard.
