==Description==
Until recently, [[Ly'leth]] was sending magical messages to us regularly from within the [[Nighthold]]. She took care to hide these messages - only the most vigilant scryers could have detected them.

They could not be within the palace... too much interference from the [[Nightwell]]...

Of course! The [[Terrace of Enlightenment]]! The [[Duskwatch]] must have set up a listening post there.

Check the scrying orbs for traces of Ly'leth's messages. We must learn what she was trying to tell us!
