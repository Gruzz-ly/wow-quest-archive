==Objectives==
Follow [[Valewalker Farodin]] into the [[Falanaar Tunnels]].
*Follow Valewalker Farodin
*Enter Falanaar Tunnels

==Description==
I have protected that seed for ten thousand years. You let it fall into the hands of the [[fal'dorei]].

I should have let you die.

Repay your debt. Help me get the seed back.

==Rewards==

==Completion==

==Notes==
On accept, Farodin runs out to the edge of the pit. Follow him.

:{{Text|say|Valewalker Farodin|Come! The fal'dorei make their home in the ruins below.}}
:{{Text|say|Valewalker Farodin|Down. Follow the water.}}

Farodin jumps into the water far below, at the bottom of the pit, and swims out. Turn in the quest to him.
