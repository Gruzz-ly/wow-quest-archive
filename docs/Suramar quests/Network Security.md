==Notes==
On accept:
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Go ahead, I am right behind you.}}

Oculeth has configured the teleporter to switch to [[Oculeth's Test Chamber]]. Simply activate it and step on the pad.

: ''Once inside, Oculeth walks to the stairs.''
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Here were are - my old sanctuary!}}
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Strange... the defenses are offline...}}

Make your way down to the next room, where Oculeth stops at the bottom of the stairs.
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Intruders! How did they get in here?!}}

Two [[Nightborne Warpcaster]]s and a [[Nightborne Enforcer]] are surrounding the first Arcane Coil. Kill them.

:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|This coil taps into the ancient leylines. Crank it up to full power!}}

Upon interacting with the coil:
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Good. If we overcharge enough coils, the entire system will failsafe into a lockdown.}}
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|There are more coils in the chamber below - and more intruders, I imagine.}}

Indeed, [[Warpcaster Thwen]] stands in the lower chamber, as well as more minions around the coils.

:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Thwen... I should have recognized your uniquely oafish meddling on my network.}}
:{{Text|say|Warpcaster Thwen|My, my. You look rather gaunt, honored teacher. Are you ''eating'' enough? Ahahaha!}}
:{{Text|say|Warpcaster Thwen|You should be proud! Your handiwork is going to help us reclaim every corner of our ancient lands.}}
: ''Thwen lifts off to hover high into the air.''
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|I can deal with Thwen. You overload the coils!}}
: ''Oculeth and Thwen teleport into the room, hovering above the floor.  Firing arcane bolts at each other, they instantly warp to new positions after several moments to attack again.''

Kill the mobs around the coils, and interact with them, just like with the first one.

Once you finish, the weakened Oculeth ends up kneeling on the floor.  Thwen hovers imperiously before him.
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Urgh!}}
:{{Text|say|Warpcaster Thwen|Face it, old man. I have surpassed you.}}
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Good trick... <cough> Warping the space around your body like that.}}
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|But what if that space is ''already'' unstable?}}
:''Before she can react, Oculeth throws up his arms and teleports himself, you and Thwen to [[The Drift]]. Thwen's shield fades.''
:{{Text|say|Warpcaster Thwen|My warp field is not working! Where have you taken us?!}}
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|<cough> Unstable... space...}}

Attack Thwen. Upon depleting her health:
:{{Text|say|Warpcaster Thwen|No! This place... it must bend to my will!}}
:{{Text|say|Warpcaster Thwen|An'ratha... an'tal-thandros!}}
: ''Thwen casts a spell which appears to gather energy to herself, and lifts into the air.  Arcane lighting emanates from her, but then the magic backlashes and she dies.''
:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|Thwen always took magic too lightly... She was a terrible student... <cough>}}

Talk to Oculeth.

: I am fading fast. Where is [[Thalyssra]]?
{{Gossip|''Thalyssra is near [[Meredil]]. Can you teleport us?''}}

:{{Text|say|Chief Telemancer Oculeth|I haven't much left... <cough> I will get us as close as I can... Come close.}}
:''Oculeth teleports himself and you near the entrance to [[Shal'Aran]] and collapses.''
