==Description==
Do you see the [[Nightborne]] outside this room?

They hold us here, like mice in a cage.  It is more convenient for them that we are pinned down while they search the [[Starweaver's Sanctum|Sanctum]] for my father's relics.

My companions and I can escape to higher ground with some help.

Take this crystal.  While standing on a [[mana]] focusing rune, use it to overcharge a Conjurer.

Their summons will turn on them and create an opening.

Use it well, and we will meet you atop the [[Moon Guard Stronghold|stronghold]] when the way is clear.
