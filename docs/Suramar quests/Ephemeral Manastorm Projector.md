==Description==
[[Oculeth]]'s theory is sound.  Reckless, but sound.

A small warp-field could trap and excite ambient mana in the conduit, thus creating a manastorm similar to when the [[arcan'dor]] fell out of balance.

I can build a device to generate the warp-field, but I need materials.

Specifically, warp-resistant alloys - such as the armor plating of high-end security constructs in the noble district.

Those constructs are deadly.  If anyone owes you a favor, now is the time to call it in.
