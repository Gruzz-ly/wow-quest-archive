This is a rare treat, I hope you know.  Not just anyone is allowed to stand on this hallowed ground.

<[[Sylverin]] adjusts his cuffs in a dignified fashion.>

To participate in the demonstration, simply take up an energy vessel and wade into the pool beside us.

Catch the delicate dewdrops of [[Nightwell]] energy in the bowl as they fall- this is the only way one can safely manage its essence.

Once your vessel is full, proceed to our demonstration cask and gently pour the amalgamation into it.

Enjoy!
