==Notes==
Sweet, sweet revenge!

On accept, you will get a five-minute "Detector" buff that shows an icon over your head (the same one shown over [[Suramar City]] guards that see through [[Masquerade]]) and a dark glow around infiltrators. When you get close to them, use the quest item to remove the disguise, which will cast a disguise-removing aura around you, again similar to the one used by the city guards.

Shouting "Something's not quite right!", "An illusion! What are you hiding?" or "Who goes there?" is optional.

If you lose the buff, you can ask Khadgar to reapply it, however you can simply just click on the NPCs and see if they have the Masquerade buff.

''{{gossip|I am having difficulty spotting the infiltrators, Khadgar. Can you help me detect them?}}''

Note that the quest gives credit for discovering the infiltrators, not killing them. You can simply let the Kirin Tor Peacekeepers take care of them once you remove their disguise.
