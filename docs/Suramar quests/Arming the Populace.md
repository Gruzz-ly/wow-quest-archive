==Notes==
Find the [[Nightborne Arms Cache]]s sitting around and loot them for [[Nightborne Armaments]]. Find Rebellious Citizens, usually gathered in groups of two and sometimes three, and use the armaments to set down a [[Crate of Weapons]] at their feet. After several moments they will focus on the closest Duskwatch mob and attack.
