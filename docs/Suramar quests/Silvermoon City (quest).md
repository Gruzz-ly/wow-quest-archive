==Objectives==
Meet with [[Lady Liadrin]] in [[Silvermoon City]].

==Description==
The [[nightborne]] have been isolated from the world for thousands of years. Now that they have reemerged, it is up to us to guide them along the right path.

[[Lor'themar Theron|Regent Lord Theron]] has extended an invitation to the nightborne to meet with us in Silvermoon.

Come, <name>, let us travel to Silvermoon with [[First Arcanist Thalyssra|Thalyssra]].
