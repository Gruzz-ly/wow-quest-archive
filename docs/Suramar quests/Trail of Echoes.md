==Objectives==
Use Khadgar's Wand to find Arcane Echoes and track down [[First Arcanist Thalyssra]]. 
* Faint Arcane Echo revealed
* Blinking Arcane Echo revealed
* Pulsing Arcane Echo revealed
* [[Erasmus Moonblade]] slain
* First Arcanist Thalyssra found
* [[Khadgar's Wand]] (provided)

==Description==
The nightborne could prove powerful allies against the [[Burning Legion|Legion]].  We must aid this Thalyssra if we can.

We should be able to find her by tracking her magical echoes, the same way I found her distress call.

I can teleport you near the location where she recorded that message.  From there, you must search for ripples in the very fabric of the [[arcane]] itself.

My wand should do the trick.  Follow the sparks it creates--they should lead you to places where Thalyssra used her magic.
