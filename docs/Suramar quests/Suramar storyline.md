# Suramar storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Suramar.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Suramar]]''' is the first {{g|legion}} max level zone on the [[Broken Isles]], with its questing experience starting with [[Khadgar's Discovery]] unlocked after reaching level {{level|legion-max}} as part of the greater [[Legion campaign]]. During [[Legion Remix]], the minimum level is 30.

==Good Suramaritan==
The progression of this part of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Good Suramaritan]].
===Nightfallen storyline===
This part of the [[Good Suramaritan]] achievement continues the Nightfallen storyline started in [[#Nightfallen But Not Forgotten|Nightfallen But Not Forgotten]]. It is linear, with every next chapter requiring the completion of the previous one, as well as a certain level of reputation with [[Nightfallen (faction)|The Nightfallen]].

Completing these chapters unlocks the [[#Insurrection|Insurrection]] storyline.
