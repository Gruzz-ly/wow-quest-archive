==Description==
I heard you were traveling to Black Rook Hold.

It was once home to the legendary [[Kur'talos Ravencrest|Lord Ravencrest]], a brilliant tactician. His knowledge may be exactly what we need to give us an edge against the Duskwatch.

While you are at the Rook, keep your eyes peeled for any books that might have survived the ages that outline the Ravencrest's strategems.
