==Notes==
The six crystals are around the broken column on the central platform of Shal'Aran. Upon approaching it, [[Keeper Selentia]] spawns. She is initially invulnerable, so don't bother attacking her right away. Instead, kill the two [[Arcane Tether]]s that are channeling energy beams into her.

Periodically she will summon [[Ancient Keeper]]s. You can mostly ignore them and go straight for Selentia.

:{{Text|bossemote|Selentia wails, calling forth ancient keepers!}}

Upon killing both Arcane Tethers:
:{{Text|bossemote|The tethers have broken. Selentia is vulnerable!}}

Now you can kill her normally, even as a melee class, although she appears to be flying high in the air.

Completing this quest awards the "Nightfall" criterion of [[Nightfallen But Not Forgotten]].
