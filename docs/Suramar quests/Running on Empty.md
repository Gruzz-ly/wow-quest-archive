==Description==

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+250 [[reputation]] with [[Nightfallen (faction)|The Nightfallen]]

==Completion==
*{{Alliance}} {{text|say|Tyrande Whisperwind|Morale has been reinvigorated by the arrival of fresh supplies. You have done well!}}
*{{Horde}} {{text|say|Lady Liadrin|These supplies will prove crucial in maintaining our campaign. The Horde marches on!}}

==Notes==
The chests and casks can be found all over the place. Be careful of the [[Vineyard Warden]]s and [[Tattered Silkwing]]s. If you can fly, you may want to consider flying near the entrance area and looting from there instead.
