==Objectives==
Speak to [[Lord Hewell]] to obtain [[Stout Mountain Horse|a ride]] to the [[Livery Outpost]] and report to [[Lorna Crowley]].

==Description==
I thank you, <name>. We can't rest on our laurels, however.

We found out about a mining town, currently under [[Forsaken]] occupation. We sent some of my men, along with [[Darius Crowley|Crowley's]] daughter to the nearby livery stables to begin operations there. Liam should be joining them shortly.

I'll stay here and organize the final push for [[Gilneas City]]. Speak to [[Lord Hewell]] to secure a horse to get you there.
