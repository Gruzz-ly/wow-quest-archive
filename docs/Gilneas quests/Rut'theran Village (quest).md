==Completion==
Are you ready to set sail, <name>? Your people have been granted shelter in the lands of the kaldorei.

Do not worry, worgen. Your people will get a chance to fight for [[Gilneas peninsula|Gilneas]] again. This time, with the full strength of the [[Alliance]].

== Rewards ==
You will receive: {{cost|||50}}

==Notes==
Nightwind offers this quest on deck of the night elf ship in [[Keel Harbor]] upon the completion of [[Endgame (quest)]].

Upon completion, players wind up on the docks of [[Rut'theran Village]] of [[Teldrassil]] on [[Kalimdor]]. Run forward to find {{NPC||Krennan Aranas}}, who offers the next quest: [[The Howling Oak]].
