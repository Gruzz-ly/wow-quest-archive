==Objectives==
Speak to {{NPC||Lorna Crowley}} at the [[Crowley Orchard]].

==Description==
Of course you remember {{NPC||Darius Crowley||Lord Darius Crowley's}} daughter, Lorna.

She never quite recovered after losing her father in [[Gilneas City]]. She's basically led the life of a hermit. The only visitor she's allowed into her house has been {{NPC||Krennan Aranas||Krennan}}... and he won't say a word about the whole thing.

Send word to her about the evacuation.
