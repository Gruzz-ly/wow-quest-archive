In '''Something's Amiss''' you just discovered the dead body of {{NPC||Lieutenant Walden}} and decide to return to {{NPC||Liam Greymane||Prince Liam Greymane}} in order to tell him and receive further instructions.

==Objectives==
Return to {{NPC||Liam Greymane||Prince Liam Greymane}} in the [[Merchant Square]].

==Description==
The lieutenant appears to have died from severe wounds. The prince might want to hear this news.

You hear the sound of fighting coming from the market.

== Rewards ==

==Completion==
It's you again. Looks like you're not one to hide in the face of danger. Very well, then.

== Notes ==
The prince is mounted and running around, helping his soldiers kill the [[Rampaging Worgen]]. He will not be in the same spot as before, but he should still be on the square.
