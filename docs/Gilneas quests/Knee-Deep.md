==Objectives==
Use the Half-Burnt Torch to get to the other side of the [[Undertaker's Pass|tunnel]] beneath [[Gilneas City]].
*[[Half-Burnt Torch]] (provided)

==Notes==
Hop down the tunnel. The tunnel is full of [[Graveyard Rat]]s.  Pet classes should put their pets on passive or dismiss them. Put the half-burnt torch on an action bar and start swinging. It works best to swing before any rats get too close to keep damage taken to a minimum. Don't stop running!
