==Objectives==
Bring 6 Moonleafs to {{NPC||Vassandra Stormclaw||Vassandra}}.
*[[Moonleaf]] x6

==Description==
What you know as [[Worgen curse|the Curse]] began as a [[Heretic|heresy]] among our brethren.

Faced with a [[War of the Satyr|losing battle]] against [[Lords of the Emerald Flame|demonic forces]], the [[Druids of the Pack]] eschewed the balance they were sworn to and allowed the beast to overtake them.

They were banished to the [[Emerald Dream]] where they live in eternal slumber under a tree identical to this one, but not before their curse spread to others.

Today our ceremony will restore balance to your people. Bring me the leaf that grows only in [[Tal'doren]]'s vicinity.
