==Objectives==
Speak to {{NPC||Sebastian Hayward}} at the [[Hayward Fishery]].

==Description==
The Hayward Fishery can be found far to the south. The [[Hayward]] brothers have been running it for years.

They don't make it to town very often, except to trade their fish. Send word to them that the evacuation is underway.

== Rewards ==
You will receive: {{cost|||85}}

==Completion==

== Notes ==
Head south out of town, past the orchard and the Wahl house. The Hayward brothers (all five of them) are being attacked by [[Forsaken Castaway]]s.
