==Objectives==
Find and slay 9 Crimson Cultists in the [[Terrace of the Chosen]].
* [[Crimson Cultist]] slain (9)

==Description==
You come at a good time, <name>. I have need of a weapon such as yourself.

[[Yazma]]'s [[spy|spies]] say dere are blood cultists in our ranks. King [[Rastakhan]] has charged me with tracking down any [[Zandalari troll|trolls]] guilty of practicing [[blood magic]] and purging dem.

Take dis totem, route out those with de stench of blood magic, and cleanse dem from dis holy place.

When you are done, tell dat lazy [[Hexlord Raal]] to get off his perch and help keep dis place secure.
