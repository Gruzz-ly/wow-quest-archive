==Objectives==
Meet [[King Rastakhan]] at the [[Zocalo]].

==Description==
[[Zul]] is pulling all of his forces back to his stronghold at the [[Temple of the Prophet]].

Rastakhan has sent word that he is marshalling an army in the [[Zocalo]] with anyone still loyal that can carry a weapon. Looks like the old king had more loyalists than Zul expected.

That, and he has three of their eldest [[loa]] gathered to his side.

Go and lend your strength to their army.
