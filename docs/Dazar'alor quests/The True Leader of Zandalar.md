==Objectives==
Present the Glaive of [[Vol'jin]] with Talanji to the people of [[Zandalar]].
* Give [[The Bloodied Glaive of Vol'jin|Vol'jin's Glaive]] to [[Talanji]]

==Description==
Now, take me glaive and walk with Talanji. Let her present your victory to her people. Let da [[troll]]s know justice, and the strength of a leader.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 17,850 [[XP]]
* +250 reputation with [[Darkspear Trolls]]
* +250 reputation with [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]]

==Completion==
Talanji is da true ruler of Zandalar, even if she be doubting it. With guidance, she will eclipse [[Rastakhan|her father]]'s reign.

For now, ya have me thanks.
