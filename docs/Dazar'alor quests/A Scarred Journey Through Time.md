'''A Scarred Journey Through Time''' is the non-max-level [[Timewalking]] quest for {{g|bfa}}. Find [[Churbro]] at {{co|70.8|17.3|Boralus}} above the [[Tradewinds Market]] in [[Boralus]] or {{co|46.1|94.9|Dazar'alor}} in the [[Port of Zandalar]] of [[Dazar'alor]] to begin.

==Objectives==
Defeat 5 Timewalking dungeons.
* [[Timewalking]] [[dungeon]]s defeated (5)

==Description==
Azeroth calls out in need, champion. She bleeds still from wounds of war. Go forth among Zandalar and Kul Tiras to reveal those who would prolong her suffering.

Vanquish foes in her name and defend Azeroth.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Timely Goodie Bag]]
