==Objectives==
Subdue 12 rioters in the [[Zocalo]].
* Rioters subdued (12)

==Description==
One of my own in de [[Rastari]] has seen fit to rile de drudges and [[guard]]s in de [[Zocalo]].

They threaten de stability of [[Zandalari Empire|our empire]] with this foolishness. If they are angry, we can address it. But this wanton destruction will not stand.

Go down into de market of de Zocalo and bring about calm however you can. Choose de method that works for you.

I care only for de result: Peace, so Talanji can finally be crowned.

==Rewards==

* 17,850 [[XP]]

==Progress==
My people must not be made to suffer, though I cannot allow them to riot in de streets.

==Completion==
I hope we are not too late to stop this unrest from growing.
