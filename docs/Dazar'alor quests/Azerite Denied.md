==Objectives==

==Description==
The [[Kul Tiras (kingdom)|Kul Tirans]] and [[Alliance]] are amassing a vast amount of their [[:Azerite]] and Azerite weapons within Tiragarde Sound.

They have a new supply of the resource, thanks to the Kul Tirans' conquest over [[Priscilla Ashvane|Lady Ashvane]] and her [[Ashvane Company|company]].

We must strike at their supplies of Azerite and destroy these weapons, or else we will be behind in [[Fourth War|this war]].

You and [[Thomas Zelling|Zelling]] will travel to Tiragarde Sound and meet with Rexxar. Lead the assault on their Azerite supply.

And do not return until you are victorious.
