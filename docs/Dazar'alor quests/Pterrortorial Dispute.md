De courtyard of [[Zanchul]] has been overrun by [[pterrordax]]es driven mad by some dark ritual. Power is coalescing here, and it is hard to tell who or what is causing it.

I need to track down de source. In de meantime, you take dis, de power of [[Gonk]], and call his allies to help you in battle. Slay dese maddened pterrordaxes.

When you are done, tell Raal to get his flock under control.
