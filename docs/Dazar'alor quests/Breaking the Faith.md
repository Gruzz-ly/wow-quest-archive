==Objectives==
Subdue 12 angered [[priest]]s, [[druid]]s, or [[prelate]]s in the [[Terrace of the Chosen]].
* Angered faithful subdued (12)

==Description==
De faithful are among de most revered in [[Zandalari troll|Zandalari]] society. They are an example for others to aspire to. This is not de example we want others to follow.

We must respond in kind.

Princess [[Talanji]] wishes for us to spare them that will see reason. Do so, if you can find any willing to surrender.

For de rest, give them a swift death.
