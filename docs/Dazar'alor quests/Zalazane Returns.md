'''Zalazane Returns''' begins the [[Spirits Be With You (achievement)]] storyline for [[Horde]] characters. It is available upon {{rep|revered}} with [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]] and having earned [[Zandalar Forever! (achievement)]].

==Objectives==
Reach [[Master Gadrin]] in the [[Echo Isles]].

==Description==
<Name>, I have never needed your help as much as I do this day. My home be under attack.

I only just heard myself. Master Gadrin be callin' for aid. [[Zalazane]], an old enemy of da [[Darkspear tribe|Darkspear]], has returned to da Echo Isles and has brought with him an army of da [[Undead|dead]].

Zalazane be a traitor who has slain many Darkspear and defiled their [[soul]]s. Will you come with me to defeat him? We can take da ship dat departs da [[Port of Zandalar]].

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|34|}}
* +10 reputation with [[Zandalari Empire]]
* +10 reputation with [[Darkspear Trolls]]

==Completion==
You... you brought da [[Zandalari troll|Zandalari]] with you?

==Notes==
On acceptance:
:{{text|say|Rokhan|I gotta borrow da champion for a bit. An old enemy somehow escaped [[Bwonsamdi]] and now be threatenin' my home.}}
:{{text|say|[[Princess Talanji]]|My dear Rokhan, de Darkspear tribe has more than earned de aid of de Zandalari. I will join you to help as I can.}}

The [[Echo Isles]] are phased to be under attack by [[Mindless Troll]]s and [[Mindless Brute]]s led by Zalazane. The troll trainers are in defense mode while [[Darkspear Tribesman|Darkspear Tribesmen]] and [[Darkspear Shaman]] fight against the Mindless Trolls. Talanji, Rokhan, Gadrin, [[Zen'tabra]], [[Bwemba]], [[Shadow Hunter Rala]], and [[Shadow Hunter Mala]] are gathered next to the [[Urn of Vol'jin]] on the eastern part of the [[Darkspear Training Grounds]]. Zalazane yells the following:

*{{Text|yell|Zalazane|Where? Where ya be hidin' Vol'jin? How ya be hidin' him?}}
*{{Text|yell|Zalazane|Search, minions. Find da ashes of Vol'jin and bring dem to me.}}
*{{Text|yell|Zalazane|Where ya be hidin' him, Darkspear? Where be my old friend?}}
*{{Text|yell|Zalazane|Crush da Darkspear and find me those ashes.}}
