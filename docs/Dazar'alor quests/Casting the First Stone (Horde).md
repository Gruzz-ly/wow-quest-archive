==Objectives==
Craft a Potion of Durability and a Potion of Shifting States using the recipes given to you. You can find the ingredients you need in [[Clever Kumali|Kumali]]'s shop.
* [[Potion of Durability]]
* [[Potion of Shifting States]]

==Description==
Hey dere <name>, gimme a hand with dis, would ya?

<Kumali motions you over to her.>

I got dis recipe dat I've been trying to remember de rest of, but I'm having de hardest time coming up with de right ingredients.

Ya see, [[Zukashi|my partner]] ran off with de other half of de recipe on account of a disagreement we had. But dis stone, it's a treasure like no other.

Grab some vials and start mixing dese ingredients together. Let's see if dey are de right ones.
