==Objectives==
Witness Talanji's ascension to Queen of the [[Zandalari troll|Zandalari]].
* Witness [[Talanji]]'s address

==Description==
De [[loa]] have seen fit to grant me de crown. My [[Zanchuli Council|council]] accepts me. My [[priest]]s have given their blessing. My people have expressed their displeasure, but I am determined to right those wrongs.

It is time to accept de responsibility of leadership. I will address them and speak on our future. I would be honored if you would take a place at my side while I do so.
