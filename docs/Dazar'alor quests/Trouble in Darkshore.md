Speak with Dread-Admiral [[Tattersail]] to travel to [[Darkshore]] and report to [[Nathanos Blightcaller|Nathanos]].
* Speak with Dread-Admiral Tattersail to Travel to Darkshore

==Description==
We have received some disturbing reports from Darkshore.

What was once a secure stronghold for the [[Horde]] is now beset by an unnatural darkness. The moon has turned completely black.

The outposts there have yet to be reinforced with our main force and we are suddenly outnumbered. Nathanos wants you to meet him in Darkshore and find out what happened.
