==Objectives==
Deliver the Words of [[Zul]] to various individuals in the [[Port of Zandalar]].
* Show [[Nokali the Scarred|Nokali]] the message
* Show [[Doomsayer Volkini|Volkini]] the message
* Show [[Dockmaster Cobo]] the message
* [[The Word of Zul]] (provided)

==Description==
I have a mundane task for you. Our esteemed Prophet Zul has words to distribute to very specific individuals in de port. 

And he requested you be de one to deliver dem.

Don't ask me why he didn't just have you deliver these when you were here de first time. De prophet is notorious for not sharing all of his visions.
