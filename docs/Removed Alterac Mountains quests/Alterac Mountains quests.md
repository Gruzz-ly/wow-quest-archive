This is a list of all '''[[Alterac Mountains (Classic)|Alterac Mountains]] quests''' in table format (arranged by level).

==Quest tables==
Quests that take place outside Alterac Mountains are denoted by a {{Outzone}}. Due to the fact that the vast majority of Alterac Mountains quests are started or finished in [[Hillsbrad Foothills (Classic)|Hillsbrad Foothills]], said zone will not be denoted.

Quests that are part of a quest chain are denoted by {{Chain}}

Quests marked with {{Sod-inline}} are exclusive to ''[[Season of Discovery]]''.
