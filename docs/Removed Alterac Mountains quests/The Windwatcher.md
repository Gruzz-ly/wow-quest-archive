'''The Windwatcher''' is the 1st quest in the '''The Windwatcher''' quest chain.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Bath'rah the Windwatcher]].

==Description==
Now that you've learned your berserker's stance, you'll want a nice weapon to use it with.

Talk to Bath'rah the Windwatcher, a [[Jungle troll|troll]] hermit who lives between the [[Alterac Mountains]] and the [[Western Plaguelands]].  He is a skilled weapon maker and a powerful [[shaman]].
