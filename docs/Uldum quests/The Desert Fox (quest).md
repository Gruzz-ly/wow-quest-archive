== Notes ==
Pick up [[A Favor for the Furrier]] before heading out.

Schnottz's gossip text offering this quest:
:So, zhis is the <race> <class> I've heard so much about, hmm?
:As you know, zhere is vone obelisk zhat has yet to be activated...
:Ven zhe time comes to restore zhis ancient mechanism, vhill you be zhere? Vill history remember you?
:Zhat depends on your villingness to cooperate!

Fly east past the [[Ruins of Ammon]] to the [[Cradle of the Ancients]]. The {{NPC||Desert Fox}} is a friendly level 83 fox idly running around. Fly up to it and catch it. Also kill a [[Diseased Vulture]] to start [[Dirty Birds]].

The item created by clicking on the mob is [[The Desert Fox]].  The actual mob does not have "the" in its name.  If you're having trouble finding him, try using "/target desert fox".
