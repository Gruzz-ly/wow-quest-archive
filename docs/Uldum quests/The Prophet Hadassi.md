== Objectives ==
Find the {{NPC||Prophet Hadassi}} in [[Orsis]].

== Description ==
Prophet Hadassi is the wisest and eldest member of the [[Orsis tribe]].

He has not been accounted for and his well-being is of vital importance.

Go into Orsis and find him. A great day of reckoning will come to {{NPC||Al'Akir}}'s lackeys if he's been hurt.

== Rewards ==
You will receive: {{cost|4|30}}

== Completion ==
Who... who are you? Sent here... to rescue my people and fight our enemies.

== Notes ==
Pick up [[Minions of Al'Akir]] and [[Under the Choking Sands]] before heading out. Hadassi can be found to the southwest, between the two pyramids at Orsis.
