'''Enter the Void''' is only available in [[War Mode]] [[Uldum]] while the Black Empire is assaulting the region.

==Objectives==
Without dying or leaving Void Wraith form, sacrifice 10 players to N'Zoth
* [[Player character|Players]] slain (10)

==Description==
<As your approach the idol, the dark shards you carry appear to melt into your hand. You are immediately overcome with unspeakable anguish. It quickly gives way to feelings of joy, serenity, and an overwhelming desire to... kill. 

A hissing voice creeps into your mind.> 

My faithful servant. I will grant you but a small taste of my unimaginable power, all I ask in return is that you complete one simple task. Kill any who would oppose me, spare no one. Do not fail me....
