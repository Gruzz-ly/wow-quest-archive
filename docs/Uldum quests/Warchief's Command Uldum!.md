Speak with [[Adarrah]] in [[Thistleshrub Valley]] in western [[Tanaris]].

All able-bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to Adarrah at the Thistleshrub Valley in Tanaris.

Ancient texts tell us that [[Uldum]] is home to untold riches and treasures beyond compare. Go forth and claim these in the name of the Horde!

You can reach Adarrah's caravan via a portal at The [[Western Earthshrine]] in [[Orgrimmar]], just north of the [[Valley of Wisdom]].
