==Objectives==
You must kill 5 Amethyst Scarabs, 5 Sapphire Scarabs, 5 Turquoise Scarabs, and 5 Emerald Scarabs.
*[[Amethyst Scarab]]s Slain x5
*[[Sapphire Scarab]]s Slain x5
*[[Turquoise Scarab]]s Slain x5
*[[Emerald Scarab]]s Slain x5

==Description==
Evidently, the ancient statues were home to various species of [[scarab]].

Determined to leave this site in the best condition possible, you resolve to ridding [[Chamber of the Stars|the chamber]] of these pests.

==Completion==
As you obliterated the final scarab, you noticed an ancient piece of ornamental jewelry on the ground...

==Notes==
After Harrison knocks the four statues down in [[Tipping the Balance]], kill a scarab to automatically begin this quest.

Kill the 20th scarab to complete the quest automatically.
