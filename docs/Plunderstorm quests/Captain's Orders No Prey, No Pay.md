'''Captain's Orders: No Prey, No Pay''' is one of several Captain's Orders quests that can be received at the beginning of a [[Plunderstorm]] match.

== Objectives ==
*[[Elite]] creatures slain (2)

== Rewards ==
{{itembox|You will receive:
|Plunder|q1=250
}}

==Notes==
*Nearby elite mobs are marked on the minimap with a star icon. They're most often found at points of interest such as towns, outposts, farms, and elemental circles.
*[[Pieces of Hate]] also count for this quest. When they spawn, they're marked on the zone map and minimap with a skull icon.
