'''Learn the Ropes or Walk the Plank''' teaches new plunderers the basics of [[Plunderstorm]], and is available after queueing for the game and entering the pre-game lobby, in [[Brew Bay]].

== Objectives ==
*Swing your sword
*Drink your Health Brew
*Check Renown at [[Da'kash Grimledger]]
*Open a chest
*Pick up [[Plunderstorm#Abilities|a spell]]
*Try reviving a dead pirate

== Rewards ==
You will receive:
*+1,000 [[reputation]] with [[Keg Leg's Crew]]

== Notes ==
*The default binding to swing your sword is {{keypress|R}}
*The default binding to Drink your Health Brew is {{keypress|T}}
*Chests can be interacted with either by right-clicking them, or by using the "Interact" key, which is {{keypress|F}} by default.
*Dead pirates having glowing rings around them on the ground. By standing atop them, you will start [[channeling]] the revive. This mechanic is used to bring slain teammates back to life in the Duos and Trios modes, but solo players can get credit by standing in the glowing ring around one of the deceased [[Clumsy Swabbie]]s in [[Brew Bay]].
