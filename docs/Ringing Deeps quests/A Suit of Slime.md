==Objectives==
Collect 20 Sticky Wax from [[Waxy Runoff]]s and [[Wax-addled Hopper]]s.
* [[Sticky Wax]] (20)
* Speak to Scrit
* Disguise chosen

==Description==
[[Glumtooth Gang]] no let you into camp looking like that. Need disguise. Make you look like bug, then we walk right in.

First, need wax. Plenty here. Take from slime beasts or scrape off hoppers.

Then we stick wax on you. Stick bug bits to wax. Perfect disguise.
