'''Mineral Buildup''' is a [[world quest]] that takes place at the [[Waterworks]] in the [[Ringing Deeps]].

==Objectives==
*Destroy excess [[Mineral Deposit|mineral deposits]] (12)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[High Speaker Brinthe]]|We've got another incursion. And we need some defenders. I know you're good at this.}}

==Rewards==
One-time [[warband]] reputation bonus:
*+50 [[reputation]] with the [[Assembly of the Deeps]]

Possible rewards:
*{{cost|824|62}}
*25x [[Coffer Key Shard]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|High Speaker Brinthe|Thank you. Efficiency and bravery are a powerful combination.}}

==Notes==
*Location: [[The Waterworks]], [[The Ringing Deeps]]
*[[Mineral Deposit]]s are located in water and on pipes in the area. At 50% health, they may turn into a [[Mineral Elemental]], which also counts toward the objective.
