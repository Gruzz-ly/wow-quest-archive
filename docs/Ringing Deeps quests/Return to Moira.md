'''Return to Moira''' is offered by [[Speaker Brinthe]] if players complete [[Battle of the Earthenworks]] before finishing [[Sympathetic Speakers]] and simply breadcrumbs players back to [[Moira Thaurissan]] to continue the storyline.

==Objectives==
Return to [[Moira Thaurissan|Moira]] in the [[Earthenworks]].

==Description==
[[Speaker Brinthe]]'s impulsiveness is getting the better of her. 

You'd best return to your comrades and let them know of her intentions!
