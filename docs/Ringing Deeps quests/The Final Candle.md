==Objectives==
Steal the final candle for the suspicious vent in the [[Gundargaz Inn]].
* Steal [[the Big Candle]]

==Description==
You are good candle-taker.

We have one last job for you, your waxiness! One more job! Then no more sticky business.

BIG candle in basement. Stone-ones use it to keep rats away from their honey juice. We no rats! We kobo--ooolds.

<There's an awkward pause.>

Nothing rhymes with [[kobold]]s, hm.

Get us big candle!
