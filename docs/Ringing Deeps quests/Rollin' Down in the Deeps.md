Get snufflin', friend!

==Rewards==

*[[Hellcaller Chest]] (during [[A Greedy Emissary]])

*[[The Assembly of the Deeps Commendation]] (+1,000 [[reputation]] with [[Council of Dornogal]]) (first time) or 200x [[Resonance Crystals]] (alts)

==Notes==
Turn on [[CVar graphicsOutlineMode|Outline Mode]] (Esc → Options → Interface → Display → Outline Mode) to make the [[Disturbed Earth]] objects all over [[Khaz Algar]] light up. They will not appear on the minimap.

Gather 100 Odd Globs of Wax to earn the [[Super Size Snuffling]] achievement.
