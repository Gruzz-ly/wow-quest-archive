'''Repurposed, Restored''' concludes the quest chain to acquire the [[Stonevault Mechsuit]] mount. To begin, loot the [[Malfunctioning Mechsuit]] from [[Void Speaker Eirich]] in the [[Stonevault]]. To complete the warband-wide questline, players must loot the [[Overclocked Gear-a-Rang Launcher]] from [[Speaker Dorlita]] in the Stonevault, the [[Synergistic Brewterializer]] from [[Goldie Baronbottom]] in the [[Cinderbrew Meadery]], and [[Burin of the Candle King]] from [[The Candle King]] in [[Darkflame Cleft]].

==Completion==
The machine is complete anew, its purpose restored.

You who have staunched its suffering are charged with carrying on this purpose.

May its metal never yield.
