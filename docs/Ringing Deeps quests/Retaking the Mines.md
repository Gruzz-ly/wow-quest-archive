==Objectives==
Unlock the gate to the [[Lost Mines]].
* Gate opened
* [[Wickbreaker]] slain

==Description==
Now that we have the keys, it is time to take the fight to [[the Candle King]].

Get that gate open. Once we're inside, we'll move on to the next step.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|46|80}}
* 13,600 [[XP]]

You will receive one from:
*[[Vengeful Fire Spirit]]
*[[Wildfire Wick]]

==Completion==
That...did not go as expected.

Our new kobold friend might have some better ideas.
