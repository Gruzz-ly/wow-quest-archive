==Objectives==
Kill [[Cogchewer]] in the [[Lost Mines]] and recover his keys.
* Enter the storage depot (Optional)
* [[Cogchewer's Keys]]

==Description==
Cogchewer is muscle for [[The Candle King|Candle King]]. So mean! Rebuilds [[earthen]] gears for wars. Also in charge of mine's defenses. 

Need his keys to get in the mines... and get me out of this thing!
