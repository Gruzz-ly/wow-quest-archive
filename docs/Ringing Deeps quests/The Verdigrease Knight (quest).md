'''The Verdigrease Knight''' begins the optional storyline of the same name for [[Sojourner of Undermine]] located in [[Campalot]] in southeastern [[Ringing Deeps]].

==Objectives==
Slay the Verdigrease Knight.
* [[The Verdigrease Knight]] slain

==Description==
Think you brave?

Been looking for brave, but not find much. Not sure you count, look like tenderfoot who run home to granny if scrape knee.

How about way to prove it? You hit me as hard as can. Try kill me.

If I not dead, then I get kill you, later.

Not look like it take more than one chop for you.

Deal?
