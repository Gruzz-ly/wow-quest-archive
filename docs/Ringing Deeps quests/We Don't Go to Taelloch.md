==Objectives==
Clear the entrance to [[Taelloch]] of encroaching beasts.
* Kill Encroaching Wildlife (6)
* Find [[Magni Bronzebeard|Magni]] and [[Dagran Thaurissan II|Dagran]]

==Description==
This troubled [[Machine Speakers|Machine Speaker]] was here to do something. But what? It looks like something really did some damage to him.

We shouldn't dally. 

We need to head into Taelloch and look for [[Kol Bruncrag|the foreman]]. Can you clear the entryway into the town? Someone could get hurt!

==Completion==
There's not much to see in the foreman's office. If we want answers, we'll need to search the town.

==Notes and trivia==
On accept:
:{{text|say|Dagran Thaurissan II|Why are all these creatures here? What happened?}}
:{{text|say|Magni Bronzebeard|This isn't one of your books, lad. Watch your step.}}
:''They walk off.''

Valid targets include [[Disruptive Deepflayer]]s, [[Emboldened Fog Wither]]s, [[Invasive Witherling]]s, and [[Territorial Deepflayer]]s.

After killing six, find Dagran and Magni inside the [[Foreman's Office]] on the north wall on the lower level:
:{{text|say|Dagran Thaurissan II|This looks like the foreman's office. But where's the foreman?}}

The office has a number of objects that can be interacted with.

A [[Rug (Ringing Deeps)|Rug]], a [[Blank Slate (Ringing Deeps)|Blank Slate]], [[Chalk Writing]] on the wall, a [[Gem (Ringing Deeps)|Gem]] and a [[Scrip]].

The quest name is a reference to the "{{wplink|Ravenholm|We Don't Go to Ravenholm}}" chapter of ''{{wplink|Half-Life 2}}''.
