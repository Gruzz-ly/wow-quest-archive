==Objectives==
Steal candles for the suspicious vent in the [[Gundargaz Inn]].
*Steal candles (6)
*Timer: 1 minute

==Description==
Ksk, ksk! You there! Yes, you!

<You hear the sound of jostling behind the vent and a new, even scratchier voice emerges.>

We, er, I have job for you! You look sharp. Look quick. Fast hands, just like us--me! Quick hands just like me.

Bring us, me, bring me candles! Yes! 

No ask question, just go get candles. 

We are, er, I am professional, yes. Professionals do not ask questions. 

Only get candles.
