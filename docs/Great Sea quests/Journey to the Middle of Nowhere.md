==Description==
Our [[tidesage]], [[Thomas Zelling]], claims to have found the [[Third Fleet shipwrecks|wreckage]] of [[Marshal Valentine]]'s ship.

Took him long enough.

Marshal Valentine's body is the final piece of our plan, and it lies within that shipwreck. When you're ready, let us depart for his resting place.
