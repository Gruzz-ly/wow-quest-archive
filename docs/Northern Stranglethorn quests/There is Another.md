==Objectives==
Go to [[Bambala]] to seek out [[Lukou]].
*Use [[Jani's Junkpile (heritage)|Jani's Junkpile]] to travel to Bambala (Optional)
*Return to Bambala
*Meet with Kevo ya Siti in Bambala.

==Description==
If [[Mueh'zala]] be involved... I will help. But for a [[loa]] trial fit to push Mueh'zala out, we will need another. 

Our sister loa is also lost. We were a team, her and I, until disagreement pushed us apart. Lukou is the loa of regeneration. She be the ideal loa to balance against death. 

I owe her an apology. You are here, and it be time. 

I last sensed her presence near the small settlement of Bambala. I be meeting you there.
