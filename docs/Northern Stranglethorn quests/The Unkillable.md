==Objectives==
Recover [[Altar of Regeneration|the altar]] centerpiece from [[Mai'Zoth]].
*[[Lukou's Altar Centerpiece]]

==Description==
I'm bettin' dat ogre Mai'Zoth is behind all dis. He should have died long ago, but his magics still be strong. 

He be using the altar's centerpiece as a conduit to drain [[Lukou]]'s power for. 

Recover the centerpiece and prove he's not so unkillable when he doesn't have a [[loa]] hostage.
