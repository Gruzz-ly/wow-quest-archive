==Objectives==
Report to [[Commander Aggro'gosh]] at [[Grom'gol Base Camp]] in the [[Northern Stranglethorn]] Vale.

==Description==
All able bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to Commander Aggro'gosh at Grom'gol Base Camp. The commander requires your aid to keep the supply routes open and free of interference from the local inhabitants and the troublesome Alliance.

To reach Grom'gol, you can take the [[zeppelin]] from the [[Zeppelin tower|tower]] outside [[Orgrimmar]] or the one near [[Undercity]].

Destiny calls!
