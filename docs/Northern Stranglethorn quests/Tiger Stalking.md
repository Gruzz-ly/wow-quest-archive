==Objectives==
Hunt 10 Stranglethorn Tigers.
*[[Stranglethorn Tiger]] slain (10)

==Description==
Young [[tiger]]s are one thing. Fully-grown cats are another. Hunting the grown Stranglethorn tigers will prove an even greater challenge.

The adult cats normally spend their days south of [[Kurzen's Compound]], east of the river.
