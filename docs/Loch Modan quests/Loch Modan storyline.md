# Loch Modan storyline

{{For|the pre-Cataclysm quests|Loch Modan quests}}
[[File:WorldMap-LochModan.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

The '''[[Loch Modan]] storyline''', intended for level 10-20 dwarves and gnomes, picks up where the [[Dun Morogh storyline]] left off in the [[High Seat]] of [[Ironforge]].

The progress of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Loch Modan Quests]].

Upon the conclusion of the storyline, or as soon as players reach level 19, the [[Wetlands storyline]] opens up.

Optional breadcrumbs: [[Reinforcements for Loch Modan]] and [[Find Bingles]] (from [[Ironforge]]) or [[Stormpike's Delivery]] (from [[Stormwind City]]) or [[Hero's Call: Loch Modan!]]
