==Objectives==
Kill 500 Orc Players in Player versus Player combat across Draenor.
* Orc Players slain (500)
* [[Echoing Betrayal]] (provided)

==Description==
<Kuros hands you the bloody heart.>

The ritual is complete. Your nemesis is marked for death.

Now your work to rid this world of the enemy truly begins. Kill all orcs that stand defiantly against the [[Alliance]]. Let their deaths serve as a warning to the rest of the [[Horde]]: they will leave {{Au|Draenor}} or they will die.

Return when you have quenched your rage with the blood of orc.

Conquer our enemy and be known as [[Orcslayer|orcslayer]]. Fail and be nothing.

==Completion==
The truth of your deeds is in your eyes. Your hunger for the enemy sated with cries for mercy and rivers of blood.

Take now your place as orcslayer.
