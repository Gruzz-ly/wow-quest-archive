'''Battle Pet Roundup''' is battle pet daily quest available from the Level 1 and 2 [[Menagerie (garrison)|Menagerie]] building at the [[Garrison]]. It is replaced by [[Mastering the Menagerie]] when the Menagerie building is upgraded to Level 3.

==Objectives==
Defeat the pets in the battle area.
* [[Mark of Pet Mastery]]

==Description==
Looks like we've got some visitors in the battle pen today, and they're looking for a fight. Why don't you show 'em how a master of pet battles handles <himself/herself>?

==Progress==
Those pets looked tough. How'd you do?

==Completion==
I knew you could do it, kid.
