'''The Cryptic Tome of Tailoring''' starts the profession quest line for tailors to unlock 700 skill and the [[Tailoring Emporium]] for your garrison.

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Aerun]] in your garrison.

* [[Cryptic Tome of Tailoring]] (provided)

==Description==
The different sections of the tome describe tailoring techniques that seem strange compared to traditional methods.

The author's name does not appear anywhere in the tome.  Maybe someone in your garrison knows more about the book's origins.

==Rewards==
* You will receive:
*{{cost|1|14}}
* 1,370 [[XP]]
* +250 [[reputation]] with {{Au|Council of Exarchs}}

==Progress==
What have you there, child? 

==Completion==
It seems that you have found a very special tailoring book.

It belongs to [[Ameeka]], a tailor of great renown from [[Embaari Village]].
