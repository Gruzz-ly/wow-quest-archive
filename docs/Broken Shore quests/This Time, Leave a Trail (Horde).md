Here goes nothing, Shadowblade. If anybody can pull this off, you can! ...Probably. Just use the rappel point behind me to get into the cave.

==Completion==

==Notes==
[[Stealth]] is mandatory for this quest, but beware the [[Felhound]]s, which can detect stealthed targets. Don't forget to also use [[Sap]] and [[Distract]] as needed.

Once you've planted the explosives, kill one of the [[Volatile Felblaze]]s to light the gunpowder trail.
