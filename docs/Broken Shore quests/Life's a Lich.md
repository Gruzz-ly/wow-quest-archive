==Notes==
[[File:Jagged Bone Wings.jpg|thumb|Jagged Bone Wings.]]
*The world quest is available only to [[death knight]]s.
*The world quest starts upon entering the [[Soul Ruin]] area in the [[Broken Shore]].

During this quest, [[Amal'thazad]] is present near the entrance to the area, protected by two [[Knight of the Ebon Blade (NPC)|Knights of the Ebon Blade]]. He has the following gossip text:
:The [[Burning Legion]] continues to try to recreate the masterpiece that was the [[Scourge]].

:They turn to their [[Nathrezim]] who make puppets of bone.

:They turn to these [[Feltotem tribe|fallen]] [[Spirit walker|Spiritwalkers]], who simply meddle at these magics.

:I am here, on behalf of the [[Knights of the Ebon Blade|Ebon Blade]], to observe and judge. And to lend assistance to you, hero, should you require it.

Upon completing this world quest, Amal'thazad will say the following and cast [http://www.wowhead.com/spell=235306/jagged-bone-wings Jagged Bone Wings] on you:
*{{text|say|Amal'thazad|Ah! Excellent specimens, Deathlord. Here, you may find this useful.}}

==Trivia==
The Jagged Bone Wings are similar in appearance to those of [[starcraft:Sarah Kerrigan|Sarah Kerrigan]] from StarCraft.
