*The quest starts upon entering the [[Foulshore Strip]] area on the Broken Shore.
*Upon mounting the elemental, three abilities become available. Use them to put out the felfire and kill [[Felflame Infernal]]s, [[Felflame Overfiend]]s, and [[Wrathful Felcinder]]s.
* Aquaclease will appear as [[Aquelian Honor Guard]] to other players.
