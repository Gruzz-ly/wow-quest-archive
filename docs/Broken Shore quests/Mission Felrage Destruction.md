==Completion==

==Notes==
The world quest starts upon entering the [[Felrage Strand]] area on the [[Broken Shore]].

Completing the quest requires any combination of the following:
* Crates Destroyed (100)
* [[Felrage Skrog]] slain (10)
* [[Felrage Marauder]] slain (34)
* [[Captured Warrior]]s Released (20)
