==Description==
Never in my life have I beheld such destruction... such misery. The very ground we walk on paved with the bones of our allies.

Even now I can see the lost [[ghost|spirit]]s of [[Alliance]] soldiers wandering the ruins.

I cannot leave this place without putting the [[soul]]s of our brave soldiers to rest. We owe them... I owe them that much.
