'''The Command Center''' is a one-time quest that becomes available when the construction of the [[Command Center (Broken Shore)|Command Center]] building is completed.

==Objectives==
Complete the construction of the Command Center.
* Complete Construction of the Command Center

==Description==
Well done <name>. In testament to the efforts of all forces fighting here, we have come together to complete the construction of the Command Center. All that remains is for you to inspect our work, and complete the construction.

==Rewards==
* +75 reputation with [[Armies of Legionfall]]

==Progress==
Inspect the Command Center, and complete its construction.

==Completion==
Our grand efforts have resulted in a boon which all can leverage in the coming days.
