==Description==
Tess's essence now wanders a section of the Emerald Dream I have twisted into her thoughts. And I have given her my gift, but she could still use a guide.

Enter the Dream through the portal behind me. It will place you near where she sleeps. See if you can help show her how rage may blind a mortal.
