==Objectives==
Gather 10 Cold Iron Ore in the {{C|Duskwood}} [[Nightmare Incursions]] and return to [[Field Captain Palandar]].
*[[Cold Iron Ore]] (10)

==Description==
While these demons distract us here, the [[Emerald Dream]] is being corrupted into [[Emerald Nightmare|nightmare]]. Quick, travel through the Emerald Dream Portal in the [[Twilight Grove]] and collect Cold Iron Ore from within the Nightmare Incursions. We can harness its magic to reverse the damage. You will need the assistance of a miner.
