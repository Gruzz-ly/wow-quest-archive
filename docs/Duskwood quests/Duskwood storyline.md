# Duskwood storyline

{{for|the pre-''Cataclysm'' quests|Duskwood quests}}
[[File:WorldMap-Duskwood.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

The '''[[Duskwood]] storyline''' takes place after the [[Redridge Mountains storyline]] for level 20-25 Alliance players, typically humans. It begins in [[Darkshire]] and proceeds to the crossroads between [[Westfall]] and [[Northern Stranglethorn]] where [[Apprentice Fess]] waits. From there the quests progress to the newly upgraded [[Raven Hill]]. Completing these quests grants the achievement [[Duskwood Quests]].

After Duskwood comes the [[Northern Stranglethorn storyline]].
