==Description==
You are worthy of passing, <class>.

Huo, the spirit of fire rests in the chamber beyond. Let him feast upon the kindling you've gathered, and use the wind to fan his flames. Doing so should endear you to him. Once he is ignited, if he is willing, lead him to the [[Temple of Five Dawns]], the building standing high in the center of the island.
