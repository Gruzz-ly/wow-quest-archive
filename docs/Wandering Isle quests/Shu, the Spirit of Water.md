==Objectives==
Coax Shu, the spirit of water, into a game.
* Cross to the [[Pool of Reflection]]
* Coax [[Shu]], the Water Spirit
* [[Sun Pearl (2)]] (provided)

==Description==
Shu, the spirit of water, is sulking in the deep pool to the north. Cross the rocks and then drop the sun pearl in the center of the pool. He will know what this means. 

==Rewards==
You will receive: {{cost|||25}}

==Progress==
Go ahead, place the pearl in the pool.

==Completion==
{{questportrait|Aysa Cloudsinger.jpg|Aysa Cloudsinger}}
It looks like you have his attention, <name>. I think he likes you.

==Notes==
From the platform where Liang and Aysa are standing, jump on to the rocks in the pool immediately to the northeast. Make it to a rock that is glowing and players will jump to the [[Pool of Reflection]] automatically. Players also receive the "Blessing of the Water Strider" and are able to walk on water. Walk to the center of the pool and use the Sun Pearl. The quest will complete automatically using the remote-turn-in interface.
