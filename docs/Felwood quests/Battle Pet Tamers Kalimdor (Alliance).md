==Description==
Now that you've had a real chance to hone your skills, you're ready to venture out into the world of pet battles in full.

There are five tamers throughout [[Kalimdor]] that I'd like you to defeat: Traitor Gluk in [[Feralas]], Grazzle the Great in [[Dustwallow Marsh]], Kela Grimtotem in [[Thousand Needles]], Zoltan in [[Felwood]], and Elena Flutterfly in [[Moonglade]].

If you are able to best them, you will be ready to venture further.
