== Objectives ==
Collect 12 Deadwood Honey Globs from the hives in Deadwood Village using Ruumbo's Honey Stick.
*[[Deadwood Honey Glob]] (10)
*[[Ruumbo's Honey Stick]] ''(provided)''

== Description ==
Ruumbo hungers!

The Deadwood jerks have stolen all their honey from the Timbermaw tribe! We saw...er, Ruumbo saw them do it! Now Ruumbo must have that honey!

Take this... this stick! Yes, this stick. Take this stick and use it to steal the honey from their beehives in Deadwood Village.

Ruumbo must have as much honey as possible. This is vital.

== Progress ==
Ruumbo can smell whether you have honey or not! 

== Completion ==
Honey! Delicious honey!

Well done, ugly creature! You may put the honey...put the honey down right there. No, to the left. Yes, there. Just leave it there.
