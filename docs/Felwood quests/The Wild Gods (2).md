==Objectives==
Deliver Wild Offerings to the [[Shadowtooth Emissary]] in {{C|Felwood}}. Use [[:Agamaggan's Roar]] on a [[Delirious Ancient]] and defeat it to earn Wild Offerings.
*[[Wild Offering]] (3)

==Description==
A darkness has taken root in the [[Emerald Dream]], and it has shadowed the ancient spirits of [[Hyjal]] with confusion and fear.

Seek out these delirious spirits, mortal, and use my roar to draw them into combat.

When you slay their corrupted forms, their spirits will be cleansed and they will be reborn anew.
