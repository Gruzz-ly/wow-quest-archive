==Objectives==
Collect a [[:Wildwhisper Draught]] from [[Jintha'alor]] in the {{C|Hinterlands}}, then use it at the top of {{C|Razorfen Downs}} to speak to the [[Spirit of Agamaggan]].

==Description==
Somethin' be wrong with de [[loa]] of [[Hyjal]]. De most powerful spirits of nature have been watchin' over the mountain for thousands of years, but dey be startin' to vanish, one by one.

[[Agamaggan]], de greatest of de ancient loa, died long ago. But de [[Amani tribe|Amani]] [[Forest troll|trolls]] have ways to speak with de dead. Find de voodoo dey be usin', and take it to de Razorfen. Talk to Agamaggan and ask him what be happenin' to his kin.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*3,900 [[XP]]
*[[Agamaggan's Roar]]

==Completion==
Even beyond the veil of death, I have sensed a shadow falling across Hyjal and my kin.

Take this, mortal. You will need it for what is to come.
