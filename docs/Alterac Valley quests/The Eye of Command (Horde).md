'''The Eye of Command''' is offered upon reaching 999/1000 {{rep|exalted}} standing with the [[Frostwolf Clan]].

==Progress==
[[Drek'Thar]] sings your praises. Kalimdor is abuzz with tales of your heroics. The Warchief glows - your stalwart defense of our clan has pleased him greatly.

You have earned the Eye of Command.

Present your insignia, Commander <name>.
*[[Frostwolf Insignia Rank 5]]

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Frostwolf Insignia Rank 6]]
*+500 [[reputation]] with [[Frostwolf Clan]]
*+500 reputation with [[Orgrimmar (faction)|Orgrimmar]]

==Completion==
Channel the power of the insignia. Let the enemy hear your words. The might of the Horde is unquestionable!
