==Description==
If you can handle rescuin' de others by yourself, [[Zulsan]] and I will head back to [[Goldtusk Inn|de inn]] and start cleanin' up de place.

Don't worry, I still be cookin' up a special batch of my famous scorpid blood drinks for you.

Bring back a few bottles of it for me and let's add some sweetness to de drinks!
