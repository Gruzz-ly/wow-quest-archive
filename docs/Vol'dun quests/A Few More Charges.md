==Objectives==
* Use the [[Lectric Frequency Modulator]]
* Feed [[lasher]]s to the [[ranishu]]

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Kiro]]|That [[Bilgewater Cartel|Bilgewater]] bunch needs to keep the ranishu fed to power their mechs. Help the [[goblin]]s out. The sooner they're done, the sooner they're gone.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Voldunai]]

{{wqr|bfa}}

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Kiro|I must admit, I admire your work ethic. Nicely done.}}

==Notes==
*Location: [[Carapace Ridge]], [[Vol'dun]].

After interacting with the [[Lectric Frequency Modulator]], the player is transformed into a [[Voracious Ranishu]] with the following abilities:
#{{Abilities|Roll Dash|id=274584|Expel electrical energy to dash forward through the air.|icon=ability_thunderking_lightningwhip}}
#{{Abilities|Shock Launch|id=274579|A powerful electrical discharge launches the ranishu into the air.|icon=spell_nature_unrelentingstorm}}
Use it to role over [[Ridge Lasher]]s, [[Cracked Pricklevine]]s, and the [[Golden Lash]] to fill up the bar. Avoid [[Caromzar Cactus|Caromzar Cacti]] which knock the player backwards or throws them a significant distance.
