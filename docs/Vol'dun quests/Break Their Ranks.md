==Objectives==
Kill 10 Faithless attackers within the [[Terrace of the Devoted|Temple Grounds]].{{sic}}
* [[Faithless]] slain (10)

==Description==
[[Sanctuary of the Devoted|This temple]] is our last bastion against the Faithless. We need every able fighter to defend its walls.

If you're offering to help, we won't turn you away.

Head into battle and do what you can to break their ranks!

* {{Cost|19|40}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
Our temple will not fall at the hands of the faithless!{{sic}}

==Notes==
Pick up [[A Powerful Prisoner]] from [[Serrik]] and [[Push Them Back!]] from Zissiah before heading out. While killing [[Faithless Aggressor]]s, [[Faithless Warden]]s, [[Faithless Skycaller]]s, [[Faithless Sandscout]]s, [[Faithless Ravager]]s, or [[Faithless Sentry|Faithless Sentries]], you can also kill [[Warlord Zothix]] and pick up [[A Lost Flute]].
