==Objectives==
Kill Fractured and Distorted Memories.
* [[Distorted Memory]] slain (4)
* [[Fractured Memory]] slain (8)

==Description==
Something is terribly wrong.

Unwanted memories have been released from their magical confines.

[[Akunda]]'s power is supposed to keep dem sealed away...

De physical manifestations of our most haunting memories have no place in dis world.

Dey must be destroyed.

==Notes==
Pick up [[Memory Breach]] before heading out. Distorted and Fractured Memories are found across the [[Valley of Sorrows]].

Note that Fractured Memories can also spawn from the [[Unwanted Memory|Unwanted Memories]] required for [[Memory Breach]], so don't kill too many of the ones that are already roaming about.
