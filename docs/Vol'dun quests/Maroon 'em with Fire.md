==Notes==
Pick up [[The Grinning Idols]] before heading out. Upon accepting both, Jamboya, [[Gunner Bosanya|Bosanya]], [[Jukanga the Snitch|Jukanga]], and [[Quartermaster Tulmac|Tulmac]] hop off the dock and head northwest.
:{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|All hands to de boat! See ya soon, <name>.}}

Use the [[Undying Torch]] to destroy [[Pillaging Canoe]]s found all over the northwestern parts of the port.

After finishing both objectives, find Jamboya, the mutineers, and [[Jamboya's Boat]] at {{Coords|33.2|81.8|Vol'dun}}. On approach, Jamboya may say one of the following:
*{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|Don't burn dis one, bucko. We be usin' it to get out to de island.}}
*{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|We be almost ready to weigh anchor. Zem'lan never gonna see us coming.}}
