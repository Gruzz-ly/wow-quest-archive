==Objectives==
Meditate at the [[Shrine of Thunder]] and confront your inner demons.
* Meditate at the Shrine of Thunder
* [[Inner Demon (Vol'dun)|Inner Demon]] slain

==Description==
If you wish to invite [[Akunda]] into your mind, it can't be bogged down by fear or regret.

Meditate at de Shrine of Thunder and confront whatever demons linger in de dark recesses of your mind.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Small Azerite Shard]] (100 [[Azerite (resource)]])
* {{Cost|19|40}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]
* +75 reputation with [[Voldunai]]

==Progress==
Some who meditate at de shrine hear de voice of de [[loa]] himself, though most only hear silence or de sounds of their failings.

==Completion==
I can tell you found success. You are at peace.

==Notes==
Pick up [[It Springs Eternal]] before leaving. The Shrine of Thunder is found in a small cave at the [[Eternal Spring]].

Upon interacting with the shrine, the player enters a state of meditation for 6 seconds. At the end, an [[Inner Demon (Vol'dun)|Inner Demon]] appears and attacks the player, and the voice of Akunda is heard.
:{{Text|say|Akunda|I grow weaker by the moment. You... you must help me before it is too late.}}

Kill the demon and return to Akunda the Exalted at the temple.
