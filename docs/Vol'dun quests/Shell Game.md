'''Shell Game''' is a [[world quest]] offered by [[Collector Kojo]] at each of the six zones of [[Kul Tiras]] and [[Zandalar]]. This memory matching game requires the player to match pairs of items.

==Objectives==
*Speak to [[Collector Kojo]]
*Complete Collector Kojo's Shell Game

==Description==
:{{text|say|Collector Kojo|I've collected many things over the years. See if you can match them.}}

==Rewards==
Depending on zone and player faction, you will receive:
*+175 [[reputation]] with [[Tortollan Seekers]]
*{{Alliance}} +75 reputation with [[Order of Embers]], [[Proudmoore Admiralty]], [[Storm's Wake]], or [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
*{{Horde}} +75 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]], [[Voldunai]], [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]], or [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*An [[Azerite (resource)]] item
*[[War Resources]]
*[[Tortollan Trader's Stock]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Collector Kojo|Well done, <name>!}}

==Notes==
;Speaking with Kajo

*There are 8 pairs of items in total, arranged in a 4Ã—4 grid. If you reveal two identical items, they will flash with light and disappear. If you reveal two different items, they will go back to being hidden. The minigame ends when all pairs have been matched.
*The items which need to be matched are: an [[arcane]] orb, a [[Val'kyr]], a [[pirate]] [[crab]], a [[turtle]], a [[shark]] jaw, an [[Anima (blood fluid)|anima]] blob, a purple crystal, and a [[:Mini Thor]].
