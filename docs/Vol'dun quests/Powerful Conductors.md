[[Akunda]] offers us a chance to start anew.

Through a sacred ritual, de [[loa]] enters our mind and removes de memories dat cause us pain.

If you seek Akunda's blessing, den you must first prepare for de ritual.

Their leader's antennae should do de trick. He's easily three times de size of de normal [[ranishu]].
