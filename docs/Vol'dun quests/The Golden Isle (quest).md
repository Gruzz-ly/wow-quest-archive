==Objectives==
Ride in [[Jamboya's Boat|Jamboya's boat]] out to the [[Golden Isle]].
*Jamboya's boat ridden

==Description==
[[Pirate-King Zem'lan]] don't stay in [[Port of Zem'lan|de port]] with de rest of us. Too good to crack a jug with his crew no more.

We gotta sail out to de Golden Isle to find him. That be de forbidden island where Zem'lan hoards all of de treasure we've pillaged and plundered, so only he can enjoy dem.

Hop in my boat and we'll row out dere together.

==Completion==
Here we are. De Golden Isle. It's beautiful, ain't it?

A pirate's paradise.

==Notes==
Interact with [[Jamboya's Boat]] to jump inside, causing [[First Mate Jamboya|Jamboya]], [[Gunner Bosanya|Bosanya]], [[Jukanga the Snitch|Jukanga]], and [[Quartermaster Tulmac|Tulmac]] to do the same.
:{{Text|say|Quartermaster Tulmac|All hand hoy!}}

The boat sets off towards the Golden Isle to the southwest, with Jamboya and Jukanga rowing, Bosanya standing on the prow with her bow drawn, Tulmac sitting on the side of the vessel, and the player sitting in the center.
:{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|We be headin' to de Golden Isle, where Zem'lan hoards all de treasure we've pillaged over countless years.}}
:{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|De only people dere be [[goldslave]]s, former crewmates forced to tend to Zem'lan's treasures for all eternity.}}
:{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|It be de only fate worse than ours.}}
:{{Text|say|Jukanga the Snitch|And yet we be headin' right into it.}}
:{{Text|say|Gunner Bosanya|Dis be a mutiny, ya bilge rat! We gonna run our blades right through Zem'lan's bony gut.}}
:{{Text|say|First Mate Jamboya|Aye, and take our share of de treasure.}}
:{{Text|say|Quartermaster Tulmac|Look at all dat gold...}}

The boat reaches the isle, and the passengers disembark.
