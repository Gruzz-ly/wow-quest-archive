==Objectives==
Slay 12 [[ranishu]] in [[Redrock Mesa]].
* [[Ranishu Gorger]] slain (12)

==Description==
Redemption must be earned.

We do good works to show [[Akunda]] we are worthy of his blessing.

De ranishu are a plague upon these lands. Dey devour everything in sight, with no regard for taste or texture.

If dey go unchecked, those vile pests will eat their way straight to de temple.

Rid de mesa of their presence. Show Akunda you are worthy of his gift.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|19|40}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]
* +75 reputation with [[Voldunai]]

==Progress==
Have you dealt with those insatiable pests?

==Completion==
Akunda smiles upon you. He knows what you have done here today.

==Notes==
Pick up [[Powerful Conductors]] and [[Sacred Remains (quest)]] before heading out to the [[Redrock Mesa]]. Accepting all three quests prompts:
:{{Text|say|Akunda the Exalted|Gathering de reagents needed for your communion is de first step towards redemption.}}

At this point, [[Jorak]] also offers a new quest in the form of [[Fond Farewells]], requiring a brief trip back to [[Zuldazar]].

Ranishu Gorgers are found in large numbers all over Redrock Mesa and [[Redrock Cavern]].
