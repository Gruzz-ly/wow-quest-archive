'''Azerite Mining''' is a [[world quest]] happening either at the [[Blistering Wastes]]{{coords|60|61|Vol'dun}} or in the southwestern part{{coords|35|65|Vol'dun}} of [[Vol'dun]].

==Objectives==
[[:Azerite]] recovered

==Description==
*{{text|say|[[Magni Bronzebeard]]|I can sense Azerite nearby... and those who would seek tae abuse it for their own ends. Ye've got tae stop 'em.}}
*{{text|say|Magni Bronzebeard|Someone in this cave is mining Azerite from the [[Azeroth|world]]'s open wounds. Put a stop to it!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Champions of Azeroth]]
* +75 reputation with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
* +75 reputation with [[Voldunai]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*An [[Azerite (resource)]] item
*[[War Resources]]
*[[Azerite Armor Cache]]

==Completion==
*{{text|say|Magni Bronzebeard|The [[:Heart of Azeroth]] is stronger now, thanks tae ye. We're one step closer tae curing [[Azeroth (titan)|Azeroth]] of her wounds.}}
*{{text|say|Magni Bronzebeard|Such a shame... puttin' their own greed afore the health o' [[Azeroth (titan)|Azeroth]]. Thank ye, champion.}}

==Notes==
*Mine [[Azerite Node]]s and [[Azerite Chunk]]s, and kill [[Sethrak Warbringer]]s, [[Sethrak Ravager]]s, [[Sethrak Warden]], and [[War Krolusk]]s until you fill the bar to 100%. In the Blistering Wastes, [[General Krathax]] also counts.
