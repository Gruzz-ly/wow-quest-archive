==Objectives==
Find and activate the final key to the Apexis device. 
* Follow the clue to activate the [[Mysterious Device (Spires of Arak)|final key]]

==Description==
One last key is required to access the Apexis device. The writings pertaining to its location read: "The final key has been housed deep within the fungal caves where the river makes its final drop. Though the [[pale orc]]s that dwell there may be vile, they should prove a worthy deterrent."

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|3|85|}}
* 3,770 [[XP]]

==Completion==
With the final key activated, the Apexis device that leads to the amulet should be accessible.
