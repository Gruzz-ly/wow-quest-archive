==Objectives==
Accept the summons and travel to [[Quel'Danas]] to aid the [[Vanguard of the Light]].
*Speak with Liadrin's image or use the Light's Summon
*Travel to Quel'Danas
*[[Light's Summon]] (provided)

<Name>, you have been chosen.

[[Xal'atath]]'s army of the [[Void]] has descended upon the [[Sunwell]]. We have put our faith in the [[Light]], and in turn, the Light has called upon the greatest champions of [[Azeroth]].

Answer this summons and stand against the [[Devouring Host]]! The fate of Quel'Danas and the world rests in your hands.
