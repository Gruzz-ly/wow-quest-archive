# Isle of Quel'Danas quests

[[File:WorldMap-Sunwell.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]
This is a list of all [[Isle of Quel'Danas]] quests in table format (arranged by level).

==Quest tables==
Quests which take place in [[Isle of Quel'Danas]] are denoted by a {{Inzone}}. Quests which take place outside of [[Isle of Quel'Danas]], but involve something in the zone are denoted by {{Outzone}}.

Quests which are part of a chain are denoted by {{Chain}}. Click the downward arrow to the right of the icon to view the chain. Smaller quest chains use ↓ to indicate the next quest is below and ⊥ to indicate the last quest.

Quest givers may have {{queststart}} to indicate they only start the quest, but may have {{queststartfinish}} to indicate they start and finish a quest.

The other quests listed either send the player out of the zone; or are in quest chains related to [[Isle of Quel'Danas]], categorized as such, but take place out of the zone (and will have no subzone listed).

==={{Neutral Icon}} Neutral quests===
All quests on the Isle of Quel'Danas are offered by neutral NPCs.
