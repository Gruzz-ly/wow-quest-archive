==Objectives==
Repel the [[Thunderlord clan (alternate universe)|Thunderlord]] invasion.
* Speak with [[Sergeant Grimjaw]]
* Repel the Invasion (1)
**[[Aggar Thunder-Reaver]] slain

==Description==
Your timing could not have been better.

Your [[scout]]s have reported the approach of a large Thunderlord army.

[[Frostwolf Orcs|My clan]] can ride out and assist in severing their reinforcements, but you will need to see to the defense of your fort against the bulk of their forces.

Sergeant Grimjaw awaits your command.
