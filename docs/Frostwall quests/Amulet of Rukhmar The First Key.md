==Objectives==
Find and activate the first key to the Apexis device. 
* Follow the clue to activate the [[Mysterious Device (Spires of Arak)|first key]]

==Description==
The [[Arakkoa]] research points to three "keys" that need to be activated in order to use the Apexis device. The first is located in [[Skettis (alternate universe)|Skettis]]. The text mentioning it reads: "The first key is kept in the high reaches of upper Skettis. It lay where the dead can protect it from the talons of the unwanted."
