'''Scrappin'''' is battle pet daily quest available from the Level 1 and 2 [[Menagerie (garrison)|Menagerie]] building at the [[Garrison]]. It is replaced by [[Mastering the Menagerie]] when the Menagerie building is upgraded to Level 3.

==Objectives==
Defeat the pets in the battle area.
* [[Mark of Pet Mastery]]

==Description==
Ever since we built da menagerie, a lot o' "visitors" been showin' up. I know da look, and they be lookin' for a fight. Brudda, you show 'em you da real master 'round here.
