'''Upper Blackrock Spire''' is a daily dungeon quest to kill the final boss. It is only randomly available from the [[Frostwall]] [[Town Hall (garrison)|Town Hall]] of a Tier 3 [[garrison]], across the same region. If it hasn't been turned in before the daily reset, it will be removed from your quest log.

==Objectives==
Defeat Warlord Zaela in [[Upper Blackrock Spire]].
*[[Warlord Zaela]] slain

==Description==
Hellscream's [[Ironmarch]] vanguard managed to reach [[Blackrock Mountain]] and has allied with Warlord Zaela of the [[Dragonmaw clan]]. Even now the [[Iron Horde]] readies to invade [[Azeroth]] while Zaela prepares a doomsday weapon in the heart of the mountain to ensure nothing stands in their way.

You must put a stop to Zaela once and for all, commander. She has been a thorn in our side for too long.
