==Objectives==
Take the Strange Engine Part to [[K. Lee Smallfry]] in [[Telredor]].

==Description==
This strange engine part doesn't look like it fits in a steam pump, much less anything else you've seen. Perhaps K. Lee Smallfry might know something about it.

==Notes==
*The part is randomly fished out of Steam Pump Flotsam nodes in Zangarmarsh.
*The item which starts the quest can be looted by [[Horde]]-players, but they will not receive any quests from it.
*The item which starts the quest, along with the quest itself and K. Lee Smallfry who the quest is turned in to, are all references to the film ''[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serenity_(film) Serenity]''. The film is an extension of the series ''[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Firefly_(TV_series) Firefly]'' which features a futuristic sci-fi/western scenario, following the adventures of the crew aboard a spaceship called "Serenity". In the movie, there is a scene where the crew mechanic, Kaywinnit Lee "Kaylee" Frye, says: "And don't fly in anything with a Capissen 38 engine; they fall RIGHT out of the sky."
