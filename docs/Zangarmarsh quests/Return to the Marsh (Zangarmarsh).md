Return to [[Ysiel Windsinger]] at the [[Cenarion Refuge]] in [[Zangarmarsh]].

==Description==

Affairs in [[Moonglade]] and [[Silithus]] have kept our members occupied, but we will do our best to send the necessary resources to the [[Cenarion Refuge]].
