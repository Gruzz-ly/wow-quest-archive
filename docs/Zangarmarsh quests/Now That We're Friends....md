==Objectives==
Kill 12 Bloodscale Slavedrivers and 6 Bloodscale Enchantresses, and then return to [[Gzhun'tt]] at [[Sporeggar (town)|Sporeggar]] in [[Zangarmarsh]]. 
*[[Bloodscale Enchantress]] slain (6)
*[[Bloodscale Slavedriver]] slain (12)

==Description==
The [[naga]] of the [[Bloodscale Enclave]] to the northeast have begun to kill our people. We cannot allow this to continue, <class>!

We suggest that you to teach them a lesson as proof of our friendship.
