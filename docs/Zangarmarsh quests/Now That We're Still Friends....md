==Objectives==
Kill 12 Bloodscale Slavedrivers and 6 Bloodscale Enchantresses, and then return to [[Gzhun'tt]] at [[Sporeggar (town)|Sporeggar]] in [[Zangarmarsh]].
*[[Bloodscale Slavedriver]] slain (12)
*[[Bloodscale Enchantress]] slain (6)

==Description==
Reports have come in that the [[naga]] of the [[Bloodscale Enclave]] to the northeast continue to kill our people. We thought that you had dealt with this, <class>!

I suggest that you to teach them a lesson as proof of our continued friendship.

==Rewards==
Upon completion of this quest you will gain:
*+750 reputation with [[Sporeggar]]
