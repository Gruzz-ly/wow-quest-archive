==Objectives==
Bring 15 Cenarion Combat Badges, 20 Cenarion Logistics Badges, 17 Cenarion Tactical Badges and 1 Mark of Remulos to [[Vargus]] at [[Cenarion Hold]] in [[Silithus]]. You must also attain Revered reputation with [[Cenarion Circle]] to be able to complete this quest.
* [[Cenarion Combat Badge]] (15)
* [[Cenarion Logistics Badge]] (20)
* [[Cenarion Tactical Badge]] (17)
* [[Mark of Remulos]]
* [[Cenarion Circle]] (Revered)

==Description==
The badges you collect from performing combat, logistics and tactical assignments for [[Windcaller Proudhorn]] are more than just for show, <name>.

You'd be wise to hold on to them. As you prove your loyalty to our organization I can offer you some of the gear we reserve for our closest allies in exchange for those badges.
