==Description==
All able-bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to Windcaller Proudhorn at Cenarion Hold. You are to aid our [[Cenarion Circle|Cenarion]] allies in their battle to turn back [[Twilight's Hammer]]. [[Cho'Gall]]'s minions must not be allowed to dominate Silithus!

You can reach Silithus by taking the northwestern road out of [[Un'Goro Crater]].

Destiny calls!
