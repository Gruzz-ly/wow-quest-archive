'''Rise, Thunderfury!''' concludes the legendary quest chain to forge [[Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of the Windseeker]]. Players must have summoned and defeated [[Prince Thunderaan]] in order to loot the [[Dormant Wind Kissed Blade]], which starts this quest.

==Objectives==
Present the Dormant Wind Kissed Blade to [[Highlord Demitrian]].
*[[Dormant Wind Kissed Blade]] (provided)

==Description==
You have defeated the [[Prince Thunderaan|Wind Seeker]]. Take the dormant blade and force the lying wretch, [[Demitrian]], to use the materials you provided for his betrayal to wake Thunderfury.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of the Windseeker]]

==Progress==
<Demitrian cowers in your presence.>

==Completion==
Please, do not harm me! Take it! Take the blade! Leave Demitrian to reshape his pathetic life!

==Notes==
Turning in the quest [[Thunderaan the Windseeker]] summons [[Prince Thunderaan]], a 40-person outdoor raid encounter. See Thunderaan's article for tactics regarding the fight.
