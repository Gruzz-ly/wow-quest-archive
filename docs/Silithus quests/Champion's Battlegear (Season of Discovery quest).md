==Objectives==
Bring 15 Cenarion Combat Badges, 20 Cenarion Logistics Badges, 20 Cenarion Tactical Badges and 1 Mark of Cenarius to [[Vargus]] at [[Cenarion Hold]] in {{C|Silithus}}.  You must also attain Exalted reputation with [[Cenarion Circle]] to be able to complete this quest.
*[[Cenarion Combat Badge]] (15)
*[[Cenarion Logistics Badge]] (20)
*[[Cenarion Tactical Badge]] (20)
*[[Mark of Cenarius]]
*[[Cenarion Circle]] (Exalted)

==Description==
The badges you collect from performing combat, logistics and tactical assignments for Cenarion Hold are more than just for show, <name>.

You'd be wise to hold on to them.  As you prove your loyalty to our organization I can offer you some of the gear we reserve for our closest allies in exchange for those badges.
