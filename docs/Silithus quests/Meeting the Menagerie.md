==Objectives==
Travel to {{C|Un'Goro Crater}}, in the south of [[Kalimdor]], and find evidence of [[Van Amburgh]].

==Description==
While you were out, a [[wisp]] visited us looking for you. The wisp indicated that you are needed in Un'Goro Crater. The wisp said that a companion of yours discovered the location of a foe that you have been tracking for some time.

Thank you again for your aid here. It seems that it is time for you to move on.
