==Objectives==
Speak with [[Highlord Demitrian]] in [[Silithus]].

==Description==
The [[elemental lord]]s must be united, but there has been no [[Windlord]] to lead the [[air elemental]]s since the death of [[Al'Akir]].

Once, there was an heir to the [[Throne of the Four Winds|Throne of Four winds]] named [[Prince Thunderaan]], but the prince was defeated in the mortal realm and still awaits rebirth. Only one man holds the knowledge necessary to complete the prince's rebirth.

There is a servant of Thunderaan who wanders the sands of [[Silithus]]. Find him, and he will show you the way.
