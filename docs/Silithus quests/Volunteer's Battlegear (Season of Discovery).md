==Objectives==
Bring 5 Cenarion Combat Badges, 3 Cenarion Logistics Badges and 7 Cenarion Tactical Badges to [[Vargus]] at [[Cenarion Hold]] in {{C|Silithus}}. You must also attain Friendly reputation with [[Cenarion Circle]] to be able to complete this quest. 
* [[Cenarion Combat Badge]] (5)
* [[Cenarion Logistics Badge]] (3)
* [[Cenarion Tactical Badge]] (7)
*[[Cenarion Circle]] ({{reputation|Friendly}})

==Description==
The badges you collect from performing combat, logistics and tactical assignments for Cenarion Hold are more than just for show, <name>.

You'd be wise to hold on to them.  As you prove your loyalty to our organization I can offer you some of the gear we reserve for our closest allies in exchange for those badges.
