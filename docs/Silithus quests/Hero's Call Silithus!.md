==Objectives==
Report to [[Windcaller Proudhorn]] at [[Cenarion Hold]] in [[Silithus]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, [[Varian Wrynn|King Varian Wrynn]], all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Windcaller Proudhorn at Cenarion Hold.

Our [[Cenarion Circle]] allies require your assistance to fight back against the [[Twilight's Hammer]] onslaught. You can reach Silithus by taking the northwestern road out of the [[Un'Goro Crater]].

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
