'''The Drowned Oracles''' is a [[world quest]] at [[Elun'alor Temple]] in [[Nazjatar]]. The area is under the effect of the {{Abilities|Drowned Sky|icon=artifactability balancedruid halfmoon|id=298988}} debuff.

==Objectives==
* [[Drowned Oracle]] slain (3)

==Description==
*{{Alliance}} {{text|say|[[Shandris Feathermoon]]|It sickens me to know that [[Queen Azshara|Azshara]] has cursed [[Elune]]'s [[Priestess of the Moon|priestesses]] with a restless death. Please give them the peace they deserve.}}
*{{Horde}} {{text|say|[[First Arcanist Thalyssra]]|The priestesses of Elune never bowed to Azshara, and were cursed for their defiance. Grant their [[Ghost|spirits]] the peace they were denied.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with {{Alliance}} [[Waveblade Ankoan]] or {{Horde}} [[Unshackled|The Unshackled]]

==Completion==
*{{Alliance}} {{text|say|Shandris Feathermoon|After I lost my family, it was the [[Sisterhood of Elune]] who took me in. Thank you for ending their torment.}}
*{{Horde}} {{text|say|First Arcanist Thalyssra|Azshara demands that her subjects worship her above all others. Her prideful vanity will be her downfall.}}
