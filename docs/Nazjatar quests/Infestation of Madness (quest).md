==Objectives==
* [[Kil'karrok]] [[makrura]] slain (10)
* [[Infestation of Madness]] slain

==Description==
*{{Alliance}} {{text|say|[[Lady Jaina Proudmoore]]|An unseen enemy commands the makrura of Kal'methir. With the void energy you gather, I will reveal the enemy so you can strike it down.}}
*{{Horde}} {{text|say|[[First Arcanist Thalyssra]]|An unseen will commands the makrura of Kal'methir. Do what you must in order to reveal and destroy it.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Unshackled|The Unshackled]]
* +75 reputation with [[Waveblade Ankoan]]

==Completion==
*{{Alliance}} {{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|I do not believe the true enemy was destroyed, but at least you have weakened its hold over the makrura. Well done.}}
*{{Horde}} {{text|say|First Arcanist Thalyssra|I doubt we have seen the last of that creature... but we have weakened its hold over the makrura. Excellent work.}}

==Notes==
*Location: [[Kal'methir Citadel]], [[Nazjatar]].
*Kill [[Alga the Eyeless]], [[Allseer Oma'kil]], [[Broken Deepshell]]s, [[Deepshell Captive]]s, [[Elder Unu]], [[Kil'karrok Commander]]s, [[Kil'karrok Hammerclaw]]s, [[Kil'karrok Hunter]]s, [[Kil'karrok Makrura]], [[Kil'karrok Scavenger]]s, [[Kil'karrok Seer]]s, [[Kil'karrok Seers]], and [[Kil'karrok Tidal Shaman]].
*After killing enough makrura, use the extra button to get to the top of a Kal'methir spire.
*After the completion, you can use the Slow Fall to get down.
