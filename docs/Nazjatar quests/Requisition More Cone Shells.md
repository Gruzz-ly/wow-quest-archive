==Description==
If we are going to arm ourselves against the [[naga]], we will need far more supplies and armaments. Cone Shells make most of our armor and weapons. It's light, it's strong, and it's plentiful in this region. However, our supplies are running short and our forces need support.

If you find yourself carrying Impregnable Cone Shells, please leave your stores with the quartermaster.
