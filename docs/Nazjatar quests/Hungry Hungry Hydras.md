*Kill [[Chitterspine Lurker]]s, [[Chitterspine Guillotine]]s, and [[Chitterspine Encroacher]]s to get [[Chitterspine Meat]]. Then click on the [[Feeding Shell]] to feed Theronar. Then mount the hydra and kill the Cracklemaw Colossus.
