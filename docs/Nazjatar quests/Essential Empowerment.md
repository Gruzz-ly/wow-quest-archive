'''Essential Empowerment''' is the first quest in the chain to unlock the [[Heart Forge]]. In order to unlock this quest, you must have completed the [[Nazjatar]] introduction questline, beginning with [[The Wolf's Offensive]] or [[The Warchief's Order]] and ending with [[A Way Home]].

If you're eligible for this quest, [[Magni Bronzebeard|Magni]] will appear in [[Mezzamere]] or [[Newhome]] to offer it.

==Objectives==
Meet [[Magni Bronzebeard|Magni]] in the repaired [[Chamber of Heart]] in [[Silithus: The Wound|Silithus]].
*[[Titan Translocator]]

==Description==
Champion, excitin' news!

I know yer busy dealin' with the [[naga]] threat right now, but we need ye to come back tae the Chamber of Heart! [[MOTHER]]'s finally finished her calculations and she's got an idea of what tae do next!

Hurry back tae Silithus as quick as ye can. [[Azeroth (titan)|Azeroth]] needs ye!
