==Description==
Champion, I have a personal favor to ask of you. This is not on behalf of the [[Horde]] or the [[Fourth War|war]] effort.

It has been over ten thousand years since I last walked these streets. I knew many of these people, even trained some of them.

Will you walk with me as I learn their fates? The road is dangerous, and I do not trust myself to maintain calm as I try to resurface old grief.
