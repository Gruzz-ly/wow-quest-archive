# Nazjatar storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Nazjatar.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]
The '''Nazjatar storyline''' becomes available to ''[[World of Warcraft: Battle for Azeroth]]'' max level characters who have unlocked [[world quest]]s in [[Kul Tiras]] and [[Zandalar]]. It takes place in three chapters, tracked in the achievement [[Sunken Ambitions]] (Alliance)/[[Unfathomable]] (Horde).
