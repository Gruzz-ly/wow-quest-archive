==Objectives==
Upgrade a piece of Benthic armor through [[Voidbinder Sturzah]].
*Identify a piece of Benthic Armor (Optional)

==Description==
That Benthic armor you carry has been lost beneath the tides for too long.

There is still power within it.

Voidbinder Sturzah may be able to provide insight.

Take your find to our intrepid [[ethereal]] friend, and see what he can do with it.
