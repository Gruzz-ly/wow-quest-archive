==Objectives==
Travel to [[Mezzamere]] with [[Blademaster Okani|Okani]].
*Follow Okani
*Speak with [[Jaina Proudmoore|Lady Jaina Proudmoore]] at Mezzamere.

==Description==
[[Ankoan|My people]] know many secrets about [[Nazjatar|this place]]. If you prove yourselves worthy allies, you will be privy to them.

First, we must regroup in Mezzamere. There, we at least have a foothold against the tide.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Money]]
* [[XP]]
* +150 reputation with [[Waveblade Ankoan]]

==Completion==
So far, these ankoan have proven themselves formidable allies.
