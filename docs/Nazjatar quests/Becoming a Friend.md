==Objectives==
Use the Calling Conch, choose an ally, and complete 3 ally quests.
* Use the [[Calling Conch]]
* Choose an [[Combat allies|ally]]
* Ally quests complete (3)

==Description==
Some of our bravest [[Gilgoblin|kelfin]] have volunteered to join you on your adventures, should you ever need a friend.

You will find they each have their own special interests and they will mark your map with different opportunities every day.

Use the conch when you're ready, choose who you would like to join you, and explore [[Nazjatar]]!

Choose wisely! You may only select one friend per day.
