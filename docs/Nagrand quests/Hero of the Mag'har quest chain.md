# Hero of the Mag'har quest chain

'''Hero of the Mag'har''' is a quest line available to the [[Horde]] only. 

==Summary==
Due to the players efforts in aiding the Mag'har where he could not [[Garrosh Hellscream]] doubts himself and suggests that the player should lead instead of him. Disheartened by this remark [[Greatmother Geyah]] ends up remarking that the player reminds her of her [[Durotan|son]]. This in turn leads the stunned adventurer to reveal that her [[Thrall|grandson]] is [[Warchief]] of the [[Horde]].

After informing Thrall of the situation he arrives to visit his grandmother. During this time he shows Garrosh [[Grom Hellscream|his father's]] last battle against [[Mannoroth]] causing him to become confident in himself and escape his doubt.

== Rewards ==
Completing this lengthy quest chain (and the prerequisites) will yield the following rewards:
* 340,409 XP (or a {{cost|204|84}} compensation at level 80)
* 12,210 [[Reputation]] with [[Mag'har (faction)|The Mag'har]] (Not including mobs that were killed and gave reputation)
* 1,200 [[Reputation]] with [[The Sha'tar]] (Not including mobs that were killed and gave reputation)
* {{cost|40|10}}
* A lengthy cut scene that explains some key points of [[Orc]]ish lore.
* Several items
