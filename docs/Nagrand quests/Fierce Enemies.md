'''Fierce Enemies''' is a repeatable quest that helps raise your reputation with the [[Kurenai]]. Requires at least Friendly reputation to start with.

==Objectives==
Warden Moi'bff Jill at [[Telaar]] wants you to obtain 10 Obsidian Warbeads from the various [[ogre]]s that inhabit [[Nagrand]].
*[[Obsidian Warbeads]] (10)

==Description==
If you seek to prove your loyalty to us, there is no better way than to fight against our enemies.

Of these, there are none that can match the ogres in strength and fierceness. Bring me the warbeads they wear around their necks as proof of their demise and I'll let the others know of your prowess.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*11650 [[XP]] (or {{cost|6|99|}} at max level)
*+500 [[reputation]] with [[Kurenai]]
