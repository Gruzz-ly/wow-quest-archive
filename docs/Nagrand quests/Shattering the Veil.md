==Objectives==
[[Gordawg]] at the [[Throne of the Elements]] in [[Nagrand]] has asked that you slay 30 Minions of [[Gurok the Usurper|Gurok]].
*[[Minion of Gurok]] slain (30)
*[[Gordawg's Boulder]] (provided)

==Description==
<Gordawg breaks off a piece of ebon rock from his back.>

You are granted a piece of the fury of the earth. Take the boulder of Gordawg and hurl it at the poison rock. Shatter their shells to discover the truth. Destroy the minions of the usurper and return to Gordawg with the name.

You find the shattered elements at the edge of the world. Southwest.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*17450 [[XP]] (or {{cost|10|47|}} at max level)

==Progress==
Gordawg wants the boulder returned.

==Completion==
GUROK! Gurok betrayed the elements!

==Notes==
At coords 48,75 you will find Shattered Rumblers. Throw the boulder at them by targettng them and then right clicking the boulder. The Rumbler will break up in little Minion of Gurok. Kill them. Repeat this until you killed 30.
