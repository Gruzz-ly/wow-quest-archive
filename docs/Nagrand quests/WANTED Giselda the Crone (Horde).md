==Objectives==
Slay [[Giselda the Crone]] and any combination of 15 Kil'sorrow Agents. Return to [[Warden Bullrok]] at [[Garadar]] should you complete this task. 
*[[Giselda the Crone]]  slain
*Kil'sorrow Agent slain (10)

==Description==
Wanted: Dead or Alive

Giselda the Crone of Kil'sorrow and her lackeys for murder, ritual sacrifice, communing with the Legion, kidnapping, and burglary.

Known to congregate at [[Kil'sorrow Fortress]] in southeastern [[Nagrand]], near the border of Terokkar.

Large gold reward to be dispensed by Warden Bullrok at Garadar to anyone able to bring Giselda to justice.
