==Objectives==
Seek out Wrathion outside the Karazhan Catacombs.
* Seek out [[Wrathion]] outside [[Karazhan Catacombs]]
* [[Torn Journal Page 25]]

==Description==
I held out as long as I could, but... the whispers were too strong.

You must hurry to [[Karazhan]]. The Black Prince said he intended to study the artifacts stored within its catacombs.

Please, do not allow my failure to bring about his doom!

==Notes==
On accept:
:{{text|say|Blacktalon Watcher|I failed... They know where to find him. Hurry!}}

Fly south to [[Deadwind Pass]]. Look below the back entrance to [[Karazhan]]. Bodies of [[Sleepless Operative]]s and a [[Slain Blacktalon Lookout]] can be found at the entrance. Interact with the lookout:
:<The body is still warm to the touch. He has not been dead for long.>
{{gossip|<Search the body for more journal pages.>}}

[[Torn Journal Page 25]] turns up. Read it, then interact with the body again to continue.
