This '''Decor Treasure Hunt''' takes place on [[Founder's Point]].

==Objectives==
Solve the riddle and find the treasure.
* Treasure Found

==Description==
Are you ready for today's challenge? I've hidden a piece from my collection here in the neighborhood. To find it, you must merely solve this simple riddle:

Cool waters, unfed.<br/>
Still against blue sky and green.<br/>
Sheltered by the cliff.

Good luck!

*{{cost|4|32|50}}
*[[Bel'ameth Beam Platform]]

==Completion==
Well done!

==Notes==
The [[Treasure Location]] is at the isolated pond west of the [[Entrance Portal]], past the river. It sits beneath a broken tree.
