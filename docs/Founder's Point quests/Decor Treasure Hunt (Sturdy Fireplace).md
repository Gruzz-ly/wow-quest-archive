This '''Decor Treasure Hunt''' takes place on [[Founder's Point]].

==Objectives==
Solve the riddle and find the treasure.
*Treasure Found

==Description==
Are you ready for today's challenge? I've hidden a piece from my collection here in the neighborhood. To find it, you must merely solve this simple riddle:

Beyond smugglers cove.<br/>
Wrecks hide a sunken saloon.<br/>
Look for the good stuff.

Good luck!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|4|32|50}}
* [[Sturdy Fireplace]]

==Completion==
Well done!

==Notes==
Past the end of the dock at [[Breakwater Bight]] on the island's southern shore, there's an underwater [[tortollan]] bar. The [[Treasure Location]] is at the back of the bar, behind a net where various drinks hang.
