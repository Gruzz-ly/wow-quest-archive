==Objectives==
Gather 10 Wildhammer Food Stores from the [[Thundermar Ruins]].
*[[Wildhammer Food Stores]] x10

==Description==
Refugees are still pouring into [[Thundermar]] from the fighting, and I'm hungry! I mean them. The refugees, they're hungry.

Get me some food from the Thundermar Ruins! Scour the ruins to the south, east, and southwest for any stores that still remain. Especially them big hocks of ham. I love those! Er, the refugees do.

And see if you can find some of them little cocktail weenies.
