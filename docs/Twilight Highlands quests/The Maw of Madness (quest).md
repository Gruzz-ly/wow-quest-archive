== Description ==
I can't believe it, <name>. Me own son - me own flesh and blood! Throwing his lot in with those fiends from the [[Twilight's Hammer]]. Did you see what he became when he tried to destroy the wedding? Oh ... why? Why?

It's that beast, <name>. The enormous mouth emerging from the earth to our west. "[[The Maw of Madness]]!" My son said it spoke to him, but I thought it was just nightmares.

The [[Earthen Ring]] is trying to destroy it. Go to Earthcaller Torunscar, west of here, and see how you can help!
