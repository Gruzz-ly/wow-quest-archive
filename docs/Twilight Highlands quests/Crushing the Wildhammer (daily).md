== Objectives ==
Kill [[Wildhammer Warbrand]]s to collect 8 Wildhammer Insignias. Named characters within the Thundermar Ruins will also carry insignias.
* [[Wildhammer Insignia]] (8)

== Description ==
The [[Wildhammer clan|Wildhammer]] grow bold with their new Alliance allies. They dare press across the [[Verrall River|river]]. Drive them back!

Seek out and crush the Wildhammer warbrands who fight in the riverbed and within the [[Thundermar Ruins]] along the north bank. The best among them carry insignias - gather them for me to prove your allegiance to the Dragonmaw cause.
