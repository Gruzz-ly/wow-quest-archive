All able-bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to High Overlord Saurfang in Grommash Hold.

High Overlord Saurfang requires your help to prepare for the glorious assault on the [[Twilight Highlands]] and our dual enemies, the [[Alliance]] and [[Twilight's Hammer]].

Meet with him in Grommash Hold at the center of Orgrimmar's [[Valley of Strength]].
