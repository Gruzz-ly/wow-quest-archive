'''Precious Roots''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
Collect 8 Residual Anima from around [[Vault of Secrets|the Vault]] in [[Korthia]].
*[[Residual Anima]] (8)

==Description==
Korthia. A sister forest, stricken.

But in the throes of destruction, there is still hope. 

Gather the [[anima]], and build anew.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+75 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Sow growth.

==Completion==
Precious anima that will be shared. A sacrifice, honored.

==Notes==
[[Seeping Cone]] doodads are on the ground all around the [[Seeker's Quorum]] and the [[Vault of Secrets]]. To get to the vault, take the [[Flayedwing Transporter]] at {{co|60.8|28.5|Korthia}} at the Necrolord camp at [[Keeper's Respite]].
