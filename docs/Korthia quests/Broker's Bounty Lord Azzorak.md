'''Broker's Bounty: Lord Azzorak''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Lord Azzorak.jpg|Lord Azzorak}}
Defeat Lord Azzorak.
*[[Lord Azzorak]] slain {{co|43.9|67.5|Korthia}}
Suggested Players [3]

==Description==
A powerful [[nathrezim|dreadlord]] named Lord Azzorak is hampering the efforts of [[Cartel Xy|the Cartel]] in [[Korthia]].

Anyone who exterminates this evil will be rewarded. 

Use caution, Azzorak is a powerful foe and you may require several allies to slay him.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+200 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Has Lord Azzorak been slain?

==Completion==
Thanks to you, Lord Azzorak will no longer hinder Cartel Xy here in Korthia. 

And you have my personal thanks as well, Maw Walker. I see many great things in our future.

Please accept this reward as a token of our gratitude.

==Notes==
[[Lord Azzorak]] is at the [[Vault of Secrets]]. To get there, take the [[Flayedwing Transporter]] at {{co|60.8|28.5|Korthia}} at the Necrolord camp at [[Keeper's Respite]].
