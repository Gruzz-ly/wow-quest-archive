'''Devoured Anima''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
Reclaim 12 Devoured Anima from [[devourer]]s in the [[Estuary of Awakening]].
*[[Devoured Anima (Korthia)]] recovered (12)

==Description==
[[Sika]] has idea!

Devourers... devour. They eat anything! So they eat anima, too.

If we take anima from devourers, then that anima is ours!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+125 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Sika wonders what anima tastes like.

==Completion==
Thank you! Wow... you can't tell this was inside devourer!

Sika will get anima to those most in need.

==Notes==
Loot all the [[devourer]]s in the [[Estuary of Awakening]] and on the [[Windswept Aerie]] for their [[Devoured Anima (Korthia)]], including [[Colossal Worldeater]]s, [[Hungering Mite]]s, [[Ravenous Gorger]]s, and [[Ravenous Scavenger (Korthia)|Ravenous Scavengers]]. The devourers for this quest can also be found on the unnamed western floating island.
