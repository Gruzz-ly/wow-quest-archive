'''Continued Efforts: Sanctuary of Guidance''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
[[File:Anima Offering.jpg|thumb|Anima Offering]]
Clear the Sanctuary of Guidance of [[devourer]]s.
*[[Sanctuary of Guidance]] cleansed (0/100%)

==Description==
The [[attendant]]'s home has been destroyed by [[the Jailer]], but we can help them in this trying time.

Nearby is the Sanctuary of Guidance. They speak of it with reverence and great sadness. It is a place they go to pray and seek truth. It's currently infested with devourers.

Clear out the devourers, so they may once more seek solace in their rituals and prayers.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Have the devourers been cleared out of the Sanctuary of Guidance?

==Completion==
Thank you, Maw Walker. I am sure the attendants will feel great relief and joy at being able to return to their holy place.

==Notes==
Fill the bar by killing or interacting with:
*[[Mindseize Controller]] slain (7-8%)
*[[Melded Gorger]], [[Nimble Scavenger]] (4%)
*[[Anima Offering]] (2%)
*[[Hungering Mite]] (1%)
