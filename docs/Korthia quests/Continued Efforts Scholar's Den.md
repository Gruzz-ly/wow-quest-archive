'''Continued Efforts: Scholar&#39;s Den''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
Clear the [[mawsworn]] from the Scholar's Den.
*[[Scholar's Den]] cleared (0/100%)

==Description==
We cannot allow the mawsworn a foothold so close to [[Keeper's Respite|our own encampment]]. The [[attendant]]s say they keep this place hidden from eyes, but I will not rest easy while I can see them.

Enter the Scholar's Den and cleanse [[the Jailer]]'s forces from it.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Has the Scholar's Den been cleared of mawsworn?

==Completion==
You have performed admirably in this task, Maw Walker. We thank you for your aid.

==Notes==
Fill the bar by killing:
*[[Kroke the Tormented]], [[Tormented Demolisher]] (25%)
*[[Mawsworn Torturer]] (7-8%)
*[[Mawsworn Mutilator]], [[Mawsworn Corruptor]] (4%)
