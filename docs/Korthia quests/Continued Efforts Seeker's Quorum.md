'''Continued Efforts: Seeker&#39;s Quorum''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
Repel [[the Jailer]]'s [[Mawsworn|forces]] from Seeker's Quorum in [[Korthia]].
*[[Seeker's Quorum]] forces repelled (0/100%)

==Description==
The onslaught from Zovaal's forces continues to devastate these lands.

We must send them a message. Show them we will not back down, <name>.

The might of [[Ardenweald]] stands with you.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Has the task been completed?

==Completion==
Quite the show you put on, <name>.

That should buy us just enough time to sharpen our blades before our next battle.

*[[Shadecoat Ridgeback]] (6-7%)

*[[Empowered Ravager]] (5%)
*Mawsworn Weapons (4%)
