'''Broker's Bounty: Hungering Behemoth''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Hungering Behemoth.jpg|Hungering Behemoth}}
Retrieve a Substantial Amount of Devourer Blood from a [[Hungering Behemoth]].
*[[Substantial Amount of Devourer Blood]] {{co|38|48|Korthia}}
Suggested Players [3]

==Description==
New lands bring new opportunity. We will provide a quantity of valuables to any interested party in return for a substantial amount of devourer blood, preferably the goliath kind.

Faint-hearted collectors need not bother.

Please bring this sample to [[Ta'nasi]] in [[Keeper's Respite]].

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+200 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Have something to trade?

==Completion==
<[[Ta'nasi]] holds the sample up to the sky.>

An excellent specimen. This will do nicely.

As promised, here is your reward in trade.

==Notes==
Look specifically for the [[Hungering Behemoth]] units, not the [[Colossal Worldeater]]s, in the [[Estuary of Awakening]], between the [[Windswept Aerie]] and [[Hope's Ascent]] in southwestern [[Korthia]].
