'''They Could Be Anyone''' concludes the side quest chain of the same name as part of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]].

==Objectives==
Take the head of [[Azodius]] to [[Bolvar Fordragon]] in [[Keeper's Respite]].
*[[Head of Azodius]] (provided)

==Description==
<The still-warm head of the [[nathrezim|dreadlord]] Azodius grimaces at you.

The [[attendant]]s at Keeper's Respite should be told of [[Fatescribe Roh-Kalo|the Fatescribe's]] abduction.

No doubt Highlord Fordragon will want to hear of this unexpected threat.>
