'''Broker's Bounty: Ensydius the Defiler''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Ensydius the Defiler.jpg|Ensydius the Defiler}}
Defeat Ensydius the Defiler.
*[[Ensydius the Defiler]] slain {{co|60.5|51.4|Korthia}}
Suggested Players [3]

==Description==
A winged [[Mawsworn]] named Ensydius the Defiler is hampering the efforts of [[Cartel Xy|the Cartel]] in [[Korthia]].

Anyone who exterminates this evil will be rewarded. 

Use caution, Ensydius is a powerful foe and you may require several allies to defeat him.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+200 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Has Ensydius the Defiler been exterminated?

==Completion==
Thanks to you, Ensydius the Defiler will no longer hinder Cartel Xy here in Korthia. 

And you have my personal thanks as well, [[Maw Walker]]. I see many great things in our future.

Please accept this reward as a token of our gratitude.

==Notes==
Find Ensydius on the rise above the [[Path of Understanding]] on the southeastern part of the mainland.
