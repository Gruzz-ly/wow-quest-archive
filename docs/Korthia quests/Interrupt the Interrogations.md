'''Interrupt the Interrogations''' begins a side-quest chain in [[Korthia]], opening up after [[Charge of the Covenants]], as part of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]]. The chain unlocks the [[Archivists' Codex]] faction and the [[Cataloged Research]] for [[Reliquary Restoration]].

==Objectives==
{{Questportrait|Interrogator Tzench.jpg|[[Interrogator Tzench]]|This interrogator is as merciless as he is odious.}}

Rescue [[Archivist Roh-Senara]].
*[[Interrogator's Key (Korthia)]] {{co|60.8|52.7|Korthia}}
*Archivist Freed {{co|60.9|52.6|Korthia}}

==Description==
[[File:Interrupt the Interrogations - semitransparent rock.jpg|thumb|Semi-transparent rocks?]]
Shhh... quietly!

The invaders will kill me if they find me here, just as they have killed so many others!

<[[Scholar Roh-Suir|Roh-Suir]] points frantically in the direction of the [[Mawsworn]].>

They're torturing the Archivist!

You must find a way to rescue her. We cannot allow the Mawsworn to learn all that she knows!
