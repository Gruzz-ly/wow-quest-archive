[[Archivist Roh-Suir]] begins '''The Final Relics''' at Tier 5 with the [[Archivists' Codex]], unlocking the final three relics to find in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
Examine the missing relics within the Reliquary of Remembrance and purchase Research Report: Ancient Shrines.
*Missing relics examined (3)
*[[Research Report: Ancient Shrines]] purchased from [[Archivist Roh-Suir|Roh-Suir]]
*Research Report: Ancient Shrines used

==Description==
I have excellent news, <name>. I have uncovered the last of our missing relics! 

It may be difficult for you to locate them, but I've developed the proper research to aid you.

Please, review the last of our missing relics. We are so close to finding them all!

==Progress==
Have you seen the missing relics?

==Completion==
Thank you for your continued aid. The reliquary will be restored, thanks to your diligent work.

==Notes==
Before looking for the ghost items, purchase and use the [[Research Report: Ancient Shrines]] for 1000 [[Cataloged Research]] from Roh-Suir:
