'''Cipher of Understanding''' starts from the [[Cipher of Understanding (item)|Cipher of Understanding]] at {{co|29.0|54.2|Korthia}} inside [[Rift (Shadowlands)|The Rift]] in [[Korthia]]. Gain access to the Rift by using a [[Repaired Riftkey]] at any nearby [[Rift Portal]], then navigate to the top of the [[Windswept Aerie]] to find the cipher, which starts this quest.

==Objectives==
[[File:Cipher of Understanding - in rift.jpg|thumb|[[Cipher of Understanding (item)|Cipher of Understanding]]]]
Bring the Cipher of Understanding to [[Archivist Roh-Suir]] in the [[Reliquary of Remembrance]].
*[[Cipher of Understanding (item)]] (provided)
