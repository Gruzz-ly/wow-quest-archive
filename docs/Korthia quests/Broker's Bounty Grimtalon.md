'''Broker&#39;s Bounty: Grimtalon''' is a [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|potential daily quest]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Grimtalon.jpg|Grimtalon}}
Retrieve a Mature Ossified Beak from [[Grimtalon]].
*[[Mature Ossified Beak]] {{co|31.1|59.5|Korthia}}
Suggested players: [3]

==Description==
New lands bring new opportunity. To any interested party, we will provide a quantity of valuables in return for an intact boney beak from the beast known as Grimtalon.

Faint-hearted collectors need not bother.

Please bring this sample to [[Ta'nasi]] in [[Keeper's Respite]].

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +200 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
Have something to trade?

==Completion==
<Ta'nasi knocks the sturdy beak against her palm, listening to the sound.>

An excellent specimen. This will do nicely.

And as promised, here is your reward in trade.

==Notes==
Find [[Grimtalon]] high up in the [[Windswept Aerie]].
