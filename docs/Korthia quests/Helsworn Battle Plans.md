'''Helsworn Battle Plans''' starts from the [[Helsworn Battle Plans (item)]] item dropped by mobs in [[Desmotaeron]] and [[Helgarde Keep]] of the [[Maw]]. Turn in the plans once a week for 200 points of standing with [[Death's Advance]].

==Objectives==
Deliver Helsworn Battle Plans to [[Duchess Mynx]] in [[Keeper's Respite]].
*[[Helsworn Battle Plans (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
<These battle plans detail possible movements into [[Korthia]], and include detailed reports of the defensive capabilities of the [[Death's Advance]] in the region.

Duchess Mynx in Keeper's Respite would be able to spread this information rapidly to those who need it.>

==Rewards==

*+200 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]
