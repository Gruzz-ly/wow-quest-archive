'''In Every Dark Corner''' is a [[Horde]]-only quest that takes place during the [[Faction Assault on Drustvar]]. It pops up automatically upon entering [[Drustvar]] when the assault is active.

==Objectives==
Lead the [[Horde]] assault into [[Drustvar]].
* [[Alliance]] targets attacked in Drustvar (4)
* Complete [[Rokhan]]'s ritual
* Alliance army crushed
* [[Vindicator Caeduum]] slain

==Description==
The Alliance's hold on Drustvar is weak. We will conquer it for the Horde!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 24,700 - 26,800 [[XP]]
* +1000 [[reputation]] with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]
*[[Azerite Armor Cache]]
*[[Honorbound Equipment Cache]]
*5x [[Honorbound Service Medal]]
