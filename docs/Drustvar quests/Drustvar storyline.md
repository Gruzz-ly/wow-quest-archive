==Side chains==
===Carver's Harbor===
After completing [[The Final Effigy]], a breadcrumb to [[Carver's Harbor]] opens up in [[Fallhaven]].

{{Carver's Harbor}}

===North Pass Caverns===
After completing [[The Final Effigy]], a breadcrumb to the [[North Pass Caverns]] opens up in [[Fallhaven]].

{{The North Pass Caverns}}

===Glenbrook Homestead===
Just south of Fallhaven on the way to Hangman's Point for [[The Waycrest Daughter]], [[Abby Lewis]] starts a short side quest in [[Glenbrook Homestead]].

{{Tea Party}}

===Barrowknoll Cemetery===
On the way to [[Hangman's Point]] from [[Fallhaven]] for [[The Waycrest Daughter]], [[Caretaker Allen]] starts a short side quest in [[Barrowknoll Cemetery]].

{{Barrowknoll Cemetery}}

===Mudfish Lodge===
On the way to the [[Timbered Strand]] from [[Hangman's Point]] for [[The Curse of Fletcher's Hollow]], [[Art Hughie]] starts some quests at [[Mudfish Lodge]].

{{Mudfish Lodge}}
