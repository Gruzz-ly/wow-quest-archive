==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Thorn Sutton.jpg|The Crimson Cutthroats|Slay The Crimson Cutthroats}}
Slay the [[Crimson Cutthroats]] near [[Falconhurst]].
* [[Mareen Sherwood]] slain
* [[Wallis Crowder]] slain
* [[Thorn Sutton]] slain
* Suggested players: 3

==Description==
For numerous and sinister crimes against the [[Katherine Proudmoore|Lord Admiral]]: smuggling, forgery, sailing under false colors, looting, poaching, brigandage, sacking, pillaging, vandalism, impersonating [[Kul Tiran fleet|Kul Tiran Naval]] officers, arson, kidnapping, torture, piracy, perjury, theft and ransacking multiple mead shipments.

A reward is placed on the heads of The Crimson Cutthroats to be paid in full upon proof of their demise.
