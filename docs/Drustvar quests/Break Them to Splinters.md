'''Break Them to Splinters''' is a bonus objective offered in and around [[Fletcher's Hollow]] after completing [[Hope They Can't Swim]] on the [[Drustvar storyline]]. 

==Objectives==
*Reclaim [[Fletcher's Hollow]] (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|39|60|}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]

==Notes==
To fill up the bar, kill:
* [[Curseformed Familiar]] (2%)
* [[Grotesque Thrall]] (2%)
* [[Curseformed Ravager]] (4%)

And interact with:
*[[Ensorcelled Pig Heart]] destroyed (2%)
*[[Captured Villager]] freed (4%)
*[[Seething Cache]] opened (14%)
