==Objectives==
Assist [[Nathanos Blightcaller]] in retrieving [[Marshal M. Valentine|Valentine's]] body.
* [[Crypt Door]] opened
* [[Echo of Marshal M. Valentine]] slain
* Crypt searched
* Valentine's location uncovered
* [[Valentine's Log]]

==Description==
We have the [[Valentine's Key|key]]. We found the crypt.

Next, Valentine's body shall be ours.

No need to wait for [[Lilian Voss|Lillian]] and [[Captain Amalia Stone|Stone]]. Let's finish this now.
