'''Wedding Crashers''' is a [[world quest]] offered at [[Whitegrove Chapel]] in northwest [[Drustvar]].

==Objectives==
* [[Terrified Guest (Drustvar)|Terrified Guests]] rescued (12)
* [[Wedding Gift]]s collected (6)
* Invader slain  (10)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Lucille Waycrest]]|What was supposed to be a joyous celebration has been ruined! Vanquish the monsters responsible and save the wedding guests!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Order of Embers]]

{{wqr|bfa}}

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Lucille Waycrest|So much tragedy. Perhaps we should cease having weddings here.}}
