'''Taptaf the Pig!''' is initiated after killing [[Idej the Wise]]. Taptaf will jump down from his crate and walk around the campfire to offer the quest.

==Completion==
Taptaf looks up at you wide-eyed, after seeing his previous master slain. He seems eager to follow you now.
