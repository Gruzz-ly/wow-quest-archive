==Description==
<The bulletin that [[Cyril White|Mayor White]] was posting at the time he was struck by the spell indicates that there have been some strange happenings in the town lately. 

It is likely these events are connected to whatever cast this spell on the village.

Perhaps there might be records from other villagers that could shed some light on the source of this magic.>
