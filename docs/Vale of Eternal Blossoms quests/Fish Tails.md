==Objectives==
Bring Fish Tails to [[Lorewalker Cho]] at the [[Seat of Knowledge]].
*[[Fish Tales (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
Dear <name>,

I have thoroughly enjoyed reading your research in to the [[jinyu]]. A fascinating people and quite clever too.

If you visit us at the Seat of Knowledge in [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms]] we can add this contribution to your personal collection.

Thank you for your diligence!

Sincerely,

Lorewalker Cho

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50}}
* 236000 [[XP]]
* +2850 [[reputation]] with [[The Lorewalkers]]

==Completion==
This is a real treat. I always did enjoy the jinyu. Such a peaceful people with a real appreciation for wisdom.

We will add this knowledge to your collection. If you wish me to narrate this for you, simply point me at the correct volume in your Private Collection.
