==Objectives==
Drive back the [[N'Zoth Assault]] in the [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms]].
*Cleanse [[N'Zoth]]'s corruption (0-100%)
* [[Vil'raxx]] slain
*Return to [[Lorewalker Cho]] at the [[Seat of Knowledge]].

==Description==
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|This cannot be! The [[Black Empire]] itself has invaded the Vale! Act swiftly, before all is lost!}}

You will receive:
* 26,800 [[XP]]
* [[Cache of the Black Empire]]
* [[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750 [[Azerite (resource)]])
