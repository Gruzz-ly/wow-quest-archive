==Objectives==
Bring Hozen in the Mist to [[Lorewalker Cho]] at the [[Seat of Knowledge]].
*[[Hozen in the Mist (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
Dear <name>,

Your research into the [[hozen]] has proven quite enlightening.

If you visit us at the Seat of Knowledge in [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms]] we can add this contribution to your personal collection.

Thank you for your diligence!

Sincerely,

Lorewalker Cho

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50}}
* 236000 [[XP]]
* +2850 [[reputation]] with [[The Lorewalkers]]

==Completion==
Hozen are, by their very nature, a rambunctious lot.

We will add this knowledge to your collection. If you wish me to narrate this for you, simply point at the correct volume in your Private Collection.

==Notes==
Upon completion of the [[Hozen in the Mist]] Pandaria [[exploration achievement]], [[Lorewalker Cho]] sends a letter via the in-game mail system. The letter has an item attached that starts this quest. See the achievement for more details.

:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|To teach him a lesson, the father dragged the cub out to the forest, to watch how the hozen live. "Do you see what the hozen are like?" he asked, "Do you want to live like that?"}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|The hozen are quite lively indeed. A short-lived race, few hozen survive past their 20th birthday. Their leaders are all 14 or 15. How mature were you at 14 years old? Mediate on that!}}

:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|"I learned that we work and toil on our little farm, but that the hozen have the whole world as their garden." the cub said. :{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|And the cub concluded, "I should very much like to live like a hozen!"}}
