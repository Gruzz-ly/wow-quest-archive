==Objectives==
Bring The Seven Burdens of Shaohao to [[Lorewalker Cho]] at the [[Seat of Knowledge]].
*[[The Seven Burdens of Shaohao (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
Dear <name>,

It would seem you have discovered the full tale of [[Shaohao]], the last emperor of Pandaria.

If you visit us at the Seat of Knowledge in [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms]] we can add this contribution to your personal collection.

Thank you for your diligence!

Sincerely,

Lorewalker Cho

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50}}
* 236000 [[XP]]
* +2850 [[reputation]] with [[The Lorewalkers]]

==Completion==
This is a story I have long sought to uncover.  Ever since the [[Sha]] was released I have felt in my belly that this tale was at the heart of it all.

We will add this knowledge to your collection.  If you wish me to narrate this for you, simply point at the correct volume in your Private Collection.

==Notes==
[[File:Emperor Shaohao.jpg|thumb|Emperor Shaohao.]]
Upon completion of the [[The Seven Burdens of Shaohao]] Pandaria [[exploration achievement]], [[Lorewalker Cho]] sends a letter via the in-game mail system. The letter has an item attached that starts this quest. See the achievement for more details.

:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Ah! You wish to learn of our last and greatest emperor, [[Emperor Shaohao|Shaohao]]? My friend, I do not have the time here to recount all of his adventures, but his actions ten thousand years ago resonate to this very day.}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|We know that he purged his [[Sha of Doubt|doubts]] in the [[Jade Forest]]. That he cleansed himself of [[Sha of Despair|despair]] in the [[Krasarang Wilds]]. That he overcame his [[Sha of Fear|fear]] in [[Townlong Steppes]]. And that he defeated [[Sha of Anger|anger]], [[Sha of Hatred|hatred]], and [[Sha of Violence|violence]] high in the mountains of [[Kun-Lai Summit|Kun-Lai]].}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|This is known; and there are temples to mark these most sacred of places.}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|But I want you to imagine standing in this very [[Vale of Eternal Blossoms|valley]] on the day he returned from his journey. Imagine being here when the gates opened for him. By then our emperor was a creature of pure light: No doubts, no fears, no hatred...}}
:''As Cho speaks, various ghostly figures appear near him to represent his words. Statues of each of the four [[August Celestial]]s to represent their temples, great gates for the entrance of the Vale, blossoming trees when Shaohao arrives...''
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|It is said that all the trees in the vale blossomed as he entered, and continue to blossom to this day.}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Would that we could all live like our most sacred emperor. That we could put aside all our burdens and exist in harmony with the cosmos. THAT, I believe, was his message to us... before he became one with the land, and hid [[Pandaria]] away from the terror of the [[Great Sundering|Sundering]].}}
:''Shaohao's ghostly figure starts praying and then disappears.''
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Thank you, dear emperor.}}
