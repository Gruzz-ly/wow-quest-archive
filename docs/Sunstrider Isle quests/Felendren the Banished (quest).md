'''Felendren the Banished''' is part of the Reclaiming Sunstrider Isle [[quest chain]] and a [[blood elf]] only quest.

== Objectives ==
Kill 8 Arcane Wraiths and 2 Tainted Arcane Wraiths, as well as [[Felendren the Banished]]; they are located in the [[Falthrien Academy]]. Bring Felendren's Head to [[Lanthan Perilon]] on [[Sunstrider Isle]].
*[[Arcane Wraith]] slain (8)
*[[Tainted Arcane Wraith]] slain (2)
*[[Felendren's Head]]

== Description ==
A betrayer of our people resides atop Falthrien Academy to the southwest.

Felendren is his name, and he was banished from blood elf lands for failing to heed the warnings of our teachers and elders. He is the worst of our society, as he lives only to feed his insatiable magical addictions. He refused to learn control; he is a shell of his former self - one of the [[Wretched]] - and he is a threat to the [[Sunspire]].

Destroy him and the wraiths he uses as minions.
