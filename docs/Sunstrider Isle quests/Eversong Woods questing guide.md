# Eversong Woods questing guide

{{tocright}}
This '''questing guide for the zone of Eversong Woods''' details an efficient method to obtain and complete the quests in [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]].

For a list of all Eversong Woods quests in a sortable table, see [[Eversong Woods quests]].

[[File:Icon-warning-22x22.png|Note]] '''Note''': The questing guides are not meant for power-leveling purposes. Rather, they are meant to show players the most efficient way to complete all quests available for a particular zone. If you know of a better order or method to accomplish this, your input is welcome (in fact, greatly desired). For major changes, feel free to edit, but make a note in the '''[[Talk:Eversong Woods questing guide|talk page]]''' as to your reasoning.

=={{Horde_Icon}} Sunstrider Isle (Level 1 - 5)==
[[Sunstrider Isle]] is the starting zone for new [[Blood elf|blood elves]], and is located north west of [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]]. The quests in this area are not available to players of other races. If you are new to the game, check out the [[Newbie Guide]] to get started, or just dive in - the game has in-game hints and the opening quests are very straight-forward, just relax and have fun!

*Begin by talking to the first [[blood elf]] you see, [[Magistrix Erona]], to get the quest [[Reclaiming Sunstrider Isle]].
*Head east or west to a [[Burning Crystal]] and kill 8 [[Mana Wyrm]]s.
*Return to Magistrix Erona and turn in the quest. Then get [[Unfortunate Measures]] and your [[Class Training]] quest.
*Head to the [[Sunspire]] to sell your loot and locate your class trainer. At your class trainer complete your class quest and receive your first training.
**{{ClassIcon|Warlock|Small}} '''Warlocks''' also pick up [[Windows to the Source]]
*Go to [[Well Watcher Solanian]] at the upper level of the spire to start [[Solanian's Belongings]] and [[The Shrine of Dath'Remar (quest)]].
*Pick up two more quests from quest givers around the Sunspire:
**[[A Fistful of Slivers]] from {{NPC|icon=BloodElf Male|Horde|Arcanist Ithanas}}
**[[Thirst Unending]] from {{NPC|icon=BloodElf Male|Horde|Arcanist Helion}}

=={{Horde Icon}} Eversong Woods (Level 5 - 10)==
[[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]] is the main zone for beginning blood elves, once they graduate from [[Sunstrider Isle]]. To get here, head over the bridge on the south-east corner of Sunstrider Isle.

Other zone options for this level include [[Durotar]], [[Mulgore]] and [[Tirisfal Glades]].

*If you're close to leveling, explore the roads to the north and east, [[Skulking Row]] and [[Feth's Way]]. The experience from discovery should set you over the edge, opening up more abilities for you to learn before heading out.

*Climb to the top of the anchorage tower and defeat {{npc|combat|Aldaron the Reckless}}. Don't forget to loot his head.

**{{ClassIcon|Hunter|Small}} '''Hunters''' should tame their new pets (Dragonhawk or Lynx for now) on the way.
