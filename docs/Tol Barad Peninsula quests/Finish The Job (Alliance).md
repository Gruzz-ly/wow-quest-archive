==Objectives==
Kill 5 Restless Infantry at the Restless Front. You can find the front on the west side of the island, just before Forgotten Hill.
* [[Restless Infantry]] slain (5)

==Description==
We have been trying to collect useful scraps from the front all week. Every time we gather a batch to bring back to camp, we get assaulted by the restless infantry in the area. Can you head over there and clear them out?

==Completion==
