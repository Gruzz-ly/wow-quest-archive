==Description==
Foreman Wellson has been a pain in our side since we got here. His shipyard holds a large amount of supplies that would be extremely useful to us... if we could get to them.

He keeps a close watch over his minions and makes it very difficult for us to get a foothold on the shipyard. I need you to take out Wellson for good this time. Be careful <name>, we have been receiving reports of filthy [[Horde]] all around the area.
