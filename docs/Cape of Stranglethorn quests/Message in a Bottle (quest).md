'''Message in a Bottle''' is a quest in Stranglethorn Vale  to find the author of a message.

==Objectives==
Find the author of the message at [[Jaguero Isle]] in the [[Cape of Stranglethorn]].

==Description==
Inside the bottle you find a note which reads, in fairly good handwriting:

<To any that can aid me->

<Please, my need for help is dire. A great beast holds me captive on an island in the South Seas.>

<The stars at night are foreign to me and obscured by the jungle trees, but on the clearest days I see shoreline to the northwest, and what seem to be large [[Sailing ship|sailing ships]] to the southwest.>

<nowiki><</nowiki>I beg of you, my would-be savior, please help me.>

==Rewards==

*2000 [[XP]]

==Completion==

==Notes==
[[Princess Poobah]], a [[Tauren]] female on [[Jaguero Isle]] off the south-east coast of [[Stranglethorn Vale]] is the author of the message. {{co|59.4|79|Cape of Stranglethorn}}
