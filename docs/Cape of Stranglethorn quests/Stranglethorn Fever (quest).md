==Notes==
You are directed to [[Witch Doctor Unbagwa]], in the [[Spirit Den]] in [[Stranglethorn Vale]].  The witch doctor will direct you to [[The Heart of Mokk|give him a Gorilla Fang]], before he will summon [[Mokk the Savage]].

The nearby [[Elder Mistvale Gorilla]]s drop {{loot|common|Gorilla Fang|Gorilla fangs}}. ''Prior to [[patch 2.3.0]], this quest required 10 Gorilla Fangs. The quest would also summon waves of 3, 4, and 5 gorillas, which made it near impossible for most classes to solo. This was also changed to 3 waves of only 1 gorilla each.''
