==Description==

The [[Explorers' League|Explorer's League]] expedition needs your help to safeguard its site and findings from marauding [[ogre]]s. Travel south from [[Northern Stranglethorn Vale]] and follow the road to the Explorer's League Digsite. 

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
