This is one of a few quests obtainable with the [[Bloodsail Buccaneers]] faction, located in the southern parts of [[Stranglethorn Vale]]. This quest is given by the leader of the [[Bloodsail Buccaneers]], [[Fleet Master Firallon]], and requires a minimum reputation of neutral or higher.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Fleet Master Firallon]].

== Notes ==
The [[quest]] can be picked up at [[Fleet Master Firallon]] only when you have reached neutral or higher [[reputation]] with the [[Bloodsail Buccaneers]]. The objective of the quest is simply to speak with Firallon, and thus involves no killing [[mob]]s or gathering [[item]]s.

== Completion ==
Yarrr... if ye be keen on enjoying the company of us experienced sea dogs, you'd best belay dressing like a blaggard!  Here, put on something proper 'fore I keelhaul yer hide.

These'll fit ye better than they ever fit that old dog, [[Red-Eyed Ben]].  Lost him in a devil of a storm at sea.

Wear 'em with pride, mate!

== Rewards ==
After completing this quest you will be given [[Buccaneer's Uniform]]:
