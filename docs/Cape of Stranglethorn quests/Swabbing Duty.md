==Objectives==
Pick up a Swabbie's Mop on the deck of the Riptide, then keep the deck clean for 2 minutes.  
*Swab the decks 

==Description==
I want the surface of the Riptide t'shine like a star ruby, Swabbie <name>. I keep a tight ship around here, and it's swabbies like you that make it happen!

Well, what're y'waitin' for, y'cockle-brained bilgerat? Head updecks and start swabbin'!
