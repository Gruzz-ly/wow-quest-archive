This quest is opened up by completing [[Vial of Visions]] for [[Drakuru]].

Completing this quest opens up [[Seared Scourge]] and [[Shimmercap Stew]], from Mack and Prigmon, respectively.

==Objectives==
[[Prigmon]] at [[Granite Springs]] wants you to bring a Mummified Troll Carcass to [[Mack Fearsen]] and place it in the fire.
* Mummified Carcass Burned

==Description==
Ugh.

Wrappin' up these unfortunate trolls be makin' my heart ache, but not so much as my back!

Help ol' Prigmon out and haul one of these carcasses over to Mack.

If we don't burn 'em they be walkin' again in no time.

You pick up a [[Scourged Troll Mummy]] by clicking on one of the bodies near [[Prigmon]].  Then go to the fire near Mack, and click on the item in your inventory.
