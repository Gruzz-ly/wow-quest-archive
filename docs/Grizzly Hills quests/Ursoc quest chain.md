Both the Alliance and Horde task their members to investigate the failure of the [[world tree]], [[Vordrassil]]. During this investigation it is uncovered the Furbolgs were able to resurrect [[Ursoc]] but the corruption from the tree drove them and Ursoc mad. Ursoc is soon slain with the aid of [[Tur Ragepaw]] and [[Purified Ashes of Vordrassil]] are used on Ursoc's Corpse in order to cleanse his spirit a task that is ultimately accomplished.
