==Objectives==
Complete the third of Ursol's trials.
* Speak with Ursol to begin the third trial
* Protect [[Lea Stonepaw]]

==Description==
You've proven to me your strength and your courage, but do you possess the ability to apply yourself when it matters most?

This final trial will reveal all.
