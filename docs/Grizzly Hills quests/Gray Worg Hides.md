==Objectives==
[[Hidetrader Jun'ik]] in [[Conquest Hold]] wants you to get 3 [[Gray Worg Hide]]s from [[Graymist Hunter]]s in the woods to the north.

==Description==
Let me tell you something, mon. When you get put in charge of outfittin' the armies of the [[Horde]], you go through a lot of hides!

[[Conqueror Krenna]], the she-wolf runnin' this place, she gave me a tight deadline. If I don't outfit her whole regiment by week's end she's gonna have me wrestling bears in the [[Conquest Pit|pit]].

Help me out and I'll pay you top price. I'll be needin' hides from graymist hunters.

You'll find them all around in pockets to the north, hunting stag. Remember, mon. Top price!
