==Objectives==
Pack 5 Shipments of Goods with [[Vashonir]] in the [[Central Encampment]].
* [[Shipment of Goods]] (5)

==Description==
Our stockpiles have taken a grave hit, <name>. 

We need to replenish our reserves as soon as possible, lest the enemy gain the upper hand. We will need far more supplies than we previously envisioned.

Once again, Vashonir can assist you with packing the shipments. Please return to me with five crates of goods.

[[Elune]], watch over you.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Gigantic Dreamseed]]
* [[Dragon Isles Supply Cache]] (100)
* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Dream Wardens]]

==Progress==
Be sure to ask Vashonir to help you prepare the shipments!

==Completion==
Thank you, <name>. We are ever in your debt.
