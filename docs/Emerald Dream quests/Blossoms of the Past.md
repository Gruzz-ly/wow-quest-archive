==Objectives==
Collect 3 Dreamseed Blossoms.
*[[Ageless Blossom]] (3)

==Description==
With your help, these seeds have grown into magnificent plants. However, the most exciting part is yet to come.

Each of these plant's blossoms contain a treasure trove of information about their history and the magic that binds them to the [[Amirdrassil|world tree]]. By studying these blossoms, we can better understand the lineage of each plant.

Gently collect the blossoms that have fallen and return them to me.
