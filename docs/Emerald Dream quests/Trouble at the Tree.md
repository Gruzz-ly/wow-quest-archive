'''Trouble at the Tree''' begins an optional side storyline in the [[Guardians of the Dream campaign]].

==Objectives==
Retrieve 6 Very Important Tree Supplies from [[Spriggan]]s.
*[[Very Important Tree Supplies]] (6)

==Description==
<Name>! [[Merithra]] needs your help.

Some nasty trickster [[spriggan]]s followed us into [[Emerald Dream|the Dream]] and now they're causing all sorts of havoc!

Like the most important thing of all, stealing top secret super important supplies.

It's urgent you stop them and get the supplies back.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|28|9}}
* 47,600 [[XP]]
* 10x [[Flightstones]]

==Progress==
If we don't get those supplies back from those pesky spriggans, we'll be so behind schedule!

==Completion==
What a relief!

Now that we got those very important supplies back, we can get back to work around here.

==Notes==
Upon accept, Lady Moonberrry casts "Spriggan Vision" on the player, revealing the [[Sneaky Spriggan]]s and [[Scheming Spriggan]]s around the encampment.
:{{Text|say|Lady Moonberry|This should help you see those pesky spriggans!}}

Kill them to loot the supplies.
