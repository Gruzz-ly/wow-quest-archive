==Objectives==
Talk to Shandris and help her back to the camp.
*Speak to [[Shandris Feathermoon|Shandris]]
*[[Anguished Dream Spirit|Spirit]] retreated

==Description==
It's all making sense now. I believe the missing dream spirit bonded with her--like [[Cyanthesa]] said.

It's making Shandris relive traumatic moments from her life.

It's not malicious, but the spirit and Shandris are feeding off each others' fears. We have to bring her back to the camp. [[Shae'lune]] might be able to help her.

Talk to her. I look like the enemy right now. But maybe she'll talk to you.

==Completion==
I thought that Shandris was venting her anger on me because I am [[Forsaken]].

I thought now that we had finished the task, she was done with me.

Wouldn't be the first time.

But I'm glad I was wrong. I have come to have... a great respect for her and her abilities.

We have to help her.
