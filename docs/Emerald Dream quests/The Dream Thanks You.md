'''The Dream Thanks You''' is the Renown 20 capstone quest with the [[Dream Wardens]] in the [[Emerald Dream (zone)|Emerald Dream]].

==Objectives==
Talk to [[Keeper Amrymn|Amrymn]] in the [[Central Encampment]].

==Description==
<[[Melsysra]] drops a slightly damp note into your hand.>

<Name>, your contributions to the dream are immeasurable. Please meet me at the Central Encampment, we have a reward we're excited to give you.

- Amrymn

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Enchanted Wyrm's Dreaming Crest]]

==Completion==
Thank you. For everything.
