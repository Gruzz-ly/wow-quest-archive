'''Blooming Dreamseeds''' is a weekly wrapper quest in the [[Emerald Dream (zone)|Emerald Dream]]. Plant three [[dreamseed]]s at [[Emerald Bounty|Emerald Bounties]] to fulfill the objective.

==Objectives==
Plant [[dreamseed]]s in the [[Emerald Dream (zone)|Emerald Dream]].
* Dreamseeds Planted (3)

==Description==
The Emerald Dream is about the harmony and balance of [[nature]]. For [[Amirdrassil]] to bloom, we need a rich variety of plants growing alongside the world tree. Plant seeds wherever you find fertile soil.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Seedbloom]]
*[[Harvested Dreamseed Cache]]

==Completion==
Thank you! It's in these many small acts that allows the Emerald Dream to flourish, and thus nourish Amirdrassil.
