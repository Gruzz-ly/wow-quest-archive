'''Drake's Dreaming Crest''' is given to players who reach various [[Renown]] levels with the [[Dream Wardens]]: Renown 8, 12, and 19.

==Completion==
Your assistance has been paramount in revitalizing [[Emerald Dream (zone)|the Dream]]. Please accept this as a reward for your efforts. Thank you for all your hard work, champion.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 15x [[Drake's Dreaming Crest]]
* 75x [[Flightstones]]
