==Objectives==
Slay Galok Wingslicer and his [[djaradin]] [[lieutenant]]s The Anvil and Moltenbinder.
* [[The Anvil (djaradin)|The Anvil]] slain {{co|56.9|28.3|Emerald Dream (zone)}}
* [[Moltenbinder]] slain {{co|54.3|25.5|Emerald Dream (zone)}}
* [[Galok Wingslicer]] slain

==Description==
Galok is here. All is going according to plan then.

We will put an end to him and any of his lieutenants.

==Rewards==
You will receive one of:
* [[Summer Soother's Hood]]
* [[Spirited Stag's Helm]]
* [[Fearless Faerie's Guise]]
* [[Chartreuse Champion's Warhelm]]

You will also receive:
* {{cost|28|9|}}
* 9,500 [[XP]]
* 10x [[Flightstones]]

==Progress==
We may have the stronger ground, but don't underestimate any of them.

==Completion==
We almost have Galok routed. Keep up the pressure!
