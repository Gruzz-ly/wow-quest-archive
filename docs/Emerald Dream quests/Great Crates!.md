==Objectives==
Pack 1 Shipment of Goods with [[Vashonir]] in the [[Central Encampment]].
* [[Shipment of Goods]]

==Description==
The assault on [[Emerald Dream|the Dream]] grows ever stronger.

We are in desperate need of trade goods and supplies to support the defense effort. Even just one crate could make the difference on the front line.

Vashonir can assist you with packing the shipments. Then bring the shipment to me.

[[Elune]] watch over you, defender of the Dream.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Plump Dreamseed]]
*[[Dream Wardens Commendation]] +250 [[reputation]] with [[Dream Wardens]]
* [[Dragon Isles Supply Cache]] (+100)

==Progress==
Vashonir will assist in preparing the shipments for you.

==Completion==
We are ever more grateful for your contributions.
