==Completion==
:{{Text|say|Merithra|These findings would have been lost for untold ages.}}

==Notes==
Location: [[Haven Cascades]], [[Emerald Dream (zone)|Emerald Dream]].

While swimming toward the bottom, interact with the Dragon Breaths from a distance to be pulled toward them, which gives the Arcane Breath buff and water breathing for a minute. Search the [[Loose Rubble]] to loot Handfuls of Rubble. Once they have been collected, head to {{co|50.88|28.74|Emerald Dream (zone)}} and hand them in:
:{{Gossip|quest=|<Hand over Handfuls of Rubble.>}}
:{{Text|say|Enaru Silverbark|A laminated scrap of paper...? Oh! It's a recipe for gingerbread cookies. Must have been an Azerothian specialty.}}
