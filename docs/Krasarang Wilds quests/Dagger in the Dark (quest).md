Enter the "[[Dagger in the Dark]]" scenario and report to [[Vol'jin]].
*Speak to General Nazgrim to queue for "Dagger in the Dark"

[[Shadow hunter|Shadow Hunter]] Vol'jin has joined a group of [[Kor'kron]] [[scout]]s on an important mission for [[Garrosh Hellscream|the warchief]].

Of course a proud member of the [[Horde]] such as yourself is welcome to go along, but be warned - it could get ugly.
