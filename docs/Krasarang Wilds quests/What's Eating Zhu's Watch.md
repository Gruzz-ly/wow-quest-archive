==Objectives==
Speak with the inhabitants in [[Zhu's Watch]] and find someone willing to talk to you.
*Speak with [[Despondent Warden of Zhu]]
*Speak with [[Sunni]] {{co|77.5|8.5|Krasarang Wilds}}
*Speak with [[Yun]] {{co|75.5|9.4|Krasarang Wilds}}
*Speak with [[Mei Barrelbottom|Mei]] {{co|75.8|7.6|Krasarang Wilds}}

==Description==
[[Pandaren]] always welcome travelers with open arms - and open kegs! But this town full of jerks!

Everyone so busy sulking, they won't talk to [[Ken-Ken]] at all!

Can you find someone in town who will talk to us? Maybe normal pandaren just hiding in the houses?
