==Objectives==
Enter the "[[A Little Patience]]" [[scenario]] and report to [[Varian Wrynn|King Varian Wrynn]].

==Description==
King Varian needs decisive adventurers to help him resolve a showdown between [[Tyrande Whisperwind]] and a detachment of [[Horde]] warriors holed up in the [[Temple of the Red Crane]].

Report to him immediately!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|9|92|50}}
*118000 [[XP]]
*+150 [[reputation]] with [[Operation: Shieldwall]]

==Completion==
Good to see you again, <name>.

We have a delicate situation on our hands here. We need to plan our next moves carefully.
