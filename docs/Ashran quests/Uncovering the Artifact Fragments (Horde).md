==Objectives==
[[Cymre Brightblade]] in [[Warspear Outpost]] wants you to go kill creatures in the outskirts of [[Ashran]] outside of the main road and bring back 5 [[Artifact Fragment]]s to [[Fura]].

==Description==
The Artifact Fragments seem to come from any creature we've found in the outskirts of Ashran. Go out and kill any you find and bring at least five to Fura the Mage near the campfire in Warspear.

==Completion==
Good work. If you happen to find anymore Artifact Fragments, bring them to me or the others in need of them to contribute to the war effort.

==Notes==
After exiting [[Warspear]], head east or west to [[Tower (Ashran)|the ogre tower]] or [[Stadium (Ashran)|the stadium]] and kill mobs. Alliance players who have Artifact Fragments on their bodies when they die will drop half of them as well.
