== Objectives ==
Board the ''[[Pincer X2]]'' from ''[[Voldrin's Hold]]'' and aid in securing [[Darkbreak Cove]].
*Board the ''Pincer X2'' once it arrives
*Secure Darkbreak Cove

== Description ==
We've located the beast that destroyed our ships. It makes its lair in Darkbreak Cove, a giant underwater cavern bordering the Abyssal Breach.

This time we're ready. The gnomes have been engineering a beauty for us back at [[Stormwind]], and it's packing firepower that'll make the fiend wet itself.

When the ''Pincer X2'' pulls up to dock beside the ship, make your way on board and help out however you can. We'll be right behind you.
