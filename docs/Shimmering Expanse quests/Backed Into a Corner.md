== Notes ==
Thirty naga must be killed. Players will gain initial aggro on the naga, but the friendly shaman will pull them off. After fifty are defeated, {{NPC||Fathom-Lord Zin'jatar}} will spawn:
:{{text|yell|Fathom-Lord Zin'jatar|Before the day is done, you will all be slaves and corpses!}}
:{{text|bossemote|Fathom-Lord Zin'jatar has entered the battle!}}
At 10%:
:{{text|yell|Fathom-Lord Zin'jatar|Enough! Have your cave, little shaman.}}
:''Zin'jatar summons a massive wave in the cave and disappears before being killed.''

Toshe continues the chain at the other end of the dry land in the cave.
