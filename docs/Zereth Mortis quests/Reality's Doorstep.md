'''Reality's Doorstep''' is an optional breadcrumb back to [[Haven]] offered after completing [[The Crown of Wills]] in the [[Zereth Mortis campaign]].

==Objectives==
Return to [[Zereth Mortis]].

==Description==
The time has come to follow [[Zovaal]] into the very heart of the [[Shadowlands]].

We must return to Zereth Mortis and finish this.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|57|40}}
* 1,000 [[XP]]
* +200 [[reputation]] with [[The Enlightened]]

==Completion==
There is more work yet to do here in Zereth Mortis before Zovaal's influence will be expunged from the Shadowlands.

==Notes==
When the player gets back to Haven, [[Taelia Fordragon|Taelia]] has joined her father's side and has a new "Stay awhile and listen" option.
