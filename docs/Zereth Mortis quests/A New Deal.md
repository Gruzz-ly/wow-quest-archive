==Objectives==
Collect 150 Protoplasmic Thread in [[Zereth Mortis]].
*[[Protoplasmic Thread]] (150)

==Description==
Ah, [[Maw Walker]]! Opportunities abound now that you have found a way into Zereth Mortis.

Let us discuss further business, yes?

We have an interest in observing the potential this new pathway has provided. I trust you will procure what we require. Do not let anyone get in your way.

If this is not agreeable, I am certain I can find someone else to solve both of our problems.
