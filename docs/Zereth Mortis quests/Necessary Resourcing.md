Obtain 30 Neomote Residue and 3 Solidified Neomotes from [[Devourer]]s in [[Zereth Mortis]].

We need neomotes for necessary creation work. Without them, we are unable to complete our work.

Eliminate the devourers and retrieve the neomotes.
