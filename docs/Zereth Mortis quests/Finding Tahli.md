==Objectives==

==Description==
After eons of tranquility, the [[Maw]]'s influence has brought sorrow to [[Zereth Mortis]]. One of our [[acolyte]]s, Tahli, has found it particularly difficult to process recent events. She left some time ago and has not returned.

Will you check on her? She was last seen heading toward the ruins that are buried under the sands.
