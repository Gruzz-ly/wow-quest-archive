*[[Cipherclad]] examined
*[[Containment Trap]] (provided)

==Description==
The wildlife of [[Zereth Mortis]] are attracted to the frenetic creation energy that flows throughout Zereth Mortis. When the wildlife finds something charged with that energy... they eat it!

The Mawsworn Cryptoseers have become aware of this and are attempting to find such wildlife to extract any remnants of that energy.

Eliminate the Cryptoseers, and use the Containment Trap to subdue and examine the wildlife for our research.
