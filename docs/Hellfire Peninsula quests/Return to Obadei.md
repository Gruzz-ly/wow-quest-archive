==Objectives==
Return to [[Anchorite Obadei]] at the [[Temple of Telhamat]] in [[Hellfire Peninsula]] and inform him of [[Vindicator Sedai|Sedai's]] death.

==Description==
With Sedai's death confirmed, you're left with the unpleasant task of returning to Obadei with the grim news.

==Rewards==
Upon completion of this quest you will gain:
*2550 [[XP]] (or {{cost||60|}} at level 70)
*+25 [[reputation]] with [[Exodar (faction)|Exodar]]

==Completion==
