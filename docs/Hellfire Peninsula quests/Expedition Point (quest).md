==Objectives==
Report to [[Forward Commander Kingston]] at [[Expedition Point]].

==Description==
Your battle prowess has been noted by [[High Command#Alliance High Command|high command]], <class>. They want to send you to the front lines to meet up with [[Field Marshal Rohamus|Rohamus]]'s crew. They're radicals, devoted to the complete extermination of the [[Burning Legion]], so you may fit right in...

Head east about 400 paces, across the [[Valley of Bones]], until you run into a destroyed [[fel reaver]]. You'll see [[Expedition Point]] where a Legion base used to stand. Once there, you are to report to [[Forward Commander Kingston]].

Now move out!

==Rewards==
* 975 XP
*+10 [[reputation]] with [[Honor Hold]]

==Completion==
Glad to meet you, soldier.

Enough chatting... now listen up -- I have a vital mission for you and I want you to succeed... and survive!

==Notes==
Go directly east from the quest giver. You'll see a [[Fel reaver|Fel Reaver]] on the ground, next to some crates and gryphons. That is [[Expedition Point]].

Completing this quest makes the following quests available:

*[[An Old Gift]]
*[[Hellfire Fortifications (Alliance)]]
*[[The Longbeards]]
