==Objectives==
Use the Flaming Torch to burn the Eastern [[Alliance Cannon]] and the Western [[Alliance Cannon]], and then return to [[Megzeg Nukklebust]] in [[Thrallmar]]. 
*Eastern Cannon Burned
*Western Cannon Burned
*[[Flaming Torch (Hellfire Peninsula)#Horde]] (provided)

==Description==
I'm a lucky, lucky [[goblin]]. I get to make explosives and killing machines, knowing they'll be used immediately on important missions!

Speaking of missions, I just got one from Thrallmar command: burn the [[Alliance cannon]]s on the far side of the [[Path of Glory]]... the ones pointing dangerously close to [[Horde]] fortifications. Take this torch and make your way to each cannon. Fire the torch and watch the sparks fly!
