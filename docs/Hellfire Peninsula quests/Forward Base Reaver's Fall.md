This quest is part of the '''Through the Dark Portal (Horde)''' quest chain.

==Objectives==
Report to [[Forward Commander To'arch]] at [[Reaver's Fall]].

==Description==
Your battle prowess has been noted by [[High Command#Horde High Command|high command]], <class>. They want to send you to the front lines to meet up with [[Overlord Hun Maimfist|Maimfist]]'s crew. They're radicals, devoted to the complete extermination of the [[Burning Legion]], so you may fit right in...

Head east about 200 paces and veer south at the jutting mass of land. You'll see Reaver's Fall where an old farm used to stand. Once there, you are to report to Foward Commander To'arch.

Now move out! 

==Rewards==
Upon completion of this quest you will gain:
*975 [[XP]] (or {{cost||11|40}} at level 70)
*+10 [[reputation]] with [[Thrallmar]]
