==Description==
By order of the Ranger Captain, a reward is hereby offered for the slaying of the vicious [[rockflayer]] matriarch known as Blacktalon the Savage.

Blacktalon was last sighted inside a cave in the [[Great Fissure]]. Individuals seeking the reward are to present the creature's claws as proof of the deed.
