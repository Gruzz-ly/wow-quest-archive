==Objectives==
[[Lieutenant Amadi]] in [[Honor Hold]] wants you to bring 1 Cursed Talisman to [[Corporal Ironridge]] at [[Expedition Point]].
*[[Cursed Talisman (quest item)]]

==Description==
<Name>, in our battles with the [[Bleeding Hollow clan]] of [[fel orc]]s, we have found upon them foreboding trinkets.  These orcs have collected the insignias of fallen [[Honor Hold]] [[soldier]]s and created cursed talismans from them.  We fear the dark [[magic]] of the Bleeding Hollow orcs have transformed our insignias into evil charms with powers against us!

Find such a talisman from the Bleeding Hollow orcs of [[Zeth'Gor]], to the east, and bring it to Corporal Ironridge at Expedition Point, northeast of Zeth'Gor.
