==Objectives==
Report to {{NPC||Commander Duron}} at the [[Stair of Destiny]] in [[Hellfire Peninsula]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, [[Anduin Wrynn|King Anduin Wrynn]], all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Commander Duron in Hellfire Peninsula.

The expeditionary forces require your help to overcome the [[demon]]ic armies of the [[Burning Legion]]! Use the portal on the upper level of the tower in the [[Mage Quarter|Mage District]] to travel to the Stair of Destiny in [[Outland]].

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!

==Completion==
