==Objectives==
Defeat 6 Bloodfang Lurkers.

==Description==
[[Josiah Avery|He]] turned into one of [[worgen|them]].... didn't he? How do we even begin to fight an enemy that can do this to us?

[[Darius Crowley|My father]]'s arsenal's certainly a good start, I suppose.

Worgen are hiding in the alleys ahead. Take one of [[Gilnean Mastiff|my mastiffs]] to find them more easily.

We're going to need to clear the way ahead if we're going to move these cannons above ground.

==Notes==
The mastiff has an "Attack Lurker" command, with a 30 yd range that can detect and attack the nearest Bloodfang Lurker. The command will cause the mastiff to "Heroic Leap" to the nearest lurker. Sending the mastiff in isn't strictly necessary, as the lurkers are merely stealthed. Wandering into them is also entirely possible.
