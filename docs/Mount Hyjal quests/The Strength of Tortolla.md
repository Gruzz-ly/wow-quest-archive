== Objectives ==
Escort a {{NPC||Child of Tortolla}} to a [[Nemesis Crystal]]. See {{NPC||Garunda Mountainpeak}} if you lose your companion.
*Nemesis Crystal Examined

== Description ==
The dark usurper {{NPC||Nemesis}} fled here when the fires of Hyjal receded. We are not yet strong enough to defeat Nemesis, <name>, but we may be able to turn his own strength against him.

I will send a child of {{NPC||Tortolla}} with you, one of the children you freed earlier from the [[Twilight's Hammer|Twilight's]] corruption.

Search the depths of this cave for a Nemesis Crystal, and allow the young turtle to examine it.

See me if you lose your companion!
