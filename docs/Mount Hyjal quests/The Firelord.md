== Objectives ==
Go through the portal at the [[Sanctum of the Prophets]] and aid {{NPC||Cenarius}} in defeating Ragnaros.
*[[Ragnaros]] defeated

== Description ==
With his gatekeeper gone, you've given the [[Guardians of Hyjal]] access to [[Sulfuron Spire]].

Ragnaros will have no choice but to defend the main entryway to the [[Firelands]] himself.

Go through the [[Twilight's Hammer]] portal at the Sanctum of the Prophets and meet me inside. {{NPC||Malfurion Stormrage||Malfurion}} and {{NPC||Hamuul Runetotem||Hamuul}} will also aid us while the others continue to fight the enemy outside.

== Notes ==
The fourth and final flamegate of Hyjal is at {{coords|55.6|66.2|Mount Hyjal}} in the [[Sanctum of the Prophets]]. Fly through to enter [[Sulfuron Spire]]! Ragnaros is at the far end of the room, submerged.

During the fight, Ragnaros, with 22 million health, has the following abilities:
*{{Abilities|Flame Wall|Deals 3063 to 3937 Fire damage per second.|icon=spell fire moltenblood|notes=Cast on the ramp blocking escape once the fight begins. Otherwise, not a part of the fight}}
*{{Abilities|Searing Flame|The intense heat of Ragnaros's presence deals 982 to 1243 Fire damage to all nearby enemies.|notes=Instant. Ticks every few seconds on units not under Cenarius' Icecrown's Chill aura.|icon=spell fire incinerate}}
*{{Abilities|Flame Tsunami|range=50,000|Inflicts Fire damage to nearby enemies and drenches them with lava, slowing them and dealing an additional Fire damage over 10 sec.|icon=spell shaman lavaflow|notes=2,000 Fire initial tick, then 1000 Fire every second for 10 sec}}
*{{Abilities|Magma Blast|range=50,000|Inflicts Fire damage to an enemy.|notes=1 sec cast. 3,868,875 Fire. Cast on random units when no units are in melee range of Ragnaros, typically indicating a wipe.|icon=spell fire flameshock}}
*{{Abilities|Lava Strike|Deals Fire damage in a small area.|notes=Cast by the fireballs falling out of the room. Ground targets light up before they land. Hits for ~1000 Fire, also has a short knockback component.|icon=spell fire fire}}
*{{abilities|Melee|120,000 to 160,000 damage on Cenarius}}

Immediately inside is Cenarius, with 12.2 million health, who has the following abilities:
*{{Abilities|Icecrown's Chill|range=50|Channels the force of Icecrown, reducing Fire damage taken by 50%, increases healing received by 1000% and deals Frost damage to Ragnaros every 1 sec.|notes=Instant. Triggers both of:|icon=spell ice rune}}
**{{Abilities|Icecrown's Chill|range=100|Deal 5225 to 5775 Frost damage to Ragnaros.|icon=spell frost arcticwinds|notes=Instant. Affected by Storm's Fury&mdash;can land for 110-120,000 Frost!}}
**{{Abilities|Presence of Cenarius|Heals nearby allies for 5% of maximum health.|icon=spell shaman blessingofeternals|notes=Affected by Icecrown's Chill, will heal for 50% on players.}}
*{{abilities|Melee|74-86,000 damage on Ragnaros}}

Hamuul and Malfurion join in, with the following abilities, respectively:
*{{Abilities|Earth's Rage|range=100|Increase the physical damage taken by the target by 2000%.|notes=Channeled on Ragnaros when Hamuul is undisturbed|icon=spell nature earthelemental totem}}
*{{Abilities|Storm's Fury|range=100|Increase the magical damage taken by the target by 2000%.|notes=Channeled on Ragnaros when Malfurion is undisturbed|icon=spell nature callstorm}}
