==Notes==
This quest is automatically offered upon completion of [[Perfecting Your Howl]]. Head to the same summoning location as used for the second stage of other dailies at {{co|42.0|55.8|Mount Hyjal}} to the northeast of the permanent flamegate. Lylagar is a level 85 elite core hound with 309,960 health. Lylagar has two main moves: Sundering Swipe and Lylagar's Breath. He will channel the breath for a few seconds, rooting himself in place. Get out of the way and unload. There is no assistance from NPCs, so pop trinkets and kite.

Should players defeat Lylagar without taking damage from the breath attack, players will complete the Lylagar stage of [[Infernal Ambassadors]].
