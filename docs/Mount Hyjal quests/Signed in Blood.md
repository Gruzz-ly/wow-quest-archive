==Notes==
Run to the southeast along the road to come across a small group of Twilight Recruits under the watchul eye of [[Twilight Recruiter Maruk]]. Talk to one of the recruits to lure him or her away from Maruk and break out the Blackjack! It will take running back to the pass to get out of Maruk's sightline. The Blackjack will one-shot the recruit, so use it and loot the papers.
