'''Guardians of Hyjal: Firelands Invasion!''' is the breadcrumb to the [[Firelands Invasion]] series of daily quests at the [[Regrowth (subzone)|Regrowth]] of [[Mount Hyjal]] and the [[Molten Front]] of the [[Firelands]] immediately available after completing [[Aessina's Miracle]].

==Objectives==
Report to [[Matoclaw]] at the [[Sanctuary of Malorne]] in [[Mount Hyjal]].

==Description==
Your work has given us the advantage we need to mobilize against the denizens of the Firelands. We are gathering our invasion force just south of our position.

We could use your strength again, orc. Speak with Matoclaw at the Sanctuary of Malorne. She is coordinating the invasion effort.
