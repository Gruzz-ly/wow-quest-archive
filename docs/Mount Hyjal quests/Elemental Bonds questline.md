# Elemental Bonds questline

{{For|the other quest chain introduced in Patch 4.2.0|Firelands Invasion}}

The '''Elemental Bonds questline''' is an epic series of quests to restore [[Thrall]] to his former self after [[Fandral Staghelm|Majordomo Staghelm]] splits him into 4 essences of elements.
