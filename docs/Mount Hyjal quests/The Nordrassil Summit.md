==Objectives==
Speak with [[Thrall]] under the [[Nordrassil|World Tree]] in [[Mount Hyjal]] and witness the [[Earthen Ring]] ceremony. 
*Witness the Ceremony

==Description==
Hello, <class>! My brothers and sisters of the Earthen Ring have spoke well of your deeds in the wake of the [[Cataclysm]]. But much remains to be done if we are to heal this broken world.

Walk with me, friend. Preparations for a ceremony are underway, a ritual that I hope will be a turning point in our battle against the [[Twilight's Hammer]].

When all is done, you will be able to spread the word about our triumph here.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*52050 [[XP]] (or {{coppercost|124050}} at max level)
*+75 [[reputation]] with [[Earthen Ring]]

==Completion==
It can't be over. We mustn't give up hope!
