== Objectives ==
Rally 5 shaman in the rear of the [[Temple of Earth]], and then speak to {{NPC||Maruut Stonebinder}}.
*Earthen Ring rallied x5

== Description ==
We must be rallyin' da [[Earthen Ring]] for dis fight. What we be lackin' in numbers, we make up for in strength.

Go to tha temple and gather da restin' shaman from tha rear rooms. {{NPC||Earthmender Norsala||Norsala}}, {{NPC||Windspeaker Lorvarius||Lorvarius}} and {{NPC||Earthcaller Torunscar||I}} will be meetin' them at [[Lorthuna's Gate]].

== Notes ==
Head back to the temple. There are crescent-shaped rooms off to both sides of the main room to the east and west&mdash;Alliance west and Horde east. Head in and rally the shaman questgivers seen throughout the zone, gathered up in one convenient building! Then talk to Maruut in the center.
