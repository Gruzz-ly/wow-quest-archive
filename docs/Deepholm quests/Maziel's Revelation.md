==Objectives==
Find [[Maziel's Journal]] at the [[Lower Silvermarsh]] in [[Deepholm]].

==Description==
The notes continue {{NPC||Maziel}}'s rambling research into the properties of quicksilver and {{NPC||Mercurial Ooze||mercurial oozes}}.

He appears to have made a major discovery. He mentions hiding his journal in the Lower Silvermash to the southwest.

==Completion==
Maziel's journal appears to be undamaged by the mercury.

==Notes==
This quest is automatically offered upon the completion of [[Twilight Research]]. Fly down to the southwest to reach the Lower Silvermarsh. On the island at {{co|67.2|70.2|Deepholm}} inside the mercury pool is [[Maziel's Journal]].
