==Objectives==
Meet Ormhun near the [[Shuddering Spires]] and help him craft a Khaz'gorian Anvil.
* Meet Ormhun near the Shuddering Spires
* Speak with Ormhun to craft the anvil
* Craft the Anvil

==Description==

You'll be doing the hammerin' while I take care of the last reagent we'll need.

==Progress==
