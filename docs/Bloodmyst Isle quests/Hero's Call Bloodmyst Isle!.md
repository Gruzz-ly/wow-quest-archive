==Description==
By order of his royal highness, {{NPC||Varian Wrynn||King Varian Wrynn}}, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Vorkhan the Elekk Herder at Kessel's Crossing.

The people of Bloodmyst need your help to deal with treacherous [[blood elf|blood elves]] and dangerous creatures loose on the island. You can reach Kessel's Crossing on Bloodmyst Isle by taking the bridge north from [[Azuremyst Isle]].

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
