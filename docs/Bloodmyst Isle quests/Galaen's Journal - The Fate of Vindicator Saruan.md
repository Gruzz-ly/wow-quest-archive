The [[Galaen's Journal#Contains|text of Galaen's Journal]] describes how both Galaen and [[Vindicator Saruan]] were beaten by [[Matis the Cruel]], on orders from [[Sironas]].

==Objectives==
Take [[:Galaen's Journal]] to [[Vindicator Kuros]] at [[Blood Watch]].

==Description==
Your hands tremble as you pick up the book. Kuros must be told about this tragedy. Return the book to him.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*1000 [[XP]]
*+150 [[reputation]] with [[Exodar (faction)|Exodar]]

==Progress==
What have you got there, <name>? Is that a journal?

==Completion==
<Kuros' face turns pale as he reads the journal; tears stream down his face.>

[[Vindicator Saruan|Saruan]] was my master... my teacher... my mentor... I took his place among the [[Hand of Argus|Triumvirate]] only recently - after we had given up searching for him.

<Kuros' face contorts in anger.>

[[Matis the Cruel|Matis]]...
