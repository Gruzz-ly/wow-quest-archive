#[[Hippogryph Master Stephanos]]

=== Clopper Wizbang ===
Strictly speaking, not all of these quests are chained.
*[[Explorers' League, Is That Something for Gnomes?]] introduces you to [[Clopper Wizbang]], who grants both:
*[[Pilfered Equipment]]
*[[Artifacts of the Blacksilt]]
Completing Artifacts of the Blacksilt earns you a [[Weathered Treasure Map]], which starts you off on the following quest chain
#[[A Map to Where?]]
#[[Deciphering the Book]]
#[[Nolkai's Words]]
