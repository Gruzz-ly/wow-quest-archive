This quest starts the '''Friend of the Sin'dorei''' quest chain. This is only available to non-blood elf players who complete [[The Traitor's Destruction]].

Take [[Dar'Khan's Head]] to [[Lor'themar Theron]] in [[Silvermoon City]].
*[[Dar'Khan's Head]] (Provided)
