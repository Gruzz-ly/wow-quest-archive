This quest is part of the '''The Farstrider Enclave''' quest chain. This quest chain starts with [[The Farstrider Enclave]].

==Objectives==
Bring Dar'Khan's Journal to [[Magister Kaendris]] at the [[Sanctum of the Sun]] in the [[Ghostlands]]. 
* [[Dar'Khan's Journal]] (Provided)

==Description==
The magisters at the Sanctum of the Sun have made great progress in analyzing the [[Scourge]]'s weaknesses with the aid of the [[Forsaken]]. Magister Kaendris will know what to do with this information.

You'll find the Sanctum of the Sun southwest of here. You must hurry, <name>. [[Dar'Khan Drathir|Dar'Khan]] is a cunning enemy and watches our every move!
