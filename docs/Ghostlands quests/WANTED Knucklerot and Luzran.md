'''WANTED: Knucklerot and Luzran''' is about killing two elite abominations and turning in their heads. You receive the quest by reading the wanted poster next to the inn in [[Tranquillien]].

==Objectives==
Bring proof of slaying the two [[abomination]]s to [[Deathstalker Rathiel]] in [[Tranquillien]].
*[[Knucklerot's Head]] 
*[[Luzran's Head]] 

==Description==
A bounty is hereby declared for the abominations known as [[Knucklerot]] and [[Luzran]]. These dreadful creations of [[Dar'Khan Drathir|Dar'Khan]] are extremely dangerous and have been sighted patrolling near the [[Howling Ziggurat|Howling]] and [[Bleeding Ziggurat]]s. Individuals seeking to claim the reward must present the abominations' heads to Deathstalker Rathiel as proof of the deed.

==Notes==
*The player must track down Knucklerot and Luzran, two abominations that wander around the [[Ghostlands]]. They start at one of the two ziggurats (Howling Ziggurat and Bleeding Ziggurat) in the center of the Ghostlands, and have different patrol areas. Luzran tends to patrol the [[Dead Scar]] from the ziggurats to the gates of [[Deatholme]], while Knucklerot has been spotted near [[Goldenmist Village|Goldenmist]], [[Windrunner Village|Windrunner]] and [[Suncrown Village]]s, as well as [[Windrunner Spire]].
