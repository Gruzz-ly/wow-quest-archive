==Description==
We have received reports that an eccentric [[Arathi]] is accosting our citizens. We have little experience with outsiders and my superiors are wary of a diplomatic incident.

I have been tasked with finding this "[[Lamplighter]]" and resolving his concerns. You have worked with the Arathi before - can you assist me?

I have a list of [[earthen]] who have reported the Lamplighter. You can interview half the list. Between us, we can hopefully locate the Arathi before he troubles more citizens.
