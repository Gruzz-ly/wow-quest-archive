==Description==
[[Undermine]] is held together by an intricate design of innovation, hard work, allegiance, and it seems like the sheer will of the [[goblin]]s. Rather than cower in their sunless cave, the goblins have built a city that thrives on motion and activity. We can learn much from that.

After not seeing new faces for years, they bring a new light to our lands. Go help them in whatever ways seem fitting.
