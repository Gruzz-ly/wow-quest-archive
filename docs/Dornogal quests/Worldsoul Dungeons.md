'''Worldsoul: Dungeons''' is a [[meta quest]] that may be offered as part of the weekly [[The Call of the Worldsoul]] quest from [[Faerin Lothar]] in [[Dornogal]].

==Objectives==
Complete any [[Season]]al Dungeon at Normal difficulty or higher.

==Description==
The enemies of [[Khaz Algar]] rise up, unified in the suffering they inflict on those who do not believe in their causes.

<Faerin pauses, then takes a quick glance at the table in front of her before returning her gaze to you.>

Show these people the light, <name>. If they cannot see the error of their ways, please show them a merciful conclusion to their beliefs.
