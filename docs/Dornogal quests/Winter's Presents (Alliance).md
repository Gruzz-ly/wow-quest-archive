==Objectives==
Speak with [[Greatfather Winter (Ironforge)|Greatfather Winter]]. He is located near the [[Smokywood Pastures]] vendor area in [[Ironforge]].

==Description==
;Wonderform Operator
I hear [[Greatfather Winter (Ironforge)|Greatfather Winter]] - with the generous support of Smokywood Pastures - has put presents for everyone under the tree in [[Ironforge]]. I wish I could go, but I've got to look after the [[PX-238 Winter Wondervolt]].

But you should definitely check it out. I'm sure Greatfather Winter has some presents with your name on them.

;Holiday Enthusiast
I hear Greatfather Winter has put presents for everyone under the tree in Ironforge.

You should definitely check it out. I'm sure Greatfather Winter has some presents with your name on them.
