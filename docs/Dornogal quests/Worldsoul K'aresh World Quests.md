'''Worldsoul: K'aresh World Quests''' is one of various quests that may be available through [[The Call of the Worldsoul]], a [[meta quest]] offered by [[Faerin Lothar]] in [[Dornogal]].

==Objectives==
Complete World Quests in K'aresh.
*[[World quest|World Quests]] completed in [[K'aresh]] (6)

==Description==
I know now that the heroes of [[Azeroth]] venture wherever help is needed. K'aresh is a strange, faraway place, but our allies there need us.

Go there and help them. See what you can do to bring respite to the [[ethereal]]s and [[broker]]s.
