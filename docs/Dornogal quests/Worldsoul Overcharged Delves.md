'''Worldsoul: Overcharged Delves''' is one of various quests that may be available through [[The Call of the Worldsoul]], a weekly [[meta quest]] offered by [[Faerin Lothar]] in [[Dornogal]].

==Objectives==
Complete an [[Overcharged Delves|Overcharged]] War Within [[Delve]] at any difficulty.
*Complete an Overcharged War Within Delve on any difficulty

==Description==
Something has caused hostile [[titan]] constructs to activate and enter various caverns and spaces around [[Khaz Algar]]. Venture to these locations and investigate and eliminate the threat they pose.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|56|18|}}
* 15,650 [[XP]]
* (Max level) [[Pinnacle Cache]]
* (Level 70-79) [[Algari Adventurer's Cache]]

==Progress==
How goes your dangerous exploration?

==Completion==
It is unnerving to not know what has forced these hostile titan units to surface. But due to your efforts their threat is at an end.
