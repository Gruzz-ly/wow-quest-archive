==Objectives==
Beat the [[Dastardly Duos]], then speak with [[Vinnie Sweets]].
*Win Dastardly Duos fight

==Description==
That's right, the Dastardly Duos are here!

Show your support! Talk to [[Xyggie Marou]] in [[Dornogal]] and sign up to fight a Dastardly Duos match.

From there, wait until you're called and away you go! Show them what you got, hero!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|46|80}}
*15,650 [[XP]]
*[[Pinnacle Cache]]
*[[Fractured Spark of Fortunes]]
*[[Hellcaller Chest]] (during [[A Greedy Emissary]])

==Progress==
Speak with Xyggie Marou and beat those Dastardly Duos!

==Completion==
And that's how it's done! You put on a good show, friend.

Feel free to sign up as much as you want, because you're good business!

==Notes==
*A [[Dastardly Invitation]] is provided on accept, even if you have previously completed the quest [[Dastardly Duos! It's on!]].
*Characters below level {{level|tww-max}} can see this quest on weeks where [[The Call of the Worldsoul]] offers it, but they cannot pick it up. Clicking the button to accept it will simply close the Call of the Worldsoul UI window.
