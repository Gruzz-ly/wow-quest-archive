==Objectives==
Defeat 5 Timewalking raid bosses or dungeons.
* Timewalking raid bosses or dungeons defeated (5)

==Description==
Your people have brought me tales of a [[Dark Portal]] wherein lie many enemies and challenges. I would like to hear more of these tales.

Travel through time and space to this outlandish place, vanquish its foes, and bring me news of their demise.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Cache of K'areshi Treasures]]

==Progress==
Some of my people may see the arrival of the outlanders as an invasion, but I am glad it was you who landed on our shoes{{sic}}, and not the [[Burning Legion]].

==Completion==
You return in glory and bring me tales of the quenching the{{sic}} fires of the Burning Legion?
