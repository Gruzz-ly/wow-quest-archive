'''The Call of the Worldsoul''' is a [[meta quest]] offered by [[Faerin Lothar]] in [[Dornogal]] every third week. It presents a choice between four<ref group="note">Can be fewer for characters below 80.</ref> Worldsoul quests in [[Khaz Algar]] and automatically completes when one of the offered quests is completed. It has no rewards of its own. The other two weekly meta quests are one of the archives quests like [[Archives: Seeking History]] started by [[High Speaker Brinthe]] or [[Delves: Percussive Archaeology]] started by [[Brann Bronzebeard]].

==Possible quests==
Every three weeks, four quests will be available to choose from, and only one can be completed.
