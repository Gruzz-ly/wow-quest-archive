==Objectives==
Collect 50 Valorstones while adventuring in [[Khaz Algar]], [[Undermine]], and [[K'aresh]], then return to [[Cuzolth]] and upgrade a piece of [[The War Within Season 3|Season 3]] equipment.
*[[Valorstones]] (50)
*Upgrade a piece of equipment with Valorstones

==Description==
It's been many months since we arrived in [[Dornogal]] and there's still more to learn of Valorstones. It turns out they can be found beyond [[Azeroth]]! This discovery opens up entirely new avenues of research.

With new research comes a need for new supplies. Bring me some, and we'll help upgrade your gear to new heights.
