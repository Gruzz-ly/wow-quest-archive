==Objectives==
Go to the top of the northern tower in [[Desolation Hold]] and help [[Warlord Bloodhilt]] "Relieve" Gar'dul of command.
* Go to the Top of the Tower
* [[Gar'dul]] "Relieved" of Command

==Description==
When Bloodhilt arrives, I'd like to - ah - talk in private. Can you accompany me to make sure things stay on the level? This Bloodhilt is a, ah, he's a, he's a loose cannon.

<Gar'dul tries to compose himself.>

Meet me atop the northern tower and we'll talk with Bloodhilt together.

==Completion==
You've just learned a little something about the inner workings of the [[High Command#Horde High Command|Horde high command]]. 

Warlord Bloodhilt is in charge of Desolation Hold now. He may have more for you to do in the courtyard below.

==Notes==
;Gar'dul's gossip text during the quest
:Don't delay, he's - ah - he's almost here!

:Meet me at the top of the northernmost tower.

Once you reach the top of the tower, the following event will take place:
:{{text|say|Warlord Gar'dul|Thank you, <name>. I trust you will, ah, defer to my authority on this matter.}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|So this is where you're hiding Gar'dul.}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Gar'dul|Hiding? Listen Bloodhilt - I've, ah, reinforced our grip on the Barrens.}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|You cower behind your walls! If it wasn't for <name> here the Alliance would be marching through Mulgore.}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Gar'dul|But they've, ah, they've gotten reinforcements! From the Eastern Kingdoms. Whereas I've been, ah, cut off -}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|Your excuses sicken me! Consider yourself relieved from command.}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Gar'dul|Relieved? Over my dead body!}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|So! You DO have a spine.}}
:''Warlord Gar'dul turns hostile! Defeat him together with Warlord Bloodhilt!''
:{{text|say|Warlord Gar'dul| Wait, ah - wait! I yield. I yield!}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|Yield? You ... YIELD?}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|What part of "Victory or Death" don't you understand?}}
:''Bloodhilt grabs Gar'dul and throws him out of the window!''
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|Garrosh's Horde does not tolerate failure.}}
:{{text|say|Warlord Bloodhilt|Meet me down below and we'll discuss our upcoming counter-attack.}}

A Desolation Hold guard patrols the tower and if present will aid the player and Bloodhilt in beating Gar'dul.
