==Objectives==
Report to {{NPC||Dockmaster Lewis}} at the docks outside [[Northwatch Hold]] in the [[Southern Barrens]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, {{NPC||Anduin Wrynn||King Anduin Wrynn}}, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Dockmaster Lewis near Northwatch Hold.

Our brave soldiers have made tremendous gains in the Barrens, but they have paid a heavy price! Your efforts are needed to consolidate our gains. You can reach the docks outside Northwatch Hold by taking the boat to [[Ratchet]] and traveling south along the coast. 

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
