==Description==
Like the rest of the gardens, [[Nozdormu]]'s hourglass has had to weather the [[elemental]] unrest. Turbulent [[Air elemental|wind elementals]] came through and scattered the [[magic]]al sand contained within.

Without this sand, Nozdormu's influence here is greatly diminished.

As a steward-in-training, I have been tasked with overseeing the hourglass at all times. If you would collect the sand in my stead, I would be eternally grateful.
