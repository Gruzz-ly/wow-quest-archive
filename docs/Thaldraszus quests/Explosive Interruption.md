==Objectives==
Meet [[Investigator Erima|Erima]] at [[Scalewatch Caverns]].

==Description==
I managed to infiltrate a [[Flightless|rebel]] camp, down in Scalewatch Cavern. It seemed like any other camp, except for frequent wagons coming into camp with their contents covered.

Eventually, I was able to discreetly make my way to where the wagons would be unloaded. The rebels are stockpiling bombs, but it's even worse!

They're piling the bombs against a pillar directly underneath the aqueduct from [[Tyrhold]].

Meet me down there, and we'll find a way to stop this!
