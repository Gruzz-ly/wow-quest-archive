==Description==
The [[Primalists|Primalist]] attack came suddenly and with no warning. My unit attempted to find our leader, [[Commander Eranog]], but before we could get to the other side of the garrison we were cut off.

In the battle my unit leader, [[Sergeant Tagrik]], held off three Primalists by himself as [[Kaora]], [[Mikor]], and myself battled at his side.

Their screams are the last thing I remember before I fell. 

Please, <race>, if you see any signs of my unit let me know what happened to them.
