==Description==
Despite that small hiccup during training, I believe you are prepared enough to aid us here.

The distortions in the valley are allowing different time periods to cross over into ours. We are unsure why these timelines connect which is quite alarming. We need more information.

Please, see if you can find anything about the creatures pouring in from differently{{sic}} timelines. I am sure their passage will have left some sort of residue. By studying it we can hopefully find a way to get ahead of this crisis.
