'''Shaping the Dreamsurge''' is the weekly wrapper quest for the [[Dreamsurge]] event added in [[patch 10.1.7]]. Find [[Hamuul Runetotem|Archdruid Hamuul Runetotem]] in the active zone to start the quest.

==Objectives==
Gather Dreamsurge Coalescence from around [[Thaldraszus]].
* [[Dreamsurge Coalescence]] collected (100)

==Description==
The [[Emerald Dream]] is thrumming with energies not felt in ages.

Some of that energy is even slipping through the barrier between the dream and Azeroth, coalescing into a glimmering, pollen-like powder.

Gather as much as you can. This is not like any event we have seen before!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Dreamsurge Cradle]]
* [[Cache of Dreams]]
* 10x [[Whelpling's Awakened Crest]]
* [[Dream Shaper]]

==Progress==
The winds sparkle with motes of dreaming essence, spreading it over the land.

==Completion==
Thank you, <name>. This will aid us greatly in understanding what marvels we are witnessing.

==Notes==
Complete tasks in the active zone to earn [[Dreamsurge Coalescence]]:
*World Quests (10)
*Normal elites (0-1)
*Fly through Dreamsurge motes (1-5)
*Loot Dreaming Growths (1)
*Rare enemies (25)
*Rare elite elites (?)
*Close a solo/small-group Waking Dream portal (25)
