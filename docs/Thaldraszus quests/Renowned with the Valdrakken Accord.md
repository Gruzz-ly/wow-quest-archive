==Description==
Your deeds are certainly in line with your fabled reputation prior to coming to the Dragon Isles. I wish to assist you further by providing you with additional supplies. Seek me out in Valdrakken.

* Speak with [[Unatos]] in [[Valdrakken]].

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Brimming Valdrakken Accord Supply Pack]] or [[Overflowing Valdrakken Accord Supply Pack]]

== Completion ==
It is my part to acknowledge those whose efforts further the accord and by acknowledgement aid them further. I take pride in aiding one such as yourself. Please take these so they may continue to aid your efforts against the [[Primalists]].
