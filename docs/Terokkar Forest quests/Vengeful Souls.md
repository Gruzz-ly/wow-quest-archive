==Objectives==
[[High Priest Orglum]] wants you to journey into the [[Tomb of Lights]] and defeat 5 [[Vengeful Draenei]].
*[[Vengeful Draenei]] slain (5)

==Description==
The [[Tomb of Lights]] has become corrupted by the [[Shadow Council]] and the [[draenei]] dead have become cursed spirits, angry and confused with a burning hatred for the living. Enter the tomb and seek out these tragic victims. Give them the rest that they deserve.
