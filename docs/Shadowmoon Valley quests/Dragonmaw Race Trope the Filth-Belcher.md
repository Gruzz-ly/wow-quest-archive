'''Dragonmaw Race: Trope the Filth-Belcher''' is the second of six race quests, available at {{reputation|honored}} reputation with [[Netherwing]].

==Objectives==
Follow [[Trope the Filth-Belcher]] while attempting to stay on your [[flying mount]]. Should you defeat Trope the Filth-Belcher, report your victory to [[Ja'y Nosliw]] at the [[Dragonmaw Base Camp]] in [[Shadowmoon Valley]].
*Trope the Filth-Belcher Defeated

==Description==
Dragonmaw treat you right, <name>. Even a worn out cursed being like me is given a chance. Now if you want to prove your worth to the Skybreakers, you will have to defeat me.

I'm going to shake you off my tail like a bad case of fleas...

We'll lift off when you are ready.
