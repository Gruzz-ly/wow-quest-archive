'''Nethermine Flayer Hide''' is one of three profession specific [[Daily quest|Daily Quests]] offered by [[Taskmaster Varkule Dragonbreath]].

==Objectives==
[[Taskmaster Varkule Dragonbreath]] at [[Dragonmaw Base Camp]] in [[Shadowmoon Valley]] wants you to bring him 35 Nethermine Flayer Hide.
*[[Nethermine Flayer Hide]] (35)

==Description==
A skinner, eh? Well you'll find plenty to skin here! You gotta search for flayers around crystal formations. Take 'em down and take their hides. Sometimes they'll have a hide worth keepin'. Bring those to me.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|4|40}} (or {{cost|7|59}} at max level)
*+250 [[reputation]] with [[Netherwing]]

==Progress==
We use the flayer hides for armor lining and structural tarps.

==Completion==
You're done for today, kid. Come back tomorrow if you're still alive.

==Notes==
The other profession quests he offers are :
* [[Netherdust Pollen (quest)]] (Skill level of 350 in [[Herbalism]] required)
* [[Nethercite Ore (quest)]] (Skill level of 350 in [[Mining]] required)

''Even if you have multiple gathering professions you still can only complete one of these per day.''
