'''A Mysterious Portent''' returns the player to Akama with a single mysterious word.

==Objectives==
Return to [[Akama]] at the [[Warden's Cage]] in [[Shadowmoon Valley]]. 

==Description==
Before he died, [[Udalo]] appears to have written a single word on the floor beneath him. The writing is barely legible but you recognize the word '''Atam'al'''.

Perhaps Akama will understand the meaning of his brethren's message.
