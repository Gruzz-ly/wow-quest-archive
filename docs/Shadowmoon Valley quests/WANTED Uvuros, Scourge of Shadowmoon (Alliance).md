==Description==
Wanted - DEAD:

Uvuros, scourge of Shadowmoon Valley.

By order of [[Thane Yoregar]], the fel beast, Uvuros, is to be killed on sight and his mane stripped from his carcass as proof of deed. Return the fiery mane of the beast to Warcaller Beersnout should you succeed.

Uvuros was last seen at the [[Hand of Gul'dan (subzone)|Hand of Gul'dan]], located in the heart of Shadowmoon Valley - northeast of Wildhammer Stronghold.
