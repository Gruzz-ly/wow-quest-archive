Part of a quest line that ends with [[Quenching the Blade]].  This quest must be done after [[Additional Materials]].  It opens along with [[The Lexicon Demonica]].  Once both are done, [[Tear of the Earthmother (quest)]] and [[Underworld Loam]] open up.

==Objectives==
[[David Wayne]] at Wayne's Retreat wants you to bring him an Overcharged Manacell.
*[[Overcharged Manacell]]
