'''Nethercite Ore''' is one of three profession specific [[Daily quest|Daily Quests]] offered by [[Taskmaster Varkule Dragonbreath]].

==Objectives==
[[Taskmaster Varkule Dragonbreath]] at [[Dragonmaw Base Camp]] in [[Shadowmoon Valley]] wants you to bring him 40 Nethercite Ore.
*[[Nethercite Ore]] (40)

==Description==
A miner!

<Varkule salutes.>

An orc after my own heart! That's how Varkule got his start - minin'! There ain't nothin' better than getting down and dirty in the mines, is there?

Now get out there and find those nethercite deposits. Strip 'em for all their worth and bring me back the ore.
