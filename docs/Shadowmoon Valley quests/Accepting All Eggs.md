'''Accepting All Eggs''' is a repeatable collection quest, available at {{reputation|neutral}} reputation with [[Netherwing]] after completing [[The Great Netherwing Egg Hunt]]. Due to this quest being repeatable and not daily you should complete this quest as many times as you can, thus earning your drake faster

==Objectives==
We don't need those eggs to get into the wrong hands, <name>. Bring me any additional eggs that you may find.

Just remember to keep a low profile and avoid raising suspicion!
*[[Netherwing Egg]]
