# Shadowmoon Valley quests

[[File:WorldMap-ShadowmoonValley.jpg|thumb|Zone Map]]
'''[[Shadowmoon Valley]]''' is a level {{level|Shadowmoon Valley}} zone in [[Outland]].

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Shadow of the Betrayer]].

Optional Breadcrumbs:
* [[Zorus the Judicator (quest)]] or [[Chief Apothecary Hildagard (quest)]] (from [[Shattrath City]])
* [[Visions of Destruction]] or [[Kroghan's Report]] (from [[Nagrand]])
* [[Building a Better Gryphon]] or [[The Apprentice's Request]] (from [[Blade's Edge Mountains]])
* [[Hero's Call: Shadowmoon Valley! (Outland)]] or [[Warchief's Command: Shadowmoon Valley!]]
