'''Bow to the Highlord''' is the first of the three {{reputation|exalted}} storyline quests, available starting at {{reputation|exalted}} reputation with [[Netherwing]].

==Objectives==
Speak to Overlord Mor'ghor at Dragonmaw Base Camp in Shadowmoon Valley.

==Description==
I am unworthy to even carry a message directed at you, sir. Please forgive me for this indulgence. Overlord Mor'ghor has requested your presence in his chambers at once!

==Completion==
You are the first highlord of the Dragonmaw, <name>. The master himself has chosen to come here to promote you.
