The [[Earthen Ring]] has sent representatives to the horde and alliance, seeking help communing with the spirits of [[Shadowmoon Valley]], and healing the damage done to the valley.  In the process, you discover how the land was shattered, and defeat an elemental lord.

This quest has several dialogs and a couple of [[Cut Scene|cut scenes]] that explain details not found otherwise.
