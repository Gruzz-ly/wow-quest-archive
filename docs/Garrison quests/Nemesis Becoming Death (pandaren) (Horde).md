==Objectives==
Kill a Pandaren Player in Player versus Player combat while in Draenor and retrieve their Pandaren Heart.
*[[Pandaren Heart]]

==Description==
You have chosen your quarry and surely soon they will feel your merciless wrath, but first you must venture out into the wilds of Draenor and rip out the still-beating heart of a pandaren in mortal combat. Return here with your prize.

Through the heart of your enemy the spirits will attune your senses, granting you second sight and keenness in hearing - all to stalk your prey and claim dominance.

==Progress==
Have you recovered the heart of a worthy foe?

==Completion==
With this heart you will see and hear things as only the great spirits can. Your enemy will be unable to hide from your unrelenting assaults.
