==Description==
Hello, <name>. It looks like you four have had time to become well-acquainted.

I have my first assignment for you. [[Velarok]], an ancient [[black dragon]] of considerable power, has joined in the hunt for the egg we are protecting. We need to stop him before he gets too close.

You will find him and his minions at the [[Dustbowl]], north of [[Dragon's Mouth]]. Hunt him down and defeat him. You will likely need the help of your new dwarven allies.

==Completion==
One less dragon to worry about, one step closer to redemption. Onward, my soldiers!
