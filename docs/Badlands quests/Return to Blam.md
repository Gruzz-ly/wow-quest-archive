==Objectives==
Bring the Eye of the Watchers to [[Dr. Hieronymus Blam]] at [[Agmond's End]] in the [[Badlands]].
*[[Eye of the Watchers (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
Inside the chest, you find a small, circular device, resting upon a cushion.

If there are any [[titan]] artifacts to be found here in [[Tomb of the Watchers|the tomb]], this is it. You resolve to bring it back to Dr. Blam.
