===Bugs===
;Sun despawns
The sun may despawn when clicked, without giving credit for the quest. This may be fixed by: Exiting the "vehicle", talking to Lucien again without abandoning the quest, growing back to full size, and clicking on one or two clouds to get the sun to reappear.

;Sun is not clickable
Attempting to click on the sun may give a "too far away" error, accompanied by the gray "cannot interact" icon. This may be fixed by: Exiting the "vehicle", talking to Lucien again without abandoning the quest, growing '''''only once''''' rather than the "required" five times, interacting with a cloud (which triggers the sun again), and then clicking on it while remaining at the "single grow" size.<ref>{{ref web|title=The Day that Deathwing Came: The Real Story (comments) |url=https://www.wowhead.com/quest=27714/the-day-that-deathwing-came-the-real-story#comments |author=cmstringers, mcapone888, Kaynos |date=2025-05-04 |publisher=[[Wowhead]]}}</ref> Some terrain spots may work better than others (the sun spawns directly above the cloud that triggered it), since the relative height of player and sun seems to be the primary factor.
