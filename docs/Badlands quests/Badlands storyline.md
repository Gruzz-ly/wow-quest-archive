# Badlands storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Badlands-cata.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]
'''[[Badlands]]''' is a neutral level 45-48 level-up zone in southeastern [[Khaz Modan]] of the [[Eastern Kingdoms]]. The primary quest line revolves around the redemption of the [[black dragonflight]]. There are a few side quests that are optional to the zone, but do fill in some of the lore.

Players are directed to the zone via [[Light's Hope Chapel]], where upon conclusion of the [[Eastern Plaguelands storyline]], a [[Steamwheedle Cartel]] goblin will connect players to [[Fuselight-by-the-Sea]] on the Badlands' east coast. Upon the conclusion of the Badlands storyline, players are next directed to [[Searing Gorge storyline|Searing Gorge]] via the quest [[To the Aid of the Thorium Brotherhood]].

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Badlands Quests]].
