==Description==
Some of the town's most respected citizens, traitors?

How long has the [[Twilight's Blade]] been here? How many scouts have gone missing? Crimes gone unreported? Innocents recruited by those they thought they could trust?

They must be stopped. Cut down the traitors. Show them that Tranquillien is no home for cultists.
