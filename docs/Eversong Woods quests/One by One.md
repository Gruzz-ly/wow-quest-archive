==Objectives==

Kill cultists of the [[Twilight's Blade]] at [[Speaker's Rest]].
*Twilight's Blade Cultists slain (15)
*[[Mordrag the Tainted]] slain

==Description==
Every last cultist is a threat to the people we're trying to save. To Silvermoon itself.

Blasted fanatics with blood on their hands and no intention of letting anyone walk out alive.

We're already in the camp. Let's make it count. I want them to feel this.
