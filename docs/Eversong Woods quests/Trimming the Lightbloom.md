==Objectives==
Kill lightbloom creatures.

==Description==
The creatures of this lightbloom see us as the intruders. They threaten the surrounding wildlife and inhabitants who are naturally here.

We should reduce their numbers and end this threat.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{Cost|2|34}}
*1,650 [[XP]]

==Completion==
This will bring some peace to the region for now, at least.

==Notes==
Fill the bar up by slaying the [[Lightbloom Lasher]]s (2%), [[Lightbloom Hydra]]s (8%), [[Lightfed Growth]]s (8%), [[Lightbloom Petalwing]]s (8%), & the [[Lightbloom Ent]].

Make sure you do [[Curious Cultivation]] and turn in both quests.
