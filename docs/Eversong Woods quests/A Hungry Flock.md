==Description==
Some think that raising [[hawkstrider]]s is an easy task, but it most certainly is not. Hawkstrider appetites tend to be notoriously fussy!

More often than not they'll turn their beaks up at anything I offer. But this time of year? Frog legs. They love the slimy little things. They can't get enough of them!

There's a pond just down the hill just swarming with the leggy little morsels. Could you gather some for me, please?
