From what I can see, it looks like the Twilight's Blade stole several [[arcane]] artifacts used by our [[magisters]] to enhance long-distance communication. If they learn to wield them, they'll cast their reach far beyond what we can contain. They might even be able to hear our communications... and we can't have that.

Recover the artifacts. If anyone gets in your way... you know what to do.
