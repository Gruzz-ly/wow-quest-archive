==Objectives==
Travel to [[Moa'ki Harbor]] in the [[Dragonblight]] and speak with [[Elder Ko'nani]].

==Description==
<race>, word just came in off the boat from Moa'ki Harbor. Something very bad happened over there this morning!

You've been very helpful to us here. Would you consider using the boat to travel to the Great Dragonblight?

By the sound of things, Elder Ko'nani could certainly use your assistance.

==Rewards==
{{itembox|You will be able to choose one of the following|Wandering Healer's Kilt|Durable Worghide Cape|Choker of the Northern Wind|Grips of the Windswept Plains}}

You will also receive:
*2050 [[XP]] (or {{cost||49|50}} at level 80)
*20 reputation with [[The Kalu'ak]]

==Completion==
What's that you say, <race>? [[Hotawa]]/[[Anuniaq]] sent you?

Good, good... there's much to do. We can use all of the help that we can get!

==Notes==
There are two versions of this quest, one from [[Howling Fjord]] and one from [[Borean Tundra]]. For the Borean Tundra version, you must first have completed [[The Tides Turn]], while Howling Fjord version you must first complete [[Avenge Iskaal]].
