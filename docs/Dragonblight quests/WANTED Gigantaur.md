==Description==
By decree of [[Overlord Agmar]]:

Target: Gigantaur

Crime: Attacks upon the Horde; "Bein' ugly"

Status: Alive

The [[magnataur]] lord, Gigantaur, is wanted in relation to a number of attacks against Horde soldiers and installations in the Dragonblight.

Gigantaur was last seen wandering the woods and roads south by southeast of Agmar's Hammer.

Gigantaur should be considered armed and extremely dangerous. Do not engage solo!

All bounties are paid out by Captain Gort at Agmar's Hammer.
