== Objectives ==
Return to [[Zelig the Visionary]] at [[Wintergarde Keep]] in [[Dragonblight]].

== Description ==
You have honored me and honored the memory of hundreds of [[Alliance]] veterans, <name>. Zelig was wise in sending you here. Please return to him and give him my thanks. He will be happy to hear of the events that transpired at the [[Forgotten Shore]].

I do not have the gift of foresight like Zelig, but I am certain that great things await you, friend.

Farewell.
