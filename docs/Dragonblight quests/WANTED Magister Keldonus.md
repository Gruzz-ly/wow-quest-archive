==Description==
By decree of [[Overlord Agmar]]:

Target: Magister Keldonus

Crime: Treason

Status: Alive

Magister Keldonus, once mage commander of Agmar's Hammer, is to be killed on sight in the name of the [[Horde]]. 

Keldonus was last seen traveling southwest toward [[Moonrest Gardens]] to join the [[blue dragonflight]] in their campaign against magic.

Approach with caution as Keldonus is a powerful Magus. Do not engage solo!

All bounties are paid out by Captain Gort at Agmar's Hammer.
