==Completion==
Impressive, little one. You have gone through much trouble to gain the favor of the [[Bronze dragonflight|Bronze Flight]]. Your dedication is noted.

The signet ring of the conqueror will greatly enhance your physical attacks.

Be warned, once you have chosen your path, you will have no recourse should you change your mind.
