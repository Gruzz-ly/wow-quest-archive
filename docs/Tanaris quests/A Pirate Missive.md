'''A Pirate Missive''' begins the 14 November portion of the [[Guest Relations]] storyline.

==Objectives==
Pick up the notes from [[Scowling Rosa]] in [[Booty Bay]].

==Description==
There you are, <name>. Someone named Scowling Rosa wants to see you in Booty Bay.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|2|34}}

==Completion==
I've done my best with those notes. I hope you find this helpful.

==Notes==
Return to [[Booty Bay]] by flying out of [[Stormwind City]], connecting through [[Ratchet]] or [[Grom'gol Base Camp]], or the [[Boots of the Bay]] from the [[Stranglethorn Fishing Extravaganza]].

Scowling Rosa patrols, so find her with <code>/tar scow</code>.
