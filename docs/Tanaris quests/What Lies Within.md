==Objectives==
Defeat the Antechamber Guardian.
*[[Antechamber Guardian]] slain

==Description ==
As you lay your hand upon the chest, a faint rumbling can be heard. The sound of grinding movement echoes behind you as the chest begins to shut itself.

Something is approaching!

==Progress==
The chest's lid does not budge.

==Completion==
The chest now grinds open again, the rest of the antechamber fallen silent.
