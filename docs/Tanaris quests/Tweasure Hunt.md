==Objectives==
Use the Torch of Pyrreth to search for treasure around the ruins of [[Tanaris]].
*Ask [[Maru]] for a hint (Optional)
*Activate the [[:Torch of Pyrreth]] from your [[Toy Box|Tox Boy]]{{sic}} (Optional)
*Treasure found (0/100%)
