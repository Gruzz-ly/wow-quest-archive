'''Pet Pursuit''' begins the seventh hidden local story of [[Guest Relations]], started on 18 November and required for [[Assistant to the Assistant Guest Relations Manager]].

==Objectives==
Find [[Jepetto Joybuzz]] near the pet playground.

==Description==
Hello, adventurer!

I spotted a group of battle pet tamers who were going on about a bingo game. Jepetto Joybuzz was among them and seemed to be in some distress over his progress.

Why don't you see if he could use some help?
