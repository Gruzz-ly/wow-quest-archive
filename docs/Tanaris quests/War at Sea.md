== Objectives ==
You seek to befriend the [[goblin]]s of [[Tanaris]] once again, &lt;name&gt;? &nbsp;We're in need of materials for sails and guns to fight off our old enemies, the Bloodsail Buccaneers. &nbsp;Bring me mageweave and strong flux and we'll be on our way to pardoning your trespasses. 
*[[Mageweave Cloth]] (40)
*[[Strong Flux]] (4)

==  Completion ==

==Rewards==

*-500 reputation with [[Bloodsail Buccaneers]]
*250 reputation with [[Booty Bay]]
*250 reputation with [[Everlook]]
*500 reputation with [[Gadgetzan]]
*250 reputation with [[Ratchet]]

== Rebuilding Reputation ==
If you are at 0/36000 hated with Booty Bay:

You will need to do 72 turn ins of 40 [[Mageweave Cloth]] and 4 [[Strong Flux]] to reach: "Hostile"

if you are at 0/3000 hostile:

You will need to do 6 turn ins of 40 [[Mageweave Cloth]] and 4 [[Strong Flux]] to reach: "Unfriendly"

If you are at 0/3000 unfriendly:

You will need to do 6 turn ins of 40 [[Mageweave Cloth]] and 4 [[Strong Flux]] to reach: "Neutral"


So in all you will need to do a grand total of 90 turn ins which is:

:* 3600 [[Mageweave Cloth]]
:* 360 [[Strong Flux]]
