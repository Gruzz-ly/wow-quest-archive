'''Warn the Detective''' begins the fourth day of the [[Guest Relations]] storyline, first made available on 1 November.

==Objectives==
Warn [[Athelton Jones|Detective Jones]] about the shapeshifter.

==Description==
Welcome back, <name>.

I saw the detective headed west. Hurry up and warn him before the shapeshifter comes for him too!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|2|34}}

==Completion==
A shapeshifter? Hah! Poppycock!

==Notes==
Head west to the entrance of the exterior [[Caverns of Time]] area to spot two [[Athelton Jones]]es, with their backs to each other, absorbed in a book.

On complete, the two detectives stop writing in their book, walk to the middle, and grumble at each other:
