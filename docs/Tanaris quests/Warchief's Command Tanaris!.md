== Description ==
All able bodied members of the Horde are hereby ordered to report to Megs Dreadshredder in Gadgetzan. Help the [[Horde]] and the [[Bilgewater Cartel]] establish a presence in Tanaris before the [[Alliance]] does!

You can reach Gadgetzan in Tanaris by traveling south from the [[Shimmering Deep]] in [[Thousand Needles]].

Destiny calls!
