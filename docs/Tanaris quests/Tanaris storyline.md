# Tanaris storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Tanaris-cata.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Tanaris]]''' is a level 45-50 zone for both [[Alliance]] and [[Horde]] in [[Southern Kalimdor]]. Breadcrumbs to the zone come from primarily [[Thousand Needles]].

You start out in [[Gadgetzan]] and there are both Alliance and Horde quests and some neutral quests scattered around the zone.

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Tanaris Quests]].
