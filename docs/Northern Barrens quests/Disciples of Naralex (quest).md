==Objectives==
Speak with [[Nalpak]] in the [[Wailing Caverns]]. 

==Description==
<Class>, the [[Barrens]] used to be one of the most beautiful places in all of [[Kalimdor]]. It teemed with life and promise.

Now, as you can see, that is no longer true. It is a land of great torment and strife.

The [[Disciples of Naralex]] seek to cleanse the Wailing Caverns and bring them back to their former beauty. Speak with Nalpak just inside the Wailing Caverns to help.
