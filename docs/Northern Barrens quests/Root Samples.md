==Objectives==
Collect 8 Root Samples from herbs around [[Northern Barrens|North Barrens]].
* [[Root Sample]] (8)

==Description==
The herbs of the [[Barrens]] are not quite like herbs in other lands. Their properties are mostly the same, but the oasis water here alters the plants; they are just slightly different.

I want to study those differences... to see if they're exploitable!

If you are skilled in [[herbalism]], then gather root samples off the herbs you find in the Barrens. Bring back these samples so that I may compare them with herbs from other regions.
