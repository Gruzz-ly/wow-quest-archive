# Blasted Lands storyline

[[File:WorldMap-BlastedLands.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

The [[Blasted Lands]] are a contested level {{level|Blasted Lands}} zone in southeastern [[Eastern Kingdoms]]. The story starts in the north and works its way through the zone to culminate at the [[Dark Portal]], where it leads to the [[Outland]]. The final quests in the zone center around {{NPC||Marl Wormthorn}} and the [[Tainted Forest]] in the southwest.

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Blasted Lands Quests]].
