==Description==
We arrived as soon as we could... but we are too late. [[Nethergarde]] has fallen. The [[Iron Horde]] has taken a number of Nethergarde warriors prisoner.

No doubt they are trying to squeeze vital information out of our troops.

I fear once they are done with their interrogation they will not hesitate to execute their prisoners. Time is running out.
