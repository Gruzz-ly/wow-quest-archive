== Objectives ==
Poison the [[Dreadmaul tribe|Dreadmaul ogre]] food supply.
*Poisoned [[Cooking Pot (Blasted Lands)|cooking pot]] x3

== Description ==
The Dreadmaul ogres think they have bested us? They will all suffer. Here take this poison, place a couple drops in their rat stew.

The poison will slowly eat away at their insides. Causing a prolonged and painful death, just like they deserve.

== Notes ==
Pick up [[Toothsmash the Annihilator (Horde)]] and [[Death to the Dreadmaul (Horde)]] before heading out.

West into [[Dreadmaul Post]]. Plenty of ogres to kill on the surface, as are the three ogre cookpots, which are arranged in a roughly north-south line. [[Toothsmash the Annihilator]] is between the two southern cookpots.
