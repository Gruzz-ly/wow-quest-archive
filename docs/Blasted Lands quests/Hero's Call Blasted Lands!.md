==Description==
By order of his royal highness, {{NPC||Varian Wrynn||King Varian Wrynn}}, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Quartermaster Lungertz at Nethergarde Keep.

Nethergarde's garrison urgently needs your help to stem the [[Horde]] advance into the [[Blasted Lands]]! Travel south from the [[Swamp of Sorrows]] and follow the road east to reach Nethergarde Keep.

For the glory and honor of the Alliance! 

==Completion==
For decades has the garrison at Nethergarde held firm against the threat of the Horde and we do not intend to surrender now.
