==Objectives==
Slay 8 Nethergarde Miners, 3 Nethergarde Engineers, and 3 Nethergarde Foremen.
*[[Nethergarde Miner]] slain (8)
*[[Nethergarde Engineer]] slain (3)
*[[Nethergarde Foreman]] slain (3)

==Description==
It costs the Alliance approximately fifty gold to train a miner, two hundred to train an engineer, and three hundred to train a foreman. Every Alliance goon that we kill isn't just a military victory... it's a financial one.

Now get down there and destroy their workers!
