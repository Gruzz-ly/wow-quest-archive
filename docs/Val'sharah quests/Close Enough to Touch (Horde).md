==Objectives==
Search for [[Malfurion]] within [[Shala'nir]].
* Follow Tyrande into Shala'nir
* Search for Malfurion at the Inn
* Search for Malfurion near the Lake
* Search for Malfurion in the Village
* Find Malfurion

==Description==
He is near, <race>, I can feel it.

We must search Shala'nir for Malfurion. If we do not find him soon, I fear he will be lost to me forever.

I was unsure of your worth when first we met. I know now that you will not fail me.

I am... grateful.
