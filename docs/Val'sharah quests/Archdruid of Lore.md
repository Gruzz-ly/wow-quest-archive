==Objectives==
Seek out [[Elothir]] in [[Thas'talah]]. 

==Description==
Elothir resides in the ancient grove Thas'talah, where he communes with nature and instructs his acolytes in the ways of healing and the preservation of lore.

Seek him out and tell him that he is desperately needed here.
