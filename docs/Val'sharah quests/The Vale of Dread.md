== Notes ==
Triggers at Moonclaw Vale and [[Bladesong's Retreat]] in [[Val'sharah]] during the [[Legion Assaults]]. Clear out the demons surrounding the [[Spire of Dread|Spires of Dread]]:

* [[Legion Vanguard (Legion Assaults)|Legion Vanguard]]
* [[Eredar Felweaver]]
* [[Felclaw Fiend]]

Use your special action button Throw Seed of Ruin (40 yd range) to destroy the Spire of Dread. If you have flying, you can just use it from the air to complete the first part, and avoid combat altogether. Once completed, Doomcaller Noromonde will spawn from the demon portal.
