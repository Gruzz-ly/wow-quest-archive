'''Ruins of Shala'nir''' is a bonus objective that unlocks after accepting [[Close Enough to Touch]] during the [[Val'sharah storyline]].

==Objectives==
*The Ruined Village (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*200x [[Order Resources]]
*14,800 [[XP]]

==Progression==
{{Tears of Elune}}

==Notes==
*The bonus objective takes place in [[Shala'nir]].
*Eligible mobs are: [[Fallen Moonfeather]]s, [[Creeping Horror]]s, [[Shadowy Nightmare]]s, [[Dreadheart Ruiner]]s, [[Defiled Grovewalker]]s, [[Defiled Grovewalker]]s, [[Desecrated Ancient]]s, [[Darkfiend Defiler]], [[Vilepetal Rooter]]. You can also interact with [[Nightmare Totem]]s.
