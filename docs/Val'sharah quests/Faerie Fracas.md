'''Faerie Fracas''' is a [[bonus objective]] that takes place at the [[Lunarwing Shallows]] in southern [[Val'sharah]], where [[Thistleleaf]] sprites are tormenting the [[Lunarwing]] faerie dragons.

==Objectives==
*Faerie Fracas (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 350 [[XP]]

==Notes==
Objectives: Kill [[Thistleleaf Ruffian]]s (3%), [[Thistleleaf Menace]]s (3%), and [[Thistleleaf Thorndancer]]s (5%); destroy [[Thistleleaf Snack]]s (1%); and free [[Trapped Lunarwing]]s (4%).
