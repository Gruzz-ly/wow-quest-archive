==Description==
Ahh, Val'sharah.

It has been an age since I have set foot in these sacred woods. Too long...

Our work here is simple, <class>. We must call upon my mentor, [[Cenarius]], and learn where he safeguards the [[Tears of Elune]]. With them, you will be one step closer to sealing [[Tomb of Sargeras|Sargeras' Tomb]] and thwarting the [[Burning Legion|Legion]].

You have many treacherous tasks in front of you. Consider your visit here a brief respite.

Come, this will not take long.
