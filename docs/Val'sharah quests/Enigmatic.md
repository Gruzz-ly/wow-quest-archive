==Notes==
You must win a total of three games to complete the quest, but not in a row. While in phase, you'll be debuffed with "Enigma: Solving a puzzle."

: What a fascinating discovery, <name>. This device seems to be guarded by some puzzle.

''{{gossip|Um, what?}}''

: Head over the{{sic}} cube and interact with it to start a round of its game. Simply follow the path it displays before filling out the entire grid and you'll score a round. Score enough rounds and the cube should activate for you.
