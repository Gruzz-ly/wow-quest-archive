==Description==
The numberless years of my life have been dedicated to learning and perfecting the arts of healing and restoration.

Nothing in this world pains me more than taking a life, traveler.

It is with a heavy heart that I must commission the death of Magula, the leader of the [[Harpy|harpies]] that threaten this grove.

May her death save the lives of more noble beings.
