== Objectives ==
* [[Maelisandra Venitox]] slain
* [[Poisonous Mushroom (Val'sharah)|Poisonous Mushroom]]s destroyed (10)
* [[Poisonous Slime]] slain (12)

== Description ==
: {{text|say|[[Celestine of the Harvest]]|A foul poison seeps into the waters of [[Mistvale]]. I feel in my bones that it must be the work of a corrupt [[druid]]. Seek out the defiler and end this affliction.}}

== Rewards ==
You will receive:
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Dreamweavers]]

== Completion ==
: {{text|say|Celestine of the Harvest|How could anyone become so twisted inside that they would infect such a beautiful lake? At least you set things right.}}

== Notes ==
Triggers at Mistvale in [[Val'sharah]]. While on the quest you'll be buffed with [[Aessina's Blessing]], a waterwalking buff that works as long as you don't get hit. Once hit, you'll need to be out of combat for it to work again.
