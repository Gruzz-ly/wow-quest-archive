# Searing Gorge storyline

[[File:WorldMap-SearingGorge-cata.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Searing Gorge]]''' is a neutral level {{level|Searing Gorge}} zone in southwestern [[Khaz Modan]] of the [[Eastern Kingdoms]]. The main storyline deals with the [[Dark Iron clan]]'s involvement with the [[Twilight's Hammer]] and the forces of {{NPC||Ragnaros}}.

Players are directed to Searing Gorge upon the conclusion of the [[Badlands storyline]], starting on the path from [[Badlands]] at [[Thorium Advance]], and then looped around the zone via [[Thorium Point]] and [[Iron Summit]], then down into the [[Cauldron]] and the [[Slag Pit]] to deal with {{NPC||Archduke Calcinder}}, the leader of Ragnaros' forces.

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Searing Gorge Quests]].

Upon the conclusion of the Searing Gorge storyline, players are directed through [[Blackrock Mountain]] to the [[Burning Steppes storyline|Burning Steppes]] with the quest [[Mouton Flamestar (quest)]].
