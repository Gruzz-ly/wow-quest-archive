==Objectives==
Report to [[Overseer Oilfist]] at [[Thorium Point]] in [[Searing Gorge]].

==Description==
You should return to Thorium Point. Overseer Oilfirst and the rest of the [[Thorium Brotherhood]] will be wondering how you fared in the fight against [[Archduke Calcinder|Calcinder]].

==Rewards==
You will be able to choose one of these rewards:
*[[Pauldrons of the Thorium Brotherhood]]
*[[Hammer of the Thorium Brotherhood]]
*[[Amulet of the Thorium Brotherhood]]

You will also receive:
*{{cost|2|20}}
*1,000 [[XP]]
*+2,000 [[reputation]] with [[Thorium Brotherhood]]
*[[Shadowforge Grinding Wheel]]

==Completion==
Our task is complete, and it's all thanks to you, <name>. I'd like to offer you your choice from our coffers. In addition, I intend to make you a full-fledged member of our order.

Welcome to the Thorium Brotherhood, <name>. I'm proud to call you a <brother/sister>.

==Notes==
Kill a path out of the [[Slag Pit]] by taking rights until reaching the metal bridge, then taking a left to head outside. Jump down into the [[Cauldron]] and run back to Thorium Point.

This concludes the [[Searing Gorge storyline]]! Up next, the [[Burning Steppes storyline]] in [[Mouton Flamestar]]!
