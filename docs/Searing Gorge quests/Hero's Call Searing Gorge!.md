==Objectives==
Report to {{NPC||Jack Rockleg}} at [[Thorium Advance]] in the [[Searing Gorge]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, {{NPC||Varian Wrynn||King Varian Wrynn}}, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Jack Rockleg at Thorium Advance.

[[SI:7]] reports that the [[Dark Iron clan|Dark Iron dwarves]] of the Searing Gorge seem to be preparing to attack. Your duty is to learn all you can about their scheming and put a halt to it.

You can reach the Searing Gorge by following the road west from the [[Badlands]]. 

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!

==Completion==
