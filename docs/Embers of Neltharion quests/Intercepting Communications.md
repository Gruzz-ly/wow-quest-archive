==Description==
<Name>, we need intel, and we need it now.

Somewhere within one of the [[djaradin]]'s encampments, you will find their field [[General (rank)|general]]. He is a seasoned and formidable opponent, responsible for many attacks on our forces.

According to our field agents, he may possess crucial military orders outlining their next offense and the locations of their new supply lines.

It is imperative that we obtain these orders. We cannot allow the djaradin to gain the upper hand.
