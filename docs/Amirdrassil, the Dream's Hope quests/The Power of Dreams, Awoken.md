'''The Power of Dreams''' is the final step to getting the [[Incandescent Essence]] helm enchant. The previous quest is time-gated and the earliest players can complete it is the week of 12 December 2023.

==Objectives==
Awaken the power within the Fused Essence.
* Empower the [[Fused Essence]]

==Description==
Are you ready, <name>? I will fuse the essences of their fiery dreams together for you. Focus the Echoed Ephemera you have collected to awaken the latent power within. Show [[Fyrakk]] that even he should fear the power of [[Emerald Dream|the Dream]]!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Incandescent Essence]]
* {{cost|421|35}}

==Completion==
The power of the Incandescent Essence is yours.

==Notes==
Use the provided [[Fused Essence]] to complete the quest objective.
