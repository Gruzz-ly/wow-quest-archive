'''What the Duck?''' begins the [[Noblegarden]] storyline added in 2024. It starts in [[Goldshire]].

==Objectives==
Scare off 10 ducks using the Horn of Honking.
* [[Furious Duck|Ducks]] scared (10)
* [[Horn of Honking]] (provided)

==Description==
What the--why are all these ducks here? Something has them agitated. They almost knocked over my beautiful golden egg heirloom!

We need to clear out these chaotic quackers so we can continue our festivities. I'll keep them from entering the inn, but can you help clear out the rest?

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Brightly Colored Egg]]

==Progress==
Show those ducks who's boss!

==Completion==
Those ducks sure made a mess of things. Thanks, <name>.

==Notes==
Use the provided [[Horn of Honking]] on [[Furious Duck]]s in the vicinity.
