This quest starts at the Bounty Board and requires you to get [[James Clark]]'s head. 

==Objectives==
Kill [[James Clark]] and take James Clark's Head.
* [[James Clark's Head]]

==Description==
By decree of the [[Stormwind Army]], a bounty has been placed on the head of James Clark.

Clark is wanted for robbery, burglary, arson, and murder. He is also a suspect in the kidnapping and disappearance of the Brackwell's prized pig, [[Princess (boar)|Princess]].

James Clark was last seen heading towards Eastvale Logging Camp.

Bring James Clark to justice and report to [[Marshal Patterson]] at [[Eastvale Logging Camp]]. 

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 200 [[XP]]
* +350 [[reputation]] with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]

==Progress==
Turning in a bounty?

==Completion==
And justice is served!

James Clark is in the house next to [[Sara Timberlain]], who gives the [[Fine Linen Goods]] quest.
