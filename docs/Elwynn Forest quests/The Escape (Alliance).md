==Description==
Take this invisibility liquor to young Maybell.  It should last long enough for her to visit Tommy Joe.

*630 [[XP]] (or {{cost||3|90}} at lvl 90)
*+250 [[reputation]] with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]
