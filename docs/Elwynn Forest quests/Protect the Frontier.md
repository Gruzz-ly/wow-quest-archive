Kill 8 Prowlers or Gray Forest Wolves and 5 Young Forest Bears, and then return to [[Guard Thomas]] at the east Elwynn bridge.
* Prowler or [[Gray Forest Wolf|Forest Wolf]] slain  (8)
* [[Young Forest Bear]] slain (5)

==Description==
