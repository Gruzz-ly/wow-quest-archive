'''Feathered Fiend''' is the daily quest of the 2024 iteration of [[Noblegarden]]. The [[Noblegarden Trinket]] drops from [[Daetan Swiftplume]], the boss of the event.

==Objectives==
Bring the Noblegarden Trinket to [[Zinnia Brooks]] at [[Goldshire]].
*[[Noblegarden Trinket]] (provided)

==Description==
The trinket appears to be some sort of [[Noblegarden]] decoration. It should probably be returned to the festivities in Goldshire.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Loot-Filled Basket]]

==Completion==
Ah, thank you for returning this. I'll see to it that any stolen decorations are restored!
