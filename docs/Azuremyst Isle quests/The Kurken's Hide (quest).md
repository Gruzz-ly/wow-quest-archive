This quest is part of the {{questchain|Stillpine Hold}} quest chain.

==Objectives==
Deliver the Kurken's Hide to [[Moordo]] at [[Stillpine Hold]].
*[[The Kurken's Hide]] (provided)

==Description==
Take the Kurken's hide to Moordo, on the other side of the village. He will fashion the hide into an item of your choice.
