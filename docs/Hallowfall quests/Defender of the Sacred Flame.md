'''A Defender of the Sacred Flame''' is the Renown 10 quest with [[Flame's Radiance]] at [[Morgaen's Tears]] in southwestern [[Hallowfall]].

==Completion==
You've certainly proven yourself in our fight against the Sureki, <name>.

Your courage and willingness to defend the Sacred Flame is remarkable, and your martial prowess has helped turn the tide of battle more times than I've been able to count.

For that, I award you this tabard. Please accept it as an emblem and acknowledgement of your undying persistence.
