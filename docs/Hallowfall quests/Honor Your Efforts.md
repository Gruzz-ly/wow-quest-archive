==Description==
Though my rank is humble, I bestow upon you this honor. A tinderbox, signifying your friendship with our expedition and your esteemed status in Hallowfall.

Should you fall in battle, your tinderbox shall be given a place of honor among the other great heroes who died defending this land.

Please accept this honor.
