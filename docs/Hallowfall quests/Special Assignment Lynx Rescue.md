'''Special Assignment: Lynx Rescue''' is a [[Special Assignment]] world quest that takes place at the [[Sanguine Grasps]] in [[Hallowfall]].

==Objectives==
*Find and befriend a lost lynx
*Hunt [[Vanishing Nightmare]]s (4)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Faerin Lothar]]|Some [[Imperial lynx|lynx]] get lost in the darkness. They're nocturnal, but it's dangerous. Bring them home.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 12,200 [[XP]]
* [[Seasoned Adventurer's Cache]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Faerin Lothar|Even fierce predators can feel scared. I'm glad you led them home.}}

==Notes==
*Lynxes that may be befriended include [[Amber (imperial lynx)|Amber]], [[Fluffy (imperial lynx)|Fluffy]], [[Gustavo]], [[Prickles]], and [[Twinkle (imperial lynx)|Twinkle]].
*While traveling with a lost lynx, an [[Extra Action Button]] is available:
*:{{abilities|Crush Windpipe|Lunges and crushes the target's throat, preventing them from escaping.|notes=[[Silence effect|Silences]] and [[root]]s the target for 15 seconds.|id=453262|icon=ability_druid_ferociousbite}}
