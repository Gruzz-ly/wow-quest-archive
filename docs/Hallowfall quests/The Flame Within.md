==Objectives==
Seek out and gain the aid of [[Speaker Kuldas]] in [[Gundargaz]].

==Description==
When sparked, a tinderbox creates a flame that embodies the essence of its bearer's life journey. It is a reflection of your strength and inner spirit.

In your travels, you have met many who could help us fashion a tinderbox capable of igniting a flame befitting your formidable spirit.

Seek the assistance of Speaker Kuldas in Gundargaz. The earthen's knowledge will help create a more powerful tinderbox.
