'''The Flame Burns Eternal''' is the weekly quest for the [[Nightfall scenario]] and the [[Flame's Radiance]] at [[Morgaen's Tears]] in southwestern [[Hallowfall]]. The Nightfall scenario takes place hourly at the top of the hour (at xx:00).

==Objectives==
Complete the [[Nightfall scenario]].
* Complete the scenario "Nightfall"

==Description==
These Sureki sure are a persistent bunch! We need to match their persistence with our blades.

Can we count on your continued support in the battles ahead?

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Radiant Service Satchel]]
* 25 [[Valorstones]]
* 15x [[Carved Undermine Crest]]
* +400 [[reputation]] with [[Flame's Radiance]] (via [[Flame's Radiance (currency)]])

==Progress==
Ready yourself soldier, the battle is still to come.

==Completion==
You've bought us a moment of peace.

Rest well, but always keep your blade sharp.
