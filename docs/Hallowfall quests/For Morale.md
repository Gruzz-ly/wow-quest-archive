==Description==
Please excuse [[Derill Fayn|my husband]]'s rudeness. When we signed up for [[Hallowfall Arathi|our expedition]], we expected to be away from our families for a few years at worst. Instead, we settled here as we guard against--well, against the end, against [[Renilash]]. Who knows when our watch will end?

I've been stockpiling a cache of wine from our church vineyards to share with everyone when tensions got high. My hiding place, Rumshackle, has been overrun by creatures. Can you get the wine for me?
