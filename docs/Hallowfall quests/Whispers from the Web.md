'''Whispers from the Web''' becomes available at [[Renown]] level 22 with the [[Hallowfall Arathi]]. It's offered by a [[Mereldar Child]], who appears and follows the player around [[Mereldar]] until they accept the quest.

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Ct'nak]] in the [[Burrows]] of [[Azj-Kahet]].

==Description==
Hey! Hey!

<The girl waves her arms to get your attention, a scrap of paper clutched in one hand.>

You won't believe me, but I found the cutest, fuzziest puppy! With a bell! Around its neck!

It gave me this to give to you. Take it, I gotta go catch that puppy!

<The missive is written in elegant script, calling you to Azj-Kahet.>
