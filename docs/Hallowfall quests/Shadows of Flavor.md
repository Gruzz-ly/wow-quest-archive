==Objectives==
Collect 10 Shadowblind Grouper, 5 Dark-Nurtured Scallions, and a Vial of Twilight Spice.
*[[Dark-Nurtured Scallions]] (5)
*[[Shadowblind Grouper]] (10)
*[[Twilight Spice]]

==Description==
I've set out to create a meal that transcends the ordinary, one that even the emperor wouldn't turn away.

I require ingredients from the darkest of shadows to craft this extraordinary meal. Many consider these ingredients forbidden, but they will become a harmony of light and shadow in my hands.

Please assist me by harvesting the following ingredients: Shadowblind Grouper, Dark-Nurtured Scallions, and a Vial of Twilight Spice.
