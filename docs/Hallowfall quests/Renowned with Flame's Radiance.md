'''Renowned with Flame's Radiance''' is offered to players who have filled out the Paragon reputation with [[Flame's Radiance]] at [[Morgaen's Tears]] in southwest [[Hallowfall]].

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Lars Bronsmaelt]] in [[Morgaen's Tears]].

==Description==
You continue to inspire us on the battlefield, <name>. Your heroic deeds have kept our people safe during these trying times, and we cannot thank you enough.

==Rewards==

==Completion==
