==Description==
The [[kobyss]] normally live deep underwater, <name>. When [[Beledar]] is at its brightest, they don't bother us. Other sources of bright light will usually keep them away, but definitely weaken them.

As [[Lamplighters]], we not only go into the dark to protect those in need, but we provide them the light that will keep them safe. Go set up these torches along the perimeter, then meet us up at the dawntower behind Velhan's Claim.
