'''The Light's Call''' starts an optional chapter of the [[Hallowfall storyline]], started by a poster outside the inn of [[Mereldar]] for players at max level.

==Objectives==
Report to [[Keyrra Flamestonge]] for further service.

==Description==
Attention, residents of Hallowfall!

Urgent assistance is needed to reclaim the shadowed outskirts.

As darkness grips countless family farms, your aid is needed to ignite the sacred flame and restore safety to our lands.
