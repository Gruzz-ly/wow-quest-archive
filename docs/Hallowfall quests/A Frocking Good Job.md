'''A Frocking Good Job''' is the Renown 5 quest with [[Flame's Radiance]] at [[Morgaen's Tears]] in southwestern [[Hallowfall]].

==Completion==
<Name>! Glad you could make it. I wanted to formally congratulate you on your new position within our ranks.

You've more than earned this, demonstrating courage and traits that show great promise as you continue serving Flame's Radiance.

On that note, please accept and don this tabard in your future endeavors. We look forward to seeing you on the battlefield.
