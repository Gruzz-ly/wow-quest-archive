'''A Radiant Call''' breadcrumbs players to the [[Flame's Radiance]] faction and the [[Nightfall scenario]] at [[Morgaen's Tears]] in southwestern [[Hallowfall]]. Players will be pushed the quest automatically after logging in during [[patch 11.1.5]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Mylton Wyldbraun]] at the [[Flame's Radiance]] camp in [[Hallowfall]].

==Description==
I have an urgent missive for <name> from Mylton Wyldbraun, second in command of the Flame's Radiance!

Hallowfall requires what strength we can muster and the Flame's Radiance seeks volunteers to join the fight against the remaining [[Sureki]] forces.

Please report to Wyldbraun without delay.

==Completion==
I see you received my message <name>, wonderful! We can always use more capable soldiers among the ranks of the Flame's Radiance.
