'''Welcome to the Field''' is the Renown 1 quest with [[Flame's Radiance]] at [[Morgaen's Tears]] in southwestern [[Hallowfall]].

==Completion==
Welcome, cadet! Glad to have you here.

No time like the present to get in there. Fight the Sureki with everything you've got--we're counting on you.
