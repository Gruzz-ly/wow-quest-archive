==Objectives==
Collect 8 Darkroot Persimmon, 30 Frog-Kissed Caviar and 1 Ethereal Sugar.
* [[Frog-Kissed Caviar]] (30)
* [[Darkroot Persimmon]] (8)
* [[Ethereal Sugar]]

==Description==
I've set out to create a dessert that transcends the ordinary, one that even the most devout followers of the [[light]] would savor!

To balance the flavors of the desert, between light and shadow, I require ingredients from several areas within the darkness.

Please assist me in harvesting the following ingredients: Frog-Kissed Caviar, Darkroot Persimmon and Ethereal Sugar.
