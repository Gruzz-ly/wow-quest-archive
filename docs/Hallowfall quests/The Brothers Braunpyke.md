'''The Brothers Braunpyke''' begins an optional chapter of the [[Hallowfall storyline]]: The Priory. [[General Steelstrike]] in the church of [[Mereldar]] begins the chapter with no other prerequisites.

==Objectives==
Meet the Braunpyke brothers near the [[Veneration Grounds]].

==Description==
[[Baron Braunpyke]] and his brother have been working on removing our lingering [[kobyss]] problem from near the Veneration Grounds.

Meet with them there and see what help you can offer.
