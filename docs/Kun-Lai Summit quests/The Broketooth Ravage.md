==Objectives==
Retrieve Rabbitsfoot's Luckydo from [[Mo Mo]].
*[[Rabbitsfoot's Luckydo]]

==Description==
They took my luckydo! Their leader [[hozen]] here, Mo Mo, stole my rabbitsfoot.

This is most bad fortune for [[grummle]]s. My rabbitsfoot is a big luckydo for grummles everywhere. If hozen learn how to get luck from my rabbitsfoot then all is lost.

Please can you get it back?

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|10|60}}
*162000 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Grummles named for the luckydo that gives the most fortune to specific grummle. It is most important that hozen not steal a grummle's luck.

==Completion==
My luckydo! Oh happy day of good fortune! Many thanks!

==Notes==
On [[The Rabbitsfoot]], adventurers are asked to find [[Brother Rabbitsfoot]] on the [[Burlap Trail]]. He can be found hanging upside down from a tree at the [[Broketooth Outpost]], on the south side of the trail near its western end. He offers this quest.

On accept:
:{{text|say|Brother Rabbitsfoot|Thank you for the rescue! Meet me at [[Burlap Waystation]].}}
Head up the hill to the back of the Broketooth Outpost to find Mo Mo, inside a dug-out hut inside the mountain. He has the following abilities:
*{{abilities|Wrath|range=40|Inflicts 9822 to 9827 Nature damage.|notes=2 sec cast. Hits for 8400-8600.}}
*{{abilities|Healing Touch|range=40|Calls upon Nature magic to heall any ally.|notes=3 sec cast. Heals for 42555 Nature}}
*{{abilities|Giant Growth|Increases damage and size by 100% and silences self for 10 sec.|notes=2 sec cast.}}
Interrupt the heal, otherwise the encounter is straightfoward. Loot the luckydo, then head to the northeast to find the [[Burlap Waystation]].

On reaching Rabbitsfoot:
:{{text|say|Brother Rabbitsfoot|I smell my luckydo! Oh please bring it to me.}}
