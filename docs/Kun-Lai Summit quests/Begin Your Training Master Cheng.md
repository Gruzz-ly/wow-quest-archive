'''Begin Your Training: Master Cheng''' is the first of several "sparring" quests designed to teach players of the {{class|monk}} class how to use their basic abilities in combat, in addition to general situational awareness. Defeating {{NPC||Master Cheng}} requires timely use of the [[Roll (monk ability)]] ability to avoid an [[area of effect]] (AoE) attack.

==Objectives==
Speak with Master Cheng in the Training Grounds and complete your training with him.
*Master Cheng's Training Completed

==Description==
Greetings, <name>. Welcome to the [[Peak of Serenity]]. It is here that we monks train and hone our skills in the martial arts. The time has come for you to begin your training. Speak with Master Cheng in the Training Grounds below us to begin.
