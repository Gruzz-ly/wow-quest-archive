== Notes ==
After killing the fourth yaungol, In Tents will complete automatically. This quest is then automatically offered. Head up the hill to the north in the cave and kill the warbringer. He is a level 87 yaungol with 429,000 health and shaman abilities.

;Aggro:
:{{text|yell|Ordo Warbringer|I've been expecting you, <name>!}}

;50% shield cast:
:{{text|yell|Ordo Warbringer|Spirits and elements of Pandaria, I call upon you!  Destroy <name>!}}
At 50% health, he will pop an immunity shield and spawn four {{NPC||Spirit Worg}}s with 53,746 health. Kill them to drop the shield.

;Killing the four spirit worgs
:{{text|yell|Ordo Warbringer|What?!  I will finish you myself!}}

;Death:
:{{text|say|Ordo Warbringer|Our homes... destroyed...  We did what we had to....}}

Finish up at the fire camp, then return to Eastwind to turn in. This quest completes "The Yaungol Invasion" part of [[Slum It in the Summit]].
