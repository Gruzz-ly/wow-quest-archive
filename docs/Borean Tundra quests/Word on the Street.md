'''Word on the Street''' starts the [[death knight]] introduction quest chain for [[Alliance]] players in [[Borean Tundra]]. It becomes available after players complete the quest [[Plug the Sinkholes]]

==Objectives==
Find [[Leryssa]] at the inn in [[Valiance Keep]].

==Description==
Hey, <name>. There's a girl in the inn that's asking about you. Her name's Leryssa.

I don't think she knows you, but she said she needed your help. She sounded really desperate.

==Completion==
Thank you for coming to see me. I didn't know who else to contact.

==Notes==
This quest becomes available after completing [[Plug the Sinkholes]].
