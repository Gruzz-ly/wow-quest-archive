==Objectives==
[[Warmage Anzim|Anzim]] on [[Amber Ledge]] wants you to fly with his war mages to free [[Lady Evanor]] at [[Beryl Point]]. You are then to report to her in the [[mage tower]] on Amber Ledge.

==Description==
At last, we attack!

Once we've cleared the platform I'll use the key.

You are to ensure that Lady Evanor remains safe after her release.

Hurry now, let us be off!

==Rewards==
You will be allowed to choose one of these rewards:
* [[Sweltering Belt]]
* [[Charred Treads]]
* [[Glimmering Ringmail Gloves]]
* [[Seething Waistguard]]

You will also gain:
* {{cost|4|70}}
* 20,100 [[XP]]
* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Kirin Tor]]

==Completion==
Your heroic efforts shall not be forgotten, <class>.

Please, accept this with my sincere gratitude.

==Notes==
The drakes behind Anzim must be waiting to attack before you start the quest.

Once on the quest, sit back and enjoy the ride.
