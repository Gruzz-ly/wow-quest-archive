==Objectives==
Use Imperean's Primal to perform a ritual at [[Snarlfang's Totem]] at [[Bor'gorok Outpost]] to gain information concerning [[Farseer Grimwalker]].

Afterward, speak with Spirit Talker Snarlfang.
*[[Farseer Grimwalker]]'s fate divined
*[[Imperean's Primal#Vision of Air]] (provided)

==Description==
Here, <name>, I've attuned the primal to you.

Open your soul to it in the presence of my totem of air and concentrate upon the fate of Farseer Grimwalker.

The spirit of the wind touches all; with any luck you will be granted a vision.
