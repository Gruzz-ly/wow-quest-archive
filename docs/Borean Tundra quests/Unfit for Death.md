==Objectives==
[[Zaza]] at the [[D.E.H.T.A. Encampment]] in the [[Borean Tundra]] wants you to use the Pile of Fake Furs to trap 8 Nesingwary Trappers.
*[[Nesingwary Trapper]] Trapped (8)
*[[Pile of Fake Furs]] (provided)

==Description==
Just south of us the [[caribou]] used to roam freely, peacefully. Then [[Hemet Nesingwary|Nesingwary]] showed up and everything changed. Now the caribou are in a perpetual state of frenzy, always in a panic. They are stalked by the hunters and trappers hidden in the field.

Nesingwary's executioners are unfit for death. They must feel the torture that they put the caribou through!

Take this pile of fake furs and lay it upon their [[Caribou Trap|caribou traps]] to the south. Watch as the hunters are caught in their own deadly traps!
