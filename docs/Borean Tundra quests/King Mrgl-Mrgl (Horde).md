This is the Horde introduction to the plight of the Winterfin murlocs, who are led by a druid in disguise!

==Objectives==
Speak with [[King Mrgl-Mrgl]] at [[Winterfin Retreat]].

==Description==
Dis is gonna sound weird, <class>, but dere be a murloc askin' for our help!

He goes by da name of King Mrgl-Mrgl, leader of da [[Winterfin tribe|Winterfin murlocs]]. Him and his people been run outa dere village and wantin' you to come save dem.

You find him by headin' west and go down da cliffs. He hangin' out on da south side of da [[Winterfin Retreat]].

==Completion==

That's alright... your people are very skilled in the art of combat. We're going to need a lot of that.
