==Description==
One day every year, usually in mid-spring the [[Winterfin]] tadpoles are given free reign of the [[Winterfin Retreat|village]]. I'm not sure why this tradition persists, as it usually results in heavy damage to their building and food stores.

I usually try not to interfere too much with the goings-on of the tribe, but today, I make an exception. Go out and see if you can keep an eye on one of the tadpoles.
