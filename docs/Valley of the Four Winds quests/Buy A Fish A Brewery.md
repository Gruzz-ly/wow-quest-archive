This is the last of several quests to buy Jogu the Drunk alcohol to mark a new level of [[Reputation#Individuals|friendship]] with him. It becomes available after becoming a {{reputation|Revered|Reputation#Individuals|Good Friend}}, providing an immediate head start on the way to becoming his {{reputation|Exalted|Reputation#Individuals|Best Friend}}.

==Objectives==
Bring 20 Mad Brewer's Breakfast to [[Jogu the Drunk]]. Mad Brewer's Breakfasts can be made using the cooking profession.
*[[Mad Brewer's Breakfast]] (20)
