==Objectives==
Bring 5 [[Giant Mantis Shrimp]] to the [[Yan Ironpaw|Master of the Steamer]].

==Description==
A great meal can lift your spirits, and none more so than food from the [[Way of the Steamer]]. Bring the Master some giant mantis shrimp and he will show you. You can fish for giant mantis shrimp in the ocean.
