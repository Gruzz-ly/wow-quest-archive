This is the first of several quests to buy Jogu the Drunk alcohol to mark a new level of [[Reputation#Individuals|friendship]] with him. It becomes available after becoming an {{reputation|Neutral|Reputation#Individuals|Acquaintance}}, providing an immediate head start on the way to becoming his {{reputation|Friendly|Reputation#Individuals|Buddy}}.

==Objectives==
Bring 1 Four Wind Soju to [[Jogu the Drunk]]. Four Wind Soju can be purchased from most bartenders in [[Pandaria]].
*[[Four Wind Soju]]
