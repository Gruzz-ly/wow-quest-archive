This is the third of several quests to buy Jogu the Drunk alcohol to mark a new level of [[Reputation#Individuals|friendship]] with him. It becomes available after becoming his {{reputation|Honored|Reputation#Individuals|Friend}}, providing an immediate head start on the way to becoming a {{reputation|Revered|Reputation#Individuals|Good Friend}}.

==Objectives==
Bring 10 Jade Witch Brew to [[Jogu the Drunk]]. Jade Witch Brew can be made using the cooking profession.
*[[Jade Witch Brew]] (10)
