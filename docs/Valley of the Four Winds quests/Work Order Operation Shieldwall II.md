==Objectives==
[[File:Empty Crate.jpg|thumb|[[Empty Crate]].]]
Harvest 8 Ripe Witchberry plants and provide 40 Witchberries for the [[Operation: Shieldwall]].
*Harvest Ripe Witchberries (8)
*[[Witchberries]] (40)

==Description==
Once your witchberries are ready to be picked, you'll need only to package them for pick-up.

Just think; your witchberries will be a part of your factions efforts in Pandaria!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50|lcgf=1}}
*236000 [[XP]]
*+400 [[reputation]] with [[Operation: Shieldwall]]

==Progress==
The crate sits empty awaiting your crops.

==Completion==
Throw the Witchberries into the crate! An Operation: Shieldwall representative will be along shortly for pickup.

==Notes==
After planting the 8 [[Witchberry Seeds]] the day before, harvest the ripe [[Witchberries]] and put them in the [[Empty Crate]] just to the southwest of the plots.

On complete:
