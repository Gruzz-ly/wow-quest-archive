==Progress==

*[[Sauteed Carrots]] (5)

==Completion==
Nothing like a delicious cold brew, and some Sauteed Carrots to wash it down.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Valor Points]] (5)
*[[Lesser Charm of Good Fortune]]
*+1,800 [[reputation]] with [[Jogu the Drunk]]

==Notes==
Like the other "dish gifts" for [[Tillers]] NPCs, this quest is only offered if the appropriate food is already in the player's bags.
