==Objectives==
Make 5 Rice Pudding and give them to the [[Sungshin Ironpaw|Guide to the Ways]].
*Make 5 Rice Pudding
*5 [[Rice Pudding]]

==Description==
You seem to know your way around the kitchen. Demonstrate to me your skills and make me 5 bowls of rice pudding.

==Rewards==
You will learn:
*Zen Master Cook

You will also receive: {{cost|1|14||it=1}}

==Progress==
I believe you have the potential to be a great chef, but the proof is in the pudding, so to speak.

==Completion==

Available at 500 skill in [[cooking]]. Rice Pudding requires 520 cooking to make, and the recipe can be learned from the quest giver.
