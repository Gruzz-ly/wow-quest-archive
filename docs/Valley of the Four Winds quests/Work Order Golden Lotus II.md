==Objectives==
[[File:Empty Crate.jpg|thumb|[[Empty Crate]].]]
Harvest 8 Ripe White Turnip plants and provide 40 White Turnips for the [[Golden Lotus]].
*Harvest Ripe Ripe White Turnips (8)
*[[White Turnip]] (40)

==Description==
Well, hopefully these crops will be suitable!

Once your White Turnips are ready to be picked, you'll need only to package them for pick-up.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50|lcgf=1}}
*236000 [[XP]]
*+400 [[reputation]] with [[Golden Lotus]]

==Progress==
The crate sits empty awaiting your crops.

==Completion==
Throw the White Turnips into the crate! A Golden Lotus representative will be along shortly for pick-up.

==Notes==
After planting the 8 [[White Turnip Seeds]] the day before, harvest the ripe [[White Turnip]]s and put them in the [[Empty Crate]] just to the southwest of the plots.

On complete:
