== Objectives ==
Collect 20 Tasty T-Bone from the [[Skyrange Mushan]] on the [[Skyrange]] in the [[Valley of the Four Winds]].
* [[Tasty T-Bone]] (20)

== Description ==
The starving, mangy dog whines piteously and attempts a brief wag of its tail.

It looks like it could use a good meal right about now.

== Rewards ==
You will receive: {{cost|19|84|50}}

== Progress ==
A light comes into the dog's eyes. It seems to recognize you.

== Completion ==
The dog perks its ears up at the sight of the juicy steak.

As soon as you throw it down, the dog starts eating ravenously.

== Notes ==
* You will need to be over 12600 into {{rep|Revered}} in order to get this quest.
* The Lost Dog is found at {{co|42.4|50|Valley of the Four Winds}}.
