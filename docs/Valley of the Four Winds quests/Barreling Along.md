==Objectives==
Lead [[Mudmug]] back to [[Halfhill]].

If you become separated from Mudmug, you can find him next to the giant banana in the [[Gilded Fan]].
*Lead Mudmug back to Halfhill

==Description==
No, no, no, don't you worry about pickin' 'em up. I can hold these here barrels.

I may not be the handsomest pandaren, or the smartest, or any fun to be around in the slightest, but I'm stronger than a mushan beast.

I'mma just need you to lead the way.
