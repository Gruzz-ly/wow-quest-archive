==Description==
[[Haohan Mudclaw]] is the big boss of the [[Tillers]] union and the richest guy in the Valley! He's been nice to let me stay here, so I want to do something nice for him.

He likes expensive stuff, like ruby shards, but I don't have a lot of money, so I need to get him something else.

I know! I've seen a great big fish in [[Cattail Lake]]! We should get one of its teeth as a gift!
