==Objectives==
Harvest 8 Ripe Green Cabbage plants and provide 40 Green Cabbages for the [[Shado-Pan]].
*Harvest Ripe Green Cabbages (8)
*[[Green Cabbage]] (40)

==Description==
Well, your work is done for the day! Now to wait for the harvest tomorrow.

Once your Green Cabbages are ready to be picked, you'll need only to package them for pick-up.

Only the finest cabbage will do for the Shado-Pan!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50|lcgf=1}}
*236000 [[XP]]
*+400 [[reputation]] with [[Shado-Pan]]

==Progress==
The crate sits empty awaiting your crops.

==Completion==
Gently place the Green Cabbages into the crate! A Shado-Pan representative will be along shortly for pickup.

==Notes==
After planting the 8 [[Green Cabbage Seeds]] the day before, harvest the ripe [[Green Cabbage]]s and put them in the [[Empty Crate]] just to the southwest of the plots.
