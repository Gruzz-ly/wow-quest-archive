==Objectives==
Harvest 8 Ripe Red Blossom Leek plants and provide 40 Red Blossom Leeks for the [[Dominance Offensive]].
*Harvest Ripe Red Blossom Leeks (8)
*[[Red Blossom Leek]] (40)

==Description==
Once your Red Blossom Leeks are ready to be picked, you'll need only to package them for pick-up.

Just think; your Red Blossom Leeks will be a part of your factions efforts in Pandaria!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50|lcgf=1}}
* 236000 XP
* +400 reputation with [[Dominance Offensive]]

==Progress==
The crate sits empty awaiting your crops.

==Completion==
Throw the Red Blossom Leeks into the crate! A Dominance Offensive representative will be along shortly for pick-up.

==Notes==
After planting the 8 [[Red Blossom Leek Seeds]] the day before, harvest the ripe [[Red Blossom Leek]]s and put them in the [[Empty Crate]] just to the southwest of the plots.
