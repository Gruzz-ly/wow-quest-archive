This is the second of several quests to buy Jogu the Drunk alcohol to mark a new level of [[Reputation#Individuals|friendship]] with him. It becomes available after becoming his {{reputation|Friendly|Reputation#Individuals|Buddy}}, providing an immediate head start on the way to becoming his {{reputation|Honored|Reputation#Individuals|Friend}}.

==Objectives==
Bring 5 Pandaren Plum Wine to [[Jogu the Drunk]]. Pandaren Plum Wine can be purchased from most bartenders in [[Pandaria]].
*[[Pandaren Plum Wine]] (5)

==Description==
