== Description ==
<Yoon's familiar writing scrolls across the tattered paper.>

The trainees out at the [[Cradle of Chi-Ji]] are in need of [[Striped Melon]]s. Apparently, they use them in their training exercises.

A representative will be by tomorrow to collect whatever Striped Melons you can spare.

== Completion ==
Well, that's all you can do today! Now to wait for the harvest.

Your crops need only to be tended and prepared for packing tomorrow.
