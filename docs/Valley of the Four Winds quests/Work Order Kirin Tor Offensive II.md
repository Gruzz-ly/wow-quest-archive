==Objectives==
Harvest 8 Ripe Juicycrunch Carrot plants and provide 40 Juicycrunch Carrots for the [[Kirin Tor Offensive]].
* Harvest [[Ripe Juicycrunch Carrots]] (8)
* [[Juicycrunch Carrot]] (40)

==Description==
Once your juicycrunch carrots are ready to be picked, you'll need only to package them for pick-up.

Just think; your juicycrunch carrots will feed so many hungry soldiers on the front lines!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|11|40|}}
* 12,320 [[XP]]
* +400 [[reputation]] with [[Kirin Tor Offensive]]
*[[Lesser Charm of Good Fortune]]

==Progress==
The crate sits empty awaiting your crops.

==Completion==
Throw the juicycrunch carrots into the crate! A Kirin Tor Offensive [[Isirami Fairwind|representative]] will be along shortly for pick-up.
