== Objectives ==
Bring 5 [[Raw Tiger Steak]]s to the [[Kol Ironpaw|Master of the Grill]].
*[[Raw Tiger Steak]] x5

== Description ==
The Pandaren [[Way of the Grill]] produces foods that increase your physical [[strength]]. Bring some raw tiger steaks to the Master of the Grill, and he will teach you what you can do with them. There are many [[tiger]]s in the [[Jade Forest]] and the [[Krasarang Wilds]], but you can also find them in other places.
