== Objectives ==
Speak with Ben of the Booming Voice.

== Description ==
Are you looking for golden carp? or some other Pandaren fish? I'm no fisherman myself, but there is a fellow by the river who has been teaching the folks traveling through here. If you go speak with him, he will likely give you some fish, as well as teach you how to fish.
==Completion==
Ah!  Have you come to learn how to fish? You're talking to the right person. First, here - have some fish!
