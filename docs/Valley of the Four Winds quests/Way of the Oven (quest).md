==Objectives==
Bring 5 [[Wildfowl Breast]]s to the [[Jian Ironpaw|Master of the Oven]].
*[[Wildfowl Breast]] x5

==Description==
If you are looking for a hearty meal that will sustain you and increase your [[stamina]], you should seek out the [[Way of the Oven]]. Bring some wildfowl breasts to the Master of the Oven and she will teach you. There are plenty of [[crane]]s in the [[Valley of the Four Winds]] and in the [[Jade Forest]], but you can obtain wildfowl breasts from other birds as well.
