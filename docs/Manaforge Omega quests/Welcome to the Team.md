'''Welcome to the Team''' becomes available at [[Renown]] level 8 with the [[Manaforge Vandals]] on [[K'aresh]]. It has no objectives, as its only purpose is to grant a Renown reward.

==Description==
My friend, we have made much progress together in bringing down the [[Shadowguard]]. For your sacrifices and efforts, my colleagues and I consider you one of us. Please, take this modified Gearglider as a show of our gratitiude - may it carry you safely to your next mission.
