==Notes==
Karanosh spawns at {{co|42|36|Dread Wastes}} in front of the entrance to the [[Heart of Fear]] in the [[Clutches of Shek'zeer]] and patrols to the north along the trail. She drops [[Enormous Kunchong Mandibles]], which starts this quest.

A level 90 elite [[kunchong]], she has 2,363,000 health and the following special ability:
*{{abilities|Corrosive Resin|range=40|Spits a bolt of resin at an enemy, creating a pool that inflicts Nature damage every second.|notes=2 sec cast. Uninterruptible. Triggers...|cd=7 sec}}
**{{abilities|Corrosive Resin|Nature damage inflicted every second to all enemies within 4 yards.|notes=Ticks for 23,000 Nature.}}

She melees for upwards of 30,000 on cloth, so watch out!
