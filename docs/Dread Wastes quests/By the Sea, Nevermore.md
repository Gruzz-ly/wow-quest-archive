==Objectives==
[[File:By the Sea Nevermore - Kaz'tik unleashed.jpg|thumb|Kaz'tik unleashed.]]
[[File:Kaz'tik the Manipulator.jpg|thumb|[[Kaz'tik the Manipulator]].]]
Use the Kaxxi Tuning Fork on the Ocean-Worn Rocks to awaken the paragon.
*Awaken [[Kaz'tik the Manipulator]] {{co|43.3|63.5|Dread Wastes}}
*[[Klaxxi Tuning Fork#By the Sea Nevermore]] (provided)

==Description==
The Klaxxi'va have spoken to me, outsider. The paragon is hidden in the [[Briny Muck]] only, he is not so hidden anymore.

The [[saurok|lizard men]] surround him. He cannot fall into their scaley hands.

You will find him; you will awaken him. Do not return without him!

If you do this the Klaxxi will be pleased with us.
