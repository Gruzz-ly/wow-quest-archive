== Objectives ==
Collect 150 [[Scarab Wing]]s.

== Description ==
I require another ingredient to create my first poison, Wakener.

This ingredient you can easily find on the scarabs nearby.

Kill them and bring me their wings.

==Progress==
Scarab wings can be crushed to produce a very fine white, deadly powder.
==Completion==
Yes, excellent, Wakener! These are perfect.

Have you ever considered learning the poisoner's craft?
