==Objectives==
Kill 12 Kor'thik mantid in [[Kor'vess]].
* [[Kor'thik]] [[mantid]] slain (12)

==Description==
The mantid here are working feverishly to harvest [[amber]] from the [[Kypari tree|tree]].

Perhaps killing a pawful of them will slow their efforts.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 17,850 [[XP]]

==Progress==
The swarm is endless. There are always mantid to be killed.

==Completion==
While it is not a permanent solution, this should buy us some time.

==Notes==
* To get this quest, you must accept [[Once More Into Kor'vess]]. When you get in the vicinity of the tree, Taoshi will join you and make the quest available.
*Kill [[Kor'thik Wingguard]]s, [[Kor'thik Deepblade]]s, and [[Kor'thik Saptender]]s.
