My brothers speak of an ancient mogu empire that once dwelled in the [[Terrace of Gurthan|ruins to the east]].

Once, this tribe knew untold secrets of sorcery and subjugation.

I have a curious mind, Wakener, and I wish to learn of the empires that ruled after my time.

Knowledge of the past will determine our future.

Go search the ruins for their ancient tablets. I will study their secrets and make them our own.
