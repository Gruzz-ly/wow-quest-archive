This is a [[Tol Barad quests|Tol Barad]] daily quest.  It can only be taken and completed while your faction has control of Tol Barad.  Once completed, it can be turned in at [[Baradin Hold]] on Tol Barad, or at the [[Baradin's Wardens]] post on [[Tol Barad Peninsula]].

==Objectives==
You have been asked to recover 12 Cellblock Rations from [[The Hole]], just southwest of Baradin Hold.
*[[Cellblock Rations]] (12)

==Description==
Supplies are running low here in camp, <name>.  We have recently learned that the cellblock just southwest of here has a number of rations remaining from years of use.  We need you to gather as many of those as you can and bring them back to camp.

One more thing, the entire cellblock is overrun with prisoners so be careful. 

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|18|80}}
*1x [[Tol Barad Commendation]]
*+350 [[reputation]] with [[Baradin's Wardens]]

==Progress==
Were you able to recover the rations?  Reports mentioned that they could be found all over the place down there.

==Completion==
These rations will really help the troops out <name>.  Thank you for your help. 

==Achievements==
Completing this quest is an objective of [[Just Another Day in Tol Barad]].
