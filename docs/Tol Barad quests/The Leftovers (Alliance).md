This is a [[Tol Barad quests|Tol Barad]] daily quest.  It can only be taken and completed while your faction has control of Tol Barad.  Once completed, it can be turned in at [[Baradin Hold]] on Tol Barad, or at the [[Baradin's Wardens]] post on [[Tol Barad Peninsula]].

==Objectives==
You have been instructed to kill 12 Horde Infantry on Tol Barad. You can find the holdouts in The Slagworks, Warden's Vigil and the Ironclad Garrison, here on Tol Barad.
*[[Horde]] Infantry (12)

==Description==
We fought hard to capture this island, <race>. We cleared out most of the horde scum but I can still smell their foul stench in the air.

I need you to investigate the slagworks, warden's vigil and the ironclad garrison. Kill any horde that you find.

You know what they say, the only good horde is a dead horde!
