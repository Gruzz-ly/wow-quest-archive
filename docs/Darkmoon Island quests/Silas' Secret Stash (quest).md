A great [[Darkmoon Faire Ticket|Darkmoon prize]] do you now seek, but be ye warned, it is not for the meek. Your very first clue to send you on this caper, you will find printed on the back of this paper.

==Notes==
*First clue is a [[Barnacle-Covered Bag]] {{co|41.8|91.4|Darkmoon Island}} located inside a tent on the docks.
*Second clue is the [[Suspicious Mound]] {{co|53.0|60.4|Darkmoon Island}} in the sandbox.
*Third clue is the [[Crystallized Note (object)|Crystallized Note]] {{co|52.4|84.4|Darkmoon Island}} laying next to the [[Careless Carnie]].
*Fourth clue is some [[Disturbed Dirt (Darkmoon Island)|Disturbed Dirt]] {{co|52.0|30.0|Darkmoon Island}} by a banner near the path leading to the [[Darkmoon Faire]].
*[[Silas' Secret Stash]] {{co|21.7|29.2|Darkmoon Island}} is an underwater box located on a ship near the small northwest island.
