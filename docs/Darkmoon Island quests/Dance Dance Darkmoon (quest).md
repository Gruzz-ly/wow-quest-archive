==Objectives==
Earn accolades by competing in the [[Darkmoon Faire#Darkmoon Faire Dance|dance competition]].
*Accolades earned (0/100%)

==Description==
Simon says, "Are you ready to dance?" Step on in and show off your sweet moves, <class>.

The rules are simple. Your opponent will perform a series of stylish moves, then you gotta match 'em.

Earn enough accolades and then come back to ol' Simon. Just know that the more challenging the opponent, the more accolades you can earn.

Simon says, "Are you up for the challenge?"

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Darkmoon Faire (faction)|Darkmoon Faire]]
*[[Darkmoon Game Prize]]

==Progress==
Simon says, "Show me what you got."

==Completion==
Simon says, "Those were some sweet moves, <name>." 

Simon is impressed!

==Notes==
Accolades are earned based on difficulty and performance in the [[Darkmoon Faire]] dance competition. Hard mode awards 100% progress for a near-perfect performance and 75% for a mid-tier score. Medium difficulty grants at most 60% progress, with more forgiving scoring thresholds. The easy option gives 35% maximum and 30% for a mid-tier score. All difficulties provide 20% progress for the worst non-zero scores.
