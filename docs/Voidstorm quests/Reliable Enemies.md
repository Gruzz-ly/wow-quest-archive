==Description==
The ren'dorei scouts report a familiar enemy upon this world. [[Ethereal]]s. Wretched scavengers and bandits that plague every corner of the [[Great Dark Beyond]]. The [[Shadowguard]] are the worst of them. We'll handle them.

This... thing we've taken prisoner must know what they're doing here, and much else. Press the Void's secrets from it. See that it is convinced to share what it knows, or I will employ my own methods.
