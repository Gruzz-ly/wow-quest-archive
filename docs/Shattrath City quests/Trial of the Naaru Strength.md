==Objectives==
[[A'dal]] in [[Shattrath City]] wants you to recover Kalithresh's Trident and Murmur's Essence.

This quest must be completed in Heroic dungeon difficulty. 
*[[Kalithresh's Trident]]
*[[Murmur's Essence]]

==Description==
Four trials you must pass before you are deemed worthy by the [[naaru]].

The trial of strength is divided in two. You must destroy the leader of the [[Coilfang tribe|Coilfang]] [[Steamvault]]s, [[Warlord Kalithresh]], and bring me his trident. Slay the extra-planar being known as [[Murmur]], in the [[Shadow Labyrinth]] of [[Auchindoun]], and bring me his essence.

Do this and you will have passed the trial of strength.

*+500 [[reputation]] with [[Sha'tar]]
