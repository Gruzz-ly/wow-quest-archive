==Description==
Here at the Gala of Gifts, we have many wonderful artisans crafting gifts just for you and your loved ones. But don't take my word for it--there are plenty of attendees enjoying the festivities as well!

Go, speak to the visitors, and see what the artisans have to offer. Maybe you will find the perfect gift!
