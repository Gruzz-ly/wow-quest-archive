== Objectives ==
Kill 4 Razormane Quilboar and 4 Razormane Scouts.
*[[Razormane Quilboar]] slain (4)
*[[Razormane Scout]] slain (4)

== Description ==
The [[quilboar]] are a stain on the land that not even the [[Southfury River|river]] can wash away. They told me to evacuate, but I told them that my job was not yet done.

Hrrrraaagh! I hate quilboar so much! Will you help me kill them?
