'''What the Duck?''' begins the [[Noblegarden]] storyline added in 2024. It starts in [[Razor Hill]].

==Objectives==
Scare off 10 ducks using the Horn of Honking.
* Ducks scared (10)
* [[Horn of Honking]] (provided)

==Description==
I cannot work under these conditions! Something has all these silly ducks in an uproar--they nearly upended my precious golden egg!

These chaotic quackers simply must be dealt with! I can keep them from entering the inn, but will you please help clear out the rest?

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Brightly Colored Egg]]
*10x [[Flightstones]]

==Progress==
Shoo! Shoo! ...have you shooed them all yet?

==Completion==
Finally, a moment's peace! Thank you, <name>.

==Notes==
Use the horn on [[Furious Duck]]s.
