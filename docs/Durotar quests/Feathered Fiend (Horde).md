'''Feathered Fiend''' is the daily quest of the 2024 iteration of [[Noblegarden]]. The [[Noblegarden Trinket]] drops from [[Daetan Swiftplume]], the boss of the event.

==Objectives==
Bring the Noblegarden Trinket to [[Sylnaria Fareflame]] at [[Razor Hill]].
*[[Noblegarden Trinket]] (provided)

==Description==
The trinket appears to be a [[Noblegarden]] decoration of sorts. You should return this to the festivities at Razor Hill.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Loot-Filled Basket]]

==Completion==
Well now, how did that go missing? Don't worry, I'll see to it that any stolen decorations are restored!
