'''The Long Knives''' takes place halfway through the [[Heritage of Draenor]] questline. The quest giver and quest text changes depending on which clan the player picked in the preceding quest, [[The Blessing of the Clan]].

==Objectives==
Talk to [[Kaltunk]] in the [[Valley of Trials]].
