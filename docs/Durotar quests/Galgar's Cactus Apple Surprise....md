'''Galgar's Cactus Apple Surprise...''' is an optional side quest offered during [[Tracking a Killer]]. The player encounters an [[Injured Adventurer (Durotar)|Injured Adventurer]] who dies while on [[Galgar's Cactus Apple Surprise|a quest to deiver cactus apples]] to [[Galgar]]. The player can pick up this quest from her [[Bag of Cactus Apples]] to complete the task on her behalf.

==Objectives==
[[File:Galgar.jpg|thumb|[[Galgar]].]]
Bring the Bag of Cactus Apples to Galgar.
*[[Bag of Cactus Apples (item)]]{{Co|50|60.1|Valley of Trials}}

==Description==
<The [[Injured Adventurer (Durotar)|young orc]]'s last words were a request to deliver these Cactus Apples to Galgar.

Will you honor her request?>
