==Objectives==
Visit [[Horde]] forces in [[Dranosh'ar Blockade]].
* Armor polished
* Weapon empowered
* Last meal eaten
* Warmed up with a duel

==Description==
Our allies have made the journey to [[Orgrimmar]] to support our war efforts.

Stop and visit with them, <name>. Their assistance will secure our victory today.

==Rewards==
{{itembox|You will receive:
|Mark of Orgrimmar
}}

==Progress==
Blood and thunder, friend.

==Completion==
You look quite prepared for battle. Do not let bloodlust overtake you out there.

The [[Warchief]] wishes to present you with this ring before you head to war. May you return with the heads of many demons.

==Notes==
Travel east from Holgar using the path heading to the docks, where you will find the quest objectives along the way. The mojo cauldron is optional, but it's recommended that you pick up some [[Extra Thick Mojo]].

==Trivia==
*Several [[garrison followers]] appear in the [[Dranosh'ar Blockade]] for this quest.
**A circle of followers can be seen watching a duel between [[Annix Strifesprocket]] and [[Marius Sunshard]], and can be asked for a duel themselves. These include [[Neejala]], [[Maska]], [[Faala]], [[Handel Shadereaver]], [[Aila Dourblade]], [[Dawn Mercurus]], [[Arienne Black]], [[Argonis Solheart]], [[Shappa]], [[Arcanist Druk'rog]], [[Yaalo]], [[Lonan]], [[Utona Wolfeye]], [[Karn Steelhoof]], [[Pinkee Rizzo]], [[Omril Keenedge]], [[Sahale]], [[Taela Shatterborne]], and [[Rizza Brassrokkit]].
**[[Mojo'kai]], [[Sulaka]], [[Rakkaha]], and [[Ja'kala]] are seen preparing [[Extra Thick Mojo]] in a cauldron.
**[[Seleria Dawncaller]], [[Merhean Sunfall]], and [[Lan'veros Furybrand]] are preparing [[Sun Sphere|Sun Spheres]] for the [[adventurer]]s' weapons.
*The ogre [[Gregg]] can be found in the Dranosh'ar Blockade surrounded by big rocks. When spoken to, he revealed that the rocks were ammunition for the catapults and that he wished to join the Horde.
