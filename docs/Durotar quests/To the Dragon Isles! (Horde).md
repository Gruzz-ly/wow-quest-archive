==Objectives==
Ride the Defiant Dragonscale to the Dragon Isles from the zeppelin tower outside [[Orgrimmar]].
*Take the portal or the zeppelin to the [[Dragon Isles]]
*Ride the [[Defiant Dragonscale]] to Dragon Isles
*Meet [[Naleidea Rivergleam]] on the shores of the Dragon Isles.

==Description==
Our preliminary expedition has made landfall in the Dragon Isles.

Thanks to the efforts of yourself and other [[Dragonscale Expedition]] members, we've established a protected landing site in the Dragon Isles and made contact with the [[dragonflight]]'s embassy.

Are you prepared to accompany us on our next voyage to the Dragon Isles?
