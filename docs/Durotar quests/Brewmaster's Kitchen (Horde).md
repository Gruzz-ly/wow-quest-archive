==Objectives==
Follow the steps to cook Brewfest food and a delicious Brew.
* Pretzel Ingredients obtained
* Cooking Table used
* Knead the Dough
* Salt added
* Toss the Dough in the Oven
* Retrieve the Pretzel
* Bring to Serving Table
* Obtain Soup Ingredients
* Stir the Soup
* Bring to Serving Table
* Obtain Brew Ingredients
* Smash Grains at the Table
* Heat up the Oven
* Add in the Hops
* Bring to the Serving Keg

==Description==
Good, you've brought the supplies. Now, we begin the feast. We'll need plenty of food to keep up our strength.

The menu's simple, but worthy: Barley Stew, Stone-baked Pretzels, and a strong brew to fuel the Horde!

Are you prepared to learn?
