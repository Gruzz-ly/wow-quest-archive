'''Remembrance''' is offered during the [[Harvest Festival]] to [[tauren]] characters who have completed the [[Heritage of the Shu'halo]] questline.

==Objectives==
Deliver the offering to your spirit guide on the cliffs overlooking [[Thunder Bluff]].
* [[Spirit Offering|Offering]] presented

==Description==
You may remember the day [[Thunder Bluff]] was attacked. It was your efforts and your guide who saved us.

Now we take time to honor our ancestors and give thanks for the path they have set for us.

Bring this offering to the cliffs overlooking Thunder Bluff and give thanks to your guide and all our ancestors.

A simple gesture to commemorate our past. This is our way.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 17,850 [[XP]]

==Completion==
On this day we remember and give thanks to the ones who have come before us. May the [[Earth Mother]] guide you.

==Notes==
The offering is located on a tall cliff overlooking Thunder Bluff from the southwest. Right-click the object to present the offering.
