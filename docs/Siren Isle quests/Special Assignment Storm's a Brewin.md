'''Special Assignment: Storm's a Brewin''' becomes available after completing [[Legacy of the Vrykul]], [[Serpent's Wrath]], or [[Tides of Greed]].

==Objectives==
*Speak with [[Suzie Boltwrench|Suzie]] to await the Storm
*Use equipment provided (Optional)
*Storm threats eliminated (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|23|40}}
* 12,200 [[XP]]
*[[Earthen Landlubber's Cache]]

==Notes==
Speak with Suzie at [[Flotsam Shoal]] at {{Co|69.06|49.26|Siren Isle}}:
{{Dialogue|
{{Gossip|quest=|I'll defend the camp during the storm.}}
}}

:{{Text|bossemote|As a storm approaches, everyone in camp retreats to safety.}}
:{{Text|say|Suzie Boltwrench|This is far worse than I expected!}}
:{{Text|say|Suzie Boltwrench|Grab any equipment that might help ya. I'm just gonna go hide over here.}}

[[Ricket's Special Delivery]] and [[Crawler Mine (Siren Isle)|Crawler Mine]] are available for use. After completing [[Juicing Up and Storming Out]], [[TR-06 Pummeler]] and [[N.U.K.U.L.A.R. Target Painter]] become available. In this special phase of the Siren Isle, new enemies and quests become available.

When finished with the phase, use [[Leave the Storm]] or speak with Suzie again:
{{Dialogue|
I'm ready to leave whenever you are!

{{Gossip|Let's get out of this storm!}}
}}
:{{Text|bossemote|You retreat until the storm clears.}}
