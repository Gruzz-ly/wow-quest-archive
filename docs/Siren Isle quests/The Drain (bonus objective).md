'''The Drain''' is a bonus objective unlocked after contributing enough [[Flame-Blessed Iron]] to The Drain [[Major Excavations]].

==Objectives==
*[[Gravesludge]] slain

==Description==
*Goblin workers are preparing to open this cave.

:{{Text|say|[[Skaggit]]|Get your feet moving. We got a tunnel to blast open! This kaboom oughtta be good!}}
:{{Text|say|Skaggit|Whaddya think washed down that drain? Maybe it's treasure. Or maybe it's monsters. And maybe those monsters have treasure!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|46|80}}
*15,250 [[XP]]
*[[Crate of Bygone Riches]]

==Progress==
*Enter the cave and defeat the threats within.

At the [[Stormtide Dregs]]...
:{{Text|say|[[Quizla Blastcaps]]|More explosives! I want this to be the second biggest boom in goblin history.}}

Once the timer runs out, [[Sally Boltwrench]] uses her Detonator and explodes the bombs placed by [[Blackpowder Specialist]]s at the entrance. Quizla jumps in happiness, and the goblins run away.
