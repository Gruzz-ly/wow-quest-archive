==Description==
This ring that you and [[Bargus]] found has taken you all over the isle learning its history and finding its treasures.

The ring has aided you in your exploration--and in battle. But, we have never truly pushed the ring to find its limitations.

Perhaps some formal testing is in order. Perhaps we can unveil more of its potential if we invite a bit of danger.

Go now and test the ring against any worthy opponents you can find. Come back to me with your observations.
