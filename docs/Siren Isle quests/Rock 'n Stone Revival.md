==Objectives==
8 Restless Pebbles or Stones revived, 8 Bloodbrine horrors and the Bloodbrine Monstrosity defeated.
* Restless Stones and Pebbles animated (8)
* [[Bloodbrine Horror]] slain (8)
* [[Brined Monstrosity]] slain
* [[Rock Reviver]] (provided)

==Description==
The attacking [[vrykul]] harness salt and brine elementals, turning them against their foes.

To counter their assault, we can harness them as well.

Use this Rock Reviver to unleash the elementals residing within nearby stones, transforming them into powerful allies. With their help, we can defeat this vrykul threat.
