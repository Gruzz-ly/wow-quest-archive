==Description==
<[[Bargus]] holds up a tiny splinter of golden crystal.>

Much like the radiant remnants found by the [[Arathi]] within [[Hallowfall]], the [[Siren Crystal]] appears to be effecting{{sic}} the surrounding geological matrix of the island.

Unlike our [[goblin]] compatriots, I don't recommend harvesting the larger shards we've seen around the isle, lest we disturb the fragile caverns beneath us or offend the Arathi.

But these tiny splinters are worth investigating.

Bring me a sizable portion. For... experimental purposes.
