# Siren Isle storyline

[[File:VZ-Siren Isle.jpg|thumb|Siren Isle map]]

The '''Siren Isle storyline''' is part of the [[Siren Isle campaign|questlines]] added in [[patch 11.0.7]] as part of the greater [[Siren Isle campaign]] of the [[The War Within campaign]].

==Introduction==
Available immediately at level {{level|Siren Isle}} in [[Dornogal]], the short intro questline directs the players to the [[Siren Isle]] and unlocks [[Cyrce's Circlet]].

{{A Song of Secrets}}

==Ring upgrades==
After completing the intro, weekly time-gated quests become available in [[Flotsam Shoal]] to upgrade the ring. These quests can be completed back-to-back for characters who are catching up.

{{Cyrce's Circlet upgrade}}

==Snapdragon mount==
After completing the intro, daily time-gated quests become available in Flotsam Shoal to raise the [[Starving Snapdragon Runt]] as a [[Prismatic Snapdragon|mount]].

{{A Loyal Friend}}

==Marmaduke companion==
Players may obtain the [[Marmaduke]] companion from a short questline after completing the intro. To start it, head back to the [[Forgotten Vault (Siren Isle)|Forgotten Vault]] (an objective of [[Reading the Runes]]).

{{Homeward Bound to Safer Shores}}
