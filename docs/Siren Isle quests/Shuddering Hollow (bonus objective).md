'''Shuddering Hollow''' is a bonus objective unlocked after contributing enough [[Flame-Blessed Iron]] to the Shuddering Hollow [[Major Excavations]].

==Objectives==
*[[Stalagnarok]] slain

==Description==
*Kobold workers are excavating the cave entrance.

:{{Text|say|[[Dawn]]|Can you sense that power? I can feel it. Elemental fury is building, resonating with the crystal.}}
:{{Text|say|Dawn|I've dispatched a team of kobolds to excavate the site and examine it firsthand. Assistance requested.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|46|80}}
*15,250 [[XP]]
*[[Crate of Bygone Riches]]

==Progress==
*Enter the cave and defeat the threats within.

[[Deeprock Breaker]]s and two [[Hardstone Digger]]s dig at the entrance.

:{{Text|bossemote|The cavern is on the verge of collapse!}}
