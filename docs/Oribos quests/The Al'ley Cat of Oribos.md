'''The Al'ley Cat of Oribos''' begins a brief quest chain to unlock [[Tazavesh, the Veiled Market]], as part of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]] in [[patch 9.1]].

==Objectives==
Meet with the mysterious contact in [[Oribos]].

==Description==
Ah, [[Maw Walker]]. I have a message.

An individual has a proposition for you, specifically. I know nothing of the details, only that they asked you meet them towards the back of the [[Idyllia]].
