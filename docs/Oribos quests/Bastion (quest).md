'''Bastion''' is received from the Scouting Map in [[Oribos]], which can be viewed during [[The Threads of Fate|Threads of Fate]] breadcrumb quests by speaking to [[Tal-Inara]].

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Tal-Inara]].

==Description==
After being set back by a surprise attack by [[Maldraxxus]] forces and an army of their own fallen kin known as the [[Forsworn]], the [[Ascended]] stand to lose control of [[Bastion]] if they are not given aid.
