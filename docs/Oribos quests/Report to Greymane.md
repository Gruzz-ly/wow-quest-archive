'''Report to Greymane''' concludes the Alliance perspective of the [[Return to Lordaeron]] storyline, part of the [[Shadowlands epilogue]].

==Objectives==
Deliver Calia's letter to Genn Greymane in [[Oribos]].
*Deliver [[Calia Menethil|Calia]]'s letter to [[Genn Greymane]]
*[[Letter to Greymane]] (provided)

==Description==
Thank you for all of your assistance, leading to this historic moment.

I believe there may be others who are interested in what happened here. Please, carry this letter, and my well wishes, when you return to them.
