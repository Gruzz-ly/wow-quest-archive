'''Ta'readon's Mount Voucher''' starts from the [[Ta'readon's Mount Voucher]] item attached to an in-game letter from [[Bobadormu]] after earning [[Master of the Turbulent Timeways IV]]. Find [[Overseer Ta'readon]] in the [[Hall of Curiosities]] of [[Oribos]] in the [[Shadowlands]].

After completing this quest, the other three mounts are available for 5,000x [[Timewarped Badge]] from all timewalking vendors.

==Objectives==
Bring Ta'readon's Mount Voucher to [[Overseer Ta'readon]] in [[Oribos]].
* [[Ta'readon's Mount Voucher]]

==Description==
This token bears the seal of Overseer Ta'readon in Oribos. They would be interested in seeing this.

==Rewards==
You will be able to choose one of these rewards:
*[[Bronze Wilderling Harness]]
*[[Bronze Corpsefly Harness]]
*[[Bronze Gravewing Harness]]
*[[Bronze Aquilon Harness]]

==Completion==
I see you have served our merchants admirably. Accept one of our finest steeds as a reward.
