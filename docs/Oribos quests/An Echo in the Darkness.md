'''An Echo in the Darkness''' begins the final chapter of the [[Patch 9.0]] portion of the [[Maw storyline|Torghast storyline]] and the greater [[Shadowlands campaign]]. To be offered this quest, players must have defeated [[Sire Denathrius (tactics)|Sire Denathrius]] in [[Castle Nathria]], completed the quest [[Redemption for the Redeemer]], and seen [[Bolvar Fordragon|Bolvar's]] vision of [[Anduin Wrynn]] in [[Torghast, Tower of the Damned|Torghast]] in the quest [[The Captive King]].

==Objectives==
[[File:An Echo in the Darkness.jpg|thumb|Bolvar hunched in pain]]

Meet Bolvar Fordragon in Oribos.
