'''Ardenweald''' is received from the Scouting Map in [[Oribos]], which can be viewed during [[The Threads of Fate|Threads of Fate]] breadcrumb quests by speaking to [[Tal-Inara]].

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Tal-Inara]].

==Description==
Ardenweald still faces threats from the Drust, as well as other nefarious forces. The Winter Queen could use your aid in fending off the forces that seek to destroy Ardenweald.

==Rewards==
