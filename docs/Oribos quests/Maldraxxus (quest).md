'''Maldraxxus''' is received from the Scouting Map in [[Oribos]], which can be viewed during [[The Threads of Fate|Threads of Fate]] breadcrumb quests by speaking to [[Tal-Inara]].

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Tal-Inara]].

==Description==
With the houses in shambles, the [[Undying Army|loyal army]] of the [[Primus]] struggles to hold their ground in [[Maldraxxus]]. They need help to tip the scales in their favor.
