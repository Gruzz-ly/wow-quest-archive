==Description==
Though they were once unified in defending the [[Shadowlands]], the houses of Maldraxxus have turned against each other.

Their armies clash within their realm and have even orchestrated an assault on [[Bastion]]. Now only the [[Necrolord Covenant|Necrolords]] remain loyal to the [[Primus]].

Speak to [[Secutor Mevix]] at the <Bleak Redoubt/Seat of the Primus> in Maldraxxus. His tactical skills will ensure you are sent where you are most needed.

[[Pathscribe Roh-Avonavi]] in the [[Ring of Transference]] above can provide you with transportation.
