==Objectives==
*Defeat enemies in the [[Valley of Wisdom]]
*Defeat [[Vez'okk the Lightless]]

==Notes==
;Entering the area
:{{Text|yell|[[Overlord Geya'rah]]|The [[Thrall (Vision of Orgrimmar)|false son]] of [[Durotan]] must die! The blood of the {{Au|Mag'har}} is on his hands!}}
:{{Text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|The Mag'har refused to drink the blood of [[N'Zoth]], and were slaughtered for their defiance.}}
:{{Text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Their [[Ghost|spirits]] demand vengeance!}}

Kill regular mobs [[Faceless Willbreaker]]s, [[Faceless Shadowcaller]]s, [[Burrowing Appendage]]s, [[K'thir Mindcarver]]s, [[K'thir Dominator]]s, [[Crawling Corruption]], [[Void Globule]]s, and [[Crawling Corruption]] as well as the elite mobs [[Coagulated Horror]] and [[Voidcrazed Hulk]].

;Aggro Voidcrazed Hulk
:{{Text|say|Voidcrazed Hulk|Smash puny weaklings!}}
:{{Text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|No... what have they done to you?!}}

;Death
:{{Text|say|Voidcrazed Hulk|Why... no smash...?}}

When the enemies are defeated, [[Vez'okk the Lightless]] appears. Kill it.
:{{Text|yell|Vez'okk the Lightless|[Shath'Yar] The [[Void]] calls to you!}}
:{{Text|yell|Overlord Geya'rah|Slay that monstrosity in the name of the Mag'har!}}

;Aggro
:{{Text|whisper|Vez'okk the Lightless|Bow before N'Zoth!}}

;Death
:{{Text|whisper|Vez'okk the Lightless|All... will... perish...}}

After its death, the objective is completed.
:{{Text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Dead... My clan is dead. And nothing I do will ever bring them back...}}
