==Objectives==
Follow the steps to cook Brewfest food and a delicious Brew.
* Pretzel Ingredients obtained
* Cooking Table used
* Knead the Dough
* Salt added
* Toss the Dough in the Oven
* Retrieve the Pretzel
* Bring to Serving Table
* Obtain Soup Ingredients
* Stir the Soup
* Bring to Serving Table
* Obtain Brew Ingredients
* Smash Grains at the Table
* Heat up the Oven
* Add in the Hops
* Bring to the Serving Keg

==Description==
Now that ye've gathered the materials, we can get to cookin'! We'll need plenty o' food for the whole crowd.

We've got a short but sweet menu: Barley Soup, Pretzels, and of course a good ol' brew!

Are ye ready to learn the ropes?
