'''Shifting Gears''' begins a quest chain for [[gnome (playable)|gnome]] characters to obtain their [[heritage armor]]. It can be picked up just outside the [[Stormwind Embassy]] within [[Stormwind City]] after reaching level {{Level|Bfa-max}}.

==Objectives==
Meet [[Captain Tread Sparknozzle]] in [[New Tinkertown]].

==Description==
<Name>! The [[Alliance]] has made a new request for [[Gnomeregan (kingdom)|Gnomeregan]]'s might in the [[Fourth War|coming war]]. It is my honor to inform you that Captain Sparknozzle wishes for your expert assistance in the matter.

You are hereby requested to meet him in New Tinkertown.

==Rewards==
You will learn: {{abilities|Heritage of Gnomeregan|id=292536|icon=inv_cape_armor_gnome_d_01|Unlock the appearances of the Heritage of Gnomeregan on completion of the Heritage storyline.}}

You will also receive:
* {{cost|11|70|}}
* 8,950 [[XP]]

==Completion==
The rest of the [[gnome]]s I requested arrived shortly before you. Allow me to brief you on the situation.

==Notes==
When you arrive, Tread is standing atop the raised platform in the eastern end of New Tinkertown.
