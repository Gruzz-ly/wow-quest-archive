# Hillsbrad Foothills storyline

[[File:WorldMap-HillsbradFoothills.jpg|thumb|Zone Map]]

'''[[Hillsbrad Foothills]]''' is a Horde-only level 20-25 zone in southern Lordaeron and a direct followup to the [[Silverpine Forest storyline]] as part of the Undead starting experience. its progress is followed through the achievement [[Hillsbrad Foothills Quests]]. 

At the end of the quest chain, or as soon as players reach level 24, the [[Arathi Highlands storyline]] opens up with [[Warchief's Command: Arathi Highlands!]]
