As the leaders of the [[Alliance]] forces in [[Hillsbrad Foothills]] gather on [[Purgation Isle]], [[Kingslayer Orkus]] and the [[player]] must put their lives on the line and stop the [[Stormpike clan]] from re-taking Hillsbrad from the [[Horde]]. One way or another, both Orkus and the player shall become '''Heroes of the Horde!'''

==Objectives==
Steal the Alliance Battle Plans and Become Heroes of the Horde.
* [[Alliance Battle Plans]]
*Become Heroes of the Horde

==Description==
Now to find their battle plans and take out their leaders! Should we succeed there is an orc high warlord who served in the war in Silverpine that is now stationed at [[Tarren Mill]] - located in the heart of Hillsbrad. he will grant both of us commendations for this! We will be heroes of the Horde, <name>!

Onward! Together we will defeat the Alliance filth!
