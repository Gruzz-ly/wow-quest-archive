This is a list of all [[Hillsbrad Foothills (Classic)|Hillsbrad Foothills]] quests in table format.

==Quest table==
Quests that take place outside of the Hillsbrad Foothills are denoted by a {{Outzone}}.

Quests that are part of a chain are denoted by {{Chain}}.

The [[Horde]] town of [[Tarren Mill]] offers many quests to players through their twenties. Later, Tarren Mill also serves as the Horde town for questing in [[Alterac Mountains (Classic)|Alterac Mountains]], though there are considerably fewer quests to be done there.

[[Alliance]] quests in [[Hillsbrad Foothills (Classic)|Hillsbrad Foothills]] are slightly higher level than their Horde counterparts, due to the long distance away from the rest of Alliance civilization. All quests come from the town of [[Southshore]].

==Notable chains in the Hillsbrad Foothills==
The following quest chains occur in the [[Hillsbrad Foothills (Classic)|Hillsbrad Foothills]]. Chains have been included if they are more than three quests long or are especially significant in some way.

==={{Alliance Icon}} Farren's Proof chain===
In this quest chain, players help the lazy {{NPC||Lieutenant Farren Orinelle}} with his assigned duty of clearing the coasts of southern Hillsbrad of the menace of the murlocs and naga that have taken up residence.

==={{Alliance Icon}} Hints of a New Plague chain===
This quest chain consists of traveling to [[Go'Shek Farm]] in the [[Arathi Highlands (Classic)|Arathi Highlands]] to investigate the undoubtedly nefarious goings-on of the Forsaken apothecaries.

Technically, almost all the quests in this chain take place in [[Arathi Highlands (Classic)|Arathi Highlands]], however, it is still categorized under Hillsbrad Foothills in game because the quest giver is in [[Southshore]].

==={{Horde Icon}} Lord Aliden Perenolde chain===
In this quest chain, players assist an orc whose companions have been kidnapped while investigating the [[Syndicate]] of the area and searching for a certain item of interest to [[Warchief Thrall]].

Technically, almost all the quests in this chain take place in [[Alterac Mountains (Classic)|Alterac Mountains]], however, it is still categorized under Hillsbrad Foothills in game because the quest giver is in [[Tarren Mill]].

==={{Horde Icon}} The Crown of Will chain===
The troublesome ogre warlord [[Mug'thol]] (who you may remember from ''[[The Frozen Throne]]'') has put on the Crown of Will, freeing he and his ogres from the mind control of [[Sylvanas]]. Of course, such rebellion merits swift retribution, and you play the part of the instrument of the Dark Lady's wrath.

Technically, all the quests in this chain take place in [[Alterac Mountains (Classic)|Alterac Mountains]], however, it is still categorized under Hillsbrad Foothills in game because the quest giver is in [[Tarren Mill]].
