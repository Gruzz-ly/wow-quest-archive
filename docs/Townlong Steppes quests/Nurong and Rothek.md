==Objectives==
Kill Rothek with the aid of [[Hawkmaster Nurong]].
*[[Rothek]] slain {{co|39.8|75.6|Townlong Steppes}}

==Description==
Hawkmaster Nurong has gone ahead to the northwest of [[Sik'vess]] to make sure the last [[mantid]] leader cannot escape.

Catch up with him and help him kill the mantid leader. Nurong will bring back the final crystal once you've aided him in this.

==Rewards==

==Progress==

==Completion==
The final crystal is ours. We are on the doorstep of our final confrontation.
