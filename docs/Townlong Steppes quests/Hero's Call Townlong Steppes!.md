Speak with [[Taran Zhu]] in [[Townlong Steppes]].

==Description==
All able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Taran Zhu in Townlong Steppes.

The [[Shado-Pan]] want to reclaim their land from the clutches of the [[yaungol]], but lack the numbers to do so.

Assist them with their assault against the yaungol.

Glory to the Alliance!
