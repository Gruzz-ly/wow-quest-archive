==Description==
Taoshi is setting up an ambush for one of the [[mantid]] leaders on the south side of [[Sik'vess]]. She is tasked with retrieving his crystal so that we can use it to open the door.

Two are stronger than one. I want you there to make sure it goes smoothly.
