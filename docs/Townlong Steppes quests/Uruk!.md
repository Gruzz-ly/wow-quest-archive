After completing [[Assault Deadtalker's Plateau]] and [[Spirit Dust (quest)]], the Shado-Pan companion will offer this quest. [[Uruk]] is on the northwest part of the plateau overlooking the [[Westersea]]. A level 90 yaungol, Uruk has 1,181,000 health and the following abilities:

*{{abilities|Call of the Keening Spirits|range=40|notes=4 sec cast.|cd=20 sec}}
