==Description==
One of my best scouts, Restless Leng, went missing during a mission on the other side of [[Sik'vess]]. I'm hopeful that he was captured rather than killed, but with the [[mantid]] corrupted so heavily by [[sha]], it doesn't seem likely.

Still, can you investigate the cages around Sik'vess? I owe him a more thorough search than even this, but I have no men to spare without allowing our southern lines to break.

Please, <class>, even if there's no life left in him, find him that I might know his fate.
