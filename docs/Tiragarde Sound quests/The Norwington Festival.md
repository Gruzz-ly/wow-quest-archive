==Objectives==
Meet [[Abbey Watkins]] at the edge of [[Foxhollow Woods]].

==Description==
To whomever receives this invitation,

You are cordially invited to the 15th annual [[Norwington Equestrian and Hunt Festival]]!

People of all walks of life from across [[Kul Tiras]] shall gather to delight in life's greatest competitions, equestrianism and hunting, while meeting those of like minds and fine tastes.

Hunting events shall start first, led by Abbey Watkins in Foxhollow Woods. They shall be followed by equestrian events at the [[Norwington Estate]].

We hope to see you there posthaste!
