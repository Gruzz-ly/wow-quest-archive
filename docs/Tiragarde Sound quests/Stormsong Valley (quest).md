'''Stormsong Valley''' begins the [[Stormsong Valley storyline]] for Alliance characters. Pick it up from the [[Scouting Map]] behind [[Taelia]] at the [[Harbormaster's Office]] in [[Boralus]].

==Objectives==
Offer to investigate the missing [[Kul Tiras|Kul Tiran]] fleet.

==Description==
The Kul Tiran fleet has been lost at sea. The [[tidesages]] of [[Stormsong Valley|Stormsong]], with their mystical oceanic magic, may be our only way to locate the fleet of legend.

;Mouseover
''The legendary Kul Tiran fleet has gone missing. Seek answers from the reclusive ocean mystics of [[House Stormsong]].''
