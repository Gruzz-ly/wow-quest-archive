'''Creature Corruption''' is a bonus objective made available after completing [[Paddle to Safety]] while in [[Outrigger Post]] during the [[Tiragarde Sound storyline]].

==Objectives==
*Purge the corruption (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|38|80|}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]

==Notes==
To fill up the bar, kill [[Distorted Scaleterror]]s (15%), [[Mindbent Thrashneck]]s (10%), [[Hunting Bloodseeker]]s (10%), or [[Voidtouched Bloodseeker]]s (20%).
