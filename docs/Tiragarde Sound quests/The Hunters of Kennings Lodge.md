HELP WANTED!

As we turn into the busy season, [[Kennings Lodge]] is looking for recreational hunters to assist with fulfilling the ever growing list of orders from [[Boralus]]. These are short term positions which will be paid relative to performance. 

Kennings Lodge can be found by following the south road past the mining yard. Report to Alanna Holton on arrival.
