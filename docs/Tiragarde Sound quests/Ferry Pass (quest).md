==Objectives==
Speak with [[Cyrus Crestfall]] and receive the [[Ferry Pass]].

==Description==
<Cyrus takes an official looking document from his jacket, signs it, then offers it to you.>

Here, this will give you access to the ferry system.

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you: [[Ferry Pass]]

You will also receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Proudmoore Admiralty]]

==Completion==
There are ferry docks all along the interior coastline of the sound. Don't forget them during your travels!
