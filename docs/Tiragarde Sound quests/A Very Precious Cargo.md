==Objectives==
Find the "Precious" cargo in Fogcliff Strand.
* Find the [[Penny "Precious" Hardwick|Precious Cargo]] {{co|55.5|35.9|Tiragarde Sound}}

==Description==
We weren't the only ship in our convoy to get caught up in this mess. Our flagship ran aground to the east. It was holding a very precious cargo and I need you to bring it back here.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|14|60|}}
* 12,340 [[XP]]
* +25 [[reputation]] with [[Proudmoore Admiralty]]

==Completion==
Who are you? Where's my dad? I'm hungry. Can I have a sword? Where's my dad?!

==Notes==
Pick up [[Captured and Enraptured]] before heading out. Also, there's a bonus objective in the area, [[Shipwreck Recovery Crew]].

Additionally, immediately to the west of the quest giver is a treasure chest and [[Squirgle of the Depths]], a rare [[deep sea murloc]]. Also, just west is [[Earless Joe]], who offers [[Can't Hear A Thing]].

Penny is found at the southeastern side of the quest area, guarded by other [[Shipwrecked Sailors]].
