'''Azerite Wounds''' is a [[world quest]] that can appear in any zone on [[Kul Tiras]] and [[Zandalar]].

==Objectives==
*Azeroth wounds healed (5)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Magni Bronzebeard]]|Several Azerite wounds lay exposed nearby. Use the [[:Heart of Azeroth]] tae mend 'em!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Champions of Azeroth]]

Depending on zone and player faction, you will also receive:
*{{Alliance}} +75 reputation with [[Order of Embers]], [[Proudmoore Admiralty]], [[Storm's Wake]], or [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
*{{Horde}} +75 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]], [[Voldunai]], [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]], or [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*An [[Azerite (resource)]] item
*[[War Resources]]
*[[Azerite Armor Cache]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Magni Bronzebeard|Ye did well, champion. I can feel [[Azeroth (titan)|Azeroth]] recoverin' her strength, thanks tae ye.}}

==Notes==
If you are doing this world quest while above level 50, note that the Heart of Azeroth DOES need to be in your inventory (though it need not be equipped).
