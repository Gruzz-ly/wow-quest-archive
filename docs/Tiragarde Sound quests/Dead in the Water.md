==Objectives==
Defeat 6 crew members aboard [[the Swift Vengeance]].
* Swift Vengeance crew defeated (6)

==Description==
Once [[Derek Proudmoore|Proudmoore]] is freed, we will need to commandeer this ship to transport him to [[Theramore]], where [[Jaina Proudmoore|his sister]] awaits us. 

[[Thomas Zelling|Zelling]]'s [[magic]] can guide the ship there, but first we'll need to take care of the crew.
