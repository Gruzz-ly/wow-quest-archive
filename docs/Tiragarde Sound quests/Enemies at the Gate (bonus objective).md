'''Enemies at the Gate''' is a bonus objective located in [[Daelin's Gate]] offered while on [[Righteous Retribution]], [[Cease Fire!]] and [[For Kul Tiras!]] during the [[Tiragarde Sound storyline]].

==Objectives==
*Defend Daelin's Gate

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|38|80}}
*16,450 XP

==Notes and trivia==
*Kill invading [[Irontide Pillager]]s, [[Irontide Brinecaster]]s, [[Blacktooth Brute]]s, [[Blacktooth Guzzler]]s, [[Irontide Cannoneer]]s, [[Vulpera Padfoot]]s, [[Brinescale Wavecaller]]s, [[Bristleback Pounder]]s, [[Vulpera Firefox]]es, and [[Cutwater Reaver]]s, recover [[Sack of Booty|Sacks of Booty]], and interact with [[Inactive Harvest Thresher]]s.
*During the beta, the ID was used by [[Burn Notice]].
