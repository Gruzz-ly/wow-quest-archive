==Objectives==
Speak with [[Tyrande Whisperwind]] in [[Central Encampment]] to choose an offering.
* Offering chosen

==Description==
<Name>. You have done so much for our people. I would have you participate in this ceremony and provide an offering of your own, should you wish it.

Let me know what it is that you would like to put forward and it will be done.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|14|4|50}}
* 4,750 [[XP]]
* [[Ensemble: Raiment of Amirdrassil]]

==Progress==
What do you wish to put towards the ritual, <name>?

==Completion==
An admirable choice. May [[Elune]] light your path, <name>.

==Notes==
This quest is only offered to [[Night elf|night elves]]. For other races, Tyrande only offers one quest for this step, [[Offerings for the Well]].

After receiving this quest, talk to Tyrande.

: What do you wish to put towards the ritual, <name>?
:* A satchet{{sic}} of rare herbs from [[Darnassus]].
:* A carefully preserved selection of herbs, gifted by [[Tarindrella]] long ago.
:* A pressed [[Moonpetal Lily|moonpetal lily]] from [[Shadowglen]].
:* A smooth stone from the road to Darnassus.
:* A sigil from your first trainer.
:* A skein of webwood spider silk from Shadowglen.
:* Something else that is valuable to me.

Upon making a choice:

:{{text|say|Tyrande Whisperwind|I accept your offering with warmth and gratitude, <name>.}}
