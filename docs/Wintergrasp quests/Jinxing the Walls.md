==Objectives==
[[Hoodoo Master Fu'jin]] in [[Wintergrasp]] wants you to retrieve 10 sets of Enchanted Alliance Armor from Alliance players at the [[Glacial Falls]].
*[[Enchanted Alliance Breastplate]] (10) 

==Description==
Hey 'mon. See dem Alliance sitting comfy in their keep?

I think they be a bit too comfy, ya? I want ta be jinxing the place, but I be needin' some souls, mon. Not any soul though, only da soul of a strong warrior could do da job.

The Glacial Falls in east Wintergrasp be havin' dem special properties to extract da soul. Slay the Alliance there, they be havin' the stuff we need.

Bring back da armor of the slain. If there be no Alliance maybe da elementals got 'em, mon.
