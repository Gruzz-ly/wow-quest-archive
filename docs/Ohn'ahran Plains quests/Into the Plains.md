==Objectives==
Meet the [[Clan Shikaar|Shikaar]] [[scout]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].
*[[Vibrant Life Bloom]] (provided)

==Description==
The [[green dragonflight]] has returned to their gardens in the nearby plains. [[Alexstrasza|Queen Alexstrasza]] wishes for you to make contact with [[Merithra of the Dream]].

The [[centaur]] of those plains are fierce hunters. To reach the [[Emerald Gardens]], you will need to establish diplomacy with them first.

Be respectful, but show no weakness.
