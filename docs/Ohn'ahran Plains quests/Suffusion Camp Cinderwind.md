'''Suffusion Camp: Cinderwind''' is one of two [[Fyrakk Assaults]]. This one takes place at {{co|74|71|Ohn'ahran Plains}} in southeastern [[Ohn'ahran Plains]]. For more details on how to complete this world quest, see the [[Fyrakk Assaults#Cinderwind|Cinderwind]] section of the assault article.

==Objectives==
* 5 [[Ward of Igira|Wards of Igira]] collected (Optional)
* Suffusion Crucible looted
* Crucible guardian slain
* 5 [[Ward of Fyrakk|Wards of Fyrakk]] collected (Optional)
* Suffusion Mold looted {{co|74.3|72.3|Ohn'ahran Plains}}
* Forgemaster slain

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]
