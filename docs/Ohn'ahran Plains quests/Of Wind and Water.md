==Description==
To the east is a great dragonspring which serves as a nesting area for wild [[eagle]]s. The feathers they shed still contain their [[magic]] for quite some time and are used in many rituals.

The area also hosts raging spirits of the elements, and their cores are of some use as well.

Bring a collection of both back to me - and remember that the eagles are sacred, I will not accept feathers that are gathered from their harm.
