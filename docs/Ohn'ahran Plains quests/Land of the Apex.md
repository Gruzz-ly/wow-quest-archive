'''Land of the Apex''', along with [[Signs of the Wind]], starts the optional "Elder Nazuun" storyline in [[Teerakai]].

==Objectives==
Collect 10 Shadespinner Webs, 2 Slyvern Talons, and 2 Thunderspine Scales.
*[[Shadespinner's Web]] (10)
*[[Slyvern Talon]] (2)
*[[Thunderspine Scale]] (2)

==Description==
You should know of [[Rellen, The Learned|Rellen, the Learned]], who gave [[centaur]] the tools to survive a merciless land. His memory requires offerings from worthy beasts.

Take care, traveler. They are not as dangerous as they were eons past, but they're still the plains' apex predators.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Money]]
*[[XP]]
*+200 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]

==Progress==
Have you returned with the offerings?

==Completion==
You've returned intact. Good. Always respect the animals' prowess.

==Notes==
A simple kill-collect quest. [[Thunderspine Scale]] drops from [[Thunderspine Crasher]]s and [[Thunderspine Rumbler|Rumblers]] in [[Thunderspine Thicket]], [[Slyvern Talon]] from [[Nimblewing Slyvern]]s and [[Nimblewing Striker|Strikers]] in the [[Primordial Vale]], and [[Shadespinner's Web]] drops from [[Rustfang Creeper]]s in the [[Deadsnare Caverns]].
