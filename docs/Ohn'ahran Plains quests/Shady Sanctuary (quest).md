We must inform the rest of the [[green dragonflight]] that the barrier to the [[Ancient Bough]] is under threat. [[Gerithus]] and I have much to discuss.

<Name>, I will place my mark upon you. The other dragons who see it will know that you are my trusted messenger.

Go and rally my flight. They must know and prepare for the battle ahead.

Alowen is guarding the northern path down from the sanctuary with a group of [[Emerald Scaleguard]]s and [[Emerald Warden]]s, in front of several scorched [[Primalist Invader]] corpses.
:The Primalists seem to have paused their attack. Do you bring news of their movements?

:{{Gossip|The Primalists are on the move. Merithra calls the flight to battle!}}
:{{Text|say|Guard-Captain Alowen|More bloodshed it is, then. My forces will head there at once. See you in the fray, <class>.}}
:''Alowen and her forces run down the path to the west.''

;Sariosa

In the area, [[Gracus]] offers a side quest: [[Some Call Me Bug Catcher]].

;Sidra the Mender
Sidra is found in the northern part of the grove, healing the wounded [[Vermera]]. She is surrounded by several other wounded Emerald Scaleguards and Wardens, one of whom is being healed by a [[Sister of the Garden]]. On approach:
:{{Text|emote|Vermera roars in pain.}}
:{{Text|say|Sidra the Mender|Easy, 'Mera. The more you stay still, the easier this will be to heal.}}

Talk to Sidra:
:The Primalists attacked us without mercy. We held our own, but it came at a heavy cost.

:{{Gossip|You are needed at the front.}}
:{{Text|say|Sidra the Mender|My sisters and I will answer Merithra's call. We will be right behind you to care for the wounded.}}

;Viranikus
Viranikus is located atop [[Merithra's Watch]] overlooking the sanctuary. [[Aronus]] offers transports to the platform if the player doesn't want to fly there on their own. Hovering in front of Viranikus are three more dragons: [[Berinus]], [[Hesthera]], and [[Fernarus]]. Talk to Viranikus:
:The Primalists are scattered for the time being. Peace descends on the Ancient Bough once more. Thank you, <race>.

:{{Gossip|The centaur clans have declared their support. Rally your troops!}}
:{{Text|say|Viranikus|You heard the <race>! To battle, dragons! Do your flight proud.}}
:{{Text|emote|Berinus roars.}}
:''The three dragons fly off to the north. After a moment, Viranikus flies after them.''
