==Objectives==
Meet Sansok Khan.
*Speak with [[Sansok Khan]]{{Co|85.7|25.3|Ohn'ahran Plains}}

==Description==
Sansok Khan is the most skilled [[hunter]] in [[Clan Shikaar|our clan]]. Though she cannot hear, she proved her capabilities time and time again as both a hunter and a leader.

She is a mentor to us all, a trusted leader, and a fierce defender of the [[Maruuk Centaur|Maruuk]].

It is an honor for you to speak with her.
