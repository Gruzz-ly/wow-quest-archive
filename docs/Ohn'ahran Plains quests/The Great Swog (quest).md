'''The Great Swog''' starts from the fished-up [[Rusted Coin of the Isles]], which breadcrumbs players to [[The Great Swog]] in the [[Roaring Dragonsprings]] of the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]]. The swog trades [[Copper Coin of the Isles]], [[Silver Coin of the Isles]], and [[Gold Coin of the Isles]] for some sacs of swog treasures.

==Objectives==
Bring the Rusted Coin of the Isles to [[The Great Swog]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].

==Description==
A peculiar coin fished up around the [[Dragon Isles]].

You've heard rumors from fellow anglers of a creature in the Ohn'ahran Plains who collects such things.
