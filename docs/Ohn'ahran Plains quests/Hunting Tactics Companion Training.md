'''Hunting Tactics: Companion Training''' becomes available at Renown level 12 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Objectives==
Choose an additional companion ability for use in the [[Grand Hunt]].
*Companion ability selected

==Description==
Your renown with the [[Maruuk Centaur|Maruuk]] grows once more. For your efforts, I will teach you to command your hunting companion more effectively in combat.

==Progress==
Speak with me to progress your hunting tactics.

==Completion==
Your growth strengthens the clan.

Return again when your renown grows, and I will continue your training.

==Notes==
*Open the Shikaar Hunting Tactics window by speaking to [[Trainer Frodrum]] and selecting "I want to choose my Shikaar hunting tactics." Then, pick an option in the third and/or fourth row.
