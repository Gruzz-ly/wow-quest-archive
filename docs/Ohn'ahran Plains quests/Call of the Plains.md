'''Call of the Plains''' is a [[breadcrumb quest]] for the [[Aylaag Camp]]. [[Roki]] will run up and offer this quest when you reach Renown level 4 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Hadari Khan]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].

==Description==
<[[Roki]] drops a message into your hand.>

<Name>, [[Clan Aylaag]] has heard tales of your great deeds. We invite you to meet our clan and share our ways.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|62|10}}
* 1,150 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Your aid is welcome, Dragonrider. There is much to be done around our camp.
