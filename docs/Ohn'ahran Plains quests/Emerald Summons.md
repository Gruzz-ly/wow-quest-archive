'''Emerald Summons''' begins the "Garden of Secrets" chapter of the [[Valdrakken Accord]]. At Renown 19, [[Herald Flaps]] will fly up to offer this quest.

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Merithra]] at [[Merithra's Watch]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].

==Description==
Merithra, the current leader of the [[green dragon]]s, wishes for you to meet her in the [[Emerald Gardens]].

[[Drakonid]] emissaries from the azure flight have come to learn about the gardens. Things have not always been peaceful with the [[blue dragon]]s, so it is important to her that relations are kept cordial and positive.

She is on her perch above the [[Shady Sanctuary]] in the Emerald Gardens, at Merithra's Watch.
