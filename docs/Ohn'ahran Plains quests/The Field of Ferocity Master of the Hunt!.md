==Description==
This is it. You and your pals have hit the big time. A match against the champ, Huntmaster Amaa herself. Don't pat yourself on the back just yet. She's ruthless. It was nice knowing you.
 
After she kills you, can I have your stuff?

==Progress==
You need to speak with Gurgthock to start the round, then defeat the final competitor of the Field of Ferocity. Good luck.

==Completion==
You are quite the impressive and fearsome combatant. The [[centaur]]s are in awe of you. [[Gurgthock]] is fuming for not betting his [[farm]] on you instead of Amaa. You win the grand prize.
