==Objectives==
Speak with [[Tyrande Whisperwind]] in the [[Central Encampment]].

==Description==
As you know, [[Amirdrassil]] is no ordinary tree. Tyrande Whisperwind and the rest of the [[kaldorei]] have been preparing to properly welcome it with a ceremony of their own, and establish a new [[moonwell]]. 

I am certain that Tyrande would want you there for the dedication.

Let us join them at the Central Encampment.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|80|90}}
* 1,200 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Amirdrassil is everything we had hoped for. Let us properly honor its arrival.

==Notes==
Upon accept, [[Merithra]] has new gossip:

{{Dialogue|
A tree raised in dreams is now in the waking world. Truly, this is a blessing to the kaldorei.

We could not have accomplished this without you, my friend.
