==Objectives==
Find Firequackers and make sure he gets to [[Gracus]].
*Search for [[Firequackers]].
*Make the [[Queasy Hornswog]] throw up.
*Speak to Gracus about what you saw.

==Description==
Hold on... there is something...

<Gracus looks around.>

Firequackers! The boy must have gone down the river again! No matter how often I tell him, he always tries to play with the [[hornswog]]s!

I need you to go check on him. I hope he didn't get swallowed again.
