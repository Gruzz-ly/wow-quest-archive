'''Well-Earned Victory''' concludes the second portion of the "The Spark of Ingenuity" quest line. This quest was originally unlocked the week of 2022-12-13, and the follow-up was unlocked the week of 2023-01-03.

==Objectives==
Return to [[Engine of Innovation]] in [[Valdrakken]].
* Return to Engine of Innovation
* Use the Portal (Optional) {{co|66.3|55.4|Ohn'ahran Plains}}

==Description==
The smallest of things can contain the greatest of treasures.

Take the essence to the Engine of Innovation.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|76|20}}
* 1,300 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Can you feel the potential emanating from the bottle?

==Notes==
On accept:
:{{Text|say|Maiden of Inspiration|Until we meet again, adventurer.}}
:{{text|say|Maiden of Inspiration|Safe travels.}}

As before, a Portal to Valdrakken appears. Take it for a shortcut back to the [[Bronze Enclave]]!

Approach the engine:
:{{text|say|Maiden of Inspiration|Welcome back, adventurer.}}

There's no quest to remind players to use their [[Bottled Essence]] to create the [[Spark of Ingenuity]], but the [[Extra Action Button]] is present while players are in the vicinity of the Engine of Innovation.
