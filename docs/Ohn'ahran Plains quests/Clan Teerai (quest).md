'''Clan Teerai''' is one of the three opening quests for the "Maruukai" chapter of the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]]. The other two are [[Clan Nokhud (quest)]] and [[Clan Ohn'ir (quest)]].

==Objectives==
Report to [[Qariin Dotur]].

==Description==
<As Sansok Khan signs, [[Okir]] vocalizes for her.>

[[Clan Teerai]] is the clan of our [[Khanam Matra]], our direct connection to the [[Maruuk Centaur|Maruuk]] ancestors. They are the holders of our traditions, and our storytellers.

To understand the Maruuk, you must understand Clan Teerai. One of the Khanam Matra's daughters, [[Qariin Dotur]], is preparing a meal for our ancestors. Aid her. She will teach you about our people.
