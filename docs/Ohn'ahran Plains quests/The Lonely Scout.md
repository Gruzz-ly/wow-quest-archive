==Objectives==
Speak to [[dryad]]s in [[Shady Sanctuary]].
*Speak to [[Sister of the Garden|Sisters of the Garden]] (2)
*Speak to [[Erynna]]
*Return to [[Scout Santuun]].

==Description==
I know, I know, it's silly. A [[centaur]] and a dryad, right? We can't be more different.

But ever since I first laid eyes on the [[Emerald Gardens]], I really have wanted to get to know one better. I just can't seem to say or do anything to keep them interested in me.

I've heard they're into... plants. And cute baby [[animal]]s. And maybe killing [[poacher]]s, but I don't even know where to start.

Please, you can talk to them, right? Could you ask around and find out what they like? Then maybe I can have a shot.
