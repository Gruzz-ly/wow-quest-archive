==Objectives==
Speak with the Aylaag Shepherd and return the lost [[sheep]] to [[Clan Aylaag|the clan]].
*[[Aylaag Shepherd]] spoken with
*[[Wandering Argali]] returned to the herd (3)

==Description==
One of our key resources is our [[argali]] herd. Every loss is felt deeply for the strength of our clan.

A few of the argali have gotten separated and have wandered away from the herd. Our shepherds could use your assistance reclaiming them.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*9,350 [[XP]]
*+150 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]
*[[Dragon Isles Supply Cache]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
Thank you. Our clan deeply appreciates the assistance.

==Notes==
