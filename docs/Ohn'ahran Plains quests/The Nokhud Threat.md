'''The Nokhud Threat''' concludes the "Maruukai" chapter of the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]].

==Objectives==
Discuss next steps.

==Description==
[[Clan Nokhud]]'s army is taking [[Maruukai]].

Let us discuss our next moves in the [[khan]] chambers. We must keep the [[Maruuk Centaur|Maruuk]] safe.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|2|62|10}}
*1,150 [[XP]]
*+150 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]

==Completion==
The very lifeblood of the Maruuk is in danger.
