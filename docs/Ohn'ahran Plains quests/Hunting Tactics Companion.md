'''Hunting Tactics: Companion''' becomes available at Renown level 9 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Objectives==
Choose to fight alongside a [[bakar]] or [[ohuna]] during the [[Grand Hunt]].
*Hunting companion selected

==Description==
Your [[renown]] with the Maruuk grows. You may now hunt alongside a tenacious bakar or swift ohuna companion.

==Progress==
Speak with me to advance your hunting tactics.

==Completion==
Treat your hunting companion with loyalty and respect and they will protect your life with their own.

Return when your renown grows and I will train you further.

==Notes==
*Open the Shikaar Hunting Tactics window by speaking to [[Trainer Frodrum]] and selecting "I want to choose my Shikaar hunting tactics." Then, pick one of the two options in the first row.
