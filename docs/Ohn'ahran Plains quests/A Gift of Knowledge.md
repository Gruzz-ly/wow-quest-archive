'''A Gift of Knowledge''' becomes available after reaching Renown 14 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Completion==
Ah, dragonrider! I know that you're a <alchemist / blacksmith / herbalist / leatherworker / skinner> of no small skill, but we [[Maruuk Centaur|Maruuk]] have methods you would do well to learn.

Our <alchemists / smiths / herbalists / crafters / skinners> assembled these notes to aid your progress. Perhaps you'll visit with them and share some of your knowledge as well.

Between friends, there are no secrets under the sky!
