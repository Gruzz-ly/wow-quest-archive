==Description==
I'm grounded indefinitely! Doesn't my Ohn know I'm turning sixteen next spring? I should be able to leave when I want to.

You look like you could help me out. I do have an escape plan: by channeling these totems, I'll concentrate the air and create quite a stir. But I can't go around placing them, because I'm under maximum wind watch. Even [[Windsage Togotseng|Togo]] won't help me.

But with your help... Please! I promise I'll show you something cool.
