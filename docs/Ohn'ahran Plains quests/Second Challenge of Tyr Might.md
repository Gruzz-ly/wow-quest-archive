==Objectives==
Complete the second Challenge of Tyr in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].
*Enemies defeated
*Speak with Maiden of Inspiration (Optional)

==Description==
<As you approach, inscription on the statue lights up with a faint glow.>

"Wield the Keeper's power and show the undaunted determination of one who stands strong."

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Bottled Essence]]
* {{cost|82|86|}}
* 15,400 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Once again, you have done well and earned your reward.
