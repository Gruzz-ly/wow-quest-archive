'''Hooves of War''' begins the "Ohn'ahra's Blessing" chapter of the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]].

==Objectives==
Deliver [[Khanam Matra Sarest|the Khanam Matra]]'s message to [[Khansguard Jebotai]] at [[Teerakai]].
*[[Khanam Matra's Message]] (provided)

==Description==
[[Balakar Khan|Balakar]] will marshal his forces for war. I will do the same, but we are outnumbered.

[[Green dragonflight|The dragons]] have asked for our aid, and we can use their help to even the odds. To answer their call for an alliance, I must blow the [[Horn of Drusahl]], but I need [[Ohn'ahra]]'s blessing. Will you help me?

Go to Teerakai and deliver my declaration of war to my lieutenant, Jebotai. He will marshal my forces and direct you to [[Clan Ohn'ir|the mystics]] who can find Ohn'ahra.
