==Objectives==
Meet up with [[Toddy Whiskers|Toddy]] near [[Szar Skeleth]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].

==Description==
According to the watcher's logs, the stone of Insight can be found in a draconic tower on a river in the center of the Ohn'ahran Plains. That should be Szar Skeleth. Be careful. We can be sure the [[Sundered Flame]] won't let us just walk in and take it.

Once you get there, rendezvous with Toddy. Then we can retrieve the stone.
