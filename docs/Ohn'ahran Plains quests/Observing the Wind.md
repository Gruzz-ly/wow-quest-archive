'''Observing the Wind''' is available after reaching Renown level 6 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

This quest has no objectives; simply choose a reward and complete. The reward you didn't pick can be purchased from [[Quartermaster Huseng]] for 50 [[Dragon Isles Supplies]].

==Rewards==
{{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards:
|Windborne Velocidrake: Horned Jaw
|Windborne Velocidrake: Yellow Horns
}}

If the manuscripts have already been obtained, you will receive:
*[[Maruuk Centaur Supply Satchel]]

==Completion==
<Name>, you are a dragonrider, yes? You show great promise as an ally.

Let me give you this, then, to celebrate our growing alliance. And I promise there is more where that came from!
