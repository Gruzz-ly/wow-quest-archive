'''Hunting Tactics: Blessing of Ohn'ahra''' becomes available at Renown level 10 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Objectives==
Select a blessing of [[Ohn'ahra]] for use in the [[Grand Hunt]].
*Blessing of Ohn'ahra selected.

==Description==
Your renown with the Maruuk grows. Listen closely, and I will teach you to call upon a blessing of Ohn'ahra to protect you and your allies.

==Progress==
Speak with me to advance your hunting tactics.

==Completion==
May the winds of Ohn'ahra guide and protect you.

Return when your renown grows, and I will train you further.

==Notes==
*Open the Shikaar Hunting Tactics window by speaking to [[Trainer Frodrum]] and selecting "I want to choose my Shikaar hunting tactics." Then, pick one of the options in the second row.
