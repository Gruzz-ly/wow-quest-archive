'''Call of the Hunt''' is a [[breadcrumb quest]] for the [[Grand Hunt]]. [[Roki]] will run up and offer this quest when you reach Renown level 5 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Trainer Frodrum]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].

==Description==
<[[Roki]] drops the note into your hand>

<Name>, [[Clan Shikaar]] invites you to the Grand Hunt. Meet with Trainer Frodrum in the Ohn'ahran Plains.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|62|10}}
* 1,150 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Dragonrider! Your aid will be welcome in the coming hunts.

==Notes==
There's no direct follow-up. Current Grand Hunts are displayed on the world map.
