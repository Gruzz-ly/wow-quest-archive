'''Hunting Tactics: Shikaar Mentorship''' becomes available after reaching Renown 16 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]].

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you:
*{{Abilities|Shikaar Mentorship|id=389628|[[Shikaar Hunter]]s will grant you a boon during the grand hunt, increasing one of your secondaries for its duration.|icon=inv_cape_special_centaurmartial_b_01}}

==Completion==
Your renown with the Maruuk rises. Seek out other [[Clan Shikaar|Shikaar]] during the [[Grand Hunt|grand hunt]], and they will improve your combat prowess while the hunt is on.

Come to me again when your renown grows, and I will train you more.
