==Description==
A [[thunder lizard]] named Mara'nar the Thunderous worked his way from [[Thunderspine Thicket]] to Primordial Vale, disrupting local wildlife. It has electrified water, knocked down trees, and broken duck nests.

This menace must be stopped. Slay him and bring one of his plates to [[Khansguard Jebotai]] as proof.

May [[Ohn'ahra]] guide you.
