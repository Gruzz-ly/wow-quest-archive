'''Waygate: Rusza'thar Reach''' is one of several quests that can be chosen when you unlock an additional [[Ancient Waygate]] beyond the first two. Waygate unlocks happen at Renown levels 8, 10, 15, 17, 20, and 23 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]].

==Objectives==
Locate the [[Dormant Waygate]] at the [[Rusza'thar Reach]] in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].
*Waygate activated {{co|81.3|39.0|Ohn'ahran Plains}}
*[[Enchanted Arcana Vessel]] (provided)

==Description==
An expeditionary member reported a dormant stone sighting atop a lookout in the Ohn'ahran Plains.

Up fer an adventure?
