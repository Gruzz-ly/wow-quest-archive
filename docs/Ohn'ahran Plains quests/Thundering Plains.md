==Description==
There is a great storm beast which wanders this ridge. Many a [[Clan Shikaar|Shikaar]] [[hunter]] has attempted to take it down, only to fall to its ferocity.

If you wish for some hunting glory, you may attempt to bring down the beast. Bring back its horn as tribute.

*+300 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]
