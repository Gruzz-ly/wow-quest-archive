'''The Highest Honor''' becomes available after reaching Renown 25 with the [[Maruuk Centaur]]. Completing this quest also awards the title 'Khansguard <name>' and unlocks access to [[A Whispering Breeze]].

==Rewards==
{{itembox|You will receive:|Primal Infusion
}}

==Completion==
My friend, you have defended [[Ohn'ahran Plains|these plains]] alongside my people, lending your aid to each of our clans without hesitation. You are an outsider no longer, but a familiar face among the [[centaur]].

To honor our bond, I anoint you with the title of [[Khansguard]] and present you with this gift. May you proudly bear the Maruuk name wherever your travels take you.
