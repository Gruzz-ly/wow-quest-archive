==Description==
You want me to train your [[bakar]] in shepherding? Well, that's certainly what we do here, but I've never trained one quite so BIG before...

The argali don't seem to be bothered by his presence, though, and they're very good judges of character. Trust me.

I'm willing to give him a shot, but a herding bakar needs to not only be gentle, but smart, too. And if they aren't able to take charge of the herd, the argali won't obey them. It's harder than it looks! Go on and give it a try, I'll watch.
