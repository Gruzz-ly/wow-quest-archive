==Objectives==
Meet [[Akunda the Cataloger]] and [[Gunner Fren]] near the [[Overflowing Rapids]].

==Description==
Pictures bring people together, <name>.

Our catalogers are typically sent out in pairs for safety, and it has been lovely to see members of different factions and backgrounds working together in pursuit of knowledge on these incredible isles.

One such eager duo is currently charting one of the central rivers of the [[Waking Shores]].

If you wish to aid our efforts, join them in cataloging the wildlife there.
