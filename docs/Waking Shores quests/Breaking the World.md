'''Breaking the World''' is a [[bonus objective]] at the [[Slagmire]] of the [[Obsidian Citadel]] in the [[Waking Shores]]. To trigger the event, turn in a [[Restored Obsidian Key]] to [[Igys the Believer]] at {{co|32.3|52.5|Waking Shores}} to help fill the progress bar. Ten keys in total are required.

==Objectives==
*[[Morchok]] slain {{co|32.8|52.5|Waking Shores}}

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Alexstrasza the Life-Binder]]|Someone has been aiding the [[Worldbreakers]]. They have summoned a vile monstrosity from our past into existence. You must destroy it.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +100 [[reputation]] with [[Sabellian (faction)|Sabellian]]
* +100 reputation with [[Wrathion (faction)|Wrathion]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Alexstrasza the Life-Binder|The past should remain in the past, though I fear the Worldbreakers will never cease in their goal to bring about the [[Hour of Twilight|hour of twilight]].}}
