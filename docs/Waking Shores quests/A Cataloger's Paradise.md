'''A Cataloger's Paradies''' is the intro for cataloging expeditions in the [[Dragon Isles]]. [[Doc Nanners]] will run up and offer this quest to players who reach Renown level 8 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]] and have [[world quest]]s unlocked.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Cataloger Coralie]] in the [[Dragonscale Basecamp]].

==Description==
<Doc Nanners looks out of breath as he hands you a letter inviting you back to the Dragonscale Expedition Basecamp for training. He eagerly tries to explain.>

[[Cataloger Coralie|Kind elf]]. [[Cataloger Wulferd|Proud tiny-man]]. Many cameras. Yes?

<Doc Nanners nods excitedly, awaiting your response.>
