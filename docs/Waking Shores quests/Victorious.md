'''Victorious''' concludes the third portion of the "The Spark of Ingenuity" quest line. This quest was originally unlocked the week of 2023-01-03, and the next quest was unlocked the week of 2023-01-10.

==Objectives==
Return to the [[Engine of Innovation]] in [[Valdrakken]].
* Return to Engine of Innovation
* Use the Portal (Optional)

==Description==
Once again I ask you to bring this essence to the Engine of Innovation.

==Rewards==

* 1,300 [[XP]]
