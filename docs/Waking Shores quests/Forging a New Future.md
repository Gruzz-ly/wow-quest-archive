==Description==
The forge of the [[Deathwing|Earth-Warder]] is the most powerful of all the [[dragon]] forges in existence. Our ancestors used it to create the strongest weapons and armor to defend [[Azeroth]].

Within the hearth of that forge is the power necessary to reform the shattered fragments of the [[Obsidian Oathstone|Oathstone]].

Let us go and prepare the forge for the work to be done.
