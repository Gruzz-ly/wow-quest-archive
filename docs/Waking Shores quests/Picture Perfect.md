==Objectives==
Ride the [[Cataloger's Raft]] with [[Gunner Fren|Fren]] and take pictures of local wildlife.
*Ride the Cataloger's Raft
*Take Pictures of Wildlife (0/100%)
*Return to [[Akunda the Cataloger]] at her camp.

==Description==
Sorry if ya be waitin' long. Sometimes I get lost in de telescope.

Do ya ever find da world be easier understood from a distance? No interference, just nature goin' about its business without us...

<Akunda the Cataloger sighs.>

I see [[Cataloger Coralie|Coralie]] gave ya a camera. Good.

Take a ride down the river with Fren, and take photographs of some wildlife in dey natural habitats for me to study.
