'''A New Mystery''' begins the "A Mystery, Sealed" chapter of the [[Dragonscale Expedition]] campaign. [[Doc Nanners]] will run up to the player and offer this quest after reaching Renown 13.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Naleidea Rivergleam]] in the [[Dragonscale Basecamp]].

==Description==
<Doc Nanners looks out of breath as he hands you a letter inviting you back to the [[Dragonscale Expedition]] Basecamp. He eagerly tries to explain.>

Sealed door. [[Titan]] secrets. Yes?

<Doc Nanners nods excitedly, awaiting your response.>
