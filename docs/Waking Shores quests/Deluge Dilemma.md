==Description==
[[Elemental]]s are killing the [[hornstrider]]s! I know how to wrangle large [[critter]]s, but I never thought I'd have to go toe-to-toe with an elemental! These aren't anything like baby [[thunder lizard]]s...

<[[Dazakros]] sighs, distressed.>

Please knock down a few of them elementals. Hornstriders have a lot more sense than we give them credit for. They'll fight back if they have the means to!
