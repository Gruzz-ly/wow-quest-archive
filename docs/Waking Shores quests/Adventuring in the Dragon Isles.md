'''Adventuring in the Dragon Isles''' is available on [[alt]] characters with [[Adventure Mode]] unlocked. This quest unlocks the [[Scouting Map]] in the [[Wingrest Embassy]], giving you the option to start any of the four [[Dragon Isles]] zone campaigns regardless of order.

==Objectives==
Review the Scouting Map to choose an area within the [[Dragon Isles]] to support.
*Zone chosen from [[Scouting Map]]

==Description==
Oh, I know what you can do!

The [[Dragonscale Expedition]] has posted a very fine looking map of the Dragon Isles nearby. 

Their drawings are quite accurate and detailed, if I do say so myself. Sorry, you're not here for an art review.

You should go check it out to see what areas of the Dragon Isles could use your help!

*'''Azure Span''' - [[Azure Span]] is the former home of the [[Blue Dragonflight]].
*'''Thaldraszus''' - [[Thaldraszus]] is the seat of the [[dragon Aspects]] and home to the [[Bronze Dragonflight]].
