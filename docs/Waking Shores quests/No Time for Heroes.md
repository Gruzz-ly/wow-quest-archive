==Objectives==
Slay Modak the Flamespit, Piercer Gigra, and Olphis the Molten.
*[[Piercer Gigra]] slain {{co|33.5|68.6|Waking Shores}}
*[[Modak Flamespit]] slain {{co|35.7|60.7|Waking Shores}}
*[[Olphis the Molten]] slain {{co|34.8|66.9|Waking Shores}}

==Description==
From what we have observed, [[djaradin]] society is built on strength. The strongest serve as examples for others to fight all the harder.

Among the forces below are three examples of the mightiest djaradin... and your targets.

You, <name>, will personally eliminate them. This should deal a devastating blow to their morale.
