'''Continued Waygate Exploration''' becomes available at Renown level 17 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]]. This is a [[breadcrumb quest]] that leads back to [[Rubyscale Outpost]], where a sixth [[Ancient Waygate]] can be unlocked.

==Objectives==
Meet [[Thaelin Darkanvil]] at the [[Rubyscale Outpost]] in The [[Waking Shores]].

==Description==
<Surprisingly, the messenger did not leave you with a satchel or a note this time, rather a single item from Thaelin's personal collection.

Upon further investigation, a tiny little message from Thaelin seeks your assistance once again.>

==Completion==
Can't get enough, can ye?

Glad tae see ye again, <name>! Let's get to it.
