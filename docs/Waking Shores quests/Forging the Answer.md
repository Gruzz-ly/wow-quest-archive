'''Forging the Answer''' is a quest that will appear after [[Forgemaster Bazentus]] at the [[Obsidian Citadel]] in [[Waking Shores]] has received 10 [[Restored Obsidian Key]]s from players.

==Objectives==
*Earth-Warder Weapon obtained from Forge
*Qalashi Forces Defeated

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +100 reputation with [[Sabellian]]
* +100 reputation with [[Wrathion]]
