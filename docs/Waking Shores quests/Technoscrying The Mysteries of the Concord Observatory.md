'''Technoscrying: The Mysteries of the Concord Observatory''' is an [[Azerothian Archives]] world quest that is unlocked upon completion of [[The Scale of it All]]. It takes place at {{co|56|44|Waking Shores}} at [[Concord Observatory]] in [[Waking Shores]].

==Objectives==
*Recover the Items (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Zenata]]|There are more secrets. We must uncover them, yes? We should start scrying!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,500 [[XP]]
* {{cost|600}}
* +300 [[reputation]] with [[Azerothian Archives]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Zenata|The paint on this palette has aged and dried. These colors are frequently used in paintings of starry skies.}} -or- {{text|say|An ancient duck carving? Fascinating! Did the birds migrate from another continent, or did they always live here?}}
:{{text|say|Zenata|You have wrested another secret from the grip of the past. Well done, <name>!}}

==Notes==
Use the [[Technoscryers]] (Zenata at {{co|56.4|43.9|Forbidden Reach}} has a spare if necessary), then use #1 Scrying Mode to look around for hidden objects. Find objects at:
* {{co|57.6|43.5|Waking Shores}} [[Fork]]
* {{co|58.1|44.4|Waking Shores}} [[Painter's Pallete]] (ground floor)
* {{co|58.0|44.0|Waking Shores}} [[Drakonid Jar]] (upper floor)
