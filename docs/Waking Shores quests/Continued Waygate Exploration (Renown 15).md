'''Continued Waygate Exploration''' becomes available at Renown level 15 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]]. This is a [[breadcrumb quest]] that directs you back to [[Rubyscale Outpost]], where you can unlock a fifth [[Ancient Waygate]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Thaelin Darkanvil]] at the [[Rubyscale Outpost]] in The [[Waking Shores]].
