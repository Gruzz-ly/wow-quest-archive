'''A Dragon's Day Off''' is a one-off side quest in the [[Hissing Grotto]] north of the [[Obsidian Citadel]] in the [[Waking Shores]]. To begin it, find [[Vazallia]] and ask "What are you doing here?" after completing the Wrathion's Gambit storyline ending in [[Restoring the Faith]].

==Objectives==
Locate the various drinks for Vazallia.
*[[Flameslinger Rum]] {{co|35.93|47.92|Waking Shores}}
*[[Riptide Brew]] {{co|62.95|79.77|Waking Shores}}
*[[Popfizz Punch]] {{co|39.30|38.32|Waking Shores}}
*[[Happy Trigger Sour]] {{co|43.10|67.30|Waking Shores}}
*[[Slam Bam Swill]] {{co|46.91|30.70|Waking Shores}}
*[[Whirlwind Wine]] {{co|76.18|42.91|Waking Shores}}
*[[Stealthy Elven Port]] {{co|25.70|55.14|Waking Shores}}

==Description==
A lifetime spent in the [[Outland]]s{{Sic}}... always fighting. So little time to relax!

Sometimes a [[dragon]] just needs a day off by the sea with a cold, cold drink in their claws.

Hey, maybe you can help me relax! Do me a favor and see if you can find a few of these drinks to refresh our supply here.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Spyragos]]
*{{Cost|51|48}}
*12,650 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Have fetched my beverages yet? No? That's too bad.

==Completion==
Yes! Time to kick back and relax!

==Notes==
Beverages are found all over the Waking Shores:
#[[Flameslinger Rum]] - Complete the "The Shadow of His Wings" side-chain ending with [[The Shadow of His Wings]], then purchase the drink from [[Dealer Vexil]] at {{co|35.93|47.92|Waking Shores}}
#[[Riptide Brew]] - Find it in the cave guarded by [[Lago the Drowned]] at {{co|62.95|79.77|Waking Shores}}
#[[Popfizz Punch]] - Look in the shipwreck at {{co|39.30|38.32|Waking Shores}}
#[[Happy Trigger Sour]] - Purchase it from [[Firstmate Kaleson]] at {{co|43.10|67.30|Waking Shores}}
#[[Slam Bam Swill]] - Find it in the tree house on the mountain at {{co|46.91|30.70|Waking Shores}}
#[[Whirlwind Wine]] - Purchase [[Kul Tiran Red]] from [[Kora Fullsails]] at {{co|43.10|67.32|Waking Shores}}, then throw it at the [[Rampaging Wind]] at {{co|76.18|42.91|Waking Shores}}
#[[Stealthy Elven Port]] - Purchase it from [[Outfitter Tipech]] at {{co|25.70|55.14|Waking Shores}}
