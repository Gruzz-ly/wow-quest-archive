==Objectives==
Introduce yourself to the [[Horde]] representatives.

==Description==
[[Light]] be with you, <name>.

[[Dragonscale Expedition|This expedition]] is under the protection of both the Horde and [[Alliance]]. Our orders are clear: give aid to any members of the expedition, regardless of their other allegiances.

The Horde's representatives, [[Warlord Grimaxe]] and her daughter [[Shuja Grimaxe|Shuja]], stand guard on the other side of camp.

I fought with Warlord Grimaxe against the [[scourge]]. She is noble and will serve this expedition well.

Go offer her your assistance. Show her that the Alliance honors its commitments.
