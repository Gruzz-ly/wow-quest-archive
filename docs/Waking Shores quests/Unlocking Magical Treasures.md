'''Unlocking Magical Treasures''' is the unlock quest for [[Magic-Bound Chest]]s in the [[Dragon Isles]]. It becomes available at Renown level 16 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]].

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you:
*{{Abilities|Magical Chests of the Dragon Isles|Knowledge of magical wards allows you to see various magical treasure chests hidden in ruins and deep in caves.|id=388264|icon=inv_misc_chest_azerite}}

==Completion==
[[Dragon Isles|This place]] has many treasures you have yet to see, as they are bound in [[magic]]. I can teach you to see them. Many lie hidden deep within caves or in magically locked in the various ruins of the land. Good luck in your search!
