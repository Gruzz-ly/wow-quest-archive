'''Next Steppes''' begins the "Into the Plains" chapter of the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]] and becomes available after completing the [[Waking Shores storyline]]. For alts, the storyline instead starts with [[Ohn'ahran Plains (quest)]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Ambassador Taurasza]] in the [[Rubyscale Outpost]].

==Description==
The [[green dragonflight]] requires your aid now, hero. [[Ysera|My sister]]'s [[Merithra of the Dream|daughter]] and her flight have entered the [[Emerald Dream]] in an attempt to empower [[Emerald Oathstone|their own oathstone]], but we have heard nothing since their slumber began.

Seek out the [[centaur]] that rule over the Ohn'ahran Plains. You may well need their connection to the land and its spirits if you are to rouse the greens.

They are a proud and territorial people who do not suffer insult lightly. Speak with our ambassador and he will prepare you.
