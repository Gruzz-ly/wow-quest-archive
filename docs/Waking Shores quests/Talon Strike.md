==Objectives==
Djaradin defenses disrupted.
*[[Djaradin]] defenses disrupted (0/100%)
*0/1 Blacktalon Marker used on Djaradin (Optional)

==Description==
With your help, my [[Blacktalon]] [[assassin]]s will cut down the djaradin defending the outer perimeter. Mark any targets with a small stun bomb, and they will come to your aid.

Proceed quickly and eliminate anything that may hinder our advance.

==Rewards==

* 9,050 [[XP]]
