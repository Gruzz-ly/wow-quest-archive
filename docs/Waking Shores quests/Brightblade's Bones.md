'''Brightblade's Bones''' is a world quest located at {{co|22.9|95.3|Waking Shores}} at the [[Apex Canopy]] in the southwestern corner of the [[Waking Shores]].

==Objectives==
*[[Climbing Gear]] acquired
*Find a [[handhold]] on the cliff
*Excavate [[Intact Dragon Bone|Dragon Bones]] in the cliff face (6)
*Rescue [[Cymre Brightblade|Cymre]]'s pet marmoset (Optional) 

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Naleidea Rivergleam]]|Our surveyors spotted something that might be of interest to our research. I trust you brought your climbing gear?}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,050 [[XP]]
* +200 [[reputation]] with [[Dragonscale Expedition]]
