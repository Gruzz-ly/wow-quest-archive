==Description==
Gliding will get you to where you want to go. But what if you want to get there faster?

Diving after jumping from somewhere high will give you a speed boost.

Fly through the rings to the target location

If you find yourself stuck, use the Bronze Timepiece and [[Celormu]] will reset you.
