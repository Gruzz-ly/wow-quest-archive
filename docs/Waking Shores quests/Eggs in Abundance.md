'''Eggs in Abundance''' begins the [[Little Scales Daycare]] questline in [[patch 10.1.5]]. [[Herald Flaps]] will spawn in and follow the player around until they accept the quest.

==Objectives==
Meet [[Amella]] at the [[Ruby Lifeshrine]] in the [[Waking Shores]].

==Description==
<[[Herald Flaps|Flaps]] does a spin and deposits a drool-soaked note into your hand.>

<Name>, the Ruby Life Pools are overwhelmed with [[dragon]] eggs ready to hatch. We are excited to welcome these blessings to [[Azeroth]], but we do not have enough whelptenders to mind them all.

Please visit me at the Lifeshrine so we may discuss a potential solution.

- Amella
