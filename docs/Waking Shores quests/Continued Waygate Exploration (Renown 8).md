'''Continued Waygate Exploration''' becomes available at Renown level 8 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]]. This is a [[breadcrumb quest]] that directs players back to [[Rubyscale Outpost]], to unlock a third [[Ancient Waygate]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Thaelin Darkanvil]] at the [[Rubyscale Outpost]] in the [[Waking Shores]].

==Description==
<The little messenger's loud, screeching call captures your attention. They hand you a note before running off.

The note is a request from Thaelin Darkanvil, seeking your assistance.>

* 1,150 [[XP]]
