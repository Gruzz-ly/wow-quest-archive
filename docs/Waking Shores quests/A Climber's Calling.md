'''A Climber's Calling''' is the intro for rock climbing challenges in the [[Dragon Isles]]. [[Doc Nanners]] will run up and offer this quest when you reach Renown level 6 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]] and have [[world quest]]s unlocked.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Pathfinder Jeb]] in the [[Dragonscale Basecamp]].

==Description==
<Doc Nanners looks out of breath as he hands you a letter inviting you back to the Dragonscale Expedition Basecamp for training. He eagerly tries to explain.>

From Big Jeb. Good friend. Climb now. Yes?

<Doc Nanners nods excitedly, awaiting your response.>
