'''Waygate Exploration: The Forbidden Reach''' is offered to players in [[patch 10.0.7]] who have been to the [[Forbidden Reach]] then returned to the mainland of the [[Dragon Isles]]. [[Doc Nanners]] will run up to offer this quest to players who have reached at least Renown 12 with the [[Dragonscale Expedition]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Thaelin Darkanvil]] at the [[Rubyscale Outpost]] in [[Waking Shores|The Waking Shores]].
* [[Tied-up Envelope]] (provided)
