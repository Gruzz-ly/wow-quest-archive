'''The Bitter End''' becomes available after the next weekly reset after completing [[Pain Management]].

==Objectives==
Bring [[Bathoras]] to [[Duroz Scaletaker|Duroz]] and [[Kolgar Flameguard|Kolgar]].
*Speak to Bathoras
*Return to Kolgar with Bathoras

==Description==
It just happened. I--He was sleeping and I looked away and-- We came back here every day, he just wanted to sit with the [[Dragon whelp|whelps]], I didn't know he--

<Kolgar's face is clearly tear-stained. He rubs his eyes with the back of his hand.>

No, I knew. I knew. But I don't know what to do now. What do I do with him?

Can you-- Can you find Bathoras?
