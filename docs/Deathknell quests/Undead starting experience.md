# Undead starting experience

[[File:WorldMap-DeathknellStart.jpg|thumb|Area map]]

The '''[[Undead (playable)|undead]] starting experience''' takes place in [[Deathknell]] in western [[Tirisfal Glades]]. In it, freshly raised Forsaken characters get to make heads of their new surroundings, take on [[Scourge]] remnants and fight to defeat the [[Rotbrain undead|Rotbrain]] rebellion, before departing for [[Brill]] in Tirisfal Glades.
