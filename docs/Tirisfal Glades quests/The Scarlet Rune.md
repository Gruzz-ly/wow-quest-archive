==Objectives==
Pickpocket or kill [[Scarlet Convert]]s to find a rune, then use it to learn a new ability. Afterwards, return to [[David Trias]] in [[Deathknell]].
*Learn Spell: Engrave Gloves - [[Shadowstrike (rogue rune)]]

==Description==
Who were you in life, <name>? A [[spy]]? A [[thief]]? A cook who was especially handy with a knife? Whatever your past, it's time to embrace new tactics. The [[rogue]]s of the [[Forsaken]] seize every advantage offered to them.

The Scarlet Converts to the east fight like they've never seen combat, but the [[Scarlet Crusade|Crusade]] has issued them mystical runes to enhance their abilities. Steal one of these runes and learn its magic, then return to me.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 85 [[XP]]
*+50 [[reputation]] with [[Undercity (faction)|Undercity]]
*[[Cracked Leather Gloves]]

==Progress==
Have you found the rune, <name>?

==Completion==
Well done, <name>. Applying the rune to your equipment will grant you powerful new abilities.

There are many such runes throughout [[Azeroth]]. Seek them out, as they will help along your journey.
