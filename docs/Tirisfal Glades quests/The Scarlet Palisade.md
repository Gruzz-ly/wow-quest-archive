==Description==
We need someone with "initiative" like yourself to drive the [[Scarlet Crusade]] to the grave.

Prove to me you are capable of serving [[Sylvanas Windrunner|The Dark Lady]] by travelling west to the tower past the [[Solliden Farmstead]] and slaying 10 warriors of the Scarlet Crusade.
