# Tirisfal Glades storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Tirisfal-cata.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Tirisfal Glades]]''' is a level 1-10 Horde-only zone in [[Lordaeron]], and begins the Undead starting experience.

After [[Take to the Skies]], or as soon as players hit level 9, [[Warchief's Command: Silverpine Forest!]] opens up, starting the [[Silverpine Forest storyline]].
