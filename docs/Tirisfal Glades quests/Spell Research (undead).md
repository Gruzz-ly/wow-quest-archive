==Description==
The arcanists of the Royal Apothecary Society commit their unlife to spell research in service to the Forsaken. Adventure may be your calling, but you can serve the Dark Lady by securing and researching spell notes you find in your travels.

Kill the Scarlet Crusade initiates camped near Deathknell, and seize their spell notes. Use this primer to decipher the notes and learn a new spell, then return to me.
