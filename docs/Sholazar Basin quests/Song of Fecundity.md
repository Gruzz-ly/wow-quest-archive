==Objectives==
[[Oracle Soo-nee|Soo-nee]] at [[Rainspeaker Canopy]] wants you to go to the [[Stormwright's Shelf]], northwest of the [[Maker's Perch]], and play the [[Horn of Fecundity]] near 8 piles [[Soaked Fertile Dirt]].
* Song of Fecundity played (8)
* [[Horn of Fecundity]] (provided)

==Notes==
Once you receive the horn fly to the area west of The Maker's Perch (around {{coords|24.5|34.0|Sholazar Basin}}). You will see yellow/brown dirtmounds scattered all across the area. All you have to do is use the horn near mounds to let a small tree grow. Repeat it until you have grown 8 trees and then fly back to Rainspeaker Canopy to turn in your quest.
