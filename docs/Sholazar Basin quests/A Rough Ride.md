==Objectives==
Ride the [[Captive Crocolisk|captive crocolisk]] to Mistwhisper Refuge and then speak to Zepik for further instructions.

If you lose Zepik's Hunting Horn, speak to [[Elder Harkek]] in [[Frenzyheart Hill]].
*Travel to [[Mistwhisper Refuge]].
*Speak to [[Zepik the Gorloc Hunter]]. Zepik can be summoned using [[:Zepik's Hunting Horn]] if he is not nearby.

==Description==
Zepik is best hunter of the [[Frenzyheart]]. Zepik hunts [[Gorloc|big-tongues]]... ugly things near swamps that try to steal all stuff they can find.

Zepik going on long trek to break big-tongue shrine and try to scare them away. We no want big-tongues in this land anymore.

There a [[crocolisk]] chained on northeast side of hill, next to tree - go with Zepik and do what Zepik tell you.
