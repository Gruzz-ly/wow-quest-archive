'''The Etymidian''' is a quest in the [[Waygate (location)|Waygate]] quest chain in [[Sholazar Basin]].

==Objectives==
Go through the [[Waygate (location)|Waygate]] in the southern end of [[Sholazar Basin]] and look for the weapon known as the {{npc||Etymidian}} on the other side.

==Description==
It is time, <name>. The Waygate has been activated for the first time in seven centuries. Use it to travel to where our final hope lies.

Let us put an end to those who would seek to bring the taint of undeath to this pristine land. Let us unleash the Etymidian.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 21,600 [[XP]]

==Completion==
<As you read the engravings on this stone colossus, you realize that this is the weapon that Freya spoke of. This is the Etymidian.>

==Notes==
The [[Waygate (location)|Waygate]] teleports you to the [[Shaper's Terrace]] in [[Un'Goro Crater]]. Walk forward to reach the Etymidian.
