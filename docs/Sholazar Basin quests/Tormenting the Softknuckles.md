==Objectives==
[[Goregek the Gorilla Hunter|Goregek]] has given you a Softknuckle Poker for use on Softknuckles to draw the [[Hardknuckle Matriarch]] out of hiding. Slay the Hardknuckle Matriarch and report her death to [[High-Shaman Rakjak]] at [[Frenzyheart Hill]].

If you lose [[Goregek's Shackles]], speak to [[Elder Harkek]] in Frenzyheart Hill.
* [[Hardknuckle Matriarch]] slain
* [[Softknuckle Poker]] (provided)
