==Objectives==
Zepik wants you to smash the Skyreach Crystal Clusters in front of the [[Mistwhisper Refuge|Mistwhisper]] weather shrine.

If you lose [[Zepik's Hunting Horn]], speak to [[Elder Harkek]] in [[Frenzyheart Hill]].
*Sabotage the Mistwhisper Weather Shrine
*[[Skyreach Crystal Clusters]] (provided)

==Description==
Okay, the crystal things...

The big-tongues use the crystals to power their rain shrines. Zepik not know quite how it works, but Zepik does know that if they use wrong crystals, the clouds and the rain go crazy!

Zepik tried to sneak in at one of their other rain shrines, but it not go so well. It sure to go much better with two!

Take crystals, place at big stone pillar shrine thing, then smash them to release power. Zepik not sure what will happen, so might want to step back after.
