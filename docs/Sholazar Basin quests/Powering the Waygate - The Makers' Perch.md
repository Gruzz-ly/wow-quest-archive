'''Powering the Waygate - The Makers' Perch''' is a quest in the [[Waygate (location)|Waygate]] quest chain.

==Objectives==
The [[Avatar of Freya]] at the [[Rainspeaker Rapids]] wants you to go to the [[Makers' Perch]] and use the Activation Switch Gamma.
* [[Activation Switch Gamma]]

==Description==
It is time then. Let us activate the waygate.

You will find the first switch inside the Makers' Perch in the western edge of the basin.

Beware, <name>. The [[titans]] used the perch to watch over their creations. Many of their stone servants still guard the place.
