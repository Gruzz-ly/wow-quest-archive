== Objectives ==
[[Oracle Soo-nee|Soo-nee]] wants you to use the [[Drums of the Tempest|Tempest Drums]] at the [[Stormwright's Shelf]] and help [[Haiphoon, the Great Tempest|Haiphoon]] devour 3 [[Storm Revenant]]s and 3 [[Aqueous Spirit]]s.
* [[Aqueous Spirit]] Devoured (4)
* [[Storm Revenant]] Devoured (4)
* [[Drums of the Tempest]] (provided)

== Description ==
Listen to storm, yes? The Stormwright's Shelf echoes with ancient song.... the Song of Wind and Water.

The great tempest devours the sons of typhoons and storms. Take this drum and play his song... play Haiphoon's song.

Help him feed on wind and water so tempest can continue.
