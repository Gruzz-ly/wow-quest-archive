'''A Timeworn Coffer''' is a side quest in the [[Waygate (location)|Waygate]] quest chain in [[Sholazar Basin]].

==Objectives==
Obtain a Huge Stone Key from the nearby [[Sholazar Guardian]]s and bring it to the [[Timeworn Coffer]] at the [[Makers' Overlook]].
* [[Huge Stone Key]]

==Description==
The stone coffer appears to be locked. It appears that a key fits inside a large stone keyhole.

Perhaps one of the guardians in the vicinity has this key in its possession.

*{{cost|6|80}}
*21,600 [[XP]]

==Progress==
The stone coffer does not budge.

==Completion==
The huge stone key turns with a deep grunt as the coffer lid opens up to reveal the hidden treasure inside.

==Notes==
You may want to do this quest at the same time as [[Powering the Waygate - The Makers' Overlook]].
