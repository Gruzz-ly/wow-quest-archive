'''A Fel Puppy Of My Own''' is the quest to get the Fel Pup pet.  This is only available once you have killed [[Ceraxas]] for the first time.  You can only have one Fel Pup.

== Completion ==
<Tentatively, the pup licks your hand. After receiving a good scratch behind the ears, the pup barks and wags its tail.>

== Rewards ==
You will receive:
*{{cost|1|54}}
*1,510 [[XP]]
*[[A Tiny Infernal Collar]]

== Notes ==
Ceraxas is a rare located at the [[Temple of Sha'naar]] in [[Tanaan Jungle]].
