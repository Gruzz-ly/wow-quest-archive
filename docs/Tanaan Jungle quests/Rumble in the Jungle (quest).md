'''Rumble in the Jungle''' is a [[weekly quest]] offered by {{NPC|Neutral|Z'tenga the Walker|Saberstalker Quartermaster}} at the [[Saberstalkers]]' camp north of [[Fang'rila]].

Along with [[Tooth and Claw (quest)]], it is one of two repeatable quests that grant reputation with the Saberstalkers.

==Objectives==
Defeat the three arena champions of Fangri'la.
*[[Akrrilo]] slain
*[[Rendarr]] slain
*[[Eyepiercer]] slain

==Description==
Dem fancy cat-men gotta be shown who is da boss 'round dese parts. Kill all their champions, and ya will earn da respect of all creatures in da jungle.

Y'gonna need dese totems to draw dem out.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|16|98}}
*{{Abilities|bracketlink=|King of the Jungle|id=189325|icon=ability_druid_kingofthejungle}} buff (lasts until the next weekly reset)
*1,350 [[XP]]
*+3,500 reputation with the [[Saberstalkers]]

== Progress ==
No lie mon - if I'd had money on you coming back in one piece, I'd be out that money right now.

==Completion==
Ya done good, mon. We gotta keep up da pressure.

==Notes==
In order to summon the three champions, one must use the challenge totems purchasable from [[Z'tenga the Walker]].
*[[Minor Blackfang Challenge Totem]] for 5 [[Blackfang Claw]] summons [[Akrrilo]]
*[[Major Blackfang Challenge Totem]] for 10 [[Blackfang Claw]] summons [[Rendarr]]
*[[Prime Blackfang Challenge Totem]] for 25 [[Blackfang Claw]] summons [[Eyepiercer]]

The [[Fang'rila]] arena is located at {{coords|54|80|Tanaan Jungle}}.
