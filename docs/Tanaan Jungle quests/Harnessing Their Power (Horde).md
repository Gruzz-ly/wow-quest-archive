Collect a Wargronn Harness from an [[Iron Wargronn (Tanaan Jungle)|Iron Wargronn]] at the [[Iron Front]] in [[Tanaan Jungle]].

Travel to the Iron Front and kill one of them big beasties, then bring back its war harness. You might be needin' help.
