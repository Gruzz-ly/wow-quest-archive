==Description==
The Shadow Council are summoning their [[demon]]ic masters upon a mountain they are calling the Throne of Kil'jaeden. Make no mistake; This is the most dangerous region in Tanaan. Gather together a war party and assault the Throne of Kil'jaeden before the [[Burning Legion|Legion]] gains a foothold here!
