== Objectives ==
Find the Eye of Kilrogg and perform the scrying ritual.
* [[Eye of a Bleeding Hollow Chieftain]] (5)
* [[Eye of Kilrogg (item)]]
* Complete the Eye Ritual

== Description ==
If you wish to know what [[Kilrogg Deadeye (alternate universe)|Kilrogg]] and [[Gul'dan (alternate universe)|his new master]] intend, you will need his eye. After the ritual all chieftains place their eye under the protection of the ghosts of the [[Bleeding Hollow clan (alternate universe)|Bleeding Hollow]] in the next chamber. Go and locate the Eye of Kilrogg in the vault. The other chieftains will not stand with us, and will fight you for it. Then place it on the ritual mark nearby to get your answers.
