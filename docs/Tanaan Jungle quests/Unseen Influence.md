==Objectives==
Collect 10 Fel-Corrupted Apexis Fragments from any Treasure or from any Rare or Rare Elite creature in Tanaan Jungle.
*[[Fel-Corrupted Apexis Fragment]] (10)

==Description==
The corruption in the Apexis has begun to affect many of my people. We are so accustomed to the power it grants us that more succumb to the whispers every day. You must bring me more samples so I can free my people from yet another curse.
