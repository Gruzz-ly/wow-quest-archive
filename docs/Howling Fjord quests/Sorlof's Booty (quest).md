This quest is part of the [[Fury of the Kvaldir quest chain]].

== Objectives ==
Slay [[Sorlof]] using the Big Gun and recover Sorlof's Booty. Return Sorlof's Booty to [[Captain Ellis]] aboard the [[Sister Mercy]].
* [[Sorlof's Booty]]

== Description ==
Me crew is gonna get [[The Big Gun|the big'un]] all primed an'ready for ya, matey. She's restin' snuggly on the bow, o'er on the other side of the ship. Go'n man it and be ready to fire when Sorlof is in our sites!

Once we take that landlubber down, ye'll have to recover the booty for us, seein' as how we can't leave the damned ship! Bring that booty back to me and ye'll get your cut - the shield...

== Progress ==
Smartly, me hearty!

== Completion ==
A deal's a deal, me hearty!

== Notes ==
Run to the bow of the ship and spam-right-click on [[The Big Gun]]. As soon as Sorlof dies, jump off the ship, collect the loot from the ground and swim back to the isle to wait for the ship to finish its circuit.
