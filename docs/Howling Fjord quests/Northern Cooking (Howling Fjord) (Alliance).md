==Objectives==
Bring four chilled meat to Brom Brewbaster at Valgarde. Chilled meat can be found on any [[Northrend]] beast. 
*[[Chilled Meat]]: (4)

==Description==
So what are ya doin' if you dinna have any stew for me. I got me a HUNGER! At least bring me some chilled meat so I kin cook me own stew! If ya do, I kin give ya the recipe so you kin make it for yourself. NOW GO GET ME SOME MEAT! 

==Rewards==
You will learn: [[Northern Stew]]

You will also receive: {{cost|2|10}}

==Progress==
WHERE IS MY MEAT!

==Completion==
Ya gots me meat!   Here is your recipe, I gots cookin to do!

==Notes==
A version of this quest is available from the cooking trainer at any of the four starting quest hubs in Northrend, but it has a pre-requisite of Cooking 350.
