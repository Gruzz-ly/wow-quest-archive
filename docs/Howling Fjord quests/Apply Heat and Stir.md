==Objectives==
Fill the Empty Apothecary's Flask at the [[Cauldron of Vrykul Blood]], then mix the [[Flask of Vrykul Blood]] with [[Harris's Plague Samples]] nearby until you've obtained a Balanced Concoction.

Return to Chief Plaguebringer Harris when you've completed this task.
* [[Balanced Concoction]]
* [[Empty Apothecary's Flask]] (provided)

==Description==
You've done well so far, <name>. I'd like to teach you some of my more advanced techniques, so that you gain a better understanding of the plague.

Take this empty flask and fill it with blood from the cauldron to my left. Mix it with the plague samples to my right until you obtain a well balanced mix.
