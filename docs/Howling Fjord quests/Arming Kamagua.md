==Description==
[[Issliruk]], the spirit of war, is depicted as a silver eagle by my people. Our stories tell of how Issliruk fought against a hundred [[worg]]s to save his young.

It is time for us, too to fight against our foes to save our brethren. We will need to craft new weapons. The horns of the [[Frostwing Chimaera|frostwing chimaera]] that make their home in the snowy parts of the [[The Isle of Spears|island]] are sharp and are easily crafted into spear tips. Bring me the best of these horns that you can find.
