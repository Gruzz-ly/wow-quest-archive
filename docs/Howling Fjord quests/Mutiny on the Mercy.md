This quest is part of the [[Fury of the Kvaldir quest chain]].

== Objectives ==
[[Captain Ellis]] aboard the ''Sister Mercy'' in the [[Howling Fjord]] has asked that you recover 5 Barrels of Blasting Powder from the ship's hold.
* [[Barrel of Blasting Powder]]: (5)

== Description ==
Aye, we've got problems, matey. Me gunners have mutinied! M'n'the crew up top be tryin' to kick [[Sorlof]]'s keester while dealin with the [[Mutinous Sea Dog|mutinous gunners]] down'blo.

Ye're gonna have to go down'blo and deal with those mutinous sea dogs and take their blastin' powder for the big'un o'er on the poop deck.

With ye mannin' the big'un, Sorlof is sure to die and give up the booty!

== Progress ==
Mutinous sea dogs and giants abound!

== Completion ==
Here's to hopin' those treacherous bilge rats learned their lessons! Now it be time to man the big'un, matey!
