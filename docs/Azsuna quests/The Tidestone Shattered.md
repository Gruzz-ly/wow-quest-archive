==Notes==
Immediately after you turn in the quest, a frostbolt freezes you inside a block of ice. [[Athissa]] and [[Warlord Parjesh]] move in.

:{{Text|say|Athissa|How perfectly convenient... the brainless <race> led us right to the Tidestone.}}
:{{Text|say|Athissa|[[Queen Azshara|Our Queen Beneath the Tides]] can reassemble the pieces.}}
:{{Text|say|Athissa|As for you... you will make an acceptable slave.}}

The screen fades to black, and you appear in the [[Hatecoil Slave Pen]], chained to a wall and guarded by two [[Hatecoil Myrmidon]]s.

:{{Text|say|Hatecoil Myrmidon|What should we do with this one?}}
:{{Text|say|Hatecoil Myrmidon|Torture? Hard labor? Kill it?}}
:{{Text|say|Hatecoil Myrmidon|I say we kill it.}}
:{{Text|say|Hatecoil Myrmidon|Good idea, let's kill it.}}

Suddenly, [[Prince Farondis]] runs in and blasts one of the guards with a fireball. The other turns to him.

:{{Text|say|Hatecoil Myrmidon|Huh?}}

Farondis kills the other guard as well, then offers the next quest, [[Save Yourself]].

If you are disconnected during the cutscene, or [[Hearthstone]] away from the Hatecoil Slave Pen before Farondis appears, you can follow the quest marker for [[Save Yourself]] on your map and pick it up from Farondis.
