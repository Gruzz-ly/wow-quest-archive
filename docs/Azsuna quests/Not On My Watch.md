==Objectives==
* [[Eredar]] at [[Soul Engine: Apocalypse]] slain (10)
* [[Dimensional Anchor]]s destroyed (4)

==Description==
: {{text|say|[[Nightwatcher Idri]]|Each [[Burning Legion|Legion]] detachment draws power from a [[soul engine]]. Incapacitate the demons' vile machine and slay the eredar who maintain it!}}

==Rewards==
* +150 reputation with [[Court of Farondis]]

==Completion==
: {{text|say|Nightwatcher Idri|Outstanding! They must make repairs before their soul engine can re-engage. You have bought us valuable time.}}

==Notes==
*As with all soul engines, the Dimensional Anchors are upstairs on the second floor, and flying mounts can be used inside. Focus on fighting to the anchors first, then kill the rest of the eredar that you need.
*Eligible mobs are [[Legion Felsmith]]s and [[Eredar Soulmage]]s.
