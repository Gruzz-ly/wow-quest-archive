==Objectives==
Find [[Lowping]] in the [[sea giant]] caves, shave his head, and make a copy of the schematic that's tattooed there.
* Find Lowping and shave his head
* [[Engineering Schematic]]

==Description==
Me wee rogue friend Lowping were [[gnome]]-napped by them dirty [[naga]] when we got 'ere. He's gone! They drug him right oft toward tha [[Oceanus Cove|cove]]!

But 'ere's tha real issue. He 'ad a schematic on 'im fer one of me greatest inventions.

And by "on 'im," I mean I tattooed it to tha top o' his head. FOR SAFETY!

When ye find 'im, jes shave 'is head and make a quick copy o' tha design.

Oh aye, yeh get me schematic! Here, keep a copy fer yerself, in case yer lookin' for somethin' to tattoo somewheres.

It's a damn shame about Lowping. Yeh know, come ta think of it, maybe we shoulda rescued him. Ah! Multi-tasking were never my thing.
