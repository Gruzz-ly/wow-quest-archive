'''Tip the Scales''' starts upon entering [[Hatecoil Warcamp]] in [[Azsuna]].

==Objectives==
*Disrupt the [[Hatecoil Warcamp]] (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{Text|say|[[Nightwatcher Idri]]|First the [[naga]] assault our city! Now it looks like they're trying to make off with our relics as well. Show them what it means to incur the wrath of the [[Nightwatchers]], champion!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Court of Farondis]]

==Completion==
:{{Text|say|Nightwatcher Idri|That should teach the naga to stay out of Azsuna! The citizens of [[Nar'thalas]] owe you a debt of gratitude, <class>, as do the Nightwatchers.}}

==Notes==
To fill up the bar:
*kill [[Hatecoil Fathom-Stalker]]s, [[Hatecoil Spitespeaker]]s, [[Tide Breaker]]s, and [[Hatecoil Skrog]]s.
*take [[Stolen Nar'thalas Relic]].
*destroy [[Murloc Hatchery|Murloc Hatcheries]], [[Coral Trident]]s, and [[Coral Trident Rack]].
