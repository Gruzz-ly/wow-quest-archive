'''A Favor for Mr. Shackle''' is the optional lead-in quest that starts the '''Mak'rana and the Fate of the Queen's Reprisal''' portion of the achievement [[Azsuna Matata]].

==Objectives==
Free Cleave Thunderhill, Gentle Rosh, and Grace the Green.
* [[Cleave Thunderhill]] rescued
* [[Gentle Rosh]] rescued
* [[Grace the Green]] rescued

==Description==
Throm'ka, <class>.  My name is [[Seska Seafang|Seska]].  I represent a... trading company.  As you can see, I've fallen victim to these giants and their [[naga]] allies.

Three of my associates were also captured while surveying this cove.  I'm certain that my employer, [[Mr. Shackle]], would be most grateful if you could procure their release.
