==Notes==
Triggers at the Ruined Sanctum in [[Azsuna]].

Kill any [[Burning Fiend (Azsuna)|Burning Fiend]]s, [[Felwing Swarmer]]s, [[Filthy Pilferer]]s, [[Legion Brutallizer]]s, [[Dreadflame Champion]]s, and [[Dreadflame Magus|Dreadflame Magi]].

Use your special action button Place Arcane Bomb to destroy the [[Demon Portal (Azsuna)|Demon Portals]]. Using the [[Disabled Sentry Pylon]]s will shoot down a Felwing Swarmer. Using an [[Ancient Spear]] will harpoon one instead and drag it down to you.
