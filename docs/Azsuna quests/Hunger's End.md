'''Hunger's End''' completes the Defending Azurewing Repose portion of the achievement [[Azsuna Matata]].

==Objectives==
Kill Ael'Yith and his construct.
*[[Orbyth]] slain
*[[Ael'Yith]] slain

==Description==
I am weak. The hunger is devastating, my friend. I understand now why your dragon rests in these pools.

<cough, cough>

I came to warn you but... it looks like I was too late.

I hear him outside now. Ael'Yith... is... here...
