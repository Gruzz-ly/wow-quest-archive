'''Those Who Remember''' is an [[Azsuna]] quest. It requires having completed the quest [[Their Dying Breaths]].

==Objectives==
Locate [[Nightwatcher Idri]] in the [[Drowned Gardens]].

==Description==
The ghostly elves, to the south, once had the [[Pillar of Creation]] known as the [[:Tidestone of Golganneth]].

Their great [[Ruins of Nar'thalas|city of Nar'thalas]] was destroyed an eon ago. The city's ruler, [[Prince Farondis]], fell with the city. You small ones die so easily.

If anyone knows where the fragments rest, it will be the ghost of the prince. Seek out his people.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1,480 [[XP]] (or {{cost|1|83|50}} at lvl 110)
* +10 [[reputation]] with [[Court of Farondis]]
