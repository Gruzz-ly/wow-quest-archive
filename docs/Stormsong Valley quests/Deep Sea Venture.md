==Description==
As mighty as [[Ai'twen]] is, this [[Venture Company]] is still a threat to him. I need you to watch over Ai'twen and [[Toki]] as you continue your great adventure.

Eliminate the Venture Company Seawrenches that lurk the waters you travel to.
