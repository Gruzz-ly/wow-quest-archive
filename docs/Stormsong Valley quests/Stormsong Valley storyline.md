# Stormsong Valley storyline

[[File:VZ-Stormsong Valley.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]
The '''Stormsong Valley storyline''' seeks to answer the question of what happened to the Kul Tiran fleet.

Completing the storyline will award the achievement [[Stormsong and Dance]].
