==Objectives==
Lead the [[Horde]] assault into [[Stormsong Valley]].
* [[Alliance]] targets attacked in Stormsong Valley (4)
* Meet up with [[Usha Eyegouge]]
* Siege Engine slain (5)
* [[7th Legion]] Troops slain (15)
* [[Bennel Swiftstrike]] slain

==Description==
The Alliance has begun a coordinated assault against our forces in Stormsong Valley. Attack the enemy forces and drive them back!

*26,800 [[XP]]
*+1,000 [[reputation]] with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]
