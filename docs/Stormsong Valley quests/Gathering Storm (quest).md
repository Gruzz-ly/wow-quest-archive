==Description==
This depravity goes against every oath we take as [[tidesages]]. To protect our people... to guide and sustain them... to make our ships strong.

This is nothing but darkness. It must all be destroyed.

Board the Abyssal Melody and place these focusing rods at any critical points you find. I will prepare the ritual.
