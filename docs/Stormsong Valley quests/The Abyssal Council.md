==Description==
The tidesages have a leading council beneath [[Lord Stormsong]]. They each oversee a different aspect of the order's work.

Wavespeaker Reid trains initiates here in the oldest form of tidesage magic: communing with the sea.

These deaths are on his hands. It's up to us to take him down.
