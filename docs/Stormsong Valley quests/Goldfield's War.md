==Objectives==
Defeat the quilboar invaders.
* Slay invaders (20)
* [[Brug Housesmash]] slain

==Description==
I'm not leaving. I'm never leaving.

Those [[quilboar]], they killed them. I gave her the watch, told her to hurry. Grab our boy and get to the house while I got the gun...

<Goldfield is choking back tears.>

I won't leave. Our family has our roots here. They would want me to stay, don't you understand? This is our farm. My boy, he wanted to join the militia. I made him a sword...

<Goldfield takes a deep breath.>

Now you know. Help me retake my farm. Grab a gun and let's kill every last one.

It's done. My family is avenged.

==Notes==
Pick up one of the [[Spare Rifles]] on the crate next to Goldfield.

Players will have access to two abilities:
#{{Abilities|Shoot|range=60|cd=0.5 sec|Shoot a quilboar with Farmer Goldfield's rifle, dealing 67,457 Physical damage|notes=Instant.|id=260129|icon=inv_weapon_rifle_01}}
#{{Abilities|Quillbomb|range=60|cd=8 sec|Throws an explosive filled with quilboar quils at a location, inflicting 27,578 Physical damage to all enemies within 10 yards and stunning them for 3 sec.|notes=Instant.|id=260148|icon=inv_misc_bomb_02}}

Use the quillbomb on groups of [[Briarback Raider]]s, [[Briarback Shaper]]s, [[Grasping Thorns]], [[Warboar]]s, and [[Briarback Crusher]]s and finish them off with Azerite shots. Once 20 units are dead, Brug Housesmash will spawn. He just as quickly goes down in a few shots.
