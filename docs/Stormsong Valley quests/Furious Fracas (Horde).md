* 26,800 [[XP]]
*[[Honorbound Commendation]] (150 reputation with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]])
* +75 reputation with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

At the [[Drowned Lands]], kill [[7th Legion Infantryman (Faction Assaults)|7th Legion Infantrymen]], [[SI:7 Assassin (Faction Assaults)|SI:7 Assassins]], and [[7th Legion Vicar]]s.

[[Unstable Azerite Flare]]s can be interacted with to gain a buff. The nearby [[Honorbound Grunt]]s, [[Honorbound Spiritcaller]]s, and [[Deathstalker Assassin]]s can be enlisted as guardians for half a minute.
