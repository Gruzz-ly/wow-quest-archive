==Objectives==
Repel the Horde forces assaulting Stormsong Valley.
* [[Horde]] attacks repelled in [[Stormsong Valley]] (4)
* Ride [[Storm's Feather]]

* [[Stormsong Prisoner]]s freed

Horde forces are pushing deeper into Stormsong Valley. We can not allow this aggression to go unchecked!
