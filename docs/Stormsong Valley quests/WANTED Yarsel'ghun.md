==Description==
By the decree of the [[Storm's Wake]].

Find and kill the foreign spy, Yarsel'ghun. 

A foreigner agent hiding on our soil, she is responsible for acts of terrorism against the citizens of [[Stormsong Valley|Stormsong]].

The individual that ends her existence will earn a fitting reward.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|70|20|}}
* [[Small Azerite Shard]] (115x [[Azerite (resource)]])
* 24,680 [[XP]]
* +250 reputation with [[Storm's Wake]]

==Progress==
The wanted poster is nailed to the wall, with tasks etched on it.

==Completion==
One less Horde in Stormsong.

==Notes==
While in [[Eckhart Lodge]] for [[Smells Like Trouble]], [[Dangerous Game]] and [[Never Outgunned]], the wanted poster offers this optional side quest.

She's on top of Stormwatch Peak, near the fifth [[Ancient Tidesage Scroll]].

Three [[Zandalari Bloodletter]]s are in stealth nearby and will aggro with her.
