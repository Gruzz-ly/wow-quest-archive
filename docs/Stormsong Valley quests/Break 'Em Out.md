==Objectives==
Free 10 [[Deadwash Worker|workers]].
* [[Ball and Chain (object)|Chain]] Broken (10)

==Description==
An [[Irontide Raiders|Irontide]] captain "[[Captain Calico McGee|Calico McGee]]" has been trying to scare us off for days now, but it's not working.

He's ordered his men to lock everyone up and sell us as slaves! They've brought enough chains for everyone!

Break the chains and set our workers free.

==Rewards==

* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]

==Progress==
You need to break the chains!

==Completion==
With everyone free, we can get back to finding the treasure!

==Notes==
Pick up [[Iron Low Tide]] and [[Two-Faced Pirate Scum]] before heading out.

Pirates are all over. The ettin is just to the northwest of town, and the townsfolk are in the lowlands off to the east and south.
