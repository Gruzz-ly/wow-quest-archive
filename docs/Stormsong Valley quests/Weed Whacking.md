'''Weed Whacking''' is a bonus objective offered while on the [[Burton Farmstead]] during [[Gadgets and Gizmos Aplenty]] and [[Stop Vining!]] on the [[Stormsong Valley storyline]].

The quest can be completed very quickly while on [[Farming Stimulator]].

==Objectives==
*Farmlands cleared (0/100%)

* [[Grasping Thorns]] slain (5%)
* [[Razorspine Thornling]] slain  (5%)
* [[Briarback Skirmisher]] slain (4%)
* [[Vine-Wrapped Farmer]] freed (5%)
