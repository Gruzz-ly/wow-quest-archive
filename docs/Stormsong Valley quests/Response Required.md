==Objectives==

* [[Lithia Costcutter]] slain {{co|36.7|29.5|Stormsong Valley}}

==Description==
Time is short and more creatures will die without your help. The [[Venture Company]] prepares a huge digging machine to tear the reef apart and destroy this cave. They've assigned a special operative to be sure the job is done right.

You must act now <name>, take out the operative in charge of the camp, this should buy us some time.

* {{cost|46|80|}}
* 20,560 [[XP]]

* +150 reputation with [[Tortollan Seekers]]

==Progress==
You must act fast, our time is short.

==Completion==
You've bought us valuable time with your heroic actions.

==Notes==
Pick up [[Flavorable Offering]] before heading out.

Lithia's on the beach just north of [[Loroja]]'s position.

While gathering Seaweed, be on the lookout for a [[Bot Buster Bomb]] in the water, which starts [[Explosive Situation]].

On the surface at the northeast end of the quest area is [[Slickspill]], a rare oil elemental that drops the [[Living Oil Canister]].
