==Description==
Thundersnout is the largest of the [[boar]]s in the valley. She is wreaking havoc on the nearby farms and their crops.

Mayor Roz of Brennadam has issued a bounty for proof of her demise.

Thundersnout's location is near [[Briarback Kraul|Briarback kraul]], so be on the look out for [[quilboar]]!
