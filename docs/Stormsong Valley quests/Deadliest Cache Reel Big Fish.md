Can ye believe the nerve of these scallywags? Questioning the very Coxswain of this here wharf?

Bah!

Lend me your ear for a moment or three. I've got a tale that'll put brew in your mug and a quiver in your boots!

It was the biggest, deadliest cache I ever did loot on these seas...
