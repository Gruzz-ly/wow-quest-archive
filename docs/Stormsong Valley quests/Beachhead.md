'''Beachhead''' is a world quest offered by [[Scrollsage Nola]] at each of the six zones of [[Kul Tiras]] and [[Zandalar]].

==Objectives==
*Speak to [[Scrollsage Nola]]
*Help the [[Juvenile Turtle]]s Reach the Ocean (12)

==Description==
:{{text|say|Scrollsage Nola|A tortollan begins its life amidst great danger, like these turtles here on the beach. Defend them as they make for the safety of the ocean!}}

==Rewards==
Depending on zone and player faction, you will receive:
*+175 [[reputation]] with [[Tortollan Seekers]]
*{{Alliance}} +75 reputation with [[Order of Embers]], [[Proudmoore Admiralty]], [[Storm's Wake]], or [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
*{{Horde}} +75 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]], [[Voldunai]], [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]], or [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*An [[Azerite (resource)]] item
*[[War Resources]]
*[[Tortollan Trader's Stock]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Scrollsage Nola|Thank you, <name>.}}

==Notes==
Speak with Nola to enter a [[Nest|vehicle]] with one ability.
{{Dialogue|
Are you ready to defend the young turtles as they make their way to sea?

{{Gossip|I'm ready.}}
}}

Use that ability to target the [[Armored Claw|crabs]] and [[Vicious Albatross|albatrosses]] that spawn and pick them off before they kill turtles on their way to the water.

When the first turtle makes it to the water, and every 60 seconds thereafter while in the vehicle:
:{{text|say|Scrollsage Nola|[[A turtle made it to the water]]!}}
