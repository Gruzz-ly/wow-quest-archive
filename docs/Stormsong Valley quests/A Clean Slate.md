While in [[Sagehold]] for [[The Storm Awakens]] and [[Twisted Intentions]] during the [[Stormsong Valley storyline]], this bonus objective appears.

==Objectives==
*Purge the corruption (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|46|80|}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Storm's Wake]]

==Notes==
*Location: [[Sagehold]], [[Stormsong Valley]].
*To fill up the bar,
**kill [[Bolstered Tideguard]]s, [[Drowned Acendant]]s, [[Veiled Cutthroat]]s, [[Drowned Hierarch]]s, [[Drowned Harbinger]]s, and [[Deep Terror (Stormsong Valley)|Deep Terrors]] (3%), [[Umbral Hulk]]s (11%),
**interact with [[Wrapping Your Tentacles Around Tidal Magic]], [[Lith Najz Wshhhhh: A Primer]], [[The Wizard of Azshara]], [[Tidemother's Pride]], [[Suction Cups & You: Writhing in Motion]], [[The Old God and the Sea]], [[Making the Best of a Sucky Situation]], [[Tidemother's Radiance]], [[Legends of the Forbidden Tide]], [[Underwater Basket Weaving: A Treatise]], [[Tidemother's Wrath]] (3%) and the [[Lord Stormsong (statue)|Lord Stormsong]] statues (5%).
