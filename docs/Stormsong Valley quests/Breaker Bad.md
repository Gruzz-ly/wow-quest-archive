==Objectives==
Destroy the Reef Breaker.
* [[Reef Breaker]] Destroyed {{co|35.4|27.7|Stormsong Valley}}

==Description==
The combined fire power of all the bombs you have collected should be able to destroy the [[Venture Company|Venture Companies]] Reef Breaker.

It's time to act and protect the reef and this sacred cave.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 16,450 [[XP]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Storm's Wake]]
* +75 reputation with [[Tortollan Seekers]]

==Progress==
Time to destroy the Reef Breaker.

==Completion==
You have done us a great service.

==Notes==
Pick up [[Deep Sea Venture]] and [[What's Yours is Mined]] before heading out.

While on this quest, players have access to a bonus ability:
*{{abilities|Bot Cluster Bomb|range=50|cd=10 sec|Specially designed bomb for destroying the Reef Breaker. Inflicts fire damage and stuns enemies nearby.|notes=1.8 sec cast|id=271011|icon=inv_misc_blackironbomb}}

The Reef Breaker is on the surface on the north side of the island. Get close and use the bonus ability, then target the ground under the mech to destroy it.
