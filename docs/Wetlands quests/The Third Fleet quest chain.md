# The Third Fleet quest chain

Haunted by the [[ghost]]s of the fallen [[Lost Fleet|Third Fleet]] [[First Mate Fitzsimmons]] sends [[Alliance]] [[adventurer]]s to end their suffering. During this, the source for their state of undeath is discovered when players find the [[Cursed Eye of Paleth]]. Seeking to put an end to the curse players work to get item cleansed as the [[Eye of Paleth]].

== The Third Fleet ==

Help some of the undead sailors in Wetlands find piece, and learn the cause.

* '''[[The Third Fleet]]'''

Buy First Mate Fitzsimmons a drink.

* '''[[Cursed to Roam]]'''
Visit First Mate Snellig.

* '''[[The Cursed Crew]]'''

First Mate Fitzsimmons wants you to kills some undead sailors.

* '''[[Lifting the Curse]]'''

Loot the key, open the strongbox.

* '''[[The Eye of Paleth]]'''

Bring the Eye back to Glorin Steelbrow.
