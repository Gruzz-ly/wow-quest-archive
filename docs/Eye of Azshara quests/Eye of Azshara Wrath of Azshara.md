'''Eye of Azshara: Wrath of Azshara''' is the dungeon quest to retrieve the [[Tidestone of Golganneth]], one of the five [[Pillars of Creation]].

==Objectives==
Enter the [[Eye of Azshara (instance)|Eye of Azshara]], then defeat Warlord Parjesh and the Wrath of Azshara.  The Eye of Azshara is a dungeon so be certain to gather some friends to undertake this perilous journey.
*Eye of Azshara entered 
*[[Warlord Parjesh]] slain 
*[[Wrath of Azshara]] slain 

==Description==
<Name>, Warlord Parjesh ran into the magical vortex just ahead.  He has returned to an [[Eye of Azshara (instance)|island off the shore to the southeast]] where [[Queen Azshara]] is staging more of her forces.

The queen is beyond angry because we opposed her.  Even now, she is summoning up a monstrosity known as her Wrath to exact revenge for our defiance and drown the [[Broken Isles]].

You must defeat the Wrath of Azshara before the queen lets it loose to destroy us all!
