'''The Box of Many Things''' is offered by the broker [[Ve'nish]], found somewhere inside Torghast. Finding Ve'nish and completing this quest is the prerequisite to unlock the [[The Box of Many Things]] mechanic, which is a form of meta-progression in Torghast added in [[patch 9.1]].

Ve'nish can be found on floor two of any layer, so if players are specifically looking for this quest, knock out a layer one run.

==Objectives==
Bring the Box of Many Things to the [[Runecarver]].
*[[The Box of Many Things (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
Ah, [[Maw Walker]], of course. Soon I shall be undone, claimed by this place forever, but you continue to walk its halls unbroken by its inevitability. 

It was my great aspiration that the box would prevail. Sadly I overestimated my own capabilities.

I trust I am not overestimating yours. Take the box. It will grant you power as you prove your power. I have labored long in its making, prove yourself worthy of it.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|25|74|}}
* 8,150 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Ah, you return.

==Completion==
Ah... a curious device. Anchoring it in this place will prove useful.

==Notes==
Before leaving, Ve'nish has some gossip:
:In my glory, I made the box so that it might grant me the power to travel these halls with impunity.
:In my hubris, I made it so the wonders within could only be accessed by one who proves their worth within the walls of this place. 
:It never occurred to me that I might not be worthy...

Finish the run (or not), then head to the [[Runecarver's Oubliette]].
