'''Chip Service''' is one of two quests available at Renown 11 with the [[Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club]]. This quest rewards a [[Counterfeit Dealer's Chip]], tradeable for cosmetics inside the raid. The other quest is [[Time is Money!!]], increasing the out-of-combat movement speed to 20% (from 10%).

==Completion==
Compliments of the house! Take this to the [[Sando the Rat|nice Hobgoblin just across the way]] and pick yourself up somethin' nice.
