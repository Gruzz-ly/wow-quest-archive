'''Need a Jump?''' is one of two quests at Renown 6 with the [[Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club]]. This quest grants players the ability to out-of-combat self-resurrect every 30 minutes. The other quest is [[Time is Money!]], increasing out-of-combat movement speed by 10%.

==Completion==
It's gruesome work out there on the floor, so your friends outside sent you a pick-me-up! Literally.
