'''Time is Money!''' is one of two quests at Renown 6 with the [[Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club]]. This quest increases out-of-combat movement speed by 10%. The other is [[Need a Jump?]], which lets players resurrect out of combat every 30 minutes.

==Completion==
Chase that paper!
