'''Time is Money!!''' is one of two quests at Renown 11 with the [[Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club]]. This quest increases the out of combat movement speed to 20% (from 10%). The other quest is [[Chip Service]], awarding a [[Counterfeit Dealer's Chip]], tradeable for cosmetics.
