'''Renowned with the Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club''' is the paragon quest for the [[Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club]], offered each time players fill out the paragon reputation with the faction.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Paula Piranha]] inside the [[Liberation of Undermine]].

==Description==
The Gallagio appreciates your continued loyalty!

We've got some extra supplies for only our most honored of High Rollers. Come see us for your gift!
