== Description ==
You may be an outsider, but you and I have a common enemy in the [[hozen]].

Many members of [[Pearlfin tribe|my tribe]] are trapped in cages nearby. I have also seen your... [[Alliance]]... soldiers as well.

I canot leave without freeing my brethren, just as you cannot leave without yours.

The [[Slingtail tribe|Slingtail]] hold the keys to those cages. Find the keys and free our friends.
