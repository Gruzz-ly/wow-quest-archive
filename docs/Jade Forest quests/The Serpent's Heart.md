==Description==
You've done great here, kid. Thanks to you, we have quite a bit of extra jade, and I know just the someone who could use it.

My friend Foreman Raike is running the great statue project at the Serpent's Heart.

Bring a shipment of extra jade to him, and tell him his old friend Mann says hello. He'll be eagerly awaiting your arrival.
