== Objectives ==
Use a [[Gunship Turret]] to kill 80 Thunder Hold troops and destroy 9 Thunder Hold cannons.
* Thunder Hold troops slain (80)
* Thunder Hold cannons destroyed (9)

== Description ==
They shot out our engines and we floated here - HERE! Right in the firing line of the enemy!

You know what to do, <name>. Get below decks and man those guns!

Show these scum that they're in OUR line of fire!
