==Objectives==
Speak to [[Instructor Tong]] at [[Windward Isle]] in the [[Jade Forest]].

==Description==
I have examined each egg, <name>. Out of the lot that you brought me, I have chosen the three eggs with the most promise.

You may choose only one egg to take with you. Are you ready to make your final selection?

==Rewards==
{{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|Blue Serpent Egg|Green Serpent Egg|Yellow Serpent Egg}}
You will also receive:
*{{cost|9|92|50}}
*118000 [[XP]]
*+500 [[reputation]] with [[Order of the Cloud Serpent]]

==Completion==
You may choose only one egg to take with you.

Choose carefully. There is no going back.

==Notes==
The egg chosen as a reward for this quest determines which color of cloud serpent the character will receive later when completing [[Riding the Skies]]:
* [[Blue Serpent Egg]] leads to [[Reins of the Azure Cloud Serpent]]
* [[Green Serpent Egg]] leads to [[Reins of the Jade Cloud Serpent]]
* [[Yellow Serpent Egg]] leads to [[Reins of the Golden Cloud Serpent]]
