==Objectives==
[[File:Syra Goldendraft.jpg|thumb|[[Syra Goldendraft]].]]
Search the [[Shrine of the Dawn]] for Syra Goldendraft.
*Speak with [[Syra Goldendraft]] at the Shrine of the Dawn in [[Jade Forest]]

==Description==
The "children" you seek are not actually children but grown pandaren, desipte what their elders might claim. Their love will not be held in check by the feuds of their families.

You will find both Syra and Lo at the Shrine of the Dawn, ever seeking an answer to their dilemma amongst the pandriarchs of old.

*110000 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Oh stranger! Please tell me you can help us. They really hurt my Lo.

==Notes==
Pick up [[The Perfect Color]] and [[Quill of Stingers]] before heading out. Head north out of the Arboretum once those two quests are completed to reach the [[Shrine of the Dawn]]. Speak with Syra.
