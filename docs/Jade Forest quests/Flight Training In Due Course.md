==Objectives==
Fly through 10 Race Flags at the Checkpoints floating above the [[Mistveil Sea]].
*Checkpoints passed (10)

==Description==
Now that you have learned the basics of the race, you should go learn the course.

Explore the spires and collect a race flag from each of the checkpoints.

Fly through each checkered flag to collect it.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|19|84|50}}
*236000 [[XP]]
*+1200 [[reputation]] with [[Order of the Cloud Serpent]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
Well done. If you hadn't been capable of finding the checkpoints, I fear you would never have done well in the Sky Race.
