==Objectives==
Deliver the Scavenged Jade to {{NPC||Lorewalker Cho}} at [[Emperor's Omen]]. Speak with {{NPC||Toya}} in [[Dawn's Blossom]] for a ride.
*[[Scavenged Jade]] (provided)

==Description==

Cho must be on to something big!

Lemme know when you're ready to go see him - I got a ride all prepared for ya.

==Notes==
Before taking off, should adventurers desire to continue the lovebird quest fork, pick up and complete [[Find the Boy]] from {{NPC||Kai Wanderbrew}}.

Otherwise, speak with Toya for a free ride to [[Emperor's Omen]] past the [[Greenstone Village]] and the [[Greenstone Mason's Quarter]]:
:{{gossip|I'm ready to see Lorewalker Cho.}}

Upon reaching Cho's location:
:{{text|say|[[Foreman Mann]]|I'd love to help you, Lorewalker, but understand - I am in a real bind here.}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|Ah, but look who's arrived! Mrs. Mann, there's someone I'd like you to meet.}}

On complete:
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|This ancient fresco was one inlaid with jade, but it has fallen into ruin and now its wisdom is lost to us.}}
:{{text|say|Lorewalker Cho|With the sudden arrival of strangers on [[Pandaria]], I believe we'll need the lessons our history can teach us.}}
:{{text|say|Foreman Mann|I understand, Lorewalker. But the [[Serpent's Heart]] project is behind schedule and we've not a stone to spare!}}

Speak with Foreman Mann to continue.
