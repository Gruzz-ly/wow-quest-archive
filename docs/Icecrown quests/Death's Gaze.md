==Objectives==
Use the Death's Gaze Orb to place orbs on the [[Abomination]] Lab Orb Stand, the [[Flesh Giant]] Lab Orb Stand, and the [[Plague cauldron|Cauldron]] Area Orb Stand, then return to [[Darkrider Arly]] at [[Blackwatch]].
* Orb placed in abomination lab
* Orb placed in flesh giant lab
* Orb placed in cauldron area
* [[Death's Gaze Orb]] (provided)
