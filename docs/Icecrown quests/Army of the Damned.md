'''Army of the Damned''' is a quest that puts the player in [[Arthas]]'s place, requiring them to slaughter a regiment of Arthas's own soldiers.

==Objectives==
[[Matthias Lehner]] at the [[First Legion Forward Camp]] wants you to raise an army of 100 Ghoulish Minions.
* Ghoulish Minion Raised (100)

The prince's true power wasn't his ability to make armies out of corpses... that was easy.

But killing his own men, with whom he'd shared many battles... the prince's true power was ability to do what had to be done.

==Notes==
Accepting the quest will send players a few years back in time as they see through [[The Prodigal Leader]]'s eyes.
