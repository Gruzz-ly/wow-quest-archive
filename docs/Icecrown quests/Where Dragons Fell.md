== Objectives ==
[[Matthias Lehner]] at [[Sindragosa's Fall]] wants you to obtain 6 [[Whelp Bone Dust]]s.
* [[Whelp Bone Dust]] (6)

== Description ==
A whole brood of [[blue dragon]]s fell here. And now, one by one, they're being raised by [[Cult of the Damned|his servants]].

[[Arthas Menethil|He]] raised [[Sindragosa|the biggest of them all]] himself. Bring me some bone dust from the whelps and I'll show you exactly how he did it. 

== Progress ==
Did you get the dust, <name>?

== Completion ==
Even the mighty wyrms had to heed his call. How is a simple [[mortal]] like you going to resist him?

== Notes ==
[[File:Raising Sindragosa concept.jpg|thumb|Concept art of raising Sindragosa]]
Complete the quest [[Futility]] in order to see Matthias Lehner at Sindragosa's Fall and accept this quest.

Completing the quest shows how the [[Lich King]] reanimated Sindragosa from the glacier.
