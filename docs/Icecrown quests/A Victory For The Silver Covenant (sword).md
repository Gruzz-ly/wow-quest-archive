This quest is part of the {{questchain|The Return of Quel'Delar|Return of Quel'Delar}} quest chain.

==Objectives==
Bring the Restored Quel'Delar to {{NPC||Vereesa Windrunner}} at the [[Violet Citadel]] in [[Dalaran]].
*[[Restored Quel'Delar]] (provided)

==Description==
In spite of {{NPC||Blood-Queen Lana'thel||Queen Lana'thel's}} boasting, you have restored [[Quel'Delar]], <name>. Long have we dreamt of reclaiming Quel'Delar.

The [[Silver Covenant]], and indeed all [[quel'dorei]] owe you a debt of gratitude.

Vereesa Windrunner, the leader of the Silver Covenant, asked to see you after you returned from the [[Sunwell]]. Take Quel'Delar with you and go to Dalaran's Violet Citadel where she waits, <name>.

==Progress==

==Completion==
This is nothing short of an amazing accomplishment, <name>. The day that [[Thalorien Dawnseeker|Thalorien]] and [[Anasterian Sunstrider|Anasterian]] fell, the same day that Quel'Delar was lost, was one of the darkest of my life. To see Quel'Delar restored... it is beyond words.

Without a doubt, you are meant to be the true bearer of the blade. Quel'Delar's magic is already adapting the blade to your skills and your strengths.
