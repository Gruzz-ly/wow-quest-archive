==Objectives==
Request the aid of [[Alexstrasza the Life-Binder]] at [[Wyrmrest Temple]]. 

==Description==
The dragon-queen, Alexstrasza possess magics over all life. In the past, she has used great fire used to incinerate undead and rebirth fresh new life in their place. Perhaps her powers might be able to save our [[Crusader Bridenbrad|Bridenbrad]] as well.

Alexstrasza makes her home at Wyrmrest Temple in [[Dragonblight]] - please go to her and humbly request her aid on my behalf.
