This quest takes the character to the new phase of Crusaders' Pinnacle ([[Justice Keep]]) for the first time.

==Objectives==
Seek out [[Highlord Tirion Fordring]] at [[Crusaders' Pinnacle]] in [[Icecrown]]. 

==Description==
Stand proud in knowing that it was you who helped establish the [[Argent Crusade]] inside the walls of Icecrown, <name>. The Crusaders' Pinnacle now stands in direct defiance of the [[Lich King]] and his twisted minions.

Highlord Fordring and the crusaders who survived await your arrival at Crusaders' Pinnacle. Travel once more through the [[Breach (Icecrown)|Breach]] and seek out the highlord! 

==Rewards==
{{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|The Argent Skullcap|Tirion's Headwrap|Crusader's Coif|The Argent Crown|The Crusader's Resolution}}

==Completion==
A long and arduous journey awaits us as we make our way to the gates of [[Icecrown Citadel]]. I, and the soldiers of the Argent Crusade, wish to thank you for all that you have done. We rest easier knowing that you will fight along side us in this cursed place.

<Highlord Tirion Fordring salutes.>

==Notes==
The Crusaders' Pinnacle is now a fully-fledged quest hub with an inn and flight point!

The quest [[A Tale of Valor]] is made available by completing the previous quest [[The Battle For Crusaders' Pinnacle]].  Completing this quest is not required.
