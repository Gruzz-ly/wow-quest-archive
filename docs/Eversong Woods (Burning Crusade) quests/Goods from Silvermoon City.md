'''Goods from Silvermoon City''' is part of the [[Blood Elf]] only quest chain that introduces players to using the [[flight paths]].

==Objectives==
Deliver Sathiel's Request to [[Skymaster Brightdawn]] in [[Fairbreeze Village]].
* [[Sathiel's Request]] (Provided)

==Description==
Welcome volunteer! We're running low on supplies here, but the blood elves have promised us materiel assistance. That's where you come in.

Take this bill of lading over to Skymaster Brightdawn. He'll have further instructions for you on how best to get up to [[Silvermoon City]].

==Rewards==
*160 [[XP]]
*25 [[reputation]] with [[Silvermoon City (faction)|Silvermoon City]]
