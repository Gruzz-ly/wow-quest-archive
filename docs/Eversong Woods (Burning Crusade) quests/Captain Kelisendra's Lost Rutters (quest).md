'''Captain Kelisendra's Lost Rutters''' starts when a player activates the item of the same name, which can [[drop]] from all [[murloc]]s at [[Golden Strand]] and [[Tranquil Shore]].

==Objectives==
Return Captain Kelisendra's Lost Rutters to [[Captain Kelisendra]] at the defended camp just to the east of the [[Sunsail Anchorage]] in [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]].
*[[Captain Kelisendra's Lost Rutters]] (provided)

==Description==
Interesting... what you have found here is a water-proofed, leather tube containing a number of sheets of parchment. By the markings upon them, the pages are clearly navigation rutters, and each bears the signature of one Captain Kelisendra.

You've heard of a small, defended camp just east of the nearby Sunsail Anchorage. Maybe the captain is there?
