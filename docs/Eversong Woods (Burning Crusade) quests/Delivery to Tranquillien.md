'''Delivery to Tranquillien''' is the final part of the 'Missing in the Ghostlands' quest chain, which introduces the young (blood elf) hero to the Ghostlands after completing the [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]] quests.

==Objectives==
Bring the Letter from Silvermoon to [[Arcanist Vandril]] at [[Tranquillien]] in the [[Ghostlands]]. 
*[[Letter from Silvermoon]] (provided)

==Description==
I... I'm too weak to continue. I carry a dire warning from [[Lor'themar Theron|Regent Lord Theron]], in the form of a letter. You'll have to deliver it for me.

Take it to Arcanist Vandril in Tranquillien. You'll find the town further to the south along this road. Go there and... find Vandril...
