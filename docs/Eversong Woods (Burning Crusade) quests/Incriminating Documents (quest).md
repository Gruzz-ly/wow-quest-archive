'''Incriminating Documents''' starts when the player activates the item of the same name which [[drop]]s from [[Darnassian Scout]]s around the [[West Sanctum]].

==Objectives==
Bring the Incriminating Documents to [[Aeldon Sunbrand]] at [[Falconwing Square]] in [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]].
* [[Incriminating Documents]] (provided)

==Description==
The documents contain detailed maps of different strategic buildings in Eversong Woods. A diagram of an [[arcane sanctum]] is also sketched in good detail. There seem to be numerous notes in a language you don't recognize.

As these were carried by a [[Sentinel spies|Darnassian spy]], it might be a good idea to bring them to the Captain of the Guard - Aeldon Sunbrand.
