==Objectives==
[[Ven'jashi]], the [[troll]] prisoner at Tor'Watha, wants you to bring him Chieftain Zul'Marosh's Head. [[Chieftain Zul'Marosh]]{{co|62|79|Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)}} can be found in [[Zeb'Watha]], across [[Lake Elrendar]].

I cannot die in peace yet, mon. Not 'til Zul'Marosh be dead too. You find him 'cross the lake in Zeb'Watha. I hold this poison off 'til you bring me his big ugly head, mon. Only then I die peacefully.
