==Objectives==
Bring [[Thaelis the Hungerer|Thaelis's]] Head to [[Sergeant Kan'ren]] at [[Falconwing Square]] in [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]].
*[[Thaelis' Head]]

==Description==
The city of [[Silvermoon]] hereby issues a reward for the head of Thaelis the Hungerer, notorious [[Wretched]] ringleader. He is wanted for repeated crimes against the general populace of [[Silvermoon City]], including the murder of two city guards.

Report to Sergeant Kan'ren to collect the reward.
