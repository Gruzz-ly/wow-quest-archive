==Objectives==
Report to [[Lieutenant Dawnrunner]] at the [[Farstrider Retreat]] in [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]].

==Description==
Your help in subduing the [[Wretched]] has been invaluable. The Farstriders would surely have use for someone like you in their fight against the [[Amani Empire|Amani]] [[troll]]s.

Look for the Farstrider Enclave to the northeast of here, speak to Lieutenant Dawnrunner and see what assistance she might need.
