'''Old Whitebark's Pendant''' is a quest started by [[Old Whitebark's Pendant (starter)|Old Withebark's Pendant]], which drops from [[Old Whitebark]], a named treant in [[The Scorched Grove]].

The goal of the quest is to learn more about the pendant by finding its maker.

==Objectives==
Find the person who might have made Old Withebark's Pendant. They might provide some insight on the item.
*[[Old Whitebark's Pendant (starter)]] (provided)

==Description==
The pendant has the initials L.R. etched on to it. Perhaps these letters will lead to the pendant's maker.
