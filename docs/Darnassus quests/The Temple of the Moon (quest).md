==Objectives==
Speak to [[Tyrande Whisperwind]] at the [[Temple of the Moon]] in [[Darnassus]].

==Description==
I have an urgent message for you from Darnassus. The message is from the High Priestess of [[Elune]], Tyrande Whisperwind, herself! The messenger did not say what the matter concerned, but that you should travel to the Temple of the Moon in Darnassus and meet with the high priestess at once.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Darnassus (faction)|Darnassus]]

==Completion==
