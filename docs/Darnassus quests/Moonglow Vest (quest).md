'''Moonglow Vest''' becomes available after reaching a [[Classic Leatherworking]] skill of 70.

==Objectives==
Bring 1 Embossed Leather Gloves, 5 Embossed Leather Boots and 5 Embossed Leather Cloaks to [[Lotherias]] in [[Darnassus]].
*[[Embossed Leather Gloves]]
*[[Embossed Leather Boots]] (5)
*[[Embossed Leather Cloak]] (5)

==Description==
We in Darnassus, especially we craftsman, are quite busy. Young adventurers are eager to test themselves in the wilderness, and are in constant need of supplies.

If you can help me fill my orders of leather goods, then perhaps I can teach you an advanced leatherworking technique.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*210 [[XP]]
*[[Pattern: Moonglow Vest]]

==Progress==
Greetings, <name>.

==Completion==
Yes, these are good pieces. You have a skilled hand and an eye for detail. Your future as a leatherworker is promising.

Here, <name>. Let me show you the pattern to make the [[:Moonglow Vest]].
