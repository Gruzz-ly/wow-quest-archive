'''Technoscrying: The Mysteries of Dragonskull Island''' is an [[Azerothian Archives]] world quest that is unlocked upon completion of [[The Hidden Hand]]. It takes place at {{co|77|39|Forbidden Reach}} at [[Dragonskull Island]] in the [[Forbidden Reach]].

==Objectives==
*Recover the Items (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Zenata]]|There are more secrets. We must uncover them, yes? We should start scrying!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,500 [[XP]]
* {{cost|600}}
* +300 [[reputation]] with [[Azerothian Archives]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Zenata|Curious. Do you think scalecommanders consumed this to enhance their performance... or provoke a vision?}}
:{{text|say|Zenata|You have wrested another secret from the grip of the past. Well done, <name>!}}

==Notes==
Use the [[Technoscryers]] (Zenata at {{co|77.1|38.4|Forbidden Reach}} has a spare if necessary), then use #1 Scrying Mode to look around for hidden objects. Find objects at:
* [[Blacksmith Tongs]] at {{co|57.8|73.1|Dragonskull Island}}
* [[Corked Vial]] at {{co|80.1|52.6|Dragonskull Island}}
* [[Drakonid Bowl]] at {{co|69.9|56.3|Dragonskull Island}}
