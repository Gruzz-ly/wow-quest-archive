'''Capsize a Crab''' is a bonus objective on the [[Sharpscale Coast]] of the [[Forbidden Reach]] available while players on [[Helping Hand and Claw]] during the [[Forbidden Reach storyline]]. Players are tasked with defeating [[Karkinus the Capsizer]], who destroyed [[Pathfinder Jeb]]'s , [[Pathfinder Tacha]]'s, and [[Cataloger Wulferd]]'s boats on the way to [[Morqut Islet]].
