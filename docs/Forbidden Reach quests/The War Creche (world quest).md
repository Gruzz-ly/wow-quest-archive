'''The War Creche''' is a world quest taking place in the [[War Creche]] and the [[Earth-Warder's Cauldron]] of the [[Old Weyrn Grounds]] in southern [[Forbidden Reach]]. The quest marker appears at {{co|51|61|Forbidden Reach}}, but the enemies to kill are further south in the {{co|52|70|Forbidden Reach}} range.

==Objectives==
*War Creche Subdued (0/100%)
