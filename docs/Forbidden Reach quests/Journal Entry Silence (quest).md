'''Journal Entry: Silence''' is one of the eight quests required for [[While We Were Sleeping]] in the [[Forbidden Reach]]. This entry is found in a random room of the [[Zskera Vaults]].

==Objectives==
Find a Journal Entry.
* [[Journal Entry: Silence]] (provided)
