==Objectives==
Speak with [[Storykeeper Ashekh]].

==Description==
Storykeeper Ashekh is here to scout for enemies of the [[Maruuk Centaur|Maruuk]].

Completing this quest will unlock her Envoy tasks today. You will be able to unlock other factions' Envoy tasks tomorrow.
