'''Sending Stone: Initial Report''' is one of the eight quests required for [[While We Were Sleeping]] in the [[Forbidden Reach]]. This entry is found at {{co|55.4|35.8|Forbidden Reach}} in the lower building of the [[Lost Atheneum]].

==Objectives==
Find a Sending Stone.
* [[Sending Stone: Initial Report]] (provided)

==Description==
<This sending stone still holds ancient words spoken from Adamanthia to Neltharion in an old draconic dialect.>

<Scalecommanders would have a great interest in the contents of this document.>
