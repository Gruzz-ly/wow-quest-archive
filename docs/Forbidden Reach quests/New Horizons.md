==Objectives==
Speak with [[Kraxxus]].

==Description==
Kraxxus represents the strength of [[Valdrakken Accord]] here on [[Forbidden Reach]].

Completing this quest will unlock her Envoy tasks today. You will be able to unlock other factions' Envoy tasks tomorrow.

==Rewards==
You will unlock access to the following:

You will also receive:
* {{cost|28|9}}
* 11,900 [[XP]]
* +10 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]
* +350 reputation with [[Obsidian Warders]]
* +350 reputation with [[Dark Talons]]
