The '''[[Forbidden Reach]] storyline''' of the [[Dragonflight campaign]] begins in [[patch 10.0.7]].

==A Creche Divided==
[[File:Morqut Village.jpg|thumb|[[Morqut Village]]]]
A Creche Divided reintroduces players to the [[Forbidden Reach]] and its new inhabitants.

{{Hidden Legacies}}

==Zskera Vaults==
[[File:Zskera Vault Az.jpg|thumb|Zskera Vault: Az]]

Early in the "A Creche Divided" storyline, players unlock access to the [[Zskera Vaults]], a single-player treasure run.

{{Zskera Vaults}}

==Verbal Archaeology==
[[File:Verbal Archaeology - the view.jpg|thumb|[[Voraxian]] and [[Veritistrasz]]]]

While entering the Zskera Vaults, [[Voraxian]] asks adventurers to find something interesting.

{{Verbal Archaeology}}

==While We Were Sleeping==
[[File:Sending Stone Protest.jpg|thumb|upright|[[Sending Stone: Protest]]]]
Bring back journal entries and sending stones to the scalecommanders for some more dracthyr history and progress on the [[While We Were Sleeping]] exploration achievement.
