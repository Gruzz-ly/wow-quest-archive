==Description==
By connecting with the [[Twisting Nether]], the [[Warsong clan (alternate universe)|Warsong]] [[shaman]] have created a ripple effect that they could not have anticipated.

Look around and you will see countless portals beginning to open, signs that the Twisting Nether seeks to connect to {{Au|Draenor}}.

Should these portals successfully open, the path will be clear for all manner of [[demon]]s and [[void]] creatures to rampage across this valley.
