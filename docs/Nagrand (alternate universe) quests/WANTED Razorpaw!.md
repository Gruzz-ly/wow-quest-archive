==Description==
Wanted for crimes against the innocent at the {{Au|Ring of Trials}}!

Last seen in the vicinity of the [[saberon]] den known as Sabermaw.

Exercise extreme caution!

Upon completion, this bounty will be fulfilled by [[Pyxni Pennypocket]] of the [[Steamwheedle Cartel]].
