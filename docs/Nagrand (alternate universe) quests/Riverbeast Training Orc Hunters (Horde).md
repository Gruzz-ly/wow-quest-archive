==Description==
The Warsong Clan of Nagrand are some of the finest hunters the world of Draenor has ever known. Their beast-killers will aid us in putting this mount to the test.

Out in Nagrand, you will find an orc by the name of Karak, the Great Hunter. He has slain more fierce beasts than any other in the clan. Face him astride your mount, and avenge the slain.
