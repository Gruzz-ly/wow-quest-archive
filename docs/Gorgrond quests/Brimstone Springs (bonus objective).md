== Notes ==
Also in the area, find water water breaker [[Sulfurious]] at the south end of the quest area, which drops the [[Bubble Wand]], and an [[Immerseus]]-sized [[Dessicus of the Dead Pools]] at the north end of the area, which drops [[Globe of Dead Water]]. That item starts [[Trophy of Glory: Dessicus]], and Dessicus is one of the elite mobs tracked in the achievement [[Gorgrond Monster Hunter]].
