==Description==
Me friend was in the [[mole machine]] wit' me. Good ol' Frenna tried to make the best of it by writin' all o' her observations down in a journal.

[[Penny Clobberbottom|Penny]] here wants me to tell ya where she is. I cannae say for sure, but she was followin' me outta here and I don't think she made it.
