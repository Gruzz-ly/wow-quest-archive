==Notes and trivia==
*Head southwest from where Burrian's original position was (or from the infested ogron) to reach the [[Compost Heap]]. Follow the path north to reach [[Evermorn Rise]]. There are plenty of Dew Masters in the area.
*During development, the quest was named '''Do the Dew'''. It was a reference to "Do the Dew," the slogan for the {{wp|Mountain Dew}} brand of soda.
