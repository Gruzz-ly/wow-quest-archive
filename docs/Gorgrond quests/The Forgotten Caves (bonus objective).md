== Notes ==
Cave Entrance: {{co|51.3|77.5|Gorgrond}}

On entering the cave, immediately take the south fork and follow the path all the way along the south end of the cave, killing [[Pale Flinger]]s and [[Pale Spiderwalker]]s, and destroying [[Spider Egg Sac]]s. At the end of the path at the east end of the cave, find a [[Strange Looking Dagger]]. Loot it for..., then jump down to the main room. Players who selected the Lumber Mill can open up some [[Hardened Thornvine]] on the north side of the pool. This batch contains an [[Unknown Petrified Egg]]. Head just to the east to kill [[Gorg the Host]].
