==Description==
All able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Yrel in Gorgrond.

Yrel and [[Vindicator Maraad|Maraad]] are requesting for a small expedition to be sent into the wilds of Gorgrond.

We hope to gain a better understanding of the land in hopes that it will show us how to exploit weaknesses in the [[Iron Horde]].

Assist them with their expedition.
