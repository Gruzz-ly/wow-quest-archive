==Notes==
Hallazeal is a level 82 elite with 1,946,000 health. He has one ability:
*{{abilities|Explosive Barrage|range=50,000|Launches explosive arcane mines at every nearby target.|notes=Instant}}
**{{abilities|Explosive Barrage|Launches a barrage of explosive orbs at all enemies. Explosive orbs deal 5644 Arcane amage when they detonate.|notes=Only hitting for 3,000 Arcane, despite the tooltip}}
Keep moving and Watery Dooming to work through Hallazeal's health. Don't Concussive Splash, as it will aggro the Ascendants outside.
