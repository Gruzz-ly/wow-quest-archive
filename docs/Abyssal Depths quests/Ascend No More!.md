== Notes ==
Pick up [[Runestones of Binding]] before heading out. The cultists are now hostile, watch out! Circle around to the southwest side of L'ghorek. Kill a {{NPC||Twilight Champion}} to loot the [[Twilight Cage Key]], which starts [[Prisoners]]. There are several {{NPC||Twilight Candidate}}s channeling Ancient Conduits, which are in the center of purple runic circles.
