# Abyssal Depths quests

[[File:WorldMap-VashjirDepths.jpg|thumb|250px|Zone map]]

'''Abyssal Depths''' is the third and final subzone of [[Vashj'ir]]. Players are led here after completing [[A Breath of Fresh Air]] in the [[Shimmering Expanse quests|Shimmering Expanse]].

Completing this part of the storyline awards the achievement [[20,000 Leagues Under the Sea]].

After Defending the Rift, players must get themselves back to [[Stormwind]] or [[Orgrimmar]] at the [[Eastern Earthshrine|Eastern]] or [[Western Earthshrine]], respectively. There, players are informed of {{NPC||Thrall}}'s actions at the [[Maelstrom]], leading players to [[Deepholm quests|Deepholm]].
