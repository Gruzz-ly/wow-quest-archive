==Objectives==
Speak to [[Loganaar]] at [[Nighthaven]] in [[Moonglade]].

==Description==
You just missed the messenger from Moonglade, <name>. He said little, except that he carried an urgent message from Loganaar, one of the honored trainers of the Cenarion Circle. Head to Moonglade as quickly as you can, <name>. The matter sounds important.

Look for Loganaar in the village of Nighthaven.

==Completion==
