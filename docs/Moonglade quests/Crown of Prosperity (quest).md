==Objectives==
Gather blooms from [[Un'Goro Crater]], [[Stranglethorn Vale]], and [[Northern Barrens]].
* [[Flower of Wealth]] (3)
* [[Flower of Peace]] (3)
* [[Flower of Felicity]] (3)
*Return to [[Myrael Lunarbloom]] in [[Moonglade]].

==Description==
Your crown is so colorful! Such beautiful pinks and yellows and blues... if only it could last.

Although those particular flowers cannot, I know some that can!

Similar flowers to those bloom every [[Lunar Festival]]. They hold a special connection to [[Elune]] that I can empower, thanks to you. Retrieve them for me, and we can fashion a crown that you can enjoy year round!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Crown of Infinite Prosperity]]
*{{cost|23|40|}}
*17,850 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Elune has a soft spot for flowers. Those that bloom during Lunar Festival are quite special.

==Completion==
Such a multitude of colors! These flowers will bring happiness to all who see them.

This crown shall bring you great prosperity.

==Notes==
*[[Crown of Prosperity]] must be owned to accept this quest.

All flowers can be found at or near the base of tree trunks.
*Flowers of Wealth are located at the [[Forgotten Pools]] in Northern Barrens.
*Flowers of Felicity are located at the [[Roiling Gardens]] in Un'goro Crater.
*Flowers of Prosperity are located north outside the [[Gurubashi Arena]] in the [[Cape of Stranglethorn]].
