==Objectives==
Travel to the hills on the south side of [[Moonglade]], on [[Kalimdor]]. Look for an air vortex east of the hills near the [[Timbermaw]] tunnel. Use the [[Dive]] skill of an animal companion to pierce the whirlwinds and retrieve a fragment of elemental air.
*Retrieve the [[Air Elemental Fragment]]

==Description==
You say you be looking for [[Van Amburgh]]? We can help each other. Da [[druid]]s here are concerned about some heightened elemental activity. Da [[elemental]]s be swarming about a fragment of pure elemental air, which manifests a whirlwind around it, on da south side of da lake.

Head to da hills and retrieve da elemental fragment. Take my [[bat]] companion, [[Zotl]], to fly into da winds, if you do not have a flying companion of your own. Zotl be hanging in da trees nearby.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*660 [[XP]]
*{{cost||39}}

==Completion==
You have da air shard? Excellent work. You see how easy it is to work with your companions when you have a shared goal and you know what to do? You be allies in this.

==Notes==
If you do not have a pet with [[Dive]], you can talk to Banteazo's bat, [[Zotl]], and it will follow you.

Look for the [[Air Elemental Fragment]], which is surrounded by three [[Wild Windtwister]]s, in southwest [[Moonglade]]. Use Dive and attack the Air Elemental Fragment to retrieve it.
