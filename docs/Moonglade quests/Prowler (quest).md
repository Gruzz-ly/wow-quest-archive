==Objectives==
Travel to the barrows on the east side of [[Moonglade]], on [[Kalimdor]]. Descend into the southeastern most [[Stormrage Barrow Dens|Stormrage barrow den]], and look for an earth elemental fragment in the depths. Use the [[Prowl (pet ability)]] skill of an animal companion to bypass the guardians and retrieve a fragment of elemental earth.
*Retrieve the [[Earth Elemental Fragment]]

==Description==
Da [[druid]]s be concerned about a fragment of earth in da barrows east of da lake. It be guarded by angry [[earth elemental]]s, and preventing da druids from carrying out dere rites.

Descend into da barrows and retrieve the elemental fragment. Take my cat companion, [[Siopi]], to sneak past da elementals, if you do not have a cat companion of your own. Siopi probably be asleep in da grass near here.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*660 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Ah, da earth shard. Da druids will take dis with da air shard and return dem where dey need to go.

==Notes==
If you do not have a pet with [[Prowl (pet ability)]], you can talk to Banteazo's nightsaber, [[Siopi]], and it will follow you.

One of the caves into the [[Stormrage Barrow Dens]] will have four [[Territorial Terran]]s surrounding the [[Earth Elemental Fragment]]. Use Prowl and attack the Earth Elemental Fragment to retrieve it.
