==Objectives==
[[Ahab Wheathoof]] at [[Bloodhoof Village]] in [[Mulgore]] wants you to feed his prized puppy, [[Kyle the Frenzied]].

Feed [[Kyle the Frenzied|Kyle]] [[Tender Strider Meat]] and return to [[Ahab Wheathoof]].
*Kyle fed

==Description==
Help, <name>! My prized puppy, [[Kyle the Frenzied|Kyle]], has run away! I just know he's running around [[Bloodhoof Village]], lost and hungry.

Will you find him and feed him? Once he eats he will settle down and come home.

To feed him you will need to find food. His favorite meal is [[Tender Strider Meat|tender strider meat]]. Just venture east and you should find plenty of striders. Hunt one down and take its succulent meat to [[Kyle the Frenzied|Kyle]]. When you see [[Kyle the Frenzied|Kyle]] in town, put the meat down and he is sure to come to you.
