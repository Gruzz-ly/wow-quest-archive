{{cost|||17}}

Upon completion of this quest you will gain:
{{cost|||40}}
*250 [[experience]] (or {{cost||1|50}} at max level)
*250 [[reputation]] with [[Thunder Bluff]]

==Rites of the Earthmother==

Upon completion of this quest you will gain:
*250 [[experience]] (or {{cost||1|50}} at max level)
*250 [[reputation]] with [[Thunder Bluff]]
