==Description==
The [[Grimtotem tribe|Grimtotem]] are a tribe that believe all of [[Kalimdor]] should belong to the [[Tauren]] alone. Their hate and anger led to a corrupting thirst for power. [[Magatha Grimtotem|Magatha]], their leader, poisoned our High Chieftain, [[Cairne Bloodhoof]], and attempted to take [[Thunder Bluff]] for herself; she was defeated, and the Grimtotem exiled.

Now they seek to ruin [[Mulgore]] itself as they are driven out. Quickly, <name>, you must go to the Thunderhorn Water Well in the north and perform the ritual!
