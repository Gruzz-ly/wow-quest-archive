==Objectives==
Recover the stolen relic from [[Brambleblade Ravine]]. Follow the relic's guidance to learn a new ability, then report back to [[Gart Mistrunner]] in [[Camp Narache]].
*Learn Spell: Engrave Chest - [[Fury of Stormrage (druid rune)]]

==Description==
The [[Tauren]] are an ancient people, <name>, and our lost relics can be found across [[Azeroth]]. Through them, the spirits can teach you ways to strengthen your connection to [[Emerald Dream|the Dream]].

The [[Bristleback tribe|bristleback]] [[quilboar]] have stolen one such relic from us. They have likely stashed it away within Brambleblade Ravine. Recover the relic, and follow its guidance to learn a new combat technique. Return to me when you have done this.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 85 [[XP]]
*+50 [[reputation]] with [[Darnassus (faction)|Darnassus]]
*[[Cracked Leather Vest]]

==Progress==
What has the relic taught you.{{sic}} <name>?

==Completion==
Well done, <name>. Applying the rune you have learned to your equipment will grant you powerful new abilities.

Seek out more of our lost relics along your travels.

==Notes==
The [[Lunar Idol]] can be found in the [[Bristleback Loot Cache]] in [[Brambleblade Ravine]]. Equip it and get six killing blows on mobs affected by [[Moonfire]], after which the idol can be used to learn the rune.
