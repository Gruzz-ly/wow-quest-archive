'''Fungus Among Us''' is a [[Daily cooking quests|daily cooking quest]] available from [[Eunice Burch]] in the [[Trade Quarter]] in [[Undercity]].

==Objectives==
Obtain 12 Sewer Caps. Deliver the fungus to [[Eunice Burch]] in [[Undercity]].
* [[Sewer Cap]] (12)

==Description==
Some chefs complain that our beloved Undercity doesn't boast the ingredients to create truly fine fare.

Such mewling little cretins. Delicious delights abound!

Gather some of the rank, slimy Sewer Caps growing in Undercity's northwest sewer line and return them to me.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|16|54|}} (at level 85)
* +1 [[Cooking]] Skill
* [[Epicurean's Award]]
*+250 [[reputation]] with [[Undercity (faction)|Undercity]]

==Progress==
Did you find enough Sewer Caps?

==Completion==
These smell absolutely wretched. Perfect!
