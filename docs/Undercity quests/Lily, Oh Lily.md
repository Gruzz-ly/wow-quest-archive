'''Lily, Oh Lily''' is a [[Daily cooking quests|daily cooking quest]] available from [[Eunice Burch]] in the [[Trade Quarter]] in [[Undercity]].

==Objectives==
Obtain 10 Stillwater Lilies. Deliver the plants to [[Eunice Burch]] in [[Undercity]].

* [[Stillwater Lily]] (10)

==Description==
The lilies that grow around [[Stillwater Pond]] can be rendered down into a delightful broth.

Well... by "delightful," I mean "won't immediately sicken any orc who drinks it."

Why don't you go gather some for me, hmmm? Just watch out for the giant flesh-eating tadpoles. 

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|16|54|}} (at level 85)
* +1 [[Cooking]] Skill
* [[Epicurean's Award]]
*+250 [[reputation]] with [[Undercity (faction)|Undercity]]

==Progress==
Did you find enough Stillwater Lilies?

==Completion==
Ah, what a luscious batch of lilies. Bravo! I'll boil them down right away...
