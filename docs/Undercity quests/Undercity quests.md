# Undercity quests

This is a list of all [[Undercity]] quests in table format (arranged by level).

For a questing guide detailing the best order to obtain and complete quests in [[Undercity]], see [[Undercity questing guide]].

The following quests can be found in [[Undercity]]:

*[[The Prodigal Lich]] - Present Sevren's Orders to Bethor Iceshard in the Undercity.
**[[The Lich's Identity]] - Bring the Lich's Spellbook to Bethor Iceshard in the Undercity.
***[[Return the Book]] - Return Gunther's Spellbook to him, on the island of Gunther's Retreat.
****[[Proving Allegiance]] - Summon Lillith Nefara and kill her.
*****[[The Prodigal Lich Returns]] - Bring the Nether Gem to Bethor Iceshard in the Undercity.

*[[Escorting Erland]] - Escort Erland through the wolves, to Rane Yorick.
**[[The Deathstalkers' Report]] - Bring the Deathstalker Report to High Executor Hadrec at the Sepulcher.
***[[Speak with Renferrel]] - Speak with Apothecary Renferrel at the Sepulcher.
****[[Zinge's Delivery]] - Bring the Wolf Heart Samples to Apothecary Zinge in the Undercity.
*****[[Sample for Helbrim]] - Bring the Wolf Heart Sample to Apothecary Helbrim in the Barrens.

* [[A Recipe For Death (2)]] - from [[Master Apothecary Faranell]] in [[The Apothecarium]] to [[Apothecary Renferrel]] in the [[The Sepulcher]]

*[[To Steal From Thieves]] - Bring Kenata, Fardel, and Marcel Dabyrie's Heads to Genavie Callow in Undercity.

*[[The Crown of Will]] - Report to Melisara at Tarren Mill in the Hillsbrad Foothills.
**[[The Crown of Will]] - Kill 14 Crushridge Maulers for Melisara in Tarren Mill.
***[[The Crown of Will]] - Kill Targ, Muckrake, and Glommus and bring their heads to Melisara in Tarren Mill.
****[[The Crown of Will]] - Kill Mug'Thol, and return his head with the Crown of Will to Melisara in Tarren Mill.
*****[[The Crown of Will]] - Deliver the Crown of Will to Sharlindra in the Undercity.

*[[To the Undercity for Yagyin's Digest]] - Bring the Sigil of the Hammer to Keeper Bel'dugur in the Undercity.
**[[The Star, the Hand and the Heart]] - Bring the items on Bel'dugur's Note to Keeper Bel'dugur in the Undercity.
***[[Forbidden Knowledge (Classic)]] - Bring Yagyin's Digest to Theldurin.

*[[Errand for Apothecary Zinge]] - Take the purchase order to Alessandro Luca in the Undercity.
**[[Errand for Apothecary Zinge]] - Bring the Field Sampling Kit to Apothecary Zinge in the Undercity.
***[[Into the Field]] - Speak to Chief Engineer Bilgewhizzle in Gadgetzan about getting a power source for the testing kit.
****[[Slake That Thirst]] - Bring 5 Wastewander Water Pouches to Chief Engineer Bilgewhizzle in Gadgetzan.

*[[Tanaris Field Sampling]] - Acquire acceptable samples for 8 basilisks, 8 hyenas, and 8 scorpions.
**[[Return to Apothecary Zinge]] - Bring the Sealed Field Testing Kit to Apothecary Zinge in the Undercity.

*[[Lines of Communication (Horde)]] - Travel to the Hinterlands and burn the Highvale Records, Highvale Report, and Highvale Notes.
*[[A Sample of Slime...]] - from [[Chemist Fuely]] in [[The Apothecarium]]; Bring 5 Corrupted Felwood Samples to Chemist Fuely.
*[[Better Late Than Never (Classic)]]
**[[Better Late Than Never (Horde)]] - Check with the Royal Overseers of the Undercity to learn the whereabouts of a Jeremiah Felstone.
***[[The Jeremiah Blues]] - Find Jeremiah Payson in the Undercity and see if he is in fact Jeremiah Felstone.
****[[Good Luck Charm (quest)]] - Return to Janice Felstone in Western Plaguelands her Good Luck Half-Charm.
*****[[Two Halves Become One]] - Locate the other half of the Good Luck Charm somewhere on the Felstone Farm in Western Plaguelands and reassemble it.


*[[Seeping Corruption (quest)]] - from [[Chemist Cuely]] in [[The Apothecarium]]; Fill all 4 Empty Vials at the tide pools along the coast of the Ruins of Eldarath in Azshara.
**[[Seeping Corruption (quest)]] - Give Cuely's Elixir to [[Thersa Windsong]] in Undercity.

*[[Melding of Influences]] - Bring a Merged Ooze Sample to Chemist Fuely in Undercity.
*[[... and a Batch of Ooze]] - Gather Un'Goro Slime Samples
*[[The Champion of the Banshee Queen]] - Speak with Nathanos Blightcaller in the Eastern Plaguelands.
