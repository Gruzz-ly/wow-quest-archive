All able bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to Okrilla at Dreadmaul Hold.

Okrilla, commander of the Horde forces at Dreadmaul Hold, requires your help to secure the outpost against rebellious [[ogre]]s and the forces of the [[Alliance]].

Travel to Dreadmaul Hold by taking the road south from the [[Swamp of Sorrows]].

Destiny calls!
