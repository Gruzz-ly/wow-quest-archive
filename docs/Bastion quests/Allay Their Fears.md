==Objectives==
* [[Korinna's Allaying Crook (usable)]]
* Allaying Crook charged
* Wildlife absorbed (10)
* Allaying Ritual completed

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Ascended (faction)|The Ascended]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Vesiphone|May the gentle sound of bells follow you always, mortal.}}

==Notes==
;Finding Korinna's Allaying Crook
:{{text|say|[[Zosime]]|The wyrms and cloudfeathers have gone into a frenzy again! Please, take Korinna's Allaying Crook and use it to put their tortured spirits to rest!}}

In the [[Garden of Respite]], kill [[Anima-Starved Wyrm]]s and [[Anima Starved Cloudfeather]]s and find [[Coalesced Anima]] and use the crook on them. Once filled, go 
to {{Co|49|60|Bastion}} and use the Korinna's Release extra button, which summons [[Pacified Wyrm]]s and [[Pacified Cloudfeather|Cloudfeathers]] and a [[Memory of Korinna]].
