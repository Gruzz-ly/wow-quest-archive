==Objectives==
Activate the Path of Ascension.
*[[Path of Ascension]] activated
*Meet [[Apolon]] and [[Artemede]] in the western wing of [[Elysian Hold]].

==Description==
With the [[anima]] you collected, we can once again start up the Path of Ascension.

The eternal training ground of the [[kyrian]], the Path is an ancient tradition and favored amongst many of the kyrian, aspirant and [[ascended]] alike.

The Hands of Courage, [[Apolon]] and [[Artemede]], have stepped up to lead this tradition. Once you herald the Path's rebirth, speak to them to learn more.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|57|40}}
* 1,000 [[XP]]

==Completion==
By your hand, the coliseum is powered by anima once more. Glory awaits you and your allies in the Path of Ascension!
