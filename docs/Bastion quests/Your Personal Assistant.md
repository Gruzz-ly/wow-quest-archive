==Objectives==
Select a steward to aid you, then return to [[Kalisthene]].
* [[Steward]] companion selected

==Description==
Here in [[Hero's Rest]], we have a beacon that is the fastest way to draw [[Polemarch Adrestes|the Polemarch's]] attention.

It has been quite some time since we have used it, and I am afraid that it has fallen into disrepair.

I authorize you to receive a steward companion during your stay here in Bastion.

I will call for some to convene here. You may select one to aid you.

==Rewards==
You will learn: [[Summon Steward]]

You will receive:
*{{cost|11|93|40}}
* 6,800 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Stewards are the force that keep Bastion running.

==Completion==
Whenever you need assistance, your steward will be available to provide many services.
