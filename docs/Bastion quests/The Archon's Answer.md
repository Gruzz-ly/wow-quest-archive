==Objectives==
Speak to Kalisthene.
* Speak to [[Kalisthene]] {{co|53.5|87.0|Bastion}}
* Accept Kalisthene's gift {{co|53.5|87.1|Bastion}}

==Description==
Your cleansing is complete, and you are whole.

Our plan was a success. Undergoing such a rite, so soon after your arrival, has attracted Kalisthene's attention once more.

Now is our opportunity to request an audience with the [[Kyrestia the Firstborne|Archon]].

Stand before her and be proud of what you have accomplished. No matter what happens now, you have done all that any aspirant could hope to.

==Notes==
On accept:
:{{text|say|Kleia|We had best not keep Kalisthene waiting, <name>.}}

Head southwest and up the hill to find Kalisthene and two Ascended Champions hovering. Speak with her:
:<Kalisthene peers down at you.>

:We meet again, mortal.

:{{gossip|I wish to speak to the Archon.}}
:{{text|say|Kalisthene|You arrived in Bastion with dire news, which I have shared with the Archon. She thanks you for your service.}}
:{{text|say|Kalisthene|As a token of gratitude, she wishes to offer you a gift. Do you accept?}}

Kalisthene spawns a blue circle on the ground. Stand in it and use the bonus ability:
:{{Abilities|Kalisthene's Grace|Accept the gift.|id=311245|icon=ability_priest_ascension|notes=Instant.}}

Kalisthene and the two champions channel a spell into the player. Adventurers are awarded their class-specific covenant ability for the [[Kyrian Covenant]].
