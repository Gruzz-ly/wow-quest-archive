==Objectives==
Collect 10 Cloudfeather Essence.
* [[Cloudfeather Essence]] (10)

==Description==
Even beings of the [[Shadowlands]] need to eat. They may not do so quite like [[Azeroth|your world]], but it is similar enough.

The pride's favorite prey is the [[Juvenile Cloudfeather|juvenile cloudfeathers]] found nearby. Prove to [[Andira]] that you are a contributing member of the pride by taking her out to hunt for their next meal.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|35|80|20}}
* 6,800 [[XP]]
* +100 [[reputation]] with [[Ascended (faction)|The Ascended]]

==Progress==
Larions bond through hunting. Working together with Andira to collect food is vital to your relationship.

==Completion==
Providing for the pride is an important step on your path to earning your loyalty here.
