==Objectives==
*Take the Colossal Core
*Charge the Colossal Core
*Insert the Colossal Core
*Push the Button
*[[Andromede, Herald of the Forsworn]] slain

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Xandria]]|The [[Forsworn]] have begun an assault on our Colossus. We have few enough as it is. You must prevent them from claiming it by any means necessary.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 6,800 [[XP]]
*+125 [[reputation]] with [[Ascended (faction)|The Ascended]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Xandria|Your courage shines brightly this day. Bastion stands!}}

==Notes==
*Location: [[Vestibule of Eternity]], [[Bastion]].

First, click on the [[Steward Mechanic]].
:{{Text|say|Steward Mechanic|Small now, but you make it BIG!}}

A bar appears. Kill the enemies to fill it: [[Darkvowed Hoplite]] (20%), [[Darkvowed Purifier]], [[Forsworn Darkblade]] (5%), [[Forsworn Watcher]], [[Forsworn Phalynx]] (4%).

Once filled, find and click on the [[Centurion Colossus]]. Then mount it, to make The Button available. Clicking it charges a charge. Continue further to find the Wounded Andromede.
