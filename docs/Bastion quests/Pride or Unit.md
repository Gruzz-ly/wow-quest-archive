==Objectives==
Meet with Pelodis and Nemea, then pick a side.
* Meet with [[Pelodis]] and [[Nemea]]
* Speak to Pelodis (Optional)
* Speak to Nemea (Optional)
* [[Phalynx]] or [[Larion]] chosen

==Description==
;Nemea

You have made great strides with our pride. I hope you found value and fulfillment in working with the larion.

The time to meet with Pelodis has come. You must decide whose way of life is more rewarding, and which you would prefer to pursue.

;Pelodis
I hope that working with my phalynx has swayed you, <name>.

Let us meet with Nemea. There you can decide whose way of life is superior, and which you would prefer to pursue.

==Progress==
;Nemea

The pack has welcomed you, <name>. I hope that you feel it.

;Pelodis
Surely you must agree that the ease of phalynx companionship suits you.

==Completion==
It is done.

==Notes==
The side that the player takes will lock them to the chosen NPC to later become a [[companion]] should the player choose to join the [[Kyrian Covenant]].
