==Objectives==
Defeat Amarth the Harvester and Nalthor the Rimebinder in the [[Necrotic Wake]].
*[[Amarth]] the Harvester slain
*[[Nalthor the Rimebinder]] slain
*Suggested players: 5
*Speak to Kalisthene at [[Hero's Rest]].

==Description==
[[Xandria]], the mighty Paragon of Courage, is the finest [[warrior]] among all the [[ascended]]. But even she may be overwhelmed by the strongest of [[Maldraxxus]]' forces.

Your fate is intertwined with hers, now. You must gather your allies and plunge into the Necrotic Wake to ensure both Amarth and Nalthor do not make it back to the safety of their realm.

May the [[Kyrestia the Firstborne|Archon]] guide your blade into their hearts.
