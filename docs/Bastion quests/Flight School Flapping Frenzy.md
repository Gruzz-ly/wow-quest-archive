'''Flight School: Flapping Frenzy''' is one of the three Flight School world quests in [[Bastion]] required for the achievement [[Flight School Graduate]].

After completing the world quest normally, Malo offers a paragon difficulty, which has much tighter tolerances. Should players complete it, they will be awarded the extra achievement [[Aerial Ace]]. 

==Objectives==
* Speak to [[Instructor Malo]] {{co|58.7|10.6|Bastion}}
* Goal reached
