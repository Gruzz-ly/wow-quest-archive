==Objectives==
Aid Pelodis in repairing Gatamatos.
*[[Gatamatos]] retrieved
*Gatamatos brought to build rig
*[[Forgefire Engineer]]s escorted (3)
*[[Anima Barrel]]s brought over
*[[Reinforced Armor]] (5)
*Reinforced Armor placed (5)
*Talk to [[Pelodis]] to finish repair

==Description==
Gatamatos needs numerous repairs to meet functional standards.

Upkeeping [[phalynx]] is part of the role. Lend a hand so we may finish the repairs thoroughly and quickly.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|35|80|20}}
* 6,800 [[XP]]
* +100 reputation with [[Ascended (faction)|The Ascended]]

==Progress==
As long as you keep your phalynx in working condition, they will obey without hesitation.

==Completion==
He may appear to be returned to perfect shape, but he must be re-tested and trained.
