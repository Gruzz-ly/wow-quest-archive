==Description==
Despite our efforts, the [[Void]] stands strong. All of the forces of [[Bastion]] fight, and fall one by one...

The Void cannot destroy us!

They are led by Tezulat the Annihilator, scourge of a thousand [[Plane|realms]]. This day, his reign of terror will end!
