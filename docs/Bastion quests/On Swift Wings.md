==Objectives==
Fly with Adrestes to [[Elysian Hold]].
* Speak to [[Polemarch Adrestes|Adrestes]]
*Speak with Adrestes in Elysian Hold.

==Description==
Our revered [[Kyrestia the Firstborne|Archon]] has decided to grace you with an audience.

It is your duty to present your discoveries to her, then follow her wise judgment.

She holds audience in Elysian Hold, part of the sprawling [[Spires of Ascension]], the home of the [[Ascended]].

We shall carry you there, for it is a place for those who can fly.
