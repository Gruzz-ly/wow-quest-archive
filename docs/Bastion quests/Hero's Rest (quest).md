'''Hero's Rest''' is an optional breadcrumb. It is mutual with [[Go in Service]].

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Kalisthene]] in Hero's Rest.
* Take the gateway to [[Hero's Rest]]

==Description==
In light of the new arrivals to [[Bastion]], Kalisthene has enabled the [[anima]] gateways to Hero's Rest once more!

Those wishing to partake of breathtaking views, unparalleled steward service, and all manner of training and repair facilities should venture to this most remarkable of locations at their earliest convenience.

Simply follow the path north and bear to the right at the crossroads.

Kalisthene will personally greet all new arrivals for this most joyous of occasions!
