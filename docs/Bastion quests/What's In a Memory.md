==Description==
It may be possible to extract memories from fragment, but I worry they will not be useful. This is not whole kyrian, but a tiny piece! A weird, broken piece. It cannot even speak!

We will apply extractor and see what comes out. Extractor might explode. It might not. For this? Worth the risk, I think.

Do not know what will emerge. Could be memories. Could be nothing. Could be something else. May try to fight you! Stay on your guard, yes? Collect all that look Forsworn.

Speak when ready.

Fragments of many forms walk from the mirrors to the locus. Most are friendly kyrian or various forms of wildlife, but a few are Forsworn kyrian. Kill [[Forsworn Memory Fragment]]s and collect the quest items. After collecting 10:
:{{text|say|Mikanikos|Fragment is ready! Take it to soul mirrors to view memories.}}
:{{text|say|Kleia|I leave it to you, <name>. You have faced Lysonia before.}}

Click on the [[Fragment of Lysonia]] and the scene fades to white for a moment. Players are debuffed with Essence of Lysonia - Carrying the Essence of Lysonia, allowing the bearer to witness her memories.

Turn in to Kleia.
