==Objectives==
Disarm the traps within the [[Third Chamber of Kalliope]] and make your way to the final hall.
* Traps disarmed (3)
* [[First Guardian of Kalliope]] slain
*Speak With Artificer Ligiea Within the final hall Of the Third Chamber Of Kalliope.

==Description==
You've proven useful. So, I am going to require you to disarm the defenses of this hall.

Please take care not to die too quickly. I'd like to have the spear before the end of this eon.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|38|61}}
* 12,250 [[XP]]
* +100 [[reputation]] with [[The Ascended]]

==Progress==
Continue to be of use and you just might make it out of this intact.

==Completion==
Your efforts have brought me what I desire. For your reward, I won't kill you.
