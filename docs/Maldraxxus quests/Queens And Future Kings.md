==Objectives==
Slay 1 marrowbore queens, and collect 5 marrowbore larvae.
*Gain [[Twigin]]'s aid

==Description==
A swarm of marrowbore queens have been laying eggs atop the ruins of our house. We may be able to use this to our advantage.

With Twigin's help, eliminate the queens and recover larvae from the eggs.

I have a plan for the larvae that may allow us to use the marrowbore to our advantage.
