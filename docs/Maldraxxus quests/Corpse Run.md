==Objectives==
Return the Bone Carcass to [[Rathan]] at [[Butchers Block]].
*Recruit [[Flytrap]]
*Ride Flytrap
*[[Salvageable Carcass]] returned to Sheilun in Butcher's Block
*[[Call Flytrap]] (provided)

==Description==
There's work that needs doing 'round here. Mmmm hmmm.

Spotted a big ol' carcass nearby. I aim to salvage it for parts.

Figure you could come along, help me drag it to Rathan. He'd like that. Mmmm hmmm.

Reckon I could even carry you there and back. You don't look too big.

Ready to get going?
