There are two versions of the '''Not Much to Goo On''' world quest, the difference being in the objective.

==Objectives==
*[[Green Slime]] slain (10)
*[[Compressed Plague]] slain

Or
*House of Plagues reclaimed (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{Text|say|[[Plague Deviser Marileth]]|It seems the area surrounding our necropolis has been affected by caustic substances. Time to gather some samples!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 8,150 [[XP]]
* +125 [[reputation]] with [[The Undying Army]]
*3x [[Grateful Offering]]

==Completion==
:{{Text|say|Plague Deviser Marileth|I simply cannot wait to analyze what you have collected! Good work, apprentice!}}

==Notes==
*Location: [[House of Plagues (subzone)|House of Plagues]], [[Maldraxxus]].
*Use Transport Pad at {{Co|59.8|71.2|Maldraxxus}} to reach the area if flying is not yet unlocked.
*For the reclaim version, also loot [[Plague Sample]] from [[Unbroken Plague Samples]] and kill [[Slime Giant]]s.
