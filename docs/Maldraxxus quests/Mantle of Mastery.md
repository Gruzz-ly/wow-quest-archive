==Objectives==
Summon an elder [[Ghost|spirit]] of [[Maldraxxus]] through the [[Prime Arcanum]] and defeat it. Summoning requires three players.
* Summoning completed {{co|60.0|29.9|Maldraxxus}}
* [[Carcaeus]] slain
* Suggested players: 5

==Description==
You have much to establish yourself as a promising [[acolyte]]. Your superiors have made it known that you are ready to be tested for your ritualist's mantle.

When you are ready, begin the Ritual of Ancient Summoning to call forth an elder spirit of [[Maldraxxus]]. Conquer the spirit, and prove your mettle!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* [[Ritualist's Mantle]]
* {{cost|77|22|}}
* 14,200 [[XP]]

==Progress==
The Prime Arcanum awaits you, acolyte.

==Completion==
You have proven yourself worthy to don the mantle, acolyte.

Wear it with honor and pride.

==Notes==
Interact with the Grimoire of Ancient Summoning to reveal two additional runes that other players must stand on.
