==Notes and trivia==
Find [[Unlit Brazier of Challenge]] to get a buff. Go through circles on the ground to gain a speed bonus. Avoid [[Slimelet]]s.
*{{Text|say|[[Seira]]|Light your torch here! Move quickly! Watch out for slimes!}}

The braziers are internally called Plague Brazier, Lich Brazier, Eyes Brazier, Construct Brazier, and Warlords Brazier.
