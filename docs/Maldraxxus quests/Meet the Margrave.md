==Objectives==
Present your runeblade to [[Margrave Krexus]].
*Present your runeblade to Margrave Krexus in the necropolis Vortrexxis.

==Description==
Word of the runeblade has reached Margrave Krexus. He wishes to see it for himself.

With such a weapon by your side, you pose a threat that the other houses cannot ignore. Perhaps we can use that to our advantage.

But enough talk. Follow me.
