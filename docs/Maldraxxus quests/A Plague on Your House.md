==Objectives==
Speak with [[House of Plagues]] survivors.
* Speak with [[Vial Master Lurgy]] {{co|59.4|73.0|Maldraxxus}}
* Speak with [[Foul-Tongue Cyrlix]] {{co|58.5|73.5|Maldraxxus}}
* Speak with [[Boil Master Yetch]] {{co|59.1|73.9|Maldraxxus}}

==Description==
Though the physical House of Plagues is lost, the knowledge contained within was not. There are survivors here that continue to research and develop new horrifying weapons.

In time we will restore the house and rise to even greater power, unleashing creations and plagues never before seen upon our enemies!

Perhaps you can help? Speak with survivors and see if any of them can use your aid.
