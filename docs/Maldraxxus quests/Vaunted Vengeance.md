==Objectives==
Collect Ritual Drafts from the three Architects.
* [[Xeeli's Ritual Drafts]] {{co|72.1|48.1|Maldraxxus}}
* [[Halen's Ritual Drafts]] {{co|69.9|52.7|Maldraxxus}}
* [[Lye's Ritual Drafts]] {{co|72.8|53.2|Maldraxxus}}
*Bring the Ritual Plans to Khalliq at the ritual stile.

==Description==
So you are from [[Vashj|my baroness's]] [[Azeroth|home world]]? It must be a special one to breed such powerful individuals.

The [[Maldraxxi necromancer|necromancers]] here have already begun preparation for [[Mor'Bitan|Mor'Bitan's]] ritual. We should capitalize on their progress. 

Three necromancers act as the ritual's architects. Should we acquire their drafts, we could modify them to suit our purposes.

Collect the plans... and feel free to savor your victims' torment.

==Notes==
Interact with the three anima crystals to acquire the [[Fractured Anima Crystal]]. Head east into [[Glutharn's Decay]], kill [[Morbid Ritualist]]s, and use the quest item on their corpses. [[Jar of Ground Maldracite|Jars of Ground Maldracite]] litter the quest area. Watch out for the [[Morbid Boneguard]]s! The named quest mobs also count as valid corpses for Tainted Cores.
