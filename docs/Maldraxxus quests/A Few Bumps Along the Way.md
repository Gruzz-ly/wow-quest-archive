==Notes==
Location: [[Spearhead (Maldraxxus)|Spearhead]], [[Maldraxxus]].

;Approaching the ballista
:{{Text|bossemote|Protect the ballista while it is repaired.}}

[[Fleshsewn Scavenger]]s and [[Steelskin Vanguard]]s will periodically attack the ballistae, but you can pull any nearby undead to the circled area to speed up the progress bar. [[Ryan Bloodstone]], [[Roc Barad]], and [[Kyle Vlaros]] may appear to assist you in defeating the enemies.

Completing this world quest awards {{Abilities|Battle Hardened|id=306272|icon=ability_warrior_shieldmastery}}, needed to obtain [[Sorrowbane]] and [[Oonar's Arm]].
