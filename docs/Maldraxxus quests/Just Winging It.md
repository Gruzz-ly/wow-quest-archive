==Notes==
Click on the Taloned Flayedwing:
:<The flayedwing looks at you expectantly.>

:{{Gossip|<Ride the flayedwing back to the Wrangler Pit.>}}
:{{Text|bossemote|The flayedwing leaps into the air! Hang on!}}

The flayedwing takes into the skies and has three abilities:
#Soothe
#Hold on Tight
#Praise

Depending on what it needs, choose the appropriate ability:
*{{Text|bossemote|The flayedwing is flying smoothly, praise them!}}
*{{Text|bossemote|The flayedwing is trying to shake you off, hold on tight!}}
*{{Text|bossemote|The flayedwing is scared, soothe it with gentle pats!}}

After a while, the flayedwing flies to [[Wrangler Pit]] and drops off the player.
