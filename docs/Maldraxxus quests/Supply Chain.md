My friend! I could sure use your expertise right now.

One of our wagon convoys was attacked nearby! It was transporting critical supplies... and new goods for me to sell! It's important--no--vital, that we recover as much of that equipment as possible.

So, you ready to go? Let's head out and salvage what we can.
