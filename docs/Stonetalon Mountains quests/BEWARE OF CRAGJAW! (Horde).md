==Objectives==
Kill [[Cragjaw]] and take Cragjaw's Huge Tooth.
*[[Cragjaw's Huge Tooth]]

== Description ==
By order of [[Overlord Krom'gar]], [[Cragpool Lake]] is OFF LIMITS!

The ferocious beast known as Cragjaw has made the lake a death trap. Should you dispatch of Cragjaw, take his huge tooth as a trophy and return it to Spy-Mistress Anara for a reward.
