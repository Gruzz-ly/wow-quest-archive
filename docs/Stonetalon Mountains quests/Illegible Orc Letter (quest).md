==Objectives==
Take the Illegible Orc Letter to [[Boog the "Gear Whisperer"]] at [[Windshear Mine]] in [[Stonetalon Mountains]].
* [[Illegible Orc Letter]] (provided)

==Description==
You've discovered a parchment with an impressive looking Horde seal imprinted upon its page. Unfortunately, the rest of the document is completely illegible as it is in orcish.

Take the letter to Boog. He might know what to do with it!

==Rewards==
*1600 XP
*+150 reputation with [[Darnassus (faction)|Darnassus]]
*+150 reputation with [[Gnomeregan (faction)|Gnomeregan]]
