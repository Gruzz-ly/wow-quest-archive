==Quest tables==
Quests that take place outside of [[Stonetalon Mountains (Classic)|Stonetalon Mountains]] are denoted by a {{Outzone}}. 

Quests that are part of a chain are denoted by {{Chain}}.

Quests marked with {{Sod-inline}} are exclusive to ''[[Season of Discovery]]''

==={{Alliance Icon}} Alliance quests===
[[Alliance]]  quests in [[Stonetalon Mountains (Classic)|Stonetalon Mountains]] come mostly from [[Stonetalon Peak]] and from the Alliance NPCs overlooking [[Windshear Crag]]. The quests range from level 18 through level 28, so players needing a break from [[Ashenvale (Classic)|Ashenvale]] quests may wish to come to this zone.

==={{Horde Icon}} Horde quests===
Most [[Horde]] quests in [[Stonetalon Mountains (Classic)|Stonetalon Mountains]] can be obtained from [[Sun Rock Retreat]] or [[Malaka'jin]]. In addition, several NPCs in the nearby [[Honor's Stand]] in the [[Barrens (Classic)|Barrens]] provide quests for the mountains. The quests range from level 17 through level 29, making Stonetalon a natural next step from the [[Barrens (Classic)|Barrens]].
