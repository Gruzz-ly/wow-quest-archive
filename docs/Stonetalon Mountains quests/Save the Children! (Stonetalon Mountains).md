==Objectives==
Use [[Master Thal'darah]]'s [[Hippogryph]] to rescue 5 Helpless Young Druids from [[Thal'darah Grove]].
*[[Helpless Young Druid]] Rescued (5)
*[[Thal'darah's Hippogryph Whistle]] (provided)

==Description==
My poor, defenseless young students are stranded at Thal'darah Grove. With the battle raging across [[Battlescar Valley]], they have no means of escaping. We must save the [[child]]ren before the [[Horde]] drops [[The Bomb|their bomb]]!

Take my hippogryph and use it to rescue the young [[druid]]s of Thal'darah Grove. Drop them off at the hippogryph post here in town.

Save them all, <name>! You truly are our only hope.
