# Stonetalon Mountains storyline

[[File:WorldMap-StonetalonMountains.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]
'''[[Stonetalon Mountains]]''' is a neutral level 25-30 zone in central [[Kalimdor]]. Both [[Alliance]] and [[Horde]] are led here from [[Ashenvale]], where some of the starting quests for this line take place.

The progress of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Stonetalon Mountains Quests]].

After this zone is complete, or reaching level 30, [[Southern Barrens]] and [[Desolace]] are possible questing areas
