== Description ==
The troops are hungry, <name>. With our supply lines cut off, we're steadily running out of food. I can cook the soldiers a feast if you can get me some ingredients.

Head into the woods west of here and look for Kalimdor eagle nests. Steal the eggs from the nests and bring them back to me.
