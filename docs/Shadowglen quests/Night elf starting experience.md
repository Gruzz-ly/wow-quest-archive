# Night elf starting experience

[[File:WorldMap-ShadowglenStart.jpg|thumb|Area map]]
The '''night elf starting experience''' takes place in [[Shadowglen]] on [[Teldrassil]], continuing afterwards to [[Dolanaar]]. In it, players help the elves of Shadowglen as they fight against the lingering corruption on Teldrassil. Together with Priestess Dentaria Silverglade, players seek out the guardian of the forest [[Tarindrella]] and help her cleanse the corrupted spiders from the land. Finally players travel onward to Dolanaar and the rest of Teldrassil.
