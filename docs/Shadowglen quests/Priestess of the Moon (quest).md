== Objectives ==
Speak to [[Dentaria Silverglade]] between the two pools in the center of [[Shadowglen]].

== Description ==
Perhaps you'd be willing to aid us in further pursuing the corruption haunting Shadowglen.

[[Dentaria Silverglade]], Priestess of the Moon, has offered to aid us. You should find her between the two pools just north of [[Aldrassil|our great tree]]. Circle around the tree to reach her and offer her any help that you can, young <class>.
