==Objectives==
Speak to [[Ezul'aan]] at the [[Training Hall]] in [[Stormwind]].

==Description==
Ezul'aan, one of the foremost [[paladin]]s in the realm, has asked to speak with you personally. I do not know what he has planned, but it sounds like he has been impressed with your rapid progress.

You should make your way to Stormwind immediately and meet him at the Training Hall in the [[Old Town]] district.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]

==Completion==
