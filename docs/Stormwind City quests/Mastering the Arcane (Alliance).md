==Objectives==
Go to [[Shadowfang Keep]] and obtain Silverlaine's Enchanted Crystal from [[Baron Silverlaine]], 5 bundles of Moontouched Wood, and 5 Ghostly Essence.
*[[Silverlaine's Enchanted Crystal]]
*[[Moontouched Wood]] (5)
*[[Ghostly Essence]] (5)

==Description==
You have proven yourself a brilliant student and a dedicated servant of the [[Alliance]]. Word of your accomplishments has even reached the ears of [[Varian Wrynn|the king]].

In recognition of your service, King Wrynn has commissioned the royal [[enchanter]] to create a special weapon.

The materials required for your staff are found only in [[Silverpine Forest]]'s cursed Shadowfang Keep. Travel there and find a lost heirloom of the Silverlaine family, a supply of moontouched wood, and the essence of the [[ghost]]s haunting the keep.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 2,300 [[XP]]
* +500 reputation with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]
*[[Staff of the Royal Wizard]]

The quest turn-in, [[Lucan Cordell]], is located in the enchanting shop along the [[Canals (Stormwind City)|Canals]], just outside of the [[Mage Quarter]] in [[Stormwind]].
