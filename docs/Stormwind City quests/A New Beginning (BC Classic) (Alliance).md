'''A New Beginning''' starts a short introductory questline for players who used the BCC [[Character Boost]]. All [[class]]es, start in front of their respective class trainer in [[Stormwind City]]. The class trainers are: {{ClassIcon|Priest}} [[High Priestess Laurena]], {{ClassIcon|Warrior}} [[Ander Germaine]], {{ClassIcon|Paladin}} [[Lord Grayson Shadowbreaker]], {{ClassIcon|Warlock}} [[Ursula Deline]], {{ClassIcon|Mage}} [[Jennea Cannon]], {{ClassIcon|Druid}} [[Theridran]], {{ClassIcon|Hunter}} [[Einris Brightspear]], and {{ClassIcon|Rogue}} [[Lord Tony Romano]].

==Objectives==
Meet with a <class> trainer in [[Stormwind City|Stormwind]].

==Description==
A letter magically appears in the lining of your armor. It reads:

"Welcome to the city of Stormwind, capital of the [[Alliance]]! 

Meet with a <class> trainer to begin your training. They will help prepare you for your journey across Azeroth and beyond."

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 900 [[XP]]
*[[Azeroth Survival Kit]]

==Completion==
Ready for your training, <race>?
