'''Keep Yer Feet On The Ground''' begins a quest chain for [[Dwarf (playable)|dwarf]] characters to obtain their [[heritage armor]]. It can be picked up just outside the [[Stormwind Embassy]] within [[Stormwind City]] after reaching level {{Level|Bfa-max}}.

==Objectives==
Search [[Gol'Bolar Quarry]] for the missing excavation crew.
*Find the missing excavators
*[[Maw'roc the Thief]] slain

==Description==
<Name>! The [[Explorers' League|Explorers League]] is setting up a new exhibit in the [[Hall of Explorers]], so we sent a team in search of new artifacts to display in it.

They headed off to Gol'Bolar Quarry in [[Dun Morogh]], but haven't returned. Would ye mind checking on 'em?
