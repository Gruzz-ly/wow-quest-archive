==Objectives==
Speak to [[Signilda Hardforge]] at the [[Training Hall]] in [[Stormwind]].

==Description==
Signilda Hardforge has sent word that she wishes to honor you for your mastery of arms and your dedication to the [[Alliance]]. Make your way to Stormwind, and speak with her at the Training Hall to the south of [[Old Town]]. I do not know what she has planned, but it sounds like she has been impressed with your rapid progress.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]

==Completion==
It's good to see that you received my message. I have something important I wish to discuss with you.
