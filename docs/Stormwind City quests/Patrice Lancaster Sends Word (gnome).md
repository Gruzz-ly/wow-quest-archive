==Objectives==
Speak to [[Patrice Lancaster]] at the [[Training Hall]] in [[Stormwind City|Stormwind]].

==Description==
I have an urgent message for you from Stormwind, <name>. Patrice Lancaster has asked to meet with you in Stormwind. The message didn't say what the [[priest trainer]] wanted to discuss, but you should make your way to Stormwind as quickly as you can. Look for Patrice Lancaster at the Training Hall in the [[Old Town]] district of the city.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Gnomeregan (faction)|Gnomeregan]]

==Completion==
I see that you received my message. Excellent!

==Notes==
*[[Mechagnome (playable)|Mechagnomes]] also have access to this quest, while also being offered [[Patrice Lancaster Sends Word|the non-gnome version]]. Only one can be accepted.
**Similarly, both subsequent priest quests are offered, but only one can be accepted.
