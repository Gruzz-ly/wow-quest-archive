==Description==
There exists a hellish gate that has loomed silent for years, but it is silent no longer.

By the order of Highlord [[Bolvar Fordragon]], all able-bodied citizens of the Alliance are to report to the Blasted Lands. Speak to [[Dungar Longdrink]], the Gryphon Master to arrange a flight.

Once at the Blasted Lands, report to [[Watch Commander Relthorn Netherwane]]. He awaits you before the Dark Portal...
