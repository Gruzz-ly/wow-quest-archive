==Objectives==
Speak to [[Dalgrun Steelpine]] in [[Stormwind]].

==Description==
Dalgrun Steelpine oversees the training and coordination of [[hunter]]s in the service of [[Stormwind (kingdom)|Stormwind]]. He has asked to speak to you personally. As soon as you are able, travel to Stormwind and meet with Dalgrun at the [[Training Hall]] in [[Old Town]].

I have no idea what he has planned, but it sounds like he has been impressed with your rapid progress.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]

==Completion==
