'''An Urgent Matter''' starts the [[Lion's Heritage]] questline. It requires a level 50 [[Human (playable)|human]] character.

==Objectives==
[[File:An Urgent Matter.png|thumb|The scene of the crime.]]
Meet [[Mathias Shaw|Master Mathias Shaw]] on the outside ramparts behind [[Stormwind Keep]].
*[[Urgent Stormwind Summons]] (provided)

==Description==
[[File:Defias Rope.png|thumb|Defias Rope.]]
<A folded piece of paper is slipped into your hand. When you turn to look who gave it to you, there is nobody there.>

<Name>, there is an urgent mission that requires a seasoned hero of [[Stormwind]], such as yourself. 

Quickly, meet me behind Stormwind Keep, and tell no one!

<The bottom bears a small crest that you recognize as of [[SI:7]]'s most covert operations.>

;From [[Agent Render]]
<The agent fixes his eyes on you and nods.>

I have an urgent letter for you, hero. Something has happened in the Keep. 

I recommend you see to its contents promptly. 

And keep this to yourself, hero.
