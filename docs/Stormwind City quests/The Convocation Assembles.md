==Objectives==
Speak to [[Maethra Slagheart]] in [[Deadwind Pass]].

==Description==
Representatives from the faiths of both [[Alliance]] and [[Horde]] have agreed to meet and unite the shards. There is a chapel next to the ruined tower of [[Karazhan]] which will serve as neutral ground.

Your ring contains the last shard. When you arrive, the convocation may commence.

==Completion==
