==Objectives==
Bring the Perenolde Tiara to [[Remington Ridgewell]] in [[Stormwind City|Stormwind]]. 
*[[Perenolde Tiara]]

==Description==
The Perenolde Tiara is an old heirloom of the [[Perenolde family|Perenolde]] "nobles" - the ruling family of [[Alterac (kingdom)|Alterac]] before that kingdom fell to ruin. It is said to have been crafted by master jewelers in [[Ironforge]], and fitted with the finest of emeralds.

When Alterac was destroyed the Tiara was lost, but there are rumors that an [[Ogre mage|Ogre Mage]], [[Grel'borg the Miser]], found the tiara and now wanders the [[Ruins of Alterac]] searching for more treasures.

We would have you acquire the tiara for us.
