'''Urgent Care''' is an optional breadcrumb leading to the [[Allied Races: Mechagnome]] recruitment quest chain.

==Objectives==
Find [[Kelsey Steelspark]] in [[Boralus Harbor]].

==Description==
Excellent timing, <name>.

Kelsey Steelspark has been sending communications to the capital cities looking for aid.

[[Gelbin Mekkatorque|Mekkatorque's]] condition is quite dire and time is of the essence.

Hurry to Boralus Harbor, she will be watching for you.
