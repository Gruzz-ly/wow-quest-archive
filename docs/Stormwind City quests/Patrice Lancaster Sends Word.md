==Objectives==
Speak to [[Patrice Lancaster]] at the [[Training Hall]] in [[Stormwind]].

==Description==
I have an urgent message for you from Stormwind, <name>. It seems that Speak to{{sic}} Patrice Lancaster has asked to speak with you at the Training Hall in Stormwind's [[Old Town]] district. The messenger did not say what the matter concerned, but that you should travel to Stormwind with all haste.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 [[reputation]] with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]

==Completion==
It heartens me to see that you received my message and responded so quickly.

==Notes==
*This quest is only available to [[human (playable)|human]], [[dwarf (playable)|dwarf]], and [[draenei (playable)|draenei]] priests. Versions for [[Night elf (playable)|night elves]] and [[Gnome (playable)|gnomes]] listed below.
**Gnomes and [[Mechagnome (playable)|mechagnomes]] also have access to this quest, while being offered the gnome version. Only one can be accepted.
