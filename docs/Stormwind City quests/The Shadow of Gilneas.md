'''The Shadow of Gilneas''' begins a quest chain for [[Worgen (playable)|worgen]] characters to obtain their [[heritage armor]]. It can be picked up just outside the [[Stormwind Embassy]] within [[Stormwind City]] after reaching level {{Level|Bfa-max}}.

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Tess Greymane]] at [[Stormwind Keep]].

==Description==
Champion of [[Gilneas (kingdom)|Gilneas]]!

I have a letter for you from Princess Tess Greymane. She requests your presence in Stormwind Keep.

==Rewards==
*{{Abilities|Heritage of Gilneas|id=292047|Unlock the appearances of the Heritage of Gilneas on completion of the Heritage storyline.|icon=achievement_zone_gilneas_02}}

You will also receive:
* {{cost|2|34}}
* 1,800 [[XP]]
