==Objectives==
Speak to {{NPC||Cenarion Emissary Jademoon}} to secure transportation to [[Moonglade]].
*Transportation to Moonglade Secured

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, {{NPC||King Varian Wrynn}}, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to {{NPC||Emissary Windsong}} at [[Nighthaven]] in Moonglade.

Our brothers in the [[Cenarion Circle]] report that {{NPC||Deathwing}}'s minions have launched a destructive offensive against the [[Nordrassil|world tree]] and threaten to burn it to the ground!

Speak with Cenarion Emissary Jademoon in [[Stormwind Keep]] to gain entry into Moonglade.

For the glory and honor of the Alliance! Fight on, <brother/sister>!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1,200 [[XP]]

==Completion==
We knew that the Alliance would send help and not a moment too soon!

==Notes==
The beginning of the [[Mount Hyjal quests|Mount Hyjal]] storyline! The Hero's Call Board can be found between the bank and auction house in the [[Trade District]] of [[Stormwind]]. Jademoon is in the garden at Stormwind Keep. She stands just in front of a portal that looks upon Nordrassil.

Talk to her:
:Are you here to help the cause? [[Mount Hyjal]] burns!

:{{gossip|Could you please send me to Moonglade, Emissary?}}

The teleport drops players off in the southwest corner of Nighthaven. There, Windsong is standing by with {{NPC||Aronus}} hovering just above the [[moonwell]].
