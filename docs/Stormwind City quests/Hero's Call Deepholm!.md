==Description==
By order of his royal highness, {{NPC||Varian Wrynn||King Varian Wrynn}}, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Naraat the Earthspeaker at the Eastern Earthshrine in Stormwind City.

The [[Earthen Ring]] requires our aid! They report that the [[elemental plane]] of [[Deepholm]] threatens to overtake our world and destroy all life on [[Azeroth]]!

The Eastern Earthshrine can be found directly northwest of [[Stormwind Keep]].

For the glory and honor of the Alliance! Fight on, <brother/sister>!
