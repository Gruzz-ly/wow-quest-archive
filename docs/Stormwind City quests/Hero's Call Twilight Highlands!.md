==Objectives==
Speak with {{NPC||Grand Admiral Jes-Tereth}} at [[Stormwind Keep]] in [[Stormwind]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, King Anduin Wrynn, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to the throne room in Stormwind Keep.

King Wrynn requires a trustworthy and proven hero for a mission of the utmost importance.

Stormwind Keep can be found in the eastern portion of Stormwind City.

Anyone willing may report to Grand Admiral Jes-Tereth for briefing. For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
