'''Summons to Lord Greymane''' begins the Alliance portion of the [[Gilneas Reclamation]] storyline in the [[Seeds of Renewal campaign]], added in [[patch 10.2.5]].

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Genn Greymane]] at [[Stormwind Harbor]].
* Meet with Genn Greymane at Stormwind Harbor

==Description==
Greetings, 

Lord Greymane has requested your presence at Stormwind Harbor to assist him with a matter regarding [[Gilneas]].

That is all I am able to say.

Good luck, <name>.
