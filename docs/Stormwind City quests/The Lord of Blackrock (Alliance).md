The Lord of Blackrock is the first quest in the '''The Lord of Blackrock''' quest chain.

==Objectives==
Return the Head of Nefarian to [[Grand Admiral Jes-Tereth]] in [[Stormwind City|Stormwind]].
*[[Head of Nefarian]] (provided)

==Description==
The old feeling returns. The welcome pangs of victory reverberate throughout your being. Before you lies the monolithic, battle scarred head of Nefarian. Bring it to the Grand Admiral on the King's behalf, champion.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 14,180 [[XP]]
* +500 reputation with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]
* +500 reputation with [[Ironforge (faction)|Ironforge]]
* +500 reputation with [[Darnassus (faction)|Darnassus]]
* +500 reputation with [[Gnomeregan (faction)|Gnomeregan]]
* +500 reputation with [[Exodar (faction)|Exodar]]

==Progress==
Inconceivable!

==Completion==
You need not be told the meaning of what you have done, <name>. This day will be recorded in the annals of history. A victory the likes of which has not been known to the Alliance for years - if ever!
