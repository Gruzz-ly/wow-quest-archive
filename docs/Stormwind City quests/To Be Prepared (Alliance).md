===Buffs===
Several unique beneficial effects are available from this quest. They last an hour, remain active in non-{{expac|Legion}} content, and can be re-acquired repeatedly as long as the quest is not completed or abandoned.
* [[Polished Armor]] - With a solid percentage-based boost to armor, this is the only buff signficant at high levels.
* [[Well Fed (Legion Invasion)]] - This bonus is fairly small and does not scale to the [[level cap]].
* [[Holy Enchantment]] - Testing has suggested the bonus damage effect may not trigger correctly in modern patches.

The [[Blessed Bandage]] buff is restricted to low levels, only lasts 5 minutes, and cannot be used in most zones. Its crates do not respawn either. Therefore, it is only relevant when actually playing through this questline as intended.
