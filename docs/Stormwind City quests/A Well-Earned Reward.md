==Objectives==
Go to [[Shadowfang Keep]] and obtain the Godfrey's Crystal Scope from [[Lord Godfrey (tactics)|Lord Godfrey]], 5 bundles of Moontouched Wood, and 5 Deathless Sinew.
*[[Godfrey's Crystal Scope]]
*[[Moontouched Wood]] (5)
*[[Deathless Sinew]] (5)

==Description==
I am not the only one impressed by your progress <name>. Your mastery of [[bow]] and [[pet]] and your dedication to the [[Alliance]] have reached the ears of [[Varian Wrynn|King Wrynn]].

In recognition of your service, the king has commissioned the royal bowyer to craft a special weapon.

The cursed Shadowfang Keep in [[Silverpine Forest]] is home to the unique materials needed for your weapon. Travel there and obtain the scope from [[Lord Godfrey]]'s gun, a supply of moontouched wood, and sinew from the [[ghoul]]s and [[geist]]s infesting the keep.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 2,300 [[XP]]
* +500 reputation with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]
*[[Crossbow of the Crown]]

==Progress==
Did you bring the materials I'll need for your weapon?

==Completion==
Who knew that cursed old keep hid such valuable items? I'll be honored to craft them into a fine weapon for you.
