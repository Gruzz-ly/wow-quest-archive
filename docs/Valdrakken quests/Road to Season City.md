==Objectives==
Deliver Yusa's Hearty Stews and Grand Banquets of the Kalu'ak to [[Phiary]] in [[Loamm]].
*[[Yusa's Hearty Stew]] (40)
*[[Grand Banquet of the Kalu'ak]] (5)
*Deliver the items to Phiary in Loamm

==Description==
The kith of Loamm request the skills of a culinary chef! We [[niffen]] are very proud of our cuisine, and wish to sample some from the surface!

One of our best cooks, Mister Phiary, has heard of the potent spices found in the food of the [[Maruuk Centaur]], and the complex flavors found in the food of the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]], and wishes to try these for himself!

Please deliver these to Loamm as soon as you can, won't you?
