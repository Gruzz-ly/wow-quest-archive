'''Innovating the Engine''' concludes the "The Spark of Ingenuity" quest chain to acquire additional {{item|Bottled Essence|s}} to create additional {{item|Spark of Ingenuity|Sparks of Ingenuity}}, a very powerful reagent used to craft end-game armor and weapons.

This quest chain was originally on a one or two-week time-gate, but now can be completed without any forced waiting. 

==Objectives==
Insert the newly attuned Power Core into the [[Engine of Innovation]].
*Power Core Replaced

==Description==
[[Tyr]] believed that the Engine of Innovation would be necessary for the battles to come. 

You have proven yourself worthy and overcome all the challenges set before you. You may now unlock Engine's full potential.
