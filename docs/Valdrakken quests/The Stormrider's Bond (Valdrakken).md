'''The Stormrider's Bond''' is the starting quest that leads to additional color sets of the [[Stormrider's Attire]] and the upgraded variants. This quest requires having purchased the [[World of Warcraft: The War Within Heroic Edition|Heroic Edition]] or [[World of Warcraft: The War Within Epic Edition|Epic Edition]] of {{g|tww}}.

==Objectives==
Collect 50 [[Storm Spirit]]s by slaying powerful creatures of the Dragon Isles.
*Collect Storm Spirits from creatures of the [[Dragon Isles]] (50)

==Description==
[[Stormrook (Valdrakken)|This beauty]] is no ordinary [[gryphon]]. It's got the storm within it. I've been trying all day tae calm it down.

It reminds me of an ol' [[Wildhammer clan|Wildhammer]] myth. [[Stormrook]]s, they were called. Only able to be bonded by [[gryphon rider]]s of great renown.

If we can show ye got the spirit of the storm, maybe it'll open up tae ye. Try proving yerself in battle.
