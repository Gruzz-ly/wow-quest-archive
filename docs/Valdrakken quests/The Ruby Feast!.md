'''The Ruby Feast!''' unlocks various chef-provided foods at the [[Ruby Feast]] in northeastern [[Thaldraszus]]. Sampling all of the food and drink will award the achievement [[Great Gourmand of The Ruby Feast]].

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Rumiastrasza|Chefs Rumiastrsza]] and [[Chef Morsel|Morsel]] at [[Ruby Feast|The Ruby Feast]].

==Description==
Attention to all aspiring culinarians!

Does your heart desire the ultimate flavors of this world? Do you seek out opportunities for grand exploration? Are you willing to risk life and limb for complete strangers?

If this is you, come speak with the eminently distinguished, world-renowned chef duo, Rumiastrasza and Morsel! Currently hosting The Ruby Feast in the city of [[Valdrakken]]!!
