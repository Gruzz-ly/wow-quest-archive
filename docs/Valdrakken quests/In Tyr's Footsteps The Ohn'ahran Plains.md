'''In Tyr's Footsteps: The Ohn'ahran Plains''' begins the second portion of the "The Spark of Ingenuity" quest line. This quest was originally unlocked the week of 2022-12-13.

==Objectives==
Find the Second Challenge of Tyr in the [[Ohn'ahran Plains]].

==Description==
Though you have done well in your first challenge, do not think you shall surpass all so easily. [[Tyr|Keeper Tyr]] meant the [[Engine of Innovation]] to be only for those worthy enough to use it wisely. 

If you are undeterred, the next challenge shall be found in the Ohn'ahran Plains. I look forward to testing your capabilities, adventurer.
