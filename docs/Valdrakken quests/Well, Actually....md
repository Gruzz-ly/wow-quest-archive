==Description==
As expected, our dear [[blue whelp]] is quite the little "bookwyrm". I knew many hatchlings just like him in [[Azsuna|Aszuna]]{{sic}}, though we only had a small collection of scrolls.

While there are books aplenty here in Valdrakken, whelps need to get up and moving! Why don't you play some tag?
