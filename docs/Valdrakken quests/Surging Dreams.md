'''Surging Dreams''' breadcrumbs players to the [[Dreamsurge]] event. Each week a different open-world [[Dragon Isles]] zone will be affected.

==Objectives==
Meet the [[druid]]s investigating the [[Dreamsurge]].

==Description==
Change is coming, <name>. Those attuned to the [[Emerald Dream]] can feel it on the wind.

We need your aid in uncovering what this change entails.

Let us reconvene in the affected area and formulate a plan.
