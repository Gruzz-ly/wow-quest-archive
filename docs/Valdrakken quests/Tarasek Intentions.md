==Objectives==
Gather 15 Draconic Scrolls from the [[tarasek]] of the [[Dragon Isles]] for [[Dothenos]] of the [[Valdrakken Accord]].
*[[Draconic Scroll]] (15)

==Description==
When the tarasek accepted power from the [[Primalists]], they stole many ancient scrolls and tomes to combine with their newfound [[magic]]. We need help getting these scrolls back in our possession.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,050 [[XP]]
* +100 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]
*[[Scribe's Glyphs]]

==Progress==
Have you retrieved any of the scrolls?

==Completion==
