==Objectives==
Gather 10 Large Tools from the [[djaradin]] of the [[Dragon Isles]] for [[Dhurrel]] of the [[Maruuk Centaurs]].
*[[Large Tool]] (10)

==Description==
The djaradin have some mighty large tools and weaponry. We can easily find a use for such tools, even if only to smelt them down into more tools for ourselves.

Bring back some djaradin tools for us to study and use.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,050 [[XP]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]
* +25 reputation with [[Valdrakken Accord]]
*[[Blacksmith's Writ]]

==Progress==
Did you find the djaradin and collect some of their tools?

==Completion==
The blacksmiths have their work cut out for them. Thank you for collecting these tools.
