'''The Preservationists''' is an optional breadcrumb quest to the [[Secrets of Azeroth]] event. Find any [[Holiday Enthusiast (Valdrakken)|Holiday Enthusiast]] in [[Valdrakken]] to begin.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Preservationist Kathos]] at the [[Roasted Ram]] in Valdrakken.

==Description==
Welcome, [[adventurer]]. Have you heard the news?

There's been a rash of artifact thefts as rumors of treasures and secret locations are being exposed.

If you'd like to learn more, seek out Preservationist Kathos. Your skills at deciphering secrets might help reveal who's behind this chaos.

==Completion==
A new recruit? Splendid! There's much work to be done, and many secrets to uncover.

Ready to get started?
