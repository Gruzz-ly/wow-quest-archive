'''Renown of the Dragon Isles''' begins the max-level portion of the [[Dragonflight campaign]], teaching players about the endgame systems now available to them. Additional questlines continue as part of the [[Friend of the Dragon Isles]] renown campaigns.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Therazal]] within [[Valdrakken]].

==Description==
There is still much that must be done to aid the [[Dragon Isles]].

I would ask you speak with Therazal. He has much information regarding who needs help within this land.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|76|20}}
* 300 [[XP]]
* +4 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]

==Completion==
Good to see you, <name>. 

There are many sources of power within the Dragon Isles, as long as you know where to look!
