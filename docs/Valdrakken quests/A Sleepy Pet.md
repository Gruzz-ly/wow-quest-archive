==Objectives==
Obtain a Bear Cub pet for [[Posidriss]].
*[[Lysindra]] visited
*[[Sleepy Bear]] awakened
*Sleepy Bear given to [[Lasztak]]

==Description==
Posidriss's sleeping troubles are still causing them many problems. This is preventing them from developing their connection to [[Emerald Dream|the Dream]].

[[Bear]]s hibernate, bears dream, bear cubs are soft, warm and cuddly. Some of the cubs in the [[Dragon Isles]] are even connected to the Dream. Can you fetch one from Lysindra for Posidriss?
