'''Neltharion's Legacy''' begins the [[evoker]]-only questline to unlock the legendary [[Nasz'uro, the Unbound Legacy]]. The starting item, [[Cracked Titan Gem]], drops from [[Scalecommander Sarkareth (tactics)|Scalecommander Sarkareth]], the final boss of [[Aberrus, the Shadowed Crucible]], on all difficulties, with a higher drop rate on higher difficulties.

==Objectives==
Meet [[Nozdormu]] in [[Valdrakken]].

==Description==
<As you stare into the cracked titan gem, a voice echoes through your mind.>

<name>! This is Nozdormu. 

The time to redeem the legacy of your creator, [[Neltharion]], is at hand. 

You hold a vital piece to [[Oathbinder|the artifact]] Neltharion once used to control you and all of the [[dracthyr]].

Meet me in Valdrakken. There is much to discuss.
