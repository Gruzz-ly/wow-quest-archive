==Objectives==
Collect 50 Sparks of Life in The [[Forbidden Reach]], while in [[War Mode]].
*[[Spark of Life (quest item)]] (50)

==Description==
The Forbidden Reach is enveloped with the vibrancy of the [[Dragon Isles]].

Go explore and capture sparks, but stay weary for any combatants you may encounter.

==Rewards==

* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]

==Progress==
Right out that door. Can't miss it.

==Completion==
The Accord thanks you, no doubt.

==Notes==
The world quests and envoy quests in the zone will provide 10 sparks while killing rares will provide 8 sparks each.

Killing rares while in a [[raid group]] will not drop sparks.
