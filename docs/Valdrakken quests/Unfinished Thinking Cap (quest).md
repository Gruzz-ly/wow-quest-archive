'''Unfinished Thinking Cap''' is the second step of the second day of the [[Secrets of Azeroth]] event, started by making an [[Unfinished Thinking Cap]] from a [[Thought Calculating Apparatus]], some [[Downy Helmet Liner]] and a set of [[Crystal Ocular Lenses]].

==Objectives==
Take the Unfinished Thinking Cap to [[Fangli Hoot]] in [[Valdrakken]].
*[[Unfinished Thinking Cap]] {{co|26.7|53.9|Valdrakken}}

==Description==
<Take the Unfinished Thinking Cap to Fangli Hoot near the Barber Shop in Valdrakken.>

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Tricked-Out Thinking Cap]]

==Progress==
<Fangli looks at you, but doesn't speak.>

==Completion==
<Fangli points to your head, then to hers. She gestures for your Unfinished Thinking Cap.

She uses an arclight spanner to adjust some wires and lenses, then flips a switch. The Thinking Cap whirs to life.

Fangli hands it back to you.>

==Notes==
Completing this quest is the Tricked-Out Thinking Cap objective of [[Tools of the Trade (achievement)]].

Use the [[Tricked-Out Thinking Cap]] to add the toy to the [[Toy Box]], then relog to get the toy to show up. Use the toy, then speak with Fangli again to start [[The Tricked-Out Thinking Cap]].
