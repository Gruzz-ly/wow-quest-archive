==Objectives==
Gather 20 Tusk Marrow from the [[Mammoth]]s of the [[Dragon Isles]] for [[Dhurrel]] of the [[Maruuk Centaur Clan]].
*[[Tusk Marrow]] (20)

==Description==
Creating new and unusual brews is one of our many specialties. While we do enjoy experimenting with new and fascinating ingredients, we have a few common ingredients that we keep stock of.

One of these ingredients is the marrow from mammoth tusks. We have run short on marrow and would appreciate your help in collecting more for our alchemists.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,050 [[XP]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]
* +25 reputation with [[Valdrakken Accord]]
*[[Alchemy Notes]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
