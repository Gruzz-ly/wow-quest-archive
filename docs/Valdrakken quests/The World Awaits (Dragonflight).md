==Objectives==
Complete 10 World Quests in the [[Dragon Isles]].
* World quests completed (10)

==Description==
You know what's a sure sign of toughness? Helping out the community in the Dragon Isles!

There's plenty of problems to solve here. Show me how tough you are by helping out!

==Rewards==
You will be able to choose one of these rewards:
*+2500 reputation for [[Dragonscale Expedition]] from [[Dragonscale Expedition Commendation]] (or [[Dragonscale Expedition Insignia#2500]] if max renown)
*+2500 reputation for [[Iskaara Tuskarr]] from [[Iskaara Tuskarr Commendation]] (or [[Iskaara Tuskarr Insignia#2500]] if max renown)
*+2500 reputation for [[Maruuk Centaur]] from [[Maruuk Centaur Commendation]] (or [[Maruuk Centaur Insignia#2500]] if max renown)
*+2500 reputation for [[Valdrakken Accord]] from [[Valdrakken Accord Commendation]] (or [[Valdrakken Accord Insignia#2500]] if max renown)
*+2500 reputation for [[Loamm Niffen]] from [[Loamm Niffen Commendation]] (or [[Loamm Niffen Insignia#2500]] if max renown)
*+2500 reputation for [[Dream Wardens]] from [[Dream Wardens Commendation]] (or [[Dream Wardens Insignia#2500]] if max renown)
