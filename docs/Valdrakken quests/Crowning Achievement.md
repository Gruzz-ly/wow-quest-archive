'''Crowning Achievement''' becomes available after reaching Renown 22 with the [[Valdrakken Accord]]. This quest exists to direct the player to [[Provisioner Thom]], who sells item level 389 versions of [[Expedition Researcher's Hood]], [[Expedition Mercenary's Helm]], [[Field Scout's Helmet]], and [[Expedition Guard's Helm]].

==Completion==
My, my, you're certainly making a name for yourself. At least, that's what the boss tells us.

As for me, I spend my days making helms. Finest in the all the Isles! Take a look. My prices are fair.

Well... fair enough, at least.
