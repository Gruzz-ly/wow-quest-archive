==Objectives==
Gather Hochenblume for the Emerald Hatchling. Hochenblume can be looted from plant-based creatures, gathered with the [[Herbalism]] skill, or bought from the [[auction house]].
*[[Hochenblume]] [[File:ReagentQuality-silver.png]] (5)
*[[Emerald Hatchling]] fed

==Description==
Every [[green dragon]] loves a leafy, crunchy snack. The plants that grow with [[Emerald Dream|Dream]] exposure are densely packed with nutrients, keeping the greens healthy and content.

Hochenblume flowers are a sweet, delightful treat for young green dragons, and contain all the vitamins that a growing hatchling needs.
