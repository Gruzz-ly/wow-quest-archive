'''Bar Tab Barrel''' follows in the vein of other holiday world-tour quests. Find each of the twelve [[Bar Tab Barrel]] pony kegs in inns across the [[Dragon Isles]] and ten on [[Khaz Algar]] for 10x [[Brewfest Prize Token]] per barrel.

==Completion==
Donate to taverns across <the [[Dragon Isles]]/[[Khaz Algar]]> to cover the tab!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*10x [[Brewfest Prize Token]]

==Notes==
Bar Tab Barrels are found at:
{{:Bar Tab Barrel}}

After completing the quest, the nearby [[Brewfest Celebrant]]s cheer.
