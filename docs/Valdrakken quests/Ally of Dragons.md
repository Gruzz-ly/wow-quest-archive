'''Ally of Dragons''' starts the "Silver Purpose" questline, beginning an expansion-spanning storyline surrounding [[Tyr]]. [[Herald Flaps]] will fly over and offer this quest upon reaching Renown level 12 with the [[Valdrakken Accord]].

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Alexstrasza]] at the [[Seat of the Aspects]] in [[Valdrakken]].

==Description==
Your aid to the [[Dragon Isles]] has not gone unnoticed, <name>.

The Life-binder herself wishes to show you something as a reward for your service to dragonkind.

Go and speak to her at the top of the tower. All the [[Dragon Aspects|Aspects]] await you.

==Rewards==

* {{cost|2|80|90}}
* 1,200 [[XP]]
