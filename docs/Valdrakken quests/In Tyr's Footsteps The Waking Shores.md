'''In Tyr&#39;s Footsteps: The Waking Shores''' begins the third portion of the "The Spark of Ingenuity" quest line, awarding another [[Bottled Essence]]. This quest was originally unlocked the week of 2023-01-03.

==Objectives==
Find the Third Challenge of Tyr in the [[Waking Shores]].

==Description==
You have proven yourself well so far, and though I have great faith you you are worthy of [[Engine of Innovation|the Engine]] I still must challenge you as is my designation. 

Go to the Waking Shores and find my statue and you shall receive further instructions.
