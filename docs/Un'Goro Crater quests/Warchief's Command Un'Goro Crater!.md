==Objectives==
Report to [[Williden Marshal]] at [[Marshal's Stand]] in [[Un'Goro Crater]]. Optionally, grab a flight there from Doras in Orgrimmar.
* Flight from Doras in Orgrimmar
* Speak with Williden Marshal at Marshal's Stand

==Description==
All able bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report Williden Marshal at Marshal's Stand. You are to provide aid to the [[Marshal Expeditions]]' personnel as they uncover the secrets of Un'Goro Crater. The Horde may be able to find a use for the unique beasts and crystals populating the crater.

You can reach Un'Goro Crater by taking the road from western [[Tanaris]] down into the crater and toward Marshal's Stand.

Destiny calls!
