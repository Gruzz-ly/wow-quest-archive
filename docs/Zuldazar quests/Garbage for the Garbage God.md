==Objectives==

==Description==
[[Adventurer|Richmon]], you found a thing I been looking for for a long time. Bring it to me in the Port of Zandalar, and I will grant you my favor.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|23|40|}} (at level 120)

==Completion==
I remember da orphan who first carved dis offering. I remember da tears and desperation and da hope for somethin' to save dem. I remember tastin' da power in dat hope.

And I remember da look of fear on da cruel richmon who made da child cry. "Never again" not just be hollow words with [[Jani]].

==Notes==
To unlock this quest, you need to have earned the achievement [[Get Hek'd]]. Once you have it, you'll be able to see a [[Lost Zandalari Relic]] treasure chest in [[Dazar'alor]] (past the [[Terrace of Crafters]]).{{co|46.6|29.8|Dazar'alor}} Loot the chest for the [[Clump of Broken Loa Idols]] and take it to the [[Shred-Master Mk1]].
