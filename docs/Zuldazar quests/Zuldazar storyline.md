===The Slough===

===Nesingwary's Trek===
The chain be done from the start, while the breadcrumb to it unlocks after "The Zanchuli Council" chapter.
{{Nesingwary's Trek}}

===Warbeast Kraal===
The quest chain itself can be done from the start, while the breadcrumb to it unlocks after "Port of Zandalar" chapter.

This is a very long quest chain that eventually awards the achievement [[How to Train Your Direhorn (achievement)]] and the mount [[Child of Torcali]].
{{Warbeast Kraal}}
After doing the dailies for 15 days, the questline resumes:
{{Torcali's Blessing}}
{{Child of Torcali}}

===Village in the Vines===
Breadcrumb unlocks after "Warport Rastari".
{{Village in the Vines}}

===Isle of Fangs===
The quest chain itself can be done after "Warport Rastari", while the breadcrumb to it unlocks after "Among the People".
{{Isle of Fangs}}

===Talanji's Rebuke===
Breadcrumb unlocks after "Warport Rastari".
{{Talanji's Rebuke}}

===Tortollan===
Consists of three separate chains (Horde-only at Port of Zandalar and neutral at Dreadpearl Shallows and Atal'Gral). The quests are all initially available, but the breadcrumb from Port of Zandalar to Dreadpearl Shallows does not unlock until after "Warport Rastari".
{{Tortollan}}

===Pterrodax Training===
