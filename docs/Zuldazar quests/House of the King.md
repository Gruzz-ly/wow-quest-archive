==Description==
I hear Rezan's call. I will answer, but first you must help me.

Our [[loa]] give us many boons, and we repay dem in worship. Offerings, sacrifices, and idols are our tools to earn favor, but dey are much more to our loa.

Dey are sources of strength.

[[Zul]]'s [[Atal'zul|dogs]] have begun to corrupt de great golden statues dedicated to Rezan. Even now, I can feel his power dwindling.

Stop dem. Cleanse de idols to de great hunter. Teach dem to respect de house of de king.
