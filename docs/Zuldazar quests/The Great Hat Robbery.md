==Description==
Along this road the [[merchant]]s ride. Taking dere wares from one place to another and lining them own pockets.

One Big Mon named [[Tik'su]] be the worst of all. He charge arms and legs outright and it even take [[troll]]s some time to regrow them parts.

His biggest pride? His hat. 

Go be my messenger, Rich-mon. Scare his burdened beast and knock off Tik'su. Then bring me his hat.
