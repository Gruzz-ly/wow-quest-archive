==Objectives==
Travel on [[Scarbeak]] to [[Dazar'alor]].
*Ride Scarbeak to King [[Rastakhan]]'s [[Golden Throne|throne]]
*Speak with [[Baine Bloodhoof]] in The [[Great Seal]].

==Description==
King Rastakhan's life is in grave danger. [[Zul]] is far more powerful than he appears.

We do not have much time. You have to warn de king before Zul can act.

[[Habutu]] and [[Zolani]] can take you dere. Now go!
