==Objectives==
Speak with [[Valeera Sanguinar]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
The leaders of the [[Horde]] are gathering together in Orgrimmar to nominate a new [[Warchief]] of the Horde.

As a champion of the Horde, your presence at the meeting has been requested. The gathering will be held at the [[Orgrimmar Embassy|embassy]] in Orgrimmar.

I will be going on ahead. Come speak with me when you arrive in Orgrimmar, I'll be waiting with the others.

Make haste, champion. The future of the Horde is about to be decided...
