'''Azerite Mining''' is a [[world quest]] occuring either in a cave entered from [[Xibala (subzone)|Xibala]] or in [[Verdant Slope]] of [[Zuldazar]].

==Objectives==
*Azerite recovered

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Magni Bronzebeard]]|I can sense Azerite nearby... and those who would seek tae abuse it for their own ends. Ye've got tae stop 'em!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Champions of Azeroth]]
* +75 reputation with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
* +75 reputation with [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*An [[Azerite (resource)]] item
*[[War Resources]]
*[[Azerite Armor Cache]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Magni Broznebeard|The Heart of Azeroth is stronger now, thanks tae ye. We're one step closer tae curing Azeroth of her wounds.}}

==Notes==
*Mine [[Azerite Node]]s and [[Azerite Chunk]]s, and kill [[Charged Sentinel]]s, [[Kao-Tien Subjugator]]s, [[Stormbound Conqueror]]s, [[Kao-Tien Marauder]]s, and [[Ku'shif the Cruel]] until you fill the bar to 100%.
