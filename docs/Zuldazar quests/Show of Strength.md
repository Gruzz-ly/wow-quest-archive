==Objectives==
Kill [[Jao-Ti the Thunderous]] and [[Subjugator Zheng]].
* Jao-Ti the Thunderous slain
* Subjugator Zheng slain
*Meet [[Kaza'jin the Wavebinder|Kaza'jin]] by the warship in [[Warport Rastari]].

==Description==
Their leader's time is coming soon now, but we need to dispose of his [[lieutenant]]s first. Our momentum will be lost if dey reform ranks.

Jao-Ti is de first. Legend has it [[Lei Shen]] himself said he showed a great connection to de storms. He is leading de [[mogu]]'s stormbinders.

De other is Subjugator Zheng, a [[slaver]] dat would make de [[Kul Tiran]]s blush. You have never seen depravity like his.

Kill dem both, and make dem suffer. De mogu are no strangers to war, but neither are we.
