==Objectives==
Take Roo'li's remains to Torcali.
* Collect [[Roo'li]]'s Remains
* Speak to [[Torcali]]

==Description==
I was going to be a [[Dinomancer]]. Roo'li was going to grow with me as I learned.

<[[Koo'li]] shakes her head.>

Dat future is gone now. Please, take him. Find a proper place to bury him.

I... can't. De shame is too great.
