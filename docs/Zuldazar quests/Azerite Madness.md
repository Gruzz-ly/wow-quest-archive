'''Azerite Madness''' is a [[world quest]] that can appear in any zone on [[Kul Tiras]] and [[Zandalar]].

==Objectives==
*Absorb Azerite from defeated [[Azerite elemental|elementals]]

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Magni Bronzebeard]]|Some [[earth elemental]]s have been infused with [[:Azerite]] and are goin' crazy. Put them down and absorb the Azerite!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[Champions of Azeroth]]

Depending on zone and player faction, you will also receive:
*{{Alliance}} +75 reputation with [[Order of Embers]], [[Proudmoore Admiralty]], [[Storm's Wake]], or [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
*{{Horde}} +75 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]], [[Voldunai]], [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]], or [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]

You will also receive one of the following:
*[[File:Gold.png]] [[Gold]]
*An [[Azerite (resource)]] item
*[[War Resources]]
*[[Azerite Armor Cache]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Magni Bronzebeard|It's a shame that Azerite is drivin' the elements out of control. More reason for us tae be puttin' an end tae this.}}

==Notes==
*Slay [[Infused Bedrock]]s, [[Azerite-Infused Elemental]]s, [[Infused Crag]]s, and [[Minor Azerite Infused Elemental]]s and use the [[Heart of Azeroth]] on them until you fill the bar to 100%.

;Locations
*[[Plains of Tojek]], [[Nazmir]]
*[[Blistering Wastes]] or the southwestern part, [[Vol'dun]]
* A cave entered from [[Xibala (subzone)|Xibala]] or [[Verdant Slope]], [[Zuldazar]]
*[[Hunter's Run]] or [[Arom's Crossing]], [[Drustvar]]
*[[Windshorn Depths]] or the southeastern mountains, [[Stormsong Valley]]
*[[Winterchill Mine]] or the [[Umber Shore]], [[Tiragarde Sound]]
