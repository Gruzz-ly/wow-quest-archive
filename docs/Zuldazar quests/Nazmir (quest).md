==Objectives==
Speak with [[Talanji|Princess Talanji]].

==Description==
The [[blood troll]]s within the cursed swamp [[Nazmir]] threaten to destroy all of [[Zuldazar]]. 

Princess Talanji wants the [[Horde]]'s help in an [[Talanji's Expedition|expedition]] to strike at the heart of the blood trolls and end their threat to Zuldazar.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|1|94|}} - {{cost|2|34|}}
* 1,650 - 1,800 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]]

==Completion==
The swamp of Nazmir represents the largest threat to my people. I am glad you chose to help me in this.

==Notes==
Interact with the [[Scouting Map]] and choose Nazmir.

"Journey with Princess Talanji to the dark swamps of Nazmir to put an end to the threat of the blood trolls."

Before ''Shadowlands'', it was preceded by [[We Need Each Other]].
