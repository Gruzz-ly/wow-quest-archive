==Objectives==
Speak with [[Princess Talanji]].

==Description==
[[King Rastakhan]] is an ancient ruler who is not quick to trust new allies. We must earn his trust if we are to earn his fleet.

[[Zuldazar]] is the seat of power for the [[Zandalari Empire|Zandalar empire]] and a good place to start in winning over these potential allies.

* 1,650 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]]

==Completion==
