==Description==
You have done a wonderful thing, <name>. With de destruction of Gloomtail, Maka'fon is no longer bothered by de presence of de razorwings. It seems dat Gloomtail destroyed her eggs some time ago.

When Ben'jin senses dat, he will approach and nature will take over.
