==Description==
You are no [[Zandalari]], but your [[ghost|spirit]] burns with a passion I have not seen in many years.

I am de wavebinder. It is through me dat de [[loa]] bless our ships. I will not see my home tainted by dese talking rocks.

Gather my bijous, my grimoire, and some of our fallen I set aside for just such an occasion.

De brutes think dey have won. Dey will answer to [[Bwonsamdi]] soon enough.
