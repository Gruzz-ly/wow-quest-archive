==Objectives==
Meet [[Jastor Gallywix|Gallywix]] at [[Bilgewater Bonanza]].

==Description==
Gallywix has a plan to deal with the [[Alliance]] and has requested your help. It involves the [[:Azerite]] you reclaimed.

Speak with him down at Bilgewater Bonanza, and do whatever you can to help him... when it comes to the interest of the [[Horde]].

Oh, and make sure Gallywix stays alive. The Alliance already tried to kill him.
