'''Forgotten Village''' is a [[bonus objective]] offered to players in the vicinity of [[Dredhollow]] after completing the breadcrumb quest [[Dredhollow (quest)]] during the [[Revendreth storyline]]. Players will have ample opportunity to kill targets on the campaign quests [[Breaking the Hopebreakers]], [[They Won't Know What Hit Them]], and [[Rebel Reinforcements]].

==Objectives==
* [[Dredhollow]] threats eliminated (0/100%)
