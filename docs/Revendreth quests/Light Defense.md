==Rewards==

* 9,450 [[XP]]

*[[Handcrafted Mirror Repair Kit]]

*[[Overflowing Ember Mirror]]
*[[Anima]]
*A [[Conduit]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|General Draven|Sinfall will remain safe thanks to your efforts.}}

==Notes==
At the [[Abandoned Purlieu]], click on the [[Ornate Mirror]].
:{{Text|bossemote|Use your movement keybinds to redirect the mirror's light beam.}}
You gain a [[vehicle bar]] with the following buttons. As the text states, your movement keys will also work; for most players, this will be WASD.
*{{Abilities|Left|id=334374|icon=misc_arrowleft|notes=Moves the beam left.}}
*{{Abilities|Forward|id=334474|icon=misc_arrowlup|notes=Moves the beam up.}}
*{{Abilities|Back|id=334375|icon=misc_arrowdown|notes=Moves the beam down.}}
*{{Abilities|Right|id=334373|icon=misc_arrowright|notes=Moves the beam right.}}

[[Carved Trickster]]s will occasionally appear with a warning on the screen:
:{{Text|bossemote|A Carved Trickster has appeared.}}
They'll attempt to reach one of the mirrors on the ground to shine at you, knocking you off of your mirror. Shoot them before they can shoot you.
