==Objectives==
Forge official documents condemning you to the [[Ember Ward]].

* [[Venthyr Calligraphy Set]]
* [[Forged Letter of Condemnation]] {{co|37.9|68.2|Revendreth}}

==Description==
Ember Ward only for bad buds!

==Notes==
Turn around and head south to the quest area. Kill [[Forbidden Dissector]]s, [[Forbidden Researcher]]s, and [[Forbidden Trapper]]s for the [[Venthyr Calligraphy Set]] and look for piles of Forbidden Research Documents to loot [[Blank Parchment (quest item)]].

Head downhill to the [[Forbidden Library]]. Once the quest items have been collected, interact with the [[Venthyr Writing Desk]] to start a Choose-Your-Own-Adventure story where the answers don't matter. Not even a little bit:
