**"Copperworth" — {{wp|Alfred Pennyworth}} from the DC Comics universe.
**"Dobb" — {{wp|Dobby}} from ''Harry Potter''.
**"Higgs" — {{wp|Jonathan Higgins}}, an estate manager in ''Magnum, P.I.''.
**"Jeebs" — {{wp|Jeeves}} from the "Jeeves and Wooster" stories.
**"Riff" and "Raff" — Riff Raff from ''{{wp|The Rocky Horror Picture Show}}''.
**"Rogi" and "Egor" — {{wp|Igor (character)|Igor}}, a name often associated with lab assistants.
**"Smither" — {{wp|Waylon Smithers}} from ''The Simpsons''.
