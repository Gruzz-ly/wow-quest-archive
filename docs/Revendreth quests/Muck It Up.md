==Notes==
At the [[Blistering Bog]], kill Primordial Sludges to loot Primordial Muck. Then toss the muck back into the pools, which will create another Primordial Sludge, a [[Bog Dredger]], or the [[Rare mob|rare]] [[Bog Beast]].
