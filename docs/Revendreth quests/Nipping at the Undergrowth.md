'''Nipping at the Undergrowth''' begins the quest chain to acquire the [[Loyal Gorger]] mount. To be eligible for this quest, players must have acquired the [[Impressionable Gorger Spawn]] from the [[Worldedge Gorger]] in [[Revendreth]]. The impressionable gorger only drops for players who have completed [[The Endmire (quest)]].

After the Impressionable Gorger drops, it will run off to the [[Endmire]], where it offers this quest.

==Objectives==
Collect Endmire Vineroot.
