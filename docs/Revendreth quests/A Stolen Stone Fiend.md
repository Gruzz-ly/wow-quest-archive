==Notes==
Use the quest item and follow the anima orb to find [[Chip (stone fiend)|Chip]] in the [[Old Gate]]{{coords|57.26|39.81|Revendreth}}, then in the [[Redelav District]]{{coords|54.37|38.42|Revendreth}}, and lastly [[Chalice District]]{{coords|52.44|47|Revendreth}}. You then find Chip being attacked held by [[Edgar the Collector]] in the Chalice District{{coords|46.11|48.81|Revendreth}}, who is the final objective.

;On pull
:{{text|say|Edgar the Collector|I won't give up the fiend without a fight!}}

;Upon defeat
:{{text|say|Edgar the Collector|My collection is... incomplete...}}

After killing Edgar, head behind the house. The [[dredger]] [[Penkle]] will give you a key that opens the cage for the battle pet stone fiend [[Dal]]{{coords|45.33|47.84|Revendreth}}.

;Penkle gossip text
:Guess you the boss now? Gonna be needin' this key then.

*The names of the stone fiends, Chip and Dal, are likely a reference to the 20th century cartoon characters {{wp|Chip 'n' Dale}}.
