==Objectives==
* [[Iron Chains|Chains collected]]
* [[Neglected Soul]] retrieved (8)
* [[Ash ghoul|Ash Ghouls]] slain (12)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[The Accuser]]|The [[venthyr]] who were supposed to absolve these [[soul]]s are driven mad by the [[Light]]. Return the neglected souls to my collector.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,450 [[XP]]
* +125 reputation with [[Court of Harvesters]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|The Accuser|Well done. These souls will be absolved in due time.}}

==Notes==
At the [[Scorched Crypt]]{{Co|24|38|Revendreth}} loot the [[Iron Chains]].
:{{Text|say|Collector Marissa|These chains will bind the souls to your grasp. Do not worry, it causes them no harm.}}

Kill [[Blistering Inquisitor]]s, [[Feral Ritualist]]s, and [[Ashen Rampager]]s.

When you retrieve the souls, you get the Shackled Soul debuff. Return them to Marissa.
*{{Text|say|Collector Marissa|The Prince will be pleased with this one, <name>.}}
*{{Text|say|Collector Marissa|Ah there's still plenty of Anima in this one.}}
