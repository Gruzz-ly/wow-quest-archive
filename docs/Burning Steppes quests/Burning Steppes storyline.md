# Burning Steppes storyline

[[File:WorldMap-BurningSteppes-cata.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Burning Steppes]]''' is a neutral level 50-52 zone in northeastern [[Azeroth (continent)|Azeroth]] on the [[Eastern Kingdoms]]. The primary storyline covers the Blackrock army's pending invasion of [[Redridge Mountains]]!

Players are directed to Burning Steppes upon the conclusion of the [[Searing Gorge storyline]] through [[Blackrock Mountain]] to [[Flamestar Post]], just south of the ramp leading into the mountain. 

From there they continue east through the zone, fighting back the [[Blackrock clan]].

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Burning Steppes Quests]].

Upon the conclusion of the Burning Steppes storyline, players are directed to {{NPC||Sharon Boomgetter}}, a neutral goblin in the southeast corner of the zone, to ride the ''[[Bogpaddle Bullet]]'' to [[Bogpaddle]] in northeast [[Swamp of Sorrows storyline|Swamp of Sorrows]].
