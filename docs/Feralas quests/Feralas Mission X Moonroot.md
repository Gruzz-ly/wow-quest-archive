Gather 10 Moonroot in the {{C|Feralas}} [[Nightmare Incursions]] and return to [[Field Captain Arunnel]].

==Description==
While these demons distract us here, the [[Emerald Dream]] is being corrupted into [[Emerald Nightmare|nightmare]]. Quick, travel through the Emerald Dream Portal in [[Dream Bough]] and collect Moonroot from within the Nightmare Incursions. We can harness its magic to reverse the damage. You will need the assistance of an herbalist.
