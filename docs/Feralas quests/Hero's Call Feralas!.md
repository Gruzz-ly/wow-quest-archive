==Description==
By order of his royal highness, King Anduin Wrynn, all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Telaron Windflight at Dreamer's Rest in Feralas.

The Alliance needs your help to overcome our enemies, the [[Horde]] and the [[Grimtotem tribe|Grimtotems]]. You can reach Dreamer's Rest by traveling south from [[Desolace]] into Feralas. 

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
