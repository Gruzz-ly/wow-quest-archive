== Description ==
Edana Hatetalon is the queen of the [[Northspring]] harpies that reside in the ruins to the west. She is pure evil; I have heard that her heart is one of crystal. She is void of any emotion aside from the hate she feels towards all others. You must face Edana and bring me back her dark heart.

==Notes==
You kill harpies in northwest [[Feralas]] until they drop the [[Horn of Hatetalon]]. The horn must be used near the Hatetalon stones, which are in the north-western edge of the ruins. They are two tall conical stones with a path running between them, and lanterns lining the path. The stones don't show a name when you hover over them.{{co|40|9|Feralas (Classic)}}
