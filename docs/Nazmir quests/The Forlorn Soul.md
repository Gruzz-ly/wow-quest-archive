==Objectives==
Help [[Keula]] locate her ma'da within the [[Necropolis (Nazmir)|Necropolis]].
* Keula's mother found
* [[The Matron Shaazula|Matron Shaazula]] subdued

==Description==
{{Questportrait|image=The Matron Shaazula.jpg|name=The Matron Shaazula|text=Keula's mother hasn't been seen in generations.}}
Where's my ma'da? You seen her?

"I'll be right back," she said. I'm a good girl, I wait. I wait and wait, I'm not sure how long I've been waiting here for.

It's cold and lonely here. Hey... could you help me find ma'da? I swear I won't cause you any trouble.
