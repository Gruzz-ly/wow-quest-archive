Use the Bag of Transmutation Stones on blood trolls and kill them. Collect 8 Vials of Altered Blood from them.

* [[Bag of Transmutation Stones#Horde]] (provided)

Now dis ingredient you might have some fun getting, little mixah. I'm gonna give you dese stones. They're able to turn things into other things.

I need their blood to finish my work here, but without de taint. Dese stones will turn dem into weaker creatures. Although, sometimes de stones turn dem into other things...
