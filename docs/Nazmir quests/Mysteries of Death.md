==Objectives==
Speak with the [[Vol'jin|Spirit of Vol'jin]].

==Description==
Hmph. Well, da [[Lich King]] and dat [[Eyir|bossy val'kyr]] not be me only rivals. But dey were da ones most likely ta be behind all dis. Someone be playin' with very ancient [[mojo]].

Tell [[Vol'jin]]'s [[Ghost|spirit]] dat da answers he be lookin' for not be found in da world of da living. He gonna need to go beyond da [[veil]] ta learn what needs knowin'.

Dere be some places on dat side only he can go.
