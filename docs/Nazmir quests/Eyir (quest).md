Meet [[Talanji]] at [[Skold-Ashil]] in [[Stormheim]].

==Description==
Not many got da stones or da [[mojo]] to take what be mine. Ol' [[Bwonsamdi]] always be gettin' his share.

Da way Talanji be tellin' it, [[Vol'jin]]'s [[Ghost|spirit]] may be gettin' help from some other power. And dere be few who love to mess with [[soul]]s like [[Eyir]]. A meddlin' one she be, with her wings and her valor.

Ya best be seein' her in person. She be found in [[Vault of Eyir|her vault]] at Skold-Ashil. And ya best not mention me name, or she might be sendin' ya back to me faster dan any death gate.
