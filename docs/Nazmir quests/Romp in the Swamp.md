==Objectives==
Repel the [[Alliance]] forces assaulting [[Nazmir]].
* Alliance attacks repelled in Nazmir (4)
* Get to the aircraft carrier
* [[Pozzik's Experimental Bomber]] ridden
* Obliterate Alliance army
* Find the Alliance [[The Stormy Pearl|flagship]] nearby
* [[Captain Beamwind]] slain

==Description==
Alliance armies are landing on the shores of Nazmir. Drive them back to the sea. [[Zandalar]] is ours!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Azerite Armor Cache]]
*[[Honorbound Equipment Cache]]

You will also receive:
* {{cost|hsm=5}}
* 26,800 [[XP]]
* +500 reputation with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]
* +500 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]]
