==Objectives==
With Hanzabu's Ritual Fetish, draw the ritual circle and sound the Drum of the Spirits to complete the ritual.
* Ritual Circle drawn
* [[Drum of Spirits]] sounded
* Speak with [[Bwonsamdi]] at the [[Edge of Oblivion]] within [[Nazmir]].

==Description==
Everything for de ritual is together. Now, it is time to begin. 

For dis to work, I must chant de words of old. It is an exhausting task, so I need you to take my mojo fetish and draw de ritual circle, then beat upon de drums.

If all goes well, Bwonsamdi may invite us into de temple.

If not, our souls are forfeit.

Let's not make any mistakes.
