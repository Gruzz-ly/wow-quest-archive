==Objectives==
Sneak into [[Zalamar]] to find Jo'chunga.
* Find [[Bloodseeker Jo'chunga]]

==Description==
I know you might think nothing has changed, but trust me, de [[voodoo]] worked... I think.

You should transform into one of de [[blood troll]]s when you are near Zalamar, one of their [[village]]s.

Jo'chunga should be dere somewhere, most likely near a large gathering of blood trolls.

Find Jo'chunga and tell him I have sent you, den see what he knows about de situation dere.

* 8,950 [[XP]]
