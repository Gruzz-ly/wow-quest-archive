==Objectives==
Recover Titan Data Cores from fallen [[Titan watcher|titan keepers]].
* [[Keeper Shavras's Core|Keeper Shavras's core]] collected
* [[Keeper Bolcan's Core|Keeper Bolcan's core]] colledted

==Description==
Major system repair possible within nearby structure. Preliminary goal: repair auxiliary systems.

Scans detect three fallen titan keepers. Recover cores. [[Titan Keeper Hezrel]] able to retrieve data from cores.

Titan Keeper Hezrel goal: reboot auxiliary systems, reach nearby structure, restore all primary systems, seal corruption.

Result: corruption contained. Titan Keeper Hezrel able to aid against [[blood troll]]s.

Blood troll definition not found in databanks.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|46|80|}}
* 22,300 [[XP]]
* +25 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]]

==Progress==
Insert data cores.

==Completion==
Cores recovered. Data validation and merge will begin shortly.

==Notes==

After accepting all the quest

:{{Text|say|Titan Keeper Hezrel|Objective cleared. Moving to forward position. Deliver completion to new location.}}

[[Titan Keeper Shavras]] is inactive and cannot be waken, his data core on the ground near him.

[[Titan Keeper Bolcan]] is still partly functional. Activate him:
:{{Text|say|Titan Keeper Bolcan|ERROR... INTRUSION...DET-DETECTED... SCANNING FOR... CORRUPTION.}}
:{{Text|say|Titan Keeper Bolcan|CORRUPTION RELEASE IMMINENT. ACT-ACTIVATING SEC-SEC-SECURITY PROTOCOLS. INTR-INTRUDER FOUND.}}
:{{Text|say|Titan Keeper Bolcan|ELIMINATE INTRUDER, CL-CLEANSE. CLEANSE.}}
Bolcan becomes hostile, but has only 10% HP, indicating his damage. Defeat him and loot his core.
