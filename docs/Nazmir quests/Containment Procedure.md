==Objectives==
Observe Titan Keeper Hezrel scan for a solution to [[Nazmir]]'s [[old god]] corruption.
* Speak to [[Titan Keeper Hezrel]]
* [[Grand Ma'da Ateena]] defeated

==Description==
Scans indicate structure has become inoperable. No possibility for repair.

Titan Keeper Hezrel's database fully restored. Believe solution may be within data.

Require time to scan data, find solution to contain Old God corruption.

Stand by while Titan Keeper Hezrel scans databanks for solution.
