==Objectives==
Kill 15 Nazwathan undead.
* [[Nazwatha]]n [[undead]] slain  (15)

==Description==
Corruption reanimating dead creatures nearby. Morphing into monstrosities. Must be stopped.

Cannot kill all, but can reduce numbers. Reduced numbers equates to less corruption outside of containment.

Destroy undead in surrounding area. [[Titan Keeper Hezrel|Keeper Hezrel]]'s systems will continue rebooting while you do so.

Reboot goal: recover data regarding nature of corruption.

==Rewards==

* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 17,850 [[XP]]
* +25 reputation with [[Talanji's Expedition]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
