==Objectives==
Face Hir'eek with Jo'chunga.
* Speak with [[Jo'chunga]] within the blood ritual pool
* [[Hir'eek]] weakened
* [[Poisoned Dagger]] (provided)

==Description==
{{questportrait|Hir'eek.jpg|Hir'eek, Lord of the sky|The loa of bats now serves the Blood God, G'huun.}}
Hir'eek knows we are here. If my knowledge of Hir'eek is correct, he will personally kill us after we present ourselves.

It is time, den. You and I will walk out dere into a horde of [[blood troll]]s who want to watch their [[loa]] kill us.

Take dis dagger. It is coated with de poison I made. I trust you to survive longer than me to use it.

You pretend to cower to Hir'eek, and I will do de talking. On my signal, you jump on Hir'eek and start stabbing!

...And den we hope for de best.
