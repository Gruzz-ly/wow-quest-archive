==Objectives==
Weaken [[Reanimated Monstrosity|Reanimated Monstrosities]] with the Titan Manipulator and collect 15 of their bones.
* [[Reanimated Monstrosity Bones]] (15)
* [[Titan Manipulator]] (provided)

==Description==
[[Undead]] entities influenced by corruption surround nearby structure. Impossible to approach. Plan required.

Calculating.

Plan achieved. Large creatures designated reanimated monstrosities focal gathering point of corruption.

Take [[titan]] weapon forged from [[Titan Plating|plating]] recovered. Use it to weaken monstrosities and collect bones.

Will run process to destroy bones, prevent creation of further monstrosities.
