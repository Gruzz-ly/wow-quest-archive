==Objectives==
The bold and pungent smelling fruit will keep your spirits high while you traverse through the {{C|Swamp of Sorrows}} and explore the [[Temple of Atal'Hakkar (Season of Discovery)|Temple of Atal'Hakkar]]. Don't let the odor fool you mon, this is a prized fruit of my people!

If you bring me back something enticing from your adventures, I'll make it worth your while, mon. A bundle of our finest durian for your troubles! Shall we make a deal?
*[[Flask of Atal'ai Mojo]]
