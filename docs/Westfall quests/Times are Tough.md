== Objectives ==
Kill 10 Harvest Watchers.
*[[Harvest Watcher]] slain x10

== Description ==
Times are tough, <brother/sister>. With the downtrodden masses of [[Stormwind]] migrating into [[Westfall]], resources are stretched as thin as they've ever been. The people are restless and in need of help.

Our own farmhouse has been serving has a halfway house for years. {{NPC||Salma Saldean||Salma}} does the best that she can to keep our guests alive, but sometimes the watchers get 'em. Watchers are those [[harvest golem|twisted abominations]] roaming [[Saldean's Farm|my fields]] - a little present left behind by the [[Defias Brotherhood|Defias]], before they were driven off. Help us!
