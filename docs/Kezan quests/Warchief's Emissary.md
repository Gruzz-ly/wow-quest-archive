==Description==
The [[goblin]]s and the Horde have accomplished too much together to part ways now, <class>. I intend for the [[Bilgewater Cartel]] to become part of the [[Horde]].

I have something that I want you to give to the {{NPC||Vol'jin||new warchief}}. Give this package to my [[Darkspear tribe|Darkspear]] friend on the dock at Bladefist Bay in Durotar. She will help you to get into [[Orgrimmar]] safely.

Speak with {{NPC||Sassy Hardwrench}} on the ship when you are ready to set sail.

Until we meet again, Aka'Magosh, a blessing on you and yours, <name>!
