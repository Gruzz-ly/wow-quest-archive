== Objectives ==
Put on your Awesome Party Ensemble ([[Cool Shades]], [[Hip New Outfit]], and [[Shiny Bling]]) and then head over to the party at the [[KTC Headquarters]] pool to entertain 10 {{NPC||Kezan Partygoer}}s.
* Partygoer entertained x10
