'''The Gloves Are Off''' begins the {{g|tww}} iteration of the [[Brawler's Guild]]. Find [[Pick Butterworth]] on the island platform between the two lines in the [[Stormwind City]] side of the [[Deeprun Tram]] to begin.

==Objectives==
Talk to a Bouncer in [[Bizmo's Brawlpub]] in the [[Deeprun Tram]] after using your [[:Blood-Soaked Invitation]].
* Talk to a [[Bizmo's Brawlpub Bouncer|Brawlpub Bouncer]]

==Description==
If you've found yourself an invite you'll be wanting to put it to good use, no?

Use your Blood-Soaked Invitation first then talk to a Bouncer in the Brawlpub and get to brawling!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|2|34}}

==Completion==
Remember to always brawl irresponsibly.

==Notes==
Head downstairs into [[Bizmo's Brawlpub]] and speak with any [[Bizmo's Brawlpub Bouncer]] to automatically complete this quest.

Speak with any bouncer to queue for fights.
