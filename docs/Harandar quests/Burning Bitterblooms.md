==Objectives==
Destroy 6 lightbloom infected plants in [[Har'mara]].
*Bitterblooms burned (6)

==Description==
Lightbloom infested rutaani are working to spread the contamination through the village. This plant showed significant signs of the disease.

Destroy any of the plants they've come into contact with. We must contain this!
