==Objectives==
Select a relic to pursue.
* Relic [[Zur'Ashar Kassameh|chosen]]

==Description==
Among these walls lie relics, silent and forgotten, their stories obscured by time.

The path to reclaiming our past is clear, take a relic to the sacred shrines and step into the echoes of its history. The visions will guide you, letting you walk the tale firsthand and breathe life into memory once more.

If you would lend your spirit to this cause, choose a relic to reawaken it's{{sic}} story.
