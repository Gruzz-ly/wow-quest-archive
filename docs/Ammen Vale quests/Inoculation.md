'''Inoculation''' is an [[Alliance]]-only quest in the [[draenei]] starting region [[Ammen Vale]] in which players use an [[Inoculating Crystal]] to protect the native owlkin from being affected by the [[Bloodmyst Radiation|radiation]] put off by the crashed bits of the [[Exodar]].

== Objectives ==
[[File:Quest - Inoculation TCG.jpg|thumb|In the [[TCG]].]]
Use the Inoculating Crystal on 6 Nestlewood Owlkin. Then return to [[Vindicator Aldar]] at the [[Crash Site]] in [[Ammen Vale]].
*[[Nestlewood Owlkin]] inoculated (6)
*[[Inoculating Crystal]] (provided)

== Description ==
[[Zhanaa]] needs our help to salvage parts from the [[Exodar]]. [[Botanist Taerix|Taerix]] prepared an inoculum that can be given to the Nestlewood [[owlkin]] that live in the area of a lot of the salvage.

Just get close to the unaffected ones and use the crystal on them. Unfortunately, you'll have to kill the others. You'll find the owlkin at [[Nestlewood Thicket]] and [[Nestlewood Hills]] to the southeast.
