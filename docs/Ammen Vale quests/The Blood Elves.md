'''The Blood Elves''' is an [[Alliance]]-only quest in the [[draenei]] starting zone [[Ammen Vale]], in which players must eliminate a group of [[blood elf|blood elves]] who followed the [[draenei]] from [[Outland]].

==Objectives==
Kill 10 Blood Elf Scouts then return to [[Tolaan]] in the [[Shadow Ridge]] of [[Ammen Vale]].
*[[Blood Elf Scout]]s slain (10)

==Description==
You must eliminate... the blood elf... threat. Find out... what they're... up to!

They're on the... ridge behind me.

==Progress==
There are too many of them...  We must whittle them down or else the survivors will have no chance against them.

==Completion==
These blood elves mean to kill us all. How are we to divert them from this course?

==Notes==
The [[Blood Elf Scout|blood elves]] are scattered all around the [[Shadow Ridge]], which is right behind [[Tolaan]]. Defeat 10 of them and return to [[Tolaan]] to complete the quest.
