# Embers of Neltharion campaign

[[File:VZ-Zaralek Cavern.jpg|thumb|Zaralek Cavern zone map]]

The '''Embers of Neltharion campaign''' of the overarching [[Dragonflight campaign]] covers the major questlines added in [[patch 10.1.0]], predominantly taking place in [[Zaralek Cavern]]. In [[patch 10.1.5]], the storyline continues with the [[Fractures in Time campaign]].

==Campaign==
The campaign is six chapters long, tracked in the achievement [[Embers of Neltharion (achievement)]]. The first three chapters were made available the week of 2 May 2023.

===Breaking Ground===
[[File:Iridikron's Gambit exterior.jpg|thumb|[[Iridikron's Gambit]] to [[Zaralek Cavern]]]]

Breaking Ground begins the [[patch 10.1]] portion of the campaign. The Incarnates have made their move, leading Alexstrasza to call upon you and the black dragonflight for aid.

{{Underground 1}}

Completing Six Hundred Feet Under once unlocks World Quests and other repeatable content in Zaralek Cavern account-wide.

===Sundered Legacy===
[[File:The Upper Hand - post-cutscene 1.jpg|thumb|Scalecommanders [[Scalecommander Sarkareth|Sarkareth]] and [[Scalecommander Emberthal|Emberthal]] inside [[Nal ks'kol]]]]
Ebyssian and Emberthal arrived in Loamm, pursuing the Sundered Flame to discover their purpose in the caves. Sabellian and Wrathion are caught up in their own plans, but you are ready to aid her.

{{Sundered Legacy}}

===The Ancient Bargain===
[[File:From Hell's Heart.jpg|thumb|[[Fyrakk]] and [[Sabellian]]]]

[[Sabellian]] has determined the most likely place to find [[Fyrakk]] and has asked you to join the hunt in the [[Zaqali Caldera]].

{{The Ancient Bargain}}

===Inherited Sin===
[[File:Air Superiority.jpg|thumb|[[Ebyssian]] taking pot shots at djaradin]]

Despite Alexstrasza's worries about the risk of exposure to the shadowflame, Ebyssian insists the black dragons must contain any other threats that may reside in Aberrus. This chapter opened the week of 9 May 2023.

{{Inherited Sin}}

===Inevitable Confrontation===
[[File:Fading Embers.jpg|thumb|[[Scalecommander Emberthal]] outside [[Brimstone Garrison]]]]

Although Wrathion and Sabellian have gone their separate ways, Emberthal is still pursuing Sarkareth alone. Ebyssian has urged you to find her. This chapter opened the week of 9 May 2023.

{{Inevitable Confrontation}}

===A Flame, Extinguished===
While you have defeated many of the threats within Aberrus, some still linger. Sabellian and Wrathion must pick up the pieces, and forge a new future for the black dragonflight. This chapter opened the week of 23 May 2023 for players who defeated [[Scalecommander Sarkareth (tactics)|Scalecommander Sarkareth]] within Aberrus (or at any time thereafter), or the week of 13 June 2023 regardless of raid progress.

{{A Flame, Extinguished}}

===Nasz'uro, the Unbound Legacy===
A [[Cracked Titan Gem]] can drop from [[Scalecommander Sarkareth (tactics)|Scalecommander Sarkareth]] in [[Aberrus, the Shadowed Crucible]] for [[evoker]]s. The lucky drop-holder gets to go on a collection quest (and complete a scenario) to acquire [[Nasz'uro, the Unbound Legacy]], their first legendary.

{{Nasz'uro}}

===Tyr's Fall===
Tyr's Fall is the second part of the overarching [[Tyr]] story. Players must have completed [[The Silver Purpose]] for [[Friend of the Dragon Isles]] to begin this storyline. Find [[Nozdormu]] at [[Tyrhold]] in [[Thaldraszus]] to pick up the story.

{{Tyr's Fall}}

The storyline continues in [[patch 10.1.7]] with the "Reforging the Tyr's Guard" chapter of the [[Fury Incarnate campaign]].
