'''Excavation: The Trove in the Tower''' is an [[Azerothian Archives]] world quest that is unlocked upon completion of [[Antiquated Antics]]. It takes place at {{co|47|49|Zaralek Cavern}} at the [[Gaze of Neltharion]] in [[Zaralek Cavern]].

==Objectives==
* Major Find Unearthed
* [[Archivist's Earth Totem]] (provided)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Roska Rocktooth]]|We shook up a real treasure with this one, I know it! Get in there and see what it is!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,500 [[XP]]
* 562x [[Mysterious Fragment]]
* 5x [[Flightstones]]
* +300 [[reputation]] with [[Azerothian Archives]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Roska Rocktooth|We're gonna be studying this find for years. Nice job digging it up!}}

==Notes==
Use the provided [[Archivist's Earth Totem]] at a Disturbed Earth location that is marked on the minimap, then interact with Roska's Earth Totem to start the minigame. Keep the bar in the middle by interacting the lava slimes if it gets too far to the right.
