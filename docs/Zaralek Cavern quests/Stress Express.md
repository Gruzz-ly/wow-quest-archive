'''Stress Express''' is a bonus objective that starts at {{co|65.9|50.8|Zaralek Cavern}} in the [[Throughway]] of [[Zaralek Cavern]], announced with a zone-wide emote: {{text|emote|Doopurt's wagon broke down in the Throughway!}}

==Objectives==
*Wagon pushed into Loamm

==Description==
Help Doopurt's wagon reach Loamm!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 2x [[Fragrant Unearthed Coin]]
* {{cost|28|9}}
* 9,500 [[XP]]
*[[Clanging Dirt-Covered Pouch]]
* +25 [[reputation]] with [[Loamm Niffen]]

==Notes==
On approach:
:{{text|say|Doopurt|*Pant* Please, help! My wagon is too heavy for me to push it!}}

[[File:Doopurt.jpg|thumb|Doopurt]]
[[Doopurt]] is standing next to his [[Busted Wagon]] full of crystals. Push the wagon by attacking it. This, understandably, upsets Doopurt, who yells the following barks at random:
*{{text|yell|Doopurt|AAAAHHHHHHHH!!!}}
*{{text|yell|Doopurt|FIRE?!! SERIOUSLY??!!!}}
*{{text|yell|Doopurt|THIS IS NOT WHAT I HAD IN MIIIIND!!}}
*{{text|yell|Doopurt|WHAT ARE YOU DOING?!!!!}}
*{{text|yell|Doopurt|WHAT'S THE MATTER WITH YOU?!}}

Part-way through, [[Bronk the Bully]] jumps on:
:{{text|yell|Bronk the Bully|BRONK HAVE FUN TOO!}}
:{{text|yell|Doopurt|No, no, no, Bronk, not now! Can't you see I'm having enough trouble already?!}}

Focus on Bronk, cleaving the wagon to keep moving it forward as well. Once Bronk's had enough, he'll hop off:
:{{text|yell|Bronk the Bully|BRONK NOT HAVING FUN ANYMORE!!}}

Keep pushing the wagon to the entrance to [[Loamm]] at {{co|58.6|54.5|Zaralek Cavern}}, where Doopurt hops off, completing the objective:
:{{text|say|Doopurt|S-somehow... all in one piece still. Th-thank you?}}
