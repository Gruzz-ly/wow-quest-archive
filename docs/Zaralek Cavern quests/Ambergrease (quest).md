==Notes==
Location: [[Cascades Column]], [[Zaralek Cavern]].

Choose one of the snails next to [[Maggrul]]:
*[[Slimes]]
*[[Gregg]]
*[[Rosy]]

Bring the snail to a [[Tasty Mushroom]]. It starts Eating it until it is devoured and [[Ambergrease]] appears.
