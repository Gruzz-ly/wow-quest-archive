'''Scattered Around the Tower''' is an [[Azerothian Archives]] world quest that is unlocked upon completion of [[Antiquated Antics]]. It takes place at {{co|47|48|Zaralek Cavern}} at the [[Gaze of Neltharion]] in [[Zaralek Cavern]].

==Objectives==
* Finds Unearthed (2)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Roska Rocktooth]]|The ground here is littered with finds! Grab your totem and get them out!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 9,500 [[XP]]
* {{cost|640}}
* +300 [[reputation]] with [[Azerothian Archives]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Roska Rocktooth|That there is a historical treasure! This'll really give our research a leg up!}}

==Notes==
Use the provided [[Archivist's Earth Totem]] near [[Disturbed Earth (Zaralek Cavern)|Disturbed Earth]], then interact with Roska's Earth Totem to have the Helpful Earth either start the minigame or spawn a hostile elemental.
