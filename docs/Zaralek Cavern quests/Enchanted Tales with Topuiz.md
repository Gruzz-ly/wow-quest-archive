==Objectives==
Deliver scrolls of Enchant Cloak - Writ of Leech, Enchant Cloak - Writ of Speed, and Enchant Cloak - Writ of Avoidance to [[Dustmonger Topuiz]] in [[Loamm]].
*[[Enchant Cloak - Writ of Leech]] {{Profquality|3}} (5)
*[[Enchant Cloak - Writ of Speed]] {{Profquality|3}} (5)
*[[Enchant Cloak - Writ of Avoidance]] {{Profquality|3}} (5)
*Deliver the items to Dustmonger Topuiz in Loamm

==Description==
The kith of Loamm request the skills of an excellent [[enchanter]]! [[Magic]] is so prevalent in [[Valdrakken|this wondrous city]], it practically floats about on the breeze!

Our chief Dustmonger and Sparklefinger, Mister Topuiz, has heard of the many different ways you enchant your clothes on the surface, and wishes to see some examples for himself!

Please deliver those scrolls as soon as you can, won't you?
