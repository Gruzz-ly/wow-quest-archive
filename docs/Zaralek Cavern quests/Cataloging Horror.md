==Objectives==
Use the Cataloging Camera to take pictures of experiment dumping sites in the [[Acidbite Ravine]].
* Photograph Evidence (6)
* [[Cataloging Camera]] (provided)

==Description==
I... I need to bring the evidence of this insanity back to [[Black dragonflight|my flight]]. It may destroy us, but my people need to know what happened here.

I borrowed some cataloging cameras from [[Tellywin Sharpfizzle|Tellywin]]. Can you join me in documenting these atrocities?

I can provide you with a similar protection to that which I gave in the vault. I will leave any fighting to you; I cannot bring myself to cause any more suffering than my flight has already wrought.

Let us be done with this as swiftly as possible.
