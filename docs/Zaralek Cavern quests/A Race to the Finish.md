'''A Race to the Finish''' is a series of quests asking players to get the named snail to victory at the snail track in [[Glimmerogg]].

==Objectives==
Help <snail> win a race.
* <Snail> Treats obtained from [[Corry]] (Optional)
* <Snail> Treats used on snail racer <snail>
* <Snail> wins a race

==Description==
;Bashful
:You look like someone that enjoys a good snail race! 
:And what makes a snail race good you ask? Each snail must have a chance to win! 
:And sometimes the snails might need a little bit of help. 
:If you grab a bag of treats from the chef and toss it in their path that'll get them moving. 
:How about we give [[Bashful]] a nudge to victory?
;Brulee/Tricky
:Everyone loves a good snail race! In the most exciting races, every snail has a chance to win.
:You can help a snail win by giving it treats. Watch how fast it makes them go!
:Grab a bag of treats and toss them in the path of the snails. See if you can <[[Brulee]]/[[Tricky]]> get to win!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|28|9}}
* 9,500 [[XP]]
* [[Bashful (companion)]] or [[Brulee (companion)]] or [[Tricky (companion)]]
* 10x [[Flightstones]]
* +100 [[reputation]] with [[Loamm Niffen]]

==Progress==
Has your snail won the race?

==Completion==
That was a great victory. I think you should be <snail>'s new trainer!

==Notes==
Speak with [[Corry]] and choose the correct snail's feed. Then use the extra action button to target the ground in front of the snail. Rinse and repeat as it makes it's way around the track.

Bashful's quest doesn't award reputation.
