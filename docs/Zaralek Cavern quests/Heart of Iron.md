'''Heart of Iron''' is a Sniffenseek dig adventure started with [[Myrrit]] in [[Loamm]] in [[Zaralek Cavern]].

==Objectives==
*[[Iron Titan Key]]
*Sniff out the [[Scorched Chest]]

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Gathered Niffen Resources]]
*[[Splintered Spark of Shadowflame]]

==Criteria of==
*[[Proof of Myrrit]]

==Notes==
Location: [[Mysterious Cave]], [[Zaralek Cavern]].

Upon entering the cave:
:{{text|say|Myrrit|Oh no--I thought this place would be empty!}}
:{{text|say|Myrrit|Who is THAT? He's making a mess!}}
:{{text|yell|[[Jalgon Stoutburn]]|Where is it? Where are you HIDING, treasure?}}
:{{text|yell|Jalgon Stoutburn|No, not here...}}
:{{text|yell|Jalgon Stoutburn|What about here?}}
:{{text|yell|Jalgon Stoutburn|I'll find the chest, even if I have to tear this place apart!}}

Upon start of combat:
:{{text|say|Jalgon Stoutburn|You'll only get the key over my dead body!}}

Upon defeat:
:{{text|say|Jalgon Stoutburn|It... was in my grasp...}}

Upon looting [[Iron Titan Key]] from Jalgon:
:{{text|say|Myrrit|He had a key! I guess it's up to us to sniff out the treasure now!}}

Have Myrrit sniff out the treasure. When Myrrit sniffs out an [[Iron Explosive]]:
:{{text|say|Myrrit|I smell... burning.}}
:{{text|say|Myrrit|Look out!}}

When Myrrit sniffs out the treasure:
:{{text|say|Myrrit|Wait! There's LESS burning smell here!}}
:{{text|say|Myrrit|Yes! A big box, Dragarus! Yay us!}}

Upon looting the chest:
:{{text|say|Myrrit|That was amazing. Let's head back with our treasure!}}
