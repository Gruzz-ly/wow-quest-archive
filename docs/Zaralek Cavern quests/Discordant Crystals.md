'''Discordant Crystals''' is a bonus objective that starts at the [[Crystal Fields]] of [[Zaralek Cavern]], announced with a zone-wide emote: {{text|emote|The delicate natural song of the cavern is disrupted by violent, discordant notes!}}

==Objectives==
*[[Discordant Lodestone]] destroyed
*[[Discordant Chip]]s destroyed (8) (Optional)

==Description==
Invasive crystals are driving nearby crystalline creatures mad!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Clinking Dirt-Covered Pouch]]
* +25 [[reputation]] with [[Loamm Niffen]]

==Notes==
Attack the Discordant Lodestone, which spawns [[Crystalline Walker]]s, [[Raging Cluster]]s, and [[Crystal Splinter]]s.
