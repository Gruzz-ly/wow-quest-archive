==Objectives==
Kill 500 Draenei Players in Player versus Player combat across Draenor.
*Draenei Players slain (500)
*[[Echoing Betrayal]] (provided)

==Description==
<Raza'kul hands you the bloody heart.>

The ritual is complete. Your nemesis is marked for death.

Now your work to rid this world of the enemy truly begins. Kill all [[draenei]] that stand defiantly against the Horde. Let their deaths serve as a warning to the rest of the [[Alliance]]: they will leave {{Au|Draenor}} or they will die.

Return when you have quenched your rage with the blood of draenei.

Conquer our enemy and be known as [[Draenei Destroyer|draenei destroyer]]. Fail and be nothing.

==Completion==
The truth of your deeds is in your eyes. Your hunger for the enemy sated with cries for mercy and rivers of blood.

Take now your place as draenei destroyer.

==Notes==
*Requires a Level 2 [[Gladiator's Sanctum]] building to access.
*Kills done in [[Battleground]]s and the [[Arena]] do not count towards this quest.
*The quest can be completed while in a raid.
