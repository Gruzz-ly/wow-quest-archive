==Objectives==
Give weapons to 10 [[Frostwolf Slave]]s.
*Frostwolf Slaves Armed (10)

==Description==
[[Ligra the Unyielding|Ligra]] was sent with extra weapons to equip enslaved Frostwolves.

Arm the Frostwolf Slaves so they can cut their way from their slavers.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|11|80}}
* 13850 [[XP]]
* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Frostwolf Orcs]]

==Progress==
Lok'tar Ogar.

==Completion==
The [[ogre]]s appear to be in disarray and many of my people are now free.

==Notes==
Pick up [[To the Slaughter]] before heading out. Interact with slaves to have them help out for a minute while killing ogres. Circle around the central room clockwise to turn in to Durotan on the western side.
