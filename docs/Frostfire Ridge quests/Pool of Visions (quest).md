==Objectives==
Speak with {{Au|Drek'Thar|Farseer Drek'Thar}} at the [[Pool of Visions]] to begin the ritual of vision.
*Ritual of Vision {{co|16.8|57.8|Frostfire Ridge}}

==Description==
A terrible storm gathers on the horizon, <class>.

I have prepared a ritual. You must consult the pool to see what destiny holds for us all.

You must stop it, <name>.
