== Description ==
Cowardly Thunderlords...

During the battle for the citadel, runners informed me that the Iron Wolf is striking at our home. He'll find no easy footing there, Draka will make them pay tenfold for any of ours that they slay, but we must return quickly and chase the mongrels off.

You and your forces have proven valuable allies of the Frostwolves. We'd welcome your aid in securing our Wor'gol.

I'll see you on the battlefield if you choose to come.

== Notes ==
On accept:
:{{text|say|Durotan|When you see Draka... tell her that I will be home soon.}}
:{{text|emote|[[Thrall]] grins at his father's words.}}
To continue with the zone storyline and skip the optional side quests, head out to the flight master and fly back to [[Wor'gol]].

At Bladespire, there are a few side quests that can be done for additional rewards, reputation, and a follower. Speak with [[Snowrunner Rolga]] to pick up [[Mopping Up (Frostfire Ridge)]], and interact with the Bounty Board next to her for [[WANTED: Gutsmash the Destroyer]]. Further southwest is [[Guse]] with [[Slavery and Strife]], and the flight master.

Arrived at Wor'gol:
:{{text|yell|The Iron Wolf|Korhol, Zhakta, Trokar... move in from all sides. Gargrak, hold for now.}}

Wor'gol has been invaded by the Thunderlord clan! After landing, speak with Durotan to pick up [[Free Our Brothers and Sisters]]:
