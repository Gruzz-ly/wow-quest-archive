==Notes==
''On accept'':
:{{text|say|[[Matron Suma]]|That's enough, child. Try not to speak.}}

Pick up this quest while on [[They Who Held Fast]], and finish that quest before heading to the [[Boneslag]].

After interacting with Roknor and Makar, keep heading northeast. On the north side of the giant skull are a [[Blackrock Siegesmith]] and a [[Thunderlord Apprentice]] working on the Siege Munitions.
:{{text|bossemote|These stones appear to retain the force and heat being hammered into them.}}
Further east, opposite the campfire where [[Gana Surehide]] was found, another pair are working on some heavy plating:
:{{text|bossemote|These thick plates are being tempered to withstand extreme heat.}}
After interacting with the second object, the quest completes automatically.
