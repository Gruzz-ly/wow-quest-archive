==Objectives==
Prove your worth to [[Mokugg Lagerpounder]] by collecting 4 Icespine Stingers.

==Description==
I will help you, but you are going to need to prove to me that you are not only a worthy fisherman, but also a fearless warrior.

The canyon north of us is full of icespine stingers.  Slaughter them and bring their stingers back to me.  They should work well as bait in these local waters.
