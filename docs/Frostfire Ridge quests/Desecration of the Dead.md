==Objectives==
Kill 15 Necrophytes and Fel Wolves.
*[[Necrophyte]]s and [[Fel Wolf|Fel Wolves]] slain (15)

==Description==
These filthy warlocks are desecrating the dead in the boneyard!

<The farseer trembles as he fails to keep his composure.>

I WANT THEM DEAD!

Destroy the necrophytes. Destroy their demonic abominations.

==Notes==
Pick up [[Eye Need That]], [[Stop the Flow]], [[Desecration of the Dead]] and after turning in [[A Clew of Worms]], pick up [[The Sleeper Has Awakened]] before heading out. Head northwest to the [[Icescar Boneyard]]. Start killing [[necrophyte]]s and [[Fel Wolf|fel wolves]], then interact with the summoning circles to destroy them. Ogzor is at the northwest end of the boneyard, with two [[Ogzor's Bones]] skeletal minions.
