==Objectives==
Speak to [[Guse]] at [[Bladespire Citadel]].

==Description==
Share word with the others of the Frostwolves you saved this day. They will be glad to know that some made it out of the mines alive.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1380 [[XP]]
*{{cost|1|18}}
*25x[[Garrison Resources]]
* +10 [[reputation]] with [[Frostwolf Orcs]]

==Completion==
You found {{Au|Mulverick}}? It shouldn't be long before those ogres clear out after you defeated their master.

==Notes==
Pick up [[Mulverick's Offer of Service]] before going anywhere. Don't forget to turn in [[Slaying Slavers]] before heading back up top. If not done with the [[Circle of Blood (Frostfire Ridge)|Circle of Blood]] side chain ending with [[The Fall of the Warlord]], do those quests too.
