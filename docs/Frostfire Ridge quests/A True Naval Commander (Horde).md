==Objectives==
Install the Blast Furnace equipment on your ship and complete the Naval Blockade mission.
*Equip Blast Furnace on a ship
*Complete the [[Blockade: South Sea|Naval Blockade Mission: South Sea]]
*[[Blast Furnace (item)]] (provided)

==Description==
Yes! Those are the blueprints I misplaced when I was fishing last week. These instructions match my prototype equipment one for one. Take this Blast Furnace and install it on one of our ships. This should let us break the naval blockade and expand our control of the {{Au|Draenor}} seas.

==Rewards==
You will receive: {{cost|1|54}}
*[[Rush Order: Shipyard]] (5)	
*[[Oil]] (1000)

==Completion==
Equipment can really make a difference out there!

==Notes==
Once this quest is completed, you will have access to all naval equipment that any character on your account has previously unlocked, except for [[Tuskarr Fishing Net]] and [[Unsinkable]], which require reputation to purchase.
