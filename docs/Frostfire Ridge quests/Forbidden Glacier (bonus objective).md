== Rewards ==

*{{cost|11|40}}
* 13690 [[XP]]

== Notes ==
This bonus objective is offered while at the [[Forbidden Glacier]] in southwestern [[Frostfire Ridge]], just outside of [[Wor'gol]]. Interact with the [[Frostwolf Grunt]]s to save them:
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|Get out before they freeze you too!}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|I have felt the absence of heat...}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|I must seek a fire!}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|I should never have come to this place!}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|So cold it burns!}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|So cold... but still aware...}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|Thank you!}}
*{{Text|say|Frostwolf Grunt|The torment..}}

Vignettes in the area:
*[[Mor'dul Tower]]: 3 [[Bladespire Brute]]s with poor-quality items, and the [[Bladespire Primalist]] atop the tower with the [[Primalist's Prismatic Pendant]] and 10-20x [[Garrison Resources]]
*Rare unbound air elemental, [[Breathless]], on the northwestern side of the area which drops [[Stolen Breath]] and 10-20x [[Garrison Resources]].
*[[Forbidden Cave]]: frozen Frostwolf Cold-Singer all the way in the back, holding a [[Frozen Frostwolf Axe]], which rewards the [[Icebound Frost-Singer Axe]] and 10-20x [[Garrison Resources]].
*Rare clefthoof [[Coldstomp the Griever]] in the clefthoof boneyard to the southwest, looted for [[Coldstomp's Sorrow]] and 10-20x [[Garrison Resources]]

==Trivia==
*Named '''Bonus Objective: Save Frostwolves and Frost Wolves''' during the beta. In addition, it was also merged together with [[Bonus Objective: Kill Malevolent Breath]], which took place in the same area.
