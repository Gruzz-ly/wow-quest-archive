==Objectives==

* [[Spy's Report]] (provided)

== Description ==
Sifting through the [[Loose Dirt]], the severed hand of an orc is found. And clutched in the hand is a bone-carved tube. And in the tube is a dirt-stained parchment.

The parchment is a report made by an orc who spent weeks spying on the city of [[Theramore]].

After scanning the parchment, it is clear that it must be brought to the orc spymaster in Brackenwall Village.
