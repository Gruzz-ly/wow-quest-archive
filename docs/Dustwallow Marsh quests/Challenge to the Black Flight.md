==Objectives==
Plant the Stonemaul Banner near the entrance to [[Onyxia's Lair]] and defeat anyone who answers.
* [[Smolderwing|Stonemaul Clan Avenged]]
* [[Stonemaul Banner]] (Provided)

==Description==
[[Brogg]] relishes the thought of taking his revenge on the [[black dragonflight|black dragonkin]] of the [[Dustwallow Marsh|marsh]]. <name>, you have been very helpful in preparing for this moment. Will you help?

Take Brogg's clan banner and travel east until you see a cave like a dragon's mouth. At the entrance to that cave, plant Stonemaul banner and issue Brogg's challenge to Onyxia.

Brogg and the [[Stonemaul clan|Stonemaul]] will have their lands back!
