[[Elixir of Shadows]] is needed to acquire items for several other quests.  This repeatable quest allows you to get more as needed.

==Objectives==
It looks like the commander was not delirious after all.  Bring me more dust and I'll prepare another elixir for you.
*[[Shadow Dust (Outland)]] (6)

==Description==
Keep bringing the dust to me. I'll prepare as many elixirs as you need.

Commander's orders.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*+150 [[reputation]] with [[Sha'tari Skyguard]]
*[[Elixir of Shadows]]

==Completion==
It looks like the commander was not delirious after all. Bring me more dust and I'll prepare another elixir for you.

==Notes==
The Shadow Dust drops about 20% of the time from Wing Guards, about 27% of the time
from Talonites and Windwalkers, and 32% of the time from Soulcallers.
Shadow Dust is not soulbound and can be purchased from the auction house.
