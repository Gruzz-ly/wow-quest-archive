'''Back to the Chamber''' can be picked up in [[Boralus]] or [[Dazar'alor]] after reaching [[Heart of Azeroth]] level 65.

==Objectives==
Return to the [[Chamber of Heart]].

==Description==
The [[Heart of Azeroth|Heart o' Azeroth]] is gettin' stronger every day, and it's makin' a difference.

Make yer way tae the Chamber o' Heart, champion. Yer talents are needed.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|34|}}
* +75 reputation with [[Champions of Azeroth]]

==Completion==
The Heart o' Azeroth never ceases tae amaze me. Well done, champion!

==Notes==
*If you find that Magni isn't present to turn in this quest, check whether [[It's Never Easy]] is currently in your quest log. If so, abandon it to make him reappear; you can easily pick it back up from him after you're done.
