==Objectives==
Earn at least Friendly reputation with the factions of [[Kul Tiras]].

* {{rep|Friendly}} with the [[Proudmoore Admiralty]] in [[Tiragarde Sound]]
* {{rep|Friendly}} with the [[Storm's Wake]] in [[Stormsong Valley]]
* {{rep|Friendly}} with the [[Order of Embers]] in [[Drustvar]]

==Description==
The people of Kul Tiras are diverse and capable. If we can bring them all together under the banner of the [[Alliance]], the [[Horde]] has no chance of stopping us.

==Rewards==
You will unlock access to the following: {{Abilities|World Quests|id=273746|icon=inv_tabard_proudmoore}}

You will also receive:
* {{cost|23|40|}}
* 17,850 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Good. The people of Kul Tiras stand with us, thanks to you, <name>.

Now, the real work begins.

==Notes==
* This will also upgrade your [[Flight Master's Whistle]] for Kul Tiras and [[Zandalar]].
* If you have already completed this quest on one character, you will be given [[Uniting Kul Tiras (alt)]] instead that completes upon talking to Wyrmbane.
