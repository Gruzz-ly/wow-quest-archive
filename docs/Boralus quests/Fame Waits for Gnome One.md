'''Fame Waits for Gnome One''' is an optional breadcrumb that starts the questline to unlock access to [[Mechagon Island]].

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Tinkmaster Overspark]] in [[Boralus]].

==Description==
Tinkmaster Overspark requests your assistance on an important research expedition.

Working with the esteemed Tinkmaster will certainly be of benefit to your career. It's done wonders for mine!

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|2|34}}
* 1,800 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Now we can begin the expedition.

==Notes==
This quest can be picked up at the [[Stormwind Embassy]] in [[Stormwind City]] or at [[Mezzamere]] in [[Nazjatar]].
