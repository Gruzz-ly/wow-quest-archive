'''Deeper Into Zandalar''' is an optional breadcrumb that becomes available after completing the questline to unlock the first foothold on [[Zandalar]] and then relogging.

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Halford Wyrmbane BfA.jpg|Halford Wyrmbane|It is time to establish another foothold in Zandalar.}}
Meet with [[Halford Wyrmbane]] on the [[Wind's Redemption]] in [[Boralus Harbor]].

==Description==
Our advisors have decided that now is the correct time to further our hold on [[Zandalar]].

Return to the Wind's Redemption posthaste. We shall leave for new lands immediately.
