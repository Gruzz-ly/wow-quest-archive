'''Operation: Heartstrike''' begins the final chapter of the Alliance War Campaign and is offered upon reaching {{rep|revered}} reputation with [[7th Legion]] and completing [[A Clean Escape]].

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Halford Wyrmbane]] on the [[Wind's Redemption]] in [[Boralus Harbor]].

==Description==
We've taken out quite a number of high priority targets, <name>. All thanks to your help.

It is time to strike directly at the heart of the [[Zandalari Empire|Zandalari empire]].

Report to the Wind's Redemption as soon as you are able. We will get started from there.

("Halford Wyrmbane: We return to [[Zuldazar]] today... to strike at the heart of the Zandalari nation.")
