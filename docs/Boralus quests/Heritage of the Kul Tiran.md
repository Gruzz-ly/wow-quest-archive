==Objectives==
Meet [[Katherine Proudmoore]] in [[Boralus]].

==Description==
Gossip in Boralus has oft reached my ears of your exceptional conduct, <name>.

Meet me before the keep. I wish to commend your efforts on behalf of [[Kul Tiras (kingdom)|Kul Tiras]].

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you:
*{{Abilities|Heritage of Kul Tiras|id=294135|Grants the appearances of the [[Heritage of Kul Tiras]] armor set.|icon=inv__faction_proudmooreadmiralty}}

You will also receive:
* {{cost|23|40}}
* 7,700 [[XP]]

==Completion==
Wherever the winds take you, know that you take the spirit of Kul Tiras with you.

Wear our colors with pride, <name>.

==Notes==
The quest is automatically received upon reaching level 50 on a [[Kul Tiran (playable)|Kul Tiran]], without having used a race change or level boost.
