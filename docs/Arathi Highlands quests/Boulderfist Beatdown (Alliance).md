==Objectives==
Kill 20 [[Boulderfist clan]] [[ogre]]s in [[Arathi Highlands]].
* Boulderfist clan ogres  (20)

==Description==
The Boulderfist ogres have plagued Arathi since the [[Second War]], and they still remain.

You know what must be done.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1,790 [[XP]]
* 200x [[War Resources]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
You have thinned the clan's numbers? Well done.
