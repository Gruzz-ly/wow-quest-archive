==Description==
<Name>, the people of [[Hammerfall]] were attacked not so long ago by saboteurs from a group called the [[Red Dawn]].

I am working to help them recover and rebuild from the attack, with my fellow Sunwalkers, [[Malu]] and [[Nadura]].

One thing they have been doing is gathering supplies needed by Hammerfall's crafters from various sources.

If you could deliver these supplies, it would be a great help.
