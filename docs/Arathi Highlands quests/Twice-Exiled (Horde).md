==Objectives==
Kill 20 Burning, Cresting, Rumbling, or Thundering Exiles or Guardians in [[Arathi Highlands]].
* [[Elemental]]s slain (20)

==Description==
Elementals of all sorts are bursting free of their bonds across the Highlands.

Return them to the primordial chaos from whence they came.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1,790 [[XP]]
* 200x [[War Resources]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Honorbound]]

==Progress==
Hostile elementals still befoul the Highlands.

==Completion==
The elementals have been banished? Well done.

==Notes==
*Eligible mobs are [[Burning Goliath]]s, [[Rumbling Goliath]]s, [[Rumbling Guardian]]s, [[Burning Exile]]s, [[Thundering Goliath]]s, [[Thundering Guardian]]s, [[Burning Guardian]]s, [[Cresting Exile]]s, [[Cresting Goliath]]s, [[Cresting Guardian]]s, [[Thundering Exile]]s, and [[Rumbling Exile]]s.
