# Arathi Highlands storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Arathi.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

The '''[[Arathi Highlands]] storyline''' is for level 25-30 players, taking place after the [[Wetlands storyline]] for Alliance or [[Hillsbrad Foothills storyline]] for Horde. 

The progress for the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Arathi Highlands Quests]].

After Arathi Highlands comes the [[Hinterlands storyline]].
