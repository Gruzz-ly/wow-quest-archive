==Objectives==
Kill 12 Witherbark tribe trolls in [[Arathi Highlands]].
* [[Witherbark tribe]] [[Forest troll|trolls]] slain (12)

==Description==
:{{Text|say|[[Nathanos Blightcaller]]|These Witherbark trolls are the last remnants of a doomed [[tribe]]. They were driven from the [[Hinterlands]] by the armies of the [[Sylvanas Windrunner|Dark Lady]]. Let's finish the job, shall we?}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]
*1x [[Honorbound Service Medal]]

==Completion==
:{{Text|say|Nathanos Blightcaller|These trolls are annoyingly resilient, but we have them on the run. They won't hold out for long.}}

==Notes==
*Location: [[Witherbark Village]], [[Arathi Highlands]].
*Eligible mobs are [[Witherbark Shadowcaster]]s, [[Witherbark Troll]]s, [[Witherbark Raider]]s, [[Witherbark Berserker]]s, [[Witherbark Shadow Hunter]]s, [[Witherbark Witch Doctor]]s, [[Witherbark Axe Thrower]]s, [[Witherbark Headhunter]]s, [[Witherbark Hunter]]s, [[Witherbark Bonebreaker]]s, [[Witherbark Fisher]]s, [[Beastrider Kama]], [[Nimar the Slayer]], and [[Zalas Witherbark]].
