==Objectives==
Kill 20 [[Boulderfist clan]] ogres in [[Arathi Highlands]].
* Boulderfist clan ogres slain (20)

==Description==
If the Boulderfist ogres will not join the [[Horde]], there is no place for them in Arathi.

You know what must be done.

==Rewards==

* 200x [[War Resources]]
* +75 reputation with [[Honorbound]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
