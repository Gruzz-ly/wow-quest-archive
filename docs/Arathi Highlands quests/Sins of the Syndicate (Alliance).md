==Objectives==
Kill 20 [[Syndicate]] members at [[Dabyrie's Farmstead]].
* Syndicate members slain  (20)

==Description==
As if the [[Defilers]] were not trouble enough, Syndicate agents still slither down from the [[Alterac Mountains|Alterac mountains]] to drive off honest [[Alliance]] farmers.

Punish them.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1,790 [[XP]]
* 200x [[War Resources]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]

==Progress==
The Syndicate still harries Dabyrie's Farmstead.

==Completion==
The Syndicate has been driven back to Alterac? Well done.

==Notes==
*Eligible mobs are: [[Darbel Montrose]], [[Singer]], [[Syndicate Highwayman]], [[Syndicate Pathstalker]]s, [[Syndicate Mercenary]], [[Syndicate Harvest Reaper]]s, and [[Syndicate Harvest Shredder]]s.
