==Objectives==
Kill 20 [[Witherbark tribe]] [[troll]]s in [[Arathi Highlands]].
* Witherbark tribe trolls slain  (20)

==Description==
The Witherbark tribe has a long and bloody history in the highlands of Arathi.

Show them how much bloodier it can get.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 1,790 [[XP]]
* 200x [[War Resources]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]

==Progress==
Witherbark drums still echo across the Highlands.

==Completion==
Have the trolls been defeated? Well done.

==Notes==
*Eligible mobs are: [[Witherbark Troll]]s, [[Witherbark Shadowcaster]]s, [[Witherbark Raider]]s, [[Witherbark Berserker]]s, [[Witherbark Shadow Hunter]]s, [[Witherbark Witch Doctor]]s, [[Witherbark Axe Thrower]]s, [[Witherbark Headhunter]]s, [[Witherbark Hunter]]s, [[Witherbark Bonebreaker]]s, [[Witherbark Fisher]]s, [[Beastrider Kama]], [[Zalas Witherbark]], and [[Nimar the Slayer]].
