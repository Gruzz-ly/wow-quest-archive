==Description==
I'm glad for your help. Between deployments to [[Khaz Algar]], responding to threats in the [[Arathi Highlands|Highlands]], and desertion, we simply don't have enough soldiers to enforce order.

[[Marran Trollbane|Marran]]'s supporters are causing trouble throughout the city. They're stealing supplies, harassing people--you name it. We've enacted a curfew, but Marran's supporters are openly defying it.

As my deputies, go around and tell the troublemakers to go home. If they get rowdy, you have my permission to teach them a lesson.
