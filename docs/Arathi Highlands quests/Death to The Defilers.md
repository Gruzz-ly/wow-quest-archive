==Objectives==
Kill 20 [[Defilers]] at [[Go'Shek Farm]].
* Defilers slain (20)

==Description==
Even with the destruction of [[Undercity]], the Defilers continue to combat our hold on the [[Arathi Highlands]].

The farmland at Go'Shek will belong to the Alliance once more. Go, and defeat the Forsaken.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Warfronts Equipment Cache]]
* 1,790 [[XP]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]

==Progress==
The Defilers still hold Go'Shek Farm.

==Completion==
You've driven them off? Well done.

==Notes==
*Eligible mobs are: [[Defiler Arbalest]]s, [[Defiler Shadow Priest]]s, [[Defiler Footman]], [[Defiler Cavalry]], and [[Doomrider Helgrim]].
