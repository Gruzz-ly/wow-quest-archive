==Objectives==
Kill 20 [[Syndicate]] members at [[Go'Shek Farm]].
* Syndicate members slain  (20)

==Description==
As if the [[League of Arathor]] was not trouble enough, Syndicate agents still slither down from the [[Alterac Mountains|Alterac mountains]] to drive off honest [[Horde]] laborers.

Punish them.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 200x [[War Resources]]

==Progress==
The Syndicate still harries Go'Shek Farm.

==Completion==
The Syndicate has been driven back to Alterac? Well done.
