==Description==
[[Danath Trollbane|Danath]] is not here. 

[[Faerin Lothar|Faerin]] says he journeyed to [[Hammerfall]] because we requested a meeting. I sent no such message to him nor anyone else. 

I will dispatch outriders immediately to search for him. 

While we wait for their return... You and [[Karga Bloodfury|Karga's]] quick thinking may have saved our human refugees. We had just run out of food to give them.

Distribute the crates directly to them. I'm sure they will be grateful for the food inside.
