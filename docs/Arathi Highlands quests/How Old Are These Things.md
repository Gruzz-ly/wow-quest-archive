==Description==
[[Marran Trollbane|Marran]] tried to declare herself the new ruler of Stromgarde. While some in the army are complicit in her coup, there are still a lot of us who are loyal to [[Danath Trollbane|Lord Danath Trollbane]].

But when we broke ranks, Marran was ready for us. She has an army of soldiers I've never seen before. They call themselves the Red Dawn, but I think they're just the bandits wearing uniforms they took from our supplies!

Help us take out her goons and collect any evidence that shows what they really are!
