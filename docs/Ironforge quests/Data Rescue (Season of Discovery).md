==Notes==
===Outside the Instance===
The first part of this quest takes place in Gnomeregan, but ''outside'' the instance.  You must first get [[White Punch Card]], which is a random drop from the mobs near the instance.  Then you must use the card in a [[Matrix Punchograph 3005-A]], which are located in the Train Depot area (on the raised platform in the center, and by the Workshop (back door) entrance).  This will give you a [[Yellow Punch Card]].

===Inside the Instance===
*[[Matrix Punchograph 3005-B]]s are located in the [[Dormitory]] (just past the [[Clean Zone]]). They will upgrade your Yellow Punch Card to a [[Blue Punch Card]].
*[[Matrix Punchograph 3005-C]]s are located on the platform with {{npc||Electrocutioner 6000}}. They will upgrade your Blue Punch Card to a [[Red Punch Card]].
*[[Matrix Punchograph 3005-D]]s are located on the lower level of the [[Engineering Labs]]. They will upgrade your Red Punch Card to a [[Prismatic Punch Card]].
