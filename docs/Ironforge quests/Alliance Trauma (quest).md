==Objectives==
Travel to [[Theramore Isle|Theramore]] in the [[Dustwallow Marsh]] and speak with [[Doctor Gustaf VanHowzen]] about joining the ranks of [[Alliance Trauma]].

==Description==
Doctor <name>, I presume. It is a pleasure to see you.

I have some wonderful news for you! [[Doctor Gustaf VanHowzen|Gustaf VanHowzen]], chief physician of [[Alliance Trauma]] has made a personal request for you to join him at [[Theramore Isle|Theramore]]. This could be the opportunity of a lifetime! I would give anything to be given a chance to study under the great doctor, Gustaf VanHowzen! Do not squander this opportunity, doctor!

==Completion==
Wonderful. Put on your scrubs, we are to start at once!

==Notes==
*You must have at least 225 skill in [[Tailoring]] to do this quest.
