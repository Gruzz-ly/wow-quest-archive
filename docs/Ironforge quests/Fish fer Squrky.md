==Objectives==
Catch 3 [[Blind Minnow]]s in the pool in the [[Forlorn Cavern]] and feed them to [[Squrky]].
*Feed Squrky (3)

==Description==
Hae I introduced ye tae me pet, Squrky?  I'm teachin' him tae fish all proper-like.  Fer now, I've been feedin' him minnows fished from the pool in this cavern.

Maybe ye'd like tae give it a try?  Just cast yer lure in the pool over there and feed Squrky any minnows ye happen tae fish up.  He'll love ye ferever!
