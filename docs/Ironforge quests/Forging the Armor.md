==Objectives==
Forge the Bulwark of the Mountain King.
* Help Bouldercrag's Ritual
* Present Ignis the Armor Shards
* Bring the [[Molten Metal]] to the [[The Great Anvil|Great Anvil]]
* Forge the [[Bulwark of the Mountain King]]s
* [[Heritage of the Bronzebeard (item)]]

==Description==
It's time to begin. [[Bouldercrag]], [[Ignis]], and I are here to assist you.

You'll be the first [[dwarf]] to forge the Bulwark of the Mountain King in generations. This ol' blacksmith is honored to be able to witness this.

Are ye ready?
