'''A New Beginning''' starts a short introductory questline for players who used the BCC [[Character Boost]]. All [[class]]es, except [[druid]]s, start in front of their respective class trainer. Druids start in [[Thunder Bluff]] instead. The class trainers are: {{ClassIcon|Warlock}} [[Grol'dar]], {{ClassIcon|Rogue}} [[Ormok]], {{ClassIcon|Shaman}} [[Kardris Dreamseeker]], {{ClassIcon|Warrior}} [[Grezz Ragefist]], {{ClassIcon|Hunter}} [[Xor'juul]], {{ClassIcon|Mage}} [[Deino]], and {{ClassIcon|Priest}} [[Zayus]] in Orgrimmar, and {{ClassIcon|Druid}} [[Kym Wildmane]] in Thunder Bluff.

==Objectives==
Meet with a <class> trainer in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
A letter magically appears in the lining of your armor. It reads:

"Welcome to Orgrimmar, capital of the [[Horde]]!

Meet with a <class> trainer to begin your training. They will help prepare you for your journey across Azeroth and beyond."
