==Objectives==
Speak with Greatfather Winter; he is located near the Smokywood Pastures vendor area in Orgrimmar.
 
== Description ==
Have a wondrous Winter Veil, friend! Be sure to say hello to Greatfather Winter, who's currently in Orgrimmar! If you've been nice this year, you'll receive wondrous treats on the day of the Feast of Winter Veil. Be sure to tell him what you hope to get this year! 

Also, be sure to stock up on treats to give those who've been nice this year. We have a great selection to choose from. Fresh from our farm to your plate - it's Smokywood Pastures wholesome goodness!

==Completion==
Ho ho hello. I am Great-father Winter, and I - courtesy of the fine folks of Smokywood Pastures... "Fresh from our farm to your plate, it's Smokywood Pastures wholesome goodness" - would like to wish you a fantastic Feast of Winter Veil.

If you've been good this year, maybe you will receive treats from Smokywood Pastures as your treat for the Feast of Winter Veil. Huzzah.
 
==Notes==
There are three quest givers who can give this quest:
*In [[Orgrimmar]], it is [[Kaymard Copperpinch]] (53,66) in the [[Valley of Strength]]
*In [[Thunder Bluff]], it is [[Whulwert Copperpinch]] (43,58) on the [[lower rise]]
*In [[Undercity]], it is [[Nardstrum Copperpinch]]
