==Objectives==
Speak with [[Loganaar]] in [[Nighthaven]].

==Description==
As you have heard, [[Moonglade]] is the home of the [[Cenarion Circle]], the brotherhood of [[druid]]s. All who dwell there do so in the peace of [[Cenarius]]. It is in that spirit that the trainer Loganaar has extended an invitation to visit him in Moonglade.

You will find him in the village of Nighthaven.
