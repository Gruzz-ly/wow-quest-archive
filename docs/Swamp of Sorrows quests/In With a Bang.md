==Notes==
If you want an easier swim back, make sure you have a [[Silversnap Swim Tonic]] from [[Trade Baron Silversnap]] before leaving in the cannon.

Head outside and talk to Jerry to take a cannon ride. It will set you right on the ship, giving the [[Going Down?]] achievement if you do not already have it. Warning: if you use [[Slow Fall (ability)]] or a similar spell during the cannon ride, you will drift out into deep water and probably die from [[fatigue]].

Once on the ship, take out the Gilblin Tresspassers and Slashers and head to the bow of the ship. The Thousand-Thread-Count Fuse is just behind the main mast near the bow of the ship topside. Then turn back and head below deck.

The Extra-Pure Blasting Powder is in the captain's quarters, on the mid-deck, aft. Further down, the Stack of Questionable Publications is aft in the hold on the bottom deck.
