==Objectives==
Report to [[Watch Captain Parker]] at [[Tower Watch]] in [[Redridge Mountains]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, [[Anduin Wrynn|King Anduin Wrynn]], all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report Watch Captain Parker at Tower Watch in the Redridge Mountains.

Captain Parker's forces have come under attack from marauding [[gnoll]]s and [[Blackrock clan|orc]]s. The people of [[Lakeshire|Lakewood]] are in peril and your aid is needed to safeguard them. Travel east from the border of [[Elwynn Forest]] and into the [[Three Corners]] area of Redridge to reach Tower Watch.

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
