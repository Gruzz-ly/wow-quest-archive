==Objectives==
Collect 6 Owlbeast Pineal Glands from [[owlbeast]]s in {{C|Winterspring}}, then return to the [[Shadowtooth Emissary]] in {{C|Felwood}}.
*[[Owlbeast Pineal Gland]] (6)

==Description==
De [[loa]] have told me of a poison gatherin' strength in {{C|Ashenvale}}, on the tainted [[Demon Fall Canyon (Season of Discovery)|land]] where the great demon [[Mannoroth]] was slain. Someone be drawin' power from the [[pit lord]]'s remains.

They must be usin' illusions to hide their rituals. If you are to stop them, you'll be needin' protectin from their deceptions.

[[Shadowtooth clan|My tribe]] uses an extract from owlbeast glands in an enchantment to discern truth from lies. Collect these glands from the owlbeasts in Winterspring.
