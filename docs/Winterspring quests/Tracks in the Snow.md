==Objectives==
Travel to the hills in {{C|Winterspring}} in [[northern Kalimdor]], north of [[Everlook]], to meet up with [[Estelenn]] and find out what else she needs.

==Description==
You done a good service, so now I help you. Last time I saw [[Van Amburgh]], he had a menagerie of three animals: a great cat, a [[raptor]], and a monstrous bug. He keep dem in cages, whip dem, drive dem to be mean. Cruelty and hunger be his training tools. Fought him in {{C|Feralas}} a while ago, not sure where he be now.

You should go meet up with Estelenn and see if she be needing ya. And be sure to give her my regards.
