== Description ==
All able-bodied members of the Horde are hereby ordered to report to Donova Snowden at Frostfire Hot Springs. Her discoveries about the [[Winterfall]] [[furbolg]] may be the key to dealing with this threat once and for all.

To reach the Frostfire Hot Springs in Winterspring, you must travel east from northern [[Felwood]] and traverse [[Timbermaw Hold]].

Destiny calls!
