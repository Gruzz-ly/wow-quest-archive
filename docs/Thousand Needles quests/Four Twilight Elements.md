== Description ==
The [[Twilight's Hammer]] have summoned {{NPC||Animus||a monstrosity}}. It's a combination of the four elements infused with the power of their special ore, [[Elementium]].

If you want to save your precious [[Thousand Needles]] from total destruction, you're going to have to destroy it. But first, you'll need a few things to get through its protective shielding.

Go east into the [[Twilight Withering|Withering]] and bring back the four Twilight elements.
