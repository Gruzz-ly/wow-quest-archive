==Description==
[[Eredar]] are adept at manipulating the [[fel]] that courses through [[Argus|this world]]. Those who do not shape it into living weapons build other armaments, like the Spires of Woe.

The spires will make it difficult for even a small force to penetrate the main stronghold. We must dismantle enough of them to clear the way before we advance upon it.

==Completion==
