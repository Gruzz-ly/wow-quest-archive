==Description==
High Inquisitor Raalgar has some connection to the [[fel]] here on [[Argus]] that makes him unkillable. Long ago, the [[naaru]] imprisoned him on the Arcatraz.

When the forces of [[Kael'thas Sunstrider]] attacked the citadel, we were forced to flee on the [[Exodar]].

Some [[Burning Legion|Legion]] prisoners escaped the Arcatraz when last the Legion invaded [[Azeroth]], but Raalgar did not. Stop them from freeing him and I will study the source of his immortality so it can be broken.

I can get you close. The rest is up to you.
