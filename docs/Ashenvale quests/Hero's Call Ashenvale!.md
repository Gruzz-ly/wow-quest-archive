==Hero's Call==
The [[Horde]] has sent its forces to Ashenvale, seeking to rout out the [[Night elf|night elves]] that live in the forest.

Stop the Horde's desecration of the night elf homeland.

[Assist the [[Sentinels]]]

==Objectives==
Report to [[Sentinel Shyela]] at [[Orendil's Retreat|Ordanil's Retreat]]{{sic}} in northwestern [[Ashenvale]].

==Description==
By order of his royal highness, [[Anduin Wrynn|King Anduin Wrynn]], all able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Sentinel Shyela at Orendil's Retreat in northwestern Ashenvale.

The [[Horde]]'s aggression in Ashenvale cannot be allowed to stand! Your help is needed to repel the invaders and restore the sacred forest. Head south through the [[Twilight Vale]] in [[Darkshore]] to reach the forest of Ashenvale.

For the glory and honor of the Alliance!
