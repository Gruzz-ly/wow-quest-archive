==Completion==
You've certainly begun to prove yourself to us, <name>.  As a reward, please take this care package!  Inside it you'll find rations and bandages that are usable only inside [[Warsong Gulch]].

As you continue to prove yourself inside the Gulch, you'll find that these items will become available to you for purchase.  Rations become available when you are Friendly with us, and bandages when you are Honored.
