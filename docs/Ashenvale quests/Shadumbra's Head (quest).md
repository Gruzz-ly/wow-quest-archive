This quest begins with a drop from [[Shadumbra]], a level 24 [[nightsaber]] which patrols the area around [[Raynewood Retreat]].

==Objectives==
Bring Shadumbra's Head to [[Captain Goggath]] at [[Hellscream's Watch]].
*[[Shadumbra's Head]] (provided)

==Description==
The once swift Shadumbra now lies still as you claim victory over it. 

This legendary creature is dear to the night elves. Its head should serve as an intimidating trophy, and as a sign of the end of the Alliance in Ashenvale.

Captain Goggath at Hellscream's Watch will no doubt be delighted to receive the head of this slain nightsaber.
