==Objectives==
Gather 10 Dreamroot in the {{C|Ashenvale}} [[Nightmare Incursions]] and return to [[Field Captain Hannalah]].

==Description==
While these demons distract us here, the [[Emerald Dream]] is being corrupted into [[Emerald Nightmare|nightmare]]. Quick, travel through the Emerald Dream Portal in [[Bough Shadow]] and collect Dreamroot from within the Nightmare Incursions. We can harness its magic to reverse the damage. You will need the assistance of an herbalist.
