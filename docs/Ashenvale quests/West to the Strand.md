==Objectives==
Speak with [[Talen]] at the [[Blackfathom Camp]] in [[Ashenvale]].

==Description==
I have a friend who could make good use of your particular talents. His name is [[Talen]] and he's an archaeologist of sorts. Recently he's been fascinated with the upheaval of the old city of Zoram and its treasures.

If you're willing to lend him a hand, you can find him at the Blackfathom Camp down the path to the west.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*165 [[XP]] (or {{coppercost|90}} at max level)
*+10 [[reputation]] with [[Darnassus (faction)|Darnassus]]

==Completion==

Very well, let's get started.

==Notes==
Quest is only available if you haven't completed [[The Ancient Statuettes]] (and the quest isn't in your quest list)
