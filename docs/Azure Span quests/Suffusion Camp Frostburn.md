'''Suffusion Camp: Frostburn''' is one of two [[Fyrakk Assaults]]. This one takes place at {{co|55|32|Azure Span}} in central [[Azure Span]]. For more details on how to complete this world quest, see the [[Fyrakk Assaults#Frostburn|Frostburn]] section of the assault article.

==Objectives==
* 5 [[Ward of Igira|Wards of Igira]] collected (Optional)
* Suffusion Crucible looted
* Crucible guardian slain
* 5 [[Ward of Fyrakk|Wards of Fyrakk]] collected (Optional)
* Suffusion Mold looted {{co|55.4|35.2|Ohn'ahran Plains}}
* Forgemaster slain

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +250 [[reputation]] with [[Iskaara Tuskarr]]
