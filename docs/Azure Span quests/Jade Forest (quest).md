==Description==
Daughter of [[Malygos]] and [[Saragosa]], [[Kirygosa]] survived the turbulent [[Nexus War]] that ripped away her life and family.

Afterwards, Kirygosa sought a permanent summer in [[Stranglethorn]]. Finding warmth but not quite peace, she moved on to [[Dawn's Blossom]], a small [[village]] nestled in the [[Jade Forest]].

The [[blue dragonflight]] would welcome her return.
