'''Dragon Isles Supplies''' quests become available at Renown level 11,... with the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]].

==Rewards==
You will receive one of:
*[[Small Iskaaran Supply Pack]]
*[[Large Iskaaran Supply Pack]]
*[[Overflowing Iskaaran Supply Pack]]

==Completion==
;Small Iskaaran Supply Pack
Greetings adventurer. Please, take these supplies to aid you in your efforts to help our community!

;Large Iskaaran Supply Pack
*Welcome, <race>. I have more supplies for you in thanks for your continued support of our community. May your steps be sure and your catch secure!
*Adventurer, your help has been instrumental in helping out our community and people. We've put together additional supplies for you and hope you are able to continue to help us! Many thanks.
*Friend, your help has been invaluable in helping out our community and people. We've put together additional supplies for you and hope you are able to continue to help us!

;Overflowing Iskaaran Supply Pack
Ah, our paths meet again, <name>. I am glad you have returned to us safely. Your deeds have made you honored among the Iskaara. Please, take these supplies as a token of our thanks!
