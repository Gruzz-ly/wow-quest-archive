'''Academic Assistance''' begins the [[patch 10.0.7]] portion of the [[Winterpelt Furbolg]] storyline. [[Sonova Snowden]] will mail a letter to players who have completed the [[Azure Span storyline#Winterpelt Hollow|Winterpelt Hollow]] and [[Azure Span storyline#Rustpine Den|Rustpine Den]] quest chains in the Azure Span.

{{book|I cracked it!|content=
<Name>! I've made a breakthrough with tha Winterpelt dialect. Ya helped get tha ball rollin' on this, so I felt like I ought ta keep ya apprised of the situation.

Meet me in Theron's Watch and I'll tell ya all about it.}}

Attached to the letter is [[Sonova's Request]], which starts this quest.

The quest can also be started from the [[Adventure Guide]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Sonova Snowden]] in [[Theron's Watch]].
