'''Tuskarr Fishing Gear''' is the unlock quest for [[Tuskarr Fishing Gear]]. [[Rowie]] will run up and offer this quest when you reach Renown level 5 with the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]].

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Tavio]] at [[Iskaara]] in The [[Azure Span]].

==Description==
Hi again, <name>.

<[[Rowie]] looks around and hums a little tune.>

Oh right! Tavio was making me a new net and said she could make you one too. She can make some other fishing gear too if you bring her all the stuff.

You should go talk to her.

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you:
*{{Abilities|Tavio Fishing Gear Crafting|Tavio will craft fishing gear and upgrades for you.|id=391473|icon=inv_fishing_netlinen02}}

You will also receive:
* {{cost|2|69|15}}
* 1,250 [[XP]]

==Completion==
My family is known as the best weavers of nets and crafters of harpoons. If you bring me the supplies I need, I can craft some new tools for you.
