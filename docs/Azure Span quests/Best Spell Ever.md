'''Best Spell Ever''' is a mage-only quest inside the [[Duck Cave]] of the [[Upper Frostlands]] in the [[Azure Span]]. It teaches [[Polymorph]]: Duck! To access the cave and the tome, [[Blink]] through the ice wall at {{co|66.5|31.7|Azure Span}} with a Duck standing guard.

Inside the cave, alongside [[Millhouse Manastorm|Millhouse]] and [[Millificent Manastorm]] is a magical tome: [[Manastorming For Beginners]], which starts this quest.

==Objectives==
Read "Manastorming for Beginners" and discover Millhouse's secret spell.

==Description==
[[File:Manastorming For Beginners.jpg|thumb|Manastorming For Beginners]]

<Gently levitating and emanating with arcane magic, this enchanted book contains countless spells and enchantments created by the chaotic yet beautiful mind of [[Millhouse Manastorm]].>
