'''A Living History''' begins the Skill 50 portion of the [[Winterpelt Furbolg]] storyline.

==Objectives==
Follow the markers to find the hidden [[Winterpelt Furbolg|Winterpelt]] elders.
*Find the first route marker {{co|61.8|18.3|Azure Span}}
*Find the last route marker {{co|57.3|20.1|Azure Span}}
*Find the Hidden Elders {{co|57.2|21.3|Azure Span}}

==Description==
[[File:A Living History - last route marker.jpg|thumb|Saza at the last route marker, the bridge]]
The Winterpelt finally trust us enough to ask for aid.

We must nurture this trust so we may one day clasp their paws in friendship!

This Winterpelt, [[Jyrra "Fuzzy Feet" Coldsoar|Jyrra]] of the, uh, Fuzzy Feet, says their elders went into hiding during the [[Primalist]] attack on their village. I don't yet understand their language, but it seems the elders left markers to help the clan find them later.

It's time to let the Winterpelt elders know it's safe to come home. Will you convey this message? It seems a simple enough task.
