'''Gnolls Must Die''' is a [[bonus objective]] taking place in [[Snowhide Camp]] in the [[Azure Span]].

==Objectives==
*[[Snowhide tribe|Snowhide]] [[Gnoll]]s disrupted
*[[Snoll the Icebreaker]] slain

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{Cost|161|48|88}}
*29,450 [[XP]]
*+400 [[reputation]] with [[Iskaara Tuskarr]]

==Notes==
To fill up the bar,
*interact with [[Snowhide Shrine]]s, [[Snowhide Pennant]]s, and [[Weapon Rack (Azure Span)|Weapon Racks]],
*kill [[Snowhide Shaman]], [[Snowhide Brute]]s, [[Snowhide Mongrel]]s, and [[Snowhide Yeti Hunter]]s.
