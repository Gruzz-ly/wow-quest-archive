'''Into the Azure''' begins the [[Azure Span storyline]], offered by [[Merithra]] immediately after completing the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]] at the [[Ancient Bough]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Masud the Wise]] in the [[Forkriver Crossing]].

==Description==
We have done our part. Though I must remain here to watch over [[Emerald Oathstone|the Oathstone]] and guide the [[green dragonflight]], know that you leave here with our gratitude and faith.

I have received word the [[Kirin Tor]] are seeking help in claiming the [[Azure Oathstone]]. Helping them with their Oathstone will bring us one step closer to healing [[Azeroth]].

Find [[Pakina]] and Masud the Wise of the Kirin Tor. They will instruct you further. Safe journey and pleasant dreams, <name>.
