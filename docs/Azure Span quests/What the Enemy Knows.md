==Description==
It is vital that we determine the Sundered Flame's mission in [[Ruins of Karnthar|these ruins]] and how it relates to the [[arcane]] surge.

We'll do this the old fashioned way: reconnaissance and espionage.

The ruins are overrun with Sundered Flame devotees. I am sure some of them carry orders that may illuminate their mysterious goals.

Use any means necessary to obtain these documents.
