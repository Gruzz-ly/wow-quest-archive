'''Wild Arcana''' is a PvP world quest at {{co|51.6|24.7|Azure Span}} at the free-for-all [[Training Grounds (Azure Span)|Training Grounds]] of the [[Cobalt Assembly]] in north-central [[Azure Span]]. Much like forced-PvP world quests of past expansions, players can complete objectives by killing enemies in the area should there not be flesh-and-blood targets active.

==Objectives==
* Destroy [[Unstable Elemental|Unstable Arcana]] or Kill [[Player]]s (100%)

==Description==

* +100 [[reputation]] with the [[Valdrakken Accord]]
