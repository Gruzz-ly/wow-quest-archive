==Description==
You were the one that came back with the others from [[Brackenhide]], weren't you? Then you probably know what happened to [[Kaqiata]]...

Her son, Rowie, has been distraught ever since Brena and the others returned without her. I'll stay here in case he comes back, but could you look for him? 

I think he ran off towards the ice cave to the east. He said something about trying to fight the [[yeti]] in there!
