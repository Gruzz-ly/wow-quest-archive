==Description==
[[Kalecgos]] and I are under heavy fire in the skies above the mountain. The [[primalists]] are unrelenting and their army endless.

Soon, Kalecgos and I may be overrun by their forces.

But I have a plan.

At the base of the mountain is a disturbed [[orc]] named [[Ruvin Stonegrinder]]. The [[Kirin Tor]] fought her numerous times.

She holds a device that can absorb and redirect [[elemental]] energies.

Take it from her, then meet me further in.
