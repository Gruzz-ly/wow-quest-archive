'''Words of the Winterpelt''' begins the skill 100 chapter of the [[Winterpelt Furbolg]] storyline.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Kranac Sagesnow]].

==Description==
The [[Winterpelt Furbolg|Winterpelt]] Chief sent word while ye were out that he wanted tae speak with ye again.

And, thanks tae all the work ye been puttin' in, we could actually understand the message!

We're on the cusp o' somethin' great here, <name>. Think of it... The trade routes we could establish! The cultural exchange that could take place! The papers I could publish!

Well? What are ye standin' around for? Go see what he wants!
