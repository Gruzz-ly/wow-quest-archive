'''Stop the Spread''' is a bonus objective in the [[Brackenhide Outskirts]] in the [[Azure Span]] available after starting [[Breaching the Brackenhide]].

==Objectives==
* [[Brackenhide]] [[decay]] spread contained (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|161|48|88}}
* 29,450 [[XP]]
* +400 [[reputation]] with [[Iskaara Tuskarr]]

==Notes==
Kill the following enemies or complete the listed tasks:
*4%: [[Brackenhide Rotflinger]]s
*3%: [[Brackenhide Deadeye]]s, [[Brackenhide Scrapper]]s
*2%: [[Decay Totem]]s, [[Rotting Treant]]s
*1%: [[Oozing Decay]], the three roots for [[Breaching the Brackenhide]]

While on this quest, players are buffed with Kalecgos' Aid:
:{{Abilities|Kalecgos' Aid|One of Kalecgos' Mirror Images has a chance to appear and aid you in combat for a short duration.|id=388320|icon=spell_nature_brilliance}}

When he spawns in, he can say one of:
*{{text|say|Kalecgos|Hang in there!}}
*{{text|say|Kalecgos|I've got your back!}}
*{{text|say|Kalecgos|On your left!}}
*{{text|say|Kalecgos|You're not alone!}}
