'''Broken Traditions, Broken Bodies''' begins the "Rustpine Den" chapter of the [[Azure Span storyline]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Garz]] and Narn on the way to [[Rustpine Den]].
*Speak to [[Narn]] {{co|65.6|54.9|Azure Span}}

==Description==
My fellow [[furbolg]] are no longer themselves. They have done... bad things. Now, they have become worse things.

Furbolg has never fought furbolg!

My brother and I escaped them. I came here looking for help. He remains by [[Rustpine clan|our clan]]'s den.

Help us before our clan is lost!
