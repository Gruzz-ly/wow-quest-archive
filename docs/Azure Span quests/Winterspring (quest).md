==Description==
[[Haleh]], Matron Protectorate, presides over [[Winterspring]]. Even after defeating the power hungry mage [[Umbranse]], she continues to find and dispel danger in her homeland. As the last living [[consort]] of [[Malygos]], she tirelessly upholds his legacy as a guardian of magic.

Her return would greatly strengthen the [[blue dragonflight]].
