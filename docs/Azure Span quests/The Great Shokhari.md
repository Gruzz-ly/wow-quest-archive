==Description==
Of all da animals I have seen here, Shokahri,{{sic}} da great sky vixen, be da most proud.

It be a shame she preyed on a [[gnoll]] [[shaman]], cause now she be crazed with [[decay]]. When I saw her eating one of her kits, I knew something was not right.

I took da kits away and brought them here, but Shokhari attacked me, swiping me good. I almost didn't make it.

Shokhari must be put down. If da decay rots more of her mind, she be coming for us soon.
