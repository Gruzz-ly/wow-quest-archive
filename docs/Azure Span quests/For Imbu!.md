'''For Imbu!''' is a rare elite world quest located in [[Imbu]] in southeastern [[Azure Span]]. As an elite quest, progress can be gained in a raid group.

==Objectives==
* Imbu liberated (100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Hanu]]|Our enemies have breached Imbu! We will not let this stand. They must be stopped.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 11,900 [[XP]]
* +250 [[reputation]] with the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]]
* +50 [[reputation]] with the [[Valdrakken Accord]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Hanu|Well fought. You've given our allies a fighting chance in reclaiming Imbu.}}
