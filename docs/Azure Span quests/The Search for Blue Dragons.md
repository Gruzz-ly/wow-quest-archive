*'''Theramore''' - Investigate a cadre of [[Hedge wizard|rogue mages]] in the [[ruins of Theramore]]. {{Text|green|Click to view the scouting report}} → [[Theramore (quest)]]
*'''Winterspring''' - Join [[Haleh]] in her artifact work in [[Winterspring]]. {{Text|green|Click to view the scouting report}} → [[Winterspring (quest)]]

Pick one quest, and this one is automatically completed.
