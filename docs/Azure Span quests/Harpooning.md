'''Harpooning''' becomes available at Renown level 7 with the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]]. This quest has no rewards; it exists only to direct you to [[Tavio]], who can craft you an [[Iskaaran Harpoon]].

==Completion==
Well now, you're a fast learner! Your fishing skills continue to improve.

Let's see how you do with a spear. I'll craft for you your very first [[tuskarr]] harpoon. We shall see what you can land with it!

I'm sure you won't fail to impress.
