'''Stolen Tuskarr Totems''' becomes available after reaching Renown level 2 with the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]]. Completing this quest allows you to find [[Sacred Tuskarr Totem]]s.

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you:
*{{Abilities|Stolen Totems|Unlock the ability to identify Tuskarr Totems that you might find in Gnoll, Furbolg and Tuskarr treasures throughout the Dragon Isles.|id=388926|icon=inv_10_dungeonjewelry_tuskarr_trinket_2_color3}}

==Completion==
You there, young one! Our warriors take totems with them when they venture out into the [[Dragon Isles]]. Our totems are part of our livelihood--they are very important to us. Our enemies delight in taking them as trophies, and we can't let that happen! Please, return any totems you find.
