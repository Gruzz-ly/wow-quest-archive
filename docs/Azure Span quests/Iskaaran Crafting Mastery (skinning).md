'''Iskaaran Crafting Mastery''' becomes available after reaching Renown 24 with the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]].

==Completion==
Ah, our aspiring skinner has returned. I can tell by your hands you have been hard at work. Excellent!

On a normal day in a normal time, I would help find a master to guide you. But this is not a normal day or time, and we must manage with what we have. Please, accept these notes to help you master your craft. Your work and your bravery make you a valued friend of the tuskarr.
