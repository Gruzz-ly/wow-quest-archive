==Objectives==
Defeat 4 Mawsworn Shacklers and 6 Deadeyes at the [[Tremaculum]] in The [[Maw]].
*[[Mawsworn Shackler]] slain (4)
*[[Mawsworn Deadeye]] slain (6)

==Description==
The shacklers in the Tremaculum are an awful sort.

[[Soul]]s are tortured there until they turn malevolent. They are then outfitted as guards so the cycle can continue.

If you wish to slow the rate at which [[the Jailer]] raises his armies, start at the source. 

Disrupt the cycle at the Tremaculum.
