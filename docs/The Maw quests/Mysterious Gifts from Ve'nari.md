'''Mysterious Gifts from Ve'nari''' is the paragon reward quest for gaining 10,000 reputation with [[Ve'nari]] at {{text|exalted|Appreciative}} (exalted) standing with her.

==Completion==
I'm impressed by your sheer tenacity to survive while remaining somewhat useful, Maw Walker. You remind me of myself when I was much younger and naive. 

Take this and continue your work... somewhere less conspicuous.
