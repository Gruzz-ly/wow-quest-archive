'''The Cost of Death''' is a weekly quest with [[Ve'nari]], trading 800 [[Stygia]] for 1000 points of standing with her.

==Progress==
Here to trade, Maw Walker?
*800x [[Stygia]]

==Completion==
With so many more interlopers in the [[Maw]] I need to secure my Stygia supply. Your efforts to keep me supplied will not go unnoticed and you always know where to find me.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* Maw Survivalist's Token (+1000 reputation with [[Ve'nari]])
