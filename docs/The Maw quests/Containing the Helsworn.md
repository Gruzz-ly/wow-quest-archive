'''Containing the Helsworn''' is an elite [[world quest]] set in [[Desmotaeron]] and [[Helgarde Keep]] of the [[Maw]]. Players must have completed chapter five of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]], through [[Victory in Our Name]], before this world quest will be offered to them.

==Objectives==
*[[Desmotaeron]] disrupted (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[The Primus]]|Our enemies amass outside [[Helgarde Keep]]. Thin their numbers, before they have the chance to strike.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 8,150 [[XP]]
* +200 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|The Primus|As their bravado gives way to fear, our foes will stay their hand... for now.}}

==Notes==
Nearly all of the targets (with the exception of the named units) are outside [[Helgarde Keep]] proper. Fill the bar by defeating the following:
;7-8%:
*[[Traitor Balthier]], [[Deomen the Vortex]], [[Mor'geth]], [[Ylva]], [[Maldraxxi Harvester]]
;4%:
*[[Frekki]], [[Girs]], [[Hrodvetni]], [[Vanargun]], [[Vargir]], [[Chained Dusksavage]], [[Helsworn Beastlord]], [[Helsworn Inquisitor]],[[Helsworn Soulbreaker]], [[Mawsworn Deathsinger]], [[Maldraxxi Defector]], [[Stygian Punisher]]
;2%:
*[[Damnation Stalker]], [[Helsworn Soulseeker]], [[Starving Corpsehound]]
