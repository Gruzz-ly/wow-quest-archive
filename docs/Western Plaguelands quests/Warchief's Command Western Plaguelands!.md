The Alliance forces are advancing into the ruined city of Andorhal, seeking to claim it for themselves. We must counter their them [''sic''] before they can gain a foothold there!

Cross the [[Bulwark]] at the eastern end of [[Tirisfal Glades]] and follow the roads southest [''sic''] to Andorhal and meet up with our forces there.
