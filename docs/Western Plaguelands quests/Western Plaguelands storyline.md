# Western Plaguelands storyline

[[File:WorldMap-WesternPlaguelands.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Western Plaguelands]]''' is a contested level 35-40 zone in north-central [[Lordaeron]] on the [[Eastern Kingdoms]]. Players are led here from either the [[Cape of Stranglethorn]] or the [[Hinterlands]]. The progress of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Western Plaguelands Quests]].

Once players have concluded the storyline, their next stop is just across the [[Thondroril River]] in [[Eastern Plaguelands]] to start the [[Fiona's Caravan]] chain in the [[Eastern Plaguelands storyline]] with the quest [[Into the Woods]].
