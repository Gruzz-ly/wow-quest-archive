== Objectives ==
Use the Bloodtalon Whistle to rescue 12 [[Lost Bloodtalon Hatchling]]s.
* Bloodtalon Hatchlings rescued (12)
* [[Bloodtalon Whistle]] (provided)

== Description ==
I actually have somethin' really important dat I could use help with, if ya be willin', mon.

Our hatchlins' are bein' stolen by someone on da next island. At first I thought dey were just runnin' away or that nature be claimin' dem, but we are missin' more than a dozen of da little guys now.

Please head to [[Zalazane's Fall (subzone)|Zalazane's Fall]] to da northwest and point da little hatchlins back dis way. Poor tings.
