# The Rise of the Darkspear

This is the first [[quest]] into the [[troll starting experience]]. This quest will direct the [[adventurer]] to meet its [[class trainer]]. Troll [[Zuni]] will start together with the player. Every [[Horde]] [[race]] and [[class]], except [[Paladin]]s and [[Death Knight]]s are able to start this quest.

== Objectives ==
Meet with <[[#Notes|NPC]]> in the [[Darkspear Training Grounds]]. 

== Description ==
Tha [[Darkspear tribe]] have long been victims. Outcasts. We found ourselves fleein' our homes time 'n time again... but dis time, it's not fleein' dat drives us here.

[[Vol'jin]] has offered us leadership through wisdom, and it has kept us alive dis long. Now dat same wisdom offers portents of a chaotic future for tha Horde unda [[Garrosh Hellscream|Garrosh Hellsceam]].

No, dis time we not fleein'. Dis time, we are fightin' for a home dat will last.

It's time to begin ya trainin', <name>. Speak with <[[#Notes|NPC]]> to the east.

Ya actually have an intense look about ya though. <[[#Notes|type]]> eyes. You might have the makings of a real <class>.

Lets not waste anymore time 'den.
