==Objectives==
Defeat 10 Kor'kron Overseers, 5 Kor'kron Subjugators, and Gul'tar the Subjugator.
*[[Kor'kron Overseer (Echo Isles)|Kor'kron Overseer]] slain (10)
*[[Kor'kron Subjugator]] slain (5)
*[[Gul'tar the Subjugator]] slain

==Description==
The [[Kor'kron]] have been trained to be the finest, most loyal soldiers of the [[Warchief]]. Their presence here means that [[Garrosh Hellscream|Garrosh]] fears an uprising.

[[Vol'jin]] and his people are as much a part of the [[Horde]] as any [[orc]].

We must break this occupation and free the [[troll]]s. It pains me to say this, but you must eliminate enough of the Kor'kron so that the trolls can retake their city. Then meet me at [[Darkspear Hold]] so we might challenge their officer directly.

These are troubling times. But for now, I must see to Vol'jin's people.
