'''Return to the Echo Isles''' begins the [[Troll (playable)|troll]] [[heritage armor]] questline, added during the [[Dark Heart campaign]]. It starts with [[Zi'guma]] in the [[Valley of Spirits]].{{Co|32.70|64.72|Orgrimmar}}

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Rokhan]] in the [[Echo Isles]].

==Description==
