==Objectives==
Remove 3 ritual circles with [[Lukou]]'s aid.
*[[Ritual Circle (heritage)|Ritual Circle]] removed (3)
*Return to [[Rokhan]] in the [[Echo Isles]].

==Description==
[[Witch Doctor Tzadah|Dat witch doctor]] has stolen de trial. She mocks us, trying to eat de life of living things to free [[Mueh'zala]]. We still have influence over it, though. 

Together, we will overwhelm her evil circles. When you find a circle, call upon me, and we will push death aside.
