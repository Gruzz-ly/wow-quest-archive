==Description==
If we mean to destroy Zalazane then we gonna need something that can damage that [[Ghost|spirit]].

Da [[Glaive of Vol'jin]] might be what we need to do this.

Zalazane be hovering near [[Zalazane's Fall (subzone)|where he fell]] so many years ago. [[Rokhan]] and [[Talanji]] have already gone to face him.

Go, slay Zalazane, and then plunge da glaive into the spirit before it can escape. Let us hope de bargain [[Vol'jin]] made be powerful enough to destroy this enemy.
