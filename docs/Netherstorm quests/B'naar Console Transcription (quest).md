This quest is part of the '''Naaru Technology''' quest chain.  This quest chain starts with [[Naaru Technology]].

==Objectives==
Deliver the B'naar Console Transcription to Anchorite Karja at Area 52 in Netherstorm.

Completing quests for the Aldor will cause your Scryers reputation to decrease.
*[[B'naar Console Transcription]] (provided)

==Description==
You try to transcribe the shapes as you see them flash across your eyes. The results are less than exact, but should serve as adequate evidence to prove or disprove the Aldor's suspicions.
