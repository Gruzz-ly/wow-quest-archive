This quest is part of the '''Report to Spymaster Thalodien''' quest chain.  This quest chain starts with [[Report to Spymaster Thalodien]].

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Veronia]] when you're ready to depart to [[Manaforge Coruu]]. Once there, speak to [[Caledis Brightdawn]].

Completing quests for the [[Scryers]] will cause your [[Aldor]] reputation level to decrease.

==Description==
All arrangements have been made to take you to where our [[Spy|spies]] are hiding. Just come see me when you're ready and we'll get you on your way.
