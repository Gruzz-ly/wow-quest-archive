The [[goblin]]s of Area 52 in [[Netherstorm]] have called for assistance from any able bodied champions willing to provide them the support they need in their endevours.{{sic}}

The [[King]] feels their position and aid in the region will provide a tactical advantage against the nearby forces of [[Kael'thas]].

Area 52 can be reached by following the northeastern road of [[Blade's Edge Mountains]] until you see the world crumbling away into the [[Twisting Nether]].
