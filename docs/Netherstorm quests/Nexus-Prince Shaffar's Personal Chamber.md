Repeatable quest to turn in a [[Mark of the Nexus-King]] for a [[Shaffar's Stasis Chamber Key]]. You need the Stasis Chamber Key to summon [[Yor]] in [[Mana-Tombs]]. This key is a one-use item, meaning you will need to farm up a Mark each time you wish to summon [[Yor]]. 

Once you are [[Exalted]] with the [[Consortium]] you can obtain [[The Eye of Haramad]] which is a permanent item.

The [[Mark of the Nexus-King]] drop from mobs summoned from [[Ethereum Stasis Chamber]]s in Blade's Edge Mountains.

==Objectives==
You will need:
* [[Mark of the Nexus-King]]

==Description==
Only the most steadfast and battle-hardened of adventurers will take me up on this offer, <name>. Should you wish to face the horrors of the second stasis chamber in the [[Mana-Tombs]], I am able to synthesize a key. Bring me a [[Mark of the Nexus-King]] and I will make it so.

==Rewards==
You will receive: 
* [[Shaffar's Stasis Chamber Key]]
* Reputation [[The Consortium]] +500

==Completion==
As promised, here is the key to Shaffar's personal stasis chamber.
