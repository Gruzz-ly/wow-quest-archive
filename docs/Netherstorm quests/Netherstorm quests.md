# Netherstorm quests

[[File:WorldMap-Netherstorm.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]
'''[[Netherstorm]]''' is a level 25-30 zone in [[Outland]]. Its storyline can be roughly divided into three parts - helping the [[Braintrust of Orbital Operations and Mechanics|B.O.O.M.]] at [[Area 52]], helping the [[Consortium]], and helping the mages trapped in [[Kirin'Var Village]].

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Into the Nether]].

Optional breadcrumbs: 
* [[Assist Exarch Orelis]] or [[Report to Spymaster Thalodien]] (from [[Shattrath City]])
* [[A Mystifying Vision]] or [[A Strange Vision]] (from [[Nagrand]])
* [[Parts for the Rocket-Chief]] or [[Out of This World Produce!]] (from [[Blade's Edge Mountains]])
* [[Hero's Call: Netherstorm!]] or [[Warchief's Command: Netherstorm!]]
