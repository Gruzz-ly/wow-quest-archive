==Objectives==
Investigate the [[Umbra Hollows]] with [[Alleria Windrunner|Alleria]] and [[Locus-Walker]].
* Meet Alleria in Umbra Hollows
* Enter the [[Void Tear]]
* [[Dismantled Portal]] examined
* [[Image of Saprish]] examined
* Voidforge examined
* Meet Alleria and Locus-Walker

==Description==
That was Locus-Walker. He taught me all that I know about the [[Void]], and he still has more to teach me.

If he is here, then the [[ethereal]]s' presence only intrigues me further. I had thought him the only one of his kind, but now that I have seen another tangling with the Void...

Come, we must play his game if we want to learn what he knows. He appears to have gone toward the Umbra Hollows to the north.
