'''The Terrace of the Makers''' is part of [[the Sons of Hodir quest chain]].

==Objectives==
Find [[Thorim]] at the southeastern edge of the [[Terrace of the Makers]], just northwest of [[Dun Niffelem]]. 

==Description==
It is time, <name>. I am prepared to face [[Loken]] now.

Let us make our way to the Terrace of the Makers and face my brother's forces. The fool will regret ever crossing me. 

==Completion==
We're here, <name>. My brother's forces will learn the meaning of defeat today.

==Notes==
Thorim is standing on a ledge due west from the upper tier of [[Brann's Base-Camp]] at {{coords|56.3|51.4|Storm Peaks}}. Southwest of the field full of iron dwarf and earthen combatants at the same elevation as the [[Plain of Echoes]].
