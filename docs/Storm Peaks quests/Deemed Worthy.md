'''Deemed Worthy''' is part of [[The Sons of Hodir quest chain]].

==Objectives==
[[Brijana]] wants you to go to [[Brunnhildar Village]] and speak with [[Astrid Bjornrittar]]. 

==Description==
You have succeeded where many have failed, sister. Whether it be by strength and courage or mere luck remains to be seen.

Go back to the village and seek out Astrid Bjornrittar. Tell her of your accomplishments. But be warned. She is not easily impressed. 

==Completion==
So, sister, you think that completing a few simple chores makes you a heroine?

You shall learn....
