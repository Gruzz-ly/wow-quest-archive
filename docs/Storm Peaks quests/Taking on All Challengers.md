'''Taking on All Challengers''' is part of [[The Sons of Hodir quest chain]].

==Objectives==
[[Lok'lira the Crone]] in [[Brunnhildar Village]] wants you to defeat 6 Victorious Challengers.
* [[Victorious Challenger]] slain: (6)

==Description==
Let's get you started. The good news is that by defeating [[Agnetta Tyrsdottar|Agnetta]], you've earned yourself a spot in the next round of matches.

The bad news is that all your opponents will also have defeated someone in the first round. Do not expect pushovers.
