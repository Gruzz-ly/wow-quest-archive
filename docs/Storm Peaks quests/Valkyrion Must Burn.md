==Objectives==
Use the [[Valkyrion Harpoon Gun]]s to burn 6 Dry Haystacks.
* Fires Started (6)
* [[Incendiary Harpoons]] (provided)

==Description==
The crate is filled with incendiary harpoons. You recall seeing harpoon guns and dry stacks of hay outside.

Perhaps [[Iva the Vengeful|Iva]] will appreciate the extra effort and reward you appropriately.
