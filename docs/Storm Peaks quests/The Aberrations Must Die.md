==Objectives==
[[Gretta the Arbiter]] in [[Brunnhildar Village]] wants you to go to [[Valkyrion]] and obtain [[Vial of Frost Oil|Vials of Frost Oil]] from the [[Valkyrion Aspirant]]s. Use the Vials of Frost Oil to destroy 30 [[Plagued Proto-Drake Egg]]s.
* [[Plagued Proto-Drake Egg]]s destroyed (30)

==Description==
It is not enough to demonstrate your prowess among your peers. You must also fight our enemies, dear sister.

The [[Valkyrion]] traitors continue to mock our noble drake-riding traditions by rearing plagued proto-drakes.

Seize the frost oil they wield and use it to freeze their abominable plagued eggs.
