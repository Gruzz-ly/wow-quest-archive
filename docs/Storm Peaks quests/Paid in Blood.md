==Objectives==
Absorb Hati's essence into the Thunderspark at the [[Silithus: The Wound|Wound in Silithus]].
* Speak to [[Grif Wildheart]] in Silithus
* [[Hati]]'s [[Essence of Hati (Silithus)|Essence]] absorbed
* Rendezvous with [[Mimiron]]
* [[Thunderspark]] (provided)

==Description==
If Mimiron's fancy gadgets be right, then the other half o' Hati is near the wound in Silithus.
