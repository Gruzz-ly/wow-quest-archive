==Description==
After driving us from the farmland and infesting our crops with insects, the [[drogbar]] placed wicked totems that further poison our harvest.

I can only hope that if you destroy them, we'll be able to heal the land using [[Jale Rivermane|Jale]]'s water magic.

Thank you for coming to help us, <name>. We are but simple farmers and fishermen, and have little prowess in combat.
