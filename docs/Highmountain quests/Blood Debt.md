==Description==
You have proven a much greater ally than the [[Bloodtotem tribe|Bloodtotem]] ever were. With my focus crystal, my wounds are healed. I fear [[Frag]] was too far gone to revive.

Torok and his Bloodtotem will suffer for what they have done to us. Let's pay them a visit before they can mount an attack against the cave of my people.

==Progress==
[[Drogbar]] are not violent by nature, despite our size. But the Bloodtotem crossed us, and they pay the debt in blood.

==Completion==
You have earned my trust. The [[Stonedark clan|Stonedark]] are not yet safe from our brothers led by [[Dargrul the Underking]]. We would willingly join [[Mayla Highmountain]] and her tauren against them.

==Notes==
While killing Feltotem at Torok's Bluff:
:{{text|yell|Torok Bloodtotem|No outsider will wield the [[:Hammer of Khaz'goroth]]! Flay them alive!}}

While in the area, make sure to kill Torok's corrupted son, [[Mellok, Son of Torok|Mellok]], who is standing at {{coords|48|27|Highmountain}}.

Upon walking onto the rope bridge between the main bluff and Torok's hut:
:{{text|yell|Torok Bloodtotem|Don't let them cross that bridge or I'll have your hides!}}

Upon fully crossing the bridge:
:{{text|yell|Torok Bloodtotem|We are Bloodtotem! Would you let the weak overcome us?!}}

Upon killing Torok:
:{{text|say|Navarrogg|Torok will not trouble the Stonedark again. Let's head to the homeland of my people.}}

Completing the quest awards "The Bloodtotem Tribe" criterion of [[Ain't No Mountain High Enough]].
