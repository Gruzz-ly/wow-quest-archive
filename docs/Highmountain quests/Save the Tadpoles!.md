== Notes ==
Triggers at [[Shipwreck Cove]] in [[Highmountain]] during the [[Legion Assaults]]. Kill: [[Felwing Horror]]s, [[Emberscale Jailer]]s, [[Cursed Pillager]]s, and [[Emberscale Sentry|Emberscale Sentries]]. Note that the Cursed Pillagers can debuff you to periodically spawn [[Blood Worm]]s. These will attack and keep you in combat.
