==Description==
The leader of the [[Cavemoss]] [[makrura]], Clawdayshus, has been ordering raids into the Cavemoss village to steal the young for snacks. This isn't going to do at all. In order to prove their power, [[Murky]] and the tadpoles must destroy him as well! This is a job for [[murloc]]s. Mindmeld with Murky in his egg and show him how it's done!
