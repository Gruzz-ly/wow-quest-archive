Live to his story, and you will know why his name imbues courage into the cowardly and sows fear in the fearless.

This quest and its follow-ups put you in an instanced version of pre-''[[World of Warcraft: Cataclysm|Cataclysm]]'' [[Azshara]], down to using the original zone map. Oddly, it is already ruined and has its post-[[Great Sundering|Sundering]] coastline, despite the flashback supposedly taking place during the [[War of the Ancients]], before the Sundering. The exact geography is the post-''Cataclysm'' version with the goblin structures and ocean removed. The craters from the battle during [[First Come, First Served]] are still present, obviously made by the demons in this context.

The instance is surrounded by invisible walls at all sides &mdash; both at the coast, preventing you from falling off cliffs, and at the road to [[Ashenvale]], which is blocked by a green fog that eventually stops at another invisible wall. Enemy targets include [[Plague Imp]]s, [[Felguard Legionnaire]]s, and [[Infernal Siegebreaker]]s.

The player controls Huln Highmountain as a vehicle with the following abilities:
#Wild Carve
#Harpoon Stomp
#Gore Charge
