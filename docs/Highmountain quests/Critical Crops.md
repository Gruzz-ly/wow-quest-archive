== Notes ==
Triggers at [[Earthmother's Bounty]] in [[Highmountain]].

If you've farmed [[Sunsong Ranch]] at [[Halfhill]] in the [[Valley of the Four Winds]], you'll know how to deal with the wiggling, jumping, and tangled maize and gourds.

Wiggling ones will spawn [[Voracious Grub]]s, and jumping ones with spawn [[Harvest Worm]]s.
