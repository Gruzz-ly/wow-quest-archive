* [[Nozcha the Twisted]] slain
* [[Taurnash the Heretic]] slain

==Description==

==Completion==

==Rewards==
* +75 reputation with [[Highmountain Tribe]]

==Notes==
*Location: [[Feltotem]]
*Eligible mobs are [[Feltotem Bloodsinger]]s, [[Feltotem Warmonger]]s, and [[Feltotem Infuser]]s. Nozcha and Taurnash also count towards the final objective, so don't focus on getting those kills first and fight your way to the bosses instead.
