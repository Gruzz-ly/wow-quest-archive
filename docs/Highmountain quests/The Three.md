When [[High Crawliac]] seized control of the harpies she placed 3 powerful and deadly witches in charge of the covens under her command.

These witches must die. Feel up for the challenge?

Head into the [[Haglands]] behind me and find these three evil creatures. Use the might of your <artifact> to slay them.

We will cover you from the air and give your prey no sky to retreat.

Look for us higher up the mountain, should you survive.
