==Objectives==
Escape the [[Neltharion's Vault|Vault]].

==Description==
This will take [[Mayla Highmountain|Mayla]] some time to adjust to.

That being said, we should return to [[Thunder Totem]].

Just south of here is a tunnel that should take us through the old [[drogbar]] slave enclave and then to an opening beyond.

We keep a small [[Skyhorn tribe|Skyhorn]] outpost there to watch and ensure nothing enters or leaves this vault without us knowing about it.  They should be able to fly us back.
