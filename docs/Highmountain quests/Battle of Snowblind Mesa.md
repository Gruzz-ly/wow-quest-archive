'''Battle of Snowblind Mesa''' becomes available after completing 6/7 storylines of [[Ain't No Mountain High Enough]], with [[The High Chieftain]], [[The Skies of Highmountain]], and [[Unexpected Allies]].

==Objectives==
Join the Highmountain Forces on the march to [[Snowblind Mesa]].
* March with [[Mayla Highmountain]] to Snowblind Mesa

==Description==
Your timing is fortunate. Our forces at Snowblind Mesa report seeing the [[Dargrul the Underking|Underking]] above ground.

What is more, he is lightly guarded, and has the [[Hammer of Khaz'goroth|hammer]] with him. Thanks to your efforts, we can meet them in battle.

Come, <name>. Join us on the warpath to Snowblind Mesa.
