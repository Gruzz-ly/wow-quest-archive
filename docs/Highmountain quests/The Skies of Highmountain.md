==Objectives==
Speak with [[Lasan Skyhorn]] at the base of [[Thunder Totem]].
* Meet Lasan at Thunder Totem
* Witness the Skyhorn Ritual
* Wait for Lasan to finish his ritual

==Description==
When you are ready, join me at the base of Thunder Totem.

I would be honored to have you at my side as [[Skyhorn tribe|my tribe]] once again swears its allegiance to the young [[Mayla Highmountain|Mayla]].

Together, we are Highmountain!
