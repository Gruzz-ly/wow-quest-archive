==Objectives==
Complete Maximillians quests and slay "demons" to earn the rank of Squire, First Class.

* Earn the rank of Squire, Apprentice Class
* Earn the rank of Squire, Journeyman Class
* Earn the rank of Squire, First Class

==Description==
The journey here was perilous Squire. It was fraught with danger. Storms, demons, storm demons... I shudder at the memory. Of course, I overcame all such dangers, at great loss to my person. This is where I need your assistance.

You must venture forth, nay, sally forth, and recover my armor, my weapon, and of course my grand steed. Once you have successfully completed these tasks three, will you be promoted to Squire, First Class!
