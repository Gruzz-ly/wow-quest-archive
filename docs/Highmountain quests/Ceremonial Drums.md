==Description==
Those [[Bluewax Drumtaker|foolish calves]]!

They've made off with our ceremonial drums again, treating them as toys... as... common drums!

Can you help me find them? They must be scattered to all corners of [[Thunder Totem]] by now, discarded as soon as the fun dries up.
