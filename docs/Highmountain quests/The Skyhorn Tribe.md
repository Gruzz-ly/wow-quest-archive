==Description==
The [[Skyhorn tribe|Skyhorn]] people are some of the greatest pathfinders and hunters this world has ever known.

They are a proud people, but should be willing to listen to reason.

If the roads feel unsafe, you may ask any flight master in Highmountain to fly you there.

Please, <name>, go to their leader Lasan and remind him that only together, we are Highmountain.
