==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 49,350 [[XP]]

==Notes==
To fill up the bar, kill [[Azureback Broodmother]]s, [[Great Cavernbat]]s, [[Azureback Broodling]]s, [[Coldscale Deatheye]]s, [[Obsidian Deathwarder]]s, and [[King Azureback]].

Duirng the beta, clicking on a [[Dragon Soul Experiment]], [[Expended Orb of Domination]], and [[Mantid Sonic Tower]] also progressed the bar.
