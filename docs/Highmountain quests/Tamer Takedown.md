==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Tamer Korgrul.jpg|Tamer Korgrul}}
Kill [[Tamer Korgrul]] within [[Rockcrawler Chasm]].

==Description==
The [[drogbar]] will continue to breed vicious [[worm]]s as long as their tamer lives.

Korgrul is responsible for turning [[Fledgling Rockcrawler|fledgling rockcrawlers]] into the monstrous siege worms that are ridden into battle.

Head deep into the chasm and end his life, so that we may also see the end of these unnatural creatures.
