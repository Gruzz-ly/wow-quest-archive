==Objectives==
Carry the weights to the statue of [[Liftbrul]] and return to [[Damrul the Stronk]].
* Carry Weights to Statue
* Return Weights to Damrul

==Description==
Do you even lift? If you can carry these weights to the statue of Liftbrul and back, maybe we can talk.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost||10|}}
* 710 [[XP]]
* +75 [[reputation]] with [[Highmountain Tribe]]

==Progress==

==Completion==

==Notes==
This quest is unlocked when completing [[Step into the Dark]].

Upon accept, the player receives the Carry Weights buff.
