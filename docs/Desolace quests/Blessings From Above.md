==Objectives==
You are to collect 10 pieces of [[Satyr Flesh]] from the various [[Satyr]]s in [[Sargeron]], and place them on [[Elune's Brazier]].
*Sacrificial Offering Accepted

==Description==
You feel a deep connection to the distant past having partaken of the ancient blessing granted by this relic.

You determine to make as much as you can of this divine gift.

==Rewards==
The following spell will be cast on you:
:[[Divine Endowment]]

==Completion==
The divine brazier graciously endows you with a blessing.

==Notes==
This is the repeatable version of its predecessor. It allows keeping the buff throughout the entire zone's playthrough as long as the player is willing to re-farm for the [[Satyr Flesh]] each time a refresh is needed.
