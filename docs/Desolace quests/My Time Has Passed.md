==Objectives==
[[Furien]] at [[Tethris Aran]] wants you to ride his [[Dragonhawk]] to [[Furien's Post]] and inform his sister, [[Cerelia]] of his death.

==Description==
<Weakly, Furien beckons you near and struggles to speak.>

Careless... I was shot down by [[Alliance]] fire...

You must... inform my sister.

Take my mount, <race>... he'll bring you to her...

<Furien expires.>
