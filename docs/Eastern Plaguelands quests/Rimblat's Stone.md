==Completion==
You notice a small heap of stones within the caravan.

They do not appear out-of-the-ordinary, yet one of them attracts your attention.  You sense a warmth coming from within it.

==Rewards==

*{{Abilities|Rimblat's Stone|Grants a chance to cast a small self-heal when killing creatures within Eastern Plaguelands.|id=85618|icon=inv_pet_scorchedstone}}

==Notes==
This quest becomes available once you complete the quest [[A Gift For Fiona]] and {{NPC||Rimblat Earthshatter}} joins the caravan.
