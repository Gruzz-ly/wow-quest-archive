# Eastern Plaguelands storyline

[[File:WorldMap-EasternPlaguelands.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Eastern Plaguelands]]''' is a neutral level 40-45 zone in northeastern [[Lordaeron]] on the [[Eastern Kingdoms]]. Players are led here from the [[Western Plaguelands]] and follow [[Fiona's Caravan]] throughout the zone.

The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement [[Eastern Plaguelands Quests]].

After the storyline concludes, or as soon as players hit level 44, the [[Badlands storyline]] opens up from {{NPC||Gek Nozzlerocket}} at [[Light's Hope Chapel]] with [[Fuselight, Ho!]].
