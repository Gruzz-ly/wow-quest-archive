==Description==
Oi! [[Paladin]]! Yes, ye! Ye did me a solid by smashing up that depressin' mess of a meatsuit I've been riding in all these years, but I dinnae feel much of a pull to move on. I'm expectin' it's because I got some "Unfinished Business" to get up to 'afore I can be on me way. Well dull-wit, ye just been conscripted to help!

I need ye to fetch me 'ammer. Should be somewhere in Corrin's Crossing{{Sic}} and it'll be hard to miss.

Oi, take this too. It's me favorite gold tooth. Give it a rub when you've found me weapon.
