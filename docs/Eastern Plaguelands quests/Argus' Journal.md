==Completion==
You see a trail-worn journal inside the caravan. Judging by the Argent Crusade emblem on its spine. It must belong to Argus.

Written within the journal are several accounts of Argus' experiences during his tenure in the Crusade. It should prove to be an interesting read while on the road.
