==Objectives==
Enter [[Tyr's Hand]] in {{C|Eastern Plaguelands}} and scout out the central cathedral, as well as the [[mage tower]] and [[keep]] inside of [[New Avalon]] before returning to [[Caldoran]] at the [[Scarlet Base Camp]].

A direct incursion is possible but may be dangerous. Speak with [[Inquisitor Jociphine]] at the Scarlet Base Camp if you'd prefer a more subtle approach.
*Scout the Cathedral in Tyr's Hand
*Scout the Mage Tower in New Avalon
*Scout the Keep in New Avalon

==Description==
I am not pleased to engage you for aid. You consort with the [[heretic]] and the unclean and it is beneath me and our sacred order to entertain your presence here. However... you seem adequately capable and will prove to be a useful instrument here for our needs.

Go to Tyr's Hand and scout the cathedral there before moving deeper into New Avalon to scout the rear keep and mage tower. Return here without delay and report what you find. We need to know what we are up against.
