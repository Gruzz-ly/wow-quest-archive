==Completion==
Fiona has left some of her charms inside the caravan.

As a trader, she seems to be pretty good with money. Maybe carrying one of her charms will help out with your finances.

==Rewards==
The following spell will be cast on you:
*{{Abilities|Fiona's Lucky Charm|Grants a chance to loot extra gold and items from creatures in Eastern Plaguelands.|id=85612|icon=inv_gauntlets_02}}

==Notes==
This quest becomes available once you complete the quest [[A Gift For Fiona]]. Having the buff is the only way to get the companion [[Mr. Grubbs]] to drop.
