==Objectives==
Follow [[Alard Schmied]]'s recipes in your Blacksmithing recipe list to build some Handcrafted Leystone Gauntlets.
* [[Handcrafted Leystone Gauntlets]]

==Description==
Well, now that we have the flux we can put it to work and see how it goes. Why don't you take a crack at it this time and make some gauntlets for me. I'll observe your work... from a distance.

==Rewards==
You will learn the following: [Recipe List: Advanced Leysmithing]

You will also receive:
* 15,340 [[XP]]

==Progress==
DId it work? I haven't heard an explosion yet.

==Completion==
Excellent work! This new flux may do wonders for other craftable materials as well.
