==Objectives==
Report to [[Archmage Celindra]] at The [[Violet Gate]] in [[Dalaran]].
* [[Teleport to Dalaran Crystal]]

==Description==
The [[Kirin Tor]] have moved their city here to [[Northrend]].

No longer does Dalaran sit under its protective dome on the shores of [[Lordamere Lake]]. Now the city [[magic]]ally floats above the [[Crystalsong Forest]].

They believe you to be ready for your journey to the city. Look for the teleportation crystal in Crystal Song Forest.

When you arrive, seek out Archmage Celindra.

* +10 reputation with [[Kirin Tor]]
