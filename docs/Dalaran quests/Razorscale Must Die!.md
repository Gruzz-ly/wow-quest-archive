== Description == 
Razorscale normally keeps to her [[Razorscale's Aerie|aerie]] near the [[Colossal Forge]] in [[Ulduar]].  Of late, however, she has been spotted raining fire down upon lands outside of the titan complex.

Though the broodmother's is a sad tale, she poses an imminent threat to all of [[Northrend]].  The [[Council of Six]] has decreed that Razorscale must die!
