==Description==
The needles are sharp, but look at them closely. Some have a curve to them, others have a spur hanging off the side. Look at this one... it's got a ninety-degree crook in it! I have no desire to sew around a corner.

Tiffany Cartier in the [[Cartier & Co. Fine Jewelry|jewelcrafting shop]] has a good eye for this sort of thing. Bring these needles to her, and she'll straighten them out.
