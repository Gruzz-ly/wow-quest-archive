==Objectives==
Create a Handcrafted Silkweave Hood on [[Tanithria]]'s Finishing Bench. You will need to gather the materials in her shop.
* [[Handcrafted Silkweave Hood]]

==Description==

After we're finished, we'll move onto the gloves.

==Rewards==
You will learn the following:
*[[Silkweave Hood]]

You will also receive:
* 16,170 [[XP]]

==Progress==

==Completion==

==Notes==
Upon accept, you learn how to create Silkweave Hood Lining, Silkweave Hood: Outer Layer, and Handcrafted Silkweave Hood. Like before, speak with Lalla Brightweave and look around the shop for the necessary items to create the components and Handcrafted Silkweave Hood.
