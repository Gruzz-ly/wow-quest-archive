==Objectives==
Approach [[Mei Francis]] in [[Dalaran]] while mounted to have your [[Leystone Hoofplates]] fitted.
* Bring mount to Mei Francis

==Description==
Here, bring these hoofplates to Mei Francis, down at the mounts and pets store, to get your mount properly shod.

I'll share the pattern when you return.

==Rewards==
You will learn: [[Recipe: Leystone Hoofplates]]

You will also receive:
*{{cost|17|67|50}}
* 15,900 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Consider these a gift, for all your hard work.

==Completion==
Now that Mei's fit the plates to your mount, you should be able to put them on easily yourself.

Oh, and before I forget, here's the pattern. Enjoy!
