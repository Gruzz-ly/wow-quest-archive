This version of the '''Uniting the Isles''' quest is automatically given by [[Archmage Khadgar]] when you reach level {{level|legion-max}}, provided you have already completed [[Uniting the Isles]] on another character.  Completing it unlocks {{expac|Legion}} [[World Quest]]s.

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Khadgar Legion Dalaran.jpg|Archmage Khadgar|We need your help!}}
Speak to Archmage Khadgar in [[Dalaran]].

==Description==
There are a great number of factions here on the Broken Isles.  We must unite them if we want to stand a chance against the [[Burning Legion|Legion]].  Prove your worth to them and report back to me.

==Rewards==
You will learn the following: {{Abilities|World Quests|id=227303|icon=achievements_zone_brokenshore}}

You will also receive:
*[[Flight Master's Whistle]]

==Completion==
Excellent work, <name>.  We still have a long road ahead of us to rid this world of the Legion, but this brings us one step closer.

==Notes==
*You are only given this quest if you have already completed [[Uniting the Isles]] on another character, otherwise you get that quest instead.
