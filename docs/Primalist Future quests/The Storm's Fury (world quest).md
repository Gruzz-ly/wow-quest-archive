'''The Storm's Fury''' is the tracking world quest for the [[Storm's Fury]] event in the [[Primalist Future]] of [[Thaldraszus]]. This world quest appears every five hours, and can be run repeatedly.

Completing this world quest is also the objective of [[Resisting the Storm]].

==Objectives==
*Complete "The Storm's Fury" event within the Primalist Future.

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Soridormi]]|The [[Primalists]] are preparing to attack the [[Temporal Conflux]]. We must not allow them to gain access to the [[time]] portal!}}
:{{text|say|Soridormi|Disable their [[elemental]] portals. They are using them to empower a monster that is to be unleashed upon us.}}
:{{text|say|Soridormi|Though tread with caution, the storm has made the valley bitterly cold. Find ways to stay warm lest you freeze.}}

*[[Primalist Cache]] (all subsequent completions)
