==Objectives==
{{Questportrait|Murloc Watershaper.png|Murlocs|Murlocs have stolen crucial first aid supplies!}}
Recover 6 First Aid Kits from Murlocs.
*{{Item|First Aid Kit|s}} recovered from defeated [[Exile's Reach murlocs|Murlocs]] (6)

==Description==
The shore is littered with shipwrecks. This must be where the lost expedition crashed.

We'll need more supplies to help the injured recover. The nearby murlocs stole most of what washed up.

Slay those murlocs to get back what belongs to the Horde.

==Progress==

==Completion==
