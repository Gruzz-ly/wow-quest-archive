==Objectives==
Cross the necrotic barrier with Divine Shield and destroy the Necrotic Altar.
You can speak with Yorah if you need to reduce Divine Shield's cooldown.
*[[Divine Shield|Divine shield]] used
* [[Necrotic Altar|Necrotic altar]] destroyed
* Speak with [[Yorah]] to lower Divine Shield's cooldown

==Description==
[[Exile's Reach|This island]] is filled with cursed, necrotic [[magic]]. The [[Light]] can barely reach this place.

Use your new power to cross through the barrier before us and destroy this vile necrotic altar.

Show the dark energies within this island that you are protected by the Light's embrace.
