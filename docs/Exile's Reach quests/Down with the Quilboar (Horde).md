==Objectives==
{{Questportrait|Geolord Grek'og.png|Geolord Grek'og|The quilboar performing a ritual on one of the expedition members.}}
Slay Geolord Grek'og.
*[[Geolord Grek'og]] slain
*Meet [[Cork Fizzlepop]] to the north of the [[Quilboar Briarpatch]].

==Description==
I have discovered that the [[quilboar]] are performing a shadow ritual on a survivor from the missing expedition.

The ritual is being performed by the tribe's leader, [[Geolord Grek'og]].

Slay Grek'og and save the survivor.

Show this brute that the [[Horde]] is not to be trifled with.
