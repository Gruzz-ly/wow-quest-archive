==Objectives==
Get 5 Raw Meat from wildlife around the [[Abandoned Camp (Exile's Reach)|Abandoned Camp]], then cook it on the campfire.
*[[Raw Meat (Exile's Reach)]] collected from wildlife (5)
*Cook the meat on the [[Campfire (Exile's Reach)|campfire]]
*Give Won'sa [[Cooked Meat|the cooked meat]].

==Description==
This is [[Won'sa]], a [[shaman]] who was with the expedition.

She is dying of starvation. A death unfit for the [[Horde]]!

Slay some of the nearby wildlife for their meat and cook it over the fire.

Won'sa can tell us what happened to the missing expedition after she eats.
