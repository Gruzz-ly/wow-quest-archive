==Objectives==
Use the Choppy Booster Mk. 5 to find [[Shuja Grimaxe]].
*Use the [[Choppy Booster Mk. 5]] to scout the area

==Description==
You're in luck, 'cause I'm the ONE goblin who can help you find the rest of the expedition!

Feast your eyes on this fancy device here. Stole it off a gnome, improved on it, and made it my own!

I call it the Choppy Booster Mk. 5. Use it to find Shuja and the others.
