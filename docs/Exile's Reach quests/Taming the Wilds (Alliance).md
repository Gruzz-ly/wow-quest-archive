==Objectives==
Learn Tame Beast from Austin Huxworth, then use Tame Beast on any nearby wildlife.
*[[Tame Beast|Tamed Beast]]{{sic}} learned from [[Austin Huxworth|Austin]]
*Any wildlife [[beast]] tamed

==Description==
As a [[hunter]], you need a trustworthy beast at your side... or else these [[quilboar]] will slaughter you.

When you're ready, I'll teach you how to tame such a beast. Then you must find one to be your loyal companion.
