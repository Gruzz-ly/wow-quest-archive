==Objectives==
Ride your mount, then speak with Happy Hal to get a Hearthstone bound to the [[Wild Coast]].
* Ride any [[mount]] from your [[mount collection]]
* Speak with [[Happy Hal]] to get and bind your [[:Hearthstone]]

==Description==
Before you head off, you'll want to get yourself a Hearthstone. 

This little stone will return you to the [[inn]] where you last bound it to.

It's not much of an "inn", but the explorer's{{sic}} have a tent set up. 

Test out that new mount of yours and ride over to [[Happy Hal]]. He'll get you sorted out with a Hearthstone.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 510 [[XP]]

==Progress==
Happy Hal nearby can get you set up with a Hearthstone.

==Completion==
If you ever find yourself lost, use your Hearthstone to get yourself back to where you should be.
