==Objectives==
Get 5 Raw Meat from wildlife around the [[Abandoned Camp (Exile's Reach)|Abandoned Camp]], then cook it on the campfire.
* [[Raw Meat (Exile's Reach)|Raw Meat]] collected from wildlife (5)
* [[Cooked Meat|Cook the meat]] on the [[Campfire (Exile's Reach)|campfire]]

==Description==
[[Alaria]] is nearly starved to death. We need to get her something to eat!

Slay some of the nearby wildlife for their meat. Bring whatever you find here to cook.

If we can help Alaria regain her strength, she might be able to tell us what happened to the others.

I'll stay here and help stabilize her wounds.
