==Objectives==
Rescue [[Meredy Huntswell]] from the [[harpy]]'s [[necromantic]] ritual.
*Meredy Huntswell rescued
*Meet Meredy Huntswell at the Harpy Roost entrance.

==Description==
Our expedition's [[mage]], Meredy Huntswell, is being held by the harpies!

We've got to stop their ritual and rescue Meredy.

You lead the charge. [[Kee-La]] and I will back you up if things look dire.
