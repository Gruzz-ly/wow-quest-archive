==Objectives==
Slay 7 [[Quilboar]] within the [[Quilboar Briarpatch]].
*Quilboar slain (7)
*Meet [[Austin Huxworth]] to the north of the Quilboar Briarpatch.

==Description==
Quilboar are primitive and savage. They rarely practice any [[magic]] other than [[Geomancer|geomancy]].

But these particular quilboar seem to be dabbling in [[Shadow (magic)|shadow magic]]. They must be stopped.

Slay the quilboar in the den. They cannot spread this magic in the island!
