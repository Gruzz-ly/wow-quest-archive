==Description==
<This note is nailed into the post with a small chitinous plate.>

For crimes impeding the Edicts of [[Dornogal]], the [[Council of Dornogal|Council]] has deemed a reward be yielded to any who slays the chitinous beast known as Stik'kar.

A sample of Stik'kar's armor is attached. If you are unable to damage it, you are urged to carry on in your duties and leave this task for another.
