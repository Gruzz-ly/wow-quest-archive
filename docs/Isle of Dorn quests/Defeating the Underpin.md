'''Defeating the Underpin''' concludes the [[The War Within Season 2]] storyline for [[delves]]. To complete this quest, players must defeat [[The Underpin]] in his [[Demolition Dome]] challenge delve in northern [[Undermine]], which requires clearing a Tier 7 delve with lives remaining to unlock.

==Objectives==
{{Questportrait|The Underpin.jpg|The Underpin|One of the meanest, craziest goblins within the Undermine.}}
Increase your item level to challenge the Underpin in his tier 8 delve within [[Undermine]].
* [[The Underpin|Underpin]] slain within [[Demolition Dome|his delve]] {{co|52.2|9.3|Undermine}}

==Description==
This blasted "Underpin" took me hat! Along with all the curios we collected.

It's time tae get some vengeance. We got tae get stronger if we're going tae take him down.

I know he's somewhere in Undermine when yer ready tae tackle him.

I'll be right there with you!
