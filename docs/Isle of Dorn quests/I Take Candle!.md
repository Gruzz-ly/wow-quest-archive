'''I Take Candle!''' is a bonus objective at the [[Mourning Rise]] on [[Isle of Dorn]]. Players will be in the area for the Remember Me, Earthen optional chapter of the [[Isle of Dorn storyline]].

==Objectives==
* Kobolds disrupted (0/100%)

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{Cost|140|40}}
* 25,350 [[XP]]

==Notes==
Fill the bar by defeating [[Candlelit Ouchpatcher]]s, [[Candlelit Big-Boomer]]s, [[Candlelit Blind-Hound]]s, [[Candlelit Boomer]]s, [[Candlelit Hexthrower]]s, and [[Candlelit Lootsnatcher]]s.
