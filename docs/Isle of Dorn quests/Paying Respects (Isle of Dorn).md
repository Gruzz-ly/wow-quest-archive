==Description==
Please excuse my absence. It was never my intention to turn my back on the [[Stormriders]], then or now. But I needed to come here and pay my respects to [[Baelgrim]].

Unfortunately, these blasted nerubians are still lingering. I think they're searching for something, but I don't know what. Whatever their reasons, I cannot abide them lurking where Baelgrim fell.

==Progress==

This is a gift. Baelgrim may be gone, but [[Zarein|his stormrook]] seems ready to carry on his fight. It's been a long time since I've felt such a connection. Such purpose. I don't intend to turn my back a second time.
