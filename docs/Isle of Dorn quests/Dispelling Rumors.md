==Description==
Many of my peacekeepers have already caught wind of [[Overseer Ardgrin|Ardgrin]]'s sedition.

I am told some are losing faith in my integrity as Councilward, believing that I have been taking bribes from smugglers to bolster my own coffers.

I can provide proof that no such transactions have taken place--clearly--but we need to stop them from murmuring amongst themselves and giving the rumors power. Lies become truths through repetition.

If you find any discussing the matter, kindly disperse them.
