'''Adventuring in Khaz Algar''' is offered to alts.

==Objectives==
Review the Scouting Map to choose an area within [[Khaz Algar]] to support.
* Zone chosen from [[Scouting Map (Dornogal)|Scouting Map]]

==Description==
We've got [[scout]]s in every corner o' Khaz Algar, an' they all report the same thing: trouble.

Seems we got here just in time, eh?

Check the scouting map on the table behind ye and lend a hand where ye can.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|2|80|90}}
* 1,300 [[XP]]

==Completion==
<Your decision has been made. You can return to the scouting map at any time to choose another location.>

==Notes==
;Upon accept
:{{Text|say|Brann Bronzebeard|Here five minutes and already neck deep in adventure and danger. This ought tae be fun.}}

Interact with the map and choose a destination:
*[[The Ringing Deeps (quest)]] - The Ringing Deeps are home to the dutiful Machine Speakers and resourceful kobolds.
*[[Hallowfall (quest)]] - Hallowfall is home to the hardened survivors of an Arathi expedition.
*[[Azj-Kahet (quest)]] - Azj-Kahet is home to the nerubians and their capital, the City of Threads.
