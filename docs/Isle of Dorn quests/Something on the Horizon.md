'''Something on the Horizon''' is provided for [[alt]]s that reach level {{level|tww-max}}.

After completion, Brann will have an option for alts to skip the [[The War Within campaign#Leveling|leveling storyline]] and start the max-level storyline:
{{Dialogue|
I'd like to join the reinforcements. 

{{Text|red|<Skip the level-up campaign.>}}
}}

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Brann Bronzebeard]] in [[Dornogal]].

==Description==
Brann's [[scout]]s have reported sightings of reinforcements approaching the shores of [[Khaz Algar]].

He's put out the call for [[adventurer]]s to join him in Dornogal.

==Rewards==
You will unlock access to the following:
*{{Abilities|Level-up Campaign Skip|Unlocks the option for this character to skip the level-up campaign and begin the max-level campaign by speaking with Brann Bronzebeard.|icon=ability_vehicle_launchplayer|id=460971}}

You will also receive: {{cost|2|34}}

==Completion==
I've 'eard reports o' reinforcements approaching the isle. Ye know Khaz Algar inside and out--they'll need yer expertise.
