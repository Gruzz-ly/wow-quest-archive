==Description==
The [[cloudrook]]s cannot possibly circle forever, and I cannot bear more of them being lost. But maybe they'd roost if we tempted them with their favorite food?

The [[eel]]s that swim in these waters carry a small electrical charge not unlike the [[mosswool]] favored by their faster, stronger cousins the [[stormrook]]s. I once reasoned that cloudrooks might enjoy the eels for similar reasons.

And I confess... I've been feeding them the eels ever since.

Gather some up and give it a go.
