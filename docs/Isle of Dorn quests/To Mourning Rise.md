'''To Mourning Rise''' begins an optional chapter of the [[Isle of Dorn storyline]], started at [[Crossroads Plaza]]. This storyline is required for [[Allied Races: Earthen]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Urtago]] at [[Fallside Outpost]].

==Description==
Greetings, outlander. My name is Urtago, and I am Caretaker [[Korgran]]'s apprentice at the [[Mourning Rise]] memorial site.

The mischievous [[Candlelit]] [[Kobold]]s have overrun Mourning Rise, and we seek assistance. Would you meet me at Fallside Outpost? It is just to the north of here. I will be able to tell you more once you arrive.
