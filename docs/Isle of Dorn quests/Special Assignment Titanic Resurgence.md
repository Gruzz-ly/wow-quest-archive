'''Special Assignment: Titanic Resurgence''' becomes available after completing three world quests on the [[Isle of Dorn]].

==Objectives==
*[[Eroded Titan Construct]] slain
*Obtain supercharged [[Stormrook (NPC)|stormrook]] (optional)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Adelgonn]]|Another of the [[titan]]s' constructs has been sighted approaching the shore.}}
:{{text|say|Adelgonn|[[Stormward Lufsela]] has sent a rook you can use to take it down.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*12,200 [[XP]]
*+200 [[reputation]] with the [[Council of Dornogal]]
*[[Seasoned Adventurer's Cache]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Adelgonn|Well done. The horizon is clear once again. Thank you.}}
