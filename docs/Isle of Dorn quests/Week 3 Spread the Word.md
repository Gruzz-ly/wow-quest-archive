'''Week 3: Spread the Word''' is the third of six weekly [[Dastardly Duos]] quests in [[Dornogal]], [[Stormwind]], and [[Orgrimmar]].

==Objectives==
Place your [[:Dastardly Banner]] during a match, then win the match.
* Win [[Dastardly Duos]] fight
* Place your Dastardly banner during a Dastardly Dome match

==Description==
Hey, you wanna help out the organization? you know, spread the word? Next time you're in a match, throw your Dastardly Banner down in the arena. Show support!

But be careful -- your opponents might take offense and come straight after you...

==Rewards==
The following will be cast on you:
*[[Winner's Podium, High Score]]

* [[Dastardly Prize Purse]]

==Progress==
