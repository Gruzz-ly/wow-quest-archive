'''Elemental Excavation''' is a [[War Mode]]-only [[world quest]] that takes place at [[Dhar Bandar]] on the [[Isle of Dorn]].

==Objectives==
Kill Elementals or [[Player]]s at [[Dhar Bandar]]
* [[Element Extractor]] (provided)

==Description==
:{{Text|say|[[Ruffious]]|Dhar Bandar has stood for thousands of years. Clear out these elementals and secure the tower!}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 12,200 [[XP]]

You will also receive one of:
*[[Forged Warmonger's Mantle]]
*[[Forged Warmonger's Shoulderguard]]
*[[Forged Warmonger's Armguards]]
*[[Forged Warmonger's Bracers]]

==Completion==
*{{Text|say|Ruffious|Hah! It'll take more than the elements to weather that stonework! Well done.}}

==Criteria of==
*[[Unbound Battle]]
*[[A Champion's Tour: The War Within]]

==Notes==
*Targets include: [[Player]]s (15%), [[Briney Kelpcore]] (12%), [[Raging Dunecore]] (8% non-elite, 20% elite), [[Tidal Hydrocore]] (8%).
*The [[Element Extractor]] can be used on [[elemental]]s to gain an ability. The ability appears as an [[Extra Action Button]] and lasts until you extract a different ability, die, or leave the area.
**Briney Kelpcore: {{abilities|Sea Extraction|Roots the target and all enemies within 15 yards in place for 6 sec. Damage may interrupt the effect. Targets that are rooted take 10% increased damage from you. 20 sec cooldown.|id=442182|icon=inv_fishing_lure_kelp}}
**Raging Dunecore: {{abilities|Sand Extraction|Douse enemies within 10 yards around you in sand, dealing Nature damage, slowing them by 40% and reducing their chance to critically strike by 25% for 6 sec. 20 sec cooldown.|id=442181|icon=spell_sandexplosion}}
**Tidal Hydrocore: {{abilities|Aquatic Extraction|Knocks back the current enemy target, dealing Frost damage. Knockback is more effective against players. 20 sec cooldown.|id=442183|icon=inv_10_elementalcombinedfoozles_water}}
