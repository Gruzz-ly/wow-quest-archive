'''A Time to Come Together''' is a quest to skip straight to the max-level storyline of [[The War Within (achievement)]], available to players who previously completed the leveling campaign on other characters. This quest requires reaching level 80 by any means on alt characters.

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Moira Thaurissan]] in [[Dornogal]].
* Speak to Moira

==Description==
We ought tae be getting a whole fleet o' reinforcements soon. Just in time, I hear [[Alleria Windrunner|Alleria]] and [[Anduin Wrynn|Anduin]] are back in Dornogal.

If I know Moira, she'll already be workin' with 'em tae figure out our next move. She knows how tae lead like a gryphon knows how tae fly. Best go find her before she comes looking for ye.
