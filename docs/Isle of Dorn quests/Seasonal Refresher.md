'''Seasonal Refresher''' begins the [[The War Within Season 2|second season]] of [[delves]] in {{g|tww}}. [[Brann Bronzebeard]] at the [[Delver's Headquarters]] in [[Dornogal]] starts it with no prerequisites.

==Objectives==
Learn about Season 2 of Delves from the Delver's Documents, then purchase supplies from Reno Jackson and Sir Finley Mrrgglton.
*[[Delver's Guide]] reviewed
*[[Delver's Cosmetic Surprise Bag]] purchased from [[Reno Jackson]]
*[[Delver's Starter Kit]] purchased from [[Sir Finley Mrrgglton]]

==Description==
We're back tae uncovering more things within delves!

Let's get ye up to speed on new items we've found first.

Then I've got some concerning information tae talk about.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*{{cost|2|34}}

==Completion==
All set? Good.

'Cause I got a story tae tell ye.

==Notes==
The [[Delver's Guide]] is on the table in front of Brann and opens the [[Delve Dashboard]] of the Group Finder interface.

[[Sir Finley Mrrgglton]] hands over the [[Delver's Starter Kit]] for free, containing a [[Restored Coffer Key]] and some consumables: a [[Delver's Pouch of Reagents]], some [[Go-Go Juice]], and some [[Incontinental Takeout]].

[[Reno Jackson]] hands over the [[Delver's Cosmetic Surprise Bag]] containing a random cosmetic item from the previous season.

After completing the quest, Brann has new gossip:

{{dialogue|{{gossip|Tell me about new treasures to be found in more difficult delves.}}

More Runed Crests will be earned from delves at tier 9 and above.

Gilded Crests will now be rewarded up to three times a week upon completing a bountiful tier 11 delve.}}
