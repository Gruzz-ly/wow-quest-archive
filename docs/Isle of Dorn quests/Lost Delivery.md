'''Lost Delivery''' begins an optional chapter of the [[Isle of Dorn storyline]], started by a [[Dropped Package]] found at several locations across the [[Isle of Dorn]]:

*[[Crossroads Plaza]] {{co|55.1|59.3|Isle of Dorn}}
*South [[Ironwold]] {{co|71.1|54.1|Isle of Dorn}}
*Southeast of [[Rambleshire]] {{co|62.5|31.2|Isle of Dorn}}

==Objectives==
Deliver the Abandoned Package to [[Harvester Farnee]] at [[Durgaz Cabin]].
