==Objectives==
Speak to [[Augermu]] to ask about what you can do with the [[:Displaced Corrupted Memento]]s.

==Description==
My assistant Augermu has been monitoring you and the [[timeways]] while you were in the [[Horrific Visions|past]]. He has a way for you to replicate gaining some of the [[Corrupted Mementos|boons]] [[adventurer]]s used the first time they faced these visions.

Give him some of the displaced corrupted mementos you gleaned from your experience for further analysis.
