'''Ingvar Must Die!''' is a Horde quest shown both in the [[Blizzcon]] and [[wikipedia:Leipzig|Leipzig]] ''[[Wrath of the Lich King]]''-tests.

==Objectives==
[[Dark Ranger Marrah]] has asked you to kill [[Ingvar the Plunderer]] inside [[Utgarde Keep]].
*[[Ingvar's Head]]

==Description==
Of our entire recon party, only I remain.

These [[vrykul]] are relentless fighters. Given their numbers and their savage tactics, our only hope is to bring down their leader and pray it brings chaos to their ranks.

Ingvar is his name. Bring me his head!
