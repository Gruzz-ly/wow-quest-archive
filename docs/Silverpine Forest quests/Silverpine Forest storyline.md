# Silverpine Forest storyline

[[File:WorldMap-Silverpine-cata.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

'''[[Silverpine Forest]]''' is a level 5-30 Horde-only zone in eastern [[Lordaeron]] and the direct followup of the [[Tirisfal Glades storyline]], part of the Undead starting experience. Its progress is followed through the achievement [[Silverpine Forest Quests]]

After the quest chain, the [[Hillsbrad Foothills storyline]] opens up with [[Empire of Dirt]]. However, as soon as players reach level 19, [[Warchief's Command: Hillsbrad Foothills!]] opens up as well from {{NPC||High Warlord Cromush}}.
