==Objectives==
Correctly answer one of <Llore's/Ju'Pa's> questions about the history of [[Azeroth]].
*Answered <Llore's/Ju'Pa's> question

==Description==
It is time to reflect on the events that have brought us to this moment. A celebration of our great history and the events that shape it.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 5x [[Timewarped Badge]]
* {{cost|93|60}} (at level 50)

==Progress==
Let us speak, then, of events that have already come to pass.

==Completion==
A moment to reminisce on how far we have come. A day to look to the future and where we might go. A year or reflection on what it means to grow and a lifetime of memories made by those brave enough to seize it.

==Notes==
This quest functions in much the same way as [[A Timeless Question]]. Like that quest, there are a huge number of possible questions, and the following list is assumed to be incomplete. The correct answer is in '''bold type'''.
