==Objectives==
Recover 3 Horde Infantry Rations. Deliver them to Marogg in Orgrimmar.
*[[Horde Infantry Rations]] (3)

==Description==
We have had a problem with [[Orgrimmar Thief|thieves]] stealing our infantry rations here in Orgrimmar for the last couple of weeks. I recently received some intelligence of where these crimes are taking place. Anywhere you see piles of crates and supplies, there are likely to be thieves nearby. I need you to hunt these thieves down and recover the stolen rations. Return to me when you are done.

==Rewards==
You will receive: {{cost|7|40}} (+{{cost|9|40}} at max level)
*+1 [[Cooking]] skill
*[[Epicurean's Award]]

==Progress==
Were you able to recover the rations?

==Completion==
Nice work <name>! I will see that these rations are returned to the troops immediately.
