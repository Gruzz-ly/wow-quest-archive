==Objectives==
Speak with [[Great-father Winter]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
We at [[Smokywood Pastures]] appreciate the recovery of the stolen treats, <name>.  For that, we'd like to offer you a special gift... presented by none other than [[Great-father Winter]] himself!

Please, speak with [[Great-father Winter]], and he will give you your [[Feast of Winter Veil]] gift from us here at [[Smokywood Pastures]].  From our farm to your plate, it's always [[Smokywood Pastures]] wholesome goodness... thanks to you, of course!

==Rewards==
You will also receive:
*9250 [[XP]]
*+250 [[reputation]] with [[Steamwheedle Cartel]]
*[[Smokywood Pastures Special Gift]]

==Completion==
Ho ho hello, adventurer!

The goblins told me that you have earned a special gift this holiday season, and I am pleased to give it to you.  Here you go - have a wondrous [[Feast of Winter Veil]] this and every season in the future.

==Notes==
The [[Winter Veil Disguise Kit]] will be mailed to the player 24 hours after completing this quest.
