'''The Gloves Are Off''' begins the {{g|tww}} iteration of the [[Brawler's Guild]]. Find [[Alton Philips]] in the [[Valley of Honor]] near the entrance to the area to begin. 

==Objectives==
Talk to a Grunt in the [[Brawl'gar Arena]] in [[Orgrimmar]] after using your [[:Blood-Soaked Invitation]].
* Talk to a [[Brawl'gar Arena Grunt|Brawl'gar Grunt]]

==Description==
If you've found yourself an invite you'll be wanting to put it to good use, no?

Use your Blood-Soaked Invitation first then talk to a Grunt in the Brawl'gar Arena and get to brawling!
