==Warchief's Command==
;Chromie's Call
The [[Bronze Dragonflight]]'s magics are powerful, and allow us to see [[Azeroth]] how we choose to.

Explore the world and rise to the challenge in your favorite period in time.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Chromie]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
Chromie is seeking veteran [[adventurer]]s to embark on expeditions through [[time]] and space.

This is your opportunity to experience the legendary adventures of the past--today!

Speak with Chromie near the [[Orgrimmar Embassy]] for more information.
