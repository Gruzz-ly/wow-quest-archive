==Objectives==
Deliver the SI:7 Emblem to [[High Overlord Saurfang]] at [[Grommash Hold]] in [[Orgrimmar]].
* [[SI:7 Emblem]] (provided)

==Description==
<The [[Darkspear Loyalist|Darkspear loyalist]] opens the package and reads the letter within. As she finishes the note from [[Thrall]], a hint of a fanged smile appears on her face.>

Take me raptor. Hurry, little one, dere be no time ta waste!
