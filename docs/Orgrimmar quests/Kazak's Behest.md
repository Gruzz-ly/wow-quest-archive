==Objectives==
Speak with [[Kazak Darkscream]] at [[Barracks (Orgrimmar)|The Barracks]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
<Name>, I have received word that you are needed back in Orgrimmar. Kazak Darkscream, one of the senior [[warlock trainers]], has something important that he wishes to discuss with you. It seems like the kind of thing that cannot wait.

Make your way back to Orgrimmar and speak with him as soon as you can. You will find him at the Training Hall in the [[Valley of Honor]].
