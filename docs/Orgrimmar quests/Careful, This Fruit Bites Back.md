==Objectives==
Retrieve 8 pieces of Prickly Pear Fruit. Deliver the fruit to [[Marogg]] in [[Orgrimmar]].
*[[Prickly Pear Fruit]] (8)

==Description==
Sometimes the troops want something sweet to eat. I don't always give them what they want but this time, I am going to make an exception. I am sure you have seen the local cactus that grows here in Orgrimmar. These cactus produce a tasty treat called prickly pear fruit. The trouble is the fruit are covered in spines! Watch your hands and bring be back a handful of the fruit.
