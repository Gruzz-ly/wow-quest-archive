'''The Pride of the Sin'dorei''' begins a quest chain for [[blood elf]] characters to obtain their [[heritage armor]]. It can be picked up just outside the [[Orgrimmar Embassy]] within [[Orgrimmar]] after reaching level {{level|bfa-max}}.

==Objectives==
Speak with [[Lor'themar Theron|Lor'themar]] in [[Silvermoon City]].

==Description==
Champion, I am here on behalf of Lor'themar Theron and the city of Silvermoon.

The Regent Lord asks for your assistance with a ceremony of great importance to our people. He requests your presence at your earliest convenience.
