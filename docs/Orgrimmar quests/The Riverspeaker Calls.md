==Objectives==
Speak with [[Hretar Riverspeaker]] at [[Barracks (Orgrimmar)|The Barracks]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
News travels upon the winds, young [[shaman]]. Your deeds have not gone unnoticed! It seems Hretar Riverspeaker wishes to discuss something with you. Make your way to Orgrimmar as quickly as you can and speak with him at The Barracks in the [[Valley of Honor]]. He will be among the others who offer training.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 150 [[XP]]
* +10 reputation with [[Orgrimmar (faction)|Orgrimmar]]

==Completion==
I see you are both obedient and talented, <name>. Stay that way, and you will go far in the [[Horde]].
