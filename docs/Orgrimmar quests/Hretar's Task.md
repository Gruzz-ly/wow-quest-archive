==Objectives==
Go to [[Shadowfang Keep]] and obtain Walden's Talisman from [[Lord Walden (tactics)|Lord Walden]], the Book of Lost Souls, and 5 Ghostly Essence.
*[[Walden's Talisman]]
*[[Book of Lost Souls]]
*[[Ghostly Essence]] (5)

==Description==
The elements and spirits speak to you with clear voices, <name>. You have become a powerful [[shaman]] and an example to others wishing to serve the [[Horde]].

Word has even reached the [[warchief]], and he has commissioned his personal [[smith]] to forge a special weapon for you.

The cursed Shadowfang Keep in [[Silverpine Forest]] is the only place to get the materials you'll need. Travel there and obtain [[Lord Walden]]'s talisman, the Book of Lost Souls, and essence from the [[ghost]]s haunting the keep.
