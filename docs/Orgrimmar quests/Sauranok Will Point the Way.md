==Objectives==
Speak with [[Sauranok the Mystic]] at [[Grommash Hold]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
I see you haven't yet spoken to the Warchiefs{{sic}}... other... advisor, Sauranok the Mystic. He has been at their side since our campaign together in Northrend.

To assault the Twilight Highlands, we're going to need a map of the shoreline. I have tasked Sauranok with digging up this information. See if he can use your assistance!
