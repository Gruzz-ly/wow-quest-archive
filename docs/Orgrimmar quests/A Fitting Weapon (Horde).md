==Objectives==
Go to [[Shadowfang Keep]] and obtain the Silverlaine Family Sword from [[Baron Silverlaine]], 5 crates of Moonsteel Ingots, and 5 Deathless Sinew.
*[[Silverlaine Family Sword]]
*[[Moonsteel Ingots]] (5)
*[[Deathless Sinew]] (5)

==Description==
From your earliest days, [[Warrior trainers|your trainers]] always knew you would make your mark on the [[Horde]]. You have fought bravely and given our enemies reason to fear.

As a reward for your dedication, the [[warchief]] has commissioned his personal [[smith]] to forge a special weapon.

The cursed Shadowfang Keep in [[Silverpine Forest]] is the only place to get the materials you'll need. Travel there and find the lost Silverlaine family sword, a supply of moonsteel ingots, and sinew from the [[ghoul]]s and [[geist]]s infesting the keep.
