==Objectives==
Use the Glowing Torch to destroy 8 copies of The Twilight Creed in the [[Domicile]] of [[Blackrock Depths]].
*Copies of [[The Twilight Creed]] burned (8)
*[[Glowing Torch]] (provided)

Our enemies be plottin' against us in the halls of Blackrock Depths. The [[Twilight's Hammer]] [[cultist]]s, they be busy preaching and handin' out books to the [[Dark Iron clan|Dark Irons]], tellin' them that it's time for war. Wit' a [[Dark Iron Army|Dark Iron army]] at their backs, the Twilight's Hammer be a formidable force. We must put an end to the agitation!

I be wantin' you to go into Domicile of Blackrock Depths and use this torch to burn those Twilight's Hammer books!
