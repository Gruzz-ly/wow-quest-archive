==Objectives==
Find Watch{{sic}} [[Warlord Dar'toon]] at the [[Blasted Lands]]. He awaits your arrival before the [[Dark Portal]].
*Visit [[Snurk Bucksquick]], the Zeppelin Master
*Speak to [[Thysta]] at [[Grom'gol Base Camp]]

==Description==
There exists a hellish gate that has loomed silent for years, but it is silent no longer.

All able bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to the Blasted Lands. Visit the Zeppelin Master outside of the city gates of [[Orgrimmar]] and ride to Grom'gol Base Camp. From there, speak to Thysta to arrange a flight to [[Stonard]].

Report to Warlord Dar'toon at once. He awaits you before the Dark Portal...
