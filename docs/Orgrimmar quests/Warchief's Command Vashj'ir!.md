==Objectives==
Report to [[Commander Thorak]] at [[Bladefist Bay]] in [[Durotar]].

==Description==
All able-bodied members of the [[Horde]] are hereby ordered to report to Commander Thorak at Bladefist Bay in Durotar.

Now, more than ever, your help is needed in battling our most hated enemy, the [[Alliance]]!

To reach Bladefist Bay you must travel south through the [[Gates of Orgrimmar|main gates]] of [[Orgrimmar]] and enter Durotar. From there, follow the path east to the coast. Thorak will be standing at his post near the docks.

Onward, <brother/sister>! Destiny calls!
