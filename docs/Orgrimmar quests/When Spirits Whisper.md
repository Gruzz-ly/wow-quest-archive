'''When Spirits Whisper''' is the first step to obtain the [[tauren]] [[heritage armor]]. To start the questline, you need to be [[level]] {{level|bfa-max}}.

==Objectives==
Meet with [[Baine Bloodhoof]] in [[Thunder Bluff]].

==Description==
Ah, <name>! I am pleased the [[Earth Mother|Earthmother]] guided you here. I have a message for you.

[[Baine Bloodhoof|Chieftain Bloodhoof]] asks that you join him in [[Thunder Bluff]] at once.

I will leave it to our leader to explain why you are being summoned. But I will say that Baine has met with the other [[spiritwalker]]s and your name was spoken with urgency.

I suggest you hurry.
