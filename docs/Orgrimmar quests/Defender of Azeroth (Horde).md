==Objectives==
Speak with Highlord Darion Mograine. Then, use the highlord's death gate to travel to [[Orgrimmar]]. Once there, talk to Chromie.
* Speak with Highlord [[Darion Mograine]]
* Listen to your commander (Optional)
* Death Gate used
* Talk to [[Chromie]]

==Description==
An excellent choice.

Troubling times come. As one of the chosen few, it is both your burden and privilege to defend [[Azeroth|our world]]. It is a great honor.

But also know you will be met with the scorn of the ignorant. Oftentimes, mortals are incapable of seeing past our fearsome appearance. Ignore these fools and do what must be done.

Speak with your commander. Then, use his death gate to travel to the land of the living and choose your destiny.
