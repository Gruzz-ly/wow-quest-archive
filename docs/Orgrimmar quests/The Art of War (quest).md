==Objectives==
Speak to [[General Nazgrim]] in [[Grommash Hold]]
* Report to Grommash Hold

==Description==
If the character freed him in [[Vashj'ir]]:
:Lok'tar Ogar, old friend. It has been too long since last we spoke.
:It is good to see that you've emerged safely from the depths of [[Vashj'ir]].
:[[Garrosh Hellscream|Warchief Hellscream]] has stationed me here in Grommash Hold.
:By his decree, all in service to the Horde are to swear allegiance to their new Warchief.
:You would do well not to keep him waiting, <name>.
If the character didn't:
:Lok'tar ogar, <race>. I am Nazgrim.
:You are hereby ordered to present yourself to Garrosh Hellscream at Grommash Hold immediately.
:By his decree, all in service to the Horde are to swear an oath of allegiance to their new Warchief.
:Do not keep him waiting, <race>.
