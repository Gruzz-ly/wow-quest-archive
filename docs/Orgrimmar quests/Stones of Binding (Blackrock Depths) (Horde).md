==Objectives==
Recover 6 Elemental Binding Stones from [[Blackrock Depths]]. You can find the stones in [[Shadowforge City]], the [[Domicile]], the [[Shrine of Thaurissan]], the [[East Garrison]], and the [[West Garrison]].
*[[Elemental Binding Stone]] (6)

==Description==
The [[Shattered Hand]] has discovered increased collaboration between [[Twilight's Hammer]] and the [[Dark Iron dwarf|Dark Iron dwarves]] in Blackrock Depths. We have an opportunity to strike at our enemies and empower ourselves, <name>.

Twilight's Hammer followers have placed a number of [[elemental]] binding stones throughout Shadowforge City, the Domicile, the Shrine of Thaurissan, the West Garrison and the East Garrison. Find and capture those stones!

==Rewards==

* {{cost|10|70|}}
* 3,450 [[XP]]
* +350 reputation with [[Orgrimmar (faction)|Orgrimmar]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
The binding stones you brought back are exceedingly powerful. Not only have we dealt a blow to the [[Horde]]'s enemies, but you have also discovered a fascinating source of power.
