==Description==
I've written a letter of thanks to [[Grimtak]]. Will you deliver it for me? If you've already been to the [[Razor Hill]] and spoken with [[Devrak|their wind rider master]], then you can fly a [[Wyvern|wind rider]] back there.

Take my letter to Doras, the wind rider master of Orgrimmar, and speak with him about transport to Razor Hill.

==Rewards==

* +25 [[reputation]] with [[Orgrimmar (faction)|Orgrimmar]]
