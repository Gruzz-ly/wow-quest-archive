==Objectives==
Catch 10 Muddy Crawfish in Orgrimmar. Deliver the crustaceans to Marogg when you are done.
* [[Muddy Crawfish]] (10)

==Description==
There are a number of small lakes scattered around Orgrimmar. These lakes full up with crawfish every year. That's where you come in, <name>. I need you to catch some of those delicious crustaceans and return to me when you are done. Assuming the catch is successful, our Horde army will feast tonight.

==Rewards==
You will receive: {{cost|7|40}} (+{{cost|9|40}} at max level)
*+2 [[Cooking]] skill
*[[Epicurean's Award]]

==Progress==

==Completion==
