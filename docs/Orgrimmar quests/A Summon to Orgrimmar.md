'''A Summon to Orgrimmar''' is an optional breadcrumb to begin the [[Heritage of Draenor]] questline. It is automatically offered to [[orc (playable)|orc]] characters who are level 50 or above.

==Objectives==
Speak to [[Eitrigg]] in [[Grommash Hold]], [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
Champion, come to Grommash Hold when you are able.

I have something important to discuss.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Money]]
*[[XP]]

==Completion==
Ah, <name>. Thank you for coming. We have much to talk about.
