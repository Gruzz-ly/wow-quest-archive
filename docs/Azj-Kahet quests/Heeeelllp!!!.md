'''Heeeelllp!!!''' is started automatically as players enter the [[Wormlands]]. [[Skedgit Cinderbangs]] is strung up above three [[Razormouth Devourer]]s and needs some help before they get to her.

==Objectives==
{{questportrait|Skedgit Cinderbangs.png|Skedgit Cinderbangs|An opportunist in way over her head.}}
Rescue Skedgit Cinderbangs.
* [[Skedgit Cinderbangs]] rescued

==Description==
Hey! You!

Get me down! Away from these worms! Please!

I'll play you! I'll even owe you one! Heck, I'll gladly sell out any cartel member you need dirt on!

Just get me down!

Also, don't walk too much. They can hear your footsteps. Move from rock to rock if you don't want a swarm coming after you.

==Completion==
Okay, okay! I'm okay.

==Notes==
Climb up on the rock to get to where Skedgit is dangling from the ceiling in a rope and interact with her to have her hop down on the rock.
