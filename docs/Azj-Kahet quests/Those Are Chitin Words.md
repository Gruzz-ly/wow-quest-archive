'''Those Are Chitin Words''' is the first of several grindy quests to obtain [[Siesbarg (mount)]] as a flying mount. To begin, level {{level|tww-max}} players should defeat [[Tka'ktath]], on a floating platform above the [[Salien Gutters]] in [[Azj-Kahet]], and loot a [[Vial of Tka'ktath's Blood]].

A good source for chitin is the [[Faithful of Zirix]] mobs outside the [[Cinderbrew Meadery]] in eastern [[Isle of Dorn]], where players can also double up on [[Crackling Shard]] drops. Additionally, repeated solo [[Follower Dungeons]] difficulty runs of [[Ara-Kara, City of Echoes]] or [[City of Threads (instance)|City of Threads]] are other options. Additionally, the invading sureki forces that the [[Flame's Radiance]] are fighting off in the [[Nightfall scenario]] also drop chitin.

Shalba is ''not'' looking for the {{g|wrath}} [[Nerubian Chitin]] crafting reagent, so don't spend a lot of gold for it on the [[Auction House]]!
