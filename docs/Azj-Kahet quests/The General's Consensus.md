'''The General's Consensus''' concludes the Unravelling the Trapped chapter of the [[Azj-Kahet storyline]]. The campaign continues immediately with [[A Most Intriguing Invitation]].

==Objectives==
Take [[Zev'kall]]'s pheromones to someone that has a use for them.
* [[Zev'kall's Pheromone Emitter]] {{co|51.8|25.9|Azj-Kahet}}

==Description==
We got lucky here. That [[Anub'azal|General Anub'azal]] is so interested in sabotaging [[Zev'kall]] that it's an enemy of our enemy is our friend situation for today.

I feel like this is gonna be a theme-- spiders tryin' to use us against each other. Ah, well.

You're gonna want to take those pheromones somewhere useful, huh? You do that. I'll take these folks safely out of harm's way.
