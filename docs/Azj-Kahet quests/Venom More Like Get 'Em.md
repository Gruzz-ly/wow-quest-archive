'''Venom? More Like Get 'Em''' is the second in a series of grindy quests to obtain [[Siesbarg (mount)]] as a flying mount. To begin, level {{level|tww-max}} players should defeat [[Tka'ktath]], on a floating platform above the [[Salien Gutters]] in [[Azj-Kahet]], and loot a [[Vial of Tka'ktath's Blood]]. Then, collect 1,500 [[Nerubian Chitin (Khaz Algar)]] from enemies in [[Khaz Algar]] for [[Those Are Chitin Words]].

For this quest, Shalba wants 1,000 [[Nerubian Venom]], specifically from [[nerubian]] enemies. Many of the same sources from the previous quest will suffice for this quest. Rare spawns [[Rhak'ik]] and [[Khak'ik]] on patrol of the [[Silken Path]] near {{co|44|30|Azj-Kahet}} drop 10-12 venom. Enemies in the [[City of Threads (instance)|City of Threads]] dungeon are also a good source.
