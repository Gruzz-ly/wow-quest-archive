==Description==
As you might have heard, things haven't gone as intended for the citizens of the Silken Ward. The [[Pale Enforcers]] have been keeping a tight grasp on the citizens.

I wasn't able to find much before an ascended kicked me out for snooping around. You are a fresh face, go around the Silken Ward and speak to the citizens, see if you can find a way to help them.
