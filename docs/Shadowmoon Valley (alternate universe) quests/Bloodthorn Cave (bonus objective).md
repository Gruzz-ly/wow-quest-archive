==Notes==

Pick up [[A Matter of Life and Death]] from [[Roona]], just inside the cave. Also find rare pale [[Gorum]] on the ceiling, who drops the [[Precious Bloodthorn Loop]] and 10-20x [[Garrison Resources]]. Beyond that, next to [[Diaani]], inside the ribcage of some dead beast is a [[Rotting Basket]], worth some Garrison Resources.
