==Objectives==
Speak with {{Au|Prophet Velen}} in {{Au|Shadowmoon Valley}} on {{Au|Draenor}}.

==Description==
All able-bodied citizens of the [[Alliance]] are to report to Prophet Velen in Shadowmoon Valley on Draenor.

The destruction of the {{Au|Dark Portal}} has stopped the [[Iron Horde]] invasion of [[Azeroth]], but now we must to take the fight to them.

With Prophet Velen's blessing, we will be able to establish a strategic position in Draenor to fight against the enemies of Azeroth.

Glory to the Alliance!
