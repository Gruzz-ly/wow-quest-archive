A note attached to the entry in the cookbook hints to where one might be found: "Reminder: Order more high-voltage detonators. The last shipment was lost off of the {{Au|Shadowmoon Valley|Shadowmoon}} coast near the [[demon]]-inhabited cliffs. Maybe we should send a diving team to salvage."
