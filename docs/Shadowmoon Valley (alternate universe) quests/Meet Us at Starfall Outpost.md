Speak with [[Archmage Khadgar]] at [[Starfall Outpost]].

==Description==
The archmage has uncovered a potential [[Shadow Council (alternate universe)|Shadow Council]] presence in [[Shadowmoon Valley (alternate universe)|the valley]]. He asked me to let you know.

As soon as you can, join us at Starfall Outpost. We're going to need your muscle to root them out, commander.
