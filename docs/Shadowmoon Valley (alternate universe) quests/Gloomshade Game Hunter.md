==Completion==

==Notes==
Pick up [[Lost Lumberjacks]] before heading out. Exit the garrison to the south and cut east across the northern [[Plains of Serenity]] to find the [[Lost Packmule]]. Kill [[Gloomshade Howler]]s, [[Gloomshade Fungi]], and [[Gloomshade Gulper]]s for ribs, spores, and legs respectively.

You can also choose to pick this up separately from the [[List of Ingredients]].
