== Notes ==
This is one of the three bonus objectives in {{Au|Shadowmoon Valley}}. The eggs are hidden at the base of trees, and there are plenty of arcane anomalies in the area. The netherbinder is in the northeast portion of the quest area.

[[File:Faebright.jpg|thumb|Faebright]]
Keep an eye out for [[Faebright]] not far to the west of the netherbinder. It drops the [[Dragonrider's Tinkered Leggings]] and 10-20x [[Garrison Resources]].
