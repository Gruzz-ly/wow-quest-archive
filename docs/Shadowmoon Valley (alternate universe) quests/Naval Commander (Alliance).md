==Objectives==
Find [[Merreck Vonder]] in your [[Lunarfall Shipyard|Shipyard]] and complete a Naval Mission.
*Meet Merreck Vonder at your fleet command table
*Start a Naval Mission from the Naval Fleet Table
*Complete a Naval Mission

==Description==
We have come a long way in a short time on this shipyard, <name>. Now it's time to run our first naval mission and control the seas of {{Au|Draenor}}!

I will need to setup shop from a high vantage point to plan out these seafaring missions. Meet me near the shipyard entrance and we can get started.
