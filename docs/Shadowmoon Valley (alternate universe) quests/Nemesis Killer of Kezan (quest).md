==Objectives==
Kill 500 Goblin Players in Player versus Player combat across Draenor.
* Goblin Players slain (500)
* [[Echoing Betrayal]] (provided)

==Description==
<Kuros hands you the bloody heart.>

The ritual is complete. Your nemesis is marked for death.

Now your work to rid this world of the enemy truly begins. Kill all goblins that stand defiantly against the [[Alliance]]. Let their deaths serve as a warning to the rest of the [[Horde]]: they will leave {{Au|Draenor}} or they will die.

Return when you have quenched your rage with the blood of goblin.

Conquer our enemy and be known as the [[Killer of Kezan|killer of Kezan]]. Fail and be nothing.

==Completion==
The truth of your deeds is in your eyes. Your hunger for the enemy sated with cries for mercy and rivers of blood.

Take now your place as killer of Kezan.
