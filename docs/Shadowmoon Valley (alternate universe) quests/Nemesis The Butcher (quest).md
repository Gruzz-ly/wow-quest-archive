==Objectives==
Kill 500 Tauren Players in Player versus Player combat across Draenor.
* Tauren Players slain (500)
* [[Echoing Betrayal]] (provided)

==Description==
<Kuros hands you the bloody heart.>

The ritual is complete. Your nemesis is marked for death.

Now your work to rid this world of the enemy truly begins. Kill all tauren that stand defiantly against the [[Alliance]]. Let their deaths serve as a warning to the rest of the [[Horde]]: they will leave {{Au|Draenor}} or they will die.

Return when you have quenched your rage with the blood of tauren.

Conquer our enemy and be known as [[the Butcher (title)|the butcher]]. Fail and be nothing.

==Completion==
The truth of your deeds is in your eyes. Your hunger for the enemy sated with cries for mercy and rivers of blood.

Take now your place as the butcher.
