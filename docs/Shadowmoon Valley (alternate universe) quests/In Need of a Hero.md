==Objectives==
[[File:In Need of a Hero - fires.jpg|thumb|The fires to douse]]
Save [[Squiggy]] by dousing fires with buckets of water.
*Bookshelf fire doused
*Floor fire doused
*Table fire doused

==Description==
I...<sob>... I can't leave...<gulp>... without... Squiggy...!

==Notes==
Step outside and pick up a Draenei Bucket next to the well. Run back in, use the quest item on a fire, then run back out and repeat the process until all three fires are out.
