'''Back to the Depths''' is a [[world quest]] that takes place at [[Mist's Edge]] in the {{g|bfa}} phase of [[Darkshore]]. It can only become available when the [[Alliance]] is in control of the zone.

==Objectives==
Exterminate the [[Greymist tribe|Greymist]] (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Tyrande Whisperwind]]|[[Murloc]] infestations continue to plague our lands and harrass our people. Exterminate them.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
*1x [[7th Legion Service Medal]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Tyrande Whisperwind|These foul pests are a blight upon Darkshore. They will not be missed.}}

==Notes==
Objectives to fill the bar include:
*Kill [[Greymist Warrior]]s (3%), [[Greymist Tidehunter]]s (3%), and [[Shallows Crawler]]s (2%)
*Destroy [[Murloc Food Stores]] (4%), [[Greymist Junk Stash]]es (4%), and [[Greymist Weaponry]] (8%)
