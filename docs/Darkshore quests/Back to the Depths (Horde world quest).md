'''Back to the Depths''' is a [[world quest]] that takes place at [[Mist's Edge]] in the {{g|bfa}} phase of [[Darkshore]]. It can only become available when the [[Horde]] is in control of the zone.

==Objectives==
Exterminate the [[Greymist tribe|Greymist]] (0/100%)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Nathanos Blightcaller]]|These intolerable [[murloc]]s are becoming more than an inconvenient nuisance. Take care of them.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +75 reputation with [[Honorbound|The Honorbound]]
*1x [[Honorbound Service Medal]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Nathanos Blightcaller|Ah, sweet silence. Their incessant gibbering can be quite taxing.}}

==Notes==
Objectives to fill the bar include:
*Kill [[Greymist Warrior]]s (3%), [[Greymist Tidehunter]]s (3%), and [[Shallows Crawler]]s (2%)
*Destroy [[Murloc Food Stores]] (4%), [[Greymist Junk Stash]]es (4%), and [[Greymist Weaponry]] (8%)
