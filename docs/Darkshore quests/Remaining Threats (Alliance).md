'''Remaining Threats''' is available when the [[Alliance]] controls [[Darkshore]].

==Objectives==
Defeat 12 rare enemies in [[Darkshore]].
* Rare enemies slain (12)
* Suggested players: 5

==Description==
We have managed to route the [[Horde]] presence in Darkshore, but several threats remain throughout the countryside.

If we wish to hold this land, they must be eliminated.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +150 [[reputation]] with [[7th Legion (faction)|7th Legion]]
*200 [[War Resources]]

==Progress==
How's your progress?

==Completion==
You have strengthened our position and brought some measure of safety to the people of Darkshore

You have my thanks.
