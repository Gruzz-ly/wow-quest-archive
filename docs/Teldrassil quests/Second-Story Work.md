==Objectives==
Retrieve the rune from the hidden idol, then use it to learn a new ability. Afterwards, return to [[Frahun Shadewhisper]].
*Learn Spell: Engrave Gloves - [[Shadowstrike (rogue rune)]]

==Description==
I have prepared a training exercise for you, <name>. There is an idol of an [[owl]] on the roof of this building. You should be able to see it from the south entrance. Inside the idol is a hidden compartment containing an engraved rune. Make your way to the roof, find and memorize the rune hidden in the idol, and return to me.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* 85 [[XP]]
*+50 [[reputation]] with [[Darnassus (faction)|Darnassus]]
*[[Cracked Leather Gloves]]

==Progress==
Have you found the rune?

==Completion==
Well done, <name>. Applying the rune to your equipment will grant you powerful new abilities.

There are many such runes throughout [[Azeroth]]. Seek them out, as they will help along your journey.
