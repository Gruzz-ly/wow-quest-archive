==Objectives==
Get in the arena at the [[Amphitheater of Anguish]] and defeat one of the [[elemental lord]]s! Return to [[Wodin the Troll-Servant]] to redeem your reward.
* Elemental Lord Defeated
* Suggested Players [5]

==Description==
I'm not gonna lie to you, pal. You might be overmatched here. This next one is a being from the [[elemental plane]]s! I don't even know what's gonna show up in that arena!

If you think you're up to it, get in there and give 'em hell! 

==Rewards==
You will receive: {{cost|19|50}}

==Progress==
No payout until the fight's over, <man/lady>. 

==Completion==
Unbelievable! I thought this whole place was going to explode when it first appeared! You really dominated that round! 

==Notes==
The elemental lord that spawns is random:
* [[Az'Barin, Prince of the Gust]], air revenant
* [[Duke Singen]], fire revenant
* [[Gargoral the Water Lord]], water revenant
* [[Erathius, King of Dirt]], earth revenant
