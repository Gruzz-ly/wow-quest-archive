==Objectives==
[[Chief Rageclaw]] at [[Light's Breach]] in [[Zul'Drak]] wants you to use the [[Drakuru "Lock Opener"|Drakuru "Lock Openers"]] found on various Drakuru trolls to free 8 Captured Rageclaw.
* [[Captured Rageclaw]] Freed (8)

==Description==
Drakuru... You know who this troll is? I think bad troll are controlled by the Drakuru troll. They call themself Drakuru trolls - that how I know. Now you see why I am chief of Rageclaw. Smartest of all [[wolvar]]!

But that not why I talk to you now. Bad troll come and kick Rageclaw out of den. Rageclaw that no run away held as prisoner in village. You go to [[Rageclaw Den]], southeast, and free captured Rageclaw. Kill troll for lock opener. You maybe kill many troll for many lock opener.
