==Objectives==
Assist Zul'jarra in lighting Hash'ey Shrines of Jan'alai.
*First Hash'ey Shrine lit
*Second Hash'ey Shrine lit
*[[Coalesced Flame]] placed

==Description==
Dis old, crazy [[Loa Speaker Brek|loa speaker]] be too busy dancin' to tell us what to do next.

But I think I know.

We could not light de shrines on de way to de temple. But now dat [[Jan'alai]]'s presence is here...

Maybe now we can.

I will take de ember to de hash'ey shrines. Den maybe de trial will start.

I jumped from de top of de temple of [[Akil'zon]]. I found my way through de mists of [[Halazzi]].

I am ready for Jan'alai's fire.

We can only hope dey will receive us after what [[Zul'jin|my grandfather]] has done.
