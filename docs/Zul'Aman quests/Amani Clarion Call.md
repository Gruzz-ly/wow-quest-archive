==Objectives==
Sound the Amani War Drums in Atal'Aman.
*Amani War Drum sounded (3)

==Description==
If you want to survive here in Atal'Aman, find and beat de city's battle drums. Dat will rally our remainin' soldiers forward to join de fight.

It will also bring all de tribes of Zul'Aman--de [[Witherbark]], de [[Revantusk]], and de [[Shadowpine]]--to our aid and tell our civilians to retreat to de [[Altar of Malacrass]] if we need to evacuate.

Use the drums to spread de word. Find me at de [[Altar of Malacrass]] when you are done.
