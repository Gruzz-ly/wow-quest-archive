# Zul'Aman storyline

[[File:WorldMap-NewZulAman.jpg|thumb|Zone map of Zul'Aman]]

The '''Zul'Aman storyline''' consists of all quests taking place in around around the revamped [[Zul'Aman]] in the [[Midnight campaign]]. Zul'Aman is one of three options for the level {{level|zul'aman}} range alongside the [[Harandar storyline]] and [[Arator's Journey]].

==Campaign==
The four-chapter campaign is tracked in the [[For Zul'Aman!]] achievement, which is one of the criteria of the zone meta-achievement [[Making an Amani Out of You]].
