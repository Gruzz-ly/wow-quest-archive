==Objectives==
Destroy the 12 [[Void Conduit]]s hindering [[Nalorakk]], [[Akil'zon]], [[Halazzi]], and [[Jan'alai]] in Broken Throne.
*Conduits near Jan'alai destroyed (3)
*Conduits near Halazzi destroyed (3)
*Conduits near Akil'zon destroyed (3)
*Conduits near Nalorakk destroyed (3)

==Description==
With de loa at our side, we can finally break de Twilight's Blade. And return to Amani'Zar with Mor'duun's head.

De loa are already attackin' de main source of Mor'duun's power: summoned spheres of pure void energy.

Go to de loa and offer your assistance. When de orbs destroyed, we can move forward and strike at Mor'duun!
