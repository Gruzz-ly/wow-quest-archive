==Objectives==
Help Warlord Akutu face the truth and speak to Kulzi.
*Staff shown to [[Warlord Akutu]]
*Find Warlord Akutu
*Give staff to Sij'ta

==Description==
So, I broke de staff. And everybody died.

Maybe dis is my mistake. Sometimes it be too hard to say we made a mistake. And den we made even more mistakes. We in this deep, now.

While you were talkin to the others. I tried to get through to [[Warlord Akutu|Akutu]] that we be dead. But he not listenin'. All he do is blame others. Maybe I deserve it.

Show Warlord Akutu de staff. Den take him to Kulzi. We need to try to talk to de loa.

De pieces of dis puzzle be fallin' into place.
