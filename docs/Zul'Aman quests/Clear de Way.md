==Objectives==
Regroup with [[Zul'jarra]], [[Zul'jan]], and [[Lady Liadrin]] at the foot of [[Conquerer's Sanctum]] in [[Broken Throne]].

We must take advantage of dey turned backs.

I will lead de charge through de ravine to de Conquerer's Sanctum beyond. Mor'duun is there, keepin' his coward-body safe from our spears and axes.

He won't be safe much longer.
