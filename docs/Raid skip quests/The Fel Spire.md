'''The Fel Spire''' is the second of two [[raid skip]] quests in {{au|Hellfire Citadel}}. After first defeating [[Gorefiend]] four times for [[Well of Souls (quest)]], Khadgar will turn around and offer this quest, requiring four more weeks of Mannoroth killing. Once done, a second portal will appear after zoning in to the raid.

As in Well of Souls, completing higher-difficulty versions of this quest automatically unlocks the skip on lower difficulties.

==Objectives==
Collect 4 Fel Essences from [[Mannoroth (tactics)|Mannoroth]] in {{Au|Hellfire Citadel}} on <Normal / Heroic / Mythic> difficulty.

If only I had powerful Fel Essences to work with, I could attune my magic to their defenses and bypass them. But the only place you might find such concentrated fel energy would be atop the Citadel itself. What a conundrum....
