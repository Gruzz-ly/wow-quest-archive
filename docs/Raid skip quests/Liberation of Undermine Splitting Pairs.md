'''Liberation of Undermine: Splitting Pairs''' is available at Renown 5 with the [[Gallagio Loyalty Rewards Club]] and serves as the traditional [[raid skip]] quest. After completing this quest, [[Lentil Sprocket]] will offer players a skip to the [[One-Armed Bandit]] and [[Mug'Zee]].

==Objectives==
Defeat [[Mug'Zee|Mug'Zee, Heads of Security]] within [[Liberation of Undermine]] on <Normal/Heroic/Mythic> difficulty.
*Defeat Mug'Zee, Heads of Security in the Liberation of Undermine raid on <Normal/Heroic/Mythic> difficulty or higher.

==Description==
If you're gonna take out the Big G, you're gonna have to bust a few heads - on the very same Ogre, as a matter of fact.

Mug'Zee's army of goons litter the Gallagio's shadiest back rooms. If you can secure the Tower of Spades, I'll drop you and your friends off there any time you like.
