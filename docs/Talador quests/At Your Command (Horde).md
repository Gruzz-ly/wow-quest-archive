==Description==
Commander, the outpost is nearly fully operational.

[[Gazlowe]] provided us with two different blueprints to fill this final plot - an [[Vol'jin's Arsenal|Arsenal]] or an [[Arcane Sanctum (Vol'jin's Pride)|Arcane Sanctum]].

Either one would provide us with the additional firepower we need to stop the [[Iron Horde]]'s advance.

The plans are on the drafting table. It's your call.
