== Description ==
In order to break through [[Kaelynara Sunchaser|Kaelynara's]] arcane shield, we will need a focusing crystal of rare power and quality.

Within the mine is a [[Colossus|crystal giant]] known to the Draenei as [[An'dure the Awakened|An'dure]]. The giant has slept far beneath the earth for ages, but due to Kaelynara's recklessness it has awakened.

The heart of a crystal giant is a truly powerful artifact. Defeat An'Dure{{sic}} and return to me with a crystal giant heart.

When you return I will construct the focusing crystal and together we will stop Kaelynara.

== Rewards ==

== Completion ==
