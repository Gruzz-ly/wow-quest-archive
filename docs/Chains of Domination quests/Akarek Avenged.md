==Objectives==
Meet with [[Margrave Sin'dane|Margrave Sin'Dane]]{{Sic}} in the [[Exoramas]] in the [[House of Rituals (subzone)|House of Rituals]].

==Description==
The last of those traitors involved in taking down [[Margrave Akarek]] are dead. 

It will not bring our Margrave back, but I am still pleased.

[[Vashj|My Baroness]] wished for us to inform Margrave Sin'dane that the job was done before departing for [[Ardenweald]].
