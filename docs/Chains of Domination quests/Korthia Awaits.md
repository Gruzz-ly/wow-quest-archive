'''Korthia Awaits''' is offered to alts of players who have completed the first three chapters of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]] by becoming [[The True Maw Walker]], opening the new [[Waystone to Oribos (Korthia)|Waystone to Oribos]], and permanently fending off the [[Eye of the Jailer]]. The next quest begins the "The Last Sigil" chapter, at [[Renown (Shadowlands)]] 44.

==Objectives==
Report to [[Tal-Galan]] at [[Keeper's Respite]] in [[Korthia]].
