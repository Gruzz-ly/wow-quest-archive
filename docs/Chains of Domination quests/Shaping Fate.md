'''Shaping Fate''' is the weekly high-level task objective for [[Korthia]] and the [[Maw]]. Players are nudged to complete one or both [[Chains of Domination campaign#Covenant assaults|Covenant assaults]] each week, alongside some [[Tormentors of Torghast]] events, and [[daily quest]]s in [[Korthia]].

Players will be rewarded with a hefty amount of reputation standing with the [[Death's Advance]].

==Objectives==
Thwart [[the Jailer]]'s efforts throughout [[Korthia]] and the [[Maw]] by completing [[Chains of Domination campaign#Covenant assaults|Covenant Assaults]], [[Chains of Domination campaign#Korthia dailies|Daily Quests]], [[Relic Fragment|looting treasures]], [[Conquering Korthia|slaying rare creatures]], and defeating [[Tormentors of Torghast]].
*Jailer's efforts subdued (0/100%)

==Description==
The Jailer's power and malevolence continue to grow. So must our resilience.

Stand amongst your allies, <name>. Push back against the darkness that would consume us all.

Together, we will yet prevail.

May the Purpose guide your hand.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[Emblem of Renown]]
*[[Death's Advance War Chest]]
*10x [[Stygian Ember]]
*2x [[Runekeeper's Ingot]]
* +750 reputation with [[Death's Advance]]

==Progress==
One cannot run from history, but one's actions may shape the outcome.

==Completion==
The threads of fate weave through you, Maw Walker.

Just as the prophecies foretold.
