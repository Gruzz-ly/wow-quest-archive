==Objectives==
Help [[Protector Kah-Rev]] defeat the invading [[Mawsworn]].
*Mawsworn forces weakened (0/100%)
*Meet with Secutor Mevix within the Vault of Secrets.

==Description==
To reach [[Vault of Secrets|the vault]], we'll need to carve a path through the attacking Mawsworn.

The covenants are doing their best to drive off these monstrosities, but our allies can't hold out forever. The Mawsworn are intent on reaching the prize before you.

We could use your help fighting against [[the Jailer]]'s forces, Maw Walker.

Purpose guide us all.
