==Objectives==
Speak with [[Avaros Dawnglaive]] at [[Barracks (Orgrimmar)|The Barracks]] in [[Orgrimmar]].

==Description==
Avaros Dawnglaive sends word from Orgrimmar -- he wishes to speak with you when you're in the city. I needn't remind you that Avaros is an honored member of our order and his requests should not be ignored.

Next time you are in Orgrimmar, travel to The Barracks in the [[Valley of Honor]] and speak with him.
