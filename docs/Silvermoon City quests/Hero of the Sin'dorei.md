This quest starts the '''Hero of the Sin'dorei''' quest chain. It is available only to [[blood elf]] players.

==Objectives==
Take Dar'Khan's Head to [[Lor'themar Theron]] in [[Silvermoon City]].
*[[Dar'Khan's Head]] (provided)

==Description==
This is the beginning of a new chapter in the history of our race, <name>. Our lands will be reclaimed and we shall cement our bond with [[Sylvanas]] and her allies.

Take the traitor's head to Regent Lord [[Lor'themar Theron]] in Silvermoon City. He'll be eager to hear the news.
