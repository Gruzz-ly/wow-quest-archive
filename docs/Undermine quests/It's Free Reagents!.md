==Objectives==
Speak with the Steamwheedle Alchemist and follow their instructions to brew a potion from scavenged ingredients.
*Speak to the Steamwheedle Alchemist
*Alchemize a Junkyard Brew

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Marin Noggenfogger]]|You will never believe the kind of chemicals people throw away! Get into that junk and cook up some free potions.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*12,200 [[XP]]
*+75 [[reputation]] with [[The Cartels of Undermine]]
*+150 reputation with [[Steamwheedle Cartel (faction)|Steamwheedle Cartel]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Marin Noggenfogger|Tetanus? Eh, don't worry about it! I'm sure one of these fantastic elixirs you helped make can cure it.}}

==Notes==
Location: [[The Heaps]], [[Undermine]]

Follow the [[Steamwheedle Alchemist]]'s instructions and add ingredients from the [[Junkyard Dogs]], [[Caustic Sludge]], or [[Rusted Gas Pump]] to the potion until ready.
