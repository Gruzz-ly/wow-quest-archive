'''C.H.E.T.T.ing In''' unlocks at Renown 13 with the [[Cartels of Undermine]].
==Objectives==
Speak to [[C.H.E.T.T.]] on the second floor of [[Incontinental Hotel|The Incontinental]] to learn about [[C.H.E.T.T. List]]s.
*Talk with C.H.E.T.T. about C.H.E.T.T. Lists

==Description==
