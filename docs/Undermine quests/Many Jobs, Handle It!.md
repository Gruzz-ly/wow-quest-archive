'''Many Jobs, Handle It!''' is one of the two weekly [[Shipping and Handling]] tasks in [[Undermine]], started by [[Smaks Topskimmer]] in the [[Incontinental Hotel]]. The other is [[Urge to Surge]].

==Objectives==
Complete 10 jobs for [[Shipping and Handling|Shipping & Handling]].
* Sign a [[Cartel]] contract for the week
* Shipping & Handling jobs completedÂ (10)

==Description==
Shipping & Handling is tryin' out a new driver incentive program.

Do some jobs and let's see how it goes eh?

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*[[The Cartels of Undermine Commendation]] (+1000 reputation with [[Cartels of Undermine]])
*[[Hellcaller Chest]] (during [[A Greedy Emissary]])

You will also receive one of:
*[[Blackwater Cartel Commendation]] (+1000 reputation with [[Blackwater Cartel (faction)|Blackwater Cartel]])
*[[Venture Company Commendation]] (+1000 reputation with [[Venture Company (faction)|Venture Company]])
*[[Bilgewater Cartel Commendation]] (+1000 reputation with [[Bilgewater Cartel (Undermine)|Bilgewater Cartel]])
*[[Steamwheedle Cartel Commendation]] (+1000 reputation with [[Steamwheedle Cartel (faction)|Steamwheedle Cartel]])

==Progress==
Get back to work. Chop Chop!

==Completion==
Great job out there. Here's the little extra you were promised--and a sweet tip from the boss lady.

==Notes and trivia==
Hit #5 in the [[G-99 Breakneck]] to clock in and start getting jobs. Don't clock out until after getting sent to the mandated 30-second break. Alternatively, use the [[Caddy Caller]] and a regular mount if desired.

The quest title is one of many references to the [[Onyxia Wipe Animation]] video.
