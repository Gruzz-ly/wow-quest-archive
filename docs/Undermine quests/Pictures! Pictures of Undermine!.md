==Notes and trivia==
*Location: [[The Incontinental Hotel]], [[Undermine]]
*The legendary picture opportunity can be either [[S'ploder-Man]] or [[Batgob]]. The two are based on {{wp|Spider-Man}} and {{wp|Batman}}, respectively.
*This quest's name is likely a reference to ''Spider-Man'' character J. Jonah Jameson's obsession with getting "pictures of Spider-Man!"
