'''Boomball''' is the world quest version of [[The Hole Deal]], and works the same way.

==Objectives==
*[[Boomball|Holes-in-One]] (4)

==Description==
*Bilgewater: {{text|say|[[Monte Gazlowe]]|Take a few swings at the Boomball course. On me. You earned it.}}
*Blackwater: {{text|say|[[Baron Revilgaz]]|Look, you've been doing some good work lately. So, go enjoy a few holes on me at the course.}}
*Steamwheedle: {{text|say|[[Marin Noggenfogger]]|Not much of a sportsman myself, but I have some Boomball tickets. Take 'em if you want 'em. I'm not gonna use 'em.}}
*Venture Co.: {{text|say|[[Grimla Fizzlecrank]]|I got a bet goin' on the Boomball course, and you're going to be my ringer. Get in there and sink a few hole-in-ones.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*12,200 [[XP]]
*+75 [[reputation]] with [[The Cartels of Undermine]]
*+150 reputation with [[Bilgewater Cartel (Undermine)|Bilgewater Cartel]], [[Blackwater Cartel (faction)|Blackwater Cartel]], [[Steamwheedle Cartel (faction)|Steamwheedle Cartel]], or [[Venture Company (faction)|Venture Company]]
