'''Reduce, Reuse, Resell''' is the weekly quest for [[S.C.R.A.P.]] in [[Undermine]].

==Objectives==
Complete 3 S.C.R.A.P. jobs in Undermine.
*Sign a cartel contract for the week
*S.C.R.A.P. jobs completed (3)

==Description==
;Smaks Topskimmer
The guys over at S.C.R.A.P. could use some extra hands this week.

You want a piece of the pie, go find one of their reps named Carlo out in the field and see if he's got any work for you.

But if you find a piece of pie in any of those trash piles, don't, uh... Don't actually eat it. I hope that kinda goes without sayin'.

;Carlo Greasegrind
You smell that? That's the smell of profit to be made. And also a lot of biohazardous waste, but eh, same difference.

If you wanna shovel some trash and make some moolah, fine me out in the field. Doesn't matter if the job's already started. You pitch in, you'll still get a cut.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +1000 reputation with [[Cartels of Undermine]]
*[[Hellcaller Chest]] (during [[A Greedy Emissary]])

You will also receive one of:
* +1000 reputation with [[Blackwater Cartel (faction)|Blackwater Cartel]]
* +1000 reputation with [[Venture Company (faction)|Venture Company]]
* +1000 reputation with [[Bilgewater Cartel (Undermine)|Bilgewater Cartel]]
* +1000 reputation with [[Steamwheedle Cartel (faction)|Steamwheedle Cartel]]

==Progress==
