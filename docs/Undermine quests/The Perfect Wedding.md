==Objectives==
Stop the wedding crashers from doing too much damage to the venue.
* Speak with [[Papa Kraz Torquewrench|Kraz Torquewrench]]
* [[Durgo the Vast|Wedding Crashers]] disuaded

==Description==
I hired some goons to crash the wedding. Only the best weddings get crashed, right?

But, I don't want to lose my deposit on this here venue. So, I need you to stop my crashers from getting too crashy, ya know?
