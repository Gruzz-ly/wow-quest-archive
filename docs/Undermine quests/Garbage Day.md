==Description==
My old man's got all these crazy ideas about helping the world... I dunno. It's all a bit over my head, but I guess that's why I was never the inventor he wanted me to be.

Still, I've got some thoughts on how to solve problems around here. Like those dang rodents getting litter all over the streets!

It might not revitalize the spirit of the community or whatever, but I bet people would be happier if we cleaned up the streets.
