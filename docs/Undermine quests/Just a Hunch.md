'''Just a Hunch''' concludes the second chapter of the [[Undermine(d) campaign]], "Undermine Awaits". The next chapter, "Uncovering the Truth", begins immediately after with [[Red Tape]].

==Objectives==
Check in with Renzik about Gazlowe, and then meet Gazlowe outside of the Heaps.
*Listen to Renzik
*Meet outside [[Heaps (Undermine)|the Heaps]]
*Look for Renzik... quietly
*Watch Out!
*Check in with Renzik

==Description==
It's time to sort out what to do next. This [[Marin Noggenfogger|Noggenfogger]] business has [[Gazlowe]] on edge and we gotta move quickly to find out what's going on.

Something's got me on edge too. We should get outta [[Heaps (Undermine)|the Heaps]] fast. But let's make sure Gazlowe's on board..
