# Undermine(d) campaign

[[File:WoW The War Within Undermine(d).png|thumb|Undermine(d) key art.]]

The '''Undermine(d) campaign''' covers all quests added in [[patch 11.1.0]], mostly taking place in the [[Undermine]].

==Campaign: Undermined==
[[File:VZ-Undermine.jpg|thumb|Undermine zone map]]
The six-chapter main campaign is tracked in the [[Undermined (achievement)]] achievement. To begin, characters must be level {{level|tww-max}} and have completed the [[The War Within campaign#Leveling|level-up campaign]].
