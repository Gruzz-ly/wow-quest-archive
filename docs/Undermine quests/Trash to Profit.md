'''Trash to Profit''' unlocks [[S.C.R.A.P.]] in [[Undermine]], first available at Renown 2 with the [[Cartels of Undermine]].

==Objectives==
Talk to [[Carlo Greasegrind]] at [[Hovel Hill|Undermine Sanitation]].

==Description==
Ey! I heard you've been cleanin' up some of the trash around [[Undermine]]. Good on ya.

Any interest in takin' that more literally?

It's a dirty job, but lots of gold to be made if you're good at it. If that sounds up your alley, go talk to the boss man Carlo back at Undermine Sanitation.

Tell him I sent ya so I get my recruitment bonus!
