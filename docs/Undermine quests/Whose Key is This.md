'''Whose Key is This?''' is offered by [[Smaks Topskimmer]] at the [[Incontinental Hotel]] in [[Undermine]] after reaching renown 9 with the [[Cartels of Undermine]].

==Completion==
Hey pal, we found a weird key lying around. No idea who it belongs to, it ain't for the Incontinental. You want it?
