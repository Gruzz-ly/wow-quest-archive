==Objectives==
Deliver newspapers to the uninformed citizens of [[Undermine]].
*Newspapers Delivered (12)

==Description==
*Bilgewater: {{text|say|[[Monte Gazlowe]]|Our people need to be better informed! Get out there and bring them the news.}}
*Blackwater: {{text|say|[[Baron Revilgaz]]|So, are you lookin' for some extra pocket change? Then why don't ya pick up a paper route!}}
*Steamwheedle: {{text|say|[[Marin Noggenfogger]]|We're gonna need cash if we wanna keep this operation running! Go get a paper route or somethin'.}}
*Venture: {{text|say|[[Grimla Fizzlecrank]]|This propaganda's not gonna spread itself. Go deliver some newspapers, why don't ya?}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
*12,200 [[XP]]
*+75 [[reputation]] with [[The Cartels of Undermine]]
*+150 reputation with [[Bilgewater Cartel (Undermine)|Bilgewater Cartel]], [[Blackwater Cartel (faction)|Blackwater Cartel]], [[Steamwheedle Cartel (faction)|Steamwheedle Cartel]] or [[Venture Company (faction)|Venture Company]]

==Notes==
Location: [[The Vatworks]], [[Undermine]]

[[Uninformed Citizen]]s will react in various different ways when given the news:

*''[[Informed Citizen]]s accept the news and walk away.''
**{{text|say|Informed Citizen|Suddenly I feel so much more aware of the world around me!}}
**{{text|say|Informed Citizen|Can you believe what they print these days? Bonkers!}}
**{{text|say|Informed Citizen|Wow! That's real interestin'!}}
**{{text|say|Informed Citizen|Hey thanks! I got a boring shift ahead of me.}}

*''[[Malinformed Citizen]]s reject the news and attack:''
**{{text|yell|Malinformed Citizen|No news is good news!}}
**{{text|yell|Malinformed Citizen|Don't tell me, I don't wanna hear about it!}}
**{{text|yell|Malinformed Citizen|I'm about to kill the messenger!}}
**{{text|yell|Malinformed Citizen|How dare you make me read!}}

Also watch out for the hostile [[Cuisine Competitor]]s.
