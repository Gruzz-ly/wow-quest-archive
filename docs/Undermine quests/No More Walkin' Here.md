'''No More Walkin' Here''' begins the brief questline to unlock [[D.R.I.V.E.]] and the [[G-99 Breakneck]] trike. This quest unlocks roughly halfway through chapter 2 of the [[Undermine(d) campaign]].

==Objectives==
Meet [[Nanny Talullah]] on the third floor at <[[Shipping and Handling (subzone)|Shipping and Handling]]/the [[Caddy Shack]]>.

==Description==
We gotta do something about how long it's taking to get you around. Luckily, I know just the gal. Go meet Nanny Talullah at the Caddy Shack and see if she'll lend you some wheels.
