==Description==
Have you seen the ghosts wandering the graveyard?

They are risen ancestors, the spirits of slain mogu who have been called back to this world.

When angered, they attack swiftly and silently, and their numbers increase by the day.

They do not belong here. Destroy them, and send them back beyond the grave.
