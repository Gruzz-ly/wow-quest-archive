==Objectives==
Kill 10 [[Shan'ze Gravekeeper]]s, [[Shan'ze Soulripper]]s, or [[Shan'ze Thundercaller]]s.
*Shan'ze mogu slain at the Court of Bones (10)

==Description==
The [[Court of Bones (Isle of Thunder)|Court of Bones]] crawls with mogu forces. Endlessly do they perform their rituals of lightning and thunder.

If our mission to take [[Isle of Thunder|this island]] is to succeed, then we cannot allow them to hold such a strategic position.

They must be eliminated.
