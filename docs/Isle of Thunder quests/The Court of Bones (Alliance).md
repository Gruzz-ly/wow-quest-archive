==Objectives==
Find [[Narasi Snowdawn]] outside the [[Court of Bones (Isle of Thunder)|Court of Bones]].

==Description==
The mogu within the nearby Court of Bones comprise of of the greatest threats to our presence on this island. Narasi Snowdawn has been entrusted with halting their efforts, but I am certain she could use a capable <class> to see this task through.

Go to her. Aid in whatever ways that you are able.
