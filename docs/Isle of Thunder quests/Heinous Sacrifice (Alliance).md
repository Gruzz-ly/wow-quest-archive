==Objectives==
Destroy the Zandalari sacrificial altars.
*[[Cha'lat's Sacrificial Altar|Cha'lat's Altar]] destroyed {{co|33.8|76.1|Isle of Thunder}}
*[[Tec'uat's Sacrificial Altar|Tec'uat's Altar]] destroyed {{co|39.7|65.2|Isle of Thunder}}

==Description==
Some of the strongest of the spiritbinders perform heinous rites to their wretched gods.

We've devised a method to destroy their altars, so the difficult task will be getting to each altar alive.

Venture into the depths of [[Za'Tual]] and destroy their altars.
