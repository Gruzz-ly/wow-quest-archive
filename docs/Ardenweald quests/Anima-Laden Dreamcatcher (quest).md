==Objectives==
Return the Enchanted Dreamcatcher to [[Droman Dawnblossom]] in [[Dreamsong Fenn]].
* [[Anima-Laden Dreamcatcher]] (provided)

==Description==
You stumble upon an enchanted dreamcatcher, placed so remotely that it has managed to collect a significant amount of [[anima]] without being harvested. Droman Dawnblossom in nearby Dreamsong Fenn would surely appreciate its safe return.
