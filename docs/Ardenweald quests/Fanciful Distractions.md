==Objectives==
Find Riverblossom, Lunarwind, and Flitterfly and convince them to return to [[Nuuminuuru]].
*[[Riverblossom]] returned
*[[Lunarwind]] returned
*[[Flitterfly]] returned

==Description==
Ever chasing their fleets of fancy, my [[Faerie|faeries]] friends are seldom seen.

Their mercurial natures oft lead them astray, tracing them paths to distraction and dismay.

Seek my friends, insist they return to my roots, and both you and they may receive recompense.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|25|74}}

==Progress==
My wards know to return to my roost, to spin stories of their pursuits and strengthen their spirits.

The longer they flit away, the deeper the creases of my concern grow.

==Completion==
The waters of your realm revitilize{{sic}} my wards in ways those of our home cannot.

Once again, they return to rest.
