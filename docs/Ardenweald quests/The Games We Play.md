==Objectives==
Win the game of wits by using emotes to correctly answer the [[Playful Trickster]]'s riddles.
* Secret discovered

==Description==
Do not worry about me. We have a bigger problem.

I heard their leader around here somewhere during the attack. I have been keeping watch from here but I have not been able to see them. They must be using some sort of glamour to hide themselves.

I have yet to meet a [[spriggan]] who can resist a game. If you find one eager to play and can win, then perhaps you can learn something about their leader.

==Notes==
;Speaking with Gwenna
{{Gossip|What can you tell me of the faerie's game?}}

:Ah, the conversation game, is it? You will find that the faerie wants you to perform specific actions in response to their prompting.

:I would suggest reading their words carefully, for there may be some hint in what they want. Make sure you respond in a timely fashion, and whatever you do, do not be rude!

;Playing the game
Find and click on the [[Giggling Basket]] to make a [[Playful Trickster]] appear.

:{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|What have we here? Someone who wishes to play! What shall we play for, hmm?}}
:{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|I know, I know! If I win, we turn you into a toad! If you win, I'll tell you a secret that nobody else knows!}}
:{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|The rules are simple! You just need to keep up with my conversation and react appropriately! Easy, right? Then let's begin!}}

As the spriggan says, there are clues in the text as to what emote to use on him. You have 30 seconds to respond. This is shown through his {{Abilities|Waiting|icon=spell_holy_borrowedtime}} buff.

He will say the following prompts in a random order:
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|One time, I managed to trick all the [[sylvar]]i in a grove into thinking I was a member of their court! The other spriggans were all cheering my name for days!}}
**Type <code>/cheer</code>
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Oh, my feet are practically jumping with excitement! I could just dance for an eternity! Dance with me!}}
**Type <code>/dance</code>
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Spriggans have our share of heroes too! The great hero [[Hollain]] was said to be able to split a mountain with a single thrust of his spear. Oh, to see such a display! How strong he must have been!}}
**Type <code>/flex</code>
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|The fae courts are very big on manners, you know. The slightest lapse in decorum can have... devastating consequences. Introductions are an important part of first impressions!}}
**Type <code>/introduce</code>
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|We do so much to help out the people of the lands. I'm sure you've heard the stories. Mending shoes, growing fields, reuniting lost loves. But what do we get in return? Not so much as a word of praise! Hmph!}}
**Type <code>/praise</code>
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Many seek us for our talents, but few can actually earn them. Some give gifts, always gratefully accepted. Some try to outwit us, usually failing. Some ask permission, and always thank us for our trouble.}}
**Type <code>/thank</code>

After each correct response, the spriggan will say one of the following:
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Correct! Not bad....}}
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Hah, perfectly said! You're pretty good at this!}}
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Oh, very good! Very good!}}

Failing any prompt afflicts you with {{Abilities|Faerie Punishment|id=312063|icon=inv_frog2_green}} and causes the spriggan to admonish you, then disappear back into the basket.
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|I'm not mad. Just disappointed.}}
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|If I didn't know any better, I would say you have no concept of proper courtesy and decorum! But I told you the rules!}}
*{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|No, no, that's not right at all! Were you even paying attention?}}

After all six prompts are successfully passed, he'll concede:
:{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|I'm impressed! You are almost as clever as a spriggan... almost.}}
:{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Fine! You won fair and square, so here's the secret: the boss is terribly allergic to puffpetal powder! It'll send him into a sneezing fit!}}
:{{Text|say|Playful Trickster|Okay, bye! Let's play again sometime...}}
