==Objectives==
* Take the [[Modified Gormling Lure]]
* Deliver [[Gormdaughter]]s to the wrangler (8)

==Description==
:{{text|say|[[Hunt-Captain Korayn]]|A clutch of gormlings has begun evolving into matriarchs. Bring them to our wranglers while there is still time.}}

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* +125 reputation with [[The Wild Hunt]]

==Completion==
:{{text|say|Hunt-Captain Korayn|Good. The wranglers will ensure that the gormlings remain docile as they grow.}}

==Notes==
Upon taking the Modified Gormling Lure at the [[Banks of Life]], the player receives the Gormling Lure Totem buff that lasts 10 minutes. With the buff, go near [[Gormdaughter]]s. As they start following you, you gain the Gormlings Lured debuff to let you know how many of the gorm you have.
