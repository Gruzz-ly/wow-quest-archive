'''A Gift of Hope''' is one of the [[Shadowlands epilogue]] side quests, starting in [[Oribos]].

==Objectives==
Meet Shandris Feathermoon in the [[Grove of Awakening]].
*Speak to [[Shandris Feathermoon|Shandris]]

==Description==
Greetings, [[Maw Walker]].

I bear a summons from the [[Winter Queen]]. She requests your presence in to witness a ceremony involving others from [[Azeroth|your world]].

Shandris Feathermoon will be among them. You can find her in the Grove of Awakening.

==Rewards==
You will receive:
* {{cost|12|87}}

==Progress==
The Winter Queen awaits us, <name>.

==Completion==
The Winter Queen's gift... what we just witnessed...

<Shandris's eyes glisten with tears of hope.>

We must ask [[Ysera]] to help us keep this precious seed safe.
