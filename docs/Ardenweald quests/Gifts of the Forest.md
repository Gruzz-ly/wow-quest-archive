==Description==
These [[gorm]] have gone out of control! We need to stop them.

I have a plan, but I cannot do it on my own. We lure the gormlings to the [[Banks of Life]] by using special [[totem]]s that are drained of their [[anima]]. If you can retrieve the [[Lumber|wood]] required for the totem, I can prepare the ritual.

Take these pipes. Find the [[Culex]] trees growing on the banks and play a song for them. You will recognize them by their pale green leaves. The wood must be given freely for the ritual to work.
