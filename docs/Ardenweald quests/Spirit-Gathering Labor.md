==Description==
You are not like the others.

The waters here carry deep power. Power most have forgotten. But the tirnenn forget very little.

Power flows through the earth just as water flows with power. Flows can be trapped for a time, dripping. Drop by drop they drip into vessels.

Search for the vessels beneath the falls. Collect the power.
