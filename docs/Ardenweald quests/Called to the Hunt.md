==Description==
The [[Wild Hunt]] is made up of [[Night Fae|fae]] who have dedicated their lives to the protection of Ardenweald and its denizens. Each member has skill sharp as a blade and sense unlike no other.

We have spoken to a duo, an [[Evoker (occupation)|evoker]] and a [[Hunter|huntress]], who will permit us to capture their memories for training if we help them on a particular hunt.

Meet Alderyn in Claw's Edge. He is one you seek.
