'''The Azure Key''' is the [[Rogue]] class-specific [[Temple_of_Atal'Hakkar_(Classic)#Quest_guide|Sunken Temple Quest]].  It is a long quest chain that yields a very good {{quality|rare}} item for its level.

==Objectives==
Return the Azure Key to [[Lord Jorach Ravenholdt]].
*[[Azure Key]]

==Description==
It will take some time to decipher the fragments and remove the enchantment. When I am complete, I'll deliver the bag to Lord Ravenholdt personally.

There is still the matter of the key, said to be held by a green drake known as [[Hazzas and Morphaz|Morphaz]]. The drake has made its home deep in the [[Temple of Atal'Hakkar (Season of Discovery)|Temple of Atal'Hakkar]], the entrance to which lies in the {{C|Swamp of Sorrows}}.

Gather your friends and venture to his lair. Lord Ravenholdt will await your return with the key.
