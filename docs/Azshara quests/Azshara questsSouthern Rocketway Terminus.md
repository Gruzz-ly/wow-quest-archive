# Azshara quests/Southern Rocketway Terminus

After aiding those at the [[Orgrimmar Rocketway Exchange]] Horde adventures are [[Dozercism|sent]] to the Southern Rocketway Terminus. While here players are sent to help fix the havoc at the [[Secret Lab]], deal with the nearby [[naga]], and with the aid of [[Assistant Greely]] uncover exactly what [[Azsharite]] is and how to acquire more of it.
