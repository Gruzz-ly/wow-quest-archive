# Azshara quests/Orgrimmar Rocketway Exchange

After successfully defending the [[Orgrimmar Rear Gate]] Horde agents are [[Arborcide|sent]] to the [[Orgrimmar Rocketway Exchange]]. While here players are directed to venture into the [[Mountainfoot Strip Mine]] for various tasks. Players are also tasked with battling with the [[Naga]] that can be found nearby such as in the [[Ruins of Eldarath]]. Lastly with the aid of [[Malynea Skyreaver]] players can uncover the last days of the [[Mennar Academy]].
