    Description

    We know there's land inside the Voidstorm, through its massive portal. If we can reach it, we can take the fight to Xal'atath.

    I have found the key. It is a relic known as the Cynosure of Twilight. For ages, it has mystified us by revealing unreachable pathways to infinite unknowns.

    My research tells me I can use it to open a path to the storm.

    There's just one problem: it's in Magister's Terrace. And as you know, , we ren'dorei are not exactly... in their good graces, at the moment.
