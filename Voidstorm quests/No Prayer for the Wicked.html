    ==Description== Alleria has a plan to use this shadowgrafting material to capture that domanaar for information.

    But what good is information given by evil? I've fought countless demons and every one of them was a liar.

    Let us follow her plan, for now. Do what she asks with this metal, then slay the domanaar's pet beast.

    Once we speak with this vile creature, I will decide how long it lives.
