    ==Description== I don't see [[Decimus]]. For all their glee at handing us over, I'd expect they'd want to watch the fight. What could be more important to a [[domanaar]] than enjoying a battle?

    Answers can wait. Right now we need to survive.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|46|80}} 17,875 [[XP]]

    ==Completion== [[Lothraxion]]'s going to tear the nexus-points apart even if it costs us [[Silvermoon]]!

    ==Notes== Upon accept, :{{Text|Say|Alleria Windrunner|Weapons ready, we'll make ourselves an opening and deal with Decimus afterward.}}

    You are given the abilities which are in a rotation:

    {{Abilities|id=1230810|Sacred Armaments|icon=inv_ability_lightsmithpaladin_holybulwark|[[Arator the Redeemer|Arator]] infuses you with his light, shielding you and blessing your attacks with Holy damage.}}

    {{Abilities|id=1231784|Lothraxion's Holy Flame|icon=spell_holy_searinglight|Signal [[Lothraxion]] to bombard a location with holy fire.}}

    Upon depleting her health to 60%, :{{Text|Say|Alleria Windrunner|Entertained, Terminas? Come down here if you dare.}}

    Upon depleting her health to 30%, :{{Text|Say|Alleria Windrunner|Not bad, but it's time to end this.}}
