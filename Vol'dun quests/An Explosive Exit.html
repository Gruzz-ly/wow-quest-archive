    ==Objectives== Find [[Nisha's Wagon|Nisha's wagon]] and ride it to safety. * Find Nisha's wagon * Board Nisha's wagon * Defend Nisha's wagon * Ride to [[Vulpera Hideaway]]

    ==Description== Alright, . I think it's time we make a run for it.

    Hopefully my wagon's still nearby.

    I'll steer us to our hideaway. I just need you to lob explosives onto any [[sethrak]] who are brave enough to follow us.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Fangcaller's Staff |Sandcaster's Conductive Scimitar |Sethrak Warden's Staff |Sandscout's Kris |Sethrak Sandscout's Bow |Sethrak Warbringer's Scimitar |Sethrak Ravager's Claws |Sethrak Aggressor's Scimitar |Sethrak Warden's Scimitar }}

    You will also receive: * {{Cost|19|40}} * 16,450 [[XP]] * +250 reputation with [[Voldunai]]

    ==Progress== Let's get out of here while we still can.

    ==Completion== This is the [[Zandalari troll|troll]] you flew in with, is it not? She lives, for now.

    ==Notes== Head a short distance southwest to find [[Nisha's Wagon]] and the two accompanying [[Alpaca (NPC)|Alpacas]]. :{{Text|say|Nisha|Right where I left it!}}

    Interact with the wagon to board it. Nisha appears in the driver's seat while the player is in the back seat. :{{Text|say|Nisha|Hang on! This is gonna be a wild ride!}}

    The player is granted an extra action button: :{{Abilities|Volatile Potion|id=268494|Blasts the target with volatile liquid, dealing fire damage.|icon=inv_potion_33}}

    As the wagon sets out away from the [[Abandoned Burrows]] and across the [[Brine Basin]], hordes of [[Sethrak Ambusher]]s appear and begin rushing after the wagon. Throw Volatile Potions at them to kill them and prevent them from reaching the wagon. :{{Text|say|Nisha|Looks like we have company. Light 'em up, !}}

    After successfully defending the wagon: :{{Text|say|Nisha|Looks like we're in the clear. Great work back there.}} :{{Text|say|Nisha|The hideaway should be up ahead. We'll be safe there. For a while, at least.}}

    Before long, the wagon reaches the Vulpera Hideaway, where a wounded [[Bladeguard Kaja]] is lying on a carpet next to [[Kiro]] and [[Meerah]].

    :{{Text|say|Kiro|Nisha, thank the sands you're safe!}} :''Nisha jumps off the wagon and walks over to Kiro.'' :{{Text|say|Nisha|I always find a way. Is this another survivor?}} :{{Text|say|Kiro|She may not survive for long. Her wounds are severe.}}

    Completing this quest rewards the "Unlikely Allies" portion of [[Secrets in the Sands]].
