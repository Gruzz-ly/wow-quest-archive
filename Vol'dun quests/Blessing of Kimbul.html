    Blessing of Kimbul

    {{Questbox | id = 47581 | name = Blessing of Kimbul | faction = Horde | category = Vol'dun | level = {{level|Vol'dun}} | start = [[Teekcha]] | experience = 16,450 (at level 110) | rewards = [[Ring of Tides]] | reputation = +75 [[Tortollan Seekers]] | money = {{Cost|19|40}} (at level 110) | previous = [[The Ruined Temple]] | next = [[Offering for the Loa]] }}

    ==Objectives== Acquire the [[Ring of Tides (quest item)|Ring of Tides]]. Trial of Wisdom completed [[Ring of Tides (quest item)]]

    ==Description== The entrance to [[Temple of Kimbul|the temple]] is sealed by a magical barrier.

    According to my scrolls, the barrier can only be lowered by one who has gained the Ring of Tides.

    There is a statue somewhere in the temple grounds with the power to bestow the ring, but the ruins are overrun with jungle [[spider]]s. [[Tulu]] and I are not strong enough to explore the lower ruins ourselves.

    If you can gain the Ring of Tides and enter the temple, we may finally be able to face the [[naga]] head on!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: [[Ring of Tides]] {{Cost|19|40}} 16,450 [[XP]] +75 reputation with [[Tortollan Seekers]]

    ==Progress== The statue is somewhere within the temple ruins.

    ==Completion== Now that you have the Ring of Tides, you'll be able to enter the temple and make contact with the [[loa]].

    ==Notes== [[File:Tablet - northeast.jpg|thumb|Northeastern Tablet]] [[File:Tablet - southeast.jpg|thumb|Southeastern Tablet]] [[File:Tablet - southwest.jpg|thumb|Southwestern Tablet]] Pick up [[All Webbed Up]] and [[Jungleweb Infestation]] before heading out. On accept: :{{Text|say|Teekcha|The statue is somewhere within the temple ruins...}}

    Head to {{Coords|61.2|15|Vol'dun}} to find the [[Statue of Eraka No Kimbul]] inside a building, along with three wall [[Tablet]]s each telling of a different lesson of [[Kimbul]]. To pass the Trial of Wisdom and acquire the Ring of Tides, the player must correctly answer a series of five questions posed by the statue. Each question has six possible answers, of which only one is correct. The correct answers can be gleaned from the texts of the three Tablets.

    ;Northeast Tablet :Thus it was heard: :On one occasion the great loa addressed a group of his followers and spake thus: :'Just as the jaguar stalks her prey for days at a time, even so the wise one acts with patience in all matters. The foolish are dragged through life by craving and emotion, but fools do not survive long in the jungle. :Just as the jungle bestows the gift of life, even so does it bestow the touch of death. Those with vision see that nature does not belong to them, but they to it. These are they who respect the natural order and take only what is required to live.' :Upon hearing these words, the followers of Eraka no Kimbul were filled with patience and respect.

    ;Southeast Tablet :Thus it was heard: :On one occasion the great loa addressed a group of his followers and spake thus: :'Just as a tiger leaps across rock and chasm while engaged in the hunt, even so the courageous one wastes no time cowering in the face of fear. Act with courage in all matters and you will not lose sight of your prey. :Just as decay devours the blossoms of the jungle, age devours the strength of youth. The source of power is not strength, but an indomitable will that age cannot destroy.' :Upon hearing these words, the followers of Eraka no Kimbul were filled with courage and conviction.

    ;Southwest Tablet :Thus it was heard: :On one occasion the great loa addressed a group of his followers and spake thus: :'Just as a king does not trade away his greatest treasure for a mere handful of dirt, even so the honorable one does not barter his integrity for wealth or fame. Live a life of honor, and it will be a life well-lived. :Just as canyons are carved by the constant flow of water, so too are all great deeds born of perseverance. Live with unyielding determination and failure will never defeat you.' :Upon hearing these words, the followers of Eraka no Kimbul were filled with virtue and dedication.

    ;Questions [[File:Statue of Eraka No Kimbul.jpg|thumb|Statue of Eraka No Kimbul]] If the player answers one of the Statue of Eraka No Kimbul's questions incorrectly, the statue will respond: : :'You have been judged unworthy. :Ponder the words of the great loa, Eraka no Kimbul, and return when you are ready.'

    ...and force the player to start over from the first question. The questions are listed below. The correct answer is in '''bold type'''.

    ;First question : :'What do you seek?' {{Gossip|I seek the power to slay my enemies.}} {{Gossip|I seek the wealth and fortune of kings.}} {{Gossip|'''I seek the loa's blessings.'''}} {{Gossip|I seek the knowledge of ancient kingdoms.}} {{Gossip|I seek great glory and prestige.}} {{Gossip|I seek nothing at all.}}

    ;Second question : :'What is the lesson of the leaping tiger?' {{Gossip|Only the strong survive.}} {{Gossip|Fear is for the weak.}} {{Gossip|Look before you leap.}} {{Gossip|Pride comes before the fall.}} {{Gossip|'''Act with courage in all matters.'''}} {{Gossip|Always watch your back.}}

    ;Third question : :'What is the source of power?' {{Gossip|'''An indomitable will.'''}} {{Gossip|Loyal followers.}} {{Gossip|A handsome face.}} {{Gossip|Great strength.}} {{Gossip|A keen mind.}} {{Gossip|Money. Lots of money.}}

    ;Fourth question : :'What is a life well-lived?' {{Gossip|A life lived in luxury.}} {{Gossip|A life filled with glory.}} {{Gossip|A life lived in peace.}} {{Gossip|'''A life lived with honor.'''}} {{Gossip|A life filled with accomplishment.}} {{Gossip|A life filled with pleasure.}}

    ;Fifth question : :'What do those with vision see?' {{Gossip|They see the world around them.}} {{Gossip|'''They see that they belong to nature.'''}} {{Gossip|They see the truth.}} {{Gossip|They see shape and color.}} {{Gossip|They see the face of their enemy.}} {{Gossip|They see the future.}}

    [[File:Ring of Tides.jpg|thumb|Ring of Tides]] Should the player correctly answer all five questions, the statue will state: : :'You have been judged worthy. :Receive the blessing of Eraka no Kimbul. The Loa of Tigers awaits you.'

    The [[Ring of Tides (quest item)]] will then appear, floating above the basin at the bottom of the statue. Loot it and return to Teekcha.
