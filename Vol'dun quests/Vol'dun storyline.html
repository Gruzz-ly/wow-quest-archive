    Vol'dun storyline

    thumb|Zone Map

    The Vol'dun storyline takes part in seven chapters, tracked by the achievement Secrets in the Sands:

    Main storyline

    General Jakra'zet has secret dealings with the sethrak out in the desert and guards those secrets with deadly intent. The desert scavengers known as the vulpera prove to be valuable allies in your time of need.
