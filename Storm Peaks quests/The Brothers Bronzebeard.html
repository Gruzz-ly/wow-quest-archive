    The Brothers Bronzebeard

    {{questbox | id = 12973 | name = The Brothers Bronzebeard | faction = Alliance | category=Storm Peaks |level={{level|Storm Peaks}} | start = [[Brann Bronzebeard]] | end = [[Velog Icebellow]] | reputation = +500 [[Explorers' League]], +500 [[The Frostborn]] | experience = 37000 | rewards = [[Ring of the Northern Winds]], [[Jagged Ice Band]],

    In this quest the player jumps in Brann's flying machine, while he flies it to Frosthold, the player must defend it against the iron dwarves as they attempt to fly to the hold.

    ==Objectives== Accompany [[Brann Bronzebeard]] and his [[Brann's Flying Machine|flying machine]] to [[Frosthold]]. * Accompany Brann Bronzebeard to Frosthold.

    ==Description== That does it! We finally have the keystone... It's beautiful! I've been around the world more than once, but this thing's got me all jittery with excitement again. I suppose now's not the time to stop and admire though, we need to get the heck out of here.

    Hop on my flying machine and help keep us covered from those [[iron dwarf|iron dwarves]] on the way out!

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|Ring of the Northern Winds|Jagged Ice Band|Amberglow Signet|Iceforged Battle Ring}}

    ==Progress== My pardons, ! I got all caught up in tha moment and I neglected ye entirely.

    ==Completion== King Stormheart sends his most sincere thanks for reuniting him with his brothers. It seems he's off to settle a score now - perhaps you'll meet with him again on Arthas's doorstep.

    ==Notes== [[File:Bronzebeard Brothers.jpg|thumb|300px|The brothers Bronzebeard, reunited]] Accept the quest and hop on {{NPC|Alliance|Brann's Flying Machine}}. [[Stormforged Soldier|Stormforged Soldiers]] will jump from the edge and land on the other wing. Kill them before they do too much damage to the plane.

    Upon landing at Frosthold, wait for the reunion scene (below) to play out completely. Once it does, the quest objective will be complete.
