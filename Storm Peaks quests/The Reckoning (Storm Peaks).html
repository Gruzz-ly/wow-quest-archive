    [[Gauntlets of the Windreacher]], or [[Gauntlets of Vigilance]] |previous=[[Loken's Lackeys]] and [[The Earthen Oath]] |next=[[Stormherald Eljrrin]] }} '''The Reckoning''' is part of [[the Sons of Hodir quest chain]].

    ==Objectives== Meet [[Thorim]] near the [[Temple of Wisdom]]. Report the outcome of the fight to [[King Jokkum|Jokkum]] in [[Dun Niffelem]]. * [[#Notes|Witness the Reckoning]] * [[Horn of the Peaks]]

    ==Description== [[Loken]] killed my wife, turned my followers and me against each other. He took away everything I had, but today... that changes.

    Today we reclaim our sacred grounds. Today... Loken dies.

    Meet me on the bridge near the Temple of Wisdom, just southwest of Ulduar. I will finish this one way or the other.

    I need you there as a witness. You will not aid me -- this is personal.

    After it's done, send word to Jokkum, King of the [[frost giant|Frost Giants]]. He's a good friend and will know what to do.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|Gloves of the Servant|Rough Climber's Grips|Gauntlets of the Windreacher|Gauntlets of Vigilance}}

    ==Progress== Ach, ! It's good to see you, little one.

    ==Completion== No... it cannot be...

    No one can defeat Thorim in single combat. Not a giant, nor a beast... no one! Especially not that coward of a brother of his.

    On my forefather's names, this I swear...

    Loken will pay for this!
