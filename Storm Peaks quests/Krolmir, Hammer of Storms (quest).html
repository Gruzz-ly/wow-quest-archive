    '''Krolmir, Hammer of Storms''' is part of [[the Sons of Hodir quest chain]].

    ==Objectives== [[Thorim]] wants you to talk to [[King Jokkum]] in [[Dun Niffelem]] and discover what he knows of [[Krolmir]]. * Krolmir's Fate Discovered * [[The Sons of Hodir]] ({{reputation|Friendly}})

    ==Description== One task remains before I can reclaim my home at the [[Terrace of the Makers]].

    Krolmir must be found.

    On that fateful day, I threw it to the earth with all the power and rage I possessed. A mighty explosion followed.

    I've thought it lost for these many years, but now I must be certain.

    Return to King Jokkum and discover what, if anything, he knows of my hammer's fate.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|Locket of Snowcrest|Storm-weathered Cuffs|Stormstalker's Clutch|Backhanded Grips}}

    ==Completion== Words cannot express my gratitude, .

    You have served me honorably. Please accept this token of my gratitude.

    ==Notes== [[File:King Jokkum tour.jpg|thumb|King Jokkum, taking a gnome to the Thunderfall.]] At {{reputation|neutral}}, the following dialog takes place: {{gossip|''If it please you, King Jokkum, may I know what has become of Krolmir?''}} :I do not trust you enough to speak of such things. :You must respect the trials our people have endured, the burdens we have borne... :Perhaps, if you were to bring great honor to Dun Niffelem, I would be inclined to share with you the knowledge of our people.

    This response is no longer possible; the reputation awarded for completing previous quests have been increased to ensure that the character is at least {{reputation|Honored}} by the time they reach this point in the chain.

    At {{reputation|friendly}}, the following dialog can take place: {{gossip|''If it please you, King Jokkum, may I know what has become of Krolmir?''}} :Very well, little . :While there is much left for you to do to mend our relations with Thorim, I am compelled to share with you this one secret. :Speak to me again when you are ready to be shown that which you seek. {{gossip|''I am ready to be shown the fate of Krolmir.''}}
