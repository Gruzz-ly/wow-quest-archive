    {{Questbox | id = 81556 | name = Foreign Fog | disambigcat = Ringing Deeps | displaycat = The Ringing Deeps | category = Ringing Deeps | level = {{level|Ringing Deeps}} | start = [[Noli Marlen]] | experience = 10,400 | money = {{Cost|23|40}} | shareable = Yes | previous = [[The High Speaker's Secret]] | next = [[Fog Tags]], [[Unnatural Predators]] }}

    '''Foreign Fog''' begins an optional chapter of the [[Ringing Deeps storyline]], available after completing the Dark Revelations chapter of the campaign. [[Noli Marlen]] is waiting outside the northern end of [[Taelloch]], just north of [[Camp Murroch]] in south-central [[Ringing Deeps]].

    ==Objectives== Place the fog collector and collect fog samples from different areas occupied by fog beasts. First fog site reached First fog collector filled (100) Second fog site reached Second fog sample collected (100) Third fog site reached Third fog sample collected (100)

    ==Description== Pleasure to meet you, adventurer. I wonder if you might be willing to help me with some research?

    I'm sure you've noticed the fog beasts that have recently started encroaching upon this area.

    People see them as a bad omen, they always seem to show up during periods of ecological imbalance. I disagree with this assertion.

    Still as an expert in all vapor-based magics, I've been hired to find out where they're coming from so they can be cut off at the source.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|23|40}} 10,400 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Have you collected the fog samples?

    ==Completion== Excellent! I'll start evaluating these samples right away.

    ==Notes== [[File:Foreign Fog.jpg|thumb|A fog collector and [[Frenzied Glowmite]]s]] On accept: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Here are three fog collection devices. And if any of those fog beasts attack, well... you'll have to defend yourself.}}

    Head north down into the ravine to find the first spot: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Perfect, that's the spot! Now just place the fog collector and let it do its thing. Stay close, though! It needs to be monitored.}}

    Use the extra action button that appears: :{{abilities|Place Fog Collector|Place Noli's [[Fog Collector|fog collector]] to start siphoning fog samples from the area.|id=443370|icon=inv_gizmo_manasyphon}}

    Place it to aggro waves of [[Frenzied Glowmite]]s: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Huh. I expected it might aggravate the fog beasts. I did not expect it to attract glowmites.}}

    Don't wander too far off: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Oops! You got too far away from the collector. You'll have to place it again to finish the job.}}

    Let the bar hit 100%: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Excellent, that's one down! The next spot is in the Rumbling Wastes.}}

    Rinse and repeat at the marked spot further north in the [[Rumbling Wastes]]: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Easy, right! Only one more. This one might be... a little more dangerous. It's inside Taelloch.}} :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|I know, I know. Dangerous and overrun with skardyn. But there's a high concentration of fog! There's no way around it.}}

    Head back south to Noli, then take a left to enter [[Taelloch]]. Clear out the enemies, then place the fog collector once more: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|Okay, those are worse than fireflies. But nothing you can't handle, right?}}

    Waves of two [[Deepflayer Hunter]]s attack. Fend them off: :{{text|say|Noli Marlen|That's all the samples! Easy! Well, easy for me. Haha. Sorry. You can come back now.}}
