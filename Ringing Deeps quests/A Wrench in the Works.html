    '''A Wrench in the Works''' begins the Dark Revelations chapter of the [[Ringing Deeps storyline]].

    ==Objectives== Encourage members of the crowd to air their grievances. * Grievance heard (4)

    ==Description== Looks like the [[High Speaker Eirich|High Speaker]] is addressing his workers. It seems something has finally shaken them from their complacency, and their time away from the [[Awakening Machine]] is affecting them.

    <[[Moira Thaurissan|Moira]] pauses to brush a bit of dust off her robe.>

    Perhaps this would be a good opportunity for everyone to hear what they have to say?

    We should convince a few of these [[Machine Speakers]] to speak up.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|23|40}} 10,850 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Recalcitrant lot, aren't they?

    ==Completion== Excellent, now we're starting to make some progress.
