    '''New Candle, New Hope''' is the climax of the By Candlelight chapter of the [[Ringing Deeps storyline]].

    ==Objectives== Recover the Candle Crown from the [[Darkflame Cleft]]. * Speak with [[Skitter]] to enter the Darkflame Cleft * [[Candle Crown]] retrieved

    ==Description== We weaken [[The Candle King|Candle King]]'s grasp. Still need symbol, rally my people.

    King has ancient relic: symbol of hope. Called Candle Crown. Heart of my people. Candle King threw away! Found new prize: Darkflame Candle.

    That crown? Still in hoard. Candle King should come out now. See what we did here!

    While he looks, we sneak in! Use old tunnels! Take what's ours! We smart.

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: [[Earthen Traveler's Boomstick]] [[Earthen Traveler's Chopper]] [[Earthen Traveler's Claymore]] [[Earthen Traveler's Crossbow]] [[Earthen Traveler's Dagger]] [[Earthen Traveler's Fist]] [[Earthen Traveler's Gavel]] [[Earthen Traveler's Handaxe]] [[Earthen Traveler's Javelin]] [[Earthen Traveler's Saber]] [[Earthen Traveler's Scepter]] [[Earthen Traveler's Shield]] *[[Earthen Traveler's Staff]]

    You will also receive: {{Cost|70|20}} 16,300 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== We take hope from darkness, .

    ==Completion== Ha! We did it! Everyone knows now: Candle King is not only way.

    ==Notes== [[File:VZ-Darkflame Cleft.jpg|thumb|[[Darkflame Cleft]] instance map]]

    Speak with Skitter again: {{dialogue|Are you ready, ?

    {{gossip|quest=|I'm ready to infiltrate Darkflame Cleft.}}}}

    This starts a scenario set in the [[Darkflame Cleft]] dungeon.

    ===Kobold Disguise=== :Apply the disguise Skitter has made for you out of the scrap recovered from the Warrens *Disguise yourself as a kobold

    After entering: :{{text|say|Skitter|I make disguise out of scrap you collect! So look like kobold and sneak past others.}}

    At any point, should the need arise, Skitter can be talked to, in order to leave: {{dialogue|Candle Crown will be in throne room.

    {{gossip|I need to leave the Darkflame Cleft. Take me back to the Warrens. {{Text|red|}}}}}}

    Use the Disguise: Kobold extra action button.

    ===Snuff the Candles=== :Extinguish the [[Wavering Candle|large candles]] scattered about the chamber, plunging the area into darkness and driving off the massive kobold guarding the door. *Candles snuffed (6)

    :{{text|say|Skitter|No get too close to kobolds. Sneak in, take back crown!}} When you snuff off a candle an unnatural darkness ({{Abilities|It's Getting Dark In Here|id=430819|Maximum Health reduced by 5%.|icon=spell_shadow_auraofdarkness|notes=Stacks up to 15 times, totalling at 75%}}) envelops the area: {{text|say|Skitter|Just dark. Dark can't hurt you if keep light inside.}} {{text|say|Skitter|Ah!}} *{{text|say|Skitter|Eeep!}}

    Enemies might say one of the following lines when extinguishing candles: {{text|say|Ah!}} {{text|say|Eee!}} {{text|say|Eeep!}} {{text|say|No eat me!}} *{{text|say|Too dark!}}

    When you try to approach the entrance to the [[Waxatory]] before snuffing out all the candles, [[Tiny (kobold)|Tiny]], who is standing guard, says: :{{text|say|Tiny|King say: no one come in when King is out. No one! Go!}} When you snuff out all the candles: :{{text|say|Tiny|I...I just remembered a thing I have to do. In another part of the mine!}} :{{text|say|Skitter|Not too big to be scared of dark, was he?}}

    ===Kill Waximus=== :The way forward is blocked with waxy buildup. Defeat [[Waximus]] to dislodge it. *Waximus slain.

    The room is patrolled by [[Candleclaw Underboss]]es, [[Candleclaw Scrambler]]s, [[Candleclaw Pyromancer]]s and [[Waxy Globule]]s. Both entrances to the room are flanked by a pair of [[Oil Packed Brazier]]s. The braziers by the door onwards can also be lit in order to melt the blocking wax. :{{text|say|Skitter|What is that?}} After defeating Waximus: :{{text|say|Skitter|The candles melted! We go deeper!}} :{{text|say|Skitter|Just ahead! Almost there!}}

    ===Enter the Throne Room=== :Make your way into the Candle King's inner sanctum. *Enter the Throne Room

    Proceed through the room, while doding [[Candleclaw Knight]]s. The [[Candleclaw Bootlicker]]s can be ignored as long as the disguise is up.

    ===Acquire the Candle Keys from Dripear=== :Defeat [[Dripear the Waxy]], the High Wax-o-mancer, and take his keys. *Acquire the [[The Candle Keys|Candle Keys]]

    After defeating Dripear: :{{text|say|Skitter|Quick! Grab key, take Crown!}}

    ===Retrieve the Candle Crown=== :Search the Candle King's treasures for the Candle Crown. *Retrieve the Candle Crown

    Find the [[Candle Crown]] in one of the chests of [[Discarded Loot]]:

    :{{text|say|Skitter|Yes! Crown symbol of hope! Candle King no take it from us!}} :{{text|say|Skitter|Now we escape! Let's go!}}

    In the Discarded Loot chests nearby, you will find [[Well Worn Doll]], [[Crusty Chalice]], [[Royal Nailpicker]], [[Cloak of Beards]], [[Chocolate Coins]], [[Backup Candles]], [[Necklace of Kobold Teeth]] and [[CANDLE KING DIARY]].

    ===Escape the Lost Mines=== :Speak with Skitter to tunnel out before the Candle King returns! *Speak with Skitter to exit the Darkflame Cleft

    Talk to Skitter to leave: {{Dialogue|We have it! Run!

    {{Gossip|quest=|Let's get out of here!}} }}

    Completing this quest is the first criterion of [[The Ringing Deeps (achievement)]].
