    ==Description== The final leg of our journey took us west, to a large hozen village. We set up a small camp in the mountains, outside of their line of sight...

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|Depending on your class and specialization you will get one of the following items: |Moonwater Ring |Serpentspine Band |Lurking Tiger Band |Ring of Restoration
    |Blossoming Ring}} You will also receive: {{cost|39|20}} 166000 XP

    ==Progress== Please, allow me to finish.

    ==Completion== So there you have it. We didn't find the White Pawn, but we came back with intel that could be of even greater importance to the Alliance.

    The Horde have allied with the hozen, and are arming them as we speak. They and their monkey army now outnumber us thirty to one.

    ==Notes== Should you have to exit the quest for some reason, you can return to it by talking to Amber.

    ''{{gossip|Let me hear the rest of your report, Agent Kearnen.}}''

    Amber starts out at the [[Scenic Overlook]]: :{{text|bossemote|We made camp in the mountains. Little Lu watched over Rell.}} :{{text|say|Rell Nightwind|Kill some hozen today, Amber.}} :{{text|say|Amber Kearnen|As you wish, commander.}} :{{text|bossemote|I walked up to the top of the mountain and took a hold of my [[Sniper Rifle|sniper rifle]].}}

    Run up the hill and interact with the sniper blunderbuss. [[Grookin Hill]] zooms into view: :{{text|bossemote|I found Sully waiting outside the village's entrance.}} :{{text|bossemote|Straining my eyes, I could just barely read his lips, as he said:}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Shoot all the guards, so I can move ahead!}}

    Simply right-click on the guards to shoot them. Sully has plenty of health but he cannot afford to take a beating for very long. Clear one screen and Sully will move forward: :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Nice work back there! One more time!}}

    Clear another screen of mobs and Sully starts planting explosive crates. :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|I brought these monkeys some presents! Try not to blow me up, Amber!}} :{{text|bossemote|Sully planted some explosive barrels for me to shoot.}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|This one's for beatin' up Amber!}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|This one's for beatin' up Rell!}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|An' this one's for Gizmo and Socks, you bastards!!}}

    Shoot all three barrels. Should Amber blow one up before Sully gets out of range... :{{text|yell|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Ouch! Hey!}}

    Sully will do a dance, then move on. :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Nothing to see here! Let's keep moving!}}

    Clear the next screen. :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Bingo... I see a Horde crate! Cover me, I'm going out in the open!}}

    Sully is caught in the open as the hozen attack. Take them out! Once the wave is done, Sully moves forward again. :{{text|bossemote|Just as I suspected... the Horde is here, in Pandaria. And they've allied with the hozen.}} :{{text|bossemote|We'd need to bring this information back to Pearlfin Village... but not before shooting a few orcs in the head.}}

    More hozen keep streaming out. Focus on them until the wave stops. [[Hellscream's Vanguard (NPC)|Hellscream's Vanguard]] orcs take 3-4 shots to kill unless Amber hits them in the head... Clear the screen. :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|I see an opening! Here I come, Amber!}} :''Sully runs down to the [[Dookin' Grounds]], then off screen.''

    Speak to Amber again to continue. Karasshi and Admiral Taylor offer the next quests.
