    Jade Forest storyline

    thumb|Zone map

    The *Jade Forest* storyline covers all of the quests leading to and in the Jade Forest, starting the Mists of Pandaria campaign. Both factions start out in their capital cities of Stormwind and Orgrimmar before shipping out to Pandaria. An encounter with the opposite faction takes place and both factions crash land at opposite ends of the zone. After getting settled, adventurers push forward to Dawn's Blossom and start uncovering the mysteries of Pandaria.

    The progression of the storyline in this zone is tracked in the achievement Upjade Complete.

    After discovering the secrets of the Temple of the Jade Serpent and the Serpent's Heart, players are sent to the Valley of the Four Winds. Very early in the valley storyline (the third quest in!), players are given the opportunity to travel to Krasarang Wilds and complete the parallel wilds storyline instead of questing through the valley.

    White Pawn

    thumb|Dreamer's Pavilion* After *Amber Kearnen finishes her report, Bold Karasshi mentions a mysterious Lorewalker who may be able to help find the White Pawn. Lorewalker Cho is intensely curious of his new visitor and is quickly willing to help find Anduin Wrynn||Prince Anduin.

    After finding (though failing to acquire) the White Pawn and proving victorious over the Forest Hozen at the Slingtail Pits, Elder Lusshan passes along a note from Lorewalker Cho in In Search of Wisdom, which sends adventurers onward to Dawn's Blossom.

    Side quests

    There are several side quests in Jade Forest that either have no prerequistes or can be skipped without affecting the greater storyline.

    Nectarbreeze Orchard

    thumb|Nectarbreeze Orchard* In the southwest, roughly halfway between the *Serpent's Heart and Paw'don Village, is the Nectarbreeze Orchard. The farmers have been surprised by mogu raiders kidnapping pandaren to enslave them!

    Goldendraft and Wanderbrew

    thumb|Dawn's Blossom**** Lovers from two opposing families wish to ignore the years of hatred between their families and start a new life together.

    Tian Monastery

    thumb|Tian Monastery* North of the great bridge across from *Dawn's Blossom over the Forest Heart is the Tian Monastery.

    The Sprites' Plight

    thumb|Terrace of Ten Thunders* Up at *Emperor's Omen, players may run across a corpse of an Outcast Sprite. It offers The Sprites' Plight, a side-chain dealing with the mogu.

    Dailies

    There are two sets of max-level daily quests in Jade Forest. One set, dealing with the Order of the Cloud Serpent at the Arboretum, is available every day of the week. The other set, involving the Jade Temple Grounds, is not offered every day--only one of the four August Celestials temples will be questable each day.
