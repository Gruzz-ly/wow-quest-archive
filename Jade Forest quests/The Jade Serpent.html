    ==Description== You came to deliver a message to the Jade Serpent, right?

    <[[Fei|The little girl]] tilts her head up at you inquisitively.>

    I guess it's time you met her.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|Depending on your class and specialization you will get one of the following items: |Ambermist Hood |Blackthicket Helm |Jade Witch Helm |Glass Lake Headcover |Silkwood Cowl |Deepwoods Helm |Fox Grove Helm |Thunderwood Helm |Jade Tiger Helm}}

    You will also receive: [[Golden Pandaren Privacy Screen]] 250 [[reputation]] with [[The August Celestials]]

    ==Completion== Did you deliver your message?
