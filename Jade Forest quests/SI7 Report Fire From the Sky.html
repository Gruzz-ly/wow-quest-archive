    ==Description== Yeah, we found [[Amber Kearnen|Amber]], she's alive, and all that. That's about where the good news stops though.

    Let's see if I can pick up where ol' [[Rell Nightwind|Rell]] left off...

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *110000 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== I'm not done with the report yet, . Settle down.

    ==Completion== So, now you know why ol Rell's all beat up.

    ==Notes== Accept and this time follow in the footsteps of Sully, up at [[Serpent's Heart]], who has one ability: *{{abilities|Light Scope|range=90|Point at an enemy so that Amber can see it.|note=Channeled.|cd=2 sec}}

    Sully starts narrating: :{{text|bossemote|We were still goin' north when we stopped near this big ol' clearing.}} :{{text|say|Rell Nightwind|Alright, agents. Listen up.}} :{{text|say|Little Lu|Mmm... he considers me an agent.}} :{{text|say|Rell Nightwind|It grows late. We should set up camp for the night. Sully, do you have an axe?}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Naw, but I can put one together in a pinch. Let's see... you need a wood-cuttin' axe, or a people-killin' axe?}} :{{text|say|Rell Nightwind|Wood-cutting. Little Lu, use this axe to gather firewood.}} :{{text|say|Little Lu|Yes sir!}} :{{Text|say|Rell Nightwind|Sully. Investigate those shrines to the west. Look for signs of the White Pawn.}} :{{text|say|Rell Nightwind|Amber, find a good perch and give Sully some covering fire. We'll rendezvous back here.}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Aye-aye, sir!}} :{{text|say|Amber Kearnen|Yes, sir.}} :{{text|say|Amber Kearnen|This way, Sully! I see the perfect spot.}} :{{text|say|Amber Kearnen|I'll hide up here in the shadows.     Head northwest and cross the water to the central island. Serpent shrines are on the southeast, southwest and north sides of the island, just inland. On the way, [[Lurking Tiger]]s will come out of stealth. Shine the laser scope (bound to #1 by default) with the tiger targeted and Amber will snipe it. You can "point" the scope in any direction and run from the tigers, which will help minimise damage to Sully. Sully will react with any of the following: {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Boom! Nice one!}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Nice shot, Amber!}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Note to self: Don't get on Amber's bad side.}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Yuck... splatter.}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Tryin' ta sneak up on ol' Sully, huh?}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|I swear, that woman never misses.}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|She hits 'em between the eyes, every time.}} {{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Didn't know I had a sniper on my side, did ya?}}

    At each shrine, Sully will note the lack of the White Pawn: {{text|bossemote|I found nothin' at the northern shrine - no White Pawn, no hozen... nothin'.}} {{text|bossemote|I found no sign of the White Pawn at the southwestern shrine.}} :{{text|bossemote|However, I did find a friendly raccoon, who began to follow me.}} :{{text|bossemote|I decided to name him [[Gizmo (raccoon)|Gizmo]].}} :''Pointing at Gizmo:'' :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Hey there, little buddy! I'm gonna name you Gizmo.}} :{{text|say|Gizmo|Squeak!}} :''Suddenly, Gizmo gets sniped!'' :{{text|yell|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|NO! GIZMO!}} :{{text|yell|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|AMBER, WHY!? I DIDN'T EVEN SHINE MY DAMN LIGHT!}} :{{text|bossemote|Shortly afterward, another raccoon befriended me.}} :{{text|bossemote|I named him [[Socks]], and quickly moved on.}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Come on, Socks. Let's get out of here before you get sniped.}}

    After visiting all three: :{{text|bossemote|After scoutin' the three shrines, I headed back over to camp.}} :{{text|bossemote|Socks followed along, faithfully.}}

    Nearing camp: :{{text|yell|Rell Nightwind|AAAHH!!}} :{{text|yell|Rell Nightwind|Over here!}} :{{text|bossemote|I returned to find Rell bein' attacked by hozen! Rell...}} Speak to Sully to continue.

    ==Bugs== If Sully is killed, your main character will spawn in his place. You will have to run back to Pearlfin Village manually, but even then, a phasing bug will hide everyone but Admiral Taylor. If this happens, log out and back in. The village dwellers should appear back, and Sully among them, letting you redo the quest.
