    A People in Need of Healing

    {{Questbox | id = 72462 | name = A People in Need of Healing | image = A People in Need of Healing - meeting.jpg | imagecaption = Audience with Drek'Thar in the Hall of the Frostwolf | category = Heritage | faction = Horde | race = Orc | level = {{level|bfa heritage}} | start = [[Eitrigg]]{{Co|49.2|72.27|Orgrimmar}} | end = [[Aggra]]{{Co|54.37|78.6|Orgrimmar}} | previous = [[A Summon to Orgrimmar]] (optional) | next = [[The Kosh'harg]] }}

    '''A People in Need of Healing''' properly begins the [[Heritage of Draenor]] questline, after the optional [[A Summon to Orgrimmar]] breadcrumb. The quest mostly place in the single-player scenario '''The Future of Our People''' in [[Alterac Valley]].

    ==Objectives== Travel with [[Eitrigg]] to [[Alterac Valley]] to discuss the future of the [[orc]] people with [[Drek'Thar]]. Portal to Alterac Valley taken{{Co|51.17|78.94|Orgrimmar}} Complete Scenario "The Future of Our People"

    ==Description== It is strange, . Despite the years of peace, I am restless. Despite having the [[Horde]] and [[Ariok|my son]], I am lonely. You feel the same, don't you? I see it in your eyes, and in the eyes of many orcs.

    I feel perhaps that while we have found our way on [[Azeroth]], we have lost our way as orcs.

    Come with me. I wish to speak with an old friend about these troubles, and I would like you by my side.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: [[Money]] [[XP]]

    ==Completion== This was meant to be a quiet family visit with Drek'Thar, and perhaps a conversation with [[Durak]] about his future.

    Instead, we have set off on a path that will affect the orcish people as a whole!

    Do not misunderstand me. I think this is the best course of action. It is just surprising how quickly it happened. I am to be a [[Farseer]]...

    There is much for us to do. We had best get to it.

    ===Stage 3: Drek'Thar's Challenge=== [[File:A People in Need of Healing - Drek'Thar's Challenge.jpg|thumb|Drek'Thar preparing to empower his wolves.]] [[File:A People in Need of Healing - Duros and Drakan.jpg|thumb|The empowered Duros and Drakan.]] :Drek'Thar has issued you a challenge!     Talk to Drek'Thar: :Eitrigg has placed his faith in you. Will you prove yourself worthy of this honor?

    :{{Gossip|I accept your challenge!}}

    Duros and Drakan walk into the center of the room while Drek'Thar briefly rises from his chair to channel elemental magic in his hands. The other orcs back away to the edges of the room. :{{Text|say|Farseer Drek'Thar|Face the might of the Frostwolves, and the strength of the elements!}}

    He empowers Duros with a Boon of Storm and Flame and Drakan with a Boon of Frost and Earth, giving them elemental-themed abilities. Both wolves then attack the player. :{{Abilities|Boon of Storm and Flame|id=404074|Enhanced by the powers of storm and flame.|icon=spell_shaman_stormearthfire}} :{{Abilities|Boon of Frost and Earth|id=404078|Enhanced by the powers of frost and earth.|icon=spell_fire_bluehellfire}}

    Upon being brought to 30% health, the wolves turn friendly again and lie back down in their old spots. Defeat both to complete the stage.

    :{{Text|say|Farseer Drek'Thar|Duros... Drakan...
