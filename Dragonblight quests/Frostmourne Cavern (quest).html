    Frostmourne Cavern (quest)

    {{Questbox | name=Frostmourne Cavern | start=[[Zelig the Visionary]] | faction=Alliance | id=12478 | level={{level|Dragonblight}} | category=Dragonblight | reputation=+350 [[Valiance Expedition]] | rewards=[[Wrap of Vigorous Destruction]] or [[Belt of Vengeful Purification]] or [[Links of Righteous Persecution]] or [[Girdle of Forceful Annihilation]] | experience=25650 | previous=[[What Secrets Men Hide]] }}

    ==Objectives== Use Zelig's Scrying Orb at the bottom of [[Frostmourne Cavern]]. Unlock the secrets of the past! * Secrets of the Past Revealed * [[Zelig's Scrying Orb]] (provided)

    ==Description== They have labeled the cave as Frostmourne Cavern. Why the [[Scarlet Onslaught]] is interested by such things is unknown, but by the look of things, the area surrounding the cave is heavily guarded. Nonetheless, we must proceed!

    Take this scrying orb and travel north out of town and then northwest towards [[Scarlet Point]]. Make your way into the cave and battle your way to the bottom. Once there, use the orb. Return here with what you discover.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|Wrap of Vigorous Destruction|Belt of Vengeful Purification|Links of Righteous Persecution|Girdle of Forceful Annihilation}}

    ==Progress== Have you searched Frostmourne Cavern?

    ==Completion== [[Muradin]] lives? Could it be? I must notify [[Magni Bronzebeard|King Bronzebeard]] at once!

    I need to consult the orb. There must be more information - somewhere!

    ==Notes== As players step inside Frostmourne Cavern, they are buffed with "Haunting Memories: Surrounded by dark memories". Deep in the cavern, as [[Deathbringer Revenant]]s patrol about, is the [[Altar of Frost (Dragonblight)|Altar of Frost]]. Lying before it are three abandoned weapons: [[Light's Vengeance]], [[Arthas Menethil]]'s former weapon, to the left, and a one-handed axe and one-handed battlehammer to the right. The two one-handers appear to be of [[dwarf|dwarven]] make, presumably [[Muradin Bronzebeard]]'s. Use the orb to activate Zelig's Vision.
