    Steamtank Surprise

    {{Questbox | name=Steamtank Surprise | image = Alliance Steam Tank.jpg | imagecaption = [[Alliance Steam Tank]] | start=[[Duke August Foehammer]] | end=[[Ambo Cash]] | faction=Alliance | id=12326 | level={{level|Dragonblight}} | category=Dragonblight | reputation=+350 [[Valiance Expedition]] | rewards=[[Ritual Neckguard]] or [[Saboteur's Wrap]] or [[Siegemaster's Torch Ring]] | experience=25650 | previous=[[Into Hostile Territory]] | next=[[Scattered To The Wind]], [[Breaking Off A Piece]] }}

    ==Objectives== Use an [[Alliance Steam Tank]] to destroy 6 Plague Wagons and deliver the 7th Legion Elites to [[Wintergarde Mausoleum]]. Speak with [[Ambo Cash]] inside the Wintergarde Mausoleum in [[Dragonblight]] should you succeed. * [[Plague Wagon]] Sabotaged (6) * [[7th Legion Elite]] Safely Dropped Off

    ==Description== This is a seek and destroy mission. You'll be driving a tank into the [[Carrion Fields]] to dismantle [[Scourge]] plague wagons. With the tank comes a crew of three 7th Legion soldiers. All must survive if this mission is to succeed!

    You'll need to get in close to a plague wagon to unleash your engineer. Let them do their deed and move on to the next one. When the field is clear of plague wagons, head to the mausoleum in front of the ruined keep and drop off the rest of your crew. Meet with Ambo Cash inside.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards|Ritual Neckguard|Saboteur's Wrap|Siegemaster's Torch Ring}}

    ==Progress== Name's Cash. Ambo Cash. And the pleasure is all yours.

    ==Completion== Why I'm not wearing any pants isn't important. Let's just say I took some Scourge shrapnel in the wrong place and leave it at that, ok?

    So, you wiped out those plague wagons up in the fields and got my soldiers here in one piece. Impressive. You're one of the first to make it this far, friend. Too bad you gotta go right back out into the Carrion Fields. Yep, we've had a bit of a setback.
