    Objectives

    Speak to Durak in Splintertree Mines. Durak Persuaded Durak slain

    Description

    I sense that Durak is more than he seems. Demonic power is dangerous and it is easy to fall to its corruption - even for a trained warlock.

    I do not trust him, .

    Find this Durak and tell him that the Horde will have nothing to do with him.

    Rewards

    {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Durak's Downfall |Ring of Pardons |Bow of Ire }}

    You will also receive: 2,300 XP*

    Progress

    Carry on.

    Completion

    As I thought, he was a threat after all. Well done, .

    Notes

    After accepting the quest, return to Durak at Splintertree. He is still friendly, but now has a Felhunter Minion with him. Speak with Durak: : :: :: :: ::

    Kill him.

    In original version of the quest, looting Durak's Head was an objective.
