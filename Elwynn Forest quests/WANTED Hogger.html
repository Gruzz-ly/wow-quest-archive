    '''WANTED: "Hogger"''' begins when a [[Wanted Poster]] is read just outside of [[Westbrook Garrison]]. As Hogger is an [[elite]] monster, defeating him may require the help of another player.

    ==Objectives== Deal with "[[Hogger|The Hogger Situation]]" and return to [[Marshal Dughan]]. * Dealt with "The Hogger Situation"

    ==Description== Wanted: [[Hogger]]

    A huge [[gnoll]], Hogger, is prowling the woods in southwestern [[Elwynn Forest|Elwynn]]. He has overpowered all attempts at his capture.

    The [[Stormwind Army]] has placed a generous bounty on the Gnoll. To earn the reward, bounty hunters should venture into the woods and deal with the beast.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Footman Tunic |Stormwind Guard Leggings |Balanced Spell Staff |Lion Guard Chainmail }}

    You will also receive: * 450 - 1,200 [[XP]] * +350 [[reputation]] with [[Stormwind (faction)|Stormwind]]

    == Progress == Yes, Hogger has been a real pain for me and my men.  You have something to report about the beast?

    ==Completion== He did WHAT?

    Great, now we have a bloodthirsty beast right beneath the streets of Stormwind, with a whole army of gnolls at his command.  Definitely an improvement, I feel safer already.

    Here's your reward, .  But promise me you'll check in with the [[Stockades]] guards in Stormwind at some point, just to ensure that things are going smoothly.

    ==Notes== [[Hogger]] is located on [[Hogger Hill]] at the southern end of [[Forest's Edge]], past several camps of [[Riverpaw pack|Riverpaw gnolls]].
