    Description

    In this, I trust Vanessa's word. I do not believe she knows what the Drakefire Amulet is. Some of the story about Onyxia was made public, but not all.

    Perhaps even you do not know.

    I will explain everything, for both of you.

    :Hmm, I suppose we have not met.

    :I am Vanessa VanCleef, daughter of Edwin VanCleef, who was once head of the Stonemason's Guild.

    :When the House of Nobles betrayed the guild and King Varian Wrynn exiled us, my father created the Defias Brotherhood to get justice.

    :Stormwind ordered him killed. It happened right in front of me. When I was old enough, I took up his mantle. Back then, I stood by his every action, hand on heart.

    :I followed his footsteps. And I realize now... I made the same mistakes. By confusing revenge and justice... I hurt the people of Westfall. People who have no one else to stand up for them.

    :Things are different, now.
