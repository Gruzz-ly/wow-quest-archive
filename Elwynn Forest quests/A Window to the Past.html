    ==Objectives== Speak with [[Mathias Shaw|Shaw]] under the [[Lion's Pride Inn]] in [[Goldshire]] to hear about the history of the [[:Drakefire Amulet]]. *Speak with Shaw

    ==Description== In this, I trust [[Vanessa VanCleef|Vanessa]]'s word. I do not believe she knows what the Drakefire Amulet is. Some of the story about [[Onyxia]] was made public, but not all.

    Perhaps even you do not know.

    I will explain everything, for both of you.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|11|70}} * 3,400 [[XP]]

    ==Progress==

    ==Completion== We all have our own versions of the past, don't we?

    I don't care if some [[dragon]] lady had a magic amulet. It wasn't complete mind control.

    The [[Nobles of Stormwind|nobles]] already hated the common people. The [[Stonemason's Guild]] would never have needed to turn to [[bandit]]ry and become the [[Defias Brotherhood]], if not for them.

    ::It was chaos at the time, but we now know how Onyxia, as Lady Prestor, worked behind the scenes. She convinced the House that the Stonemason's work was poor, and they were being ripped off.
