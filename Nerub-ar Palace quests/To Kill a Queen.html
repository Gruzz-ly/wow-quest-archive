    '''To Kill a Queen''' concludes the [[Nerub-ar Palace]] storyline. To begin this quest chain, find [[Webster]] near the portal to [[Azj-Kahet]] in [[Dornogal]] after completing the whole [[The War Within (achievement)]] campaign on the first character, or just the first chapter on alts.

Her rule has been marked with a tightening grasp of the populace and an uneasy alliance with Xal'atath. She grows increasingly paranoid and sequestered herself in Nerub-ar Palace.}} Kill Queen Ansurek. * [[Queen Ansurek (tactics)|Queen Ansurek]] slain * Enter Nerub-ar Palace in [[Story Mode]]Â (Optional) {{co|31.5|64.0|City of Threads}}

    ==Description== Tightening her grip on the citizens is something I expected of the Queen but forcefully ascending the population of the [[Burrows]] is too far.

    Queen Ansurek has fallen far greater than I ever expected. I can't sit idly by and watch her lead our people to ruin.

    The time is now. We must kill Queen Ansurek and lead the people to a brighter future. Otherwise who knows how far she will fall.
