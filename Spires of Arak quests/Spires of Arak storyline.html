    Spires of Arak storyline

    {{shortcut|soaquest}} [[File:VZ-Spires of Arak.jpg|thumb|Zone map]]

    The '''Spires of Arak storyline''' is the level-up experience in the [[Spires of Arak]]. Players are led here from their faction outposts in [[Talador]]. The [[Adherents of Rukhmar]] are waging a war of extermination of the cursed [[arakkoa]], and one of the first things that players see is the [[Skyreach]] [[Breath of Rukhmar|superweapon]] obliterating [[Veil Akraz]]!

    The storyline tracking achievement is called [[Between Arak and a Hard Place]] and has nine chapters. The chapters are somewhat interleaved, as the zone storyline does not play out linearly.

    Eventually, the player will receive the quest [[News from Nagrand]], which leads them to {{Au|Nagrand}}.

    ==Main storyline== ===Shadows Gather=== [[File:The Shadows of Skettis.jpg|thumb|The Skyreach beam weapon obliterating Veil Akraz]]

    {{Spires of Arak 1}}

    The storyline continues with the "Securing Southport" / "Establishing Axefall" chapter with [[One of Our Own]].

    ===Securing Southport / Establishing Axefall=== [[File:Inspecting the Troops - Alliance.jpg|thumb|Southport]] [[File:The Ebon Hunter finale Alliance.jpg|thumb|Mining out Veil Zekk]]

    After getting to Veil Terokk, a member of the adventurer's team finds himself captured. Release him and choose an outpost to build at town.

    {{Spires of Arak 2}}

    After completing [[Orders, Commander?]], several chains open up. Percy will resume the zone storyline with [[Return to Veil Terokk]]; [[Jasper Fel]] or [[Shadow Hunter Ukambe]] want to check out Admiral Taylor's Garrison in [[Old Friends (Spires of Arak)]] / [[What's Theirs is Ours]]; and a short chain will be offered depending on which building was chosen.

    After finishing the building quest chain, the breadcrumb to [[Pinchwhistle Gearworks]] opens up in the eponymous quest, [[Pinchwhistle Gearworks (quest)]].

    ===Secrets of the Talonpriests=== [[File:Ishaal's Hut.jpg|thumb|The Shattered Hand care not what's happening in arakkoa culture]] [[File:Veil Akraz.jpg|thumb|...like Veil Akraz getting destroyed by the Skyreach beam weapon]]

    After choosing the inn or the trading post at town, [[Percy]] pops up with [[Return to Veil Terokk]].

    {{Spires of Arak 3}}

    The storyline continues with the "Lessons of the Apexis" chapter with [[Power Unearthed]].

    ===Legacy of the Apexis=== [[File:Hot Seat combat.jpg|thumb|The Hot Seat at Apexis Excavation]]

    After coming [[Back from Beyond]], [[Darkscryer Raastok]] offers a breadcrumb to the [[Apexis Excavation]].

    {{Control is King}}

    The storyline continues with [[Rites of the Talonpriests]].

    ===The Gods of Arak=== [[File:Sethe.jpg|thumb|Sethe resurrected]]

    The maddened, cursed, followers of Sethe are attempting to resurrect him, but Anzu stands in their way.

    {{Talon Watch}}

    ===Terokk's Legend=== [[File:Rites of the Talonpriests.jpg|thumb|Preparing to summon Terokk]]

    The Talonpriests are attempting to summon Terokk so they can use his power against the Adherents.

    {{Spires of Arak 4}}

    ===When the Raven Swallows the Day=== [[File:When All Is Aligned.jpg|thumb|When All is Aligned]]

    After completing the "The Gods of Arak" chapter, [[Anzu (alternate universe)|Anzu]] begins the "When the Raven Swallows the Day" chapter with [[Cult of the Ravenspeakers]]. The [[Ravenspeakers]] are an addled sect of the cursed arakkoa, but the deity they worship, [[Ka'alu]], the consort of [[Anzu (alternate universe)|Anzu]], actually exists.

    {{Spires of Arak 5}}

    ===Admiral Taylor's Garrison=== [[File:Old Friends - Spires of Arak.jpg|thumb|Something is wrong at Admiral Taylor's Garrison]]

    After choosing a building in [[Orders, Commander?]], [[Jasper Fel]] or [[Shadow Hunter Ukambe]] want to check out [[Admiral Taylor's Garrison]] in [[Old Friends (Spires of Arak)]] / [[What's Theirs is Ours]]. Something has gone horribly wrong.

    {{Taylor's Garrison}}

    ===Pinchwhistle Gearworks=== [[File:The Mother Lode.jpg|thumb|A zeppelin has crashed and the survivors say something about a fungus]] [[File:Flame On - town blown up.jpg|thumb|Drastic measures to stop what's happening at Pinchwhistle Point]]

    A mysterious fungus has sprung up and is causing all kinds of problems at the goblin towns at the southern tip of Spires of Arak. Completing this part of the storyline is necessary to obtain [[Salvage Yard, Level 1]] building blueprint.

    {{Pinchwhistle Gearworks}}

    ===Bonus Objectives=== There are four bonus objectives in Spires of Arak. Completing them all awards the achievement [[Arak Star]]. * [[The Howling Crag (bonus objective)]] * [[The Writhing Mire (bonus objective)]] * [[Bloodmane Pridelands (bonus objective)]] * [[Bladefist Hold (bonus objective)]]

    ===Talonpriest Ishaal=== After completing the storyline in its entirety and obtaining [[Between Arak and a Hard Place]] achievement, speak to {{Au|Talonpriest Ishaal}} to recruit him as a follower. : [[Talonpriest Ishaal (quest)]]
