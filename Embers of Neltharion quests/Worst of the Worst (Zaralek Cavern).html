    '''Worst of the Worst''' concludes the fourth chapter of the [[Embers of Neltharion campaign]], "Inherited Sin", alongside [[Break Them Out (Zaralek Cavern)]]. The campaign continues with [[Fading Embers]].

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Overseer Xymra.jpg|[[Overseer Xymra]]|As ruthless as she is heartless, Xymra's top priority is recapturing all of the slitherdrakes.}} Find and eliminate [[drakonid]] leading the [[poacher]]s. * [[Overseer Xymra]] slain {{co|57.0|30.3|Zaralek Cavern}}

    ==Description== Everything here suggests this is a well-coordinated effort. These [[slitherdrake]]s are almost like wild beasts. They will not stand a chance without our aid.

    Find whoever is leading their operation and put an end to this.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|56|18}} * 11,900 [[XP]] * [[Regurgitated Half-Digested Fish]] * [[Winding Slitherdrake]] * 10x [[Flightstones]] * +130 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]
