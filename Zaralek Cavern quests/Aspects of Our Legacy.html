    Aspects of Our Legacy concludes the second chapter of the Embers of Neltharion campaign, "Sundered Legacy". The next chapter, "The Ancient Bargain", begins immediately after with Where There's Smoke, There's Fire.

    Objectives

    Meet Ebyssian in Loamm. * Meet Ebyssian in Loamm

    Description

    I am proud to see that Emberthal is blossoming into a confident leader once more. Her people need her.

    But that Sarkareth found Oathbinder is a reminder of the horrors of Neltharion's legacy and the folly of following him.

    I have a feeling that we will discover more than his trinkets and whispers here in these caverns.

    Come. We have much to discuss. Let us go back to Loamm and see if my brothers have found a way to subdue Fyrakk.

    Rewards

    You will receive one of: Endowed Breeches* Inherited Leggings Bequeathed Tassets *Bestowed Greaves

    You will also receive: * Shiny Token of Gratitude * 28g 9s * 10x Flightstones * 9,500 XP * +50 reputation with Obsidian Warders * +50 reputation with Dark Talons

    Completion

    I am... startled by Ebyssian's censure.

    Any objects of power Neltharion was hoarding would have been a boon to us.

    But he is right... if I want to be an Aspect, I need to show everyone what power the true heir to the black dragonflight holds.

    I will start with Fyrakk.
