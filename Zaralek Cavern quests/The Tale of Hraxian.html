    '''The Tale of Hraxian''' is offered by [[Veritistrasz]] at [[Dragonscale Camp]] the next daily reset after completing [[Stain Removal]], the capstone of the "Medicinal Fungi" optional storyline of the [[Embers of Neltharion campaign]]. Players must have also completed the full "Verbal Archaeology" questline from the [[Zskera Vaults]] in the [[Forbidden Reach storyline]], concluding with [[A Dusty Vase]], for Veritistrasz to offer this quest.

    ==Objectives== Listen to the tale of [[Hraxian]]. * Listen to the tale of Hraxian

    ==Description== Hello , it is good to see you.

    Would you like to join us around the fire? I think it is time we heard a positive story about the [[Black dragonflight|obsidian flight]] of old.

    I once promised yourself and [[Voraxian]] I would tell you the story from the vase you found in the [[Zskera Vaults]]. I believe it is that time.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * [[Dragon Isles Supply Cache]] (30x [[Dragon Isles Supplies]]) * 9,500 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== It is always a pleasure, .

    ==Completion==

    Thank you for listening, . I have forgotten much over my life, but not this.

    I hope Hraxian's story gives the obsidian flight hope after the discoveries we made here.
