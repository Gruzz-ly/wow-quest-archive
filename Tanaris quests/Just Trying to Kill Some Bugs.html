    Just Trying to Kill Some Bugs

    {{questbox |name=Just Trying to Kill Some Bugs |faction=Neutral |start=Zeke Bootscuff | level= |category=Tanaris |id=24953 |experience=7550 |rewards=Pendant of the Gaping Chasm or Mostly-Amazing Gloves or Bootscuff Boots or Bootlegger Legplates |money= (+ at max level) |shareable=Yes |previous=A Great Idea |next=Land's End }}

    Objectives

    Talk to Narain Soothfancy to pilot the Mind-Controlled Swarmer into the Gaping Chasm hive.

    Description

    Now it's time for my plan to go into full effect. We've got the captured bug strapped up with as many explosives as it can carry, and with Soothfancy's mostly amazing mental powers, we're gonna drive that sucker straight down into the center of the underground hive.

    And then...BOOM! No more bugs!

    You get the personal honor of directin' the ugly thing. Go talk to Narain and get set up with your bug-pal.

    Rewards

    You will receive: (+ at max level) +350 Reputation with Ratchet, Gadgetzan, Booty Bay and Everlook

    Progress

    I didn't hear no cave-ins, pal! Try again!

    Completion

    Oh man, that was awesome! You should've seen the flames coming out of the hive!

    Here's your reward, for entertaining me as much as for clearing the hive!

    Notes

    Talking to Narain Soothfancy offers: : The Hazzali Swarmer captured in the previous quest becomes a remote-controlled vehicle strapped with explosives at this point, with two abilities: * * The other Hazzali silithid will be friendly to the Swarmer, so this is a no-risk finale to the storyline, simply requiring getting to the right place in the hive and then detonating. The trail sensed by the ability shows as bright, hazy orange lines between orange clouds of pheremones; reaching the hive entrance (towards the southeast of the Gaping Chasm) by a different path is also fine.

    In the first large circular chamber reached underground (noticeably filled with bugs), the Swarmer must climb on top of the "pedastal" in the center of the room to detonate, completing the quest.

    Progression

    Next, Narain Soothfancy offers an alliance-only breadcrumb to Land's End Beach: Land's End

    Completing this quest fulfils the "Bug Free" portion of the Tanaris Quests achievement.
