    |start=Izaik Kadarov |experience=Scales |rewards= |repeatable=Yes |category=Seasonal }} The Originals is one of two world boss weekly quests offered at the Bronze Celebration. Each week alternates between Timely Gate Crashers and this one. Unlike the other quest, the required bosses are found in locations across both Kalimdor and the Eastern Kingdoms.

    Objectives

    Defeat Lord Kazzak in Blasted Lands, Azuregos in Azshara, and the Dragon of Nightmare at one of the portals to the Emerald Dream. Lord Kazzak* slain Azuregos slain Dragon of Nightmare slain

    Description

    Long ago, great terrors walked our lands, and drove even the smallest to flights of heroism.

    I speak, of course, about Lord Kazzak, Azuregos, and the Dragons of Nightmare.

    Legends have it that so sought after were their treasures, that armies would camp overnight to hunt them down.

    The dragons have felt disturbances in the timeways, coincidentally located where these giants once roamed.

    They have tasked me with finding champions to investigate.

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 16,450 XP 100 Timewarped Badge

    Progress

    So were they just memories?

    Completion

    Oh the memories. I myself ventured out to tackle these foes of the past. These battles taught me much. Take what you have learned in defeating these foes and use it to prepare you for whatever might step into your path next.

    Notes

    All bosses have very short respawn timers, unlike their classic versions. Lord Kazzak and Azuregos will spawn close to where they originally did: Lord Kazzak* spawns at the north end of the Tainted Scar in the Blasted Lands. Azuregos spawns southeast of the Secret Lab in Azshara*.

    Rather than randomly spawning at any of the four portals, the Dragons of Nightmare will spawn at fixed locations, cycling between each dragon on a daily basis:

    Ysondre spawns at Dream Bough in Feralas

    Lethon spawns at Seradane in the Hinterlands

    Emeriss spawns at Twilight Grove in Duskwood

    Taerar spawns at Bough Shadow in Ashenvale

    Each world boss is marked clearly on world and zone maps, including which Nightmare Dragon is currently active, if it has not yet been killed for the week.
