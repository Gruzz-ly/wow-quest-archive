    A Surprising Investigation concludes the fourth chapter of the Guest Relations storyline, first made available on 21 November. This awards the I Saved the Party and All I Got Were These Lousy Hats achievement, a criterion of Azeroth's Greatest Detective.

    Objectives

    Solve the case given to you by Detective Jones. * Ask Maru for a hint (Optional) * Impostors identified (5) * Return to Detective Jones * Letters distributed (10) * Return to Detective Jones * "Lucky Feast" obtained * Northern beach found...? *Talk to Alyx at Wavestrider Beach.

    Description

    Well, look who's back to solve more cases.

    It seems that Chromie has been fiddling with time again and some imposters have popped up around the celebration area.

    All you need to do is identify who doesn't belong and send them to Chromie. This should be a breeze for you.
