    '''The "Great" Detective''' concludes the second day of [[Guest Relations]]. On 31 October, the storyline continues with [[Tracking Quest]].

    ==Objectives== Solve the mystery of [[Herrkrop]]'s disappearance. * Ask [[Maru]] for a hint (Optional) * Mystery solved (0/100%)

    ==Description== The missing guest? Yes, some Herrkrop fellow. Not to worry, I've already solved the case.

    He probably just got lost. Relax, . Enjoy the festivities! Our missing guest will show up soon enough.

    If you don't believe me, go... I don't know... solve it yourself. Interrogate someone or other.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{Cost|23|40}} * 5x [[Timewarped Badge]]

    ==Progress== Have you any luck in finding our missing guest?

    ==Completion== So that's what happened?

    <[[Alyx]] sighs.>

    Thank you for your help. I will work on finding a lead to track down [[Scrapes]].

    Check back in tomorrow, if you would.

    At this point, you can ask [[Maru]] for another hint: {{dialogue|Last seen at a beach, huh? Wavestrider Beach is not too far from here.

    Maybe your torch can reveal da clues!}}

    [[File:Abandoned Beach Chair.png|thumb|[[Abandoned Beach Chair]]]] At this point, players should be at 48% progress. If players head south out of the celebration area, they may come across an [[Abandoned Beach Chair]] at {{co|66.0|59.5|Tanaris}} on [[Southbreak Shore]]. Attached is a note:

    {{Dialogue|

    Sun's better on the other side.
    - [[Herrkrop]]}}

    [[File:The Great Detective - Nat and the sand.jpg|thumb|Nat Pagle and some suspicious sand]] Head north out of the celebration area to [[Wavestrider Beach]] to find [[Nat Pagle]] at {{co|65.9|40.4|Tanaris}} doing his thing, fishing. Nat doesn't have anything relevant to say, but the Idol keeps pointing at the sand pile that can be revealed as a [[Buried Object (Tanaris)|Buried Object]] next to him, so break out the [[Torch of Pyrreth]] to reveal a [[Desert Crawler]] crab from the sand pile, adding 12% progress. It skitters off to the east into the water. Other objects may be burned away to reveal [[Discarded Trash]] that can be kicked great lengths, for no other benefit that the joy inherent in the action.

    [[File:The Great Detective - Scrapes, Herrkrop, and Athelton.jpg|thumb|Athelton, Herrkrop, and Scrapes]] This changes the Idol's target, so head over to [[Scrapes]]. If the torch is still out, it will burn away the sand pile behind him, revealing the corpse of [[Herrkrop]] and adding another 12% progress!

    Revealing the corpse causes Athelton Jones to suddenly ride up in a [[Rowboat (Anniversary)|Rowboat]] and hop out onto the beach. :{{text|say|Athelton Jones|Just as I suspected, our missing persons case is now... a murder investigation!}} :{{text|say|Athelton Jones|As I say, always trust your first guess!
