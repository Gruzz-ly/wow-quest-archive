    Lost and... Found? is the eighth hidden local story of Guest Relations, started on 18 November and required for Assistant to the Assistant Guest Relations Manager.

    Objectives

    Deliver the lost items to their correct owners. * Collect items from the Lost & Found chest * Ask Maru for a hint (Optional) * Well-Worn Almanac returned * Tiny Mechanical Mouse (Anniversary) returned * Crude Ivory Locket returned * Potion Bottle returned * Jade Amulet returned * Alyx's Lost & Found Notes (provided)

    Description

    I am so overwhelmed. A group of folks were here a little while ago, all complai... I mean, submitting lost-item notices with great passion, but all at the same time.

    I tried to take down all the details, but in the stress of the moment, I fear they're all jumbled.

    I have this collection of items that have been found over the day, and I'm fairly sure these are the items those folks were talking about. Do you think you could use my notes and figure out which item belongs to each person?

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 23g 40s * 5x Timewarped Badge

    Progress

    How's it going? Have you found the owners of all the lost items?

    Completion

    Have you finished with those items? Oh, bless your heart! I'm actually quite surprised at how few people have lost things during these festivities. Still, I'm simply bursting with gratitude at your willingness to help those unfortunate few who have.

    Notes

    All NPCs for this quest are in the celebration area. Don't head across the world to find them at their usual locations!
