    {{WorldEvent/Anniversary|type=quest|doc=}} {{Questbox | id = 84296 | name = The Trail's Gone Cold | displaycat = Secrets of Azeroth | category = Guest Relations | level = {{level|holiday 30}} | start = [[Rexxar]] {{Co|77.31|46.18|Borean Tundra}} | end = [[Alyx]] {{Co|63.02|50.25|Tanaris}} | money = {{Cost|23|40}} | rewards = 5x [[Timewarped Badge]] | shareable = Yes | previous = [[Tracking Quest]] | next = [[Warn the Detective]] }}

    '''The Trail's Gone Cold''' concludes day three of the [[Guest Relations]] storyline. On 1 November, the storyline continued with [[Warn the Detective]].

    ==Objectives== Find [[Scrapes]]' hideout. Ask [[Maru]] for a hint (Optional) Mystery solved (0/100%) Return to [[Alyx]] outside the [[Caverns of Time]]. [[Spirit's Whistle (Anniversary)]] (provided)

    ==Description== I've tracked the scent of Scrapes as far as here, but this cold air smothers our lead. I found a couple scraps of notes. Nothing useful yet, but he seems to have left a paper trail in his hurry.

    Look for clues that may indicate where our villain's lair is hidden. I will mark what I found on your map.

    Take [[Spirit (hawk)|Spirit]]'s whistle. Call upon us when you are in need of assistance.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{Cost|23|40}} * 5x [[Timewarped Badge]]

    ==Progress== How goes the hunt?

    ==Completion== A shapeshifter? Oh, this is very bad.

    We should warn the detective. I've not seen him recently, though.

    Come back tomorrow. I am sure he will be back by then.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{Text|say|Rexxar|I have marked your map where the trail went cold. Start there and call upon us when you have a lead.}}

    ===Borean Tundra=== Rexxar has gossip, and Maru has a new clue: {{Dialogue|title=Rexxar|Prepare yourself for the hunt.}}

    If you blow [[Spirit's Whistle]] while next to Rexxar: :{{Text|yell|Rexxar|You don't need to blow so hard. I'm right here!}}

    {{Dialogue|title=Maru| {{Gossip|{{Text|red|(Hint)}} }}

    Look around the X on your map using your torch and idol. You're sure to find a clue!}}

    Open the world map and head to the X, Marked Trail - The last known location of Scrapes at {{co|86.0|34.0|Borean Tundra}} in [[Transborea]]. At the lake are [[Distracting Wisp]]s, so ignore the [[Idol of Ohn'ahra]] near them. Approach the shed near the lake and melt the snow pile on the outside with the [[Torch of Pyrreth]] to reveal a [[Torn Note (Borean Tundra)|Torn Note]] to loot [[Torn Diary, Page 7]]. Another Torn Note is inside the shed, containing [[Torn Diary, Page 4]].

    The idol is useless with the wisps nearby, so summon Rexxar: {{Dialogue|The wind blows strongly to the east here. We must continue the hunt without Spirit for now.}}

    Get away from the shed and the wisps and the idol will lock on a location to the east, where the last Torn Note is at {{Co|88.11|34.60|Borean Tundra}} in some brush, which contains [[Torn Diary, Page 6]].

    The relevant parts of the notes read, in order: {{dialogue|...dreading the return to the humidity, but I have to go somewhere safe.

    ...at least the crystals are pretty up on that nearby plateau...

    ...and purple IS my favorite color... }}

    Hmm. Maybe this guy went somewhere north to somewhere humid? If you find tat place, give Rexxar a whistle! }}

    Before leaving, note that the Distracting Wisps are in the shape of {{wp|Crater (constellation)|the constellation Crater}} and some curious junk can be fished from the open water under the wisps. This is part of the secret to obtain {{item|Keys to Incognitro, the Indecipherable Felcycle|Incognitro, the Indecipherable Felcycle}}. More information is on the Incognitro article, or in [[Guest Relations#Incognitro, the Indecipherable Felcycle|the Incognitro section of the Guest Relations article]].

    ===Sholazar Basin=== Nearby plateaus with crystals on them are in [[Sholazar Basin]], so head northwest into the jungle. Calling Rexxar in Sholozar Basin will mark two locations on the map:

    [[Nesingwary Base Camp]]: Potential Witnesses - Someone here may have information on Scrapes.

    [[Glimmering Pillar]]: Fresh Marks - Scrapes may have come this way.

    {{dialogue|Spirit flies above. I will mark on your map what I see through his eyes.}}

    Maru also offers advice: {{Dialogue| {{Gossip|{{Text|red|(Hint)}} }}

    Maybe someone at Nesingwary's camp seen him...? Ask around! Ask around lotsa times! }}

    At Nesingwary Base Camp, the innkeeper [[Purser Boulian]] has some information about Scrapes: {{dialogue|{{gossip|Have you seen any undead come through this way?}}

    Actually, yeah. He wandered through here complaining about the heat. Said he was headed north.}}

    The plateau with purple crystals specifically is the [[Glimmering Pillar]] at {{co|50|38|Sholazar Basin}} north of the central lake. Fly to the top of the Glimmering Pillar and bring the [[Idol of Ohn'ahra]] back online. It points to a [[Mounted Spyglass]] at {{co|49.7|36.0|Sholazar Basin}} aimed to the north-northeast.
