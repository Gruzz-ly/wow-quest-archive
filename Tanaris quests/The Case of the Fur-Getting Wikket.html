    The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket

    {{WorldEvent/Anniversary|type=quest|doc=}} {{Questbox | id = 84595 | name = The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket | displaycat = Secrets of Azeroth | category = Guest Relations | level = {{level|holiday 30}} | start = [[Riko]] {{co|61.89|50.48|Tanaris}} | money = {{cost|23|40}} | rewards = 5x [[Timewarped Badge]]
    [[Gizmo the Pure]] | previous = [[A Hozen On My Back]] }}

    '''The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket''' concludes the 8 November step of [[Assistant to the Assistant Guest Relations Manager]], part of [[Guest Relations]]. Five minutes after completing this quest, players will receive mail from [[Riko]] with [[Gizmo the Pure]] attached.

    ==Objectives== Find a companion pet for [[Riko]] and [[Shademaster Kiryn|Kiryn]]. Mystery solved (0/100%) Ask Maru for a hint (Optional) *[[My Journal: The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket]] (provided)

    ==Description== Riko has had enough of these blikkety tik-digglers! All Kiryn wants is to be a furry-haver, and these mankers won't give any!

    Kiryn says Riko needs to stop jabberings. Says taking their furries is "stealing."

    You a helpful wikket? Find Kiryn a furry friend! Better take notes so wikket don't get stuck.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|23|40}} 5x [[Timewarped Badge]] *[[Gizmo the Pure]] (via mail after five minutes)

    ==Progress== If wikket can't find a furry, Riko will have to be a stickypaw.

    ==Completion== You really horkin' did it! You da best, wikket!

    ==Notes== Riko hands over [[My Journal: The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket]]. It initially reads: {{Book|content= Riko wants me to find a pet for him and Kiryn. I should ask them both what kind of pet they want. }}

    Maru offers a hint: {{Dialogue| {{Gossip|{{Text|red|(Hint)}} }}

    Hmmm, have you tried asking around? Talk to Riko and Kiryn to figure out what pet they both want, and then look around for clues! Maybe da pet lady [[Breanni]] knows somefing. }}

    Speak with Riko and Kiryn. The journal updates after speaking with both.

    {{Dialogue|title=Riko| {{Gossip|What kind of pet do you want?}}

    Riko wants something cute, like those wikkin' bandit weasels that eat trash. }}

    {{Book|content= Riko wants something cute like a raccoon. I wonder if anyone around here has seen a raccoon. }}

    {{Dialogue|title=Kiryn| {{Gossip|What kind of pet do you want?}}

    I think something that doesn't need to be fed would be ideal. Living creatures have so many needs. }}

    {{Book|content= Kiryn wants something undead. }}

    [[File:The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket - Kieule and Breanni.jpg|thumb|[[Kieule]] and [[Breanni]]]] Riko and Kiryn's gossip each add 20% progress. [[Petra]] the pet greeter can be found just below the pair:

    {{Dialogue| {{Gossip|I'm looking for a pet.}}

    Breanni would be more than happy to help you find a good pet! }}

    Follow Maru and Petra's advice and speak with Breanni in the stall at {{co|62.35|49.92|Tanaris}} in the battle pet area:

    {{Dialogue| {{Gossip|I'm looking for a pet.}}

    I've got all kinds! What are you looking for?

    {{Gossip|Bird of Prey / Cat / Dog / Mechanical / Raptor / Serpent / Spider}} {{Dialogue-child| I think I have something like that. Check it out! }}

    {{Gossip|Dead / Raccoon}} {{Dialogue-child| Hmm... I know [[Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Sully McLeary]] used to have a couple of raccoons, but they passed away. }}}}

    [[File:The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket - Sully.jpg|thumb|[[Sully "The Pickle" McLeary]]]] Choosing either Dead or Racoon advances the quest. Head over to Sully "The Pickle" McLeary at {{Co|63.05|49.42|Tanaris}} near the Photo Booth. Before talking to him, rumage through the nearby [[Trash Pile]]:

    :{{Text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Gizmo used tae love rummagin' through trash piles just like that...}}

    Speak with Sully:

    {{Dialogue| {{Gossip|I'm looking for a pet.}}

    [[Socks|SOCKS]]! [[Gizmo (raccoon)|GIZMO]]! Ach, they were taken too soon.

    {{Gossip|Where are they now?}}

    It's been so long... I remember looking fer a place tae give 'em a proper burial.

    I was at the [[Serpent's Heart]] when I headed west on my [[gryphon]], 'cross a stone bridge and turned to follow a path. I dinnae remember what happened next, was just flyin' through the tears. But it can't have been more than ten paces east an' forty south.

    I remember the watchers and I held a torchlight vigil, overlooking a blossoming orchard. In the glow o' the light, we could feel their presence with us still. }}

    [[File:The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket - stone bridge at Serpent's Heart.jpg|thumb|The stone bridge west of [[Serpent's Heart]]]] This gets to 60% progress, and the journal gets an update: {{Book|content= Sully says he buried Gizmo and Socks near Serpent's Heart. He flew west across a stone bridge with his gryphon. Then he turned onto a path and flew no more than ten paces east and forty south. There he held a torchlight vigil with the watchers while overlooking a blossoming orchard. The glow of the light summoned their presence. }}

    As does Maru: {{Dialogue| We need to follow in Sully's footsteps! His directions seem to lead to da [[Serpent's Overlook]]. }}

    [[File:The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket - Serpent's Overlook graves.jpg|thumb|Grave markers at [[Serpent's Overlook]]]] Head to the [[Jade Forest]] via portals in [[Orgrimmar]] or [[Stormwind City]] and fly to [[Serpent's Heart]] near the center of the zone. The stone bridge Sully mentions is at {{co|42.5|60.2|Jade Forest}} over the [[Slicky Stream]]. Equip the [[Tricked-Out Thinking Cap]] to reset it to (0, 0) and head in the direction of (10, -40) or {{co|43.4|68.8|Jade Forest}} to reach [[Serpent's Overlook]]. Bring the [[Idol of Ohn'ahra]] online to find two grave markers at {{Co|43.40|68.69|Jade Forest}} near [[Sky Dancer Ji]], and burn the right-hand marker with the [[Torch of Pyrreth]]: :{{Text|emote|Gizmo emerges from the grave and flees!}}

    This advances the quest to 80% and updates the journal: {{Book|content= When the torch light hit Gizmo's grave, the undead Gizmo emerged and fled somewhere nearby. I should try to catch him. }}

    And Maru offers a hint: {{Dialogue| And then Gizmo showed up and ran away? Wowie! You gotta catch him!

    Maybe be reeeally sneaky-like and you won't spoop him. }}

    [[File:The Case of the Fur-Getting Wikket - Gizmo.jpg|thumb|[[Gizmo (raccoon)|Gizmo]]]] The undead Gizmo runs away! He is very skittish: :{{Abilities|Skittish|id=469903|Gizmo is easily startled by sudden movement.|icon=inv_pineconecritter_white}}

    Toggle run/walk (with the default hotkey of {{keypress|Num Pad /}}), then follow and slooowly catch up to the undead racoon, who never hurt anyone. Consider targeting Gizmo with /tar Gizmo, then placing a [[Target marker]] over his head to make the search easier. [[Druid]]s can simply switch to [[Steady Flight]] and catch the raccoon in [[Flight Form]].

I've missed ye so much!}} :{{text|say|Sully "The Pickle" McLeary|Eauchh!     Five minutes after completing the quest, players receive mail from Riko:

    {{book|Fur-having STINKS|content=Kiryn likes bandit friend, but Riko thinks he smells like ikkin' dook.

    Maybe you borrow furry-friend on your adventures sometimes. Get smell away from Riko. Bring him back before Kiryn notices or wikket will get grooked in the dooker.}}

    Attached is [[Gizmo the Pure]].

    • In Russian version, completion phrase is localized as "Ай да шнуропс! Ай да дукин сын!" (''Ay-da shnurops! Ay-da dukin syn!''), which is wordplay with the Russian catchphrase "{{wp|What a Pushkin, what a son of a bitch!|Ай да Пушкин, ай да сукин сын!}}" (''Ay-da Pushkin, ay-da sukin syn!'', What a Pushkin, what a son of a bitch!) from the letter of famous Russian poet and novelist {{wp|Alexander Pushkin}} to his friend, poet {{wp|Pyotr Vyazemsky}}.
