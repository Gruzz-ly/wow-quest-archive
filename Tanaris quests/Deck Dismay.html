    {{WorldEvent/Anniversary|quest|doc=}} {{Questbox | id = 84882 | name = Deck Dismay | displaycat = Secrets of Azeroth | category = Guest Relations | level = {{level|holiday 30}} | start = [[Alphonse Constant]] {{Co|63.9|52.4|Tanaris}} | rewards = 5x [[Timewarped Badge]] | money = {{Cost|23|40}} }} [[File:Darkmoon Fortune Card.jpg|thumb|Darkmoon Fortune Card]]

    '''Deck Dismay''' is the first of the Local Stories objectives of [[Guest Relations]], started on or after 4 November 2024 by picking up the [[Six of Frost (Anniversary)]] from the [[Darkmoon Fortune Card]] behind the crate by [[Alyx]] at the celebration area at the [[Caverns of Time]] in [[Tanaris]]. Check in with [[Alphonse Constant]] with any card in hand to actually pick up the quest.

    While the quest is active, the [[Idol of Ohn'ahra]] will point to remaining cards.

    This quest is the first in a series required for [[Assistant to the Assistant Guest Relations Manager]].

    ==Objectives== Collect the missing cards. *Find the missing Darkmoon Fortune Deck cards (0/100%)

    ==Description== Gather up the cards that got scattered around the area and I will be forever grateful!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * 5x [[Timewarped Badge]] * {{Cost|23|40}}

    ==Progress== How's it going? Have you found all my missing cards?

    ==Completion== You've found them all? Thank you so much! I'm forever in your debt!

    ==Notes== On 4 November, the crate beside [[Alyx]] had a [[Darkmoon Fortune Card]] appear behind it, containing the [[Six of Frost (Anniversary)]]. Speak with Alyx after looting it:

    {{dialogue|{{gossip|I found this Darkmoon Fortune card.}}

    Oh, how curious! I wonder if some guest is missing their Darkmoon cards. If so, could you please help them out?}}

    The [[Darkmoon Faire]]-aligned character at the celebration is [[Alphonse Constant]] at {{co|63.9|52.4|Tanaris}} near the food vendors and Cho's stage:

    {{dialogue|title=Alphonse Constant|Oh, hello there. Sorry, but I'm not open for business just yet.

    {{gossip|No problem. What business will you eventually be doing here?}}

    Fortune telling!

    Well... eventually, I hope.

    I guess I should have looked into my OWN future before leaving home though, eh?

    {{gossip|quest=|Fortune telling, you say? You wouldn't happen to be using a Darkmoon Fortune Deck for that, would you?}}

    As a matter of fact, I do!

    But it looks like there was a tear in my satchel and some cards fell out. Without a complete deck, there's no way I'll be able to do this.

    Not to mention I'm borrowing this deck from my friend Marie-Anne, and what kind of friend would I be if I returned a deck with half of the cards missing?

    {{gossip|You know what? I actually found a card earlier! Is this one of the ones you're missing?}}

    Oh my, yes! Serendipity be praised!!

    Do you think I could bother you to find the rest? I would gladly compensate you for your time.

    {{gossip|quest=|Not a bother at all! I'm happy to help.}}}}

    This starts the quest [[Deck Dismay]], and each card found adds 10% progress. Alphonse also has new gossip:

    {{dialogue|Hail, friend! Well met! Are you enjoying the festivities?

    How goes your search? Oh, if I manage to get out of this pickle without Marie-Anne finding out I "borrowed" her deck, that WOULD make for an enchanted day, wouldn't it?

    {{gossip|I'm working on it. Do you remember where you might have dropped any?}}

    I've been all over since I got here.

    I got some food, went to the bathroom, met with the coordinators, and checked out all the events going on.

    {{gossip|So, just about anywhere, then. Fantastic. Is that all?}}

    Yes, absolutely.

    Well, other than when I ducked under one of the pavilions to change into my fortune-telling costume. I figured it was as fitting a place as any to get changed, seeing as how everyone above me was also changing into different costumes.

    And then I wondered if maybe I should hop up there myself to claim some ribbons! This outfit would surely grab enough attention to win, right?

    I mean, yes! Right! What... what was the question again?

    {{gossip|Oh, boy...}}}}

    [[Maru]] as always has advice too: {{dialogue|{{gossip|{{text|red|(Hint)}} }}

    Sounds like da cards could be anywhere around the celebration area. Maybe da idol can find them!}}
