Find the [[Royal Finder]] {{co|23.1|30.7|Valley of the Four Winds}} [[Mallet Head]] {{co|25.0|28.0|Valley of the Four Winds}}

    ==Description== [[Monkey King]] receive word on where to find the head,

    The hozen who got it has long since been dead.

    Kunzen chiefs pass the relic from father to son,

    But then [[virmen]] [[Skiggit]] came and the head it won.

    Now the wikket worships it in a hollow.

    Since it was too ookin' big for him to swallow.

    I sent a royal finder north to find him and stand.

    Look for finder in [[Skyrange|mountains]] west of the [[Heartland]].

    ==Notes== {{Zone Map|zone=Pandaria|notes= {{Zone Map Note|50.6|91.6|Cross|Mallet Handle @ Krasarang Wilds 34.1,86.1, Pandaria}} {{Zone Map Note|45.5|61.1|Cross|Mallet Head @ Valley of the Four Winds 25.0,28.0, Pandaria}} {{Zone Map Note|34.0|33.1|Cross|Harmonic Ointment @ Townlong Steppes 62.6,34.1, Pandaria}} {{Zone Map Note|64.3|75.2|ActiveQuest|The Monkey King}} }}

    :In pieces three the mallet be. Handle, head and ointment. You ring bell without the mallet? You going to get an ear and face full of evil.

    Pick up [[The Handle]] and [[The Harmonic Ointment]] before heading out.

    Head southwest to [[Unga Ingoo]]. On the isle's western end on the northern side at {{coords|43.1|86.1|Krasarang Wilds||1}} close to the [[Cradle of Chi-Ji]], [[The Monkey King]], riding on a few [[Personal Assistant]]s with a few [[Royal Keg-Haver]]s are having a party over the objections of [[Chief Ingoo Ingoo XVII]].

    :Poor Chief Ingoo Ingoo. His family forget what rod was for. He so mad. :If he keeps, he will corrupt his people. Very sad.

    Speak with him: :No! Monkey King no take rod! You no take rod! This not even real Monkey King. :Chief Ingoo Ingoo have rod from father Ingoo Ingoo, and his father before him, and his father before him. :Monkey King give rod to my family in long long ago time. My family becomes chiefs. :No hozen live that long! None! :{{gossip|''But that is the real Monkey King.''}} :{{text|say|Chief Ingoo Ingoo XVII|No! Silly wikket cannot take the hozen mighty rod!}} :{{text|say|Chief Ingoo Ingoo XVII|Unga Ingoo is mine!}}

    Finish him off, then turn in to the Monkey King right next to him: :{{text|say|The Monkey King|We will return to Lion's Landing. Meet us there when you have everything.}}

    Head north-by-northwest out of Krasarang to the [[Valley of the Four Winds]]. On the western side of the valley up on the western side of the [[Skyrange]] overlooking the [[Singing Marshes]] and the [[Paoquan Hollow]] at {{co|23.1|30.7|Valley of the Four Winds}} is the [[Royal Finder]] standing at the mouth to a virmen den. Speak with him: :Fluffy wikkets stole the mallet head! Kunzen hozen lost it many years ago. Now the fluffy wikkets hide insie their lair and worship the head like it some kind of fancy shmancy carrot. :Be careful, those fluffy wikkets bite hard.

    Inside is the [[Lair of Skiggit]] where at {{co|25.0|28.0|Valley of the Four Winds}} [[Skiggit]], the Keeper of the Light Thingy, is being admired by a dozen [[Light Watcher]]s. Skiggit has a million health, so take it out, then loot the [[Mallet Head]] hanging from the ceiling. There is also a [[Virmen Treasure Cache]] on the left-hand side of the cave from the entrance...

    Exit the virmen den and start heading north along the [[Serpent's Spine]] to it's northern end in northeastern [[Townlong Steppes]]. An unnamed mogu camp has taken over the area, where at {{co|62.6|34.1|Townlong Steppes}} [[The Blade]] can be found. He is a level 90 elite mogu with more than a million health. Kill him, then loot the [[Harmonic Ointment]] from him. There is also an [[Abandoned Crate of Goods]] in the tent behind him...

    Finally, return to [[Lion's Landing]].

    On completion of all three, adventurers will complete the "In Search of Harmony" portion of [[Operation: Shieldwall Campaign]], and a short script event plays out: :{{text|say|Anduin Wrynn|Alright, we have the handle, the head, and the ointment like you asked for. Can you re-assemble the mallet?}} :{{text|say|The Monkey King|Ayup! Silly youngin, just put rod in hole. And the mallet shall again be whole.}} :{{text|say|The Monkey King|Good as nibby new! But listen to the Monkey King. You only have enough ointment for one true swing.}} :{{text|say|Anduin Wrynn|Only one swing? Are we sure this will work? Is there any way to get more ointment?}} :{{text|say|The Monkey King|The Monkey King was there, the last time this mallet struck true. Tears came to eyes, and harmony was felt through and through. But no more ointment was ever made, for the mogu came with their raid. Use what you have, be grateful it is there. For the bell is a dangerous weapon, so handle with care.}}

    Connelly also sends a letter afterward. {{book|Be Careful|content=Old friend -
