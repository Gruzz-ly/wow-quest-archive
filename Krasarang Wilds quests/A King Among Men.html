    ==Description== There's no time to waste, we need to find Troteman and secure the beach.

    We should check on the dwarves, too - the [[Explorer's League]] is tough, but they're no army. They should be along the cliff just north of here.

    Let us ride, . For our friends. For the [[Alliance]].

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|19|84|50}} 118000 [[XP]] *+300 [[reputation]] with [[Operation: Shieldwall]]

    ==Progress== The Alliance will be a shield, protecting Pandaria from the Horde.

    ==Completion== I'm satisified with this site, it's time to bring in our fleet.

    ==Notes== On accept, Varian joins the party. A boss-level NPC with 590 million health, he flies the Banner of King Varian Wrynn, increasing health, healing and absorption effects, and damage dealt by 25%. Slaying members of the Horde will trigger King Varian Wrynn to release a warcry, restoring 20% of total health and mana.

    Head northwest along the beach and start killing [[Horde Raider (Krasarang Wilds)|Horde Raiders]], [[Horde Priest]]s, and [[Horde Hunter]]s past the roots.

    West along the beach is [[Marshal Troteman]] and a few [[7th Legion Scout (Krasarang Wilds)|7th Legion Scouts]]. On approach: :{{text|yell|Marshal Troteman|Brace yourselves, they're coming back around!}} :''A squad of Horde troops swarm Troteman and his scouts.''

    Help fend them off. When Troteman's out of combat: :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|Troteman, you old dog, I knew I'd find you stirring up trouble.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|The survey team has done well. This is an ideal deep-water port.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|I want to see a tower built right here.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|Marshal, see to it that you hold this position until construction is complete.}} :{{text|say|King Varian Wrynn|Now, let's see to our supplies...}}
