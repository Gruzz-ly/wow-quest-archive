    He's In Deep

    {{Questbox | image=He's In Deep.jpg | imagecaption=Goottooth at Fire Camp Bataar | name=He's In Deep | start=[[Admiral Taylor]] {{coords|89.5|32.6|Krasarang Wilds||1}} | faction=Alliance | id=32382 | level={{level|Krasarang Wilds}} | category=Pandaren Campaign | end=[[Cousin Goottooth]] {{coords|59.2|84.3|Kun-Lai Summit}} | reputation=+150 [[Operation: Shieldwall]] | experience=23600 | money={{cost|1|98|50}} | previous=4900/6000 {{rep|Friendly}} with [[Operation: Shieldwall]] | next=[[Bugging Out]], [[He Won't Even Miss It]] }}

    ==Objectives== [[File:Cousin Goottooth.jpg|thumb|[[Cousin Goottooth]].]] Meet Connelly in [[Kun-Lai Summit]]. Speak to [[Fennie Hornswaggle]] at [[Lion's Landing]] for transportation. Find Connelly in Kun-Lai Summit [[Grummle Disguise Kit]] (provided)

    ==Description== Agent Connelly has managed to infiltrate Garrosh's camp in Kun-Lai! He's been following their every move.

    Connelly requested you specifically to assist him in gathering some vital intelligence.

    He sent along a disguise for you. Make sure you put it on before approaching the camp! It will no doubt be heavily guarded.

    He insists you will know him when you see him. Something about your keen eye for details...

    Go talk to Fennie over there for a drop-off.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|1|98|50}} * 23600 XP * +150 reputation with [[Operation: Shieldwall]]

    ==Completion== You've arrived just in time. From the intel I've gathered, Garrosh is hot on the trail of some sort of ancient mogu artifact of power.

    If we can figure out what he's after, we can stop him from getting it!

    We have to act fast to gather as much information as possible.

    ==Notes== Taylor will offer this quest upon completion of [[Stirred, Not Shaken]] and reaching 4900/6000 {{rep|friendly}} with [[Operation: Shieldwall]].

    Head southwest 50 yards to speak with [[Fennie Hornswaggle]] near the flight master: :{{gossip|''Take me to Fire Camp Bataar.''}} :{{text|say|Fennie Hornswaggle|Oh my.
