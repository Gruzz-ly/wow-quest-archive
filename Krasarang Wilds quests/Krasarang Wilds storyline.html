    Krasarang Wilds storyline

    thumb|Zone map

    The *Krasarang Wilds* storyline runs parallel to the Valley of the Four Winds storyline during the Mists of Pandaria campaign, starting about three quests in with Ken-Ken (quest) and Kang Bramblestaff (quest) acting as breadcrumbs to the zone. Ken-Ken is the first quest giver encountered on the road coming out of the Valley of the Four Winds heading toward Zhu's Watch.

    The progression of the storyline is tracked in the achievement Mighty Roamin' Krasaranger.

    After reaching the end of both halves of the storyline, adventurers are sent up to Stoneplow on the west side of Valley of the Four Winds. Players should complete all of the The Search for the Hidden Master-started quests (of which Ken-Ken and Kang Bramblestaff are two) to continue the storyline further. After saving Stoneplow (or as soon as adventurers reach level 87), Messenger Grummles at Stoneplow, Halfhill and the Grassy Cline bring news from Lorewalker Cho in the form of The Road to Kun-Lai, who requests the adventurer's presence atop the Kun-Lai Summit, starting the Kun-Lai Summit storyline.

    In patch 5.1: Landfall, a new series of quests open up on either end of the southern coast. Alliance adventurers start Operation: Shieldwall, setting up Lion's Landing in the Krasarang Cove, while Horde adventurers call in the Dominance Offensive, constructing Domination Point on one of the western isles. The progression of 5.1 storyline is tracked in the achievements Operation: Shieldwall Campaign and Dominance Offensive Campaign

    Zhu's Watch

    thumb|Zhu Province, with Zhu's Watch in the background Searching for Ken-Ken, players first find Zhu's Watch. A strange lethargy has come over almost all of the inhabitants and it is up to Ken-Ken and the adventurer to solve the mystery of what's causing it.

    Mystery solved, Mei sends adventurers forward in Into the Wilds.

    Into the Wilds

    Heading to the southwest from Zhu's Watch, players come across their first faction camp in the zone. From there, both sides run in to Koro Mistwalker on the path crossing the Krasarang River north of the Temple of the Red Crane.

    The Incursion and the Waters of Youth

    thumb|An Arcanic Oubliette has trapped the Incursion* Night elf *Sentinels have found themselves trapped at the Incursion. After dropping the shield and freeing the trapped night elves, the Pools of Youth prove too tempting a target, so adventurers get sent out to clean out the Ruins of Dojan and bring a sample of the youthful water back for testing.

    Fed up that the Pools of Purity aren't going to make the night elves immortal again, Lyalia orders adventurers onward to the Sentinel Basecamp in Going on the Offensive.

    Thunder Cleft

    thumb|Thunder Cleft* Players must help *Sunwalker Dezco's wife give birth, as well as investigate the mogu threat from the south.

    Onward to Kor at Dawnchaser Retreat in Going West

    Temple of the Red Crane

    thumb|Koro and the saurok north of the Temple of the Red Crane* After discovering the secret of the *Pools of Youth, adventurers are sent toward their second camps further in the zone. Before they can get there, though, they run across Koro Mistwalker north of the Temple of the Red Crane. The sha have come out in force and the saurok are causing problems too. Help Koro escort a group of evacuees to the Crane Wing Refuge, but not before he takes an arrow to the knee, then help clear out the temple.

    Taking on the mogu

    thumb|The Ruins of Korja* The temple cleaner for the moment, keep heading to the second faction base. The *mogu are pushing hard from the Ruins of Korja and the Sentinels and Sunwalkers could use some time and space to lick their wounds. After discovering pandaren captured alongside members of Alliance and Horde, the pandaren from Stoneplow mention that the mantid shattered their convoy. While helping save pandaren near the border with the Valley of the Four Winds, Kor and Lyalia both get themselves captured by Groundbreaker Brojai, the leader of the invading mogu forces. Brojai is slain, but not before he kills Kor and Lyalia.

    Sentinel Basecamp

    thumb|Sentinel Basecamp* After *Lyalia orders adventurers onward from the Incursion, they reach the Sentinel Basecamp in the northwest part of the zone.

    At the conclusion of the quest chain, players head back in to Valley of the Four Winds to Warn Stoneplow (Alliance).

    Dawnchaser Retreat

    thumb|Dawnchaser Retreat* Players are directed against *mogu and mantid threats.

    At the conclusion of the quest chain, players head back in to Valley of the Four Winds to Warn Stoneplow (Horde).

    Dailies

    There are three sets of level-90 daily quests in the Krasarang Wilds. One set, involving the Anglers faction at Anglers Wharf, is offered daily. The other set, involving the Temple of the Red Crane, is not offered every day--only one of the four August Celestials temples will be questable each day.
