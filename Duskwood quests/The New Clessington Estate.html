    ==Description== Excellent, . Finally, something we can work with.

    [[Count Clessington]] is a member of the [[House of Nobles]] and a powerful [[mage]]. He has fallen on hard times since [[Anduin Wrynn|Anduin]] cracked down on corruption in [[Stormwind]].

    I would not put it past him to have something to do with this.

    He recently purchased an old manor in Duskwood. I will contact my sources and see what other information I can find. You and [[Vanessa VanCleef|Vanessa]] should go to the manor and investigate.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|23|40}} 6,800 [[XP]]
