    '''How It's Made: Arcwine''' is a [[quest]] in [[Suramar]] given by [[Margaux]].

    ==Objectives== Follow [[Margaux]] through the Vineyard. Exchange information where possible. [[Aging Chamber]] Visited [[Small Orchard]] Visited [[Distribution Center]] Visited [[Infusion Station]] Visited

    ==Description==

    While we walk through the [[Twilight Vineyards|Vineyard]], we may find time to exchange information.

    Try not to look alarmed at what you see, hm?

    Shall we press on?

    ==Rewards== The following will be cast on you: [[Arcwine Connoiseur]]

    You will also receive: {{cost|20|60}} 16,870 [[XP]] *+25 [[reputation]] with [[Nightfallen (faction)|The Nightfallen]]
