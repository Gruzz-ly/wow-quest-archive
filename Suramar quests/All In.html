    ==Objectives== Complete the "All In" scenario.

    [[Chief Telemancer Oculeth]] can begin the scenario.

    • [[Nightflow Conduit]] sabotaged
    • Check on [[Arluin]]
    • Enter [[Shal'Aran]]

    ==Description== It is time to put our plan into action.

    Oculeth will send you, Arluin and myself into the [[Arcway]], where we shall trigger a manastorm in the Nightflow Conduit.

    [[Valtrois]] will stay here and prepare Shal'Aran for a massive jolt of arcane power - hopefully enough to satisfy the [[arcan'dor]].

    ... We have a saying... "To lead is to stand alone." It means only those who bear the weight of leadership can truly appreciate it.

    Thank you for standing beside me. In truth, I do not feel alone.

    ==Rewards== {{Itembox|You will receive: |Residual Manastorm Energy }}

    • +500 [[reputation]] with [[Nightfallen (faction)|The Nightfallen]]

    ==Completion== Behold. The redemption of the Nightfallen.

    ===Stage 1: Hitting the Books=== : ''Slay the Duskwatch Archivists to escape the [[Forgotten Library]].'' * [[Duskwatch Archivist]] slain (3)

    :{{Text|say|Arluin|This is not looking real conduit-y. You sure the old man knows his stuff?}} :{{Text|say|First Arcanist Thalyssra|Yes. We are close.}}

    ===Stage 2: The Nightflow Conduit=== : ''Reach the door to the [[Nightflow Conduit]].'' * Reach the door to the Nightflow Conduit.

    Follow Thalyssra and Arluin. Soon you will run into the bane of all adventurers: a locked door.

    ===Stage 3: Knock Knock=== : ''Defend Arluin while he opens the door to the Nightflow Conduit.'' * Defend Arluin while he opens the door to the Nightflow Conduit.

    :{{Text|say|Arluin|I never met a lock I could not open.}} :{{Text|say|First Arcanist Thalyssra|Look out!}} :{{Text|say|Arluin|Just give me one moment.}}

    The enemies come in waves, filling the progress bar as they are defeated. Thalyssra will assist you in defending the door while Arluin is opening it.

    ===Stage 4: E.M.P.=== : ''Place the Ephemeral Manastorm Projector in the Nightflow Conduit.'' * Place the E.M.P.

    :{{Text|say|Arluin|Whatever you gotta do, do it quick! Lots of guards heading our way.}} :{{Text|say|First Arcanist Thalyssra|There! Place the manastorm generator near the conduit.}}

    ===Final Stage: Weather the Storm=== : ''Survive the manastorm.'' * Survive the manastorm.
