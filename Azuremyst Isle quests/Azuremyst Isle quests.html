    Azuremyst Isle quests

    This is a list of all Azuremyst Isle quests in table format (arranged by level).

    For a questing guide detailing the best order to obtain and complete quests in Azuremyst Isle, see Azuremyst Isle questing guide.

    The following quests can be found in Azuremyst Isle:

    Ammen Vale quests

    Ammen Vale is the beginning area for draenei. New draenei will spend their first levels (usually 1-5) in this area before graduating to the main Azuremyst Isle area outside this sheltering vale.

    Azuremyst Isle quests

    The main area of Azuremyst Isle is the starting zone for draenei. Most new draenei will spend levels 5-10 here, before moving on to either Bloodmyst Isle or Darkshore.
