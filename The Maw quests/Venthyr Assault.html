    The '''Venthyr Assault''' is one of four covenant assaults in the [[Maw]]. Taking part are [[Lady Moonberry]], [[Prince Renathal]], [[General Draven]], [[The Curator (venthyr)|The Curator]], [[Temel]], [[Cryptkeeper Kassir]], [[The Accuser]], [[Gresit]], [[Rendle]], [[Cudgelface]], [[Nadjia the Mistblade]], [[Tubbins]], [[Gubbins]], [[Theotar]], [[Niya]], [[Hunt-Captain Korayn]], [[Boot the Beaut]], [[Stonehead]], [[Clumpdump]], [[Burly Hurly]], and [[Muckhands]].

    [[File:Maw Mirror Venthyr Assault.png|thumb|upright|Sinfall's mirror to the Maw.]] While the Venthyr Assault is active, a mirror to the Tremaculum appears in [[Sinfall]], the [[Venthyr Covenant]]'s sanctum.

    Lots of interactibles litter the area: {{Abilities|Mending Moth|Restores 30% health. A little chewy. Can only eat one at a time.|icon=inv_mothardenwealdmount_mint}} {{Abilities|Overflowing Chalice|Most power costs reduced by 100%. Cooldown recovery rate increased by 50%.|icon=inv_offhand_pvealliance_d_01|notes=30 sec buff}} *{{Abilities|Unstable Animapod|Inflicts Arcane damage to all nearby enemies and stuns them for 10 sec. While stunned, damage taken is increased by 30%.|range=10|icon=spell_animaardenweald_nova}}

    ==Rewards== You will receive: [[Harvester's War Chest]] (may contain [[Harvester's Dredwing Saddle]]) [[Emblem of Renown]] (catchup only) * +350 reputation with [[Death's Advance]] * +1000 reputation with [[Court of Harvesters]]

    ==Walkthrough== Find [[Burly Hurly]] outside the eastern gate to the [[Tremaculum]]: :{{text|say|Watchmaster Boromod|Thank the stone you've arrived, Maw Walker. Prince Renathal awaits you on the barricade above.}} :{{text|yell|Burly Hurly|I throw you there, !}}

    Interact with Burly Hurly to get thrown: :{{text|say|Burly Hurly|Don't worry, I throw good!}}

    Speak with Renathal: :No enemy can stand against a united Shadowlands. {{gossip|How can I help the assault, Prince Renathal?}} :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|Your arrival heralds our victory. Lead our charge into the fortress!}}

    Four quests unlock. Pick up enough of them and Renathal, [[Lady Moonberry]], and [[General Draven]] fly off: :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|Lady Moonberry, might you kindly cover our advance into the fortress?}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|A simple trick. No one can see us unless we want them to!}}

    Finish them, then check back in with Renathal under the steps by the [[Conjured Death]] and [[Talaporas, Herald of Pain]]. Draven is behind Renathal and Moonberry, so hop on: :{{text|say|General Draven|Hold tight!}} :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|With the power of Revendreth and Ardenweald united, even the most dreaded of the Jailer's beasts will fall with ease!}} :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|However, I did not expect it to be quite so large...}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Look, now it's easier to hit!}}

    [[File:Venthyr Assault - light mirror.jpg|thumb|A Light mirror]]

    [[Gothra the Trembler]] is on the platform where [[Talaporas, Herald of Pain]] usually spawns, but he can't be attacked. Walk through a Maw Mirror back to Revendreth, clear out the hostiles defending the mirror, then aim the Light back through the mirror. Rinse and repeat for the other three mirrors.

    [[File:Venthyr Assault - finale.jpg|thumb|Gothra dead]] After running back through the last mirror, a cutscene plays. :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|Allies, brace yourselves!}} :''Light from the four mirrors slam into the behemoth.'' :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|Burn in the light of the Ember Ward, wretched beast!}} :{{text|say|Gothra the Trembler|Raaagh!}}

    On the other side, Gothra is dead: :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|And so the Tremaculum is ours. For the Shadowlands!}}

    Prince Renathal gossip: :Today's victory is not just for Revendreth or Ardenweald. Every win is shared among all who wish the best for the Shadowlands. :We will not rest until our fight is complete.
