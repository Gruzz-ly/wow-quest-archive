    The Venthyr Assault is one of four covenant assaults in the Maw. Taking part are Lady Moonberry, Prince Renathal, General Draven, The Curator, Temel, Cryptkeeper Kassir, The Accuser, Gresit, Rendle, Cudgelface, Nadjia the Mistblade, Tubbins, Gubbins, Theotar, Niya, Hunt-Captain Korayn, Boot the Beaut, Stonehead, Clumpdump, Burly Hurly, and Muckhands.

    thumb|upright|Sinfall's mirror to the Maw. While the Venthyr Assault is active, a mirror to the Tremaculum appears in Sinfall, the Venthyr Covenant's sanctum.

    Walkthrough

    Find Burly Hurly outside the eastern gate to the Tremaculum:

    Interact with Burly Hurly to get thrown:

    Speak with Renathal:

    No enemy can stand against a united Shadowlands.

    Four quests unlock. Pick up enough of them and Renathal, Lady Moonberry, and General Draven fly off:

    Finish them, then check back in with Renathal under the steps by the Conjured Death and Talaporas, Herald of Pain. Draven is behind Renathal and Moonberry, so hop on:

    thumb|A Light mirror

    Gothra the Trembler is on the platform where Talaporas, Herald of Pain usually spawns, but he can't be attacked. Walk through a Maw Mirror back to Revendreth, clear out the hostiles defending the mirror, then aim the Light back through the mirror. Rinse and repeat for the other three mirrors.

    thumb|Gothra dead After running back through the last mirror, a cutscene plays.

    ''Light from the four mirrors slam into the behemoth.''

    On the other side, Gothra is dead:

    Prince Renathal gossip:

    Today's victory is not just for Revendreth or Ardenweald. Every win is shared among all who wish the best for the Shadowlands. We will not rest until our fight is complete.
