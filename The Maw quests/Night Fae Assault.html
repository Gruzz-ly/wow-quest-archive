    The '''Night Fae Assault''' is one of four bi-weekly [[Chains of Domination campaign#Covenant assaults|covenant assaults]] on the [[Maw]] in [[patch 9.1]]. This assault focuses on the [[Crucible of the Damned]]. At camp are: [[Lord Herne]], [[Margrave Sin'dane]], [[Thiernax]], [[Dreamweaver]], [[Lady Moonberry]], [[Niya]], [[Bonesmith Heirmir]], [[Choofa]], [[Krrprripripkraak]], [[Rathan]], and [[Emeni]]. Others taking part include [[Droman Aliothe]] and [[Droman Tashmur]].

    [[Night Fae Covenant]] players with at least rank 1 of the transport network will find a Mushroom to the Maw in the Ring. There's a Mushroom to the Ring at the Night Fae camp in the Maw that will send players back.

    If [[A Shady Place]] is one of the active quests, players will be able to access [[Rift (Shadowlands)|The Rift]], an alternate plane of existence in the Maw filled with secrets, rare enemies, and a mount to find.

    Additionally, find the [[Etherwyrm Cage Key]] to open the [[Etherwyrm Cage]] beneath the camp in the [[Desolate Hollow]].

    ==Walkthrough== :{{text|yell|Lord Herne|The hunt has begun!}}

    The Night Fae and Necrlords have set up camp in the [[Crucible of the Damned]], just south from where [[Apholeias, Herald of Loss]] spawns. Speak with Herne: :The Queen has ordered that we cleanse this area. We shall cull the Jailer's minions in a great hunt. :The champions of Maldraxxus have already arrived. Will you join us? {{gossip|I will join the hunt, Lord Herne.}} :{{text|say|Lord Herne|Your prey awaits, Maw Walker. Good hunting.}}

    [[File:Night Fae Assault - on bridge.jpg|thumb|The party on the bridge]] Four quests open up. On accept of most of the quests: :{{text|say|Lord Herne|Ride forth! We strike at the heart of this encroaching darkness.}} :{{text|say|Margrave Sin'dane|You will clearly have need of my sorcerous expertise.}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Well, this should be fun...}} :''The majority of the assault party charges uphill to the southwest.

    Catch up with the party on the bridge over [[Gorgoa: River of Souls]] and turn in the quests. On completion of all four, a cutscene plays: :{{text|say|Lord Herne|What vile horror is this?}} :{{text|say|Margrave Sin'dane|I know this magic...}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Don't worry, Maw Walker. I have you.}} :{{text|say|Mawsworn Watcher|Darkness awaits you!}}

    [[File:Night Fae Assault - dark.jpg|thumb|It's rather dark]] On the other side, the Crucible has gotten quite dark. Players are buffed with: :{{Abilities|Moonberry's Shadow|Stealthed from the watchful eye of Mawsworn Watchers by Lady Moonberry. Avoid their direct gaze!|icon=spell_animaardenweald_groundstate}} :{{Abilities|Desperate Assault|Helping Lady Moonberry and the Night Fae assault Crucible of the Damned.|icon=inv_misc_sigil_ardenweald01}}

    ''Dismount'', then speak with Moonberry: :The mawsworn took our friends into that foreboding fog ahead... :I can keep us hidden, but it will not be easy to find them in there. {{gossip|Keep us hidden while we search for our friends.}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|You'd better stay close. The Mawsworn are looking for us.}}

    They also enter the vehicle interface and gain three abilities:

    {{Abilities|Rescue Prisoner|Release the bonds of a prisoner, allowing them to escape with you.|range=5|notes=3 sec cast|icon=inv_misc_key_11}}

    {{Abilities|Distracting Illusion|Creates a distraction, attracting the attention of all nearby Mawsworn Watchers for 5 seconds.|range=20|cd=3 sec|notes=Instant|icon=spell_animaardenweald_orb}}

    {{Abilities|Blinding Dust|Blinds a Mawsworn Watcher, causing it to lose sight of its current target and unable to see for 4 sec. Usable while caught by watchers.|range=40|cd=4 sec|notes=Instant|icon=inv_enchant_dustsoul}}

    :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Quick! Distract that watcher so we can sneak past.}}

    Move forward to the edge of the guard's searchlight, then use the '''Distracting Illusion''' ability and target the ground underneath it: :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Good! Now let's move before it notices us.}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Ha! Fooled him! If we get caught, the blinding dust can help us escape.}}

    [[File:Night Fae Assault - stealth section.jpg|thumb|Dodge the lights]] Cross the bridge to the south to be afflicted by Overwhelming Darkness: :{{Abilities|Overwhelming Darkness|Mawsworn magic reduces your visibility and movement speed.|icon=spell_animamaw_nova}}

    It's a stealth section! Use the distraction ability to force the watchers to focus wherever the illusion looks for a few seconds, then use that time to sneak past or free a prisoner while nobody's looking: :{{text|say|Lord Herne|A clever trick, Lady Moonberry. You have my thanks.}} :{{text|say|Bonesmith Heirmir|My weapons don't work here. Find me something to hit.}} :{{text|say|Margrave Sin'dane|The leader of these Mawsworn lurks nearby. But not for long.}} :{{text|say|Thiernax|Have you found the others? I will leave no one behind.}}

    Should players get caught: :{{text|yell|Lady Moonberry|Quick, use the blinding dust!}}

    [[File:Darkmist Ascendant Relyana.jpg|thumb|Darkmist Ascendant Relyana]] Once all four are freed, head uphill to find [[Darkmist Ascendant Relyana]] :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Phew! I've never felt such a dark presence before.}} :{{Text|say|Margrave Sin'dane|It will pale in comparison to my wrath.}}

    Aggro her: :{{text|say|Darkmist Ascendant Relyana|Your soul shall soon be bound to the Jailer.}}

    The NPCs will tank, so just focus on dealing damage. :{{text|say|Lord Herne|The darkness recedes and our quarry is slain! A victory well earned!}}
