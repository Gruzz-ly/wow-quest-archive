    '''Into Torghast''' is offered by [[Bolvar Fordragon]] in [[Oribos]] after players complete their first chapter of their covenant campaign.

    ==Objectives== Find a way into Torghast, Tower of the Damned. * Jump into the [[Maw]] from the [[Ring of Transference]] (Optional) * Ask [[Ve'nari]] about a way into [[Torghast, Tower of the Damned|Torghast]] {{co|46.9|41.7|Maw}}

    ==Description== The bond to your covenant is growing stronger, . Good. The power lies within you to venture into Torghast and rescue our allies.

    You saw [[Baine Bloodhoof]] while assisting the [[broker]] in the [[Maw]]. Perhaps this [[Ve'nari]] knows a way to breach [[The Jailer|the Jailer's]] tower and find the chieftain.

    When I peered into Torghast once before, you stood at my side. I believe that connection will allow me to see through your eyes as you navigate its torments.

    Seek out Ve'nari and request her aid, Maw Walker.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{Abilities|Access to Torghast|Unlock access to Torghast, Tower of the Damned|id=334746|icon=inv_torghast}} * {{cost|3|8|50}} * 4,750 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Do whatever you can, Maw Walker. We are depending on you.

    ==Completion== Well, Maw Walker, what are you waiting for?

    ==Notes== Upstairs to the [[Ring of Transference]] and a high-dive off the ring into the Maw once more. Speak with Ve'nari: :You were not followed, were you? {{gossip|How do I enter Torghast?}} :{{text|say|Ve'nari|Torghast, eh? Once I told you that your friends were being held inside, I knew it was only a matter of time before you came to me for aid.}} :{{text|say|Ve'nari|Because I was able to anticipate your request, it just so happens I have already acquired a portal device that leads into the tower.}} :{{text|say|Ve'nari|I will dispense with the usual warnings and disclaimers, as your unique combination of courage and foolhardiness would render them moot.}} :{{text|say|Ve'nari|Do not ask how it was obtained. Simply consider it a gesture of goodwill that you are now obligated to repay. I look forward to our future transactions, Maw Walker.}}
