    ==Description== There, [[Dalaran]] is back to how it should be.

    I'm sure you have many questions, . Who are we? What are we doing here? Why are you here?

    The simple answer is this: We are [[Infinite dragonflight|the Infinite]], we are searching through time for answers to questions larger than any of us, and we need your help to do it.

    I think you will come to understand this answer more if you meet the rest of our flight. [[Moratari]], if you would make the introductions?

    ==Completion== As you can see, , there are many minds at work here in the infinite dragonflight. Minds that are all working to answer some pressing questions.

    What knowledge can we gain to prepare us for even greater threats?

    Would we have triumphed if things had been worse?

    What are the implications of [[Sword of Sargeras|Sargeras's sword]] being [[Silithus: The Wound|plunged into Azeroth]], and was it the artifacts that saved Azeroth or something else?
