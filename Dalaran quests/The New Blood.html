    Objectives

    Complete the summoning ritual with the Council of the Black Harvest in the Circle of Wills in Dalaran.

    • Ritual Perfomed
    • Escape from Dreadscar Rift

    Description

    It is time to begin the ritual, warlock. Be prepared, for the summoning we perform today will be more challenging than any you have known or seen before.

    Are you ready?

    Rewards

    You will receive: 15g 40s * 15,050 XP*

    Completion

    Calydus sorry we not save your friends, but maybe they survive if we hurry.

    Jagganoth torture them, yes, but he likes to keep victims alive... mostly.
