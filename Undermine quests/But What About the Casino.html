    But What About the Casino? begins an optional chapter of the Undermine(d) campaign, "Hard Ways at the Gallagio". This chapter, tracked in Sojourner of Undermine, unlocked the week of 4 March 2025 after defeating Chrome King Gallywix for Liberation of Undermine: The House Loses on any difficulty of Liberation of Undermine. Story mode unlocked the week of 11 March 2025.

    Objectives

    Ask about Rosebud Gallywix at the Gallagio's opening party. Speak with Tarbonara Grimclaw Speak with Marin Noggenfogger Speak with Baron Revilgaz Speak with Grimla Fizzlecrank Report to Monte Gazlowe Introduce yourself to Rosebud Gallywix Take the Mega-Savor from Gazlowe's Toolbox Attempt to Mega-Save Rosebud Gallywix (3)

    Description

    Times are a changing. Now that ol' Jastor is kaput, the cartel heads are chomping at the bit to settle the fate of Undermine's crown jewel: the Gallagio.

    There's a court proceeding tomorrow to make a final determination, but luckily the deed is squarely in the hands of someone I trust.

    Their name: Rosebud Gallywix.

    I know. I know. But Rosey is a good egg. I'll introduce you to him later on.

    First, do me a favor. Ask the power players here at the party how they feel about the new owner.

    Rewards
