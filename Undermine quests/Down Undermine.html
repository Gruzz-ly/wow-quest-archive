    '''Down Undermine''' concludes the "Trust Issues" chapter of the [[Undermine(d) campaign]]. The second chapter of the campaign, "Undermine Awaits", begins with the direct followup: [[Welcome to Undermine]].

    After the first character on the account completes this quest, all other characters have instant access to repeatable content in Undermine, such as world quests and public objectives.

    ==Objectives== Talk with [[Gazlowe]], then go to [[Undermine]] using the rocket drill. Speak to Gazlowe Ride [[Pamsy's Rocketboard]] (Optional) [[Stelliya|Rocket Drill]] used {{co|77.1|73.2|Ringing Deeps}} Find Gazlowe in Undermine

    ==Description== We've done what we could here. It's time to get a look at Undermine.

    [[Pamsy]]'s got a contact, a Venture goblin named [[Grimla Fizzlecrank|Grimla]]. We can start there when we arrive.

    Let's get Gazlowe and get going. Undermine's not the kinda problem you solve with a knife. We need someone like Gazlowe on board for this. You know, the builder and leader type.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40}} * 15x [[Valorstones]] * +750 reputation with [[The Cartels of Undermine]]

    ==Completion== I hope Renzik and Pamsy know what they're doin' with Grimla here.
