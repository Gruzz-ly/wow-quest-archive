    ==Description== Our presentation showed that we were the ones that could actually operate the device! If [[Sammy Wrencharm|Sammy]] weren't so focused on the documentation bein' in [[Mite Sparkcog|Mite]]'s hands...     But this ain't over. You saw how Mite overloaded the Smellersweller? We can do that too! Punch a bunch of buttons all at once and we can exploit his half-baked, all-stolen idea!
