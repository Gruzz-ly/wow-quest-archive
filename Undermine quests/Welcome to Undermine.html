    '''Welcome to Undermine''' begins the second chapter of the [[Undermine(d) campaign]]: "Undermine Awaits".

    ==Objectives== Go to the [[Incontinental Hotel]] with [[Grimla Fizzlecrank|Grimla]] and learn more about [[Undermine]]. Ride the [[Gramps|hot rod]] Speak to [[Paks Topskimmer]] {{Co|43.4|51.6|Undermine}} Speak to [[Gas Grimeshank]] {{Co|42.6|51.4|Undermine}} Speak to [[Smaks Topskimmer]] {{Co|43.6|50.8|Undermine}} *Speak to [[Kaydee Racketring]] {{Co|42.8|52.0|Undermine}}

    ==Description== There ya are! I'm Grimla. Welcome to Undermine. If you wanna get up to speed with the current situation, you've come to the right goblin. [[Pamsy]] vouched for you, that's good enough for me.

    I've got a coupon you can use at the Incontinental Hotel. We'll start there.

    Let's take a ride in "Gramps," my hot rod! Hop in when you're ready.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * 10x [[Valorstones]] * +2,500 reputation with [[Cartels of Undermine]]

    ==Progress== Did you hear anything good while you were gettin' yourself settled?

    ==Completion== So, that's the Incontinental Hotel. Things go downhill from here.
