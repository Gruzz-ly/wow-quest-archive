    Undermine(d) campaign

    thumb|Undermine(d) key art.

    The Undermine(d) campaign covers all quests added in patch 11.1.0, mostly taking place in the Undermine.

    Campaign: Undermined

    thumb|Undermine zone map The six-chapter main campaign is tracked in the Undermined (achievement) achievement. To begin, characters must be level and have completed the level-up campaign.

    This chapter unlocked the week of 4 March 2025 and the first quest requires defeating Chrome King Gallywix in the Liberation of Undermine. Story Mode for Gallywix unlocked the week of 11 March 2025.
