    ==Objectives== Rally the people of Undermine against [[Jastor Gallywix|Gallywix]]. Rabble Roused (0/100%) Gallywix Statue Demolished

    ==Description== The trade princes ain't exactly been fair to the goblins here, so this can't be just another cartel war. The crowd needs to know they ain't fighting for [[Bilgewater Cartel|Bilgewater]] or [[Steamwheedle Cartel|Steamwheedle]].

    They're fightin' for [[Undermine]].

    We need someone who ain't a cartel big shot to help rile 'em up and keep 'em safe. Do whatever it takes to get them to the [[Gallagio]] and show these goblins this is their city.

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: [[Legguards of the Undermine Revolutionary]] [[Boots of the Undermine Revolutionary]] [[Chainmail of the Undermine Revolutionary]] [[Headgear of the Undermine Revolutionary]]

    You will also receive: * {{cost|23|40}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * 10x [[Valorstones]]
