    ==Objectives== Save any hostages [[Jastor Gallywix|Gallywix]] is keeping in the warehouse and slay 10 Ethereal Heartseekers. Hostages rescued (3) Ethereal Heartseeker slain (10) *Cave entrance found

    ==Description== We need to split up.

    Alleria is the best one to look for the Dark Heart. You, me, and Renzik will look for the hostages. Here's hoping they're still safe.

    They left some ambushers behind, but we got the best of Sl:7 on our side. You think you see one of them hiding, point them out to Renzik. He'll toss a smoke bomb. That'll flush 'em out and stun 'em, so we can turn the tables.

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: * [[Sabatons of the Liberator]] * [[Robe of the Liberator]] * [[Shoulderguards of the Liberator]] * [[Cinch of the Liberator]]

    You will also receive: * {{cost|23|40}} * 12,200 [[XP]] * 10x [[Valorstones]]

    ==Completion== The hostages are safe. Gallywix doesn't have leverage on any of the cartels anymore. Time to finish this.
