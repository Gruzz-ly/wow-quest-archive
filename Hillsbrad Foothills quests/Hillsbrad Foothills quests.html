    Quest table

    Quests that take place outside of the Hillsbrad Foothills are denoted by a .

    Quests that are part of a chain are denoted by .

    The Horde town of Tarren Mill offers many quests to players through their twenties. Later, Tarren Mill also serves as the Horde town for questing in Alterac Mountains, though there are considerably fewer quests to be done there.

    Alliance quests in Hillsbrad Foothills are slightly higher level than their Horde counterparts, due to the long distance away from the rest of Alliance civilization. All quests come from the town of Southshore.

    Notable chains in the Hillsbrad Foothills

    The following quest chains occur in the Hillsbrad Foothills. Chains have been included if they are more than three quests long or are especially significant in some way.

    Farren's Proof chain

    In this quest chain, players help the lazy Lieutenant Farren Orinelle with his assigned duty of clearing the coasts of southern Hillsbrad of the menace of the murlocs and naga that have taken up residence.

    Hints of a New Plague chain

    This quest chain consists of traveling to Go'Shek Farm in the Arathi Highlands to investigate the undoubtedly nefarious goings-on of the Forsaken apothecaries.

    Technically, almost all the quests in this chain take place in Arathi Highlands, however, it is still categorized under Hillsbrad Foothills in game because the quest giver is in Southshore.

    Battle of Hillsbrad chain

    In this quest chain, players wage war against the human inhabitants of the region, in order to claim the area for the Forsaken.

    Elixir of Agony chain

    This quest chain involves assisting the brilliant but sadistic Apothecary Lydon in brewing yet another plague to unleash upon the unsuspecting living.

    Lord Aliden Perenolde chain

    In this quest chain, players assist an orc whose companions have been kidnapped while investigating the Syndicate of the area and searching for a certain item of interest to Warchief Thrall.

    Technically, almost all the quests in this chain take place in Alterac Mountains, however, it is still categorized under Hillsbrad Foothills in game because the quest giver is in Tarren Mill.

    The Crown of Will chain

    The troublesome ogre warlord Mug'thol (who you may remember from ''The Frozen Throne'') has put on the Crown of Will, freeing he and his ogres from the mind control of Sylvanas. Of course, such rebellion merits swift retribution, and you play the part of the instrument of the Dark Lady's wrath.

    Technically, all the quests in this chain take place in Alterac Mountains, however, it is still categorized under Hillsbrad Foothills in game because the quest giver is in Tarren Mill.
