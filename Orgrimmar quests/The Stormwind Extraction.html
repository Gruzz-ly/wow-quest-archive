    Objectives

    Complete "The Stormwind Extraction" scenario. Take a potion Transport from the Ramparts taken *"The Stormwind Extraction" scenario complete

    Description

    My lady is shrewd indeed. If we are to infiltrate Stormwind City, we will need the very best the Horde has to offer, and yet a team so small as to be able to infiltrate the heart of Alliance territory unseen.

    Yes, I see.

    The path ahead is clear. Speak to me when you are ready to undertake the Warchief's mission, .

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 24,700 XP*

    Progress

    We must not fail the Warchief.

    Completion

    Well, here we are. Zuldazar. We had better stick with Talanji and Zul for now. The Warchief will be interested to know what happens next.
