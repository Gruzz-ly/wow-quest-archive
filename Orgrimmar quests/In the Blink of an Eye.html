    In the Blink of an Eye

    {{Questbox |id=44663 |name=In the Blink of an Eye | displaycat = Dalaran City | category = Dalaran |level={{level|legion}} |start= Automatic |end=[[Emissary Auldbridge]] {{co|57.6|45.8|Dalaran}} |experience=3,700 |rewards=[[Dalaran Hearthstone]]

    ==Objectives== Witness the teleportation of Dalaran to the [[Broken Isles]]. *Talk to the [[Kirin Tor Emissary]] to teleport you to Dalaran (Optional) * Dalaran's Teleportation Witnessed

    ==Description==

    Azeroth needs you, !

    After scouring [[Ulduar]] and [[Karazhan]] for answers, we know the key to defeating Legion: five ancient artifacts called the [[Pillars of Creation]].

    To find them, [[Dalaran]] must be moved to the [[Broken Isles]]. As [[Jaina Proudmoore|Jaina]] is... absent, [[Kalecgos]] of the [[blue dragonflight]] has offered the [[Kirin Tor]] his aid.

    The [[Burning Legion|Legion]] is assaulting our current position over [[Deadwind Pass]]. Hurry!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|3|65|}} 3,700 [[XP]] [[Dalaran Hearthstone]] 5x [[Tome of the Tranquil Mind#BoP]]

    ==Completion== Ah, , it's good to see you on your feet! The members of the [[Council of Six]] are recuperating from their ordeal.

    The intensity of the teleportation spell can be disorienting. It seems you lost consciousness for a time, but at least you didn't awaken to find yourself stuck in a wall!

    Dalaran has been relocated to the Broken Isles. From here, we will spearhead the effort to acquire the Pillars of Creation and drive the Legion from [[Azeroth]] once and for all!

    ==Notes== At level 98 in ''Legion'', you will receive the quest [[Legion: The Legion Returns]] automatically, from which you can go through to reach this quest, or take the option to skip ahead directly to this quest.

    There will be no follow-up quest offered immediately. Walk around Dalaran for a bit, and eventually a representative from your [[Class Order]] will show up and offer a quest to start establishing your [[Class Hall]] and acquiring your first [[artifact]].

    • Death Knight: [[The Call to War (quest)]]
    • Demon Hunter: [[Call of the Illidari]]
    • Druid: [[A Summons From Moonglade]]
    • Hunter: [[Needs of the Hunters]]
    • Mage: [[Felstorm's Plea]]
    • Monk: [[Before the Storm (quest)]]
    • Paladin: [[An Urgent Gathering]]
    • Priest: [[Priestly Matters]]
    • Rogue: [[Call of The Uncrowned]]
    • Shaman: [[A Ring Unbroken]]
    • Warlock: [[The Sixth]]
    • Warrior: [[An Important Mission]] / [[A Desperate Plea (Legion)]]

    Evokers receive [[Aiding Khadgar]] immediately upon entering Dalaran.

    ==Trivia== Originally, [[Khadgar's Upgraded Servant]] would appear near you with this quest on login, wherever you were in the world. (Should the elemental despawn before you accepted the quest, moving to a different subzone would make the questgiver quickly reappear.) About two weeks after launch it was changed, likely because the large servant would follow you around closely and get in the way if you didn't want to take the quest immediately.
