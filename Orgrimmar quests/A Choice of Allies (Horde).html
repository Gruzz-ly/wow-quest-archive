    A Choice of Allies (Horde)

    {{Questbox | id = 50242 | name = A Choice of Allies | category = Allied Races | faction = Horde | level = {{level|bfa}} | start = [[Ji Firepaw]]{{co|37.8|81.2|Orgrimmar}} | experience = 180 | money = {{cost||2}} | previous = [[The Call for Allies (Horde)]] | next = {{plainlist| [[A Feast for Our Kin]], [[Thalyssra's Estate]], [[Vision of Time (quest)]], [[A Royal Occasion]], *[[Guests at Grommash Hold]]}} | other-faction = A Choice of Allies (Alliance) }}

    ==Objectives== Learn more about potential allies. Learn about the [[Highmountain tauren (playable)|Highmountain tauren]] Learn about the [[Nightborne (playable)|nightborne]] Learn about the [[Mag'har orc (playable)|Mag'har orcs]] Learn about the [[Zandalari troll (playable)|Zandalari trolls]] *Learn about the [[Vulpera (playable)|Vulpera]]

    ==Description== As ambassador, it is my duty to bolster the [[Horde]]'s ranks and strengthen our numbers in the face of danger.

    Your adventures have led you to many different people who can aid the Horde if we can convince them to join our cause.

    I've kept a record of the most promising potential allies. The choice of which to approach first is yours.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost||2}} 180 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Is there a problem with my notes?

    ==Completion== As the one who inspired our allies to fight beside us, I believe it should be you who reaches out to invite them into the Horde. I entrust the task to you.

    If you like, I can also recount how our existing allies came to join the Horde.

    ==Notes== Inspect the racial banners behind Ji to complete the objectives, then speak to him again.
