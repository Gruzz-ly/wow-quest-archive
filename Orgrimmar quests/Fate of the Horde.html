    ==Trivia== A bug can occur which causes Sylvanas to act is if the player is a warlock, regardless of their actual class, when turning in the quest. During the duration of the quest, Orgrimmar is phased twice, before and after Vol'jin's death. During the first phase, various inhabitants are seen gathered in front of [[Grommash Hold]] in the [[Valley of Strength]] facing High Overlord Saurfang. After his death, majority of NPCs in the other city districts reflect his passing and the naming of the new Warchief, with guards and other inhabitants slightly moving their positions, offering various remarks, crying and some are even absent.
