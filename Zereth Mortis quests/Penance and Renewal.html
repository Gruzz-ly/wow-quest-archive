    {{Questbox | id = 65297 | name = Penance and Renewal | image = Penance and Renewal.jpg | imagecaption = In the Ring of Transference | category = Zereth Mortis | level = {{Level|Zereth Mortis}} | levelreq = {{Level|Zereth Mortis}} | start = [[Arbiter Pelagos]]{{Co|68.4|49.5|Oribos|3}} | end = [[Tyrande Whisperwind]]{{Co|55.23|59.31|Oribos|1}} | previous = [[The Fate of Sylvanas]] | next = [[Eyes of the Wolf]] or [[Call to Lordaeron]], [[A Gift of Hope]] (both optional) }}

    '''Penance and Renewal''' concludes "Epilogue: Judgment", the final chapter of the [[Zereth Mortis campaign]], thereby also making it the final quest of the overarching [[Shadowlands campaign]]. A few quests remain in the [[Shadowlands epilogue]], however.

    ==Objectives== Speak to Tyrande at the Ring of Transference in [[Oribos]]. *Speak to [[Tyrande Whisperwind|Tyrande]] in the [[Ring of Transference]]{{Co|55.23|59.31|Oribos|1}}

    ==Description== When I became the new [[Arbiter (title)|Arbiter]], I vowed to myself that I would listen to those around me, to take their counsel into consideration before passing eternal judgment.

    I have an inkling of what Tyrande intends for [[Sylvanas Windrunner|Sylvanas]]. Her fury is tempered by wisdom, and I trust in the decision she has made.

    Meet her in the Ring of Transference and see her judgment come to pass.

    ==Completion== Justice for [[Teldrassil]]. Peace for the [[kaldorei]].

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{Text|say|Arbiter Pelagos|True justice brings both punishment... and renewal. I trust in Tyrande to achieve both.}}

    Using the exit portal brings the player not to the [[Enclave]] like usual, but to just outside the [[Idyllia]] in the [[Ring of Fates]]. Take the portal back up to the [[Ring of Transference]], where a small gathering awaits: Tyrande, [[Dori'thur]], [[Shandris Feathermoon|Shandris]], and [[Maiev Shadowsong|Maiev]] are standing in front of the [[Soul Pillar]], while Sylvanas is standing with [[Alleria Windrunner|Alleria]] and [[Vereesa Windrunner|Vereesa]]. The Alliance leaders [[Anduin Wrynn|Anduin]], [[Jaina Proudmoore|Jaina]], and [[Genn Greymane|Genn]] are standing close together on the northern side of the gathering, the Horde leaders [[Lor'themar Theron|Lor'themar]], [[Baine Bloodhoof|Baine]], and [[Thrall]] on the eastern side, and [[Bolvar Fordragon|Bolvar]], [[Tal-Inara]], and [[Uther]] on the southern side.

    Just as the player approaches, Sylvanas finishes a conversation with her sisters: :{{Text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|...and now, after so long... there is much we need to say to one another...}} :{{Text|say|Sylvanas Windrunner|I know, sisters. But judgment comes.}}

    ===Post-quest dialogue=== After completing this last part of the expansion storyline, a number of "Stay awhile and listen" dialogues unlock around Oribos, similar to after [[Covenants Renewed]] at the end of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]]. Characters with dialogues display a large {{Gossip icon}} icon above their head. The icon disappears after the first time the player triggers the dialogue, but the option remains permanently available from the NPCs in case the player wants to see the dialogue again. Unless otherwise noted, the NPCs continue to have the same initial gossip text as they did before the finale cutscene. The player also, for the first time, unlocks permanent access to the Crucible and can travel to and from it at will by talking to Tal-Inara in the Enclave.
