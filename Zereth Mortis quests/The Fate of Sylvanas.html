    The Fate of Sylvanas

    {{Questbox | id = 65263 | name = The Fate of Sylvanas | category = Zereth Mortis | level = {{Level|Zereth Mortis}} | levelreq = {{Level|Zereth Mortis}} | start = [[Arbiter Pelagos]]{{Co|68.4|49.5|Oribos|3}} | previous = [[A Long Walk]] | next = [[Penance and Renewal]] }}

    ==Objectives== Speak to Pelagos to witness the judgment of [[Sylvanas Windrunner]]. *Speak to [[Pelagos]]{{Co|68.4|49.5|Oribos|3}}

    ==Description== The time has come.

    Let this bring the dark saga of Sylvanas Windrunner to a close and herald a new dawn for the [[Shadowlands]].

    Speak to me, and we will begin.

    ==Completion== How could I possibly pass judgment to [[Tyrande Whisperwind|Tyrande's]] satisfaction when she and [[Night elf|her people]] were the ones most wronged by the Banshee Queen?

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{Text|say|Arbiter Pelagos|Let us begin.}}

    Talk to Pelagos again: :All that remains is for judgement to be passed. :{{Gossip|}}

    A real-time cutscene plays.
