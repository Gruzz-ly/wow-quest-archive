    ==Objectives== Bring [[Khadgar]] a drink in [[Bel'ameth]]. * Drink ordered * Drink delivered to Khadgar

    ==Description== Ah, ! You're just the person I was looking for. I'm a little concerned about Khadgar. I don't think it's anything dire, but he doesn't seem to be enjoying himself like everyone else--he just seems... distracted.

    I don't want to pry, but perhaps you could bring him a drink for me? He may open up to you.

    ==Rewards== You will be able to choose one of these rewards: * [[Luscious Leaf Loop]] * [[Summer Sky Signet]] * [[Bramble Bark Band]]

    You will also receive: * {{cost|14|4|50}} * 4,750 [[XP]] * 10x [[Flightstones]]

    ==Progress== Were you able to cheer up Khadgar, ?

    ==Completion== Thank you, friend. I knew he'd speak to you.

    Maybe now we can both relax.
