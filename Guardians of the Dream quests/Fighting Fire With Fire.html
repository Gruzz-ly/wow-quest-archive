    ==Objectives== Clear out [[Skyfire Turret]]s and mark Primalist Flame Wards. * Northern Skyfire Turrets destroyed (3) {{co|26.1|27.6|Emerald Dream (zone)}} * First Flame Ward marked * Western Skyfire Turrets destroyed (3) {{co|24.4|32.1|Emerald Dream (zone)}} * Second Flame Ward marked * Southern Skyfire Turrets destroyed (3) {{co|28.2|34.8|Emerald Dream (zone)}} * Final Flame Ward marked

    ==Description== The notes you found, combined with [[Merithra]]'s reconnaissance, have revealed [[Ashendir Hartwood|Ashendir]]'s plan. The flame wards that surround the [[Wellspring Temple]] are blocking our reinforcements.

    Our allies will distract [[Fyrakk]] while you and I slip behind enemy lines to destroy the wards.

    The skies are filled with drakes, and I must fend them off. Once you find a flame ward and thin out its defenses, signal me to destroy it.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Chartreuse Champion's Legplates |Summer Soother's Leggings |Spirited Stag's Legguards |Fearless Faerie's Trousers }}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * 10x [[Flightstones]]

    ==Completion== Fyrakk may have retreated, but he still aims to destroy the barrier protecting the Wellspring Temple. We must break through the enemy's ranks and stop him.

    It is there we will put an end to his machinations, once and for all.
