    ==Objectives== Investigate the scene. Speak with Khasar {{co|87.0|27.3|Ohn'ahran Plains}} Inspect [[Remains of a Fallen Shikaar|pile of ashes]] Inspect [[Spear of a Fallen Shikaar|broken spear]] Speak with [[Sabellian]] {{co|87.4|27.5|Ohn'ahran Plains}}

    ==Description== [[Khasar|This centaur]] practically attacked us upon our arrival. He seems quite protective over this burning pile of ashes.

    I know you have worked alongside [[Clan Shikaar|the Shikaar]] before. Would you speak to him? I would like to confirm what happened here.

    There is no chance that this is all a coincidence. If it connects to [[Primal Incarnates|the Incarnates]] at all, we must learn all we can.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * 10x [[Flightstones]] * {{cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * +200 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]
