Ride [[Dragonriding]] Mount into [[:Winds of the Isles]] (Optional) [[Boku]]'s report heard{{Co|25.72|44.27|Ohn'ahran Plains}} Aru and Belika's report heard{{Co|27.57|46.01|Ohn'ahran Plains}} Ohn Seshteng's report heard{{Co|26.18|40.06|Ohn'ahran Plains}} *Speak to Merithra{{Co|25.63|40.52|Ohn'ahran Plains}}

    ==Description== [[Khanam Matra Sarest|The Khanam Matra]] sent word that she and her forces have arrived in the [[Emerald Gardens]]. They've located [[Koroleth]] and her [[Primalists]]. It appears a massive enemy invasion is gathering on a beach to the north.

    Wisely, she has several scouts watching strategic locations around the battlefield. Go and collect their reports, then meet me and my forces at the battlefront. Glory awaits us in battle, and, if we succeed, there will be vengeance.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|13|10|50}} * 4,700 [[XP]] *+100 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]

    ==Progress== Every moment we delay, our enemies advance. We must move quickly, .

    ==Completion== Our enemies are one step ahead of us. Do not despair. Our resolve is as strong as our alliance. Prepare yourself, .

    ==Notes== [[File:The Primalist Front - leaders.jpg|thumb|Viranikus, Merithra, and Sarest at the Storm Scar]] While mounted on a Dragonriding mount, the player can optionally jump into the [[:Winds of the Isles]] effect behind Merithra to get a boost that helps them fly north faster.

    [[Aru]] and [[Belika]] are waiting in a grove, surrounded by the corpses of [[Nokhud Skirmisher]]s. Aru's gossip text: :The [[Clan Nokhud|Nokhud]] chose the path of betrayal. They broken{{Sic}} our ancient vow. These are the consequences.

    Talk to Belika: :With the clans gathered as one, our vengeance is at hand.

    :{{Gossip|Merithra sent me to hear your report.}} :{{Text|say|Belika|Our report? Ha! Our report is that the rear flank is secure. You stand amidst the carnage that secured our victory.}}

    [[Boku]] waits further northwest, standing on a cliff overlooking the barrier around the [[Ancient Bough]]. :Glad to see you again, . Do you see the barrier surrounding the Ancient Bough? Beyond it, the green dragons defend sacred territory. I do not know what the green dragons are protecting, but whatever it is, the Primalists want it.

    :{{Gossip|I'm here to hear your report.}}

    A brief cutscene plays. {{#ev:youtube|at9yqQ2nWIU}} :''The camera pans out to give the player a better view of the barrier.'' :{{Text|say|Boku|See those stone pillars? They are part of a barrier that keeps anyone from approaching. We must protect them, or the island's defenses will fall.}}

    Continue north, past an [[Emerald Warden]] and [[General Chartrue]], to a ledge overlooking the Storm Scar where [[Viranikus]], Merithra, Sarest, and some [[Shikaar Ranger]]s await. On the battlefield below, centaur and dragon forces are battling Nokhud and Primalists. [[Ohn Seshteng]] is standing on a lower outcropping, accompanied by [[Avvara]]: :The Khanam Matra asked us for more details on enemy positions. Bond with my eagle once more to see through its eyes. Are you ready?

    :{{Gossip|Sounds like a breeze. I'm ready!}}

    The player sees through Avvara's eyes as the bird takes off and flies over the battlefield. :{{Text|say|Ohn Seshteng|Become the eagle!}} :{{Text|say|Ohn Seshteng|The Primalists have not only enhanced the Nokhud's abilities but also joined their ranks. Look at the size of those [[Stormbound Colossus|Stormbound Colossi]]. This does not bode well for us.}} :''Avvara flies into the cave containing the [[Storm Corridor]] portal.'' :{{Text|say|Ohn Seshteng|That portal must lead to where the Primalist reinforcements await. I wonder if slaying those summoners would disrupt it. Perhaps it would collapse.}} :{{Text|say|Ohn Seshteng|Quickly, Avvara, return before they see you.}}

    Avvara suddenly dies and falls to the ground. :{{Text|say|Ohn Seshteng|No!}}

    The player's vision gets yanked back to where they're standing next to Seshteng. :{{Text|say|Ohn Seshteng|Return to the winds, Avvara... blessed child of Ohn'ahra...}}

    If the player talks to Seshteng again: :Avvara was a noble creature. A longtime friend.

    :Her sacrifice will not go to waste.

    Head up the ledge and talk to Merithra: :The battle has begun. Whatever happens, we must not allow the Primalists to set foot within the Ancient Bough.

    :{{Gossip|Show me what you see.}}

    Another cutscene plays. {{#ev:youtube|jsnphy_lpDU}} :''Merithra and the player walk closer to the edge of the cliff as the camera pans out and focuses on one of the [[Primalist Pylon]]s channeling storm magic into Koroleth, who is in turn mounted on a [[Stormbound Proto-Drake]] and etching storm magic into one of the barrier pillars.'' :{{Text|say|Merithra|Do you see? Those pylons empower Koroleth's destructive power. If we cannot stop her, she will destroy the pillar, and the ward that protects the Ancient Bough will fall!}}

    Completing this quest unlocks [[The Storm Scar (bonus objective)]], which the player can work on during subsequent quests.
