    A Difficult Legacy

    {{questbox |name=A Difficult Legacy |id=74996 |category=Ohn'ahran Plains |level={{level|df-max}} |levelreq={{level|df-max}} |start=[[Voraxian]] {{co|85.6|35.0|Ohn'ahran Plains}} |previous=[[A Dusty Vase]] |next=[[The Tale of Hraxian]] }} [[File:Verbal Archaeology - the view.jpg|thumb|The view of Voraxian and Veritistrasz]]

    '''A Difficult Legacy''' concludes the Verbal Archaeology storyline for the [[Zskera Vaults]] during the [[Forbidden Reach storyline]]. After completing the "Medicinal Fungi" optional storyline of the [[Embers of Neltharion campaign]] at [[Dragonscale Camp]] in [[Zaralek Cavern]], [[Veritistrasz]] will offer [[The Tale of Hraxian]].

    ==Objectives== Talk to [[Voraxian]] and [[Veritistrasz]] at [[Rusza'thar Reach]]. * Find out what the Vase is * Listen to Voraxian and Veritistrasz

    ==Description== Let us talk to Veritistrasz about this vase, .

    I want to temper your expectations though. It is just a vase, but you never know. It could be exciting.

    Maybe it could point us to a cache of weapons?

    ==Progress== It is always a pleasure, .

    ==Completion== Thank you for being here, . You have both given me a lot to think about.

    I will stay with Veritistrasz for now, but I look forward to when we next meet.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{text|say|Voraxian|Veritistrasz, found a vase. Is it anything special? It has a [[black dragon]] fighting a [[djaradin]] on it.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Show me... Oh... This vase depicts the last stand of Hraxian, during the final battle with the djaradin elders.}}

    Speak with Veritistrasz: :Helllo{{sic}} , nice to see you again. :I went to Valdrakken to find Ebyssian, but it turns out he had already left, so I came here for the view instead. :Do you want to stay a while? {{gossip|{{text|rare|(Optional)}} I would like to hear the story of Hraxian.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|It is a sad tale that is worth telling, but not today.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|I will need to ready myself if I am to tell it properly.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|I... would also like to hear the tale when you are ready to tell it.}} {{gossip|{{text|rare|(Optional)}} Who were the djaradin elders?}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Djaradin of unbelievable power. Their mastery over Earth and Fire was incredible, rivalling [[Neltharion]] himself.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|They united the djaradin armies and cut a bloody swath through dragonkind that still echoes in our numbers today.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Eventually, they were defeated by Neltharion. I know not how, the Earth-Warder was ever secretive.}} {{gossip|{{text|rare|(Optional)}} The djaradin elders that were defeated by Neltharion?}} (available if you have heard [[Tarjin the Blind]]'s tales.) :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Yes, I am impressed with your knowledge of draconic history , well done.}} The last two lines of dialogue are the same as from the default option. {{gossip|quest=|Thank you for looking at the vase for us, Veritistrasz.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|A shame though, I was hoping we would find something of power in the vaults.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|This is history. The kind [[Distyia]] would have loved. She would go on about the brush work and glazing. This is... amazing.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|But... I want to help my flight and be a great warrior, as we were in the past.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|I am not as strong as the others, I thought I could find something to make the difference.}}

    Speak with Voraxian now: :I will stay here with Veritistrasz, he has some interesting stories. :I suppose it is a nice view, if you like that sort of thing. :{{gossip|quest=|Why do you want to be a great warrior?}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|We were monsters in the dark for ten thousand years. I want to show dragonkind we can be useful.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|I need to show we can fight for good. That we have changed.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|We need to regain our legacy; we need regain our strength.}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Oh Voraxian...     Back to Veritistrasz: :{{gossip|quest=|What do you think is the legacy of the [[black dragonflight]]?}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Courage, not strength. Before the fall, your flight was known for their courage in all things, not just combat.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|But... What is courage without battle?}} :{{text|say|Veritistrasz|Many of the first draconic artists and philosophers were black dragons.     And to Voraxian: :{{gossip|quest=|Voraxian you seem troubled, what's wrong?}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|In [[Outland]] we were so focused on fighting, on beating the [[gronn]]. Strength and survival were all that mattered.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|I had not dreamed there was more to us than that.}} :{{text|say|Voraxian|You...
