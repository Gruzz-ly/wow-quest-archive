    '''Renewal of Vows''' concludes "Bonds Renewed", the final chapter of the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]].

    ==Objectives== Help [[Sansok Khan]], [[Tigari Khan]], [[Khanam Matra Sarest]], and [[Merithra]] exchange gifts. [[Shikaar Spear|Sansok Khan's offering]] accepted{{Co|22.28|50.89|Ohn'ahran Plains}} [[Ohuna Feather|Tigari Khan's offering]] accepted{{Co|22.37|51.05|Ohn'ahran Plains}} [[Horn of the Khanam Matra|Khanam Matra]]'s offering accepted{{Co|22.34|50.96|Ohn'ahran Plains}} [[Everblooming Soil|Merithra's offering]] given{{Co|22.15|51|Ohn'ahran Plains}}

    ==Description== Centuries ago, I made a vow of friendship and cooperation with the first [[centaur]]. Although much has changed, I feel it is time for us to renew that vow again.

    A recitation was composed to help future generations of centaur remember their bond with the [[green dragonflight]]. Each verse must be answered with an offering from the [[khan]]s. I hope the centaur can still remember the original meanings.

    The choice of gift is critical. Choose the right offering, and our allegiance is bound.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: [[Emerald Shroud of Loyalty]] [[Renewed Proto-Drake: Curled Horns]] * {{cost|78|63}} * 14,050 [[XP]] * +400 [[reputation]] with [[Maruuk Centaur]]

    ==Progress== The recitation is as follows:

    From [[Clan Shikaar|the hunters]] who value skill above all, a rite of passage: a first.

    From [[Clan Ohn'ir|the mystics]] who commune with [[Ohn'ahra|the wind]], her lightest touch: a lift.

    From [[Clan Teerai|the descendants]] of [[Teera|the matriarch]], a drop of unbroken progeny: a line.

    ==Completion== Now that our bonds are renewed, nothing can stand in our way. By wind and wing, so be it.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{Text|say|Merithra|From the hunters who value skill above all, a rite of passage. A first.}} :{{Text|say|Merithra|From the mystics who commune with the wind, her lightest touch. A lift.}} :{{Text|say|Merithra|From the descendants of the matriarch, a drop of unbroken progeny. An ancient line, unforgotten.}}

    The player has to suggest what each khan should give as an offering, from three possible options. Only one option is correct, but there is no penalty for picking an incorrect option; that option will simply disappear from the dialogue window, and the player can pick a new one. The correct option is marked with '''bold type''' below.

Take mine.}} :{{Gossip|}} ::{{Text|say|Okir|Absolutely not! Try again.}} :{{Gossip|}} ::{{Text|say|Okir|My first kill was quite some time ago. Nothing of it remains.}}

    Completing this quest concludes the [[Ohn'ahran Plains storyline]] and rewards [[Ohn'a'Roll]]. Merithra offers [[Into the Azure]] to begin the [[Azure Span storyline]], while Sarest offers the optional dungeon quest [[The Nokhud Offensive: The Wind Belongs to the Sky]]. Back in [[Emerald Gardens]], [[The Lonely Scout]] becomes available, given by [[Scout Santuun]].
