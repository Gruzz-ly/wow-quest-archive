    '''The Price of Peace''' concludes the storyline of ''[[Battle for Azeroth]]''.

    ==Objectives== Witness [[Anduin Wrynn|King Wrynn]]'s address. * Speak with [[Mathias Shaw]]

    ==Description== The king will be addressing us shortly with news from his diplomatic mission to the [[Horde]].

    The other leaders of the [[Alliance]] have already arrived. I am sure they are eager to hear what King Wrynn has to say.

    Speak with me when you are ready, and we will greet the king together.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 17,850 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Speak with me when you are ready, and we will greet the king together.

    ==Completion== Hmm... the meeting did not seem to go quite as the king expected. We know where [[Genn Greymane|Genn]] and [[Tyrande Whisperwind|Tyrande]] stand, but I wonder what the others have to say.
