    ==Objectives== Speak with 8 residents of the Dragon Isles and listen to their stories. *[[Dragon Isles]] residents interviewed (8)

    ==Description== I worry about [[Emberthal]]. Her mood has grown increasingly dark.

    By sticking to citizens of the city, she is looking for ways she is similar to them, instead of what makes her unique.

    Perhaps a broader sample size is in order?

    She needs stories to guide her. There are many such individuals with stories to tell in the Dragon Isles.

    Go, and speak with them.

    Would that I could go myself, but the duties of a [[Scalecommander]] never rest.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{Cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]] * [[Personal Tabard]]

    ==Progress== Have you spoken with everyone?

    ==Completion== Thank you, . If these do not help Emberthal, I do not think anything can.
