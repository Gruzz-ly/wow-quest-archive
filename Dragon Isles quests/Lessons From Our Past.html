    ==Description== ;Non-dracthyr I am Emberthal, a [[scalecommander]] of the [[dracthyr]]. [[Wrathion]] aided us after we awakened from our long stasis, and now it is my turn to help him.

    No doubt you have questions about my people and our history. Until recently, I did as well.

    But [[Nozdormu]], the Timeless One, has revealed lost truths about the dracthyr. He granted me a vision of our origins... and why we were locked away.

    Let me tell you of the things I have seen.

    ;[[Dracthyr (playable)|Dracthyr]] ! It is good to see you again. Wrathion aided us after we awakened from our long stasis, and now it is our turn to help him.

    But first... there are things you must know.

    After we left [[Forbidden Reach|the Reach]], I was granted visions by Nozdormu, the Timeless One. He showed me many truths about the [[dragon]]s, [[Neltharion]]'s fate, and our people's origins.

    Let me tell you of the things I have seen. You must hear them for yourself.
