    '''The Fate of Val'sharah''' is the climax of the [[Val'sharah storyline]].

    ==Objectives== Defend the Temple of Elune. * Speak to Tyrande * [[Ysera|Defend the Temple of Elune]]

    ==Description== [[Malfurion Stormrage|Malfurion]], my love.... My heart breaks for him, but I cannot follow where he goes.

    I have sworn myself to [[Elune]], my goddess, to defend her temples and lands with all that I am. I cannot stand by while [[Sisterhood of Elune|my order]] is in danger.

    May she keep you in her graces, Malfurion.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will receive: {{cost|30|80}}|Crystallized Moon Drop}}

    ==Completion== This shall be remembered as one of the darkest days in the history of our world. But hope remains. Elune's light has strengthened our resolve.

    ==Gains== * 18,500 [[XP]] * +350 reputation with [[Dreamweavers]]
