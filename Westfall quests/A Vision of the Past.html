    ==Objectives== Enter the [[Defias Hideout]] and follow the trail of homeless to the [[Deadmines]] dungeon. Enter the Deadmines [[dungeon]] and use the Incense Burner to see into the past. Vision of the Past uncovered Return to Marshal Gryan Stoutmantle at Sentinel Hill in Westfall. *[[Incense Burner]] (provided)

    ==Description== This incense will put your body and mind in a deep meditative state. Once the trance begins you will be pulled into the spirit world. Do not fight the trance once it has begun, . Allow the spirits to show you what they know.

    You must leave now. Go to the Deadmines, a place which I am told holds great significance in the history of [[Westfall]]. Once inside, use the incense.

    You may enter the Deadmines through the old farmhouse south of here. Follow the homeless.

    Return to [[Gryan Stoutmantle|Gryan]] with your discovery.

    ==Trivia== The party of five Alliance adventurers is a reference to the original, pre-''Cataclysm'' [[Deadmines (Classic)|Deadmines dungeon]], where Edwin VanCleef was the final boss. The intent of the developers was to replicate the appearance of a Deadmines dungeon run in vanilla ''WoW'', complete with a full party of five "players", and the hunter using [[mana]] rather than [[focus]], as hunters did before ''Cataclysm''.

    However, some anachronisms are seen in the encounter: the rogue uses [[Shadowstep]], which was only added in [[patch 2.0.3]] (and at level 60, at that), and the party wears capital city tabards ([[Stormwind Tabard]] and so on) that were only added with the [[Argent Tournament]] in [[patch 3.1.0]], and the earliest point they could possibly be wearing these tabards at level 17 is [[patch 4.0.3a]] (when they were added to capital city vendors; however, that was also the patch that removed the original Deadmines dungeon and added this very quest).
