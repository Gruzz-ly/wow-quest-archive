    Ashran quests

    thumb|Map of Ashran.

    While a Player vs. Player-focused zone, Ashran is home to a surprising number of quests - though a large number of those quests have since been removed.

    Patch 8.0.1 have discontinued Ashran, and removed almost all quests associated with it. As of patch 8.2.0 most of those quests are now done on the Battleground version of Ashran.

    Arrival to Ashran

    The player is initially sent to Ashran after establishing their Garrison on Draenor.

    Ashran Appearance

    Host Howell / Warspear Welcome

    Inspiring Ashran

    A Surly Dwarf / Burning Beauty

    Delvar Ironfist (quest) / The Dark Lady's Gift

    In addition, players have an additional lead to Ashran once they obtain their first Outpost Building Assembly Notes, though only if the player didn't previously buy any building upgrades. Resources in Ashran* / Upgrades in Ashran**

    Having arrived to Ashran, the player is then sent to familiarize themself with the island. This part is removed in patch 8.0.1.

    Ashran Dominance

    Now that the player is familiarized with the island, it's time for action. The main quest requires defeating the opposing faction in four primary areas, and then defeating enemy leader. There is another quest focused on just defeating the leader. Easier said than done. Ashran Dominance* Continue the Domination (removed) Tremblade Must Die* / Volrath Must Die**

    Artifact Fragments

    After completing Uncovering the Artifact Fragments, the player can keep bringing in Artifact Fragments for additional reputation. A Few Artifact Fragments* A Bunch of Artifact Fragments A TON of Artifact Fragments

    Token of demise

    While killing other players on Ashran, the player can occasionally obtain tokens of their demise.     Sealing Fate

    Not related to Ashran itself, the player can turn in various things to get bonus roll tokens for . The player can obtain up to three of those.

    Warzones

    Initial version of Ashran had a number of Bonus Objective quests related to fighting in specific areas of Ashran. In those quests were reworked into World Quests. These quests have since been removed. Slay Them All!* Ashran Excavation (quest) Don't Hate, Excavate! *Brute's Rise (quest)

    Vintage potions

    There is a pair of quests that allows the player to turn in Ashran-specific herbs to obtain Ashran-specific potions. While these quests have not been removed per se, the herbs were. Phantom Potion (quest)* Vintage Free Action Potion (quest)*
