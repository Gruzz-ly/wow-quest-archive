    Description

    We've found the blades, bound them together with wood and shade, and blessed them with an ancient incantation. If I'm not mistaken, it's time to use this thing.

    The sprites spoke of an ancient piece of sap within the Amber Hibernal, a mantid cave near their empress' palace. That must be the "Blood of Ancients" that the prophecy spoke of.

    Whatever's inside that sap must be important. Good luck, .

    Notes

    Pick up Blood of Ancients before heading out.

    Run southwest through Rikkilea to the ''north side'' of the Heart of Fear. On the northwest side of the tree is a chunk of Solidifed Amber in front of an Enormous Landslide blocking a cave. Loot the amber, then interact with the landslide, summoning three Rikkitun Chanters that cast the Incantation of the Rikkitun to clear it, much like how they lifted the Motherseeds in Great Vessel of Salvation. This is the Amber Hibernal.

    Run down the steps inside. Inside is the Klaxxi paragon Iyyokuk the Lucid inside a huge chunk of crystallized amber and... Mama Stormstout? Use the fork near Mama Stormstout to free her. Suddenly... :

    Adjunct Okzyk, an imperial chitinel, Adjunct G'kkal, an imperial blade-dancer, and Adjunct Pivvika, an imperial pain-rouser, spawn and are focused on Iyyokuk. They can each be pulled individually, so take advantage of that.

    Aggro each one: : : :

    On the death of the third: : : : :''The paragon flies off as Chen Stormstout runs in.'' : : : : : :

    Head back to the Sunset Brewgarden to turn in.

    This quest resolves Mama Stormstout's fate, following up after Han Stormstout (quest). The Stormstout family saga at the Brewgarden gets one last installment after turning in Blood of Ancients.

    This quest completes the "Iyyokuk the Lucid" portion of Amber is the Color of My Energy.
