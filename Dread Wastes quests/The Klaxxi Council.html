    ==Objectives== Travel to [[Klaxxi'vess]] at the center of the [[Dread Wastes]]. You may speak with [[Kil'ruk the Wind-Reaver]] in the [[Terrace of Gurthan]] for a ride, or travel on your own. *Travel to Klaxxi Hub

    ==Description== I must report to the [[Klaxxi]]. They would not have woken me unless the situation were dire.

    You have not been entirely useless. For that, I will spare your life.

    The council may even find a use for you.
