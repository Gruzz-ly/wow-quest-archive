    '''The Master of Lies''' is the climax of the [[Revendreth storyline]].

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Sire Denathrius.png|Sire Denathrius|The Master of Revendreth.}} Defeat [[Denathrius|Sire Denathrius]]. * Speak with the [[Projection of Prince Renathal]] * Assault the Menagerie of the Master

    ==Description== Once more unto the fangs of death himself, sword drawn, peace with oblivion made. Let us meet death together and bring him low.

    Be sure you are prepared for a sustained assault. For once we reveal ourselves, there will be no stopping.

    When you are ready, direct the Focal Mirror at the gates of [[Castle Nathria]]. Let us descend upon our burning enemies with ferocity and swiftness of action.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Robe of Pained Absolution |Cowl of Pained Absolution |Nightcloaked Chestpiece |Nightcloaked Helm |Soul Hunter's Vest |Soul Hunter's Headgear |Stoneforged Hauberk |Stoneforged Cover }}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|77|22|}} * 12,250 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== I do this, not because I wish to face oblivion or worse, but because I do not wish for reality itself to face its end.

    ==Completion== What has he done?

    ==Notes== Speak with the projection again: :When you are ready, we shall attack. The dredgers here will operate the mirror to deter reinforcements from Castle Nathria while you, myself, and our few remaining allies do a push assault Denathrius directly. :It is down to a Maw Walker, three Harvesters, the former general of the Stone Legion, and what few venthyr stand by our side as we take the fight to the being who created all you see before you. :Let me know when you are ready to face death. :{{gossip|Begin the assault. (Queue for Scenario.)}}

    A cutscene plays: :''On either side, [[Rendle]] and [[Cudgelface]] direct the large mirror to face toward the Castle Nathria entrance, combusting all venthyr in the light's path to ash. The camera then shows the rest of the Sinfall gang as they rush forward with the player close behind to join Renathal in the Court of Harvesters.''

    ===Stage 1: Rally with the Rebels=== :''Meet Prince Renathal in the Court of the Harvesters.'' *Rally to Prince Renathal {{co|37.5|41.5|Revendreth}} :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|Maw Walker! Come, gather up as much of this anima as you can. This type of binding is temporary but should strengthen you for the battle to come.}}

    The prince spawns a handful of anima pools on the ground. Run through them to get up to 30%.

    In case you wander away: :{{Text|say|Prince Renathal|This way, Maw Walker.}}

    ===Stage 2: Awaken the Army=== :''Awaken stoneborn who are loyal to Prince Renathal.'' *Awaken Darkwing Stoneborn (6) :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|We are going to use the anima Denathrius stole against him.}}

    Keep interacting with [[Anima Container]]s and killing [[Honored Duelist]]s and [[Royal Arcanist]]s while moving east. At the steps: :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|When I led my assault against the castle, the Master turned my entire army to stone with a simple wave of his hand.}} :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|We will need these soldiers. Denathrius is not the only threat we will need to face. Find enough anima to awaken as many of the stoneborn as you can.}}

    Interact with Darkwing Rebels to awaken them. Each one costs 8% anima. They will follow along as guardians.

    ===Stage 3: Rally at Dominance Gate=== :''Meet Prince Renathal and your allies at Dominance Gate.'' * {{co|42.1|37.7|Revendreth}}

    No need to stop at five guardians. Keep awakening more on the way to the gate to make future stages easier.

    ===Stage 4: The Stone Legion Gauntlet=== :''Reach the far side of Dominance Gate.'' *Menagerie of the Master reached {{co|44.7|31.2|Revendreth}}

    The prince and the rest of the party meet with the player. Head down the steps and they run forward. :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|This was once the muster field for the Stone Legion. Watch the skies, for they are watching us.}}

    [[Stone Legion Nightblade]]s drop down.

    ===Stage 5: General of the Stone Legion=== :''Defeat General Kaal'' *Defeat [[General Kaal]] :{{text|say|General Kaal|Draven! Do not make me destroy you.}} :{{text|say|General Draven|Kaal, if you ever cared for me then trust me now and stand down.}} :{{text|say|General Grashaal|You selfish pup! We do not stand down. We do as we are ordered!}} :{{text|say|General Draven|Grashaal, they woke you to take my place?}} :{{text|yell|General Draven|If you want me, come and get me.}}

    ===Stage 6: To Dam the Damned=== :''Disable Anima Diverters'' The Accuser empowered {{co|47.6|32.8|Revendreth}} The Curator empowered {{co|47.2|30.2|Revendreth}} *Renathal empowered :{{text|say|Prince Renathal|Quickly, Maw Walker, while Draven keeps them distracted. We must gather as much anima as we can if we are to face Denathrius.}}

    Just to the northwest after killing [[Dread Siphonist]]s: :{{text|yell|The Curator|Here, Maw Walker, let me extract as much anima from this font as I can. You will need it.}} :{{text|say|[[:Remornia]]|Ooooo, look who's coming! Can I butcher the Maw Walker? Please?}} :{{text|say|Sire Denathrius|Restrain yourself, Remornia. This is Revendreth. We do not butcher, we... educate.}}

    Counterclockwise to the southeast: :{{text|yell|The Accuser|Let us put this anima to better use. Maw Walker, be ready.}} :{{text|say|Remornia|Master! I want to teach these usurpers a lesson. A very sharp, pointy lesson!}} :{{text|say|Sire Denathrius|They are of little consequence. They will see the futility of their rebellion soon enough.}}

    Further counterclockwise to the south: :{{text|yell|Prince Renathal|We are out of time. Gather what anima you can and then we move on Denathrius.}} :{{text|say|Remornia|The Prince is here... along with his harvester traitors!}} :{{text|say|Sire Denathrius|My poor, poor Renathal. I had so hoped he would be standing at my side to share in this glorious moment.}} :{{text|say|Remornia|He is unworthy! I will present his head to you as a trophy!}}

    ===Stage 7: Clear the Menagerie=== :''Crush Denathrius' remaining forces in the Menagerie of the Master.'' *Slay Myskia {{co|50.3|31.3|Revendreth}}

    Get to the center of the area and take out Myskia.

    ===Final Stage: The Master=== :''Join Renathal and the Harvesters for the final assault.'' Speak with Prince Renathal. Sire Denathrius confronted {{co|50.4|30.3|Revendreth}}

    Get to the center of the area and speak with Renathal: :Can you feel it? The shift in order? Something is happening. We must move swiftly! :Three Harvesters and a Maw Walker infused by the raw power of anima from the many realms of the Shadowlands against one lord of death, whose dominion we stand upon the precipice of. :Let us face this dread together! :{{gossip|Ready to face the Master.}}

    ===Finale=== On the other side of the cutscene, Renathal, the Accuser, the Curator, and General Draven are looking off the north end of Castle Nathria at an enormous torrent of anima getting funneled into the Maw. You awake being Drained.

    This concludes the [[Revendreth storyline]], awarding the achievement [[The Master of Revendreth]].

    Renathal continues the [[Shadowlands campaign]] with [[The End of the Beginning]].
