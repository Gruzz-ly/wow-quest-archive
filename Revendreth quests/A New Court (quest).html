    {{For|the order advancement|A New Court}} {{Questbox | id = 59660 | name = A New Court | category = Ember Court | faction = Venthyr | level = {{level|sl-max}} | levelreq = {{level|sl-max}} | start = [[Theotar]] | end = [[Temel]] | experience = 750 | money = {{Cost|12|87}} | shareable = No | next = [[Ember Court Rehearsal]] }}

    ==Objectives== Recruit the personnel needed to open the [[Ember Court]]. * [[Picky Stefan]] recruited * [[Hips]] recruited * [[Lord Garridan]] recruited * [[The Accuser]] recruited * [[Sinfall Surface Flyer|Bat]] used to reach Sinfall's surface *Meet Temel, the Party Herald, in the tower above Sinfall.

    ==Description== My dear , I have heard a delightful rumor that you are considering hosting a new court in [[Sinfall]].

    If that is true, you simply must permit me to recommend staff to help you host a perfect court.

    I have been developing ranked lists for every court staff position since the moment I stepped foot in Sinfall.

    I do not wish to be a braggart, but courts are kind of my specialty.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{Cost|12|87}} * 750 [[XP]]

    ==Completion== This court isn't going to throw itself. Let's get to work.

    ==Notes== This quest becomes available once the [[A New Court]] upgrade has been researched. After recruiting the personel within Sinfall, head upstairs to the [[Spire of the Unseen Guests]].
