    Tyranny of the Light

    {{Questbox |id=52955 |name=Tyranny of the Light |category=Mag'har Orc |faction=Horde |level={{level|bfa allied race}} |start=[[Overlord Geya'rah]] |money={{cost|23|40||}} |previous=[[Bonds Forged Through Battle]] |next=[[The Uncorrupted]] }}

    ==Objectives== Assist [[Overlord Geya'rah|Geya'rah]] against the Tyranny of the Light.

    *"Tyranny of the Light" scenario completed

    ==Description== If it is not an uprising of ogres or the promise of starvation, it's the constant threat of the [[Lightbound]].

    Should the reports prove true, this is the closest they have dared approach our stronghold in quite some time.

    Come or stay, but make your decision quickly.

    ==Completion== To think of those we just lost... It is... No, it is of no use.

    ==Notes== Accepting this quest begins the "Tyranny of the Light" scenario.

    ===Travel to Beastwatch=== Description: Go to the aid of [[Beastwatch]]! It's the site of an impending assault by the [[Lightbound]]. Accompany Geya'rah to Beastwatch

    Exit the [[Throne of Kor'gall]] and travel to Beastwatch.

    ===The Lightbound=== Description: Defend Beastwatch against the Lightbound assault. Defend Beastwatch against the Lightbound assault (0/100%)

    :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Lantresor! Report!}} :{{text|say|Lantresor of the Blade|We are holding the line, overlord. But the enemy looks to be mustering for a bigger attack.}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|This is not your fight, outsiders. You are free to return to [[Azeroth]].}} :{{text|say|Eitrigg|I have never walked away from a battle. I do not plan to start today.}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Then we fight for blood and honor together! With me, heroes!}}

    Engage the Lightbound forces at [[Razorbloom Fallow]]. {{text|say|Overlord Gey'arah|Take out those siege engines!}} {{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Hold your ground! This land belongs to the Mag'har!}} {{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Back, draenei! Your Light is not welcome here!}} {{text|say|Overlord Gey'arah|Strike down the Light's corruption!}}

    ===Evermorn Hold=== Description: Track down {{Au|Exarch Orelis}} at [[Evermorn Hold]]. Look for Exarch Orelis in Evermorn Hold

    Travel to Evermorn Hold.

    ===Exarch Orelis=== Description: Defeat Exarch Orelis. Exarch Orelis slain

    Upon entering Evermorn Hold, a [[Radiant Naaru]] materializes above the field.

    :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|No... a naaru... Not again!}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Curse this vile [[Light]]!}}

    The naaru unleashes a wave of Light that stuns the {{Au|Mag'har}} in the hold (they gain the Blessing of Eternity buff), just as Exarch Orelis lands in his [[warframe]].

    :{{text|say|Exarch Orelis|Cease this pointless defiance, Mag'har! Stand down and welcome the Light!}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Retreat while you still can! You will not force your doctrines upon us, [[draenei]]!}} :{{text|say|Exarch Orelis|The High Exarch orders you to submit... or face judgment!}} :{{text|say|Eitrigg|For {{Au|Draenor}}! For the Mag'har!}}

    Orelis becomes hostile. Destroy his warframe.

    :{{text|say|Exarch Orelis|The naaru offer us peace and order!}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|You mean subjugation!}} :{{text|say|Exarch Orelis|Give up your hate! The Light can heal the scars of war!}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Our scars are not yours to take!}} :{{text|say|Exarch Orelis|This once-fertile land is now lifeless dust. That's what your hate has done!}}

    After his warframe falls, Orelis himself jumps out and continues the fight. Kill him. Orelis drops [[Sermon of the High Exarch]] upon his death.

    :{{text|say|Exarch Orelis|[[Yrel]]... I have failed you...}}

    ===Report to the Warchief=== Description: Return to {{Au|Grommash Hellscream}} at Beastwatch. Meet with Grommash

    :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Your High Exarch cannot save you. My people will never stop fighting until Draenor is free!}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Come, champions of Azeroth. We must tell the warchief of our victory...}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|And, of the fate of my brothers and sisters in Evermorn Hold. I am afraid there is little we can do to save them now.}}

    Return to Beastwatch.

    ===Orders from Grommash=== Description: Await orders from Grommash. Await orders from the Warchief

    :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|The draenei attack was thwarted, warchief. But the High Exarch will not relent. Fanatics never do.}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|We will be ready. And what of these heroes from Azeroth? Were they of aid to you?}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|They fought with courage. I admit... I judged you harshly, Eitrigg. You as well, .}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|Now, on the matter of the debt we owe your Horde, we will send--}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Me. Along with a company of volunteers. With your permission, warchief.}} :{{text|say|Eitrigg|You, overlord? But what of your people's fight here?}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|The warchief can hold off the draenei and those traitorous Lightbound. The Mag'har will help you win your war, and then our debt will be paid.}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|I suspected that once you bonded with them in battle, you would wish to join these heroes, Geya'rah. I give you my blessing.}} :{{text|say|Eitrigg|You have our thanks. , prepare to return to--}}

    At that moment, Lantresor rides up.

    :{{text|say|Lantresor of the Blade|Warchief Hellscream! An army approaches!}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|How many do we face, Lantresor?}} :{{text|say|Lantresor of the Blade|All of them.}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|All?}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|...}}

    ===Defense at Beastwatch Tower=== Description: Use the turret at the top of Beastwatch Tower to defend the Mag'har. Use the turret at the top of Beastwatch Tower to defend the Mag'har (0/100%)

    :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|Lantresor, ride as hard as you can! Gather every orc you see and bring them here!}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|Overlord, Eitrigg, hold the line here with me!}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|, head to the top of the tower and rain hell upon the Lightbound!}}

    Go to the top of the tower and man the [[Mag'har Turret]]. It has two abilities: Turret Blast (standard cannon shots) and Iron Lightseeker (fires an [[iron star]]). Use them both upon the [[Lightbound Vigilant]]s and [[Lightbound Soldier]]s.

    ===Return to the Warchief=== Description: Return to Warchief Hellscream at Beastwatch. Report to the Warchief and await further orders

    Head down from the tower and back to Grommash, who has assembled an army of orcs, ogres, and cannon-equipped [[gronn]].

    :{{text|yell|Grommash Hellscream|They are too many! We cannot hold this line forever!}} :{{text|yell|Overlord Geya'rah|We must! It is NOT our fate to fall to this corruption!}}

    From the Lightbound lines, [[Yrel|High Exarch Yrel]] calls out.

    :{{text|yell|High Exarch Yrel|Grommash Hellscream. It has been a long time.}} :{{text|yell|Grommash Hellscream|High Exarch. A rare... honor.}} :{{text|yell|High Exarch Yrel|I have come to offer you one last chance to embrace the Light.}} :{{text|yell|High Exarch Yrel|Your people are choking the life from this world... dooming the land to desiccation.}} :{{text|yell|Grommash Hellscream|How can you be so blind, Yrel? It is the Light that has doomed this world!}} :{{text|yell|High Exarch Yrel|It pains me to lose an old friend, Grommash. But... very well. We will settle this your way.}} :{{text|emote|}} :{{text|say|Grommash Hellscream|Geya'rah, get everyone out of here. Now. No questions.}} :{{text|yell|Grommash Hellscream|For the Mag'har! For Draenor! Lok-tar ogar!}}

    ===Narrow Escape=== Description: Escape from Draenor with Eitrigg and the Mag'har. Escape from {{Au|Draenor}}

    Grommash and Lantresor charge the Lightbound lines. [[Eitrigg]] prepares to activate the shard of the [[:Vision of Time]].

    :{{text|yell|Eitrigg|Gather round quickly! We have only one chance!}} :{{text|yell|Overlord Geya'rah|No! I will stand with my Warchief!}} :{{text|yell|Eitrigg|Do not let his sacrifice be in vain! We go! NOW!}} :{{text|say|Overlord Geya'rah|Die well, Grommash Hellscream.}}

    There the scenario ends, and the survivors - orcs, ogres and gronn alike - materialize at [[Rocktusk Farm]] outside of [[Orgrimmar]]. Behind the group, [[botani]] and [[saberon]] can be seen running southwest into the [[Northern Barrens]], and [[goren]] south into [[Durotar]]. Turn in to Geya'rah.
