    ==Objectives== Speak to [[Rangari Rajess]] to fly to the [[Iron Approach]] and attack the [[Iron Horde]]. Fly to the [[Iron Docks]] {{co|52.8|59.3|Gorgrond}} The Iron Approach completed {{co|44.1|15.7|Gorgrond}}

    ==Description== With the [[Heart of the Magnaron]], we wield a powerful weapon against the Iron Horde.

    They amass far to the north at the Iron Approach. This will not be easy, but we must seize the opportunity to deal a blow to the Iron Horde while we possess the artifact.

    We are prepared to depart at your command. Speak with Rangari Rajess when you are ready.

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: {{itembox|Item level 548 trinkets|Goc's Trophy|Genesaur's Greatness|Commander Gar's Iron Insignia}} You will also receive: [[Outpost Building Assembly Notes]] and {{cost|1|89}} 1410 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== We must eradicate the Iron Horde.

    ==Completion== We have dealt a great blow to the Iron Horde, commander. We will defeat them yet.

    ==Notes== Head to the flight master: :Even the skies can be dangerous in Gorgrond. Be on your guard. :{{gossip|I am needed urgently at the Iron Docks.}} This begins a single-player scenario. As it can only be done once, it is documented here.

    ===Stage 1: The Confrontation=== :''Speak with Commander Gar. *Speak with Commander Gar {{co|44.7|17.8|Gorgrond}}

    Speak with Yrel: :I'm glad you made it here safely. Are you ready? {{gossip|''Let's go.''}}

    :{{text|say|Yrel|The Iron Horde have no idea how much potential lies within the artifact. Let us give them a proper demonstration, shall we?}} :{{text|say|Commander Gar|Ahh, there it is. There's MY artifact. How foolish of me to trust Brak with something so valuable.}} :{{text|say|Commander Gar|It's a shame that you're too late to stop me. Surrender the artifact, and I will allow you to escape with your lives.}} :{{text|yell|Yrel|We will never surrender to you! Champion, now is the time! Use the artifact!}} :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|Hahahaha, so be it! IRON HORDE, ADVANCE!}}

    ===Stage 2: Harnessing the Power=== :''Use the artifact to eliminate your enemies.'' *Use the artifact to eliminate your enemies {{co|44.7|17.8|Gorgrond}}

    The bonus access button appears: *{{abilities|Accessing Primal Artifact|notes=Instant|cd=1 min}}

    Using the bonus action button will summon a primal for 30 seconds that can defeat nearly everything on the field. Once everything's dead, the next stage begins.

    ===Stage 3: Bolstering their Forces=== :''Defeat the incoming enemies using the power of the artifact.'' *Defeat incoming enemies {{co|44.1|15.7|Gorgrond}} :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|Gronnlings, attack!}}

    Several waves of forces charge in. When the cooldown on the bonus action button comes up, use it again. On a timer, Gar will release some iron stars. Just like in the [[Garrosh Hellscream (tactics)|Garrosh Hellscream]] encounter, dodge them. Fortunately, there's no wall for them to run in to and explode, though. :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|RELEASE THE IRON STARS!}}

    Once the gronnlings are dead, the next stage begins.

    ===Stage 4: The Son of Gruul=== :''Defeat Goc.'' *Defeat Goc {{co|44.1|15.7|Gorgrond}} :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|You will never withstand the might of Goc!}}

    As before, wait for the bonus action button to come off cooldown and use it to finish the fight.

    ===Final Stage: The Final Confrontation=== :''Defeat Commander Gar.'' *Defeat Commander Gar {{co|44.1|15.7|Gorgrond}} :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|NO! I will DIE before I let you take what is mine! YOU... WILL NOT... WIN... THIS... WAR! AAARRRRGH!}} :''Gar manages to launch an artillery attack which destroys the artifact!'' :{{text|say|Yrel|The artifact... What has he done?! We must restore it, we must get it to safety!}} : ''Gar runs forward to engage.'' :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|Hahaha! Not so confident now, are we?! Face me on even ground, cowards!}} :{{text|say|Yrel|With pleasure.}} :{{text|say|Yrel|Brace yourself, champion. The light is with you. I will not let you fall.}}

    Gar will still release Iron Stars on a timer, but also has his own special ability, launching a barrage of attacks from his person, attacking everything indiscriminately within 40 yards or so. :{{text|yell|Commander Gar|See what weapons I have in my arsenal!}}

    Finish him off.

    ===Finale=== : {{text|say|Yrel|It is over, their defenses are shattered. We have won this battle, but without the artifact we have far to go.}} : {{text|say|Yrel|Let us rally back at your outpost, commander. We must plan our next course of attack.}}

    Run down the road south and go around the last tower to find Nisha, serving as the flight master for the area. This flight point also connects directly to Highpass.
