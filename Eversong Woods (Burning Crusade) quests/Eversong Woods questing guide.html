    Eversong Woods questing guide

    {{tocright}} This '''questing guide for the zone of Eversong Woods''' details an efficient method to obtain and complete the quests in [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]].

    For a list of all Eversong Woods quests in a sortable table, see [[Eversong Woods quests]].

    [[File:Icon-warning-22x22.png|Note]] '''Note''': The questing guides are not meant for power-leveling purposes. Rather, they are meant to show players the most efficient way to complete all quests available for a particular zone. If you know of a better order or method to accomplish this, your input is welcome (in fact, greatly desired). For major changes, feel free to edit, but make a note in the '''[[Talk:Eversong Woods questing guide|talk page]]''' as to your reasoning.

    =={{Horde_Icon}} Sunstrider Isle (Level 1 - 5)== [[Sunstrider Isle]] is the starting zone for new [[Blood elf|blood elves]], and is located north west of [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]]. The quests in this area are not available to players of other races. If you are new to the game, check out the [[Newbie Guide]] to get started, or just dive in - the game has in-game hints and the opening quests are very straight-forward, just relax and have fun!

    Begin by talking to the first [[blood elf]] you see, [[Magistrix Erona]], to get the quest [[Reclaiming Sunstrider Isle]]. Head east or west to a [[Burning Crystal]] and kill 8 [[Mana Wyrm]]s. Return to Magistrix Erona and turn in the quest. Then get [[Unfortunate Measures]] and your [[Class Training]] quest. Head to the [[Sunspire]] to sell your loot and locate your class trainer. At your class trainer complete your class quest and receive your first training. {{ClassIcon|Warlock|Small}} '''Warlocks''' also pick up [[Windows to the Source]] Go to [[Well Watcher Solanian]] at the upper level of the spire to start [[Solanian's Belongings]] and [[The Shrine of Dath'Remar (quest)]]. At the Burning Crystal, inspect the glowing book sitting near the crystal's base and loot {{item|Solanian's Journal}}. Go back to Magistrix Erona, on the way continue to grind springpaws and mana wyrms until you have 8 collars and 6 slivers. Turn in [[Unfortunate Measures]]. Accept [[Report to Lanthan Perilon]]. Complete [[Thirst Unending]] and [[A Fistful of Slivers]]. *Report to [[Lanthan Perilon]] to the west and accept [[Aggression]].

    Head further west towards the shore until you reach the [[Shrine of Dath'Remar]], killing [[Tender]]s and [[Feral Tender]]s you come across. Read the plague on the shrine. Head southeast to the fountain and inspect the glowing scroll on the ground, looting {{item|Scroll of Scourge Magic}}. Afterwards, follow the road east until you reach a pool of water with a platform. On the platform is {{item|Solanian's Scrying Orb}}. Loot it and head back to Lanthan Perilon. Here you have the chance to grind more Tenders and Feral Tenders, if you have not already slain 7 of each. Turn in [[Aggression]] and accept [[Felendren the Banished (quest)]]. *Head back to the Sunspire to turn in [[The Shrine of Dath'Remar]] and [[Solanian's Belongings]]. Learn new class abilities if you haven't already.

    Head southwest to [[Falthrien Academy]]. Kill [[Arcane Wraith]]s and [[Tainted Arcane Wraith]]s as you ascend to the top of the academy. On the top platform kill [[Felendren the Banished]] and loot [Felendren's Head]. The Tainted Arcane Wraiths drop a {{item|Tainted Arcane Sliver}}. Inspect it and start [[Tainted Arcane Sliver (quest)]]. {{ClassIcon|Warlock|Small}} '''Warlocks''' be sure to loot 4 {{item|Wraith Essence}} and 1 {{item|Tainted Wraith Essence}}. Turn in all quests and get [[Aiding the Outrunners]] from Lanthan Perilon.

    =={{Horde Icon}} Eversong Woods (Level 5 - 10)== [[Eversong Woods (Burning Crusade)|Eversong Woods]] is the main zone for beginning blood elves, once they graduate from [[Sunstrider Isle]]. To get here, head over the bridge on the south-east corner of Sunstrider Isle.

    Other zone options for this level include [[Durotar]], [[Mulgore]] and [[Tirisfal Glades]].

    Speak with [[Outrunner Alarion]] and accept [[Slain by the Wretched]]. Continue down the road until you find the [[Slain Outrunner]], finish the quest and start [[Package Recovery]]. Bring {{item|Erona's Package}} to Alarion and get the follow-up [[Completing the Delivery]] Follow [[Dawning Lane]] all the way through the [[Ruins of Silvermoon]] until you reach [[Falconwing Square]]. Complete [[Completing the Delivery]] with [[Innkeeper Delaniel]] and rebind your [[hearthstone]]. In the inn you can also learn: [[Cooking]] from {{npc|Horde|Quarelestra}} [[First Aid]] from {{npc|Horde|Kanaria}} [[Jewelcrafting]] {{npc|Horde|Aleinia}} {{ClassIcon|Priest|Small}} '''Priests''' should speak to [[Ponaris]] in the same building and pick up [[Cleansing the Scar]]. Head to the other building. Read the wanted poster and accept [[WANTED: Thaelis the Hungerer (quest)]]. Left of the sign is [[Aeldon Sunbrand]]. Talk to him and start [[Unstable Mana Crystals]]. Inside the building you can receive weapon training from [[Duelist Larenis]]. Speak with [[Magister Jaronis]] and pick up [[Major Malfunction]]. If you're close to leveling, explore the roads to the north and east, [[Skulking Row]] and [[Feth's Way]]. The experience from discovery should set you over the edge, opening up more abilities for you to learn before heading out.

    Head out into the [[Ruins of Silvermoon]], looting {{item|Unstable Mana Crystal|Unstable Mana Crystals}} as you find them (these appear as barrels of shiny rocks). Kill [[Arcane Patroller]]s for their {{item|Arcane Core|Arcane Cores}}. As you make your way through the ruins, head for the [[Commons Hall]] on the north-eastern side of the ruins, where you can find [[Thaelis the Hungerer]]. If you approach his camp from the east or west, you can pull one Wretched and him away from the rest of the group guarding the hall. Once you have everything you need, return to [[Falconwing Square]] and turn in all three quests. *Accept [[Darnassian Intrusions]] from [[Aeldon Sunbrand]] and [[Delivery to the North Sanctum]] from [[Magister Jaronis]].

On the way to [[Fairbreeze Village]], speak with [[Apprentice Ralen]] and start [[Roadside Ambush]]. Follow the road south and speak to [[Apprentice Meledor]], completing the quest you just got in exchange for [[Soaked Pages]]. Dive into the river and fetch the pages from the "Soaked Tome" and return them to Meledor and accept [[Taking the Fall]]. Head north, then east to the [[The Dead Scar]] and speak with {{npc|Horde|Ranger Jaela}} to start [[The Dead Scar (quest)]]. Kill {{mob|level=5 - 6|Plaguebone Pillager}}s until you have completed the quest, then return to Ranger Jaela and turn it in. Continue east to [[Stillwhisper Pond]] to speak with [[Instructor Antheol]] and complete [[Taking the Fall]] and accept [[Swift Discipline (quest)]]. Use {{item|Antheol's Disciplinary Rod}} on the two apprentices and report back for a bag. You can leave the road to the north to learn: [[Leatherworking]] from {{npc|Horde|Sathein}} [[Skinning]] from {{npc|Horde|Mathreyn}} * Visit Falconwing Square once more and turn in [[The Dwarven Spy]], accept the follow up [[Fairbreeze Village (quest)]].

    ===Fairbreeze Village and Saltheril's Haven=== {| class="darktable" width="80%" !width="40%"| Quest Name !width="40%"| Quest Giver ! Subzone

    Once at [[Fairbreeze Village]], pick up the following quests (you will need to be level 8 for most of them): Speak with [[Velan Brightoak]] and pick up [[Pelt Collection]]. At the largest building, start [[Saltheril's Haven (quest)]] and [[The Wayward Apprentice]] from [[Magistrix Landra Dawnstrider]]. Downstairs, rebind your Hearthstone with [[Marniel Amberlight]], starting [[Ranger Sareyn (quest)]], and start [[The Scorched Grove (quest)]] from [[Ardeyn Riverwind]]. Upstairs, complete [[Fairbreeze Village]] with [[Ranger Degolien]], following up with [[Situation at Sunsail Anchorage]]. Head west to [[Saltheril's Haven]] and complete [[Saltheril's Haven]]. Accept the follow up quest [[The Party Never Ends]]. The following [[profession]]s are available at [[Saltheril's Haven]]: [[Alchemy]] from {{npc|Horde|Arcanist Sheynathren}} [[Enchanting]] from {{npc|Horde|Magistrix Eredania}} [[Herbalism]] from {{npc|Horde|Botanist Tyniarrel}} *[[Tailoring]] from {{npc|Horde|Sempstress Ambershine}}

    Follow the road west and kill any [[Springpaw Stalker]]s as you see them, looting their {{item|Springpaw Pelt|Springpaw Pelts}}. Before you reach the harbor, you will see a fortified camp to your right. Go there and start [[Grimscale Pirates!]] and [[Lost Armaments]]. Down the road to the [[Sunsail Anchorage]], collect {{item|Sin'dorei Armaments}} from the crates scattered around the area. Once you have enough armaments, kill any remaining wretched needed for [[Situation at Sunsail Anchorage]]. Return to the fortified camp and turn in the armaments to get the next quest, [[Wretched Ringleader]]. Climb to the top of the anchorage tower and defeat {{npc|combat|Aldaron the Reckless}}. Don't forget to loot his head. Cross the river to the north and head west along it until you encounter some murlocs. Start grinding them, collecting [[Grimscale Murloc Head]]s for [[Fish Heads, Fish Heads...]] and [[Captain Kelisendra's Cargo]] for [[Grimscale Pirates!]]. Once you have enough heads, return to [[Hathvelion Sungaze]] to the east and turn in the quest, then pick up the follow up [[The Ring of Mmmrrrggglll]]. Cross the river to the south and kill [[Mmmrrrggglll]], return to [[Hathvelion Sungaze]] to turn the quest in. If the murlocs did not drop {{item|Captain Kelisendra's Lost Rutters}} by now, to start a quest of the same name, keep killing then until they do *Turn in [[Wretched Ringleader]], [[Captain Kelisendra's Lost Rutters]] and [[Grimscale Pirates!]] at the fortified camp.

    Head on to Saltheril's Haven to turn in [[The Party Never Ends]]. Head south the [[Goldenbough Pass]], speak to [[Larianna Riverwind]] to complete [[The Scorched Grove]]and accept the follow up [[A Somber Task]]. While grinding tenders, you will see the named mob [[Old Whitebark]], kill him to start [[Old Whitebark's Pendant (quest)]] from the item of the same name. Return to [[Larianna Riverwind]] to finish [[A Somber Task]] and [[Old Whitebark's Pendant (quest)]] and accept [[Whitebark's Memory]]. Use the pendant near the runestone in the Scorched Grove to summon Whitebark's Spirit, during the following fight it will turn friendly and allow you to complete [[Whitebark's Memory]] Head east to the next runestone and talk to Runewarden Deryan to complete the quest of that name and start [[Powering our Defenses]]. While continuing east complete [[Missing in the Ghostlands]], and accept [[The Fallen Courier]]. Further east, at the third runestone, finish [[Powering our Defenses]].

    Complete [[The Magister's Apprentice]] and accept [[Deactivating the Spire]] and [[Where's Wyllithen?]] from [[Apprentice Loralthalis]]. First find Wyllithen up north the path and then start and complete [[Cleaning up the Grounds]]. Enter the [[Duskwither Spire]] using the orb of translocation, deactivate the three crystals and pick up [[Abandoned Investigations]] from the journal there. Use the orb of translocation on the top level to leave. Head back to Apprentice Loralthalis to finish [[Deactivating the Spire]]. *Return to Farstrider Retreat to turn in [[Word from the Spire]] and [[Abandoned Investigations]].

Go to [[Knight-Lord Bloodvalor]] in Silvermoon City to finish this quest and accept [[The First Trial]]. Travel to the Ghostlands to the [[Isle of Tribulations]], complete the quest objectives in the cave. *Return to Silvermoon City, turn in the quest and accept and complete [[A Study in Power]], [[Claiming the Light]] and [[Redeeming the Dead]].
