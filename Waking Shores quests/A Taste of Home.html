    {{Questbox | id = 70159 | name = A Taste of Home | faction = Horde | disambigcat = Waking Shores | displaycat = The Waking Shores | category = Waking Shores | race = Dracthyr | level = {{Level|Waking Shores}} | start = [[Talon Scaralesh]] | experience = 9,050 | money = {{cost|25|74}} }}

    ==Objectives== Ask around members of the [[Horde]] at [[Dragonscale Basecamp]] to see if they can loan you the ingredients you need. [[Bilgewater-Imported Imitation Crab|Serving of Protein]] [[Fine Suramar Vintage|Emulsifying Liquid]] *[[Charred Zandali Chilis|Serving of Vegetable Matter]]

    ==Description== Oh, finally! Another [[dracthyr]] to lend a hand.

    [[Talon Rommen|My brother]] and [[Talon Scaralesh|I]] have been trying to cook up some rations to eat, but...

    Everything is so different around here. It's hard to find the same ingredients we used to cook with.

    But we can adapt. Go ask the Horde if they have anything we can substitute for vegetable matter, protein, and emulsifying fluid. Then we'll really have a meal.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|25|74}} * 9,050 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Any luck, ?

    ==Completion== They don't look much like they did back home, but we dracthyr are resourceful. We'll give it a try.

    Who knows, maybe it'll end up being even better.
