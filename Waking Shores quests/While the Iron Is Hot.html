    '''While the Iron Is Hot''' concludes the Renown 11 chapter of the [[Iskaara Tuskarr]] campaign.

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Zhurtan the Riverboiler.jpg|Zhurtan the Riverboiler|Kill Zhurtan the Riverboiler.}} Slay Zhurtan the Riverboiler. *[[Zhurtan the Riverboiler]] slain {{co|48.6|46.0|Waking Shores}}

    ==Description== I spoke to our lookouts on the way down. They say this [[djaradin]]--Zhurtan the Riverboiler--has begun his heinous ritual southwest of here, close to the waterfalls where many of our [[ottuk]]s build their nests.

    If they're boiling the river upstream from us now, I assume they've decided to be done with us more quickly. We would be forced to starve or retreat. We will do neither.

    They've prodded us like cowards afraid of a true fight. So let's bring the fight to them this time, shall we?

    ==Rewards== The following will be cast on you: *{{abilities|[[Ottuk Taming]]|Allows hunters to tame ottuks within the Dragon Isles.}}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|56|18}} * [[Tutaqan's Commendation]] * Completion of "The Chieftain's Duty" for [[Friend of the Dragon Isles]]

    ==Progress== We have your back, as you have ours.

    ==Completion== Our river--and our people--could not have been saved without your help.

    The [[Uktulut tribe]] owes you a great debt.
