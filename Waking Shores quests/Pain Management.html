    ==Description== It is a good thing you brought him here when you did. I can tell he is in a great deal of pain, though he bears it well. So must we all when it comes time.

    I am afraid that it is age that ails him and not illness, but I may be able to relieve some portion of his suffering.

    Would you mind gathering some snapflowers, shallowlilies, and pure water from the garden nearby? I can create a pain medicine with these components, if you find enough.
