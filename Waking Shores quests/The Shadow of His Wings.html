    '''The Shadow of His Wings''' concludes the self-named chapter of the [[Sojourner of the Waking Shores]] side-storyline.

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Cygenoth.jpg|Cygenoth|Self-appointed Voice of Neltharion and leader of the Worldbreakers.}} Confront Cygenoth, Voice of Neltharion. [[Cygenoth]] the Corrupted confronted {{co|35.0|45.5|Waking Shores}} Cygenoth the Corrupted slain

    ==Description== The Voice of Neltharion resides below, spreading lies amongst his followers.

    He appears to be [[dragonspawn]] like me.{{sic}} but his [[Draconic]] is clumsy--fit for the unscaled.

    I suspect duplicity: an illusion, perhaps. If we can subdue him, I'm certain the truth will out.

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Neltharian Channeler's Rod |Obsidian Watcher's Crossbow |Obsidian Guardian's Cleaver |Obsidian Spellsplitter |Doomsayer's Greatsword |Blacktalon Claw |Blacktalon Blade }}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|51|48|}} * 11,350 [[XP]] * +150 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]

    ==Completion== We quenched the last embers of the [[Twilight's Hammer]] today. But the [[Worldbreakers]] are another matter--these scaleborn will find new explanations for their misfortune, new myths, and new leaders.

    My kin and I will watch over them. Should another Voice rise, we will intervene.

    You've done a good thing this day.

    Completing the quest also awards the "Shadow of His Wing" criterion of [[Sojourner of the Waking Shores]]. Additionally, a new weekly quest, [[Same as the Old Boss]], is available on the surface near [[Igys the Believer]].
