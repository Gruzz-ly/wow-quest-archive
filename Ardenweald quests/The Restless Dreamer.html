    The Restless Dreamer

    {{Questbox | id = 58589 | name = The Restless Dreamer | category = Ardenweald | level = {{level|Ardenweald}} | start = [[Dreamweaver]] {{co|60.1|53.0|Ardenweald}} | experience = 6,800 | money = {{cost|25|27|20}} | shareable = No | previous = [[For the Sake of Spirit]] | next = [[Visions of the Dreamer: Origins]], [[Visions of the Dreamer: The Betrayal]] }}

    ==Objectives== Attune the filled heart of the tree at the shrines in the [[Dreamshrine Basin]]. * South Dreamshrine attuned {{co|61.2|56.7|Ardenweald}} * North Dreamshrine attuned {{co|57.8|50.0|Ardenweald}} * Heart Bound to [[Dreamer's Wildseed]] {{co|60.0|52.9|Ardenweald}}

    ==Description== Now that we have the [[anima]] we need, we must attune the heart of [[Tirna Noch]] to the shrines of [[Hibernal Hollow]].

    Then we can bring the [[wildseed]]'s nightmares into the light and dispel them.

    Take the heart to the shrines just outside of Hibernal Hollow, in the [[Dreamshrine]] basin. Then bring the heart back to the wildseed to complete the preparations for the ritual.

    But be careful. Minions of the [[Drust]] seem to be amassing near the grove.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|25|27|20}} * 6,800 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== The spirit dreams.

    ==Completion== You're a natural at this. Now we can bring the spirit's nightmares into the open and deal with them directly.

    If we succeed, she will be so much more at peace.

    ==Notes== On accept, players gain a bonus ability: :{{Abilities|Anima Expulsion|range=100|cd=20 sec|Expel anima from the Heart, damaging all enemies within 15 yards for x Nature damage.|id=329131|icon=spell_animaardenweald_nova}}

    Pick up both quests (and [[Despoilers]]) before heading out.

    Down to the south is the first [[Unattuned Dreamshrine]]. Interact with it, and it transforms into [[Dreamer's Vision]] and a few small [[Disturbed Nightmare]]s pop out: :{{text|say|Dreamweaver|I've seen spirits have nightmares before, but nothing like this. The trauma this one must have been through...}}

    Far to the northwest is the second Dreamshrine. Interact with it and a large Disturbed Nightmare pops out: :{{text|say|Dreamweaver|Well, it's pretty clear that this spirit won't dream peacefully until we confront what haunts it.}}

    Once all objectives are done, return to Dreamweaver and interact with the Wildseed. :{{Text|say|Dreamweaver|Good. All that's left is to begin the ritual.}}

    ;Dreamweaver dialogue {{Gossip|Tell me about the spirit in the wildseed.}}

    :Within [[Young Wildseed|this wildseed]] rests a powerful agent of nature. When they died, their spirit was brought directly to Ardenweald.

    :It is our honor to care for them as they dream and prepare for rebirth on their world.

    :The interesting thing is, though... they seem to know you. Isn't that something?

    {{Gossip|Tell me more about the ritual.}} :This ritual is meant to help restless spirits contend with the troubles that keep them from recovering their strength.

    :I have a sense that this spirit, the one so attached to you, has been through a great trial in their life. I hope that we can bring her back into a peaceful slumber.
