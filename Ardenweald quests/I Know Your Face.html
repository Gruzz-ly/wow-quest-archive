    ==Description== This voice... I have heard it before. Though it is distorted and vengeful now.

    He hunts us.

    But I am not prey. And I will not let him gloat over his slain quarry. That is not the way of the [[Wild Hunt]].

    Though he may draw us to ground of his choosing, we will fight him.

    And we will win. Are you with me?

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will receive one of: |Dreamthorn Spaulders |Grove Warden's Shoulderplates |Spirit Tender's Mantle |Starshroud Shoulderpads }}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|49|60|80}} * 8,500 [[XP]]

    ==Completion== It cannot be easy for Korayn to hunt one of our own... but she is right. The forest is not safe so long as Lorkron lurks within it.

    What a terrible fate... both for those who become possessed, and those who must hunt them down.

    ==Notes== Head west to the large clearing. Lorkron is riding a [[Blighted Monstrosity]]. Aggro it: :{{text|yell|Lorkron the Hunter|Your hunt ends here, Korayn!}}

    Korayn's ability from before works on the monstrosity and on Lokron... Use it on cooldown for some free damage! :{{Abilities|Hunter's Strike|Tell Korayn to charge and strike a Blighted Brute.|range=30|cd=12 sec|notes=Instant.     Defeat the monstrosity and Lorkron hops off: :{{text|say|Lorkron the Hunter|I serve a new master! One who will watch Ardenweald wither!}} :{{text|say|Hunt-Captain Korayn|Then you are a huntsman no longer.}}

    Get Lorkron below 50% health: :{{text|yell|Lorkron the Hunter|No! I will not fall to my prey again. Beware the shadows, Korayn...}} :{{text|emote|Lorkron the Hunter makes his escape!}} :''The player has the Vile Roots debuff, stunning them.''

    Speak with Korayn: : :{{gossip|Who was that? What now?}} :{{text|say|Hunt-Captain Korayn|A dear friend... twisted into a dire foe. He must be slain.}} :{{text|say|Hunt-Captain Korayn|Return to Glitterfall Basin. My hunt has only just begun.}}

    Head northeast up the hill to return to Glitterfall Basin. Turn in to Ara'lon.
