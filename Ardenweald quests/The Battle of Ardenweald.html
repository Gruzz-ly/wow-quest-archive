    ==Objectives== Protect the [[Winter Queen|Winter Queen's]] sigil in the Battle of [[Ardenweald]]. Mount the Etherwyrm outside the Enclave {{co|52.8|89.9|Oribos}} Fight in the Battle of Ardenweald

    ==Description== With the betrayal of [[Denathrius|Sire Denathrius]], and the absence of the [[Primus]] of [[Maldraxxus]], we must assume that the Winter Queen is [[The Jailer|the Jailer's]] next target.

    And we won't let anyone take her [[sigil]]!

    Ardenweald has endured much, but it is a realm of great and mysterious power. Things aren't always what they seem.

    We'll use this to our advantage, no matter who comes for the Queen!

    ==Rewards== {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards: |Faesworn Barb |Faesworn Spellblade |Faesworn Warclub |Faesworn Gavel |Faesworn Falchion |Faesworn Glaive |Faesworn Warbow |Faesworn Warmaul |Faesworn Reaper |Faesworn Channeling Root }}

    But it's not over. We can't let our guard down!

    ==Notes== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Etherwyrms.jpg|thumb|Etherwyrms at Oribos.]] On accept: :{{text|say|[[Highlord Bolvar Fordragon]]|Both Ardenweald and Oribos could come under assault. If there is even a chance to save [[Anduin]]...}} :{{text|say|Highlord Bolvar Fordragon|I understand, Jaina. We depart at once.}}

    Follow the party out to the southern tip of [[Oribos]], where three [[Etherwyrm (anima wyrm)|Etherwyrms]] await. Covenant troops continuously run up to the left and right ones, mount up, and fly into the gateway to Ardenweald. Follow their example by interacting with the central wyrm to enter the Battle of Ardenweald scenario.

    ===Stage 1: Bumpy Arrival=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Bumpy Arrival.jpg|thumb|Mawsworn Realmbreakers flying in Ardenweald]] :''Fly through the aerial onslaught and arrive in [[Glitterfall Basin]].'' *Reach Defense Forces :{{text|say|[[Lady Moonberry]]|[[Sylvanas Windrunner]] herself is leading the [[Mawsworn]] attack! We can't let her steal our [[covenant]] sigil!}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Several night fae have volunteered to disguise themselves as the Winter Queen. These decoys will scatter across the forest to misdirect the Banshee.}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|That should keep her occupied until the [[Wild Hunt]] can push back the Mawsworn and focus their efforts on her. I just hope it's enough...}}

    Untold numbers of [[Mawsworn Realmbreaker]]s are flying in near [[Glitterfall Basin]], where Jaina is spamming arcane blasts to knock them out of the sky.

    If at any point, the [[Maw Walker]] should stray too far from the battle, [[Mawsworn Abductor]]s will grab them and bring them back to the action.

    ===Stage 2: Deploy the Decoys=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Deploy the Decoys.jpg|thumb|[[Marastar]] and [[Seaharper]] about to use an Animacone to disguise [[Glittermint]].]] :''Use the animacone to transform Glittermint into the Winter Queen.'' *Animacone used to create decoy {{co|51.2|33.1|Ardenweald}}

    A group of [[Wild Hunt Enchanter]]s are seen casting a spell on [[Sparklepuff]], disguising her as the Winter Queen.

    [[Niya]] has special gossip: :The fight has come to our home. We'll do everything we can to protect the Queen. :For Ardenweald!

    As does [[Hunt-Captain Korayn]]: :We will drive these interlopers from Ardenweald!

    The northern entrances are guarded by groups of [[Sinfall Defender]]s, [[Maldraxxi Gladiator]]s and [[Maldraxxi Lineguard]]s. Here, they make short work of incoming [[Mawsworn Swarmer]]s. If passed they will urge the Maw Walker to return. :{{Text|Say|Maldraxxi Gladiator|We've got this covered! Go help to the south!}}

    Run past Jaina: :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|One more to go. Oh, this glamour just has to work!}}

    [[File:Glittermint.png|thumb|[[Glittermint]].]] Speak with Moonberry: :We have to do everything we can to protect the Winter Queen! :Fortunately, I know a lot of tricks that should help buy us time, so we can drive the Maw out of Ardenweald! :{{gossip|What's the plan?}} :We're making look-alikes of the Winter Queen, to draw our enemies away from the real Queen. :We're making decoys! :Unfortunately there's only so many of them we could make. And we know that it's a dangerous job. But we're prepared to sacrifice everything to protect Ardenweald.

    Interact with the [[Animacone (object)|Animacone]] held by Seaharper and Marastar to empower it: :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|The true Winter Queen will be protecting the Heart of the Forest. It's up to us to stop the invaders!}} :{{text|say|[[Lord Herne]]|The ruse will only distract them for so long. We must end this battle swiftly.}}

    ===Stage 3: Maw Invasion=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Maw Invasion.jpg|thumb|Covenant defenders, Mawsworn Raiders, and Maw Portals.]] :''Defeat Maw forces and heal Wild Hunt.'' *Push back Maw forces (0/100%) :{{text|say|Sylvanas Windrunner|All forces, forward! Find the Winter Queen so I can claim that sigil!}} :{{text|say|Lord Herne|Wild Hunt, we ride!}} :{{text|say|[[Kleia]]|They already fill the skies...}} :{{text|say|[[Plague Deviser Marileth]]|What a beautiful realm! But where's all the slime? Not to worry, I can rectify this!}} :{{text|say|Kleia|Marileth, wait! We must fly together!}}

    Head south into the basin and start taking out [[Mawsworn Raider]]s, [[Mawsworn Gatekeeper (Ardenweald)|Gatekeepers]], and [[Mawsworn Interrogator (Ardenweald)|Interrogators]] for 4% progress each. Additionally, the [[Maw Portal]]s guarded by the gatekeepers can also be destroyed for 6% progress. Interact with the [[Injured Hunter (Ardenweald)|Injured Hunters]] for 6% progress and to gain them as a guardian (Wild Hunt Archer or Wild Hunt Guardian). While on this stage, players gain 5% health every few seconds. {{text|say|[[Wild Hunt Archer]]|Something large looms just beyond those gates...}} {{text|say|Wild Hunt Archer|The wildseeds are undefended. We must drive out the invaders!}} *{{text|say|[[Wild Hunt Guardian]]|They keep coming! How many gates can they open?}}

    ===Stage 4: The Banshee Queen=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Winter Queen idle.jpg|thumb|The [[Winter Queen]]?]] :''Confront Sylvanas Windrunner and stop her invasion of Ardenweald.'' *Confront Sylvanas Windrunner {{co|48.5|37.2|Ardenweald}} :{{text|say|Lord Herne|Sylvanas has been spotted near the falls!     Head southwest to where what looks to be the Winter Queen is standing out in the open, as a cutscene begins: :''Sylvanas swoops into the area in banshee form and lands in the hero pose on one knee, before getting to her feet, drawing an arrow, and firing it through the queen's heart.     [[File:Sylvanas Windrunner - Battle of Ardenweald.jpg|thumb|upright|Sylvanas ready to strike]] On the other side of the cutscene, Sylvanas stands idle, as a boss-level elite. With the player are Kleia, Lord Herne, Niya, and Hunt-Captain Korayn. Should players wait too long to engage her: :{{text|say|Sylvanas Windrunner|Well?}} :{{text|yell|Lord Herne|Strike now!}}

    Upon entering Sylvanas' aggro radius, she doesn't attack and the player is struck with the '''Merciful''' debuff. :{{Abilities|Merciful|You have not yet shown aggression toward Sylvanas. But the Banshee Queen is only so patient.|icon=achievement_leader_sylvanas|notes=15 sec debuff}} One of three things will happen.     Alternatively, if the player has completed [[Most Loyal]]: :{{text|say|Sylvanas Windrunner|You were loyal, once. You should know better than to interfere with me.}}

    If the player doesn't attack, and allows Merciful to wear off, Sylvanas will still stun the player and say: :{{text|say|Sylvanas Windrunner|You are wise to show restraint. But it changes nothing.}}

    [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Kleia healing.jpg|thumb|Kleia healing after Sylvanas's attack]] Sylvanas jumps back and prepares to enter Banshee Form: :{{text|say|Kleia|Hold on, I've got you!}} :''Kleia runs forward and casts Kleia's Shield in an attempt to shield the Maw Walker.     ===Stage 5: Air Reinforcements=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - Air Reinforcements.jpg|thumb|[[Plague Deviser Marileth]] astride a Trained Flayedwing]] :''Mount the [[Trained Flayedwing]] to fly to the top of the falls.'' *Mount Trained Flayedwing {{co|48.7|37.6|Ardenweald}} :{{text|yell|Sylvanas Windrunner|To the skies!     ===Stage 6: A Wing and a Slime=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - A Wing and a Slime.jpg|thumb|Fending off Mawsworn Realmbinders alongside Ysera.]] :''Launch slime at the Mawsworn to clear the skies.'' *Slime Mawsworn (30)

    Use it to take out the [[Mawsworn Realmbinder]]s hovering in the area, while [[Ysera]] casts Sleep at them: :{{text|say|Plague Deviser Marileth|Slime for you! Slime for you!}}

    After a bit, Ysera flies in: :{{text|say|Ysera|Begone, servants of the Maw!}}

    Take out thirty to continue.

    ===Stage 7: To the Top of the Falls=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - To the Top of the Falls.jpg|thumb|Fighting [[Soulburner Fraxin]].]] :''Clear Soulburner Fraxin from the falls.'' *Slay [[Soulburner Fraxin]] {{co|48.0|40.2|Ardenweald}} :{{text|say|Plague Deviser Marileth|Oh drat, I'm all out of slimes.     Take out Soulbinder Fraxin alongside Draven, Kleia, and Nadja.

    Head south uphill, taking out [[Mawsworn Eradicator]]s and [[Mawsworn Pillager|Pillagers]] on the way to the wildseeds in the area, including [[Asotera]], Might of Earth; [[Zoukai]], Ancient River; [[Malorandar]], Herald of the Dawn; [[Thynen]], Wind of the East; and [[Darkmute]], the Unknowable.

    As one is saved, a covenant combatant will run up: :{{text|say|Wild Hunt Guardian|Well hunted! We will secure this one.     Save four to continue.

    ===Stage 9: The Forest Answers=== [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - The Forest Answers.jpg|thumb|The southern rally point, with the Winter Queen in the background]] :''Summon allies at the Rally Points.'' *Rally Allies (2) {{co|47.9|46.2|Ardenweald}} {{co|49.1|43.6|Ardenweald}} :{{text|say|Sylvanas Windrunner|Surrender your sigil or the wildseeds will perish.}} :{{text|say|Winter Queen|A hollow threat from an empty soul.}}

    Just east, near the Heart of the Forest, are ghosts of two Rally Point banners. Inside an impassable barrier, the Winter Queen and Ysera are protecting a large number of wildseeds, with the Winter Queen channeling a '''Cosmic Starfall''' to shoot winged Mawsworn out of the sky and kill any ground troops that try to flee from her. Interact with both of the banners.

    [[File:Battle of Ardenweald - The Forest Answers northern.jpg|thumb|The northern rally point, with Sylvanas in the background]] To the north: :{{text|yell|Lady Moonberry|Get ready! I sense a terrible darkness looming on the other side of that huge gateway!}} :''Moonberry, Kleia, Dreamweaver, and Draven fly in, holding just short of Sylvanas, who is floating in banshee form, protected by a shield, and channeling something into the large Maw Gateway.''

    To the south: :{{text|yell|Lord Herne|The Mawsworn gather for a final assault! All forces, rally to me!}} :''Lord Herne, Hunt-Captain Korayn, Nadja the Mistblade, and Niya run in.''
