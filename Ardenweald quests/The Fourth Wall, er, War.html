    The Fourth Wall, er, War

    {{Questbox | image = The Fourth Wall.jpg | imagecaption = Getting ready to upset Ysera | id = 58158 | name = The Fourth Wall, er, War | faction = Night Fae | category = Ardenweald | level = {{level|sl-max}} | levelreq = {{level|sl-max}} | start = [[Featherlight]] {{co|40.7|42.8|Ardenweald}} | experience = 9,450 | money = {{cost|25|74|}} | previous = [[Break a Leg]] | next = [[What's My Motivation?]] }}

    ==Objectives== Set the Stage then help the reenactment of events after the [[Burning Legion|Legion]]'s fall. * Place [[Teldrassil]] * Place [[Azeroth]] * Become [[Sargeras]] * Stab the [[Silithus: The Wound|World]] * Become [[Nathanos Blightcaller|Nathanos]] * [[Burning of Teldrassil|Burn Teldrassil]] * Become [[Reverence]] * Upgrade your Costume * Trample [[Horde]] siege engines (15) * Remove your costume * Heal Azeroth's [[Wound]]s (5)

    ==Description== It's time for the next act of your theatrical introduction: the Grand Drama Of Azereth!

    Azeroth. Right. All these [[Planet|worlds]] are hard to tell apart.

    Help set the stage, then come back here. You'll need to play a few other characters before you appear as yourself again.

    Get a move on! We have a lot to cover!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|25|74|}} * 9,450 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Come on, come on! We have a crowd to please!

    ==Completion== I can honestly say I have never seen a performance quite like this.

    Now... On to our grand finale! A battle for your world that is certain to make the court sit up and take notice!

    ==Notes== [[File:The player as Sargeras.jpg|thumb|Wielding a very large sword]]

    On accept: :{{text|say|Featherlight|Help them set the stage. Quickly, now!}}

    On stage, interact with the the Azeroth prop behind the wall to pick it up. Your movement speed is slowed by the {{Abilities|Weight of the World|id=313449|icon=achievement_worldevent_childrensweek}}. Walk forward and place it at the marked spot at stage center, then plant the Teldrassil banner stage left: :{{text|say|Featherlight|Costume change! Get backstage!}}

    Speak with [[Satinstitch]]: :It's been short notice but I have something which might work well enough. :Mind the flammable bits of the stage. :{{gossip|Give me my costume, please}} :{{text|say|Featherlight|Good, now hit your mark!}}

    [[File:Azeroth impaled.jpg|thumb|Azeroth, impaled, though not quite in Silithus]]

    You are now {{Abilities|Portraying Sargeras|id=313458|icon=spell_shadow_curseofsargeras}}: You have flaming horns and wings and are holding a wooden replica of the [[Sword of Sargeras]]. Get to the marked location and use the bonus ability {{Abilities|Stab the World|id=313536|icon=ability_backstab}}. :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|But as the titans were imprisoning Sargeras, he plunged his sword into the heart of Azeroth!}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Fortunately, he didn't hit any major population centers.}} :{{text|say|Featherlight|Next costume!}} :{{text|yell|[[Ysera]]|This cannot be...}} :{{text|say|[[Awool]]|Wait, so there is a giant sword stuck in 's world? How does that work?}} :{{text|say|[[Choofa]]|It kinda goes in like "Shunk"?}} :{{text|say|[[Slanknen]]|I was an environmental protector in life! You can't just stab a planet without serious repercussions to, well, everything!}} :{{text|say|Choofa|Good point. How would you even make a sword that big anyway? Transmute another world?}} :{{text|say|Awool|No idea. I don't think I want to meet this Sargeras guy.}}

    Return to Satinstitch backstage: :I could have made the likeness better if I'd had more time. This shall have to do! :{{gossip|Give me my costume, please}}

    [[File:Sylvanas and Tyrande.jpg|thumb|Sylvanas and Tyrande]] [[File:Teldrassil burning.jpg|thumb|Teldrassil burning]]

    You are now {{Abilities|Portraying Nathanos|id=313557|icon=ability_rogue_disguise}}. Despite Satinstitch's worry, you appear exactly like Nathanos. Head back on stage, where [[Astra]], as [[Sylvanas]] {{title|A Warchief with Questionable Motives}} faces off against [[Mi'kai]], as [[Tyrande]] {{title|A Fierce Warrior-Princess}}: :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|Not long after, Sylvanas Windrunner besieged the night elf home of Teldrassil!}} :{{text|say|Astra, As Sylvanas|Lay siege to the World Tree!}} :{{text|emote|Ysera leans forward.}} :{{text|say|Mi'kai, As Tyrande|You cannot kill hope!}} :{{text|say|Astra, As Sylvanas|Can't I? All those souls!}} :{{text|say|Slanknen|Indeed, Choofa. This is a terrible tragedy.}} :{{text|say|Awool|Wait... They aren't here! I'd have noticed a big group like that!}} :{{text|say|Awool|... Oh...}} :{{text|emote|Ysera weeps.}}

    Head to the Teldrassil banner and use the bonus ability {{Abilities|Burning Teldrassil|id=313558|icon=inv_torch_lit}}, then head backstage once more and speak with [[Rickrack]]: :The costume you are wearing next is a character named [[Reverence]]. :I wanted to go for something avant-garde! Material. Tactile. Corporeal. :Something you can feel. Something that allows you to physically inhabit the character... :{{gossip|Give me my costume, please}}

    [[File:Reverence costume.jpg|thumb|Reverence, not quite living up to [[Reverence|the namesake]]]] [[File:Reverence costume 2.jpg|thumb|A slightly higher-fidelity costume of Reverence]]

    You become a {{Abilities|Pedestrian Equestrian|id=313591|icon=inv_misc_horsecostume_front}}; you're wearing the front half of a mediocre horse costume. :{{text|say|Rickrack|HA! My greatest work yet!}} :{{text|say|Satinstitch|It's a piece of work, all right.}}

    Speak with Satinstitch: :An intriguing idea, but I think this is a bit too avant-garde for this production. :We just need to change a little bit of this. : :Now, hold still! :{{gossip|}}

    You're now {{Abilities|A Noble Steed|id=313715|icon=spell_nature_swiftness}} instead, taking the form of a [[unicorn]]. Windy, as Anduin, appears on your back. :{{text|say|Windy, As Anduin|Forward ho, my noble steed!}}

    [[File:Adorable siege engines.jpg|thumb|Adorable siege engines]] Head back on stage as some downright ''adorable'' siege weapons are lined up, ready for a trampling. :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|The boy king of Stormwind assailed the undead city of Lordaeron.}} :{{text|say|Astra, As Sylvanas|You are a terrible leader, King Wrynn!}} :{{text|say|Windy, As Anduin|Well, you're a terrible person, Warchief!}} :{{text|say|Astra, As Sylvanas|How dare you!}}

    Run over fifteen of them: :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|But before he could capture Sylvanas, she blighted the land and made her escape!}} :{{text|say|Lady Moonberry|This made everyone very unhappy.}}

    Head backstage and speak with Satinstitch again: :And now back to your usual... outfit for the next act. :{{gossip|Please remove my costume.}}
