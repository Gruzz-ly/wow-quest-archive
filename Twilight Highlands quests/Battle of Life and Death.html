    Battle of Life and Death

    {{questbox | name=Battle of Life and Death | start=[[Calen]] {{co|14.2|15.5|Twilight Highlands}} | id=28758 | level={{level|Twilight Highlands}} | category=Twilight Highlands | end=[[Calen]] {{co|22.1|55.6|Twilight Highlands}} | rewards=[[Red Dragonheart Ring]] or [[Pendant of Victorious Fury]] or [[Cloak of the Red Flight]] | experience=86750 | money={{cost|18|80}} | previous=[[Enter the Dragon Queen]] | next=[[And the Sky Streaked Red]] }}

    [[File:Battle of Life and Death.jpg|thumb|Battle of Life and Death]]

    == Objectives == Mount a [[Vermillion Vanguard]]. Slay 8 Twilight Shadowdrakes. * [[Twilight Shadowdrake]] slain x8

    == Description == [[Alexstrasza]] and [[Deathwing]] have taken into the skies over [[Grim Batol]]! Her plan unravels - the Dragon Queen is outside the Circle of Life she prepared earlier. We'll need to support her with everything we've got.

    Hurry! Climb aboard one of the Vermillion Vanguard behind me.

    If we find any Twilight Drakes aiding Deathwing, we will need to kill them off. Mount up, quickly!

    == Rewards == {{itembox|You will be able to choose one of these rewards:|Red Dragonheart Ring|Pendant of Victorious Fury|Cloak of the Red Flight}} You will also receive: {{cost|18|80}} (or {{cost|20|67}} at max level)

    == Completion == Thanks to your quick action, the Dragon Queen still lives. She is being brought back to [[Wyrmrest Temple|Wyrmrest]] to recover.

    But the battle wasn't a complete failure. Deathwing was gravely wounded. I suspect he will go into hiding to recuperate. Alexstrasza has bought us time to strike!

    == Notes == Hop on one of the vanguard. It has the following abilities:

    {{abilities|Fireblast|range=60|Blasts a Twilight Shadowdrake for fire damage.|notes=1 sec cast}}

    {{abilities|Lifebinder's Boon|range=60|Heals both yourself and a targeted Vermillion Vanguard for six seconds. Also restores mana.|notes=Channeled}}

    {{abilities|Finishing Strike|range=30|Dives at a weakened Twilight Shadowdrake, tearing out its throat and finishing it.|notes=Instant}}

    It, and Calen, will take a flight to the peak of Grim Batol: :{{text|say|Calen|Don't let them out of sight! It will take one Fireblast and either another blast or a Finishing Strike to kill each drake.
