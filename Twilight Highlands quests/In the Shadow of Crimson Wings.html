    In the Shadow of Crimson Wings

    {{Questbox | id = 55657 | name = In the Shadow of Crimson Wings | image = In the Shadow of Crimson Wings.jpg | imagecaption = [[Vexiona]] stunning [[Kalecgos]] and [[Alexstrasza]] | category = Heart of Azeroth | level = {{level|bfa-max}} | levelreq = {{level|bfa-max}} | start = [[Kalecgos]] | end = [[Alexstrasza]] | reputation = +500 [[Champions of Azeroth]] | rewards = [[Tempered Scale of the Scarlet Broodmother]], [[Radiant Azerite Matrix]] (1,800x [[Azerite (resource)]]) | experience = 26,800 | money = {{cost|70|20|}} | shareable = No | previous = [[Investigating the Highlands]] }}

    ==Objectives== Speak with Kalecgos to fly to the [[Vermillion Redoubt]], locate Alexstrasza and discover the source of the threat. * Speak with [[Kalecgos]] * [[Alexstrasza]] reached * Threat discovered * [[Vexiona]] confronted * [[Lost Scale of the Scarlet Broodmother]] * Ride [[Corastrasza]] to [[Silithus: The Wound|Silithus]] (optional) *Meet Alexstrasza in the Chamber of Heart.

    ==Description== It is unlike Alexstrasza to remain out of contact for so long, particularly when the very essences of the [[dragonflight]]s' power are being made manifest.

    While our allies here tend to the injured, we must seek out the Life-Binder at once.

    When you are ready, we shall ride to the Vermillion Redoubt together.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|70|20|}} * 26,800 [[XP]] * +500 reputation with [[Champions of Azeroth]] [[Tempered Scale of the Scarlet Broodmother]] [[Radiant Azerite Matrix]] (1,800x [[Azerite (resource)]])

    ==Progress== We must rely on your aid once more, champion.

    ==Completion== We owe you a great deal of gratitude. You have the support of the [[red dragonflight]] in your efforts to save [[Azeroth]].
