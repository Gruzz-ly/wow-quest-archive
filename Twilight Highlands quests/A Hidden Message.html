    A Hidden Message

    {{questbox | name = A Hidden Message | level = | levelreq = | category = Rogue | start = Lord Devrestrasz | end = Corastrasza | money = 16g 54s | previous = Proving Your Worth | next = To Ravenholdt }}

    This rogue-only quest is the second part of the chain to obtain the legendary dagger set Fangs of the Father. It is a courier quest, sending the rogue to aid the red dragonflight in the Twilight Highlands.

    Objectives

    Charge your Cryptomancer's Decoder Ring and use it to decode the Corastrasza's Singed Cipher. * Decoder Ring Charged * Acquire the Singed Cipher * Solved Cipher * Cryptomancer's Decoder Ring (2) (provided)

    Description

    Corastrasza, currently stationed at the Vermillion Redoubt in the Twilight Highlands, has intercepted a coded message of grave importance. I'll let her explain the full situation, but we need your expertise to unravel the cipher!

    Take your decoder ring to an ethereal thaumaturge in your capital city - he should know how to charge it. Acquire the encoded cipher from Corastrasza and use the ring to decode it for her. Hurry! Time is ticking...

    Rewards

    You will receive: 16g 54s

    Notes

    Take the Cryptomancer's Decoder Ring (2) to a capital city to pay the ethereal 10,000 gold for the Charging Decoder Ring and wait 12 hours to charge it up in to the Charged Decoder Ring. Then head to the Twilight Highlands and fly to the Vermillion Redoubt. There, Corastrasza has relocated from her previous location at Coldarra in Borean Tundra. Interact with her, acquire the Singed Cipher and use the Charged Decoder Ring on it to create the Solved Cipher, then hand that cipher back in to Corastrasza.
