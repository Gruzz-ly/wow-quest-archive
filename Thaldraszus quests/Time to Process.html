    ==Objectives== Invite [[Tyr]] to join the [[Tyr's Guard]] at [[Tyr's Rest]]. Speak to Tyr Speak with [[Travard]]

    ==Description== Tyr is back after thousands of years. I don't even know how many thousands. His world has changed more than we can even imagine. He's going to have many questions and need answers.

    Meanwhile, there's nothing any of the Tyr's Guard would rather do than talk with Tyr. Tyr's Rest seems like the perfect place to give him time, answer questions, and process those answers. Would you ask him to join us there?

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 9,500 [[XP]] [[Dragon Isles Drakes: White Scales]] * 10x [[Flightstones]] [[Of the Tyr's Guard]]

    ==Progress== We certainly live in interesting times, ...

    ==Completion== It is so good to be able to talk with Tyr directly. We have so much to tell him, and I know we all want to ask him questions.

    Thank you for making this possible, !
