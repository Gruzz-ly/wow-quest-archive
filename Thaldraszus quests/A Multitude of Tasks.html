    Objectives

    Perform a series of tasks in the Everywhere Inn in Eon's Fringe. Talk to Chromie to come up with an alternative solution. Become a Quest Giver Deal with Adventurers Deal with another Adventurer Handle a Bronze Dragon Problem Send Adventurers off on their first journey *Wait for Chromie to return

    Description

    Hi ! I have a super exciting assignment for today. I need you to place seven loaves of bread around Stormwind, and five jugs of milk in Orgrimmar. You'll finish that up with...

    <Chromie looks at a note in her hand.>

    Seven hundred and forty two grains of rice scattered across Pandaria!
