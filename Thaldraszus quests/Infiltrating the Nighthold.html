    Objectives

    Retrieve the Disc of Tyr from Elisande in the Nighthold. * Teleport to the Nighthold * Retrieve the Disc of Tyr

    Description

    The information from our beacons suggests that Elisande has locked the disc away in Astromancer's Rise.

    It is a place of learning, and the inside will have minimal protection. I have opened a portal where you and the Tyr's Guard will have the least resistance... except for Elisande and whatever guards are under her employ.

    All of you are on your own once you get there, so try not to muck it up.

    Rewards

    You will receive: * 84g 27s * 14,300 XP * 10x Flightstones

    Progress

    I wonder what Tyr is like? I bet he's super nice!

    Completion

    Glad everyone made it, ! We have the disc, the :Silver Scale, and Tyr is reforged. Amazing, let's bring Tyr back!

    Notes

    Upon accept :

    Follow suit and take the Portal to Nighthold. Doing so starts the Retrieving the Disc scenario.

    Stage 1: Ready Check

    Speak with Travard to ensure everyone is ready. *Tell Travard you are ready

    Speak to Travard:

    We're ready when you are, . Let's take back Tyr's disc.

    ''The Tyr's Guards charge into the room, attacking the nightborne guards.''

    Stage 2 – Secure the Exits

    Defeat the guards in Elisande's research facility. *Astromancer's Guards defeated

    * *

    Defeat them. The paladins then face and stand in front of Observatory Stairs.

    Stage 3 – Defeat Senior Researcher Alydrus

    Defeat Senior Researcher Alydrus. *Senior Researcher Alydrus defeated

    There are also several Focused Researchers and Runas the Bright in the room, and on the ground are Alydrus' Research Notes for additional lore.

    Runas' gossip :I do not recognize you. But I sense we are somehow connected in a way I do not understand. Go in peace, outsider, and be safe.

    Travard's gossip :Be careful, --there's no telling what tricks we may yet encounter from Elisande.

    Open the door and attack Senior Researcher Alydrus on the stairs.

    And kill her:

    Stage 4 – Staircase Guards

    Clear the guards blocking the stairs. *Guards on stairs defeated.

    Follow the paladins upstairs and more nightborne guards appear. Kill them.

    Upon death

    Stage 5 – Guardian Ordan

    Elisande's Guardian, Ordan, blocks the way. *Guardian Ordan defeated

    Attack Ordan near the top of the stairs.

    And kill it:

    Stage 6 – Speak with Travard

    Speak with Travard before facing Elisande. *Tell Travard you are ready

    The paladins continue to the top and stand in front of Elisande's Research Chamber.

    Speak to Travard again:

    Through this door, we'll face Elisande and the disc, . Are you ready?

    Stage 7 – Elisande

    Face Elisande with the Tyr's Guard. *Elisande confronted

    The paladins charge at Elisande.

    Fight with her until she reaches 50%, she turns , and resets the attackers.

    Stage 8 – Speak with Talthis

    Speak with Talthis about his plan. *Hear Talthis' plan

    Speak to Talthis:

    A plan to defeat Elisande requires precision and focus.

    Both things I have in abundance, .

    So, how about this?

    Hadwin and I will attack first from the left and distract Elisande.

    Once we have her attention, everyone else can join.

    If she is distracted and our attack divided, she won't be able to throw all of us out!

    Ready?

    Stage 9 – Elisande

    Face Elisande with the Tyr's Guard. *0/1 Elisande confronted

    The group attacks according to Talthis' strategy, but Elisande casts Frozen in Time on him and Hadwin and focues on the rest of the group.

    At 50%, she repeats her rewind spell.

    Stage 10 – Speak with Nolaki

    Hear Nolaki's plan for the Tyr's Guard. *Hear Nolaki's plan

    That was a good plan, but Elisande is still a step ahead of us. We need to get two steps ahead of her to have a chance.

    Stage 11 – Elisande

    Tell Valunei you are ready. *Tell Valunei you are ready

    Speak to Valunei:

    Ready to do our part to get the disc, ?

    A cutscene plays:

    ''Valunei and the adventurer approach Elisande as she has trapped the rest of the paladins in time. Valunei kneels and removes her weapon and shield.''

    ''Valunei stands up.''

    ''Valunei steps towards Elisande.''

    ''Elisande glances behind her toward the disc and the trapped paladins.''

    ''Elisande cancels the time spell, opens a portal to Suramar, and walks towards it.''

    ''She stops in front of the portal and looks at Valunei.''

    ''Elisande then takes the portal.''

    Final Stage – Return to Tyrhold

    Return through the portal. *Portal to Tyrhold Taken

    ''The paladins take the Portal to Tyrhold.''

    Before taking the portal, Travard has briefly a new gossip:

    We finally have the disc.

    We can finally bring Tyr back to our world.

    Although... a part of me is apprehensive. What if Tyr isn't who I think he is? Or what if he doesn't approve of the Tyr's Guard?

    Well, the only way to find out is to get the disc back to Nozdormu. Shall we go?

    Follow suit and take the portal. Back in Tyrhold, Chromie greets, and the group is now joined by Alexstrasza.
