    Memories of Sindragosa and Malygos

    {{Questbox | id = 75023 | name = Memories of Sindragosa and Malygos | category = Blue Dragonflight | level = {{Level|Df-max}} | levelreq = {{Level|Df-max}} | start = [[Senegos]]{{Co|67.09|11.77|Thaldraszus}} | experience = 47,600 | rewards = 15x [[Flightstones]] | money = {{cost|28|9}} | previous = [[Sindragosa and Malygos's Rest]] | next = [[Archives Return]] }}

    ==Objectives== Dispel the lingering memories of [[Sindragosa]] and [[Malygos]]. [[Memory of a Betrayer]] dispelled [[Memory of a Master]] dispelled *[[Memory of a Traitorous Sister]] dispelled

    ==Description== I admit I forgot about this phenomenon. [[Blue dragon]]s who meet violent ends occasionally have some lingering [[arcane]] energy.

    That energy may manifest in various ways once their essences are laid to rest.

    Luckily it is a simple enough process to dispel them. Simply wave anything through the energy and it will dissipate.

    I shall investigate the essences of Sindragosa and Malygos within this gravestone while you handle that.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 47,600 [[XP]]

    {{itembox||q1=15|Flightstones }}

    ==Progress== How strange that these memories still linger...

    ==Completion== That should do it, though it is odd not all of the memories were dispelled as quickly as I had hoped...

    Let us speak with [[Kalecgos]] to let him know we should keep an eye on this.
