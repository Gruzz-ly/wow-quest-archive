    '''A Sliver of Silver''' concludes the "Tyr's Fall" storyline of the [[Embers of Neltharion campaign]]. In [[patch 10.1.7]], the storyline continues with the "Reforging the Tyr's Guard" storyline of the [[Fury Incarnate campaign]].

    ==Objectives== Escape [[Tomb of Tyr|the tomb]] and return to the [[Dragon Aspects|Aspects]] at Tyrhold in [[Valdrakken]]. Use the [[Titan Transporter]] to return to [[Tyrhold]]. Return to the Aspects [[:Silver Scale]] activated Speak to [[Nozdormu]] at Tyrhold in Valdrakken.

    ==Description== [[Tyr]]'s final memories are in our hands. This is not a responsibility to be taken lightly.

    Let us take the Silver Scale to Nozdormu in Tyrhold. We will attempt to use the console there to awaken him.

    If he is whole again, there will be much to discuss.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|28|9}} * 47,600 [[XP]] * [[Titan's Containment Device]] * 10x [[Flightstones]] * +2500 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]

    ==Progress== We will do whatever we must to restore Tyr.

    ==Completion== Thank you, .
