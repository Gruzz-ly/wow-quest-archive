    Objectives

    Use the Titan Console to witness Tyr's final memories. Place the Silver Scale in the Titan Console Witness Tyr's Fall *Silver Scale (provided)

    Description

    This console will allow us to examine the scale's contents, but I do not know what we will find.

    The other titan servants here should leave us alone; accessing it should not be as disruptive as transferring what we sensed of Tyr.

    Something is making Travard uneasy. Be prepared for anything, .

    Place the Silver Scale in the console when you are ready to proceed.

    Rewards

    You will receive: 56g 18s 71,350 XP * 15x Flightstones

    Progress

    There is much we still do not know about Tyr.

    Completion

    Such nobility even in his final moments. Zakajz used the dragons to taunt him... But did Tyr really know they would betray them? Will Alexstrasza?

    This would be a great blow to them...

    If Tyr cannot be raised, perhaps it is not something they need to know.

    In addition, Tyr has a permanent buff:

    Should Tyr's health get low, he automatically heals to full:

    Defeat the spawn and use Light of Tyr to rally nearby vrykul.

    Stage 2 – Now Kith'ix

    Defeat forces of the Old Gods in these woods and rally the vrykul. *Old God forces defeated (0/100%)

    Continue fighting the spawn (3%) as well as Faceless Devourers (12%) and Faceless Subjugators.

    Stage 3 – Allied Chronicles

    thumb|Archaedas and Ironaya fighting Kith'ix.

    Help Ironaya and Archaedas defeat Kith'ix. *Kith'ix defeated

    Kith'ix spawns and fights Ironaya and Archaedas in the center of the forest (in the same spot as the mushroom ring in the present-day Whispering Forest). Upon being brought to 1% health, Kith'ix runs off to the west and despawns.

    Stage 4 – Umbral Glade

    thumb|Faceless Subjugators and Corruptor Tentacles keeping Iron Vrykul stunned.

    Clear the woods of any remaining Old God forces and rally the vrykul. *Old God forces defeated (0/100%)

    ''Ironaya and Archaedas leave.''

    Continue defeating the enemies and rallying the vrykul. New enemies include Corruptor Tentacles, a Horrific Aberration, and Mordoth, while new vrykul are a group of Battleworn Vrykul fighting Mordoth. If Mordoth is brought to 1% health, he teleports away in a burst of Void energy.

    Attacking Mordoth :

    Stage 5 – Tyr's Last Stand

    thumb|Zakajz. thumb|Tyr confronting Zakajz.

    Confront Zakajz. *Approach Zakajz

    Zakajz waits in the northwest by the shore of the lake. When the player gets close, numerous Iron Vrykul run up to cover his flanks.

    Stage 6 – The Aspects of Zakajz

    Defeat Zakajz's illusions. Illusion of Neltharion defeated Illusion of Malygos defeated Illusion of Ysera defeated Illusion of Nozdormu defeated

    Zakajz retreats slightly north to the edge of the lake. First duo is Malygos (who teleports in) and Neltharion (who flies in).

    Defeat them, and Nozdormu (who teleports in) and Ysera (who flies in) attack.

    The illusion initially appear normally, but at 75% health they become corrupted. Get them to around 50% health to force them to disappear in a burst of Void energy.

    Final Stage – Tyr's Fall

    thumb|Zakajz in the form of Alexstrasza.

    Defeat Zakajz. Witness Tyr's final sacrifice. *Zakajz defeated

    Zakajz transforms into an illusion of Alexstrasza and attacks.

    Get Zakajz to 50%:

    An in-engine cinematic starts:

    ''Zakajz knocks Tyr back, forcing him into a kneeling position, and entraps him in Void energy.''

    ''Zakazj resumes his true form and starts channeling a massive Void spell above him.''

    ''Tyr glances at the Iron Vrykul around him, then gathers holy magic in his left hand and unleashes it to free himself and interrupt Zakajz's spell.

    ''Tyr charges up himself and the Silver Hand with holy energy, then launches himself at Zakajz, sending them both flying into the lake.''

    ''Some of the Iron Vrykul run up to the edge to watch but then quickly run away as an immense explosion of energy rocks the lake and causes the screen to fade to white.''
