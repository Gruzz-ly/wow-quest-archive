    ==Objectives== Search for leads amongst the [[shade]]s at the [[Scarlet Monastery]]. Search for leads along the path [[Fearful Crusader]] confronted {{co|81.3|39.6|Tirisfal Glades}} Search for leads along the path [[Dathrohan]] confronted {{co|82.5|36.4|Tirisfal Glades}} Search for leads along the path [[Suzannah]] confronted {{co|82.7|33.8|Tirisfal Glades}}

    ==Description== I need your help, . I thought I had achieved some semblance of peace--although my sins might not be forgiven, I could at least reunite with the spirits of my beloved family. Yet... [[Suzannah Thomson|my wife]]'s [[Ghost|spirit]]. It is as though she never existed.

    [[Hallow's End]] draws the shades that haunt the Monastery out from the shadows. Trapped in the past, they walk the grounds repeating their nightmares.

    With any luck, one of them might give us answers as to what happened to her.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|28|9}} 3x [[Tricky Treat]] *10x [[Flightstones]]

    ==Completion==

    ! My apologies for running off back there. There are... some memories I wish never to revisit.

    It would seem these cruel shades know just how to salt our wounds.

    Come, I think I know where to look next.
