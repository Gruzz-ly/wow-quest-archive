    Pirate Gastronomy concludes the third chapter of the Guest Relations storyline, first made available on 14 November.

    Objectives

    Use Rosa's Boonful Banquet translation notes to search for the ingredients. * Ask Maru for a hint (Optional) * Rogue's Thistle (2) * Banana Rum Berries (2) * Fire Breathing Chill Sac * Koatini's Oil (2) * Deadwind Catfish (5) Giant Growth Fish* (4) * Sholazar Nettles (2) * Highland Crawdaddies (3) * Return to Bonenose * "Lone Watcher" located (Optional) * "Cold Storage" located * Secret ingredient acquired *Return to Bonenose on the coast of Tanaris. * The Case of the Superstitious and Hungry Pirates (provided) * Boonful Banquet** (provided)

    Description

    Here are Cooktholomew's notes deciphered. Even though this still looks strange, I am positive I deciphered it correctly.

    I was able to work out how much of everything you need, but it looks like he had his own names for different ingredients, so... good luck!

    Notes

    Rosa hands over The Case of the Superstitious and Hungry Pirates, with new notes starting on page two. It initially reads:

    She also hands over the Boonful Banquet, which has been translated on page two. It reads: {{book|content=Translation Notes from Rosa
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    • Equal parts Rogue's Thistle and Banana Rum Berries (2 of each).
    • Half as many Fire Breathing Chili Sacs as Rogue's Thistle (1 of these).
    • As much Koatini's Oil as Banana Rum Berries (2 of these).
    • As many Deadwind Catfish as Nettles and Crawdaddies altogether (5 of these).
    • Twice as much Giant Growth Fish as the two Nettles (4 of these).
    • Equal parts Sholazar Nettles and Oil (2 of these).
    • Three Highland Crawdaddies (3 of these).}}

    After exhausting all of Maru's options, the journal adds: {{book|content= * Rogue's Thistle suggests :Thistle Tea, which would mean I need :Swiftthistle. * Banana Rum Berries suggests :Witchberries, which you need to brew Banana Rum. * Fire Breathing Chili Sacs suggests :Dragonbreath Chili, meaning I need Small Flame Sacs. * Koatini's Oil suggests :Aromatic Fish Oil since Koatini is the only vendor I know of who sells that. * Deadwind Catfish suggests :Bloodfin Catfish since Deadwind Pass is where those fish can be found. * Giant Growth Fish suggests :Deviate Fish, which you need to make Elixirs of Giant Growth. * Sholazar Nettles suggests :Nettlefish since Sholazar Basin is where those fish can be found. * Highland Crawdaddies suggest Furious Crawdads, which are found in Highland Mixed Schools in the Terokkar Forest.}}

    With that, it's time for a world tour. All items can be bought (at drastically inflated prices) from the Auction House, or acquired via the methods below:
