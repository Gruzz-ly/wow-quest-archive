    {{questbox |name=Hidden Legacies |id=74381 |level={{level|df-max}} |levelreq={{level|df-max}} |category=Valdrakken |start=Automatic |end=[[Kurazidaia]] {{co|61.0|26.1|Valdrakken}} |money={{cost|21|6|75}} |experience=9,500 |reputation=+150 [[Valdrakken Accord]] |next=[[Return to the Reach]] }}

    '''Hidden Legacies''' begins the [[patch 10.0.7]] portion of the [[Dragonflight campaign]], starting the [[Forbidden Reach storyline]]. The quest will be pushed to players automatically upon zoning into [[Valdrakken]].

    ==Objectives== Speak to the [[Healing Wings]] Envoy at the [[Seat of the Aspects]] to learn about events in the [[Forbidden Reach]]. * Hear [[Kurazidaia|the envoy's]] report.

    ==Description== [[Raszageth]] and her storms are gone, but our troubles are far from over.

    The way to the Reach is clear again, and some of our allies have returned to establish a base camp. However, [[Sarkareth]] and the [[Sundered Flame]] have begun making their way there as well.

    We need to secure the island. We would appreciate your aid.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|21|6|75}} 9,500 [[XP]] *+150 [[reputation]] with [[Valdrakken Accord]]

    ==Progress== Ah, an adventurer! Did Emberthal send you?

    ==Completion== Thankfully, our enemies have not discovered our scouts. The [[Primal Incarnates|Incarnates]] and the Sundered Flame are both formidable foes. I'm uncertain whether we'll be able to safely monitor them again without retribution.

    The situation is heating up. Some of the [[dracthyr]] have already returned to the island, but they're going to need a lot of help building up their base of operations.

    You're about to be very busy, friend.

    ==Notes== [[Kurazidaia]] offers a skip for alts who have already done the introduction to the [[Forbidden Reach storyline]]: : {{gossip|You can count on me. {{text|red|}}}}
