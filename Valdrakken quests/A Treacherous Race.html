    '''A Treacherous Race''' begins the final day of [[Secrets of Azeroth]] activities.

    ==Completion== [[Bobby Carlisle|Bobby]] and [[Preservationist Kathos|I]] finally deciphered the tablets. They tell of an artifact of immense power that was locked away in [[Tyrhold]]. And I fear [[Tithris]] is after whatever that artifact is.

    You must secure whatever the tablets speak of before Tithris. Bobby and I will find a guard and join you as soon as we can.

    Take our notes. There are many safeguards that you will need to get through to find the artifact's vault.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *[[Ancient Tyrhold Artifact Notes]]
