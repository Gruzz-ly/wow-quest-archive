    '''Fair Skies and Strong Winds''' begins the [[Dragonflight epilogue|''Dragonflight'' epilogue]]. Find [[Alexstrasza]] at one of many locations across the [[Dragon Isles]], but likely beneath the open-world version of [[Amirdrassil]] in [[Bel'ameth]] by the moonwell to begin.

    ==Objectives== Speak with Alexstrasza at the [[Seat of the Aspects]] in [[Valdrakken]]. * Speak with Alexstrasza at the Seat of the Aspects {{co|58.1|34.9|Valdrakken}}

    ==Description== It has been a long road for us all, .

    But after persevering through hardship and strife, [[dragonkin]]d stands poised to enter a new age.

    Before you leave for your next adventure, my fellow [[Dragon Aspects|Aspects]] and I wish to thank you for all you have done for us.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|14|4|50}} * 4,750 [[XP]] * [[Champion of the Dragonflights]]

    ==Progress== The Dragon Isles stand in your debt.

    ==Completion== Fair skies and strong winds to you, .

    ==Notes== Return to Valdrakken and get to the top of the Seat of the Aspects to speak with Alexstrasza:

    {{dialogue| Long ago, [[Neltharion]] was a dear, dear friend. I regret that I was never able to reach him, to help him break free of the corruption that blighted [[Black dragonflight|his entire flight]].

    I should not linger on the past. It is far too late to make amends with him, I know.

    But... perhaps there are others I can reach.
