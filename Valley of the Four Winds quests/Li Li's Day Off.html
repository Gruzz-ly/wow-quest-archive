    Bring [[Li Li]] to the Silken Fields, New Cifera, and Huangtze Falls. Bring Li Li to the [[Silken Fields]] {{co|61.8|59.0|Valley of the Four Winds}} Bring Li Li to [[New Cifera]] Bring Li Li to [[Huangtze Falls]] {{co|74.9|42.6|Valley of the Four Winds}} [[Li Li's Wishing-Stone]] (provided)

    ==Description== [[Chen Stormstout|Uncle Chen]] asked me not to get into any fights while he was away... but that doesn't mean I can't go exploring. Finally, a chance to look around [[Pandaria]] without stopping every forty-seven steps for another rest!

    You want to come with me? I'll bet there's all kinds of cool places we can visit, not too far away from here.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|10|20}} * 129000 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== You ready to go out yet?

    ==Completion== That was great! You and I should go exploring again sometime.

    And it looks like we finished before Uncle Chen got back, too. Perfect.

    ==Notes== ;Speaking with Li Li * I can't believe Uncle Chen would rather sit down and drink beer than walk around and explore this place! * Are you seeing all this stuff? This place is great! * Thanks for taking me out exploring with you, .

    {{gossip|Why aren't you helping me fight things?}}

    Because Uncle Chen made me promise not to!

    Listen, Chen may be a stupid, boring, lazy, fat old uncle, but that doesn't mean that I don't respect him. He told me not to get into any trouble, and I'm not about to ignore him.

    Besides, you seem to be doing just fine on your own. You don't need a little girl to help you kill things.

    Pick up [[Muddy Water]] before heading out. Li Li provides a Wishing-Stone to summon her in case she gets separated from the adventurer. Li Li will comment on the quests mentioned in this guide as you accept them and turn them in.

    The closest location is to the east up the [[Yan-Zhe River]] to the [[Huangtze Falls]]. :{{text|say|Li Li|Hey! Look at that waterfall!}} :{{text|say|Li Li|I wonder if there's a cave behind it.}} :{{text|say|Li Li|You should always check behind waterfalls for caves.}}

    No cave behind this waterfall, so head just a bit downstream and complete [[Muddy Water]]. After that, optionally head northwest to [[Halfhill]] for the breadcrumb quest [[Got Silk?]]. Then, return southeast (or, if you skipped the breadcrumb quest, go west from the river) to find the [[Silken Fields]]: :{{text|say|Li Li|Check out all the cool silk!}} :{{text|say|Li Li|Do you think that they really get this stuff from the silkworms' rear end?}} :''She walks over to the feeding pit.'' :{{text|say|Li Li|Eww... they look like maggots.}} Speak with [[Silkmaster Tsai]] to pick up a side quest, [[Where Silk Comes From]]. Journeyman Chu just to the northwest offers [[The Moth Rebellion]]. That is quickly followed up by [[Mothallus!]].

    Finally, head to [[New Cifera]], which is also a quest hub. :{{text|say|Li Li|Look at this!}} :{{text|say|Li Li|There's a village up here... and a big fish statue, too.}} :{{text|say|Li Li|You think the [[jinyu]] built this place?}} {{NPC||Clever Ashyo}} is here as well. Feel free to turn in [[Clever Ashyo (quest)]], and pick up [[Ashyo's Vision]], as well as the side quests [[Bottletoads]], [[Watery Woes (quest)]], and [[Snap Judgment]] while at New Cifera.

    Upon engaging [[Krosh]]. :{{text|say|Li Li|Look how big and ugly he is!}} :{{text|say|Li Li|It's a good thing my Uncle Chen told me not to get into any fights, otherwise you'd be dead meat, turtle!}} :{{text|yell|Li Li|Are you listening to me, turtle?}}

    On complete: :{{text|say|Li Li|Look, here he comes now! How was the brewery, uncle?}} :{{text|say|Chen Stormstout|Bah! I do not want to talk about it.}}

    Speak with him to continue.
