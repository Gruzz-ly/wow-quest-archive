    ==Objectives== Retrieve the Abyssal Scepter from the Zandalari Treasury. * Speak with [[Jaina Proudmoore]] * Lady Jaina Proudmoore slain * Retrieve the [[:Abyssal Scepter]] from the [[Zandalari Treasury]].

    ==Description== The Abyssal Scepter is a powerful [[tidesage]] artifact and a serious threat to the [[fleet]]. The [[Horde]] stole it, but it is being held by the [[Zandalari]] in their treasury.

    The [[Storm's Wake]] has crafted a fake Scepter. Your first job is to obtain the real one without alerting suspicion.

    Speak to me when you are ready to go over the plan.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|70|20|}} * 26,800 [[XP]] *[[Radiant Azerite Core]] (750x [[Azerite (resource)]])

    ==Progress== Were you successful?

    ==Completion== Mission accomplished on all fronts, it seems. I will ensure that the [[Gelbin Mekkatorque|High Tinker]] gets the data you collected.

    I remember hearing stories of the Abyssal Scepter when I was young. Aside from being a tactical victory, it is good to have this relic back in our hands. Thank you.
