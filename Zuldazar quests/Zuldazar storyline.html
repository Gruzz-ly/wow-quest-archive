    Zuldazar storyline

    thumb|Zone Map

    To start this storyline, visit the scouting table in the Great Seal (2nd level). The Zuldazar storyline takes part in seven chapters, tracked by the achievement The Throne of Zuldazar:

    Main storyline

    Breadcrumbs: Zuldazar (quest), Trust of a King

    Portents and Prophecies

    Port of Zandalar

    The Zanchuli Council

    Warport Rastari

    Among the People

    Web of Lies

    March of the Loa

    Side quests

    The Slough

    Can be done from the start.

    Nesingwary's Trek

    The chain be done from the start, while the breadcrumb to it unlocks after "The Zanchuli Council" chapter.

    Warbeast Kraal

    The quest chain itself can be done from the start, while the breadcrumb to it unlocks after "Port of Zandalar" chapter.

    This is a very long quest chain that eventually awards the achievement How to Train Your Direhorn (achievement) and the mount Child of Torcali.

    After doing the dailies for 15 days, the questline resumes:

    Village in the Vines

    Breadcrumb unlocks after "Warport Rastari".

    Isle of Fangs

    The quest chain itself can be done after "Warport Rastari", while the breadcrumb to it unlocks after "Among the People".

    Talanji's Rebuke

    Breadcrumb unlocks after "Warport Rastari".

    Tortollan

    Consists of three separate chains (Horde-only at Port of Zandalar and neutral at Dreadpearl Shallows and Atal'Gral). The quests are all initially available, but the breadcrumb from Port of Zandalar to Dreadpearl Shallows does not unlock until after "Warport Rastari".

    Xibala

    Unlock after "March of the Loa".

    Sandscar Breach

    Unlock after "March of the Loa".

    Pterrodax Training

    This is a long questline that becomes available at level 50. Completing it awards the achievement How to Ptrain Your Pterrordax and the mount Kua'fon's Harness.

    Alliance

    While Zuldazar is a Horde zone, it doesn't mean that there are no quests for Alliance to do there.

    Alliance characters can do "Nesigwary's Trek" and Tortollan quests above, plus they have some quests exclusive to them.
