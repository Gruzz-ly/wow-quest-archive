    Zandalar Forever!

    {{For|the achievement|Zandalar Forever! (achievement)}} {{Questbox | id = 50954 | name = Zandalar Forever! | category = Zuldazar | faction = Horde | level = {{level|Vol'dun}} | start = [[King Rastakhan]] | reputation = +250 [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]] | experience = 26,800 | money = {{cost|70|20|}} | previous = | next = [[Zalazane Returns]] }}

    '''Zandalar Forever!''' becomes available after completing the [[The Throne of Zuldazar]], [[The Dark Heart of Nazmir]], [[Secrets in the Sands]], and [[A Bargain of Blood]] questlines.

    ==Objectives== Complete the Zandalar Forever scenario. * Zandalar Forever [[scenario]] completed

    ==Description== [[Bwonsamdi]] has whispered a warning to me: the enemy is on the move.

    I have gathered what defenses I could in the time allowed but we are dangerously under powered.

    I can only hope that, thanks to your efforts, the [[loa]] will be with us this day.

    Are you ready to defend [[Zandalar]]?

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|70|20|}} * 26,800 [[XP]] * +250 reputation with [[Zandalari Empire (faction)|Zandalari Empire]]

    ==Completion== We have won the battle, not the war.

    ==Notes== ;King Rastakhan

    :[[Zul]]'s treachery cuts deeper than I realized. With his rebellion, he practically depleted my army.

    :Those still loyal to me are dying on the battlefield behind me to these invading, endless swarms of [[blood troll]]s.

    :I should have listened to [[Talanji]].

    :{{Gossip|I am ready to defend Zandalar.}}

    You are teleported into the scenario phase of the zone.
