    '''A Brighter Tomorrow''' concludes the [[Khaz Algar]] series of [[Children's Week]] quests.

    ==Objectives== Speak to the recently arrived benefactors. * Speak to the Mereldar Lamplighter * Speak to [[Linny Leadfinger]] * Speak to [[Professor Neltik]] * Speak to [[Stormrider Torena]]

    ==Description== , look at this! All of these people are here to discuss the children's futures.

    They expressed a desire to speak with you as well.

    ==Rewards== You will be able to choose one of: * [[Argos's Bell]] * [[Goggles's Favorite Chew-Wrench]] * [[Threadis's Workshop Bot 2.0]] * [[Arsenal: Children's Stormwind Guard Weapon Set]] * [[Arsenal: Children's Orgrimmar Guard Weapon Set]] * [[Well-loved Figurine]]

    You will also receive: * [[Khaz Algar Orphan Whistle]]

    ==Progress== Have you had a chance to speak with the benefactors?

    ==Completion== , I can't thank you enough. All of these children look like they have bright futures ahead of them, and you played no small part in that.

    Thanks to you, I also feel as if I understand children a bit better. They are truly wonderful, even if they are a handful at times.

    I know you are very busy, but I do hope we will see you next year!
