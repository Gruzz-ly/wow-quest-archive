    {{Questbox | id = 78546 | name = Recompense | category = Isle of Dorn | level = {{level|Isle of Dorn}} | start = [[Merrix]] {{co|32.0|59.7|Isle of Dorn}} | experience = 10,400 | money = {{Cost|28|9}} | rewards = One of: {{plainlist| [[Rubble-Salvaged Slippers]] [[Rubble-Salvaged Grips]] [[Rubble-Salvaged Shoulderpads]] [[Rubble-Salvaged Waistguard]] }} | reputation = +2,500 [[Council of Dornogal]] or 500x [[Resonance Crystals]] | shareable = Yes | previous = [[Return to the Coreway]] | next = [[Into the Deeps]] }}

    '''Recompense''' concludes The First Blow chapter of the [[Isle of Dorn storyline]], and thus, of the storyline itself. The [[The War Within campaign]] continues with [[Into the Deeps]], beginning the [[Ringing Deeps storyline]].

    ==Objectives== Speak to [[Adelgonn]] to inform [[Merrix]] of [[Baelgrim]]'s fate. * Speak to Adelgonn * Listen to the debriefing * Witness the [[Coreway]]'s restoration

    ==Description== We heard the echo of the explosion from here.

    I presume you have found victory, but your solitary arrival suggests not all is well.

    What happened?

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: [[Rubble-Salvaged Slippers]] [[Rubble-Salvaged Grips]] [[Rubble-Salvaged Shoulderpads]] [[Rubble-Salvaged Waistguard]]

    You will also receive: {{Cost|28|9}} 10,400 [[XP]]

    One-time warband bonus: *[[Council of Dornogal Commendation]] (+2500 [[reputation]] with [[Council of Dornogal]]) (first time) or 125x [[Resonance Crystals]] (alts)

    ==Progress== Tell me what happened.

    He died to preserve our people--our unity. We must not let that be in vain.
