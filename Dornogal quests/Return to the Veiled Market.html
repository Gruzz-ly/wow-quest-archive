    Return to the Veiled Market

    {{Questbox | id = 84957 | name = Return to the Veiled Market | disambigcat = The Knife's Edge | displaycat = The Knife's Edge | category = K'aresh | level = {{level|K'aresh}} | levelreq = {{level|K'aresh}} | start = [[Locus-Walker]] {{co|42.1|26.9|Dornogal}} | end = [[Locus-Walker]] {{co|59.8|83.4|Tazavesh||1}} | money = {{cost|2|34}} | experience = 1,200 | previous = [[A Shadowy Invitation]] | next = [[Restoring Operational Efficiency]] }}

    '''Return to the Veiled Market''' unlocks the portal to [[K'aresh]] from [[Dornogal]] during the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]].

    Once the first character in the warband completes the campaign through chapter three ([[The Tabiqa]]), gets their [[Reshii Wraps]], and completes the [[Phase Diving]] quests through [[The Untethered Horror]], alts can skip ahead to the same point by speaking with [[Om'en]] after landing in Tazavesh, then fly directly to [[Hashim]] at {{co|50.3|36.3|K'aresh}} to pick up their Reshii Wraps. {{dialogue|I see that you are answering Locus-Walker's call again?

    {{gossip|I have heard this tale before. {{text|red|}}}}}}

    ==Objectives== Accompany Locus-Walker and [[Alleria]] to [[Tazavesh, the Veiled Market|Tazavesh]]. Follow [[Locus-Walker]] Take the [[Spatial Rift (Dornogal)|Spatial Rift]] to [[Tazavesh]]

    ==Description== I have been retained by the [[K'aresh Trust]], a coalition of both [[broker]] and [[ethereal]] [[cartel]]s who seek to reclaim [[K'aresh]]. The Trust is in desperate need of one of your skills, .

    The [[Shadowguard]] assaults our city and threatens the work of the eco-dome project.

    Please accompany me.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|2|34}} 1,200 [[XP]]

    ==Completion== Welcome to Tazavesh, both of you.

    We have much to discuss. Your assistance is crucial, as always.

The [[Shadowlands]] demanded stronger containment suits than our wraps.}} :''He opens a Spatial Rift in a nook.''
