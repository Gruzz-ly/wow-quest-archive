    '''The Expedition Awaits''' is the first quest in the [[Siren Isle campaign]], added with the launch of [[Patch 11.0.7]].

    ==Objectives== Meet [[Skaggit]] by the [[East Gate]] of [[Dornogal]].

    ==Description== Greetings, ! Are you here to answer the [[Siren Isle]]'s call to adventure?

    Recently, a [[goblin]] salvage team discovered an isle with a strange "singing crystal" at its center.

    Like the goblins, the [[Arathi]] of [[Hallowfall]] have taken a keen interest in this crystal--as have we. We've been preparing an expedition to the isle!

    Most of the expedition has gone ahead, but the goblins last vessel is set to leave momentarily.

    If you hurry, you can catch them before they leave and join us!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|2|34}}

    ==Completion== Ah, so you're the one Dawn convinced to join us, eh?

    Well, hurry up! They've been adamant about getting us a proper [[Stormrider]]-ranger-whatever escort out of the city, but it's takin' too long!

    The Arathi and other earthen are already on the Isle and [[Ricket]] is probably wonderin' where the heck we are!

    C'mon! I can give you the deets on the way!

    ==Notes== In front of Dawn are [[Chief Officer Hammerflange]], a [[Greasemonkey Surveyor]], and a [[Zeppelin Engineer]]. She is accompanied by [[Stormranger Guide]]s, [[Saradi]], a [[Mole Machine Operator]], [[Alta]], and [[Flint]].
