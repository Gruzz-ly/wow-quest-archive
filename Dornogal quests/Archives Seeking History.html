    '''Archives: Seeking History''' is a [[meta quest]] offered by [[High Speaker Brinthe]] in [[Dornogal]]. It ends in the [[Vault of Memory (Isle of Dorn)|Vault of Memory]], which is accessed via the Waygate inside the [[Archive]] to the west of the [[Coreway]].

    ==Objectives== Collect 100 titan disc fragments.

    These disc fragments can be looted from dangerous creatures and treasures across [[Khaz Algar]]. *[[Titan Disc Fragment]] (100)

    ==Description== We're still processing the data in the [[Archive]] and restoring what we can. But... the discs we've recovered so far in the Archive itself are unusable.

    Finding more of these discs are a priority. We must reclaim our memories.

    Return what you find to Dagran. He is assisting me in rebuilding the Archive.
