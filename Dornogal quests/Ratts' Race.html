    Ratts' Race is part of the journey to obtain Incognitro, the Indecipherable Felcycle as part of Ratts' Revenge. To begin, players must have the title from the Azeroth's Greatest Detective achievement, which requires completing most of the Guest Relations quests and crates. After WoW's Anniversary concludes, Alyx relocates to Dornogal, and all eleven crates can be turned into them there, which is enough to earn the achievement and title.

    Objectives

    Track down Ratts. * First clue found * Second clue found / * Third clue found * Ratts confronted * Carefully Penned Note (Anniversary) (provided)

    Description

    I saw a vulpera acting very suspicious. She seemed... on edge. I think I saw a knife at her side and a look of dark magic in her eyes.

    She noticed me staring and immediately fled. Is she a wanted criminal?

    She... dropped this. Here.

    Notes

    Speak with the Dalaran Survivor at in the Stoneshaper's Atrium of Dornogal:

    {{dialogue|I... I think I witnessed a crime. Or at least a criminal.

    N-no, sorry. I would rather speak to a detective.}}

    Equip the Detective title and try again:

    O-okay.

    }}

    The survivor then starts this quest and hands over the Carefully Penned Note (Anniversary), which reads:

    {{book|content=If you're reading this, you must be after him too. Or are you after me?

    Either way, the game is afoot. Are you clever enough to play?

    Hurry up, I think the fairy ring has gone rotten.}}

    Head to Deepwalker Pass in Azj-Kahet to find some Rotglow Settlers at in a ring around an Unfinished Note. It reads:

    {{book|content=Think what we could accomplish together. We could finally find him.

    -186, 96}}

    Equip the Tricked-Out Thinking Cap and head northwest to (-186, 96) or in City of Threads coordinates or in Azj-Kahet coordinates to find the Hastily Scrawled Note (City of Threads) up high overlooking the city. It reads:

    {{book|content=Hallowfall
    Azj-Kahet
    Water you waiting for}}

    Head up the western path to Hallowfall from Azj-Kahet and hug the eastern wall to find a hidden pond at with the Water-Resistant Note on the bottom of the pond. It has the flavor text of "A magic seal protects this note from getting wet. It faintly smells of fel magic." and reads:

    {{book|content= You're persistent, I'll give you that. Fine. If you can find me, we can talk.

    Careful, it's a long way down this dark rabbit hole.

    I'll be watching.}}

    The rabbit hole is a reference to the No Harm Ever Came From Reading A Book achievement that tasks players with jumping down a secret hole in Pillar-nest Xesh. For this step, instead enter Pillar-nest Vosh at and get to the east end of the cave. At do some creative jumping to get to the top of the wall and fall in.

    In the bottom, Ratts is channeling: : : : :

    If attacked: :

    She teleports away and leaves the Peculiar Gem behind at . Pick it up to complete the quest before leaving!

    On the other side of the pond is a Cake of Death, that will do as warned when eaten, and an Eye of the Void. Interacting with the Eye will teleport the player to any The Last Relic of Argus location to escape the hole.
