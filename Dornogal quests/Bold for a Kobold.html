    ==Description== Big-Tall! Nice [[Earthen|Talk-Rock]] tell Skibbles that she get to do whatever she want today!

    Well, me have big dream. One so big, me get in trouble for it. A lot.

    Me want to be brave candle knight, like [[Arathi|shiny humans]] with [[Arathi airship|floaty boats]]!

    A-and me hear there is [[Beledar|crystal]] that's brighter than any candle!

    Would...would you take Skibbles to see candle knights with [[Imperial lynx|big kitties]]?

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|2|34}}

    ==Progress== Oh, hello . I've had a wonderful time chatting with the orphanage patron here. After watching the children play, I am starting to see why my previous activities weren't as exciting for the younglings I am watching.

    They do not seem as interested in city tours but instead have an incredible capacity to pretend! Fascinating.

    Oh, I almost forgot--did you get a chance to take Skibbles to see the Lamplighters?

    ==Completion== My goodness! I have never seen Skibbles this excited! It sounds like she made quite the impression with the Lamplighters.

    Speaking of children, I was watching the other Arathi children play and noticed that [[Destien]] seems more interested in his book than joining in.

    Perhaps you could talk to him? Surely there is something he would rather do than stay here.
