    Lorewalking: The Blade and the High Priest

    {{Questbox | id = 84371 | name = Lorewalking: The Blade and the High Priest | category = Lorewalking | level = {{level|holiday 10}} | start = [[Lorewalker Cho]] | money = {{Cost|2|34}} | rewards = [[Lorewalker's Crate of Memorabilia]], 10x [[Valorstones]], 3x [[Weathered Undermine Crest]] | next = [[Lorewalking: The Blade's Gambit]] }}

    '''Lorewalking: The Blade and the High Priest''' begins the [[Xal'atath]]-focused [[Lorewalking]] campaign. Speak with [[Lorewalker Cho]] in [[Dornogal]], [[Stormwind City]], or [[Orgrimmar]] to begin.

    ==Objectives== Follow Cho's guidance to learn the story of [[Xal'atath|Xal'alath]]'s{{sic}} past. Talk to [[Lorewalker Cho]] to learn about the [[Third invasion of the Burning Legion|Legion invasion]] Talk to Lorewalker Cho to learn of the High Priest's journey *Follow the High Priest's journey and acquire Xal'atath

    ==Description== Xal'atath, the Harbinger of [[Void]], is a cunning and dangerous foe. She has been careful to make her presence known only to a precious few.

    We first learned of her during the Legion's third invasion of Azeroth, but whispers say she has been around since long, long before that.

    This is a story of power, darkness, and deceit. Join me and I will tell you the tale of Xal'atath's mysterious past.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|2|34}} [[Lorewalker's Crate of Memorabilia]] 10x [[Valorstones]] 3x [[Weathered Undermine Crest]]

    ==Progress== Xal'atath was a mysterious creature indeed. I wonder if the High Priest ever imagined how far Xal'atath would go...

    ==Completion== Xal'atath was a powerful weapon and a... talkative presence.
