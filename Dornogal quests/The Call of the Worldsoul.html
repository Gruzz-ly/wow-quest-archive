    The Call of the Worldsoul

    {{Questbox | id = 82449 | name = The Call of the Worldsoul | category = The War Within meta | displaycat = Meta Quests | level = {{level|tww}} | start = [[Faerin Lothar]]{{co|46.1|49.6|Dornogal}} | end = Automatic | next = [[#Possible followup quests|Various]] }}

    '''The Call of the Worldsoul''' is a [[meta quest]] offered by [[Faerin Lothar]] in [[Dornogal]] every third week. It presents a choice between fourCan be fewer for characters below 80. Worldsoul quests in [[Khaz Algar]] and automatically completes when one of the offered quests is completed. It has no rewards of its own. The other two weekly meta quests are one of the archives quests like [[Archives: Seeking History]] started by [[High Speaker Brinthe]] or [[Delves: Percussive Archaeology]] started by [[Brann Bronzebeard]].

    ==Possible quests== Every three weeks, four quests will be available to choose from, and only one can be completed. {{Worldsoul quests}}

    ==Description== [[File:The Call of the Worldsoul.jpg|thumb|The Call of the Worldsoul choice UI]] How will you aid the peoples of [[Khaz Algar]]?

    ;Ara-kara, City of Echoes Information from [[Azj-Kahet]] comes in mere whispers. We need to find out what's going on. Bring together your strongest companions and investigate!

    Complete the [[Ara-Kara, City of Echoes]] dungeon at [normal/heroic/mythic] difficulty or higher.

    ''To arms!''

    ;Awakening the Machine The [[Awakening Machine]] is vital to the [[earthen]]. It needs maintenance and, as always, you're our most dependable for a job.

    ''Let's get fixin'!''

    ;The Dawnbreaker The [[Dawnbreaker]]... the magnus{{sic}} opus of the [[Hallowfall Arathi]]. It is ready for launch. My friend, will you join its charge?

    ;Dastardly Duos Seek out [[Xyggie Marou|Xyggi Marou]] to take part in the epic Dastardly Duos competitions.

    Will you be able to get the top score?

    ''I will have the top score!''

    Complete any [[Delve]] in [[the War Within]], and claim your victory!

    ''Let's get exploring!''

    ;Delves (overcharged) Hostile [[Titan]] constructs have surfaced in many of the dark recesses of [[Khaz Algar]]. We must deal with the threat they pose.

    Complete any [[Season]]al dungeon.

    ''I'll defend Khaz Algar!''

    ;Forging a Pact Some [[nerubian]]s seem willing to work with us. Aid them, but be cautious...

    Select a patron to represent, then complete objectives in their name and slay rare creatures.

    ''Make your name known.''

    ;Horrific Visions This [[Black Blood]] has been seen before in past [[Old God|old god]] attacks.

    Visit [[Soridormi]] in [[Dornogal]] to return to the [[Vision of N'Zoth|Horrific Visions of N'zoth]] and see if we can learn something that can help us face the new void threats.

    ''I will return to the past!''

    ;K'aresh World Quest [[K'aresh]] is a shattered world and its people need all the help it can get.
