    The Fall of Shan Bu (Alliance)

    {{Questbox | image=The Fall of Shan Bu.jpg | imagecaption=The Fall of Shan Bu | name=The Fall of Shan Bu | start=[[Lady Jaina Proudmoore]] {{coords|63.3|72.2|Isle of Thunder||1}} | faction=Alliance | id=32656 | level={{level|isle of thunder}} | category=Isle of Thunder | reputation=+300 [[Kirin Tor Offensive]] | experience=236000 | money={{cost|19|84|50}} | previous=Starting stage 5 (with [[A Bold Idea]]) | other-faction=The Fall of Shan Bu (Horde) }}

    '''The Fall of Shan Bu''' ends the fifth and final stage of the [[Isle of Thunder storyline]], gaining access to the [[Foot of Lei Shen]] and the world boss [[Nalak]]. [[Lady Jaina Proudmoore]] will only offer this quest from her position on the Violet Rise once enough realm-wide progress has been made, irrespective of the individual player's standing with the Kirin Tor Offensive.

    Much like with the previous progression quest, [[A Bold Idea]], this quest mostly takes place in a single-player scenario also called "The Fall of Shan Bu". As the scenario can only be completed once, it is documented here and not in a separate article.

    ==Objectives== Defeat [[Shan Bu]] Speak to [[Lady Jaina Proudmoore]] Capture the [[Foot of Lei Shen]]

    ==Description== [[Lei Shen]] found a clutch of rare storm dragon eggs early in his campaign. One - known as [[Nalak]] - was particularly brutal and killed all of its handlers, so he took it for his own.

    Now, Shan Bu, one of Lei Shen's most loyal servants, works to resurrect the dragon.

    It is time for us to strike against Shan Bu!

    Speak to me when you are ready to launch the assault!

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|19|84|50}} 236000 [[XP]] *+300 [[reputation]] with [[Kirin Tor Offensive]]

    ==Progress== The time to defeat Shan Bu is now!

    ==Completion== You've done it, ! Shan Bu is dead once and for all, and we've learned that Lor'themar may not be all that he seems.

    Try to stay near Yalia, who has a Divine Renewal aura that will heal for 13-16% of total health every few seconds. That aura will outheal incoming damage from the Shadow Crash and the Electrical Storm. Do take out the summoned Onyx Stormclaws - as they will build up as the fight progresses.

    At around 15% of total health remaining, Shan Bu stops what he is doing and grabs Taran Zhu in a chokehold: :{{text|yell|Shan Bu|I tire of your insolence! I will choke the life from you, impudent slave!}} :{{text|yell|Taran Zhu|Auugh - kill him. Quickly!}} :{{text|yell|Taran Zhu|Finish him!}}

    Finish Shan Bu off: :{{text|yell|Shan Bu|I ... no... My Emperor! My Emperor, I have failed you!}} :{{text|say|Taran Zhu|You see? No power of this world can trample the pure of spirit.}}
