    '''The Assault on Shaol'mara''' begins the second stage of the [[Isle of Thunder storyline]]. Jaina will only offer this quest on deck of ''[[Seabolt|The Seabolt]]'' once enough realm-wide progress has been made, irrespective of the individual player's standing with the [[Kirin Tor Offensive]].

    The quest mostly plays out in a single-player scenario also called "The Assault on Shaol'mara". As the scenario can only be done once, it is documented here and not in a separate article.

    ==Objectives== Complete the Assault on Shaol'mara. Speak to [[Jaina Proudmoore]] to begin the attack. Speak to Jaina Proudmoore Complete the Assault on Shaol'mara

    ==Description== Our scouting efforts along the coast have given us valuable intelligence about the island, .

    However, we will accomplish nothing if we do not advance forward onto the mainland.

    I have assembled a team of our most elite aerial units to begin the attack.

    I would like you to join the assault. A champion such as yourself could turn the battle in our favor at the critical moment.

    When you are ready to begin the attack, speak to me.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|19|84|50}} 236000 [[XP]] *+300 [[reputation]] with [[Kirin Tor Offensive]]

    ==Progress== We must claim a better strategic position on the island itself.

    ==Completion== Yes! You have done it, champion!

    Now, the Thunder King will taste the full force of the Kirin Tor!

    ===Stage 4: Kill Anki and Beastcaller Pakutesh=== [[File:Assault on Shaol'mara 4 - Anki and Pakutesh.jpg|thumb|Anki and Pakutesh]]

    Kill [[Anki]] {{co|60|69|Isle of Thunder}} Kill [[Beastcaller Pakutesh]] {{co|60|69|Isle of Thunder}} :{{text|say|Arcanital Mara'kah|Crush the intruders. The barrier must not fall.}} :{{text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|Be quick on your feet - that is no ordinary Zandalari!}}

    Pakutesh is riding Anki, a level 90 direhorn with 2 million health. Anki must die before Pakutesh can be attacked. Pakutesh also has 2 million health. There is a [[Devil's Blood Elixir]] on a table near outside the front of the pen that will heal for 5% of total health every three seconds for 30 seconds once consumed. There is another in the back-left corner of Anki's pen. :{{text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|I like your style, hero.}}

    ===Final Stage: Kill Arcanital Mara'kah and his Spirit Mask=== [[File:Assault on Shaol'mara 5 - Take down the shield.jpg|thumb|The spirit barrier]] [[File:Assault on Shaol'mara 5 - Vereesa and Mara'kah.jpg|thumb|Vereesa and Mara'kah]]

    *Kill [[Arcanital Mara'kah]] {{co|64|73|Isle of Thunder}}

    Vereesa keeps moving southeast down toward the island, stopping at the base of the steps. There are a few spearrangers and a beastcaller blocking the way. The beastcaller will summon a huge amount of raptors! :{{text|bossemote|Shaol'mara Beastcaller summons raptors to his aid!}}

    Once all of the raptors are dead, take a moment to heal up and prepare to fight Mara'kah. :{{text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|They are protecting that mask. It must be the source of the barier. Destroy it!}} :{{text|say|Arcanital Mara'kah|Face me now! My will is iron. My people, unbreakable!}} :{{text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|Destroy the spirit mask to bring down the barrier!}}

    Marakah is a level 90 zandalar troll with 2.7 million health and the following abilities: {{abilities|Arcanital's Spark|Strike an enemy with the power of the heavens, dealing 10,335 to 10,865 Holy damage.|notes=1.83 sec cast. Interruptible|range=30}} {{abilities|Arcanital's Barrier|range=20|notes=Instant. Summons a barrier that lasts roughly a minute, reducing damage taken by 50% and removing all silence effects.}} *{{abilities|Waves of Light|range=300|Inflicts 103,040 to 107,016 Holy damage to enemy players in its path.|notes=2.75 sec cast. Uninterruptible. Hits in a ~8-yard-wide path that extends a long distance from Maraki's position.}}

    Additionally, the untargetable Spirit Mask has two abilities: {{abilities|Spirit Gaze|Inflicts 4,875 to 5,125 Holy damage to enemies within 2 yards of the point of focus.|notes=Instant.}} {{abilities|Hymn of Silence|range=20|notes=1.38 sec cast. Silences units for 10 seconds and ticks for ~52K Holy damage}}

    Try to stay in one of the '''Arcanital's Barriers''' he spawns, but keep Mara'kah out of it, as it will limit the damage he takes as well. Try to interrupt his '''Arcanital's Spark''' ability and sidestep out of '''Waves of Light'''. Make sure to get under a shield when the spirit mask winds up a '''Hymn of Silence''', which is cast every 30-40 seconds. There is a Devil's Blood Potion on the altar behind him, and there is another inside the big tent.

    Finish him off. :{{text|say|Arcanital Mar'kah|Zandalar...}} :{{text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|The shield - it is down! We did it!}} :''A large portal from Dalaran appears at the top of the steps and many members of the Kirin Tor Offensive teleport through it.'' :{{text|say|Vereesa Windrunner|Jaina was right to recommend you. You saved this mission from disaster... and saved my life.}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|That's the Alliance spirit for you - unstoppable!}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Vereesa, establish a perimeter. Modera, place the sentry wards. Narasi, begin porting in tents and supplies.}} :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Everyone take up positions! Enemies surround us on all sides! There is no time to waste.}}

    ===Wrap-up=== [[File:Assault on Shaol'mara 6 - finale.jpg|thumb|Finale]] Leave the instance and turn in to Jaina.

    On complete: :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|Champion, you have exceeded my expectations. Our position on the isle is secured, thanks to your courage and your will. Now, the true work begins.}} This completes the "Build a Base" portion of [[Isle of Thunder (achievement)]].

    Immediately behind Jaina's new position on the [[Violet Rise]] is an [[Assignment Map]], which starts the day's round of questing by offering a choice for daily questing, PvE or PvP. See the Assignment Map article for more information.
