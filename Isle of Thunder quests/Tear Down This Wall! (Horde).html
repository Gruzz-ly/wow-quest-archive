    Tear Down This Wall! (Horde)

    {{questbox | image=Tear Down This Wall 0 - Lei Shen and Al'chukla.jpg | imagecaption=Lei Shen and Al'chukla | name=Tear Down This Wall! | start=[[Lor'themar Theron]] | faction=Horde | id=32276 | level={{level|isle of thunder}} | category=Isle of Thunder | reputation=+300 [[Sunreaver Onslaught]] | experience=236000 | money={{cost|19|84|50}} |other-faction=Tear Down This Wall! (Alliance) }}

    == Objectives == Speak with Lor'themar Theron, then break into the lower city and secure it for the [[Horde]]. Speak to [[Lor'themar Theron]] to begin your mission Secure the lower city

    == Description == The time has come. Our forces are well-enough entrenched here that we can risk pressing forward, into the [[Thunder King]]'s lair. Unfortunately, it doesn't look like he'll be opening that gate anytime soon.

    I suppose we'll have to make a gate of our own then. Let me know when you're ready, .

    == Rewards == You will receive: *{{cost|19|84|50}} * 236000 [[XP]] * +300 [[reputation]] with [[Sunreaver Onslaught]]

    == Progress == Let's tear down this wall.

    == Completion == With your help, we're one giant step closer to our goal.

    ===Stage 1: Find Lost Explosives=== [[File:Tear Down This Wall 1 - Find the bomb.jpg|thumb|The bomb cart beset by Zandalari]] [[File:Tear Down This Wall 1 - Heavy Explosives.jpg|thumb|Heavy Explosives]] :Acquire the Heavy Explosives. *Acquire the Heavy Explosives {{co|48.4|50.9|Isle of Thunder||1}} :{{text|say|Scout Captain Elsia|It is chaos out there! Hurry!}} Once the cinematic is done, head from the central building in the Diremoor to the northeast and look for the Heavy Bomb. Before getting too far, a [[Zandalari Jaguar Warrior]] and a [[Zandalari Spiritbinder]] will jump the champion. After the ambush, head due north to find the bomb at {{co|48.4|50.9|Isle of Thunder||1}} which has fallen off the back of a cart that was beset (and currently guarded) by four more jaguar warriors.

The Zandalari should all be engaged, so mount up, try to ignore them, and ride east to Elsia's position at {{co|55.8|57.7|Isle of Thunder||1}} beneath the [[Conqueror's Terrace]] wall and speak with her: :The explosives! :Good work, .     ===Stage 3: Detonate Explosives=== [[File:Tear Down This Wall 3 - Al'chukla escaping.jpg|thumb|Al'chukla escaping.]] :''Light the fuse on the Heavy Explosives.'' *Detonate the Heavy Explosives {{co|56.3|56.1|Isle of Thunder||1}} :''Elsia runs toward the wall and plants the bomb.'' :{{text|say|Scout Captain Elsia|The charges are in place.     ===Final Stage: Kill War-God Al'chukla=== *Kill War-God Al'chukla {{co|54.7|45.7|Isle of Thunder||1}} :{{text|say|War-God Al'chukla|Destroy them, me brothers!}} :''Al'chukla realizes what's about to happen and jumps off the wall mere seconds before the bomb explodes, opening a massive hole! Elsia, Lor'themar and the Sunreaver Onslaught war party rush through.'' :{{text|yell|Lor'themar Theron|Spectacular! Move up - press onward. Show them no quarter!}} :{{text|yell|Scout Captain Elsia|This way! The Zandalari are falling back!}}

    Follow Elsia as she fights a path through three [[Zandalari Bloodguard]]s, one [[Spirit-Bound Sentry]], another bloodguard, another sentry, a [[Zandalari Acolyte]]/[[Zandalari Haruspex]] pull, another sentry, and one last harsupex on the way from the broken wall through the [[Conqueror's Terrace]] to the other side of the [[Emperor's Gate]]. Elsia stops short of War-God Al'chukla, getting a good look at him for the first time: :{{text|say|Scout Captain Elsia|What... what did they DO to that troll!?}}

    [[War-God Al'chukla]] is a level 90 charged Zandalari troll with 2.7 million health and the following special ability: {{abilities|Lightning Pole|range=40|Summon a swirling cloud of lightning that attracks players, dealing damage when they reach the center.|notes=1.84 sec cast. Will suck players in the pole's area of effect toward it. If players get too close, they trigger...}} *{{abilities|Lightning Pole|Fill an enemy's body with lightning, inflicting 24,512 to 28,488 Nature damage every 0.50 sec for 8 sec.}}

    Wind up an attack to start the encounter: :{{text|yell|War-God Al'chukla|Step forward! Witness the gift of the Thunder King!}} Al'chukla's only ability outside of basic melee is to summon a '''Lightning Pole''' at a player's feet that will suck the player toward the center of the area of effect and do 25K/0.5sec Nature damage to players if they get too close. Start running the instant he winds up a cast and only stop once the pole's fully formed.

    At 75%: :{{text|yell|War-God Al'chukla|I was chosen. My blood burns with his power!}}

    At 25%: :{{text|yell|War-God Al'chukla|Too much... the spirits cannot contain his might.}}

    Finish him off: :{{text|yell|War-God Al'chukla|You will be punished! Your life a lesson in futility!}} :{{text|yell|War-God Al'chukla|For Zandalar!!}}

    This completes the "Tear Down This Wall!" portion of [[Isle of Thunder (achievement)]], but the scenario continues in the finale.
