    Ghosts of K'aresh campaign

    thumb|Ghosts of K'aresh key art. The Ghost of K'aresh campaign covers all quests added in patch 11.2.0, mainly taking place in and around K'aresh as part of the greater The War Within campaign.

    Campaign: The Knife's Edge

    thumb|K'aresh zone map

    The five-chapter campaign is pushed to level players on login after the launch of the patch and starts in Foundation Hall of Dornogal. The first three chapters are available immediately, and chapter four is available in week two. The campaign is tracked in the achievement The Knife's Edge, which awards the Warband campsite The Fate of the Devoured.

    Once the first character in the warband completes the campaign through chapter four (That's a Wrap), gets their Reshii Wraps, and completes the Phase Diving quests through The Untethered Horror, alts can skip ahead to the same point by speaking with Om'en after landing in Tazavesh, then fly directly to Hashim at to pick up their Reshii Wraps. {{dialogue|I see that you are answering Locus-Walker's call again?

    }}}}

    During week one, the skip unlocked after completing The Tabiqa to end chapter three. In week two and beyond, the skip requires completing chapter four.

    A Shadowy Invitation

    Completing this chapter unlocks optional storylines all over K'aresh, awards 2,500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust, and rewards Explorer 1 necks, shoulders, rings, and weapons.

    Void Alliance

    This chapter awards 2,500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 helms, belts, and legs.

    Desert Power

    Completing this chapter awards the Reshii Wraps, 2,500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust, unlocks Phase Diving, world quests and Reshanor, the world boss. Additionally, quests reward Explorer 1 chests, gloves, boots, and bracers.

    Shadows En Garde

    This chapter unlocked the week of 12 August 2025. Completing this chapter awards 2,500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and rewards Explorer 1 trinkets, necks, and helms. Completing this chapter is also required to unlock the campaign skip for alts.

    The Light of K'aresh

    The final chapter of the campaign requires defeating Dimensius on any difficulty (including Story Mode or Raid Finder), and then sends players into Eco-Dome Al'dani, which must also be completed on any difficulty (including Follower Dungeon).

    Sojourner

    Eleven optional storylines are tracked in Sojourner of K'aresh. All optional chapters are unlocked after completing the first chapter of the main campaign.

    Of Boughs and Bonds

    Find Om'fennad at Hosaas' Rest on the south shore of the western island of K'aresh to begin. This chapter awards 500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 belts.

    On a Technicality

    Find Associate Xy'nell outside the Xy Imports Offices in northeast Tazavesh to begin. This chapter awards 500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 legs.

    In Search of Darkness

    Find Magister Umbric at Shan'dorah on the southeastern shore of the northwestern island of K'aresh to begin. This chapter awards 500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 boots.

    Untethered Potential

    Find Researcher Essarae at Shan'dorah to begin. This chapter has no notable rewards.

    Chasing Echoes

    Find Om'dalan at the Ruins of Yaathron to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 necks.

    Avoiding the Void

    Find Driruj at The Oasis to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 chests.

    Priest of the Old Ways

    Find Outcast Hizfran on the eastern edge of the eastern island in the Merciless Wastes southeast of The Oasis to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 shoulders.

    That Tazavesh Taste

    Find Trader Ba'kril inside Ba'kril's Iniquities in central Tazavesh to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 wrists.

    Lost and Found Storage

    Find Vaultkeeper Xy'vez in Astral Getaways in northeastern Tazavesh to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 gloves.

    Anyway, Here's Firewall

    Find Assistant Engineer Om'vek at Eco-Dome: Primus to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 rings.

    A Stranger's Gift

    Find Om'taha outside Naakroa on the western island to begin. This chapter awards 350 reputation with the K'aresh Trust and Explorer 1 rings.

    Ecological Succession

    Starting the week of 12 August 2025, after completing chapter four of the campaign, players unlock the Ecological Succession system with a short questline starting from Om'talad near Tazarest in Tazavesh.

    Eight storylines are then tracked in Otherworldly Ecologist. Players will get one or two questlines a week.

    The Bees Knees

    This chapter opened up the week of 12 August 2025.

    Nesingwary Necessities

    This chapter opened up in the second half of the week of 12 August 2025.

    Roaming Free

    This chapter opened up in the first half of the week of 19 August 2025.

    Fox-strut and Lil' Lapbugs

    These two connected chapters opened up in the second half of the week of 19 August 2025.

    Rays of Sunshine

    This chapter (through step 9) opened the week of 26 August 2025. Step 10 and beyond opened up in the second half of the week.

    Hard K'arroc

    This chapter opened the week of 2 September 2025.

    A Slither of Snakes

    This chapter opened in the second half of the week of 2 September 2025.

    Other

    Diving Into Phase Diving

    Unlocked after The Tabiqa and acquiring the Reshii Wraps. Gives access to Phase Diving.

    A Key Threat

    Unlocked at Renown 3 with the K'aresh Trust, warrants are weekly collect-and-kill quests. The subsequent quests to the top one are on a weekly rotation. Each week is worth 500 reputation with the K'aresh Trust.

    Ba'eth's Request

    Unlocked at Renown 9 with the K'aresh Trust. Completing this time-gated chapter awards the Blue Barry mount.
