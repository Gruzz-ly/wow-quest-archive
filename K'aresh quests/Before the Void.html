    ==Objectives== Convince [[Soul-Scribe]] to join forces. * Dagger returned * Talk to [[Xal'atath]] * [[Tabiqa Dagger]] (provided)

    ==Description== You've impressed these [[Wastelanders]] with your violent prowess, my champion. Well done. Now let me do my work.

    I know what these former oracles want. They dream of hearing the [[Song of K'aresh]] once more. I must make them believe it is possible, then pull on that string of desire. Soon we will have our army, and another Reshii Ribbon.

    First, let's return the Tabiqa ritual dagger.

    ==Rewards== You will receive one of: [[Wastelander's Worn Handwraps]] [[Wastelander's Worn Gloves]] [[Wastelander's Worn Grips]] [[Wastelander's Worn Gauntlets]]

    You will also receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 12,200 [[XP]] * 10x [[Valorstones]]

    ==Completion== We would gladly war against the Shadowguard. But I cannot give up this Reshii Ribbon to someone I don't trust.

    Promises of revenge are easy. Trust is hard won among the Wastelanders.
