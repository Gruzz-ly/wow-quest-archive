    ==Objectives== Meet [[Xal'atath]] at the [[Oasis]] eco-dome. *Report to [[Om'rajula]] at the Oasis.

    ==Description== [[Soul-Scribe]] would fight for us, even die for our cause. Yet she still denies us her [[Reshii Ribbon]]. She sees it as a tool of the [[Ravel]], and her oaths are getting in the way of our mission.

    If she could feel the presence of the [[Worldsoul]] [[K'aresh]] once again, she would give anything to protect him--even the ribbon. The Oasis is the [[K'aresh Trust|Trust]]'s eco-dome that has a stable biome. The perfect place to commune with a Worldsoul, if one still exists.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * 10x [[Valorstones]] {{cost|2|34|}} 1,200 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== Our research here is essential to the future of K'aresh. That we have stabilized this area means that we can replicate this feat in other domes--perhaps even heal the shattered remains of K'aresh.

    ==Completion== This is most irregular. [[Ve'nari]] may have asked that we extend all courtesy to the Harbinger and her champion. But these Wastelander barbarians?

    I hope Ve'nari knows what she's doing.
