    '''On a Technicality''' concludes the optional chapter of the same name of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. Find [[Associate Xy'nell]] outside the [[Xy Imports Offices]] in northeast [[Tazavesh]] to begin.

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Coordinator Xy'thir.jpg|Coordinator Xy'thir|Coordinator Xy'thir exists as the current head of Xy Imports. His mismanagement and threats have led to a hostile work environment.}}

    Confront Coordinator Xy'thir in Tazavesh. * Speak with [[Port Authority Zo'rau]] (Optional) * [[Coordinator Xy'thir]] defeated

    ==Description== It's time. I cannot tell you what to expect, . I only hope this is the last day I must keep such a dreadful oath.
