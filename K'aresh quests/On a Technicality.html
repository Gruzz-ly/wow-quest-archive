    On a Technicality concludes the optional chapter of the same name of the Ghosts of K'aresh campaign. Find Associate Xy'nell outside the Xy Imports Offices in northeast Tazavesh to begin.

    Objectives

    Confront Coordinator Xy'thir in Tazavesh. * Speak with Port Authority Zo'rau (Optional) * Coordinator Xy'thir defeated

    Description

    It's time. I cannot tell you what to expect, . I only hope this is the last day I must keep such a dreadful oath.

    Let us hurry. Port Authority Zo'rau has already begun questioning Coordinator Xy'thir.

    Rewards

    You will receive one of: * Legwraps of the Auditbreaker * Breeches of the Auditbreaker * Greaves of the Auditbreaker * Legguards of the Auditbreaker

    You will also receive: * * 15,250 XP * 15x Valorstones * +500 reputation with K'aresh Trust
