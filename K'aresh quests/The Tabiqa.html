    The Tabiqa concludes the third chapter of the Ghosts of K'aresh campaign. Chapter four begins starting the week of 12 August 2025, but in the meantime, the Reshii Wraps quest chain opens, unlocking access to Phase Diving. Equip it, then speak with Shad'anis nearby to start Another World. That leads to Hashim at Overlook Zo'Shuul who offers Wrapped Up. Completing this quest also unlocks world quests in K'aresh.

    Objectives

    Help Soul-Scribe with the ritual. * Meet Soul-Scribe by the water * Pollen and petals placed * Vial of water placed * Ritual dagger placed * Talk to Soul-Scribe

    Description

    My followers long for the days when they were oracles. They yearn to hear the Song of K'aresh. I am a realist. The evil of the Ravel, of Dimensius--they have destroyed the beauty that was our Worldsoul.

    But you have gathered the items for the Tabiqa. So I will attempt it. When I hear the void of K'aresh's absence, there will be consequences.

    Rewards

    You will be able to choose one of these rewards: * Precise Fiber * Chronomantic Fiber * Energizing Fiber * Dexterous Fiber

    You will also receive: * * 12,200 XP * +2,500 reputation with K'aresh Trust * Reshii Wraps * 3x Ethereal Strands * 15x Valorstones
