    '''A Song for Our Future''' concludes the final chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]], and that of the [[The War Within campaign]].

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Soul-Scribe.jpg|Soul-Scribe|Notorious leader of the Wastelanders. Her guilt for not being able to restore K'aresh is only matched by her hatred of Ve'nari's attempt at redemption and the eco-dome project.}} Defeat [[Soul-Scribe (tactics)|Soul-Scribe]] within [[Eco-Dome Al'dani|Eco-Dome: Al'dani]]. Soul-Scribe defeated Talk to Ve'nari

    ==Description== There is a site, Eco-Dome: Al'dani, which I have kept hidden. It is built on the remains of one of our most sacred places with a powerful connection to K'aresh. It is the site of our greatest triumph of the project. This is the place where we can take the [[Worldsoul]]'s essence so it can survive.

    This is also the eco-dome where Soul-Scribe and her Wastelanders are attacking.

    Clear the way for me one last time, . For K'aresh to survive, we need both the dome and Soul-Scribe intact.

    ==Rewards== You will learn how to create: * {{abilities|The Fate of the Devoured|Unlocks [[Campsites|Warband Campsite]] The Fate of the Devoured.}}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|93|60|}} * 1,200 [[XP]] * 15x [[Valorstones]]

    ==Completion== What transpired here will echo for years to come. The change will not happen within a day, a week, or even months. But with hope rekindled, we will get there, and we will be united once again.

    You have saved us all, champion.
