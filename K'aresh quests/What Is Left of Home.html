    '''What Is Left of Home''' begins the second chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]].

    ==Objectives== Speak to citizens of [[Tazavesh]] and members of the [[K'aresh Trust|Trust]]. Talk to the [[Ba'orr|Innkeeper]] Stay a while and listen to [[Xal'atath]] in the inn (Optional) Talk to the [[Om'sirik|Quartermaster]] Talk to the [[Om'thenai|Flight Master]] *Stay a while and listen to [[Alleria]] at the docks

    ==Description== [[Dimensius]] threatens us once more. There is no one who knows his methods better than his former Harbinger, Xal'atath. I would not say we can trust her, but we can work with her. She fears the All-Devouring's wrath after her betrayal.

    We can't afford to turn away any potential ally. Think how many we will save from the [[void lord|Void Lord]]'s hunger. Get your bearings in this city the four of us saved together. I will convince Alleria.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{cost|11|70}} 6,100 [[XP]] *15 [[Valorstones]]

    ==Progress== There will be a day when [[K'aresh]] is restored.

    ==Completion== This is what we are fighting for, . We can only save fragments of K'aresh, but we can prevent other worlds from sharing our dreadful fate.
