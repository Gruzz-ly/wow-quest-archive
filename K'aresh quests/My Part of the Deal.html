    {{Questbox | id = 90517 | name = My Part of the Deal | disambigcat = The Knife's Edge | displaycat = The Knife's Edge | category = K'aresh | level = {{level|K'aresh}} | levelreq = {{level|K'aresh}} | start = [[Ve'nari]] {{co|47.1|54.5|K'aresh}} | experience = 12,200 | money = {{cost|23|40|}} | rewards = 15x [[Valorstones]] | reputation = +2,500 [[K'aresh Trust]] | previous = [[Unwrapped and Unraveled]] | next = [[Eco-Dome: Rhovan (quest)]] }}

    '''My Part of the Deal''' concludes the second chapter of the [[Ghosts of K'aresh campaign]]. The third chapter, "Desert Power", starts immediately after with [[Eco-Dome: Rhovan (quest)]].

    ==Objectives== Take [[Ve'nari]]'s [[Reshii Ribbon]]. * Ve'nari's Reshii Ribbon acquired

    ==Description== The threat of the [[Shadowguard]] to [[Eco-Dome: Primus]] is over. My Reshii Ribbon is no longer needed to protect this asset. Take it. Use it wisely. Protect the greatest investment of all--our existence.

    Stay focused! [[Xal'atath|The Harbinger]] has already driven off one member of your team. Once you get enough Reshii Ribbons, you still need to fight [[Dimensius]]. Find allies who will stand with us, in addition to artifacts.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 12,200 [[XP]] * +2,500 [[reputation]] with [[K'aresh Trust]] * 15x [[Valorstones]]

    ==Progress== The Reshii Ribbons are a means to get you and your allies to Dimensius. You will need at least two more if this plan is going to work.

    ==Completion== You have my ribbon. Go--track down the rest. Have [[Locus-Walker]] contact the other members of the [[Ravel]]. Ask the Harbinger if she knows any ancient horrors that have acquired one. Remember rule twelve--it's who you know.

    ==Notes== Upon accept: :{{Text|Say|Ve'nari|Take my Reshii Ribbon. Defeat the All-Devouring.}}
