    Where the Void Gathers

    {{Questbox | id = 84856 | name = Where the Void Gathers | disambigcat = The Knife's Edge | displaycat = The Knife's Edge | category = K'aresh | level = {{level|K'aresh}} | levelreq = {{level|K'aresh}} | start = [[Xal'atath]] {{co|48.2|36.7|K'aresh}} | experience = 12,200 | rewards = 10x [[Valorstones]] | money = {{cost|23|40|}} | previous = [[The Reshii Ribbon]] | next = [[Eco-Dome: Primus (quest)]] }}

    ==Objectives== Search [[Shadow Point]] in [[Untethered Space]] with [[Xal'atath]]'s help. * Talk to Xal'atath * Conduit examined * Conduit Monitor Station slain * Manaforge entrance examined

    ==Description== With [[Locus-Walker]]'s [[Reshii Ribbon]], I can transmute your flesh to energy. It won't hurt much.

    You will have the ability to travel within [[Dimensius]]'s aura without being destroyed. There you can search for a path into the manaforge. You will travel in Untethered Space as a creature of energy, ignored by the [[Shadowguard]]. Their void beasts however... stay well away or be devoured.

    Talk to me when you are ready. Time is of the essence.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|23|40|}} * 12,200 [[XP]] * 10x [[Valorstones]]

    ==Progress== We will need more than one Reshii Ribbon if we want to reach Dimensius with a force capable of stopping his emergence.

    ==Completion== Through this, you have learned what I already knew.

    [[Dimensius]] is a terrible threat. We must work together, , if we are to survive.
