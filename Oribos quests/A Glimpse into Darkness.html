    A Glimpse into Darkness

    {{Questbox | id = 61716 | name = A Glimpse into Darkness | category = Oribos | level = {{level|Maldraxxus}} | start = [[Highlord Bolvar Fordragon]] {{co|40.3|68.7|Oribos}} | experience = 7,500 | rewards = [[Seal of Fordragon]] | money = {{cost|25|27|20}} | previous = [[Request of the Highlord]] | next = [[Journey to Ardenweald]] }}

    ==Objectives== Witness Bolvar's vision of the [[Maw]]. * Focus on the [[Helm of Domination]] * Behold [[Bolvar Fordragon|Bolvar]]'s Vision * Discuss the Vision

    ==Description== When I put the [[Lich King]]'s crown upon my head, I wanted the [[Azeroth|world]] to believe I was dead. Even my daughter, [[Taelia Fordragon|Taelia]]. I... thought a lie would be kinder than the truth.

    But she is no longer the little girl I left in [[Kul Tiras]]. I see my choice was a selfish one.

    She and [[Calia Menethil|Lady Menethil]] have asked me to peer into the Maw to see if our allies yet live. It is a dangerous request, but one I cannot refuse.

    Help me focus on the broken helm. Together, we may find a glimpse of truth.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * [[Seal of Fordragon]] * {{cost|25|27|20}} * 7,500 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== There is much to do.

    ==Completion== A troubling vision indeed. But we must not give up hope.
