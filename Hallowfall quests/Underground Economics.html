    {{Questbox | id = 82217 | name = Underground Economics | category = Hallowfall | level = {{level|Hallowfall}} | start = [[General Steelstrike]] | experience = 11,150 | money = {{Cost|23|40}} | shareable = Yes | previous = [[A Batty Request]], [[Does Anyone Like Wasps?]], [[Regular Fiber]] }}

    '''Underground Economics''' concludes the Striking Steel optional chapter of the [[Hallowfall storyline]]. [[Auralia Steelstrike]] will start the quest in [[Mereldar]] once players come across her while on [[Nightwatch]], during the third chapter of the main storyline.

    ==Objectives== Assist the [[Arathi]] with trade negotiations in [[Dunelle's Kindness]]. * [[Arathi Trade Goods]] (3) * Display Trade Goods (3) * Convince [[Andee Seabraid]] to trade with the Arathi * Convince [[Teha]] to Trade with the Arathi

    ==Description== After the [[Day of Darkness]], we haven't seen or heard from the earthen. I don't know why.

    We used to be on good terms, I'd even have considered [[Merrix]] a friend, albeit an odd one.

    We need this to go well.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: {{Cost|23|40}} 11,150 [[XP]]

    ==Progress== We need this trade deal to go through.

    ==Completion== Flames preserve me. that went better than I had hoped.

    Thank you, once again. You have done us a great service.
