    Objectives

    Slay 6 Fiery Behemoths or Seething Pyrelords. Invader slain at Sethria's Roost* x6

    Description

    We need to place pressure on the assault camp at Sethria's Roost. Ragnaros' minions are using this area as a point to push their battlefront beyond the bounds of Hyjal. I'm putting you in charge of a small squad to deal with them.

    I have selected an elite group of fighters to assist you. Travel to Sethria's Roost. Your compatriots have been instructed to meet you once you arrive. Then put an end to the elementals' assault.

    Rewards

    You will receive: 18g 80s 86750 XP (or at max level) +350 reputation Guardians of Hyjal Mark of the World Tree x2

    Progress

    I believe your assignment requires you to be in Hyjal, .

    Completion

    A job well done, soldier. While our mission in the Firelands is critical, we must not forget about the well-being of Hyjal.

    Speak with my brother again tomorrow, and he will have another squad prepared for you.

    Notes

    Head back to the Sanctuary of Malorne to pick up this quest. Return to Malfurion's Breach on the Molten Front to turn in.

    Sethria's Roost has been taken over by level 85 elite Fiery Behemoths and Seething Pyrelords with 774k health.
