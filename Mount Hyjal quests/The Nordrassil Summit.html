    The Nordrassil Summit

    {{Questbox | name=The Nordrassil Summit | start=[[Thrall]] {{co|60.6|31.5|Mount Hyjal}} | end=[[Aggra]] {{co|60.2|30.1|Mount Hyjal}} | id=29326 | level={{level|cata-max}} | levelreq={{level|cata-max}} | category=Elemental Bonds | reputation=+75 [[Earthen Ring]] | experience=52050 | previous=[[The Call of the World-Shaman]] | next=[[Into Slashing Winds]] }}

    ==Objectives== Speak with [[Thrall]] under the [[Nordrassil|World Tree]] in [[Mount Hyjal]] and witness the [[Earthen Ring]] ceremony. *Witness the Ceremony

    ==Description== Hello, ! My brothers and sisters of the Earthen Ring have spoke well of your deeds in the wake of the [[Cataclysm]]. But much remains to be done if we are to heal this broken world.

    Walk with me, friend. Preparations for a ceremony are underway, a ritual that I hope will be a turning point in our battle against the [[Twilight's Hammer]].

    When all is done, you will be able to spread the word about our triumph here.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: 52050 [[XP]] (or {{coppercost|124050}} at max level) +75 [[reputation]] with [[Earthen Ring]]

    ==Completion== It can't be over. We mustn't give up hope!

    ==Notes== Speak to Thrall again: :I believe [[Aggra]] is almost here, and the other [[dragon aspect|aspects]] have assembled. :Are you ready to begin, ? :{{gossip|I am ready.}}
