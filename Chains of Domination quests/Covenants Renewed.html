    '''Covenants Renewed''' is the third and last quest in the final chapter of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]], "What Lies Ahead", at [[:Renown]] 58.

    ==Objectives== Speak to the [[Primus]], then claim the [[Sigil]]s from each [[covenant]]. Speak to the Primus{{co|63.0|25.4|Korthia}} Sigils claimed (4) *Present sigils to the Primus

    ==Description== Our efforts were focused on preventing [[Zovaal|the Jailer]] from shattering the barrier around the [[Maw]] and invading the rest of the [[Shadowlands]].

    Instead, he expanded the Maw itself to envelop [[Oribos]] and claim the [[Arbiter|Aribiter]]'s{{Sic}} sigil.

    I do not fully understand what we witnessed atop [[Torghast]]. But the Primus certainly does.

    It is time to enact his contingency plan. The covenants will arrive with their sigils shortly.

    Inform the Primus that we are ready to begin.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|12|87}} * +1,500 [[reputation]] with [[Death's Advance]] [[Concentrated Anima]] x5 [[Emblem of Renown]]

    ==Completion== Excellent, mortal. You have done your part.

    Now my work begins.

    ===Post-quest dialogue=== After the dialogue with the Primus completes, Bolvar returns to his old spot next to [[Tal-Galan]] while Jaina and Thrall head to different ends of Keeper's Respite. Several "Stay awhile and listen" dialogues are now available from NPCs throughout the respite, as shown by a large {{Gossip icon}} icon above the NPC's head. The icon disappears after the first time the player triggers the dialogue, but the option remains permanently available from the NPCs in case the player wants to see the dialogue again.

    In addition, a [[Misplaced Venthyr Tome]] on the lower level of the respite offers the optional extra quest [[A Loose Thread]].
