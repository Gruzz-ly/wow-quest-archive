    Help [[Uther]] forge a connection to the Remnant of Justice. *[[Remnant of Justice]] placed {{co|40.2|20.4|Bastion}}

    ==Description== The arrival of Uther's wounded soul was the impetus for many events that led us to our current struggles.

    [[Devos]]'s broken trust. Her involvement with [[Helya]]. The rise of the [[Forsworn]].
