    '''Focusing the Eye''' is the climax of the "Focusing the Eye" chapter of the [[Chains of Domination campaign]]. Upon completion of this quest, players will be free of the [[Eye of the Jailer (mechanic)|Eye of the Jailer]] effect in the [[Maw]].

    ==Objectives== {{questportrait|Eye of the Jailer.png|Eye of the Jailer|The Eye of the Jailer searches the Maw for enemies of the Jailer.}} Go to the Terminus Platform and use the Ilskahorn to summon the Wrath of Odyn. [[Ilskahorn]] used {{co|42.3|21.1|Maw||1}} [[Eye of the Jailer]] defeated

    ==Description== [[File:Focusing the Eye.jpg|thumb|Focusing the Eye]] The combination of grief and rage you have harvested is sufficient for your needs. Most likely.

    If you insist on seeing this ill-advised endeavor through, use the horn at the Terminus Platform, where the River of Souls flows upward to Torghast. The energies there are most potent indeed.

    The presence of your golden allies should draw the Jailer's Eye. I only hope this does not prove to be the end of you. I hate to see an investment wasted.

    ==Rewards== You will unlock access to: *{{Abilities|Blind the Eye|The Eye of the Jailer no longer falls upon you in the Maw.|icon=inv_misc_eye_04}}

    You will also receive: * {{cost|25|74|}} * 8,150 [[XP]] * +500 reputation with [[Ve'nari]]

    ==Completion== There will be songs immortalizing the deeds of this day, .

    ==Notes== [[File:Chosenseeker Eye.jpg|thumb|Chosenseeker Eye]]

    Head back into Gorgoa, north to the platform where [[Ekphoras, Herald of Grief]] normally appears, then use the bonus ability: :{{Abilities|Use Ilskahorn|Summons the Wrath of Odyn to challenge the Eye of the Jailer.|icon=inv_misc_horn_04|notes=3 sec cast}}

    :{{text|say|Danica|We hear the call of Ilskahorn, ! The Eye will be compelled to focus upon us. Prepare for glorious battle!}} :{{text|yell|Danica|To think that Odyn's eye could be twisted into something so vile... Strike this abomination down!}} :{{text|yell|Danica|The smaller eyes protect it, Maw Walker. Use my spear to ground them... then destroy them!}}

    While in the encounter, the usual Gorgoan Lament effect is suppressed, as players instead gain '''Odyn's Wrath''': :{{Abilities|Odyn's Wrath|Summons the Wrath of Odyn.|icon=inv_misc_horn_04|notes=Untimed buff that increases damage by 20%, and regens 5% of max health every 4 seconds}}

    To start out, all of the eyes are unattackable by players. Watch out for the occasional swirlies, as getting caught by one will debuff players caught in it with ''three stacks'' of '''Shattered Soul''': :{{Abilities|Shattered Soul|A shard of the target's soul inflicts X Shadow damage every 1 sec., reduces movement speed by 20%, and damage and healing done by 30% until the target collects their soul shard.|icon=sha_spell_shadow_shadesofdarkness|notes=30 sec debuff. Stacks three times. Players can also get 30 yards away from the soul fragments to lift the debuff.}}

    Run into a spear somewhere on the platform, then use the bonus ability '''Valorous Harpoon''' to stun one of the [[Chosenseeker Eye]]s guarding the Eye of the Jailer: :{{Abilities|Valorous Harpoon|Throw a harpoon to pull down one of the Choseenseeker Eyes protecting the Eye of the Jailer.|range=100|notes=Instant|icon=inv_polearm_2h_odynspear_d_02}}

    This makes the targeted eye attackable, so defeat it, then rinse and repeat until all the smaller eyes are dead. A cutscene plays: :{{text|say|Danica|The Eye is vulnerable!}} :{{text|say|Danica|Strike swiftly, sisters! For Odyn!}} :''The four val'kyr wail on the eye.'' :{{text|say|Danica|Our enemy flees. After it, sisters!}} :''It turns tail, flying in the direction of Torghast.'' :{{text|say|Danica|The Eye retreats to its master's side.}} :{{text|say|Danica|Your part in this is done, Maw Walker. Come.}}

    On completion, the [[Eye of the Jailer (mechanic)|Eye of the Jailer]] mechanic goes away, and the UI on screen is removed.
