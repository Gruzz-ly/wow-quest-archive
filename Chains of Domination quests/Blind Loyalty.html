    ==Objectives== [[File:Blind Loyalty.jpg|thumb|Nikolon and Adrestes at the [[Eonian Archives]]]] Join [[Polemarch Adrestes]] at [[Elysian Hold]] for an audience with [[Kyrestia the Firstborne|the Archon]]. Speak to [[Kyrian Courier]] to fly to Elysian Hold (Optional) Meet with Adrestes at Elysian Hold {{co|55.7|41.9|Elysian Hold}}

    ==Description== The path forward for the [[kyrian]] is clear to me now.

    First we will speak with [[Nikolon]]. I urge you to stay and listen.

    Afterward, we must return to Elysian Hold.

    There I will request an audience with the Archon, and I need you all by my side as I do so.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: *{{cost|2|57|40}} * 1,000 [[XP]] * +75 [[reputation]] with [[The Ascended]]

    ==Progress== The kyrian must forge a new path.

    ==Completion==

    It is an honor. But we have only just begun.

    Nikolon has some gossip: :The anger, animosity... we must put it aside. :The forsworn deserve to be listened to, to be respected. As do the kyrian.
