    {{Questbox | id = 63576 | name = The First Move | category = Chains of Domination | level = {{Level|Sl-max}} | levelreq = {{Level|Sl-max}} | start = Automatic | end = [[Highlord Bolvar Fordragon]] {{co|40.2|68.1|Oribos}} | money = {{cost|2|57|40}} | shareable = No | previous = [[The Weak Link]]; [[The Jailer's Grasp]] | next = [[A Gathering of Covenants]] }} [[File:The First Move.jpg|thumb|The covenant representatives in Oribos.]]

    '''The First Move''' begins the [[Chains of Domination campaign]] -- the [[patch 9.1]] portion of the [[Shadowlands campaign]]. This quest will be pushed to all eligible players automatically on login. To be eligible, see the prerequisites in the [[Chains of Domination campaign#Unlocking the campaign|unlocking section]] of the campaign article.

    ==Objectives== Speak with [[Polemarch Adrestes]] in [[Oribos]]. *Speak with Polemarch Adrestes in Oribos {{co|41.3|68.0|Oribos}}

    ==Description== While the covenants labor to defend the [[Shadowlands]], [[the Jailer]] has not sat idle within [[Torghast]]. It seems he intends to set the next phase of his scheme into motion.

    Polemarch Adrestes has brought us disturbing news from [[Elysian Hold]]. He will recount what he witnessed once you arrive.

    Make haste. I fear that time grows short.

    ==Rewards== You will receive: * {{cost|2|57|40}}

    ==Progress== We will continue to fight.

    ==Completion== Anduin... now the Jailer's pawn...

    We must take immediate action, . It is time for the covenants to convene.

    ==Notes== On accept: :{{text|say|Highlord Bolvar Fordragon|Maw Walker, Polemarch Adrestes brings grim tidings from Bastion. You are needed in Oribos at once.}}

    On approach in the Enclave: :{{text|say|Lady Jaina Proudmoore|By the Light... Adrestes, this cannot be!}}

    Alts of players who have completed the first two chapters [[Chains of Domination campaign#Skips|can skip ahead]] by speaking with Bolvar: :We know the Jailer is watching us. And that he has Anduin. :Our only hope is for you and other heroes to make the covenants strong, . :The day will soon come that the Jailer and Sylvanas reveal the next phase of their plan. :When they do, it will require all our strength to overcome them. :{{gossip|I have heard this tale before. {{text|red|}}}}

    Be warned that this skip will forfeit 10x [[Stygian Ember]], a [[Death's Advance War Chest]], some reputation with [[Death's Advance]] and [[Ve'nari]], and the 4x [[Emblem of Renown]] granted by the quests!

    In attendance are [[Bolvar Fordragon]], [[Jaina Proudmoore]], [[Thrall]], [[Baine Bloodhoof]], [[Darion Mograine]], [[Nadjia the Mistblade]], [[General Draven]], [[Lady Moonberry]], [[Plague Deviser Marileth]], [[Secutor Mevix]], [[Polemarch Adrestes]], and [[Kleia]]. Speak with Adrestes: :We were caught unaware. The Jailer's power has grown more than any of us could have imagined. :{{gossip|}}
